EdLab Seminar: Evan Samek from imagiLabs

Jun 28, 2013 12:57 PM

imagiLabs deliver exciting educational experiences that help children discover their interests and passions. ChallengeBox, their first product, combines adventure games with off-screen, DIY projects and contextualized learning. Kind of like Montessori in a box, delivered to the home or classroom. In this EdLab Seminar, we will demonstrate one of our prototype boxes for discussion and feedback. Evan Samek is a Storyteller and Business Designer. He got in trouble in elementary school for renting pencils to his classmates. Evan was previously head of operations for SpeakLike, a translation technology company. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by August 5th by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account and click on ). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY).
