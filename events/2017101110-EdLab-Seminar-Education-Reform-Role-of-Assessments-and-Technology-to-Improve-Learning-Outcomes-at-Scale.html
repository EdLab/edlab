Topic: Role of Assessments and Technology to Improve Learning Outcomes

Leader: Pranav Kothari is currently the Domain Architect and Vice President of the Large Scale Education Programs at Educational Initiatives (EI). He was a member of the development team of the Mindspark product, which is a technology-based, personalized and adaptive learning tool for Mathematics and Language. Description: Technology has held a lot of potential to improving learning outcomes at scale. Only a few technology-based initiatives, however, have shown clear learning gains. Recently, a J-PAL RCT conducted on Mindspark - a personalized adaptive software - showed impressive gains. Harvard also wrote a HBS case study on their efforts to bring personalized adaptive learning programs to government schools. The speaker will also be describing the journey from using research from large-scale assessments to build a learning solution and deploying it in settings ranging from rural/tribal parts to elite private schools in India and elsewhere. He will also highlight their experience of doing multiple forms of assessments in government schools as well as building capacity within government institutions to do student learning assessments as part of their entrepreneurial journey that started in 2001 to improve systemic school quality.
