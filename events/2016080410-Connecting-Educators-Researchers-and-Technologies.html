Connecting Educators, Researchers, and Technologies

Aug 4, 2016 10:00 AM - Aug 4, 2016 2:00 PM

How can we create the conditions to try new technologies in schools and classrooms, and share knowledge of those that improve learning and teaching?

Teachers College recently launched TCEdTech, a initiative focused on ensuring that the technology reaching our nation's teachers and students effectively supports learning outcomes. As part of this initiative, we plan to build a network schools, educators, and researchers to support the selection and assessment of learning technologies.

As we think through what it would take to build such a network, we want teachers' input as to how we can meaningfully connect our academic researchers with the classrooms these technologies are meant to serve.
