Designing What's Next: Post Grad

Apr 11, 2019 9:15 AM - Apr 11, 2019 11:45 AM

This workshop will empower women at Teachers College and beyond to make informed and intentional decisions for what's next. The workshop applies design thinking principles to transitional times through three sessions covering 1. analyzing our current state, 2. managing debt and building financial abundance, and 3. goal setting for the future.

9:15am Doors open

Attendees will pick up the worksheet packet as they walk in. 

Light snacks will be provided at this time


9:30am Event starts


9:30-10am Mini workshop one

Small groups of 4-6 will sit at tables. Workshop leaders will provide introductory remarks, the agenda, and walk participants through how to complete the worksheet. Attendees will discuss collaborate with table mates. 


10-10:45 Speaker 1

The speaker may have a slide presentation to educate attendees.


10:45-11:00 Break


11:00-11:45 Speaker 2

The speaker may have a slide presentation and conduct experiential activities for attendees. 


11:45-12:00 Wrap up
