Designing Learning Spaces Workshop

Apr 28, 2014 11:30 AM

Join us for a design thinking workshop hosted to help Teachers College re-imagine its learning spaces! Design Thinking for Education is an interactive and exciting way to use creative thinking to imagine new possibilities for learning. No design experience required! Come work in teams and re-imagine the spaces we learn in. The aim of this hour and a half design thinking workshop is to give participants an opportunity to learn and use the design thinking method in a hands-on way and allow participants an opportunity to help Teachers College re-imagine learning spaces in the college. The workshop will feature two design challenges, one pre-determined and one participant generated. When: Tuesday, May 13th, 3pm-4:30pm Where: 306 Russell Hall
