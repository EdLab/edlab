EdLab Seminar: Bursting Media Bubbles

Aug 22, 2018 12:00 PM - Aug 22, 2018 1:30 PM

Annafi Wahed, founder and editor of TheFlipSide.io, will discuss how her experience working for the Hillary campaign shaped her understanding of what is needed to bridge the gap between liberals and conservatives in the world of Facebook. The Flip Side is a daily digest of liberal and conservative commentary, and it's geared toward the average person who wants to burst their media bubble, but does not have the time or desire to read 20 op-eds a day.

Topic: Civic Engagement 

Leaders: Annafi Wahed, Founder of The Flip Side 

Description: In the months following the election, as talk of “fake news” and media bias became commonplace, Annafi set out to burst her own media bubble and the bubbles of those around her.

Pre-Read/Media: Check out Annafi’s two op-eds in the WSJ (see here and here) and her appearance on Fox and Friends
