EdLab Seminar: Kara Carpenter from Teachley

Sep 17, 2012 4:07 PM

Kara Carpenter will join us for this seminar to discuss her work at Teachley. Teachley is an educational technology company founded by teachers and cognitive psychologists. We believe learning software should promote rich conceptual understanding in addition to fostering strong skill development. Teachley provides a unique blend of instruction, learning tools, scaffolded support, and feedback within a series of fun and engaging games. We offer a high quality alternative to rote, drill-based practice. Teachley's first app, Think Facts, gives kids tools to visualize the different strategies they can use to solve single-digit addition problems. Characters provide a narrative context and help explain each strategy, while individualized progress reports help kids keep track of what they've learned. Open to all members of the university community.
