Hard Times at Douglass High: A No Child Left Behind Report Card

Jun 12, 2008 10:06 PM

Join Gary Natriello, and Oscar-winning filmmakers Alan and Susan Raymond (“I Am a Promise: The Children of Stanton Elementary School”) as they watch and discuss the Raymond's latest HBO Documentary Film: ‘Hard Times At Douglass High: A No Child Left Behind Report Card.' The screening will take place on the 5th floor of the Gottesman Libraries. For more information about the film, please visit: http://www.hbo.com/docs/docuseries/hardtimes/index.html
