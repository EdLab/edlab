Unpacking the policy backpack: Considering the ideas we carry

Dec 18, 2017 4:45 PM - Dec 18, 2017 7:00 PM

A “school day” of interactive experiences to explore important issues in education policy

Join the EPSA’s 2017 Education Policy cohort for an evening of exploring the implications of education policy in a variety of contexts. During this event, you will attend different “classes” that will expand your understanding of topics ranging from the “geography of education policy” to out-of-school learning and everything in between.
