EdLab Seminar: Launchpad Check-in

Apr 2, 2012 11:28 AM

At this seminar, the Launchpad team will lead an exercise to expand the lab's current launchpad-related activities. We will also discuss EdLab's outreach activities at TC's upcoming Academic Festival. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by April 3rd by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account and click on the Contact button to send a message). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY). For detailed directions within the College visit TC Route Finder: http://maps.tc-library.org
