EdLab Seminar: The EdLab Account and the TC Smith Learning Theater

Feb 24, 2016 12:08 PM

At this seminar, the EdLab team will explore creative ways to leverage the EdLab Account to advance "events" in the Learning Theater. The 2016 opening of the Teachers College Smith Learning Theater will generate new opportunities for the EdLab to support the work of the TC Community and advance its educational mission. A key feature of the TC Smith Learning Theater is that all users of the space will be required to create an EdLab Account and RSVP via an EdLab Events application. In your small color groups, generate a list of possible ways we might use data from the EdLab Account system to create more powerful learning experiences in the TC Smith Learning Theater. You ideas can pertain to pre-event activities, in-event experiences, and post-event followup activities. You have 20 minutes to discuss and write your ideas on the oval.
