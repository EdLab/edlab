EdLab Seminar: Jesse Willmon

Mar 28, 2014 10:20 AM

Jesse Willmon will be leading us through an interactive illustration project. As a group we will explore how illustration can help us expand our creative process, encourage collaborative thinking, and open our minds to new discoveries in the creative process. Jesse is an Illustrator and Designer from Queens NY who has been working in the design industry for 15 years. His varied client list includes Comedy Central, BBC America, Nickelodeon, the School of Visual Arts, the Sikh Coalition and most importantly a local fish distributor. His professional work reflects his belief that even the most straight forward project can benefit from a spirit of fun and the addition of a personality into a project. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by March 31st by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account and click on ). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY).
