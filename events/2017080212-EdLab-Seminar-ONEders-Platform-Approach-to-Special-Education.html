EdLab Seminar: ONEder’s Platform Approach to Special Education

Aug 2, 2017 12:00 PM - Aug 2, 2017 1:30 PM

ONEder’s teaching platform empowers educators with the ability to support the needs of students with Individualized Education Plans (IEPs) in any classroom setting. This session will start by highlight the challenges districts face in driving results for students with IEPs in special education systems that were built on compliance. We will then explore ONEder’s system-wide solution to address these challenges by supporting all stakeholders in educating students with IEPs.

Topic: ONEder’s Platform Approach to Special Education

Leaders: Jonathan Izak
