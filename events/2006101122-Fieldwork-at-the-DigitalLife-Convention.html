Fieldwork at the DigitalLife Convention

Oct 11, 2006 10:53 PM

Hui Soo, Skye and Dennis will attend the DigitalLife, consumer electronics and entertainment convention at the Javits Convention Center, located on 34th Street and 11th Avenue. We would like to make it a group event, so please let us know if you would like to come along. Tickets are $12.00. For further details on the event, check out Dennis' blog entry. At the event, we will be conducting video interviews with assorted conference attendees to capture the vibe of the event with an emphasis upon the products or services that have educational value.
