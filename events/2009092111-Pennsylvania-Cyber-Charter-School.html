Pennsylvania Cyber Charter School

Sep 21, 2009 11:58 AM

PRESENTER: Andrew Oberg Director Pennsylvania Cyber Charter School PA Cyber is a public K-12 charter school providing tuition-free online education to the children of Pennsylvania residents. http://www.pacyber.org Mr. Andrew Oberg, the director of the largest public charter completely virtual 'cyber school" in the state of Pennsylvania, will share insights on what it is like to educate over 9000 students at PA Cyber. The school was founded in 1997 and has grown from 500 students with a budget of $4.4M to over 9000 students with a current budget of $90M. In a recent newspaper article Mr. Oberg shared, "four out of nine students that come to us are performing below grade level. After two years with PACyber, 40% of students who tested at "basic" or "below basic" improved to "proficient" or "advanced" levels". In state PSSA math and reading tests administered in March 2009, PACyber students met or surpassed every performance target, hitting 29 of 29 of their AYP goals.
