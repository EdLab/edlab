EdLab Seminar: Sudden Industries - Early 21th Century Learning Digital Ecosystem Prototype

Jun 17, 2011 10:45 PM

Sudden Industries Bob Holmes and Lan Wong will lead a discussion and demonstration of the educational platform that provides the building blocks of pre-school learning through play and interactivity. The Digital Ecosystem leverages mobile, web and social media platforms to create a learning environment that engages child, parent, family and friends. The product is based around a story-driven cast of characters known as "Ranger Ralph and the Round-Up Ranch". The combination of mobile and web applications provides an engaging experience for kids while allowing parents and family to be involved through social media platforms. The Ecosystem even allows for new digital goods funding and revenue opportunities for the learning environment. Open to all members of the university community.
