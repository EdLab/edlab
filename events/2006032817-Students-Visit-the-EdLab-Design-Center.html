Students Visit the EdLab Design Center

Mar 28, 2006 5:11 PM

The EdLab Design Center hosts a visit by fourth and fifth grade students from The School at Columbia University. EdLab graphic designers will help the students envisage and produce advertisements and storyboards for their assignment on school safety. Jenny Howland, the Technology Integrator at The School, arranged the event with EdLab. She oversees technology projects that integrate different aspects of the curriculum, and hopes the visit educates the students about the work of professional designers.
