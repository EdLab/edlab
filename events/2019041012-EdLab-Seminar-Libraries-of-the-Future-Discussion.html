EdLab Seminar - Libraries of the Future Discussion

Apr 10, 2019 12:00 PM - Apr 10, 2019 1:30 PM

Libraries of the Future Discussion ***AT THE NORMAL TIME OF 12PM

We will conduct two fishbowl discussions, each one on a different set of videos.


If your first name begins with a letter A - J, please watch this group of videos on libraries as hubs of innovation (you can use the text power of Vialogues to breeze through the hour-long seminar video if you like!).


If your first name begins with a letter K - Z, please watch this group of videos on libraries as sources of instructional program support.


Please come prepared to discuss the set of videos you are assigned.


You may watch both sets if you like, and are encouraged to do so to get the most out of the seminar activity!
