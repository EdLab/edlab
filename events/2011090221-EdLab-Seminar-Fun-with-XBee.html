EdLab Seminar: Fun with XBee

Sep 2, 2011 9:01 PM

Rob Faludi, Professor at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts (ITP program) and Jordan Husney, Solutions Architect at Digi International, will present on their shared work on wireless mesh networks, and in particular, the XBee technology. They have also recently collaborated on the book, Building Wireless Sensor Networks. They will share examples of XBee in use in educational settings, and invite us to think about future applications and technical possibilities. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by November 7th by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account and click on the Contact button to send a message). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY). For detailed directions within the College visit TC Route Finder: http://maps.tc-library.org
