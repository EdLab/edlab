EdLab Seminar: Teachers Network

Oct 30, 2009 3:29 PM

At this seminar representatives from Teachers Network will join us to discuss their experience offering materials and resources to support new and veteran teachers in the online environment. Representatives from Teachers Network, a major national and international non-profit organization that has been supporting public school teachers for nearly 30 years (i.e., spanning from new teachers through veteran teachers; K-12), will discuss their approach to online learning for teachers. Specifically, they will be talking about the myriad resources available for teachers on their award-winning website (www.teachersnetwork.org), and in particular, their self-guided and/or facilitated, interactive online courses, including companion multi-media materials (e.g., video, print publications)--all designed "by teachers, for teachers" that are offered nationwide and beyond--primarily sold in bulk to colleges, universities, and school districts (www.teachersnetwork.org/tnoc, www.teachersnetwork.org/videos, etc.). Following this introductory presentation, Teachers Network staff will also available for further discussion and/or to answer any questions attendees might have.
