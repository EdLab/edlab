EdLab Seminar: George Khachatryan - Reasoning Mind: A systemic approach to blended learning in mathematics

Dec 3, 2012 10:13 AM

George Khachatryan, Semior VP at Reasoning Mind, will join us for this EdLab Seminar. Blended learning is usually used as a tool for individualizing instruction. However, the curriculum itself -- in choice of topics, sequencing, and mathematical approach -- is usually similar to curricula traditionally used in US classrooms. Reasoning Mind is a non-profit that has been using blended learning as a vehicle for introducing the Russian mathematics curriculum to US students. In the course of our work, we've found that this requires a systemic approach: designing the curriculum materials, delivery platform, and teacher training (for content & pedagogy knowledge) in context of each other. In this talk, we'll explain what makes the Russian curriculum a natural choice for this, and will then discuss our experiences, lessons learned, and future directions. Open to all members of the university community.
