Seminar: Jess Hammer on Game Design

Feb 25, 2009 2:08 PM

Jessica Hammer will join us to discuss the question "Why games?". What is the essence that makes games unique for education? Specifically she will address 5 themes (Meaning, Identity, Systems, Desire, Culture) that are integral for designers to inform their creations and analyses of games. The interactive entertainment sector is continuously growing and more and more higher education institutions are taking interest.
