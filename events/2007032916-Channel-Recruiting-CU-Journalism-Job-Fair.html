Channel Recruiting - CU Journalism Job Fair

Mar 29, 2007 4:30 PM

Emissaries from the Channel team will attend a job fair accross the street to spread the word about the Channel, and recruit talent.

During the daylong conference in the Journalism School building, employers are matched with students who have skills and the experience you are seeking and interest in the jobs you seek to fill. Interviews are 20 minutes long, and news organizations are welcome to bring more than one interviewer. We will set aside an open "walk-up period" in the afternoon so students may visit with any news organization in which they have an interest. Recruiters may also use this period to schedule additional appointments with any other students.
