EdCast Discussion and Ideation

Nov 8, 2006 2:06 PM

If you could design a new channel for broadcasting your content, what would you do? Would you push your content to the web? TV? Cell phones? An institutional archive? Would you connect it to external resources (e.g., YouTube, iTunes) to take advantage of new technologies? How would you carve out your niche in today's edutainment market? The Media Design Group is currently producing a new multimedia publication. Our design parameters are very flexible, and our overarching goal is simply to cull compelling content from the Teachers College community and its surrounds. We are hosting this event to elicit help from other EdLab members. Come prepared to look at case studies of multimedia content and share your knowledge of educational research on areas that intersect with this project. This event is by invitation only.
