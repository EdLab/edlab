EdLab Seminar: Robots Come to the Learning Theater

May 18, 2016 12:00 PM - May 18, 2016 1:30 PM

What is a robot? What role can they play in teaching and learning? Come explore these questions at this EdLab Seminar

What is a robot? A machine with a face and body and does daily chores or risky tasks for us? A digital assistant with artificial intelligence to help us coordinate our schedules? As technology advances, so are the meanings of robots but certainly they will soon be ubiquitous in our world. We have voice assistants (e.g. Siri, Alexa) as well as household robots (e.g. Roomba), and more affordable family robots are on their way into our daily life. What are robots' role in education and learning?

In this EdLab seminar, Dr. Ching-Fu Lan will lead a mini design thinking session to redefine the meaning of robots and explore what robots can and should do at the Learning Theater. Collaboratively, we will draft a Robot Wanted Ad for the Learning Theater and interview a humanoid robot candidate on site.
