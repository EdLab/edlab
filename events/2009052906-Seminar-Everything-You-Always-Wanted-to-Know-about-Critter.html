Seminar: Everything You Always Wanted to Know about Critter

May 29, 2009 6:13 AM

Critter is a tool that supports interactive discussions around video or audio among social groups. To achieve this goal, Critter users can create their own social groups and then hold discussions on a video with individual users or everyone in a social group. The natural sciences are concerned with how things are. Design, on the other hand, is concerned with how things ought to be.â€œ (Simon 1969). In this seminar, a designerly way of thinking is used. Every design project has different phases of iteration to not only improve the prototype but also to align the prototype with possible user needs. Participants will be guided by the DesignEd framework to support the Critter team. They will collaboratively achieve the goals of producing ideas for possible fields of application, design instructional scenarios, test user scenarios and provide insights for the next round of iteration.
