A Conversation on Learning and Learning Institutions

Nov 1, 2006 8:37 AM

Joel Weiss, Senior Fellow at the Knowledge Media Design Institute at the University of Toronto, will consider the implications of recent developments–such as the view of learning as more than a school-based phenomenon, the growing influence of virtual worlds, and concerns with changes to our planet–for our educational institutions. The seminar will focus on exploratory thoughts about future directions–changes to existing, and possible creation of new, institutions. Dr. Weiss will draw on his experiences in several recent projects–to define learning as trans-disciplinary, to understand virtual learning environments, and the UN initiative on The Decade of Education for Sustainable Development .
