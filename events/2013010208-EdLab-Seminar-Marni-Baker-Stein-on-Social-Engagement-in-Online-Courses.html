EdLab Seminar: Marni Baker Stein on Social Engagement in Online Courses

Jan 2, 2013 8:12 AM

Marni Baker Stein will share recent findings from ongoing studies of student social engagement in online courses. This work examined courses offered in the School of Continuing Education at Columbia. Marni Baker Stein is the Senior Associate Dean for Curriculum and Instruction and Lecturer in Continuing Education at Columbia University. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by January 14th by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY).
