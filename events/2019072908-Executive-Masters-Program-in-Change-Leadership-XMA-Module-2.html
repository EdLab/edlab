Executive Masters Program in Change Leadership (XMA) Module 2

Jul 29, 2019 8:30 AM - Aug 6, 2019 8:30 PM

Module 2 of the Executive Masters in Change Leadership focuses on how individual behavior in organizations is influenced by covert processes that occur while working in groups and teams.
July 29th - August 6th

Module 2 focuses on how individual behavior in organizations is influenced by covert processes that occur while working in groups and teams, thereby deepening students’ understanding of the change process. Grounded in systems theory and psychodynamic approaches to understanding group life, Module 2 includes core content areas related to group relations, diversity, intercultural competence, and intergroup dynamics. Students participate in a three-day experiential conference as a hands-on opportunity to learn about group dynamics. Module 2 also provides the cohort with opportunities to apply group dynamics to their own learning community. Students leave the second Module with an integrated framework for understanding how the rational change processes and models explored in Module 1 are further illuminated or undermined by anxiety, psychodynamic forces, and intercultural challenges.
