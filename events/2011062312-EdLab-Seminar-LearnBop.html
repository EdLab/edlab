EdLab Seminar: LearnBop

Jun 23, 2011 12:01 PM

LearnBop LearnBop is an online platform for instructors to create interactive math lessons that simulate a one-on-one learning experience. LearnBop was co-founded by two Carnegie Mellon Alum who were inspired by the renowned Open Learning Initiative. What is different about LearnBop is that anyone can now create lessons that scaffold math-based problems, step-by-step with hint messages without any programming experience. Using LearnBop,top-tiered college students have created interactive math lessons for the AP calculus curriculum in less than 8 weeks.Although these lessons have not been evaluated by an expert yet, the vision that anyone can rapidly create engaging math lessons to benefit the learning community has been realized. In addition, these lessons are open source so that any instructor around the world can make them their own and modify them for local use. LearnBop seeks to support the open education resource community by offering a platform that enables users to make static math content, interactive and customized to individual student needs. Open to all members of the university community.
