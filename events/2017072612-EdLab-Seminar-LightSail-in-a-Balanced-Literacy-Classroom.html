EdLab Seminar: LightSail in a Balanced Literacy Classroom

Jul 26, 2017 12:00 PM - Jul 26, 2017 1:30 PM

Learn about how LightSail’s adaptive reading platform accelerates learning in balanced literacy classrooms. After a brief review of balanced literacy, this session explores how the features of LightSail support the instructional strategies typically used in a balanced literacy environment. This session is designed to help educators determine how LightSail fits into their current reading programs.

Topic: LightSail in a Balanced Literacy Classroom

Leaders: Gideon Stein, Lauren Bochicchio Beaver
