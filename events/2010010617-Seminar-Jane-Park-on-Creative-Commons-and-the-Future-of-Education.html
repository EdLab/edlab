Seminar: Jane Park on Creative Commons and the Future of Education

Jan 6, 2010 5:05 PM

What is Creative Commons? How did it originate? And how did it become part and parcel of worldwide movements towards open education, open scientific practices, and open culture? Jane Park, the Communications Coordinator for CC Learn (the education program at Creative Commons), will discuss these basic questions, and how Creative Commons is integral to the future of educational practices that capitalize on transformative technologies in innovative and completely legal ways.
