Seminar: Pete Elam on Building Training and Tutorial Applications

Jan 6, 2010 2:38 PM

Pete will present on two projects: The first project is a training system for students that need training in certain multimedia software and concepts. The system is designed to replace or supplement face to face workshops, but it was also designed to teach this subject matter online in a more effective manner. To that end, the assessment methods built into the system are designed to be as authentic as possible. The second project is a prototype for an online tutorial system that is adaptive and flexible to the needs of the student. The student is able to access different levels of assistance on demand, and the difficulty of the material varies depending upon the success of the student. Pete is a developer, instructor, and multimedia specialist at The Division of Instructional Innovation and Assessment at the University of Texas at Austin.
