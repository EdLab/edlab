Ethics Symposium for Educators

May 22, 2007 6:54 PM

The Mountain Lakes Board of Education in New Jersey is organizing an Ethics Symposium for Educators. General session speakers include Dr. Bruce Weinstein, Mr. Dith Pran, Dr. Willa Spicer, and Dr. David Hansen. Breakout session leaders include Dr. Margaret Crocco, Professor of Social Studies and Education at Teachers College Columbia University, and Dr. Courtney B. Smith, Associate Dean of Academic Affairs at Seton Hall University. Members of the EdLab who were involved in supporting the conference will also attend as participants. If you are interested in attending the conference, send an email to the Collaborative Solutions Group at CSG@tc.columbia.edu.
