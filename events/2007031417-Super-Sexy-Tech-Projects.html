Super Sexy Tech Projects

Mar 14, 2007 5:31 PM

Want to learn about the sexy tech projects currently underway at the EdLab's Technology Solutions and Innovations Group? The March 20-21st EdLab seminars is the place to learn all about them, provide feedback, and get involved in their development. The presentations at this Tuesday's seminar include: Anthony Cocciolo - Meety, making F2F collaborations more substantial Digital technologies have changed the ways we collaborate on written texts, as evidenced by the popularity of blogs, wikis, and revision control in word processing programs. But what can digital technologies do for face-to-face collaborations? This session will discuss Meety, a tool for delivering on-demand research to face-to-face contexts using speech-to-text translation, semantic analysis, and Super Search!. Related Document: Initial Meety Design Document Jamison Judd - Managing Support with the new Ticket System and Knowledge Database Related Documents: Support Management & Eval System Design Document, Implementation Plan The presentations will be held on the fifth floor of the Gottesman Libraries.
