EdLab Seminar: Amphetamobile

May 22, 2012 1:12 PM

Amphetamobile, LLC is a Philadelphia area mobile development company that specializes in the delivery of mobile information and education. We have expertise in strategic development, deep understanding of scholars and their unique needs and workflows, and the resources and technology to ensure successful implementation. Amphetamobile, or AMMO for short, provides web, mobile web, and native (iOS and Android) mobile application development, design, and consulting. AMMO has developed mobile publishing platforms for periodical, textbook, image bank, and Q&A content, which are customized and developed in partnership with leading international publishers. SiNae Pitts, PhD (sinae@amphetamobile.com) is Founder and CEO of Amphetamobile. SiNae will present AMMO's mobile App platforms in four categories of use cases and highlight how we: 1) mobilize content; 2) allow end users to personalize and 3) socialize the content; and 4) get deeper into users' workflows. SiNae is a serial entrepreneur with a passion for creating user-centric, elegant technology that is online, mobile and good for the world. Her first independent web venture, www.sciencecircle.com, a project management tool and online home/blog for collaborations and research projects, has been active since 2004. SiNae earned a PhD in Neuroscience from Columbia University and has published in several peer-reviewed journals. Open to all members of the university community.
