EdLab Seminar: The Internet of Things

Apr 1, 2015 2:54 PM

The Internet of Things (IoT) has been anticipated as "the next big thing" to bring revolutionary changes to the society. Innovation and advancement in embedded and connected sensors, cloud computing, and mobile technologies foster rapid IoT development. Smarter daily life experiences enabled by IoT have started to present in every corner of our world. What is IoT's potential for a more intelligent educational environment? In this seminar, Ching-Fu will lead EdLab members to brainstorm, hack and prototype IoT for learning. The seminar will start with a brief background introduction to IoT. Edlab members will then: (1). learn and brainstorm IoT ideas that are relevant to their daily life; (2) play with IoT prototyping kits and hack IoT demonstration projects (by Ching-Fu and Panisuan) for the Gottesman Libraries social learning environment; and (3) design potential IoT kits for a more intelligent Learning Theater.
