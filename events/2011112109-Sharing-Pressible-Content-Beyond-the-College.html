Sharing Pressible Content Beyond the College

Nov 21, 2011 9:24 AM

At this seminar, library staff will share analytics and insight into the current Pressible Network -- especially content-creation and traffic patterns. Our goal is to discuss specific steps we can take over the next three months to advertise Pressible content to increase the visibility of the Network beyond TC. Ideas that we will discuss include:

  • Advertising opportunities across internal projects
  • Sharing viral marketing experience from recent grant projects
  • External ad placements
  • Knowing if a particular ad worked well -- possible approaches to analytics
Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by November 28th by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account and click on the Contact button to send a message). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY). For detailed directions within the College visit TC Route Finder: http://maps.tc-library.org
