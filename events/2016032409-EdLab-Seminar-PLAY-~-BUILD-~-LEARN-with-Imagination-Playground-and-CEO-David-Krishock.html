EdLab Seminar: PLAY ~ BUILD ~ LEARN with Imagination Playground & CEO David Krishock!!

Mar 24, 2016 9:53 AM

Bring your energy and imagination to this session ~ get ready to play and create. David Krishock is bringing an Imagination Playground 105 piece Big Block Imagination Playground set to his seminar (that’s right – the BIG blocks). Imagination Playground (IP) has been “around” since 2009 ~ but only available to the public since 2012. In four short years, IP has grown to over $10 million (revenue) – and available in over 70 countries world-wide. The education community that supports development is children 2-10 years of age has fallen in love with IP. This product breaks down barriers, boosts the use of imagination and creativity, encourages complex thinking and problem solving, and more. Seeing is believing – and when kids play with IP magic happens. We’ll discuss the importance of play in early childhood education. The benefits of creative tools like Imagination Playground. And IP’s desire in the coming year to find its place in the “modern classroom” – where children learn in a more stimulating environment – child and teacher led curriculum development, theme based learning, demonstration based learning, project based learning, more. David Krishock is the President, Chief Executive Officer of Imagination Playground. Beginning his career in advertising (McDonalds, General Mills, Proctor & Gamble) ~ shifting to corporations – Philip Morris Kraft, RJR Nabisco, Suntory ~ eventually running businesses over $2 billion (revenue). Krishock has worked in child care and development for close to 20 years ~ helping McDonalds pilot the Ronald McDonald House ~ Running Scholastic Book Fairs ~ Leading his own reading initiative and not-for-profit – Bright Day Learning and The Bright Day Foundation. And today, partnering with designer David Rockwell to bring the world Imagination Playground ~ a truly breakthrough play space adopted by schools, family centers, and homes ~ helping children 2-10 learn by playing,
