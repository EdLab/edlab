Seminar: EDAR Project Presentations

Jul 31, 2009 12:47 PM

The EdLab Digital Art Residency (EDAR) hosted three digital artists this summer to create projects that reflect on the impact of digital culture and engage the Teachers College community. Jeff Goldenson worked to devise an online interface for browsing the contents of Gottesman Libraries, inspired by Google Street View. Dan Paluska investigated possibilities for freeing access to information in the Gottesman Libraries. Dan Torop explored a computer's ability to turn library database searches into spoken verse. These three artists documented their progress on the EdLab blog, and on August 12th will present their final projects, their process, and their experiences as artists-in-residence at EdLab. We request that visitors to EdLab RSVP for this event by emailing the EdLab Media Design team by Monday, August 10th. Event location: EdLab at Gottesman Libraries 5th floor, Russell Hall Teachers College, Columbia University
