EdLab Seminar: Torsh

Mar 28, 2012 5:16 PM

Torsh Inc. is a New York based education technology startup that aims to improve and accelerate student achievement by providing teachers, administrators and parents with the 21st century tools and services needed to support rich and engaging learning environments through the use of highly personalized instructional strategies and practices. Our first product is TALENT, an online instructional support community where teachers can upload user generated classroom instructional videos and share those videos with their peers and professional coaches. We firmly believe that video is a powerful and effective tool for self reflection as well as a scalable means of disseminating highly effective instructional strategies to large groups of teachers. TALENT aims to solve many of the paint points associated with the capture, upload, storage and management of long form video content.
