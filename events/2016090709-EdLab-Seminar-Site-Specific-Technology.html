EdLab Seminar: Site-Specific Technology

Sep 7, 2016 9:31 AM

In today's world, technology has infiltrated every aspect of our society. Not only at the smaller, more portable world of cell phones and laptops, but also at a larger scale, as technological elements find their way into architecture, spaces, and facades. In this presentation, technologist Patrick Proctor will discuss the technology choices available today that make large scale spaces more beautiful, productive, and engaging. He will use both examples from his own work, as well as a more general discussion of a wide range of technologies, including LEDs, sound, sensors, data, projection, and more. Patrick Proctor is a Senior Technologist at the Rockwell Group, as well as an independent creator. He is a 2011 graduate of NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program, as well as a veteran of Dolby Laboratories. He has created technology projects for a wide diversity of spaces including public plazas, live rock shows, corporate sales centers, night clubs, and art installations.
