Building Engaging and Visually Immersive Learning Experiences for K-12 Students with Buncee

Feb 28, 2018 12:00 PM - Feb 28, 2018 1:30 PM

Buncee is an award-winning, powerful and easy-to-use creation tool for learners of all ages and abilities. We will highlight a wide variety of use cases for Buncee and demonstrate the dynamic ways classrooms around the globe are utilizing Buncee to engage students in deeper learning and help them build a voice and choice in their learning.
