EdLab Seminar: Power Up: How Media Literacy and Student Voice Can Awaken the Hero in Every Classroom

Sep 12, 2018 12:00 PM - Sep 12, 2018 1:30 PM

In an age where students are surrounded by more media than ever, educators of all levels are struggling to determine the best way to prepare their students for the digital age. However, with media literacy, educators can empower students to embrace their agency like never before.

Topic: Media Literacy


Leaders: Tony Weaver Jr., Founder of Weird Enough Productions 


Description: In this seminar, we discuss how media literacy can empower students.

Pre-Read/Media: Why This 22-Year-Old Founder Created A Company To Combat Media Misrepresentation (Forbes, January 2017)


Light Lunch provided


Producers: George Nantwi, Cinthia Fabian
