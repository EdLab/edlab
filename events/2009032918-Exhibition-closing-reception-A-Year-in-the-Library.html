Exhibition closing reception: A Year in the Library

Mar 29, 2009 6:54 PM

A Year in the Library, now on display at the Gottesman Libraries visualizes traffic in the Library and on its website collected through the 2008 calendar year, including population density over time and common search terms. This visualization of data serves the dual purposes of providing quantitative information that may improve Library use, and models design practices that the Teachers College community may employ in various areas of research. When: 4/7 - 6/26 Where: Collaboration Space, 2nd Floor Closing reception: Thursday, July 2, 4pm
