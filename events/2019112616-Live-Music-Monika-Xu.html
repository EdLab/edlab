Live Music: Monika Xu

Nov 26, 2019 4:00 PM - Nov 26, 2019 5:00 PM

Featuring a combination of familiar/unfamiliar music by a range of artists

Monika Xu is a current first year Arts Administration student at TC. She was born and raised in Shanghai. Monika earned her BA degree in Music Production from Shanghai Theater Academy and her MBA degree from Brandeis University. She used to work for Jazz at Lincoln Center Shanghai to enlarge jazz community and develop education outreach program in Shanghai. The Everett Cafe Music Program sponsors performances by TC student and affiliated musicians. Come enjoy a variety of genres and styles!
