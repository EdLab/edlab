EdLab Seminar: Internet of Elephants

Jul 19, 2018 12:00 PM - Jul 19, 2018 1:30 PM

Internet of Elephants aims to create games that make conservation fun and educational for anyone. In the past 40 years, the world has lost 50% of its wildlife, and it is a struggle to keep the public involved, despite a common love for animals. To keep the public informed and interested in conservation efforts, Internet of Elephants is in the process of developing their first app, called Safari Central. The app preview is available for download now, and IoE is currently testing out the beta version. They hope to release the finished product by the end of 2018. This seminar will involve learning about Internet of Elephants and testing out the new features of Safari Central.

Topic: Educating people about endangered species and conservation efforts

Leaders: Jeff Bates

Description: An augmented reality app aimed at educating people about endangered species and conservation effort
