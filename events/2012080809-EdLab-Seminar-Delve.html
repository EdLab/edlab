EdLab Seminar: Delve

Aug 8, 2012 9:10 AM

Sandeep Ayyappan, co-founder & CEO of Delve, and Tom Weingarten, co-founder and CTO, will talk about how they're using news stories to create better collaboration in teams and organizations. Finding and sharing relevant content is something all of us do, but as the number of relevant news items, blogs, perspectives, and research pieces expands,it's tougher and tougher to keep up with it on our own. They'll discuss the evolution of the education news landscape over the past few years, how it may look in the years to come, and how a good story can become the basis for better problem-solving inside a team. What is Delve? Delve is a team news reader that helps teams and organizations find and share relevant stories. It starts with a personalized newsfeed tailored to the organization's interests from a variety of major news organizations, industry publications, newsletters and blogs, and combines it with a social layer that facilitates a conversation around a relevant story. Using thoughtful editorial curation as well as integration with Twitter and email, Delve makes staying informed a team sport. Open to all members of the university community.
