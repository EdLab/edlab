EdLab Seminar: Elemental Path and Cognitoys

Sep 20, 2016 5:20 PM

Elemental Path creates "internet-connected smart toys that learn and grow with kids." Elemental Path's Friendgine is a hardware/software platform that enables objects for internet connectivity and equips them with speech and a personality. The Wi-Fi based technology delivers personalized, engaging experiences that can adapt to the brand or product line for which it's been configured. Elemental Path's flagship product, the Cognitoys Dino, implements this technology to provide a personalized, speech-based learning experience. It blends educational and emotional intelligence to create a true learning companion. Donald Coolidge is the CEO and Co-founder of Elemental Path. He is a former athlete, tech entrepreneur, and Marine Corps Vet. He is a huge pet lover and is active in a number of Veteran non-profits. His expertise and passion lies in the way we use technology and he is optimistic that technology can change the world, in a good way.
