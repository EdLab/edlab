EdLab Seminar: OfficeHours with Jeff Levy

Jun 20, 2014 3:31 PM

At this seminar we will be joined by Jeff Levy, co-founder of Office Hours. Co-founded by former H-P CTO Rich DeMillo and serial entrepreneur Jeff Levy, OfficeHours has developed the world's first online collaboration platform purpose-built for higher-ed learners. The average college student today spends 9.5 hours a day online. None of that activity is currently visible to universities or professors. The OfficeHours platform, OCP 1.0, brings new visibility to this sort of online activity. OCP 1.0 enables students to collaborate in one-on-one or group settings, without having to arrange meeting times through services such as Skype or Google Hangouts. The platform is easily accessible to students via a downloadable browser extension, so students can get the support they need to stay engaged and complete their coursework. OCP 1.0 is equally well suited to fully online or on-campus courses. The OfficeHours platform has been successfully utilized in multiple courses at Georgia Tech and Kennesaw State, and the company is in advanced discussions to expand to other universities. OCP 1.0 is available through a SaaS enterprise license to educational institutions, or as an add-on module to leading LMS platforms, including Blackboard, Canvas, Desire2Learn and Moodle. Open to all members of the university community.
