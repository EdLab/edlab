EdLab Seminar: Project Empower with Zach Graumann

Mar 7, 2012 8:39 AM

Zach Graumann will join us to discuss recent developments with Project Empower. Project Empower is an innovative educational movement that facilitates life and career readiness for students through interactive educational programming. Currently designed for middle school students, the programs include business competitions, non-profit competitions, an interactive career networking forum, and web-based career simulation video games. Most importantly, all of the programs are offered to schools free of charge. Project Empower aims to prepare students not just for school, but also for life, promoting passion and awareness of career and life opportunities to students at a young age. Programs currently involve more than 50 middle schools in Manhattan, Brooklyn and Queens, with plans to scale nationally in 2013. For more information please visit www.proempower.org.
