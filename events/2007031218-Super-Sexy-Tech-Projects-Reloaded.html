Super Sexy Tech Projects, Reloaded

Mar 12, 2007 6:23 PM

Want to learn about the sexy tech projects currently underway at the EdLab's Technology Solutions and Innovations Group? The March 20-21st EdLab seminars is the place to learn all about them, provide feedback, and get involved in their development. The presentations at this Wednesday's seminar include: Dan Mallinger - Doc.Life Doc.Life, a tool for doctoral students, aims to a) engage the user at a personal level, b) provide a means of passing down tacit and non-procedural knowledge, and c) ease feelings of frustration with honest stories and dialogs of problems one's mentors encountered Related Document: Doc.Life Proposal Related Document: Initial Doc.Life Design Document Nabeel Ahmad - A Smarter Survey Tool The survey tool aims to replace the current TC Survey Wizard and add value to the TC community through three phases: Information, Tool and Analysis Christine Tsai - Making Websites more accessible Related Document: Web Accessibility at the Gottesman Libraries The presentations will be held on the fifth floor of the Gottesman Libraries.
