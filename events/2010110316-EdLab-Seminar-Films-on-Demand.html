EdLab Seminar: Films on Demand

Nov 3, 2010 4:03 PM

DESCRIPTION FROM FILMS MEDIA GROUP: : "Films Media Group is pleased to announce Films On Demand, our streaming video service on an annual subscription basis. Subscribers are given unrestricted, asynchronous, 24/7 access to all video content by authorized faculty, students, and staff from any location. Our web-based service was built with tools to help educators harness the power of digital video to enhance learning in today's anytime, anywhere environment. - Search or browse for whole videos or segments - Create playlists with segments from multiple video titles - Organize videos and playlists in your Favorites folders - Share playlists or whole videos for off-hours viewing & distance learning - Integration with course management systems - View content windowed or full screen with Flash or Windows Media, with closed captioning on available titles - Administrative tools include access control and usage report features, plus help, support, and promotional materials to spread awareness and encourage usage - MARC records (coming soon) Films Media Group is the recognized leader in video for the higher education market and offers licensed programming from more than 700 producers worldwide, plus original productions from Films for the Humanities & Sciences, Cambridge, Meridian, and Shopware.
