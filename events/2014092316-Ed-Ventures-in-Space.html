Ed Ventures in Space

Sep 23, 2014 4:56 PM

TC's New Learning Times presents: Ed Ventures in Space (as in: learning space!) Join us for a night of Seen in New York videos and discussion about creating powerful and effective educational spaces with: IndyKids Fat Cat Fab Lab Rocking the Boat The Laundromat Project We will screen their Seen in NY episodes and hear from them about how and why they got started with their organization. The event will begin at 6:30 with the presentations starting at 7:00. A Q&A session and networking will take us to the end of the event at 9:00!
