After Ed TV Karaoke Party

Sep 4, 2008 4:32 PM

Sing Your Heart Out, After Ed TV Style! After Ed TV is hosting their first annual After Ed TV Karaoke Party. You can come relax from the stresses of starting a new semester by meeting new people, eating some delish free food, and singing your heart out, karaoke style! (Singing is optional.) Where: Second floor of the Gottesman Libraries Free appetizers and drinks! The best part is that you will get to meet producers and other great staff members from After Ed TV and learn about new upcoming shows, as well as the current productions that are up for your viewing pleasure on our website. Find out how you can get involved with After Ed! Whether you have a knack for video projects, or just want to get involved in something exciting and different, After Ed TV is the place to be! You might be asking yourself, "Wait, just what the heck is After Ed TV?" After Ed TV is a web video channel that features news, special reports on education, as well as fun and interesting insights on the future of education. Our mission is to explore this future through all genres of video media. Check us out at: http://aftered.tv
