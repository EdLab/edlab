The Metamorphosis: A Night in Celebration of Literature & Art

Jun 5, 2018 5:00 PM - Jun 5, 2018 7:00 PM

The art exhibit is based on a research project that asks professional artists and high school students to read Kafka's The Metamorphosis and create art that is based on or inspired by the text. The night will celebrate the novella, literature, and art.

Whoever has read Franz Kafka's 'The Metamorphosis' can attest to the power behind its words. But what happens when those words and ideas get transferred through artistic materials? The exhibit will showcase 10 artists with a diverse set of artistic mediums (film, performance art, paintings, video game coding, fashion, writing, photography, etc) all in the hopes of understanding the novella on a deeper level. People who will attend the show will walk away with a deepened appreciation of the text on many different levels and those who haven't picked it up yet will certainly want to. Aside from the novella itself, to witness art and literature meet allows educators, researchers, and students to blur the lines between forms and to see the beautiful products that transpire when given the opportunity to think and create in different ways.
