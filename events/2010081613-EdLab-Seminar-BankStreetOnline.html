EdLab Seminar: BankStreetOnline

Aug 16, 2010 1:15 PM

Dr. Steven Goss, the Director of Online Education at Bank Street College, will talk about the unique challenges Bank Street faces in the development programs online and the strategies being used to move forward. The discussion will focus on the key issues for year one: faculty and technology support. BIO: Dr. Steven Goss is the Director of Online Education for Bank Street College. Dr. Goss joined Bank Street after working at New York University for ten years as a faculty member and director of several teaching and learning initiatives, including SCPS Distance Learning and The Center for Faculty Innovations in Teaching and Learning at NYU-Poly. He has held a long dedication to education, receiving his Ed. D. in Instructional Technology and Media from Teachers College Columbia University and an M.A. from New York University and a B.S. from Pennsylvania State University in Art Education. Prior to starting his career at NYU, he worked in the New York City public high school system as an arts educator; teaching arts-based technology courses. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the EdLab, please RSVP by November 1st to edlab@tc.edu so we order enough lunch!
