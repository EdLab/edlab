Learning Analytics Fall 2016 Gathering

Sep 29, 2016 5:00 PM - Sep 29, 2016 7:00 PM

Students and faculty in the Learning Analytics Program will hold their fall gathering in the Smith Learning Theater. A tour of the space will focus on the collection of data for research purposes in instrumented space. The focus will be on ways that learning analytics be used for research on events in the Smith Learning Theater.

Students in the Teachers College Learning Analytics Program will tour the Smith Learning Theater and discuss ideas on how to use the space to advance research.
