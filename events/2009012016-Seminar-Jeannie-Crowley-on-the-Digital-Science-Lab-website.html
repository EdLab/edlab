Seminar: Jeannie Crowley on the Digital Science Lab website

Jan 20, 2009 4:36 PM

Jeannie Crowley is the technology coordinator for a K-8 special education school in the Bronx. Her primary role is to design and implement new technology initiatives to enhance traditional subject areas. Her most recent project, the Digital Science Lab, is a science website designed and maintained by students. The goal of the project is to leverage students' enjoyment of technology to generate more positive science attitudes. The students develop their own questions for investigation, design experiments, and publish the results online. Through this process students are given the opportunity to engage in meaningful dialogue about science and develop a deeper understanding of the experimental process.
