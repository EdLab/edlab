Podfest

Dec 15, 2017 5:00 PM - Dec 15, 2017 7:00 PM

You're invited to Podfest, an evening of interactive storytelling. Join the Media and Social Change lab in launching their podcast series. This celebration takes place in the Smith Learning Theater. Join us in this amazing space where you will have the opportunity to engage, share, and participate in the storytelling and media making process.

This event is potluck-style with MASCLAB members bringing delicious homemade dishes to share. If you would like to contribute a dish, please bring it with you to the event along with a list of ingredients for those with allergies. If you are unable to bring a dish to share, please consider bringing and donating canned goods to the event as part of our canned food drive. All food donations will go to to a local food bank.
