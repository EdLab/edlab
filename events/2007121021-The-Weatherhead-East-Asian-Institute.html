The Weatherhead East Asian Institute

Dec 10, 2007 9:40 PM

The EdLab will host a meeting with Dr. Karen Kane and Dr. Robin Martin of the Weatherhead East Asian Institute to discuss the delivery of on-line educational opportunities on East Asia to pre-service and in-service teachers around the country. Funded by the Freeman Foundation the group has a mandate to experiment with a variety of on-line, “modular” courses. This project is part of the Asia for Educators (AFE) program based at the Weatherhead East Asian Institute in the Columbia School of International and Public Affairs. The program, began in 1977 as part of Columbia's DOE grant for a “National Resource Center” (NRC) in East Asian Studies. Their charge is to draw upon Columbia's outstanding resources in East Asian studies to provide materials and enrichment courses for other educators who will be teaching about East Asia.
