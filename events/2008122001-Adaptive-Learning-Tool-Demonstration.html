Adaptive Learning Tool Demonstration

Dec 20, 2008 1:08 AM

Anne Kel-Artinian, Director of New Product Design for the Achieve3000 team will be leading an orientation session on their web based individual instruction products. Specifically KidBiz3000 and TeenBiz3000 are Achieve3000's literacy products for K-12 grade levels that have been proven to accelerate students' academic reading and writing skills as well as boost standardized test scores. This is accomplished through one-on-one instruction provided at each students' own level. Achieve3000's technology includes a software engine LevelSet, which is and online summative assessment tool that differentiates instruction based on each student's Lexile level. The orientation session is the first step in allowing the Development and Research team limited time access to the web based system and will be apart of ongoing research exploring effective adaptive learning technologies. * If you are able to attend please bring your laptop.
