Engaging Educators with Digital Collections

Jul 5, 2018 12:00 PM - Jul 5, 2018 1:30 PM

Learn how librarians address the challenges K-12 teachers face when approaching digital collections for classroom use. Projects like Brooklyn Public Library's Teacher Lab and its Digital Public Library of America (DPLA) educator workshop serve as case studies for increasing educator success when upgrading curriculum to include digital collections and resources.
