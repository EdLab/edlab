EdLab Seminar: Dalia Majumdar on Creature 101 and Middle School Nutrition

Dec 21, 2012 9:48 AM

Creature-101 is an interactive virtual world game that uses “creature care” as the game motif and draws its educational content from a middle school nutrition science curriculum. The game is a collaborative effort between the Program in Nutrition at Teachers College and Stottler Henke Inc., CA through a NIH-SBIR grant. It targets middle school children and adolescents with an intention to promote healthy eating and physical activity behaviors through science inquiry lessons presented as mini-games, slideshows, interactive dialogues, creature care etc. This presentation will focus on the results of the game evaluation in New-York city public middle schools, its feasibility in the classrooms, and future directions of research. Open to all members of the university community.
