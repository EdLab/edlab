EdLab Demo Night Spring 2014

Feb 26, 2014 8:30 PM

The EdLab at Teachers College Columbia University will host a Demo Night on Tuesday April 1st, 2014 from 7-9pm featuring: Writer Key Watch Writer Key founder Doug Silver's GroundBREAKERS interview and EdLab seminar. Open Assembly (@OpenAssemblyEdu) Watch Open Assembly founder, Domi Ender's GroundBREAKERS interview and EdLab Seminar. Ponder (@PonderLabsInc) Watch Ponder founder, Alex Selkirk's GroundBREAKERS interview and EdLab Seminar. Where: Fifth Floor of the Gottesman Libraries (networking and light refreshments to follow) When: 7PM Tuesday, April 1st, 2014 To RSVP to this event, send an email to bms2175@tc.columbia.edu. Space is limited. The EdLab Demo Night will take place in the EdLab space at the Gottesman Libraries at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, 5th Fl., NY, NY). Please create an EdLab Account prior to the event.
