EdLab Seminar: Making Learning Visible with Kaymbu

Oct 15, 2014 3:56 PM

This seminar will feature interactive discussion around moving beyond rigid frameworks, quantitative assessment and traditional grading mechanisms to capture the essence of student development to strengthen relationships between home and school. Kin Lo is the Chief Executive Officer of Kaymbu and manages high-level product direction, business development and strategy for the company. Kin's has held senior roles in operations and strategic planning in the digital media industry over the last 15 years. He completed his undergraduate studies at Princeton University with a degree in Political Economy, completed his MBA at the Sloan School of Management at MIT, and served as a Captain in the United States Marine Corps. Kin's daughter turns four this year and was the inspiration for harnessing technology to keep parents and teachers better connected with their young children during these precious years of early development.
