EdLab Seminar: NYPL Labs

Jun 21, 2012 4:12 PM

David Riordan is the Product Manager for NYPL Labs, the experimental technology unit at The New York Public Library. David has been involved in product development of the inaugural issue of Biblion, NYPL's iPad publication, NYPL's online advocacy platform, and the recently launched Stereogranimator, DirectMe NYC, and What's On The Menu. David currently oversees the development efforts around NYPL's online access to archival materials. When not finding new ways to more deeply integrate technology with NYPL, David skis, wakeboards, runs, bikes, and enjoys cooking using lukewarm water and plastic bags (sous vide). NYPL Labs is an experimental technology unit within The New York Public Library that works closely with curators to create tools that expand the range of interaction, interpretation and reuse of research library collections and data. Open to all members of the university community. If you are coming from outside the Edlab, please RSVP by October 1st by sending a message to Edlab at http://ednode.com/profiles/edlabtc/about (You must have/create an Ednode account and click on ). EdLab Seminars take place on the 5th floor of Russell Hall at Teachers College Columbia University (525 W. 120th Street, NY, NY).
