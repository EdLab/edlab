Conditions for Innovation

Jun 21, 2007 6:50 AM

This seminar session will initiate a discussion of the conditions under which we can become more creative and innovative in our work, both in the EdLab and elsewhere. To structure our discussion a bit we will read one article, How to Kill Creativity, by Teresa Amabile. The article identifies six elements that can affect creativity, and we have six volunteers who will kick-off the discussion: 1) challenge - Steven 2) freedom - Evan 3) resources - Eric 4) work-group features - Laura 5) supervisory encouragement - Gonzalo 6) organizational support - Ian For each element we will consider how our own personal creativity seems to be affected by the element and whether we are using the element to foster innovation in the EdLab. The volunteers will start the discussion of each element with a few minutes of comments.
