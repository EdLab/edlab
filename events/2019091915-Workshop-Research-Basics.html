Workshop: Research Basics

Sep 19, 2019 3:00 PM - Sep 19, 2019 4:00 PM

The A-Z of Library Research

Topic: An Overview of Library Resources


Overwhelmed by the maze of information? Can't get hold of a certain journal article? Where do you turn for help on your first paper? This session covers the A-Z of research, guiding you through essential library resources, solid construction of searches, useful Google tips, the road to full text, and much more.


In preparation, be sure to check out our collection of research guides, descriptive bibliographies organized at course, program, and department levels in support of Teachers College offerings and with links to a full range of subscribed e-resources.


Please rsvp with your interest and details by Wednesday, September 18th.


Where: 305 Russell
