Seminar: Project work on the "New Day Grant Proposal"

Feb 13, 2009 3:32 PM

A Project Team (Jessica Blum, Ching-fu Lan, and Erin Murphy) will introduce the New Day Grant Proposal and ask you to engage the project by working on possible next steps. The proposal is an EdLab project that aims to create a partnership between Teachers College and New Day Films, with the goal of creating new learning opportunities around New Day-affiliated films. The seminar will present the current grant proposal (in draft) and ask seminar participants to brainstorm curriculum ideas related to specific New Day films. Before the seminar, please review the Proposal (which you will need to sign in to PocketKnowledge (PK) to download -- if you haven't used PK yet, please make an account and use your CUNIX to get "University-level" access).
