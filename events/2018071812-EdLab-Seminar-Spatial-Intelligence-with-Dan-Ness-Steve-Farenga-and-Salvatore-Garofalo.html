EdLab Seminar: Spatial Intelligence with Dan Ness, Steve Farenga, and Salvatore Garofalo

Jul 18, 2018 12:00 PM - Jul 18, 2018 1:30 PM

At this seminar we will be joined by Dan Ness, Steve Farenga, and Salvatore Garofalo who will present highlights of their new book on spatial intelligence.

Topic: Spatial Intelligence

Leaders: Dan Ness, Steve Farenga, and Salvatore Garofalo

Description: Issues of spatial cognition and spatial relations in spaces for teaching and learning, as well as in the everyday spontaneous experiences of children, students, and teachers.
