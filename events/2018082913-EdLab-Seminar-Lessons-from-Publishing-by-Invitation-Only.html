EdLab Seminar: Lessons from Publishing (by Invitation Only)

Aug 29, 2018 1:00 PM - Aug 29, 2018 2:00 PM

In this seminar, Rob Crawford, an editor, writer, and publisher, will discuss his experiences and projects, both analog and digital, from working at publishers large and small, to running a magazine and book press, to designing an app, to displaying poems based on the weather. Recently, he completed his MFA writing thesis at Columbia. During the seminar Rob will share some of his work, along with thoughts on the beguiling “technology” of writing, and the connections between rhythm and style, including poetic meter, with considerations on “the never-ending sentence.”

Topic: Publishing
