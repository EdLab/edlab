EdLab Karaoke Party

May 9, 2008 2:51 PM

EdLab? Great. Karaoke? Awesome. EdLab AND karaoke? Unbeatable! If you love to sing and/or listen to others massacre hit songs, then you'll want to be at the EdLab karaoke party, going down at Duet 35 (53 W. 35th St. 2F) on May 24th from 2-8pm. A karaoke marathon! They have thousands of songs in English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Vietnamese and more. Click here for the complete playlist. They also have a huge bar, ensuring all of the liquid courage you could need. Plus, it'll take place in a private room. There's no reason to miss it! If you are interesting in attending please RSVP to Jo Agnitti before May 21.
