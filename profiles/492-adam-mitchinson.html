Adam Mitchinson

EdLab Alumni

Adam is a member of the development and research team at the Edlab. He is working towards his PhD in Social & Organizational Psychology at Teachers College, Columbia University and his research interests range from Conflict Resolution, Leadership, Team- and Group-Dynamics. Originally from Edinburgh, Scotland, Adam attended Hartwick College in Oneonta, New York where he double majored in Psychology and Business Administration and was also captain of their D1 Soccer team. Adam hopes to use his education in a broad number of fields ranging from Organizational Consulting, Conflict Mediation and Sports Psychology.
