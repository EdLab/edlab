Mason Hooten

EdLab Alumni

Might be walking around a lake,

it's not clear

through the surface

or the trees.

You jump in to find

out, then

wonder what you might have learned

if you had just kept walking.

Same lake.


Wherever he is, Mason is there to help. He enjoys writing slow, cycling fast, and occasionally taking the scenic route.
