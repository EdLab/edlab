Ketaki Kulkarni

EdLab Alumni

Ketaki Kulkarni is currently a student at the Computer Science dept in Columbia University. She has done her under-graduation in Computer Engineering from Pune university. After that she worked in Microsoft IDC for two years in the "Visual Studio for Mobile" team as Software Design Engineer in Test(SDET). Her current interests are in Graphics and UI design fields and her concentration track for her Masters is "Graphics and Vision". She is currently working at Edlab as a Software Development associate. She works as a front end developer on the first version of a new web based application being developed at Edlab, which creatively brings the magic of social networking in the education field.
