Toland Lawrence

EdLab Alumni

I've always loved learning. The world is such a complex and mysterious place. It brings me such joy to explore, discover, and learn. It's a process that never be complete. If you choose, every moment can be an opportunity to grow. Technology offers so many opportunities to expand the way we learn from VR that allows us to live in different worlds to platforms that make it easier to access information we need. I'm interested in writing and telling stories about how we can use these technologies to increase human potential.


As a master's student in psychology, I'm particularly interested in the tools and techniques that are supporting the mental well-being of students. In response to the epidemic levels of mental health concerns among young people, educators are trying new techniques to nurture the whole child, from social and emotional learning to mindfulness. As much as technology, such as social media, may play a role in the mental health struggles of young people, many companies are using technology as a tool to provide students with the tools they need to cope with high-pressured lives. As a content creation associate, I write content that explores these issues as well as any intriguing stories at the nexus of education and technology.
