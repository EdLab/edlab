Jessica Pang

EdLab Alumni

Jessica is currently enrolled in the Psychology in Education program under the Clinical Psychology Department at Teachers College. Before coming to New York for her masters degree, Jessica has worked in a lifestyle magazine and a non profit organization in Hong Kong. She completed her undergraduate studies in University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and was in the School of Art and Design for 3 semesters before transferring to the Psychology Department. Her interests include traveling, doing research for traveling, painting, drawing and "creative" cooking.
