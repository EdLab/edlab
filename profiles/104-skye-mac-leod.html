Skye MacLeod

EdLab Alumni

Skye is an animator, puppeteer, professional developer, and documentary filmmaker. He has explored various intriguing stories related to all things educational and some things outlandish. In his spare time he works with teachers and students to integrate technology and digital video into their work and lives, plays jazz & klezmer, and travels the world seeking stories he can share through film.
