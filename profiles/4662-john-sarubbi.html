John Sarubbi

EdLab Alumni

John Sarubbi is a Services Associate at the Gottesman Libraries at Teachers College. He has a Master's in Library Information Science (MLIS) from Pratt Institute, a BA from Marist College and an AAS in Communication Design from Parsons The New School for Design. He has worked at the New York Public Library, The Sophie Gimbel Library at Parsons The New School for Design, the Barnard College Library (BLAIS) and the Research Library at the American Museum of Natural History. In addition, John has previous theoretical knowledge and practical experience in pedagogy. Current work includes the Image Collection database, a project at the Special Collections division of the Research Library at the American Museum of Natural History, where he created and assigned metadata to describe digital reproductions of photo negatives of the Lumholtz Expedition to Mexico Collection and the Jesup North Pacific Collection and the Photographic Collection. John is happy to join the EdLab and Gottesman Libraries services team and is eager to serve the needs of the Teachers College academic community.
