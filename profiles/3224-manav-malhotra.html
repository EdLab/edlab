Manav Malhotra

EdLab Alumni

Manav is a Product Manager on the Development & Research team at the EdLab. Both academically and professionally, his interests lie in applied technology. Manav believes the educational field is ripe for a technological transformation. He sees tremendous opportunity to collect and learn from educational data, informing new products that make education more nimble and accessible while driving down costs.

Manav received his undergraduate degree from Columbia University in Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics. He completed his graduate studies at the California Institute of Technology, where he helped design a photovoltaic device which would enable a renewable fossil fuel cycle. Hailing from the mountains of Pennsylvania, Manav is adept at braving the cold.
