Shane Chin

EdLab Alumni

Shane is a third year Computer Science student in the Artificial Intelligence track of Columbia University. He loves both technology and education, things that make him an ideal candidate for EdLab. He thinks the educational sector is lagging behind in the sphere of education, placing EdLab at the perfect spot to innovate. He is the technical lead of a startup called SwapCollege, which aims to help Columbia University students make academic connections. He has also created a small email cryptography client, as well as worked on Dropbox and Google Doc integration for World Software Corporation. He loves basketball, coding, and romantic comedies.
