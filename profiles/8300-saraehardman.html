Sara Hardman

EdLab Alumni

Sara is passionate about many things in life, three of them being writing, creating, and educating. As a content creator on the publishing team, she is able to do all three. Her main pursuit at EdLab is to write content for New Learning Times with the goal of informing others on new trends in education and technology. Yet she also helps brainstorm, develop, and create learning events for both her colleagues and the TC community at large. Finally, she aims to educate others in the workspace on topics she is excited about and events she has attended that pertain to EdLab’s mission.


When Sara is not at EdLab, she is generally a couple floors below it in the library, studying for her coursework as a PhD student in the Philosophy and Education program at Teachers College.
