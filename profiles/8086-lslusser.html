Lauren Slusser

EdLab Alumni

Lauren is pursuing her MA in Cognitive Studies in Education at Teachers College with a concentration in Children's Media Analysis & Evaluation. Before coming to Columbia, Lauren worked at Nickelodeon Animation Studios in Los Angeles, most recently as Post Production Manager on the preschool series Dora the Explorer. Lauren attended UCLA for undergrad, where she majored in Film, Television, & Digital Media (Directing) and minored in Women's Studies. Lauren enjoys yoga, making comics, bubbles, balloons, camping, playing keyboard, and watching anime. Lauren is trained as a birth and post-partum doula.
