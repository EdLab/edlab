Jennifer Prediger

EdLab Alumni

Jennifer Prediger is a video journalist, writer and fabulist. She has produced videos for Newsweek, Onion News Network, Sudance.org and the Washington Post. Jennifer has reported from locales as far-flung as Burkina Faso, Colombia and Iceland. She was the creator and face of Grist.org's popular video advice series "Ask Umbra." She’s made videos with The Yes Men and interviewed luminaries like Thom Yorke of Radiohead, Tom Friedman, Tom Hanks and Tim DeChristopher. Jennifer’s been a commentator on The Today Show and NPR's All Things Considered. Putting her M.A. in Organizational Psychology to use, she dished out workplace advice on a series known as "Hey Penny" on SlateV. Themes in her work have included sustainability, new activism and how to do unusual things yourself, like making organic Twinkies. Jennifer makes microbudget, independent movies. When not telling multimedia stories, she hangs out with her charming asthmatic cat, Pigeon, who she really ought to film more.
