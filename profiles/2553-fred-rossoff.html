Fred Rossoff

EdLab Alumni

Fred Rossoff has lived in New York City his entire life, except for the four years he spent getting a B.A. in English at Pomona College in California. After writing a thesis on "The Liberal Tradition in American Literature," and graduating in 2009, he went to work for a year at the Queen Literary Agency. In his spare time, he writes science fiction. Fred currently works for the EdLab Development & Research group
