Ashish Mehta

EdLab Alumni

Ashish Mehta began his professional career in India as the marketing lead of a start-up TolMol.com. Having worked with Avake Technology, the parent company of TolMol.com that worked with Google USA, Ashish believes in integrating technology with education to scale the impact of his work. After spending three years in the digital marketing space, Ashish founded the 'Tagore Foundation' in 2009 to empower the youth through holistic education. He continued to work with the Foundation until he joined the masters program at Columbia University in August 2012. Besides conducting training and skill development workshops for the youth (16-25yrs), Ashish produced several educational short-films and hosted interactive talk shows on contemporary issues on national television in an attempt to make education accessible, affordable and fun. For past 2 years, Ashish has been working with LIQVID eLearning Services where he is managing a team of edtech professionals that develop gamified mobile first Modules to make learning fun.
