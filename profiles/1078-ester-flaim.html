Ester Flaim

EdLab Alumni

Ester graduated in 2005 from the University of Trieste in Italy with a degree in Intercultural Sciences and Methods and a concentration in Central Europe and the Balkan Region. She is interested in the symbolic power that language has in creating barriers and bridges. She is exploring the potentials of English as lingua franca and if the extent of English usage on the web is contributing in the creation of a shared meaning between different cultures. She is currently pursuing a Masters in Applied Linguistics at Teachers College and working for Teachers College Record as book review coordinator.
