Ellen Qian

EdLab Alumni

Ellen Qian is a graduate student in Computer Science, she is now studying for a MS degree in Computer Science with an emphasis on Database, security and Software systems at Columbia University. Before came here,Ellen got her BE degree in Computer Software at Tsinghua University, China, specializing in the topics of web service.During her undergraduate study, she worked as a Research Assistant in ICSE lab in Tsinghua university from 2007 till 2009. Her research includes web service developing, privacy policy of service and protocols of network.In 2007, she implement a new method to improve web service's performance by bundle N services invoke into 1 package in SOAP protocol.Compared with the old one service in one package,the performance is improved when network is low.This experiment leaded to a paper published on WAIM international conference in Suzhou,2009.Besides this project, she also joined in the intelligence traffic scheduling and travel system projects at Tsinghua University. Now, she is doing projects on social networking platform called Elgg. Some of her recent projects: AED(Advanced_edu_database):advanced_edu database aimed to manage the data of students who are willing to continue their study to graduate school.Standing from application aspect, AED is a specifically designed for a web front-end which helps students applying for graduate schools including the history record of what kind of student got accepted by your dream university, including their undergraduate school,GPA,GRE and vice versa.Furthermore, you can find roommate,housing and flight information about your new school mate.
