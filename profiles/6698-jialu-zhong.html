Jialu Zhong

EdLab Alumni

Jialu is a master candidate in Computer Science at the Columbia University. Her concentration is in Machine Learning. Before came to Columbia, Jialu received her bachelor of science degree from the University of Minnesota in May 2013, with dual majors in Mathematics and Statistics, and a minor in Computer Science. She was also a teaching assistant for the course Discrete Mathematics and the course Applied Fourier Analysis at the University of Minnesota. Jialu also worked full-time as a software developer in the IT industry during the year 2013-2014. She joined the Open Access Technology International Company located in Minneapolis, MN, where she worked with other talented technical people in the Research and Development department. She developed front end user interface of new web applications and also designed database architecture and structure for data management. She worked closely with other team members and efficiently interpreted her coding ideas to managers.
