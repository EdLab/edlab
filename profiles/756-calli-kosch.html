Calli Kosch

EdLab Alumni

Calli is a summer intern who has recently graduated from the University of Michigan with a B.A. in Psychology and a minor in History. From Michigan, this is Calli's first time living in New York but enjoys traveling and getting to know new cities. She studied abroad in Paris, France for about 7 months and took psychology and history courses at the University of Paris VII. She will begin working for City Year Boston this August and is applying to graduate school in the fall. In her spare time, Calli enjoys cooking, reading, and exercising.
