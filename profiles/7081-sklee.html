Seungkyun Lee

EdLab Alumni

Seungkyun (SK) is a user experience + industrial designer on the Design team, who is particularly curious about where the digital and the physical meet. She received her BFA in Industrial Design from Rhode Island School of Design, where she fell in love with making things and studying people. She recently completed her MFA in Design and Technology at Parsons School of Design, and developed a passion for exploring the intersection of people, design and technology. Seungkyun grew up in six different countries including Korea, Japan, the Philippines, the Netherlands, Jamaica, and the United States. She also briefly lived in South Africa prior to coming to New York for grad school. She enjoys learning about different cultures, and that is probably where her passion for studying people comes from.
