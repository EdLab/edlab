Aoi Nakazawa

EdLab Alumni

Aoi is a graduate of Wesleyan University, who is currently pursuing a MA in Communication and Education at Teachers College. She is fascinated by the intersection of media, education, and social justice, and as a Publishing and Content Creation Associate at EdLab, she especially enjoys writing about digital activism and ways that technology is making learning more accessible. Prior to coming to EdLab, Aoi worked in a variety of positions in digital strategy, media, and edtech.
