Pranav Garg

EdLab Alumni

Pranav is a Senior Solutions Architect at the Edlab having focus on use of data to improve educational applications and make learning more personalized. Pranav won the Apple student scholarship's in 2009. Pranav is currently working on developing next generation learning ecosystems such as mSchool. Pranav has done Masters program in Computer Science Department of Columbia University. Prior to coming to New York, Pranav was working as Technical Consultant with Headstrong (Financial Consulting firm).

He has ten years of work experience and hails from India. He completed his Bachelors in Information Technology degree in 2004 from University of Delhi.

His interests include traveling, playing cricket, badminton and chess.
