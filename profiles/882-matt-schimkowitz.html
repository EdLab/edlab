Matt Schimkowitz

EdLab Alumni

After recently receiving a Bachelor's degree in English at Rutgers University, Matt continues his interest in film and video through the production internship at the EdLab. At Rutgers, Matt completed the English Honors program through his independent research of classic Hollywood genre films and the Coen Brothers. Aside of his interest in film, Matt plays the bass guitar in number of bands including Seasick, Kicking Spit, and Casual Fridays.
