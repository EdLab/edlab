Wan Park

EdLab Alumni

Wan is a first year doctoral student in Instructional Technology & Media within Communication, Computing and Technology in Education program. He's been a math teacher in a New York City public school for 9 years and a couple of different kinds of engineers before that. He is interested in mobile tech-enhanced learning and assessment, especially in PBL settings. His hobby, which became an asset at EdLab, is creating multimedia pieces for capturing and presenting interesting events and/or findings.
