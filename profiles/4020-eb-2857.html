Jordyn Blakely

EdLab Staff

I moved to Brooklyn, New York two years ago after graduating from Berklee College of Music, where I studied drum set performance and music business. I've wanted to work at a library again ever since my experience working for the Berklee Library and Media Center. I love reading, playing and seeing music (of course), biking, cooking, and am always interested in acquiring new skills and hobbies. I'm here helping library patrons by day and out playing shows at night.
