Meredith Powers

EdLab Alumni

Meredith has been involved in libraries since childhood, when she volunteered for the summer reading program in her local public library. Since then, she has worked in bookstores, record stores, art libraries, and academic libraries—constantly cataloging and documenting along the way! Whether she's preserving art websites for the Internet Archive or wireframing the world, Meredith is especially interested in leveraging new technologies to make information accessible and usable for everyone. Meredith has a B.A. from the City College of New York and a Masters of Library & Information Science from Pratt Institute. She loves collecting records, books, and vintage sewing patterns, and she's excited to be part of the EdLab team!
