Panisuan Chasinga

EdLab Alumni

Panisuan graduated with a BA in Architecture from Bangkok, Thailand. As a child, he had been educated in an international background, harvesting cross-cultural mindset along the way. While studying, he became interested and sought apprenticeship in traditional art and craft. He created his first web page in Front Page a while ago in 1997. A few years after graduation, inspired by Picasso's mural Guernica, he became interested in glyphs, semiotics and communication design of different cultures. His interest in communication eventually led him to immerse himself in web design and technologies. Before moving to New York in 2014, he co-founded Soulify, a startup focusing on web-sensor interactions, IoT solutions and media consultancy for private and commercial clients. He loves making toys and games that talk to the internet and making people happy in his spare time.
