Ian Toledo

EdLab Alumni

Ian is cath/artist who creates art to procure a sense of identity. He works in the hybrid artform of comic/graphic novel, the marriage of image and text. He is a comic advocate. He considers himself a counter-culturalist because established hierarchies diminish alternative ways of communication.
