Marcina Zaccaria

EdLab Alumni

Marcina Zaccaria has published articles in Howl Round, Cafe Onda, and Theater Pizzazz, a trade publication in New York City. She completed a writer's residency at chaNorth, part of chashama, and wrote "Revolution: An Artistic Explosion.” She is also a reader for the New York Musical Theater Festival, and was an Adjudicator for the NY International Fringe Festival. Monologues from her plays are published in "InterJACtions: Monologues from the Heart of Human Nature (Vol. II)," available on Amazon. At New York Law School, she submitted all changes for a book published by Palgrave Macmillan. She also edited profiles for the Faculty Members. At The Stage Directors and Choreographers Foundation, she assisted with The Journal, a bi-annual publication. She edited the Stagebill program while working at the Programming Department at Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts. She holds an MFA from Columbia University.
