Jeff Goldenson

EdLab Alumni

Hello, I'm a resident this summer - find me, I love banging around ideas.

I'm a designer interested in how we explore information. More specifically, I'm interested designing ways to better browse information.

This is the definition of browsing I resonate with: Searching is when you know what you're looking for, browsing is when you don't.

I received a Masters from the MIT Media Laboratory in 2007 focusing on audio browsing. In 1999, I received a BA in Architecture from Princeton University.
