Weiqi Ma

EdLab Alumni

Weiqi Ma is a software development associate within the Development & Research division at Edlab. He is also a graduate student at Columbia University, currently pursuing a MS degree in Computer Science with an emphasis on Software systems. His present research interests include web technologies, network security, database system and software system. He has worked on various projects regarding to database, web technologies and network. Weiqi enjoys exploring cutting-edge technologies and applying them into practice within various fields.

Prior to Columbia University, he received his BE degree in Automation at Tsinghua University, China, in 2009. During his undergraduate study, he worked in the System Engineering Lab in department of Automation from 2007 to 2009. His research included exploring general procedure to preprocess raw traffic data and its software implementation, pattern recognition and feature extraction in typical traffic information. As a senior at Tsinghua University, he also worked on developing an improved algorithm for optimal quantum control based on numerical computations.
