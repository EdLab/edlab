Amy Oneal

EdLab Alumni

Amy is happy to have the opportunity to combine her passion for storytelling, technology, and literacy by writing and producing for a variety of projects at the EdLab. Since graduating from Indiana University in 2002 with a B.A. in both English and Telecommunications, Amy has been fortunate to have had a wide variety of opportunities in the field of visual and technical communications. In the past, she has worked as a freelance producer and writer with the PBS series "Across Indiana" at WFYI in Indianapolis. Before moving to New York City, she worked with the Montana Supreme Court, conducting videoconferencing education trainings and workshops for district courts statewide. At Teachers College, Amy is pursuing a Master's degree in Instructional Technology and Media, focusing on interactivity and narrative in new media.
