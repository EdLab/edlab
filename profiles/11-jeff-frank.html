Jeff Frank

EdLab Alumni



Jeff Frank received his PhD in Philosophy and Education from Teachers College, Columbia University. He is interested in the intersection of philosophy, literature and education. His work has appeared, or is scheduled to appear, in Educational Theory, Education and Culture, the Philosophy of Education Society Yearbook, and Educational Philosophy and Theory.
