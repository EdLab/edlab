I-Ching Wang

EdLab Alumni

I-Ching is an EdLab Services Associate at Gottesman Libraries. She holds a M.S. in TESOL from University of Pennsylvania and is currently pursuing her doctoral degree in Instructional, Technology, and Media at Teachers College, Columbia University. She specializes in instructional design of educational technology with a special research interest in how technology and social-cultural factors effect students’ motivation and performance in learning. She has a thirst for knowledge and enjoys learning new things.
