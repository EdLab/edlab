Nichole Hornig

EdLab Alumni

Nichole graduated from the University of Southern California in 2007 with a double major in Spanish and American Studies and a minor in Psychology and Law. Directly after graduation, she moved to Madrid with a Fulbright grant to be an English teaching assistant in a public elementary school and to research bilingual education. She is now working towards a Master's degree in Bilingual/Bicultural Education at TC.
