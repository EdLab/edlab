Josh Lucas

EdLab Alumni

Josh graduated from his high school in Stamford, CT in 2002, but soon realized that he was not yet ready to continue his education in the college setting. In 2003, he enlisted in the US Marine Corps. While serving, he was stationed on the small, sub-tropical island of Okinawa, Japan before becoming a guard at the American embassy in Moscow, Russia. In 2008, after several years abroad and several more years stationed in Virginia, Josh decided he was now ready to continue his academic education. In 2010, he attained his Associate's Degree from Norwalk Community College in CT. Josh is currently a Film Studies student at Columbia University's School of General Studies.
