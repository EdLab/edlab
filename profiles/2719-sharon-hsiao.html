Sharon Hsiao

EdLab Alumni

Dr. Hsiao is a postdoc innovation fellow in EdLab. She develops mSchool courses and researches across products in EdLab. She also teaches Data Visualization in Columbia University. She works at the intersection of social science, computer science & education. She specializes in adaptive technologies in education. She obtains her PhD from School of Information Sciences, University of Pittsburgh. She publishes in a wide range of peer reviewed journals and conferences. She also actively participates in academic services, including teaching, organizing workshops, reviewing papers and volunteering. Her other research interests include Intelligent Tutoring Systems, Computer Supported Collaborative Learning, Visualization, Recommender Systems, Data Sciences and Social Web. Sharon's google scholar page.

In her leisure time, she enjoys sports(tennis, skiing, jogging), traveling, movies, sweets & wine.
