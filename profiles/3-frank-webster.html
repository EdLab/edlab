Frank Webster

EdLab Alumni

Frank Webster a Senior Librarian at the Gottesman Libraries. His role is melding the spheres of traditional library public and technical services, and running like mad to keep up with the libraries of the future. His current assignment includes exploring the links between traditional library archives and modern records management. He will also cop to resident geezer, occasional curmudgeon and wannabe thought leader.
