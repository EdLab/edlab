Alexandra Provo

EdLab Alumni

Alexandra Provo is currently working toward a masters degree in library and information science at the Pratt Institute. You'll find her at the Services Desk in the Gottesman Libraries, where she's excited to work as an EdLab Services Associate. Alex's interest in libraries started in an undergraduate studio art class and developed in Florence, Italy, where she worked as a cataloger in the historic photo archive at Harvard's Villa I Tatti. Before that life she spent a year as an elementary school art teacher in California. Her academic interests lie at the intersection of her experiences with education, libraries, and image resources.
