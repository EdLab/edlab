Carlos Pena

EdLab Alumni

Carlos Pena is a senior at University Heights High School. Carlos interns under EdLab's Digitization Queue (DigiQ) team. He will be attending Carnegie Mellon University in the fall and plans to double major in Economics and Mathematics. The commitment to utilize technology, and innovation, to make a positive impact in the world is what attracts him. He stresses learning, creativity, and imagination, in everything he does. Through his extra-curricular activities, he has had the opportunity to study the increasing importance in financial technologies, the uses of digital marketing, and the drastic impact of educational innovation and entrepreneurship in the lives of young adults. He has also had the opportunity to contribute to the growth and well-being of several individuals across North and South America, experiencing and learning from different cultures and lifestyles. Carlos believes that the research, design, and development of new technologies is imperative towards developing a more prosperous and inclusive society.
