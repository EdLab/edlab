Joachim Castellano

EdLab Alumni

Joachim Castellano is a faculty member and the CALL Coordinator at New York University’s School of Continuing and Professional Studies American Language Institute Tokyo Center. He specializes in media education and production for language learning. In 2013 he was selected as an Apple Distinguished Educator. He has worked previously at Northwestern University’s School of Education and Social Policy, the JET Programme, Apple Computer, and Teachers College Columbia University’s EdLab research unit.
