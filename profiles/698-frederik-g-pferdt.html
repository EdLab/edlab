Frederik G Pferdt

EdLab Alumni

Frederik started his studies in Business and Economics Education just across the Lake of Constance in Germany away from his little hometown Ravensburg (southern Germany). Soon after this small step, he started crossing bigger lakes and seas working, studying, living and got to know exciting people and cultures in cities like Shanghai, Long Beach, Buenos Aires, Cape Town and Stanford working for Deloitte Business Beat, Siemens and Shanghai JTU Ressource Business Consulting Ltd. and studying at Cal State Long Beach and Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

All the time restless and on the run, inspired by people and ideas, Frederik decided back from Silicon Valley in Spring 2009, to make a change in education. Thus, he founded his own consulting business named Learning Designs Lab, where he wants to consult teachers and educational managers using design thinking and learning by design approaches to create innovations in education.

Now, Frederik enjoys the vibrant city of New York where he is a visiting research scholar at Teachers College EdLab and since 2005 he has been a research assistant, lecturer and advanced Ph.D. candidate at University of Paderborn.

Besides never missing a chance for great food and wine, what keeps him up at night, is his research focus of how to design education using innovative technologies like social media. His passion besides innovating education and all kinds of technology gadgets carrying around with him lies in Asia where he spent a lot of time and based on his experience in teaching chinese kids (and getting taught!) and consulting in a HR consultancy, designed a program for Master students in Germany to develop an Asian competence in Japan, Korea and China. At Paderborn, he co-authored two books about Mentoring and organizational development, worked in the Locomotion project about Low-Cost Multimedia Organisation and Production and won an award with his innovative university lecture “LearnLab Media Didactics“ and the d.prize of the Stanford d.school. Design can be found nearly in every aspect of what he is doing.
Because for him, the major aspects in designing are not only the creative aspects, they are also the social aspects where he has special feelings for bringing the right people together at the right time. Want to design an idea? Just talk to him and join him to change the world during the next cooking event!
