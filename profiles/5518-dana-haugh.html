Dana Haugh

EdLab Alumni

Dana is a Materials & Acquisitions Librarian at EdLab and has been working in libraries (public and academic) for the better part of a decade. After a short, but enlightening 3 year stint as a web marketer, she decided to switch career paths and follow her childhood dream of becoming a librarian. She's interested in being at the forefront of emerging technologies in the library and has a particular affinity for the academic setting. She received her MLIS from Queens College and B.A. in English Literature from Stony Brook University.
