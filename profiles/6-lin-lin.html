Lin Lin

EdLab Alumni

Lin is a doctoral student in Communication, Computing and Instructional Technology. She is currently working on her dissertation focusing on asynchronous written texts and online teaching and learning. Her interests include communication, instructional technology, adult and online learning. Lin is head of Teaching Support Group at EdLab. Within her role in TSG and with her academic research, Lin seeks to build a bridge between pedagogy and technology within higher education with computer-mediated communication technologies.
