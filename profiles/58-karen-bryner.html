Karen Bryner

EdLab Alumni

Karen is an educaiton specialist with more than 14 years for professional and academic experience in international education development. In May 2013, she graduated from Teachers College, Columbia University with a PhD in Comparative and International Education. Her doctoral research focused on new trends in Islamic schooling in urban Indonesia. Professionally, she has fulfilled short- and long-term assignments for Harvard University, the World Bank, and domestic and international NGOs in Latin America, Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia working with ministry of education officers and grassroots organizations. She has assisted in the planning and management of training workshops in Ghana and Thailand and research projects in Egypt, Jordan, and Ecuador. She has also prepared reports covering specific educational issues as well as education sector overviews for African and Middle Eastern countries for the World Bank and USAID.
