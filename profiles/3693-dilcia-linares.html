Dilcia Linares

EdLab Alumni

Dilcia started working at Teachers College Library as a part time staff in the Serial Department in the Fall of 1977. After a recommendation from the late Professor George Z. Bereday, she entered Columbia University School of General Studies. In her current position as part of the Material Management Team, Dilcia is in charge of processing all interlibrary loan/document delivery requests for the TC community as well as requests made from other institutions world wide. Dilcia hobbies include traveling, cooking, and dancing just to name a few.
