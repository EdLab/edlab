Rebecca Beck

EdLab Alumni

Rebecca Beck received a master's degree in information and library science at Pratt Institute. At Pratt Rebecca focused her studies on library assessment and usability testing. Rebecca also worked as an English teacher in Japan and at Brooklyn College. Rebecca is interested in many facets of education and information literacy and loves that the TC library and the EdLab work hard to support educators in diverse and exciting ways. Rebecca entered the EdLab as a summer intern and then made her way to the services desk as a member of the library services team and is now an Innovation Fellow.
