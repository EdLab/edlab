Palak Baid

EdLab Alumni

Palak Baid is a Master's student (Computer Science) at Columbia University, New York. He completed undergrad degree (B.Tech in Computer Science) from MANIT - Bhopal, India.

Palak has development interest in the fields of Computer Networks, Distributed systems, Databases, and Web services. He excels in programming languages like C/C++, JAVA, PHP, SQL, JavaScript, etc.

@ EdLab, Palak is working on the LMS discussion board enhancement tool, to build a recommendation system, based on discussions in the forum and mine essential information from database to be made available to authorized users.

Palak enjoys Traveling, Gaming, Sports, Music and Movies, networking with people, and more.
