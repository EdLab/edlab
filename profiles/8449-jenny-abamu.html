Jenny Abamu

EdLab Alumni

Jenny Abamu is originally from Texas, but has spent the last 6 years abroad (Egypt, Turkey, Philippines, Nigeria, Israel, Greece, China and the UAE) and in New York City, gaining experience in journalism, education and digital marketing while simultaneously completing her master’s degree from Columbia University.

She worked as a broadcast journalist and assignment editor for NY1 News Channel (also part of the Time Warner Cable Network), and she is the designer, co- founder, reporter and editor of a popular digital blog on international education, where she spends countless hours monitoring analytics, producing stories and strategizing digital marketing campaigns for education news.

She recently joined Columbia University’s EdLab’s Development and Research Group, producing and publishing content for their digital education publication, New Learning Times.
