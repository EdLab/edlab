Santosh Kumar

EdLab Alumni

Santosh is a Senior Solutions Architect at the Edlab, working on making the education and web awesome. Prior to this, Santosh worked at numerous startups in the Bay Area and NYC. He believes in the power of technology to change lives and few places bring to bear this aspect of technology more than the education space. He is also a believer in the free web being the ideal delivery mechanism for education.

In his earlier roles, Santosh functioned as an angel investor and advisor with a few startups and prior to that he worked as an engineer in several early stage startups. Stuff that excites Santosh include - programming computers, yoga, travel, teas and spending time with his family.
