Xiaobin Zheng

EdLab Alumni

Xiaobin Zheng is a current MS student in Electrical Engineering at Columbia University with an enphasis on Networks & Communications. His present research interests include computer & communication networks; web information management and database system management. He took part in various projects in database systems and web techonologies. Before coming to Columbia Univ., Xiaobin received his Bachelor’s degree in Electrical engineering from Tsinghua University in China, 2009. During his undergraduate study, his research interest included signal & image processing and he worked in the Institute of Imaging and Graphics of Department of Electrical Engineering from 2008 to 2009. During that period, he evolved in a research project of super resolution enhancement of low-resolution human face images, which is a sub-project in human face image recogonition. Xiaobin is a person who is always seeking challenges and willing to learn new things in his life.
