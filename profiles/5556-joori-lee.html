Joori Lee

EdLab Alumni

Joori (Julie) lee is currently pursuing a MFA program at Parsons School of Design studying Design and Technology, and she is a design intern at Edlab. She is a New York based UX designer as well as interaction designer. Julie has a varied background as a designer, holding a BFA in Graphic design and studying an architecture for two years. She has experience designing user interfaces, data visualization, web, and mobile applications with HTML, CSS, Java and C++. Her primary goal as a designer which is focusing on human needs, and the people-first design approach has motivated and influenced her design. Julie Lee loves making, coding and crafting to explore her creativity. Using these techniques she communicates and expresses her design thinking. She is also grateful to be part of the Edlab's design team and, she loves coffee, and traveling around the world.
