Rebekah Judson

EdLab Alumni

Rebekah Judson is a new summer intern at the EdLab as well as a rising sophomore at Swarthmore College. She is a prospective special major in History and Education, with a minor in Russian. In the past, she has worked for City Year New York, tutoring and coordinating a literacy program in the South Bronx, as well as for literary non-profits in New York and Philadelphia. She enjoys creative writing, participating in social action projects, modern and African dance, as well as exploring new cities.
