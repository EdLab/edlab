Featured Vialogues Trend Explorer

Janice Joo

February 15, 2013

Hi Team, Proposing another data visualization... Trying to propose something unique for each product... First up, I'd like to share my idea about new ways to explore Vialogues data: Here, I am proposing an interface and interactive visualization to explore trends that may be found in our featured video discussions. On the backend, it looks like only the featured vialogues (FV) from June 2012 and on are clearly indexed... so it will visualize June 2012 to present. Here's the quick sketch: And for those who need a more hi-fi visualization to imagine what I'm imagining, here's a hi-fi mockup, but not necessarily the final concept: Please feel free to visit me and ask questions about the interactions. Basically, I will stack each month's FV banners (what you see when you're logged out) across an axis. (I was designing the FV banners since June 2012, and Demetri has taken over since the last 2 weeks). Users can search key terms, sending an AJAX request to see if any of the FVs have discussions around that request. For example, if someone were to type the word "violence", all FVs with any mention of "violence" will become highlighted. Then, hovering over that Vialogue will give you access to the video. ...AND/OR... I could pre-determine some trends that might be interesting (indicated by the icons) and give users a chance to identify some EdLab-generated trends. Feedback? Care to help? Let me know! I will not be creating an interactive mockup, and jump straight into developing the functionalities next... Yudan and Josh have helped me get set up as a developer... so stay tuned. Let's see how this goes.
