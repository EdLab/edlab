Can Any Civilization Withstand Planetary Climate Change?

Melanie.Hering

August 20, 2018


While climate change is a fairly new and scary concept for Earthlings, what if it's happened before, to other civilizations on other planets? How might they have weathered serious environmental changes and how might we learn from this? Concerns about creating a sustainable civilization have been plaguing humans for a while now, but astrobiologists, who study life on earth and in space, point out that we need to determine whether sustainability is even possible.

Astrobiologists at the University of Rochester have created mathematical models to understand how a technologically advanced population and its planet might develop alongside climate change. The four different outcomes their models predict are mostly rather dire, and definitely sobering.

Of the models generated, which do you think we're headed towards now? Do you think there are any factors the scientists have forgotten to consider? Add your voice to the discussion on Vialogues.
