Vialogues Got Award!

zhou

June 24, 2012

Although Pranav had spread the secret over a month ago that Vialogues would be one of the Best Websites for Teaching and Learning 2012 elected by American Association of School Librarians, a division of American Library Association, the award list was not announced until yesterday in their annual conference in Anaheim, CA. Here is the link to ALA's Top 25 Sites for Teaching and Learning 2012. I am not familiar with the other brand names this year except Wiggio. But from last year there are a couple I heard about and used, such as Khan Academy, CK-12 Flexbooks, and Microsoft Tag. We should bear the AASL Best 2012 badge on Vialogues.com
