Circulation and Room Booking Roundup, Fall 2011

Michelle Pronovost

December 22, 2011

Two of the library's most popular services are meeting room reservations and materials checkouts. Here they are in numbers for the Fall semester: From September through today, there have been 13,456 checkouts and 11,646 checkins at the Gottesman first floor desk. This includes physical books and equipment like laptops. The TC Community booked almost 5,000 meetings in the library's 12 meeting rooms this fall, with the rooms being used for almost 13,000 hours. Stock up on books for the holiday and get ready to start booking your rooms for the Spring semester in January!
