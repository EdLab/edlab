Vialogues to Teachers

abagigah92

November 8, 2013

Recently I have been working on promoting Vialogues and the idea that stood out was reaching out to teachers and after school programs because they are better at reaching out to students. The task to get individuals to sign up is very difficult compared to reaching out to teachers who have easy access to students in their classrooms. Incentives are very big and teachers and administrators give students the push needed to be active participants on Vialogues. The goal is to persuade teachers to incorporate Vialogue as part of their curriculum or extra credit. This will correspond to students starting meaningful educational conversation in class or at home. Vialogues can serve as a learning tool which can help student express their opinions and share materials that are relevant to the course. My Vialogue videos lately have been on Economics and Foundations that are working to improve the lives and economies of many third world countries. Nationally, I focused on current events concerning the issues on budgets, fiscal and monetary policies. In addition to that, I have been working on series of videos concerning the economies of third world countries and the foundations that are working to improve it.
