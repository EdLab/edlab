2010 AERA: The Presence of China

Ting Yuan

April 25, 2010

Ms. Xiaoya Chen, the Vice Minister, Ministry of Education, People's Republic of China will be leading a delegation from China to the incoming AERA in Denver. The delegation consists of professors and students and two presidents of the best renowned colleges of education in China (i.e., Beijing Normal University and East China Normal University). This is probably the first formal presence of China in AERA which implies China's increasing attention to educational research in the U.S. and a move towards open academic environment and global collaboration. The following are the focal sessions (and a reception with special musical performance) to be presented by the delegation: Developing Students' Creativity: Challenges and Opportunities for Chinese and American Educators and Researchers Sat, May 1 - 12:25pm - 1:55pm Building/Room: Colorado Convention Center / Korbel Ballroom 4 CDE Participant: Qi Dong (Beijing Normal University) Participant: Youqun Ren (East China Normal University) Participant: Jin Huang (Ambow Education Group) Participant: Robert Siegler (Carnegie Mellon University) Participant: Michael S.
