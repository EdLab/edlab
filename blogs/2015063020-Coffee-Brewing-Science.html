Coffee Brewing Science

EdLab Studios

June 30, 2015


New Yorkers are notorious for making coffee-fueled haste. As long as it's strong and in a reliable cup, they spend little time distinguishing between beans and brews. But what if they did want to know where their coffee comes from and how to evaluate it? To solve just that problem, as well as offer a professional development space for baristas around the city, Irving Farm Coffee Roasters opened a new training lab on the border of Flatiron and Chelsea. Hobbyists and professionals alike gather here for hour-long classes on coffee cupping and tasting, as well as longer classes on espresso extraction and preparation, milk science and pouring technique.
