An Autism Story With A Happily Ever After

jvazquez

May 8, 2014


Renowned author Ron Suskind and his wife Cornelia came up with a brilliantly simple way to reach their son who doctors diagnosed with regressive autism. Every day, Owen would watch a Disney animated movie, transfixed. In a lightbulb moment, Ron discovered how to turn an obsession into a lesson. By carefully observing his son and listening to his attempts to communicate, Ron learned how magical the world of Disney could be. Watch the clip from filmmaker Roger Ross Williams's Animating Owen to witness the extraordinary way the father and son talk to each other.

The Suskinds came up with a creative intervention for their son. Have you come across (or, even better, implemented) equally thoughtful approaches for autism? If so, share your experiences in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
