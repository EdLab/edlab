E-Textbooks

gnatriello

January 26, 2007

An article in the Miami Herald describes the slow uptake of e-textbooks by college students and considers the reasons why adoption of this alternative is not moving more quickly. One of the issues noted is the lack of standards for formats and the lack of a popular reader. The standards issue is being addressed by the International Digital Publishing Forum, an organization of publishers seeking to promote international standards for e-books.
