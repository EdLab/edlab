Timely Documentaries

Erin Murphy

March 24, 2009

Robert Greenwald is publishing his new documentary, "Rethink Afghanistan" as a series on YouTube. The first two parts of the series is already up. The interesting thing is that he is still in Afghanistan shooting. He is trying to limit the amount of time it takes to film and edit a feature-length documentary. So he is doing it as he goes along. Greenwald says “It's an interesting juggling act ... between researching to find people, interviewing the people, editing the pieces, seeing what is happening in the news – and raising funds at the same time.” It would be interesting to figure out if he is able to raise more funds because of the quick turn around time. I also question categorizing this as a "documentary," what makes it different from a 60 minutes segment? Isn't it just reporting at this point?
