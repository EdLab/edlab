Running weather is here and that means it is time to restart the EdLab Running Club! 






Join us! 


We usually meet every Wednesday evening around 5:15pm and run in Riverside Park down till 102nd street [for a 2-mile round trip], or if we are really feeling it, down till 96th street [for a 3-mile round trip]. We also go to Morningside Park or Central Park sometimes. 


All EdLabbers are welcome!
