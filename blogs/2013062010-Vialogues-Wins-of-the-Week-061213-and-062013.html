Vialogues Wins of the Week: 06.12.13 & 06.20.13

Kate Meersschaert

June 20, 2013

***Private!!! Hi Everyone! Once again, Jo and I would like to share the week(s) "V-Wins", updates and ideas with you! Usage stats: New Users: 121 Vialogues Created: 172 Total Visits: 8,686 Private Vialogues: 70 Public Vialogues: 102 New Vialogues Usage Right here at TC! Jo and I met with the heads of TC Career Services last Thursday (following an earlier meeting with Megha and Kate re: ResearchBroker and our suite of EdLab applications) and shared Vialogues with the team. The TCCS team is now interested in using Vialogues to share recruiting sessions, presentations and panels with existing TC students and their global Alumni network. To quote Victoria Gochenour from a thank you note she sent after our meeting:

Yes, we are thrilled about the prospect of using Vialogues! I think we and our students will benefit greatly from this magnificent platform! We are eager to use it this coming academic year to meet many of our programming needs!"
We will keep you posted as the TCCS team tests the platform this summer!
