TCR Individual Subscribers - Who are they?

Christine.Odenath

January 24, 2019

Safe to assume we're all fans of peer reviewed articles, commentaries, and book reviews on education. Now imagine your workplace didn't have an institutional subscription to TCR... What would you do? Well you might just become a TCR Individual Subscriber!


There are 2,081 TCR accounts with individual digital subscriptions (so they pay $25/yr for digital TCR content). Lucky for you, I googled all 2,081 email addresses to answer what must now be your burning question: who are these subscribers? Let's check out the findings!


TLDR: a lot of educators, higher ed mostly. Some researchers, government employees, education advocates, one boat captain, some famous people. 


**Interesting finds at the bottom**


Individual Subs by Primary Employment Affiliation:



Finding #1: The majority of individual subscribers are employed at higher ed institutions (n=1,222; 59%)
  • 71% of these individuals are educators (blanket term for "Instructor" or "Lecturer" or any type of "Professor"). 
  • These subscribers are affiliated with >600 different higher ed institutions.

Finding #2: The 2nd largest group of subscribers are employed in roles affiliated with primary/secondary education (n=300; 15%)


  • These individuals work as teachers (~1/3), principals, school staff, school board members, etc. 
  • 99% of these prim/secondary ed affiliated subscribers are based in the U.S.

Finding #3: Educators and institutional leadership comprise the bulk of education-based roles assumed by individual subscribers 


Subscriber Roles in Higher Ed and Prim/Sec Ed



  • Education "Leadership" is a blanket term for any school "president," "dean," "head of upper/lower school," "chancellor," etc.



Finding #4: If not employed in education, individual subscribers are typically self-employed, or work at nonprofits or various businesses (n=340; 16%)


Subscriber Roles in Business, Self-Employment, and NonProfits 



  • Many subscribers who work in businesses or nonprofits are executives (blanket term for "Chief ___ Officer", "Executive ___", etc.) ... Various staff or director roles were also common.
  • Consulting is an oft-cited profession, especially among self-employed subscribers.
  • Authors and practicing mental health professionals also often fall in the self-employed bucket. 



Finding 5#: Individual subscribers come from places outside the United States! (n=243; 12%)

International Subscriber Roles



  • The bulk of international individual subscribers work in higher education (73%).
  • One interesting find was the government affiliation (4%), where most subscribers were affiliated specifically with education branches of their governments.



FuN FaCtS 
  1. There are over 23 TC Alumni with individual TCR subscriptions
  2. Over 30 accounts are duplicates (are these people paying double?)
  3. Over 30 subscribers are retired
  4. Over 120 hold positions in research or work at research companies
