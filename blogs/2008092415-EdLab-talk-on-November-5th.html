EdLab talk on November 5th

Laura Scheiber

September 24, 2008

Hello EdLab: We are inviting Luis Nascimento to speak at the November 5 EdLab seminar. Luis was in the movie City of God and now runs a non-profit called Cinema Nosso Cinema Nosso . This non-profit focuses on empowering youth through media arts education. There are a few different ways that Luis could focus his presentation, and we're interested in your input and suggestions. What's most relevant or interesting to you? Your work at Edlab? * Producers, not consumers: How Cinema Nosso works toward empowering marginalized youth to be producers of media. * International collaborations: Working on international collaborative media projects. * Balancing the technical with the conceptual: How to teach youth technical media skills while also encouraging critical thinking skills and creative expression. * MediaEd in schools: How media education can be included in school curriculum. * Access and empowerment: How Cinema Nosso works with underserved youth and provides new opportunities that are relevant and can be incorporated into their day to day lives. We are open to other ideas. Thanks for the feedback!
