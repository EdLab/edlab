Google Buying War Robot Construction Firm

Oumar Soumahoro

December 17, 2013

I previously posted a blog about Amazon's Prime Air, an innovative drone that will allow Amazon to offer fast delivery service its customers. It claims to have deliveries made within half an hour after purchase. However, I am more than certain that Amazon's drone plans would not go unanswered by its competitors. I came across this article that talks about Google's new acquisition: the Boston Dynamics, an engineering company renowned for constructing robots for the military. Several questions immediately came to mind: Why does Google need a war robot? Is this in response to the Amazon drone? What do you think Google's intentions are with this purchase?
