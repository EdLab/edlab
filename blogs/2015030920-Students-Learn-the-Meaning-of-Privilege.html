Students Learn the Meaning of Privilege

jvazquez

March 9, 2015


What do a piece of paper and a recycling bin have to do with privilege? The answer is not what you might expect, if you expect anything at all.

The above video describes a masterfully conceived lesson from a high school teacher who sought to teach his students about privilege and social mobility. His lesson can be easily reproduced with any group of students and has the potential to spark great debate and conversation. It carries a poignant message for students to take away.

What are other creative ways in which teachers have broached sensitive topics within a class? Would you do this privilege lesson with your students? If so, would you modify any part of it? Let us know in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
