Over Night Trip to Monmouth University

Mingoro Fanny

July 19, 2012

A friend and I from school have been selected to participate in a college summit workshop at Monmouth University in Central Jersey. We have been selected because of our good academics and leadership skills. It will be a four day trip, starting tomorrow until Sunday evening. During my stay over, I will have the opportunity to do the following: -Live in a dorm -meet new people -explore and participate in sessions that will give me a feel of the college setting -and I will be able to learn the whole process for applying to college. From this I would be able to start my college application earlier than others and complete the applying process months beforehand.Therefore I would be ahead and should have more time on my hands for other activities such as improving my goalkeeping for soccer, which I want to continue playing in college. When school starts, I should also be able to share with peers in school the skills and experiences I've learned from this workshop. This would benefit both me and my peers because we would be able to understand and complete the college applying process with out as many problems or difficulties as we would have otherwise.
