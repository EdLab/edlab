"School ReDesign, Season 1: The Exhibit" now online

Molly Riordan

May 1, 2009

We're really excited about the work going on across the room at AfterEd, which is why we're happy to announce a new exhibit featuring the first season of School ReDesign, on display on the first floor. The Media Design team wants to promote the great work going on at EdLab across TC and beyond, increasing our exposure and engaging others in the innovation bursting from EdLab every day. The exhibit is also up in a Flickr Set and in Pocket Knowledge. School ReDesign Season Two is now in production; read up on the details on the ReDesign website. Finally, this is the first of what will surely be many calls for ideas about collaborative partners. The Media Design team is looking for researchers collecting data or developing ideas we might turn into engaging displays at TC. If you know of some groundbreaking or highly-topical research going on in other departments that should get more exposure at TC, let us know. We want to tap these TC resources!
