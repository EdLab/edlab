Trends in Ed, 5.6.09

Arianna Choi

May 6, 2009

Avant-garde Cellist Zoe Keating's performance is like the audio experience equivalent of watching a bricklayer being stacked on another bricklayer to build an entire house. Her compositions start out simply, but she adds successive layers of sound by stacking and removing them to create music. Without computers, playing her composition would require a dozen musicians or more.

My music is the fusion of information architecture and classical music.
