BioDive Digital Journal 

Take a sneak peak at the Digital Journal component of the BioDive VR experience here.  


Feedback on Your Experience 

Have some burning thoughts/questions that you didn't get to share during seminar? No worries! Jessica and Lindsay have set up a convenient Google Doc for you. 


Building and Using Virtual Reality in Education Meet-Up

Because launching her own edtech company isn't enough work, Jessica also hosts a VR in Education meet-up on the third Wednesday of every month (e.g. today). If you're interested in making the trek over to Brooklyn, you can RSVP here. 


Call for Teacher/School Connections 

If you know of any schools/teachers who might be down for demoing Killer Snails products, email Jessica at jessica@killersnails.com.
