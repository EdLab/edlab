Apple's iWatch Coming Soon

Oumar Soumahoro

March 14, 2013

After encountering a not too successful sale with the iPhone 5, Apple plans on bringing its iWatch out soon. This technology will cover up the latest updates Apple on its iPhone and iPads, iOS 6. Not only will this device work as a an ipod nano and a watch, it will also be utilized as a cellphone. It will allow you to control your phone without having to physically touch it, and will also notify you of every activity going on your mobile device, such as calls, messaging, and emails. The iWatch is predicted to have a battery life of up to five days, work as a fitness device (recording health data), etc. This article covers more details regarding Apple's next big move in the market.
