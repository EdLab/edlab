EdLab Review: Yamisee

Arianna Choi

April 8, 2010

*please keep this private* Based on the comments received during the last Development & Research meeting, here goes a 3-minute sneak peek at an experimental (and hopefully improved?) version of the EdLab Review. Thank you in advance for your continuous feedback! Yamisee is a live online learning platform that creates a marketplace for teachers and subject matter experts to share their knowledge. Much in the way eBay connects buyers with sellers, the platform connects independent experts with paying students. Yamisee.com is currently in an early adopter release phase and is on target for a full release in Q2 2010. p.s. I am terribly embarrassed to hear my recorded voice...
