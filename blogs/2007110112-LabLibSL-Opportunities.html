Lab/Lib/SL Opportunities

Anthony Cocciolo

November 1, 2007

I've been getting back into Second Life lately (mostly because I committed myself to doing a consumer research project on it for the class I am taking at CBS, not to mention the AERA presentation that might get accepted!). Being back in SL, it is interested to see all the developments on the TC Island. I was particularly amused by the re-creation of Zankel hall, where I got to pretend to be a TC Security guard. Some details are particularly well done (I am impressed by the entrance-way and those lamp posts). I do think there may be an opportunity to run some EdLab/library events in the space.
