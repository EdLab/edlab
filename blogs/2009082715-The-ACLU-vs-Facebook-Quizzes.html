The ACLU vs. Facebook Quizzes

Jeannie Crowley

August 27, 2009

Did you know that Facebook quiz apps and other third-party apps have access to most of your profile information? If you think your privacy settings will protect you, think again. The ACLU has created a quiz to show you how much of your information you're giving away on Facebook every time you take the Which Soda am I? quiz. Check out the article and the quiz.
