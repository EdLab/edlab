Stanford Online AI Course Instructors & Sal Khan Hangout on Google+

Kate Meersschaert

December 13, 2011

In honor of today's stellar D&R meeting from Fred & Demetri, I created a vialogue from this amazing Google+ hang-out between the founders/instructors of the online Stanford AI class and Salman Khan from Khan Academy. Some of the topics discussed include why & how they created their online platform, what the role of badges & game dynamics plays and what is the future of education using technology. Questions are fielded from institutions ranging from USC to MIT.
