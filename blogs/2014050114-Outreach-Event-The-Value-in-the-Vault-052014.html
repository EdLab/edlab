Outreach Event: The Value in the Vault, 05.20.14

Kate Meersschaert

May 1, 2014

Friends of EdLab, NYC Media Lab, is hosting an upcoming panel discussion, "The Value in the Vault" @ Shutterstock HQ on Th. May 20th from 6-7:30 during Internet Week. The evening will focus on the future of sharing archival materials (multimedia, images, articles etc.) across digital devices & multiple platforms. The panel looks outstanding (& includes a Columbia U Data Sciences/Engineering Prof) and well worth the FREE price tag! ;) I am hoping to dig-up more NLT leads (profiles etc.) and to meet new potential readers of NLT and users of our tools. Panel Includes: -Jim Chou, CTO at Shutterstock -Marc Frons, CIO at The New York Times -Owen Rambow, Co-Chair, New Media Center, Institute for Data Sciences and -Engineering at Columbia University -Dirk Van Dall, VP, Multimedia Technology Development at Major League Baseball Advanced Media
