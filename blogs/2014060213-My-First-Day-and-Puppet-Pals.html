My First Day and Puppet Pals

Ting Yuan

June 2, 2014

Hi Everyone, I'm so glad to be back at EdLab and I look forward to collaborating with many of you on the exciting development and research projects ongoing here. To celebrate my first day, I started a mini video recording by using an iPad app named Puppet Pals.

Puppet Pals is a wonderful digital storytelling app that I learned when I was teaching a summer course at Teachers College. You can create your own characters through photos of your friends or choosing some puppet characters within the app. Further, you can create your voiceovers along with the animations of your characters. A great example of how you can use Puppet Pals with students can be seen here. In this example, students in a first grade class were asked to create their own version of a BrainPop video explaining the hatching of a baby check. Cheers, Ting
