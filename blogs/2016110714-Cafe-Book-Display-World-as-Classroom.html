Cafe Book Display: World as Classroom

Jennifer

November 7, 2016

Hello, All. Next month our cafe collection will include books about international education, inspired by the work of faculty and alumni, and in support of the imminent International Education Week (11/14-11/18) Also to showcase major issues and challenges in the field. Here are a few books suggested by Chiara Fuller, TC International Services and masters’ candidate, as well as Jennifer. We would love your suggestions!. What books have you read, or would like to read, that are missing from this list? Any you'd take out? (Leave titles in the comments, please!), and with many thanks for your help!
