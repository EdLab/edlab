Street Labs

EdLab Studios

June 2, 2013


It is a little known fact that Israelis invented the flash-drive. In the middle of NYC at Union Square, an Israeli science and technology delegation has created "Street Labs" in order to continue to carry on their tradition of technology innovation and inspire others to do the same. In a tent filled with interactive exhibits and eager Israeli students, tourists and New Yorkers alike can drop in to see what students in Israel are working on while conversing on topics ranging from physics to technology to recycling and more. This tent in Union Square provides a concentrated week of cultural exchange where new ideas and inspirations can be birthed through a meeting of minds. Have something to say about "Street Labs"?
