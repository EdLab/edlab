Graduates Income in STEM

rendy

November 17, 2013

Grads with a bachelor's degree and less than three years of experience in their occupation earn $39,700 a year but people in STEM occupations are able to earn twice as much. The top occupation in STEM with the most income is petroleum engineer with a starting salary of $88,700, followed by nuclear engineer with $62,900, and marine engineer with $62,200. According to Forbes, there is an expected 17% growth in petroleum and marine engineering, while there is an expected a growth of 10% in nuclear engineer. Earning that amount of many as a recent graduate makes you feel good and proud of the work you've done. Some people might be against it because they believe it's not fair that a recent college graduate should earn that amount of money. Earning this income shows all the dedication they've put in to working in a STEM field.
