Take a Walk with Pundit!

Kate Meersschaert

December 5, 2011

(Private!) Pundit needs your help... After much work from Yudan, we have Spring 2012 courses updated and are ready to promote our intelligent search and recommendation tool to the TC community! Today starting at noon we will have small teams roam the halls handing-out our infamous fortune cookies. What follows is a potential "script" to help share the value of Pundit and explain why we are handing-out cookies: EdLaber: "Have you tried Pundit yet?" TCer: "No!" EdLaber: "This cookie holds the secret to an easier, more enjoyable course search experience." or "Pundit will help you find your ideal course match!" (If they are still with you) "Pundit is an intelligent search and recommendation tool developed here at TC. Please explore the site and let us know what you think!" Please reply to this post with the time slots you would be interested in volunteering for: 12-12:30 4-4:30 5:30-6 (I might solo this run as it is "after hours!") Also, we will scatter cookies on tables and feature them in bowls along with postcards and signage designed by Janet throughout TC in the following locations: -Near the entrance to the first floor library -On the break tables in the basement, near the vending machines -Whittier dining tables -In the cafe near the existent signs -On the dining room tables -On the third-floor food-friendly tables Please feel free to reply and suggest additional locations for both cookie "drops" & more formal bowl-based displays! Thanks in advance for helping to make this guerilla/cookie-based marketing initiative a success & helping with our #1 goal of recruiting new users to the world of intelligent course search and recommendation!
