.NYC Meetup

Kate Meersschaert

December 6, 2013

I discovered an upcoming Meetup focused on the newly announced top-level domain for NYC, .NYC. Although not directly related to edtech, I feel that this potentially groundbreaking change (the first time a city has registered for a dedicated top-level domain) will have potential implications for entrepreneurship and technology in the region and beyond. Also, my NLT Profile, Jonathan Askin, shared this event and is hosting/sponsoring it at along with Brooklyn Law/BLIP Clinic. If you are interested in participating in this free workshop/meetup please RSVP here (space is limited). Details: Monday, December 9, 2013 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM Feil Hall - Brooklyn Law School 205 State Street, 22nd Floor, Brooklyn, NY
