Outreach Update: Macmillan New Ventures Happy Hour

Kate Meersschaert

June 8, 2012

Private please! Details... Michelle and I attended a Macmillan New Ventures happy hour event this past Wednesday night. Packed to the gills with eager edtechers, the event was REALLY WELL attended. I would guestimate about 200 people were crammed into a downstairs space at the Flatiron Lounge that was no bigger than our seminar area. Goal of the event... When I asked Mike Berlin, the organizer of the event and Director of Strategic Initiatives for Macmillan New Ventures about the purpose and goal of the event his response was, "to establish MNV as the go-to for edtech incubation and to establish our credibility as knowledge-holders in the space." Much like the bars and "clusters" of experts in the early days of Silicon Valley. EdLab Relevance... What struck me as especially relevant for our work at the EdLab, upcoming demo night and overall LaunchPad goals and initiatives are: 1) The NYC edtech scene this is a rapidly growing space! 2) People just want an area to network and share ideas... set-it-up & get-out of the way! 3) Startup founders want a platform to discuss their budding companies... Which brings me to my final points... how this all ties my mission to evangelize for the EdLab! 1) I see outreach as a chance to spread the "EdLab Gospel!" Why are we special, who we are and what we DO! 2) To gauge interest in potential LaunchPad & NLT initiatives by asking questions like: "what would be most helpful to you as an early stage startup? Demo night? Advice on VCs etc." 3) To gather contacts for us to promote our apps & services to and to consider for demo nights, EdTech talks and more! I am excited to report-out on any upcoming events an and as always, the more the merrier!
