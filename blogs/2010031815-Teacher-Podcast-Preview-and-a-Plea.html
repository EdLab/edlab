Teacher Podcast Preview and a Plea!

Jessica Blum

March 18, 2010

Chingfu and I have been hard at work experimenting on a teacher-supportive podcast (ala SECTtOR's NTR, for those of you who are familiar with that project). Below we've embedded a quick “trailer” to share the flavor of our first episode (about the highs and lows of teaching), but we're having a hard time coming up with an effective, inclusive name for our show, and would love to learn from your always wise insights and ideas! Since we anticipate the show taking multiple formats (i.e., conversational panel discussions like the one in this first episode, interviews with special guests from the college and beyond, and case- or issue-based collaborative reflections), we'd like the name to be general enough to suggest variety, but education-specific in a way that doesn't sound . . . well, clichÃ©. So far, no luck. Time for reinforcements? Any feedback (re: the name, or otherwise) would be much appreciated, and we'd also like to extend a giant thanks to Jeannie, Hui Soo, and Skye for making this first experiment possible. Thanks, guys!!!!
