NBC acquires social media journalism app out of ITP

Nara Kasbergen

August 12, 2013

The grad program I attended, ITP, is a great place that's earned a fair amount of recognition for the work done by its graduates -- though more commonly for projects produced after they leave, and not for projects that were done while they were at ITP. The most notable exception up until now was Foursquare, which Dennis Crowley began building while at ITP and which was bought by Google shortly after he graduated. Well, today I was happy to discover that there's finally another such ITP success story: Phil Groman, who just graduated this past May, developed a thesis project, an app promoting citizen journalism through social media, which was just acquired by NBC News. You can read about it in the New York Times.
