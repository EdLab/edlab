Trends in Ed, 12. 22.2010: College interview app

Angela Lee

December 22, 2010

Options for College has created the Entoview College Edition app to prepare high school students who have never experienced a formal interview to be ready for the moment that may determine what the next four years of their lives may be like. The content was written by Entoview expert Keith Berman, a certified educational planner who has counseled hundreds of students through the application process and worked as an interviewer at Harvard and Yale. Maybe this can be an element to be added to the National Center for Disaster Prevention (NCDP) project; an interactive app that would prepare people for disasters. Other than the app, I would like to add a bonus to today's Trends in Ed - I came across this article by Teacher John T. Spencer, a simple but yet powerful piece.
