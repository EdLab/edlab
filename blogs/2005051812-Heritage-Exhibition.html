Heritage Exhibition

bhughes

May 18, 2005

fyi: the design group is preparing for a summer exhibition (may 30 - july 29) in the m.w.offit gallery. this exhibition will feature heritage high school students' work in a computer art class run by hugo ortega. the exhibition is special for a few reasons... 1) heritage is a special nyc public school overseen by tc faculty and students. 2) hugo is joining the staff of the design group this summer as a video producer. 3) the collaboration with his students used the library plotter as a resource, and as a result, the school's principle decided to purchase a printer for the schools. the students' work is very creative. the opening will be from 4-6:30 on friday, june 10th. there will be publicity send out to advertise the event. please spreaad the word!
