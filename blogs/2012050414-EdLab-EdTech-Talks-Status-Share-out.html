EdLab EdTech Talks Status Share-out

Kate Meersschaert

May 4, 2012

Private Please! Reintroduction & background... I wanted to share the latest of my EdLab EdTech Talk interview series (3rd in the Series!)... and ask for your feedback! As we move forward with exciting inaugural New Learning Times and LaunchPad initiatives I wanted to share thinking and work I have been doing to explore these initial informal (for now!) interviews with startup founders. Focus so far... The focus of these talks has been to capture an informal conversation around key issues in the edtech startup world including: tips for budding "edupreneurs", struggles as a founder & future predictions for the field. Many of these start-ups are based outside of NYC and are only available in NYC for brief periods of time as they demo (demo D&R this Tuesday!) their products and meet with potential investors. As such, these interviews are often last-minute and due to this factor, are often unable to be worked-into the seminar schedule. Future goals... I imagine these interviews (with higher production-value) could fit under the LaunchPad, mSchool & NLT umbrellas! For LaunchPad they could serve as an artifact or asset for both promotion of our LP initiatives on social media and as a resource for startups to share with potential investors. The interviews could be a part of an mSchool course on edupreneurship. Finally, these interviews could be featured as a part of the StartupSpotlight stream of NLT. Look-out for more developments soon as we explore a higher production value for these EdTech Talks and I look-forward to your thoughts (any naming suggestions?)!
