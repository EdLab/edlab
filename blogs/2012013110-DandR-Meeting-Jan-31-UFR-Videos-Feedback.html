D&R Meeting Jan. 31: UFR Videos Feedback

Nantwi

January 31, 2012

Below are a set of videos we have produced for the UFR project. For this D&R meeting, we are looking for specific feedback on the videos: - What is unclear in each video? - What can be clarified through the addition of text, images, lists, infographics, etc? - What should be removed in the videos? - Which segments might be moved around in a video to clarify a concept or pedagogical practice? Group 1: Promo Economics Group 2: Why Teach UFR? Civics Group 3: Games and Interactive Media UFR Promo Video #2 If you finish your group discussion early, please share your ideas for how we might distribute the three promotional videos.
