BlackBerry Z10

Daniel Vergara

March 20, 2013

When I heard the news about the new Blackberry phone, I thought it was going to be a great improvement that might persuade me to go back as I currently have an iPhone. But I came across this article that reveals facts about the phone that quickly changed my mind. Apparently the phone is still lacking many things that would help it compete against phones like the iPhone or Samsung Galaxy. One of the crucial things that changed my mind was that it has a small amount of apps for people to download. One of the things I like about my iPhone is looking at the Apps store and seeing what new things are available for me to download. The aforementioned article notes that you would only be able to download apps like Facebook and other social networking sites in addition to probably a few games that are not popular now. But despite the fact that it has a lot of downsides, here's a video that shows the best features of the new Blackberry.
