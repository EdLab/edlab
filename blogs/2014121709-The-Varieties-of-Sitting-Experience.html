The Varieties of Sitting Experience

Gonzalo Obelleiro

December 17, 2014

EdLab's entrance lobby these days is a welcoming sign to the wonderful world of chair diversity. There is something compelling about the varieties of shape, materials, and style. Chairs, perhaps more than most other objects, have personality. A room full of different chairs can be an eloquent expression of the value of difference. So what if our library reading rooms and learning theater were equipped with sitting devices of all kinds? It could be a wonderful experimental opportunity, and it would allow us to update furniture gradually, piece by piece, without disrupting a sense of coherence in style (the organizing principle would be difference anyway!).
