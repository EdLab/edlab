D&R Meeting: Rethinking Materials Team Training

LauraCostello

October 18, 2011

(please keep private) Demetri and I have been working to update the training structures in the materials team to move from documentation to production and create training that is dynamic, organized, and automated. Our primary targets at this point are DocDel and archival retrievals. We'll be conducting use experiments during DR&M to test our newly developed materials and solicit ideas for reforming these processes. After the activities, please answer any or all of the following questions based on your experience. Thank you in advance for your assistance and participation! Archival Retrievals Questions: -How can we automate training for tasks like retrievals and Docdel? -What forms of media (video, screenshot slides etc) do you believe would be most effective for training? Think also about effective combinations of media. -What's a good way to familiarize someone with the basement rooms, before they actually going down there? DocDel Questions: -How do you feel about the created flow chart? Is the process easy to understand? -Do you think we will be able to fully automate this process? If not, what time frame and resources do you think would be helpful to devote to training? -Do you think multimedia resources like videos and screencasts would improve retention for DocDel training?
