Glimpse into the Future of Learning

Jenny Abamu

December 20, 2016


What if you could reprogram objects all around you? What if robots could inherit memories of deceased loved ones? What if hacking turned into an Olympic sport? These vialogues highlight the not-so-distant future possibilities of learning with cutting edge technology:

1. Now Technology Allows You to Customize Your Reality
Jumping onto the exhilarating Internet of Things wave is opening up so many learning possibilities beyond the computer screen; MIT shares their latest research in this fascinating video.

2. How an Interactive Tabletop will Transform Reading
Talk about words jumping off of the page. This Sony tabletop made our list for making me wish I was a kid again, so I could grow up in a world where your imagination really comes to life.

3. Good Idea, Bad Idea: Uploading the Human Mind to a Robot
The future of tech is both bright and dark as a scientist explores the future of learning machines with human feelings and memories. This video made the list for making us simultaneously excited and terrified.
