PART IV: POCKETKNOWLEDGE DIGITAL OUTREACH

Following the relaunch of the PocketKnowledge website, Jo authored an email notifying past and present users that PocketKnowledge is new, improved, and open to their contributions. For the purposes of email distribution, users were segmented into two groups: members of the TC community (i.e. has a uni) and non-TC users (i.e. does not have a uni).

 

A total of 4,602 emails were sent. The distribution of emails delivered is as follows:

 

 ]

 

The campaign has generated 152 clicks to-date. Between TC and non-TC folks, the former was most inclined to take the bait.

 

 

Moving forward, we will be sending newsletters to PocketKnowledge users on a monthly basis. These will be supplemented by Pressible and social media posts.

 

PART IV: NEXT STEPS

In addition to supporting the outreach/LX team in developing an online presence for PK, I will be experimenting with small adjustments to our social media strategy:

  • Begin using campaign links for the @TCRecord Twitter account so as to more accurately track the impact of our social media presence on website traffic.
  • As some content this month earned much higher engagement rates than what we normally see (Karl Marx, scrum), I’ll be recycling some of these more popular tweets/Facebook posts moving forward.
