One Million Users for Vialogues

Francisco.Mendoza

August 21, 2013

At today's EdLab seminar, Hui Soo pointed out something that is very true. How can we get new users for our projects (e.g. NLT, Vialogues), if we don't use them regularly ourselves? I for one can admit that I use vialogues but find it very hard to find myself making a video even if I wanted to, but it is also very hard to stay on the website. I think that we can get a lot of more users for Vialogues and our other projects if we were to offer what other social networks do such as tagging a friend in a comment, liking a comment (or use an "agree" button since Facebook has the like button), being able to subscribe to someone's channel in case you like the focus of their videos. Even making a response video to a video you highly agree or disagree with can make Vialogues a very sociable site and will lead to more new users. I enjoy Vialogues the way it currently is, but I truly believe that with a little bit of add ons, we can have a lot more users. I was able to recruit about 10 users in the past month. My method was asking these potential users what videos they would be interested in having a discussion on and I posted such a video accordingly. I included alot of questions in the Vialogue, and they actually responded to them. I feel this is one way to get new users for not just Vialogues but our other products as well.
