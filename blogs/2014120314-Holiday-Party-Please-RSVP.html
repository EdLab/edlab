Holiday Party - Please RSVP

christophergu

December 3, 2014

** PRIVATE* Hi All, The EdLab Holiday Party is fast approaching! Next Thursday, December 11th, we will begin with a scavenger hunt at The Museum of Natural History (West 77th Street between Columbus and Central Park West). We will convene at the Museum around 10:45 AM and the hunt will begin promptly at 11:00 AM. Teams will be divided into 5-6 members each. The hunt will last approximately 90 minutes and should wrap up by 12:45. We will then head across the street to The Shake Shack for lunch at 1:00 PM. The lower level party room has been reserved for the EdLab from 1:00 to 2:30 PM. We will have a catered lunch of burgers, fries, and of course, their world famous shakes! If you are planning to attend next Thursday, please RSVP using the link below, no later later than Monday, December 8th. https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1HKJh31Fs-1eZylIeNvUDx-FxXmaZMP9--jrLw6xzhQo/viewform?usp=send_form Hope you will join us for the festivities next Thursday! Chris
