Simple Storytelling

Rebecca.Sullivan

November 11, 2018


Our Story is a straightforward app that turns looking at family photos into an exercise in reading. It’s goal is to prepare young children for reading by helping them curate personalized content. Our Story couldn’t be more simple. You just take or select a photo, and add words or an audio recording. These images can then be used as vocabulary cards, or combined into a story to record memories of a trip or holiday. The free iPad app was created by researchers at Open University.

Pros:

With very little work, family photos can be converted into a picture book. What better way to inspire children to read than by creating a storybook about them?

Cons:

Our Story offers no frills and little room for creativity. While this makes it easy to use, the app could be enhanced by offering options for digital and audio effects. Our Story allows users to make books, but with a little enhancement, it could be a tool for creating art projects.

Our Takeaway:

Our Story is very basic, but offers an easy way to organize memories and foster interest in reading.
