forget that flash drive!

Karen Bryner

February 14, 2006

Google announced last week a new version of Google Desktop which allows remote access to your pc. It gives you the option of placing local files, along with your recently viewed Web pages, on Google's servers so you can access them from anywhere. These files are stored on Google for 30 days. The concern regarding privacy is huge.
