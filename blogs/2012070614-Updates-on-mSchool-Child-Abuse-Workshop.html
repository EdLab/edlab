Updates on mSchool Child Abuse Workshop

Lu Wang

July 6, 2012

Private Please. It has been about six weeks since our last D&R meeting regarding the mSchool child abuse workshop required for all NYS pre-service teachers. Here are some recent progress updates. Content Delivery Thanks to the team (Laura, Jo, Janice) effort, we finalized the format of the course delivery to be videos which will fit into ten different moodle modules. Supplementary information to each module will include quiz, appendix, glossary, additional info and discussion forum. The videos will contain a presentation of animated slides accompanied with lecturing. Three demo videos were created to show ideas of embedded sources, data visualization, and raised questions. If interested, feel free to ask for the access. Demo 1 Demo 2 Demo 3 Transcript and Timing So far, the transcript of all nine modules of the child abuse workshop and the raw slides with bullet points have been created. First proofreading of the transcript has also been finished. The course was roughly timed, and the content is subject to further revision in order to fulfill the NYSED's requirement of two clock hours. Data Visualization Quite a few data visualization ideas were brainstormed so as to ease the audience's comprehension toward various data throughout the whole course. Basically simple animation tools (such as powerpoint, prezi) would be used to effectively transfer important points contained in each module. Next Steps...

  • Finish proofreading transcripts and timing the course
  • Design animated slides with visualized data
  • Finish tape recording and screen shot
  • Edit videos
  • Upload content to moodle
  • Finalize business model and logistics
  • Open course to TC community
We will have a course test section on D&R meeting in mid August. Feel free to raise any question before, during and after!
