Trends in Ed 7.6.09

Calli Kosch

July 6, 2009

alltop.com as an Educator's Resource I might be completely behind the 8 ball on this, but alltop.com is my new obsession. It provides a customized feed of news on topics that interest you, anywhere from online gaming to recipe finding to the news in Switzerland. Sorted alphabetically, when one clicks the "E" tab, the page is directed to topics that start with "E", such as "Education". In browsing through the resources and topics, I have noticed an array of news links related to tech ed and news in education. For example, I found this article on interactive lessons for kids under the "EdTech Talk" heading, and there are related headings such as "Teachers Teaching Teachers", "Technology Education Know-How", and yes, even the "TCRecord"...the list really does go on and on. Hopefully this will be a technological addition to teachers who have the ability to spice up their lessons and assignments through online learning.
