Taking Holograms to the Next Level With the "Princess Leia Project"

Melanie.Hering

April 29, 2018


Researchers in the Brigham Young University's electrical and computer engineering department have created a method to produce a 3D image display, or volumetric image, similar to those used in Iron Man, Avatar, and of course, iconically, Star Wars. While a hologram only displays scattered light on 2D surfaces, a volumetric display is much more sophisticated, scattering light throughout a 3D space and allowing you to view the image from all angles.

Functioning as a "3D printer for light," the display created by the BYU team is the first to use optical trapping and color effectively.
