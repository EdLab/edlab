NYC School Plans on Giving Away Condoms For Prom Night

Francisco.Mendoza

May 26, 2013

A public school in Brooklyn has caused controversy by planning on handing out condoms to its students on prom night. After the plan grew the ire of parents and school officials, the school decided to backdown from the plan.
