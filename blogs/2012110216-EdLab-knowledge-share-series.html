EdLab knowledge share series

Sharath Gururaj

November 2, 2012

Hi Everyone! For the last couple of weeks, you might have seen edlab developers disappear for an hour or so in the meeting room! While our code was compiling we started a new series of lightweight knowledge share sessions where one person shares his/her knowledge about a particular tool or technology. We believe its a good way of teaching each other skills that can be tedious to learn on our own. Please have a look at the videos of the screencast here if you find the topics helpful. ANy feedback/suggestions would be appreciated. If you would like to do a session about a particular tool/technology/best practices, please get in touch with pranav ot me. Thanks, Sharath
