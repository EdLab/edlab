Roomer Update & Poll

LauraCostello

May 11, 2012

(Please keep private) Yesterday Roomer was cleared to move into the pilot stage! We'll be thinking about positioning, analysis, and electricity soon, but I wanted to take a quick enclosure poll before we commit to anything. You may remember from my long-ago post on iPad wall mounting that we decided to go with the iPad Wall Bracket. I have one on my desk right now if you want to swing by and get a feel for it. It's a sturdy little thing! We initially ordered it in matte black, but there are options! shown: silver, glossy white, matte black, not shown: glossy black For your consideration, I also put together a little inspiration board with the colors of the mounting walls, the system itself, and peripheral objects: Roomer is shown with a black border here, but the case will cover everything but the screen. I'm looking at you design-y folks! Does matte black hold the key to inoffensive wall mounting or should we make a leap?
