Join Us for a Trip to the DigitalLife Convention on Friday, October 13th

Dennis Stevens

October 11, 2006

On Friday, October 13th, Hui Soo, Skye and I intend to attend the DigitalLife, consumer electronics and entertainment convention at the Javits Convention Center, located on 34th Street and 11th Avenue. We would like to make it a group event, so please let us know if you would like to come along. Tickets are $12.00. For further details on the event, check out the attached document. At the event, we will be conducting video interviews with assorted conference attendees to capture the vibe of the event with an emphasis upon the products or services that have educational value. We intend to target five groups. We would like input from our EdLab colleages regarding our proposed questions, as outlined below. Some of our questions are serious, while others are intended to mix things up a bit. Students - How do your teachers use technology in the classroom? - What do you see here that is fun? How might you want to use this in the classroom? - Are there things that are new that you are interested in exploring further? - What was the coolest/lamest thing you saw here today? - Would you want to work for any of these companies? Why? - How is any of the technology here being used in your school? Teachers - Do you see these technolgies having any value in your teaching? Have you seen any relevant examples of educatioonal innovations here today? - How do you use technology in the classroom? - What do you see here that is fun? How might you want to use this in the classroom? - Are there things that are new that you are interested in exploring further? - What was the coolest/lamest thing you saw here today? - Would you want to work for any of these companies? Why? - How is any of these technology being used in your school? Exhibitors - How do events like this drive product innovation within your company? - How do see your products helping students and teachers? - Are you products just for entertainment purposes? - Does your company produce products that have educational value beyond the devlopment of psychomotor skill? - How much money did you make last quarter? Were you in the red? TC Students & Other Higher Ed. Students ??? Clueless People - What do you think about this event? - Why are you here today? - Why do you look so confused?
