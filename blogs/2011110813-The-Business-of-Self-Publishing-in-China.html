The Business of Self-Publishing in China

LauraCostello

November 8, 2011

China Daily has an interesting article explaining the prevalence of self-publishing and web reading in China. Since the late 90's, original serial fiction has exploded on the web in China, leading to the development of several websites with interesting business models and methods of content selection. I tend to think electronic publishing in the English-speaking world will begin to adhere to a serial, on-demand model and the Guardian article covering this story makes connections to new publishing strategies like Unbound.
