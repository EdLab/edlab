Tweet Remembrance

Meredith Powers

August 23, 2015


When the Library of Congress announced a few years ago that it was going to preserve all of Twitter, most reactions were enthusiastic. Yet several years later, the Twitter Research Access project seems to be stuck in the chrysalis phase, and the Library of Congress doesn't appear to have a practical plan in place at all. It's hard to say what the long term impact will be, but maybe the Library's struggles provide an example that establishing clear goals, engaging collaborative partners, and assembling the right set of skills is at least as important as a grand, visionary idea when it comes to revolutionizing the future of scholarship.
