Developing Empathy in Global Crises

Rebecca Beeson

March 24, 2016


Nonny de la Pe√±a is a journalist and filmmaker who used Project Syria, hosted at the USC School of Cinematic Arts, to immerse participants in one of the most important conflicts of our time. She designed a virtual experience that eliminates the usual barriers that cause passive encounters. With recorded sounds, environmental textures, and realistic visuals taken from the actual source, her virtual reality experience caused provocative and undeniable emotional responses.

How can technology take us even closer to foreign conflicts than ever before? What can we expect to be developed next, propelling human connections beyond emotion and into action? Join the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
