Content On YouTube

Nantwi

September 23, 2008

Earlier this morning, a 20-something male student shot and killed nine of his schoolmates at a university in Kauhajoki in western Finland. He then turned the gun himself. The gunman posted a video of himself on YouTube last week firing a gun. Finnish police interviewed yesterday but didn't revoke his gun license.

Just last November, another college student also shot and killed eight of his schoolmates. He also posted a video on YouTube prior to the attacks pledging to eliminate those who deemed as unfit. It seems apparent that such people are using the web to broadcast their sadistic plans to everyone, therefore, should YouTube and other video sharing sites regulate the kind of video its members produce or refer such videos to the authorities to avoid future massacres?
