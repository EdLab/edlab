Introducing... EdTech Times

Kate Meersschaert

August 6, 2012

Private Please! The details... While researching leads for New Learning Times I happened-up this new site, EdTech Times and thought I would share my takeaways as they relate to both New Learning Times and the content creation in general... My Initial thoughts... -While initially visually appealing (interesting, consistent typeface etc.) all visuals are not original and infographics were created by outside sources... -They have a "Start-up Spotlight" section (see screen shot below)... the companies are interesting, but the "who, what, when, where & why" format is boring and not very in-depth enough to be informative or useful. -"Articles" are really just 1-2 paragraph teasers linking to other site's original content... basically, EdTech Times is just a glorified edtech "content farm" that regurgitates other people's work! -On a positive note, their Twitter feed is active and promotes their "content" efficiently... NLT Take-aways: -Our High 5 leads will be an interesting way to highlight valuable content from around the web... however, it does not make a compelling publication on its own! -Spotlights or Pro-files need more in-depth coverage and genuinely answer "why should I care" & how is this person impacting learning... not just a laundry list of stats from CrunchBase! But, don't let me sway you! Add your thoughts below... is this an effective model to follow?
