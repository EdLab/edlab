D&R: Testing EBSCO EDS!

Meredith Powers

March 3, 2015

*private* Some things to think about when searching (and comparing) EDS and Summon: - If results return a book, can you find out if it's available to check out? - If you find articles, are they recent? Do they appear to be in any particular order? - When searching for a specific title, did you find a lot of irrelevant results before the book/article you wanted? - Did you try to access any full text articles? Were you successful? - Did the system help you catch a typo? If yes, was that useful? - Any other impressions, thoughts, notes, concerns, annoyances, likes, etc.? Please share your thoughts in the comments after the activities! Activity 1: Link to EDS Pick a research goal! - Something that interests you - Can be general or very specific (general vs. advanced search) Notate your likes & dislikes Post individually about your findings on the blog Activity 2: Please use both these links to conduct your search, and compare the experience between the two. EDS Summon (Use the search bar at the top of the page) Group search!
