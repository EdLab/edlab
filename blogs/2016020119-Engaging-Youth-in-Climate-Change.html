Engaging Youth in Climate Change

dinosossi2

February 1, 2016


Climate change is such a complicated issue that it can be difficult to know where to begin. Multiple stakeholders separated by national borders and ideological divides can lead to inaction, stagnation, and negative feelings.

Many of today's young people are passionate about climate change and preserving the planet where they live. Unfortunately, they easily lose enthusiasm when they don't have an effective platform to share their concerns. As a result, hope can devolve into cynicism, and a chance for significant change is lost.

In this Vialogue, the United Nations invites youth to participate in campaigns for meaningful change, including a Global Youth Video Competition. What do you think about the climate change debate? Do you engage in any daily routines that minimize your carbon footprint. Tell us about your thoughts on the future of the climate change movement in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
