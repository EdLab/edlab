Classify the MOOC contents by majors/specializations instead of subjects?

Luming Nie

July 14, 2014

When opening the catalogs on some resourceful MOOCs(the ones with many courses like Coursera and Edx), we can see a variety of ways to classify and search courses. Taking Coursera as an example: we can filter courses by “Languages”, “Categories”, “Start date” and “Certification". Also, there is a tap for us named “Specializations”, by which we can find some related and organized courses in a specific field, i.e. Data Science. In this situation, some users might feel hard to target what courses they SHOULD be enrolled as a student in a specified field. This happens because there exist many courses with barriers like “prerequisites".
Two examples on Coursera of the classification based on specializations and other attributes:
My question comes - "With so many organizable resources, why not build another classifying feature that can help users sort all the desired courses based on “major” and “the path from newbee to master” ?" Image this situation - I am a none-CS major student who would like to learn computer science starting from basics. Then I can simply click a button on the page named “To be a computer scientist.” After the clicking, I will be showed what I should learn or read start from level-0 and grow up step by step to be a “graduate" OR even a tree-like structure showing me all the skills that I should handle and all the courses I should take, just like what we have in or real colleges. Computer Science, again, as an example - the classes schedule start from fundamental courses like “Discrete Math”, “Algorithms” to higher level “Computer Architecture”, “Computer Networking”, and finally “Artificial Intelligence”, “Machine Learning”. All these courses are available on Coursera. In off-line college, we take carefully suggested courses based on our goals in our majors. I guess, with this feature, MOOCs might becomes more similar to real “online universities”. Students will feel they are truly one of the student in this online college with their classes schedule from Junior to Senior.
