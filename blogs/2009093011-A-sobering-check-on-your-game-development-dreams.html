A sobering check on your game development dreams

Gus Andrews

September 30, 2009

A professional game designer explains to you why your brilliant game idea sucks. While this is more aimed at people outside of established tech development studios, it gives just a flavor of how the game design process works within major studios. And it should sober up those of us who, like I have, dreamed up some brilliant game they think will be the salvation of education. (That said, I'm sure now that I've said this nobody will flock to my aid to help develop OMG Ponies, the pony-themed game which will teach tween girls biology and coding.)
