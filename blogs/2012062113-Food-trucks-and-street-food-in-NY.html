Food trucks and street food in NY

Rebecca Hyams

June 21, 2012

[Keep Private] There's been lots of talk recently in the EdLab about food trucks and carts both up by TC and just in general in the city. For a while now, I've been following New York Street Food, a blog with news on food trucks and other street food choices in the NYC area, plus reviews of the food itself. The guy who runs New York Street Food also has a curated Twitter feed called Mobile Munchies which has up-to-date info on where you can find your favorite vendor each day. By the way, Wafels and Dinges is one of the few trucks that comes up near TC over the summer (during the school year I know trucks like Korilla BBQ come up here). They tend to have a truck on Broadway by 115/114 on Mondays. They're one of my favorite trucks to stop by (surprise! I have a sweet tooth!) and they serve authentic Belgian Wafels (the owner is Belgian) with a variety of toppings (the "dinges").
