First Day in California

Nantwi

July 11, 2011

After an early (and I mean) early flight from Newark to San Francisco, we took our rental car over the Golden Gate Bridge to Sausalito for breakfast. A trip to downtown San Francisco's Fisherman Wharf was next on the docket, where I had my first clam chowder inside a bread and then Ankit, Megha and I had out first In-N-Out Burger. We couldn't leave the bay area without a trip to Gary's dear old Stanford. A 45 minute drive to our hotel in San Jose ended a fun filled first day in California.
