Who's Who at TC? You Tell Me!

Molly Riordan

July 6, 2009

In a truly collaborative effort, members of the Research, Video, and Design teams are working on several TC 101 projects which will become a multi-platform experience of TC history. As part of our research, we would like to interview influential people at TC today--professors, researchers, administrators, even students!--to find out what TC historical figures or philosophies have inspired them and their work. These interviews will inform our research, help us prioritize our content, and may even provide content for an audio tour. We are asking EdLabbers who are also students at TC to help us identify these important people. Do you have a favorite professor or influential advisor who might have a good story to tell? Let us know! Post to the blog or give one of us the info: Calli, Rebekah, Jo, Skye, Stephanie, Laurel, Patrick and I thank you for your help.
