The Trouble With Teacher Training Evaluations

Brian.Sweeting

April 30, 2014


As with education secretaries before him, Arne Duncan is struggling with the paradox of needing national education standards while recognizing that they may not be reliable.

By calling for teacher evaluations that look more like those used to assess standardized medical programs, the Secretary is being criticized for relying on student test scores, which as every teacher well knows, are not necessarily accurate assessments of teaching or learning.
