"Whatever it Takes" - A documentary about a new public school in the Bronx

Erin Murphy

July 30, 2009

I just came across this new documentary - it will be premiering on PBS's Independent Lens series in the fall. The filmmaker, Christopher Wong, filmed during the first year that the Bronx Center for Science and Mathematics was open. According to the website "the personal stories of this school's students and staff call to mind larger themes of school reform and the need for educators, parents, and policymakers to prioritize the transformation of the public school system so that all children can receive a quality education." As a filmmaker I have been told by the Department of Education that they do not allow any filming to happen during school hours. I wonder how Wong was able to get permission to make this film?
