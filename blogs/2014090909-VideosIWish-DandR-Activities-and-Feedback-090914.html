#VideosIWish D&R Activities & Feedback: 09.09.14

Kate Meersschaert

September 9, 2014

Activity #1 (report individually):

  • Pick a vialogue from the #VideosIWish collection that you have not watched
  • Watch it
    • Grade it individually based on:
  • How useful would this be to a learner in the class it is meant to support?
  • Does it have all the required elements (Relevant #'s, specific course this would support, accurate, helpful description, Q&A & at least 3 questions)?
  • Would you assign this as an educator?

Activity #2 (report as Group):

  • How can we best reach-out to a community of practice with this content?
  • How do we further engage the communities we are already reaching-out to?
  • What EdLab capabilities and resources should we utilize to do this?

Feedback: Please add feedback regarding this project thus far, thoughts for the future and suggestions for extensions and additions to current and future EdLab work, tools and programming.
