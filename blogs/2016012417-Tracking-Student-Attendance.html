Tracking Student Attendance

AbdulMalik

January 24, 2016

Educators constantly try to come up with new strategies to improve the learning experience for their students. However, there are several reasons that make it hard to implement strategies and policies developed by teachers. Absenteeism and lateness tend to be major hinderances to effective teaching and learning. Eva Convington is a teacher in Harlem who shares her experience with students not showing up to her living environment class or showing up late. She states that lateness in her class is one of the reasons why most students will end up flunking.

Perhaps a solution to this teacher's and every other teacher's problem of attendance will be the Kinvolved app. This app helps teachers track student attendance with just a swipe. Teachers are able to send notifications to parents and also display the trends in lateness for each student in the class. Even though some parents find it "annoying" to receive push notifications on a regular basis, it has been proven that the app has helped fix the attendance issue in schools that have utilized it.
