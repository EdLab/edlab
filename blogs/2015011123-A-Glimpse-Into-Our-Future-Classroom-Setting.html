A Glimpse Into Our Future Classroom Setting

Khalil Abubakar

January 11, 2015

A recent article, describes how a young futurist and innovator is using creativity and STEM knowledge to improve learning efficiency. Sahil Doshi has created a device that converts carbon dioxide into electricity. Doshi also want to create caps that read our brain waves for better search results, and a glass desk to solve or answer simple problems rather than working it on a board. Doshi's ambition might be described as science fiction for now but this might be the reality of our future classroom setting.
