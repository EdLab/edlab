A Spellcheck Pen

Khalil Abubakar

July 22, 2013

Never did I imagine a simple pen will have the ability to correct spelling mistakes.This article explains how a German genius developed the pen when his child was having difficulty doing his homework. The pen, Lernstift, was created by Daniel Kaesmacher. Its functions are the calligraphy mode, which helps you correct individual letters, and the orthography mode, which vibrates when a word is misspelled. The pen is powered by an AAA battery including a non optical motion sensor and a vibrator. The pen consists of many sensors including a gyroscope (for measuring orientation), accelerometer (for calculating propulsion) and magnetometer (a device that measures the strength and direction of magnetic fields) -- all to calculate the pen's 3-D movements. Lernstift has two options that can be used on paper and also built-in Wifi, which allows scribblers to connect to their smartphones or computers within the same network.
