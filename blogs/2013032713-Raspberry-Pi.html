Raspberry Pi

pramnab1

March 27, 2013

So last year in december I got my much awaited Raspberry Pi Model B which shipped after 4 long months of being awaiting. TO people who don't know what raspberry pi is it's a miniature circuit board which is a functional computer but doesnt have the frills of todays desktop computers like enclosure case or even the power cord and the plus point is it's just under $45 for the higher model. My Plan is to get it to work on my home TV and atleast make it functional in terms of a computer connected with TV and potentially later on some fun projects on it. With this blog post I am hoping that I will start my series of blog post on Raspberry PI and its nuances as i take my journey to explore computers from Apple I days when it was just a circuit board. So stay tuned.
