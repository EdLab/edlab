Staff Picks of the Month

Dina Elsibay

March 17, 2016

You may have noticed the “Staff Picks of the Month” section on the second floor of the library. Each month, one or more library staff members select 20-25 books from our collection to display in the collaboration space. For the month of March, I put together the collection based on the theme “Growth”. Each of these books has changed my perspective is some way. These books range in topic from food to synchronicity to nonviolent communication to joy to love. If a book strikes your fancy, please feel free to check it out at the first floor Services Desk! If you have any questions or comments, you may reply to this post or visit us at the Services Desk.
