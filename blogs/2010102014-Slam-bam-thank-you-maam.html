Slam bam thank you ma'am

jvazquez

October 20, 2010

Have you heard of Google demo slams? It's a new marketing endeavor from the company's creative department. I originally thought that this meant Google was opening up its products for brutal reviews and critiques but... they meant "slam" in the poetic sense. Admittedly, I was somewhat disappointed. Not that i have anything terrible to say about any of their products, I just liked the idea of a company being so unabashedly open. But, alas. Still, Google demo slams is an interesting project. Its goal is to collect user-generated videos that explain how to use a certain Google product (with the end goal, I imagine, being to make these products appealing and accessible to even more audiences). These videos are pitted against each other in a battle royale where the winner moves on to compete with the winner of the next week's competition. It looks like slams only happen once a week. The ones I saw were fun and entertaining enough but I might very well forget about this whimsical website in that amount of time. The lifespan of internet gimmicks is fleeting: you gotta fly or die!
