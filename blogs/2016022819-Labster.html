"Labster partners with top universities and their leading faculty to create and develop innovative, scientifically advanced laboratories. Among these contributors are Stanford University, The Technical University of Denmark, Novo Nordisk, Hong Kong University, and many others in academia and professional industries." (About Labster )

Breathing Life into the Life Sciences

Labster is an international company that is driven by the belief that all students should be able to have unfettered access to life-science studies. To achieve this goal, Labster creates virtual laboratory spaces to support conventional science lab instruction with multimedia.

Building Real-World Scenarios to Improve Learning

Labster focuses on the real world. It integrates 3D animations that visualize life sciences for the purposes of increasing student learning. Labster achieves this by integrating animation, hands-on virtual practice, quizzes, and theory to improve student engagement.
