Just Food

EdLab Studios

January 11, 2014


Just Food - a NYC group dedicated to food justice, including sustainability, accessibility and knowledge of healthy foods for all people, and economic justice for farmers and consumers alike - trains volunteers to be "community chefs" who then go out and lead classes on buying, cooking, storing, and preserving healthy food. Just Food sees the need not just for the availability of healthy, local, sustainably produced food, but for consumer knowledge of just what to do with all this bok choy and winter squash they're supposed to be growing or buying. So, through Community Food Education, community chefs make these foods accessible to a wide range of people, making sustainability, health, and economy neither mutually exclusive nor intimidating, but something that just makes sense for everyone.
