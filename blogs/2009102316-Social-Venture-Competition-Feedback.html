Social Venture Competition Feedback

Nantwi

October 23, 2009

Jo, Adrienne and I have been working on a social venture competition to be headlined by the EdLab. Check out Jo's earlier blog with the proposal. Initially, we looked at business competitions from various educational institutions to see how our competition can offer something different and exciting. Through our analysis of the other competitions, we have drawn up a preliminary set of eligibility requirements for teams, submission requirements, and a schedule of events in the attached documents. We are hoping to solicit feedback from the Lab on a number of questions we harbor about the competition. So read the questions and documents and give us your feeback. Thanks. 1) How do you feel about the timeline of events? Is it too short or too long? 2) Should the website for the competition be a part of TC or EdLab or should it a completely separate site? 3) What should we base our assessment on when it comes to judging? 4) Is anyone interested in serving as a coach or know anyone who will be a good contact for a judge or mentor?
