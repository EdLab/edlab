II. Site Goals and Critical Tasks

Write down everything you think of, don’t worry about prioritizing.


  • Site goals: What our organization wants our library website to accomplish
  • Critical tasks: What users want to be able to do on our library website

IV. Design Challenges

As a group, create a scenario that falls within the scope of your category. The scenario doesn’t have to cover all of the areas within your category. Make your scenario specific and plausible. The solution you come up with, however, can be as wild as you want. Bonus points for defining the killer feature of your experience.


  1. Is database in your pocket? = Finding and using articles
  2. Dewey, Findem, and Howe = Finding and using books
  3. A cite for sore eyes = Conducting research
  4. Forever a-loan = Accessing and borrowing materials
  5. Prints Charming = Using tech and equipment
  6. Of course guac is extra = Getting course support
  7. Let me speak to your manager = Getting more help
  8. (Who you gonna call?) Dust busters! = Accessing and browsing archived material
