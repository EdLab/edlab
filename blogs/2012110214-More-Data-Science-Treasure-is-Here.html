More Data Science Treasure is Here!

Sharon Hsiao

November 2, 2012

I found an interesting course from Columbia University offered by Dr. Rachel Schutt in their Statistics Department. COLUMBIA UNIVERSITY, INTRODUCTION TO DATA SCIENCE, STATISTICS W4242, FALL 2012 There are a lot of course collective intelligence blogged on the course website. For example, What is data science?, 10 important data science ideas, Data Art != Data Visualization etc. Might be worth visiting our New Learning Times (NLT) research digest on Visualization Nation piece. The course and its materials are modern, interesting, and easy to read. Additionally, it is updated in the intersections of statistics, computer science, data visualization and the social sciences.
