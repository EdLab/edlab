The Role of Storytelling in Product Marketing

Kate Meersschaert

October 29, 2012

This TechCrunch article explores the role of "storytelling" in product marketing. I thought it could be especially interesting as we both launch and describe the value proposition of New Learning Times, Survey Sidekick and ResearchBroker! The TechCrunch article asks: -Are you telling a story through your marketing and launch? -If so, is the story compelling? -What stories are users telling you & others about your product via social media & feedback? What do you think of the role of narrative in historically successful product launches? Would you argue that Apple has been the master of this with the launch of their seminal products from the MAC to iPod? What about the recent launch of the Microsoft Surface and Windows 8?
