iStopMotion

Ting Yuan

September 3, 2014


Stop motion is an animation technique that brings single pictures of objects together as a continuous sequence. iStopMotion, developed by BoinxSoftware, is an app available on both iPad and Mac for creating stop motion animations. As the winner of Macworld Best of Show 2012 and 2013, iStopMotion makes the creation of animated movies accessible to all. The rich video capture and organizing functions in iStopMotion make it a portable studio to create animations on the go.

Pros:

iStopMotion surpasses most of its counterparts by providing more than an intuitive interface. Users enjoy multiple features including a time lapse feature to speed up the normal time of a clip, "onion skinning" to organize an animation based on superimposed frames, and flashing the current image and previously recorded images through the preview function of "blinking." In addition, iStopMotion Remote Camera, a free companion app, can be installed in other iOS Devices and allows users to access the photos taken by other devices. The app further features a voice activation function to trigger image capture using voice commands. This function lets users manipulate movie objects and work on camera capture at the same time.

Cons:

iStopMotion offers two types of access. While the iPad app is offered at a cost of $9.99, the Mac version, with advanced animation software, costs $49.99. Both versions lack built-in video editing capabilities aside from the options of deleting or duplicating frames. Users cannot reorder frames or create separate scenes to build a more complex movie. Some specialized video editing apps such as iMovie and Final Cut are needed to perform a more thorough edit for the exported animations.

Our Takeaway:

The time lapse feature and its ability to connect to another iOS deviceâ€™s camera over the internet make iStopMotion notably versatile. While the iPad version is plenty for novice stop motion animators, the Mac version is a must-have for producers with greater ambitions. Users at different age groups will enjoy designing their very first animated stories with the app. It could also be used as a teaching and learning tool across many subjects in both school and home settings.

The Bottom Line:

iStopMotion lets adults and children enjoy the magic of animation.
