Video Games and It's Skills

Youssef Ballo

June 12, 2015

Video games are a popular sensation among children around the world. The advancement of technology means todayâ€™s video games are more sophisticated and real. Many teachers are now using video games to educate students. They use it to supplement or enhance the curriculum. When a student plays video games, it gives the brain a real workout. In many video games, the skills required to win a game has to do with mental and high level thinking. Video games help improve these skills:

    Following Instructions
  • Problem Solving and Logic
  • Hand-eye coordination
  • Planning, Resources management and logistics
  • Accuracy, Memory, Mapping
Are you a video gamer? If so, have you seen an improvement in these skills as a result?
