Teachers Salaries

Francisco.Mendoza

June 12, 2013

One of the things mentioned during today's seminar was that in Florida, part of a teacher's salary is based on their student's performance, meaning some get paid more than others. In my opinion this is fine, it will motivate more teachers to make sure that they are doing their job. Also, I feel that more teachers will show that they care by making sure they communicate with students who are doing poorly in class. In the high school I attended, I felt that teachers could have done more to make sure that the students were keeping up with their school work. On the other hand, it might not be as fair for the teachers who put in all their effort to make sure that their students are doing everything they should, but some students just don't keep up.
