New Learning Times is in the U.S. DoE's "Ed Tech Developer's Guide"

zhou

May 4, 2016

Yesterday I ran into U.S. Department of Education's Ed Tech Developer's Guide published in April 2015 and was excited to find New Learning Times was mentioned as a useful resource for EdTech developers nationwide!

New Learning Times is produced at the Ed Lab at the Teachers College at Columbia University and provides daily coverage of learning opportunites to transformation education.
(they misspelled our name and misspelled "opportunities"). That was part of the reason why I asked the question in today's seminar: what we can do to make our great works known to more people? I agree with Brian that we should reach to the right audience. So I would rephrase my question into: * Who are the proper audiences for TCR, NLT, Vialogues, Rhizr, etc.? * What we can do to promote our works to the proper audiences, in more effective ways?
