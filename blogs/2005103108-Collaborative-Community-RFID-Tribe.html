Collaborative Community: RFID Tribe

Frank Webster

October 31, 2005

I have been invited to join a collaborative community called "RFID Tribe: The World's RFID Collaboration Forum" because I attended an institute recently. I note the following addendum to the membership application: How to Gain Privileges and Status as a RFID Warrior in RFID Tribe RFID Warriors find and connect with others who have an interest in RFID, communicate through articles and email and collaborate on ideas, challenges and business ventures. RFID Tribe membership is free, but this is not a group of freeloaders. This is a group of active, productive industry experts. Members gain privileges and become RFID Warriors by giving to the Tribe... - Authoring articles as a subject matter expert - Hosting and speaking at RFID Tribe events - Helping Tribe members with technical and business challenges Those that give to the Tribe are granted status as RFID Warriors. Warriors are granted access to contact information for connecting with Tribe members and Warriors. RFID Warrior status is granted through an electronic invite. RFID Tribe members become RFID Warriors by giving to the Tribe. RFID Warriors become RFID Chiefs by giving to the Tribe.
