A Way To Decrease Poverty

idrissa10

June 11, 2015

The Obama Administration is trying to decrease the 60 million primary school age children who are not enrolled in school around the world. Due to poverty, social, and cultural issues some children do not have the opportunity to enroll in school. In many cultures, females are expected to stay home and perform house chores while men are encourage to find jobs at an early age to help support their families. The U.S congress wants to invest 125 million dollars in the Global Partnership for Education and to insure an increase in primary school enrollment. This could prove beneficial for both the U.S and countries with low rate of enrollment. It is stated that if a child receives an education, the chances of them becoming or staying poor is very slim. With this in mind, educated children could bring stability to their nations while contributing to the global economy. This could prove to be a worthy investment because one does not know who could be the next Albert Einstein.
