Flipboard - Update December

Toland.Lawrence

December 12, 2018

After our interesting discussion about outreach, I thought it would be a good idea to check in on one of our projects already in progress.


Thanks again for all who have been flipping NLT content. We've increased our follower count from a few people to 65 and seen promising changes. 


To measure progress, there are three areas we want to focus on:


  1. Number of View of NLT content
  2. Referrals to NLT from Flipboard
  3. Number of conversions


Here are our initial findings.


Views of NLT Content on Flipboard

Flipboard only provides analytics for the past 30 days. Since November 13th, NLT content received an average of 2.8 views a day. 




Referrals to NLT from Flipboard

Flipboard is NLT's 4th largest source of traffic. 


In the month of November, we had 149 users come into New Learning times from Flipboard and on track for similar numbers this month.


This is actually down from October when we had 184 users referred.


This is due to the fact that one article in October, Hire Your Own Personal Coding Coach, received a significant amount of flips and traffic. Further investigation is needed to figure out why this article caught on and others did not. 


Number of Conversions

Because all signups for NLT occur on myedlab.tc.columbia.edu, we are unable to track who signs up from Flipboard. 


We can see the number of people that have come from newlearningtimes.com to myedlabtc.columbia.edu, but we don't know their original source.


It would be great if we could track that in the future. We think if we could have a tracking URL for people who come from Flipboard that might solve the problem.


If anyone has any ideas about how we could track signups based on referral source, we'd love to hear them!
