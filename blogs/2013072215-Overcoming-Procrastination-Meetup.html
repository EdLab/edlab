Overcoming Procrastination Meetup

Brian.Sweeting

July 22, 2013

*PRIVATE* I don't know about you, but there are too many tasks in my life for which I find a million reasons to put off. POW! Wednesday, July 24, 2013 8PM The Alley 500 7th Ave, 17th Floor, New York, NY Also, The Alley is a work-space worth visiting if you haven't been before. Let me know if you want to come with.
