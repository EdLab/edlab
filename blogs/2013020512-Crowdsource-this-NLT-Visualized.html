*Keep private please* Hello EdLab folk, From now on, the NLT VIsualized Team will begin to post some sketches... and we ask you to please provide your thoughts on how to make these cartoons better... Will diner-style booths, lit with fluorescent bulbs and filled with the aroma of cheeseburgers become the next best learning space?
