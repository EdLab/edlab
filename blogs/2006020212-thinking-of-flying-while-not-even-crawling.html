thinking of flying while not even crawling

Hugo Ortega Lopez

February 2, 2006

Part of being succesfull is noticing when things are not functioning anymore, if they ever did. Fancy gadgets become obsolete when they were design for specific environment and put into another physical context. I am referening to the seminars projector. Allthough no doubt, any power point presentation seem more relevant using the 3M Wall Display, when it comes to imaging.... a $50 tv monitor would do a better job. The inability to run a PP with video...well not my area. That is why i tend to generaty one video file, either quictime or DVD when possible and just use the pause key to control the presentation. Rarelty have I seen a smooth ride during a PP/internet/presentation. Also, the group is growing, so we need to figure out new physical arrangements. Does the failure to provide basic technological support to a presenter affects the "image" of edlab as it attempts to incorporate tech into ed? Although we may be working under the perception that and edlab seminar is an edlab seminar. is not. Each one has its own characteristics. Lets think about the heterogeneity of the seminar. On a good note, the "nutrition system" has evolve to a coherent level. Still missing coffee. Lets master our crawling before fulfilling the dream of flying.
