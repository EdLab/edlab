Virtual spaces in portable digital domes for Learning in science and Cultural Heritage

Ankit Ranka

April 12, 2011

This session is about virtual learning space in domes. It costs from $8,000 to $150,000 virtual forest - allows kids to visit the forest inside the dome and take measurements. Flight simulators - thats where virtual reality began We went inside a virtual dome for 20 minutes where presenters explained about the construction of the dome, how they made movies and graphics to display inside the dome and they also acted one of the ritual of egyptians inside the dome.
