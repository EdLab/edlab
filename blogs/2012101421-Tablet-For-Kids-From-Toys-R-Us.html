Tablet For Kids From Toys R Us

Francisco.Mendoza

October 14, 2012

The improvement and advance of technology in recent times has largely left out one key demographic: children. Smartphones, tablets, and laptops, among others have all attracted the interest of teens and adults but yet there has been little or no new technology that has being made specifically with children as the target audience. Though one can make an argument that all existing technology can be adapted to children, Toys R'Us is taking the opposite view and will soon be releasing a tablet specifically created for children. The tablet will allow children to browse the Internet and will have 50 free applications with more accessibility to 1,000 more through Google. The tablet is also is not as expensive as the iPad and others and it affords parents the opportunity to control and monitor how their children are using it.
