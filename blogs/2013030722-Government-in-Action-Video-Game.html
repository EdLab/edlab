Government in Action Video Game

Daniel Vergara

March 7, 2013

I recently came across this article which talks about an interactive game created to make learning about Congress more fun. The game was made for college freshmen taking American government courses. The game was true because the course was so complex that they needed to go through every single chapter in a textbook, and many times it's difficult to keep students' interest that way. The way it is played is amazing as you actually get elected to the House of Representatives and charged with making all the hard decisions in order to win people over and get re-elected again in two years. Compared to reading a textbook everyday, I feel that this game would help students learn way more about Congress than just reading a book. Actually having students play a game would not only make them learn more about it but also would help them become more interested in learning about that subject. I posted a video on Vialogues about this and hope that you can check it out.
