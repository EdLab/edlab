Musique a la Mode

Jenny Taira

February 22, 2008

Just in case any of you are into classical music and are looking for something to do this weekend, I wanted to send a quick invitation to a concert this Sunday at 7:30 PM at the St.Mark's Church-in-Bowery, 131 E. 10th Street (@ 2nd Ave). I've co-founded a chamber music ensemble and concert series in the east village, and this is our 2nd concert of the season, entitled, "Once Upon a Time: Stories and Poetry told through Music". Works include a NYC premiere by composer Adam Silverman, as well as a really cool dramatic reading and performance of Stravinky's "A Soldier's Tale".
