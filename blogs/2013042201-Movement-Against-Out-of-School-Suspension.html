Movement Against Out -of- School Suspension

Khalil Abubakar

April 22, 2013

According to this article, statistics show that 3.3 million students were suspended out of school during the 2009-10 school year and the National School Boards Association (NSBA) has labeled the use of out-of-school suspension as a crisis. The NSBA has urged school board members to start thinking about ways to reduce and eventually eliminate the practice of out of school suspensions. The guide, "Addressing the Out-of-School Suspension Crisis: A Policy Guide for School Board Members” provides steps school board members can take and questions they should ask about their districts' disciplinary code.
