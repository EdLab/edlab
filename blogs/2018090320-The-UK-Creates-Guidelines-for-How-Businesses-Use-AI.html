The UK Creates Guidelines for How Businesses Use AI

saraehardman

September 3, 2018


The UK became the first country to create guidelines for how humans should develop and use AI. The country’s government laid out a set of five principles companies should follow while creating new uses for AI. The ultimate goal is to steer businesses into considering how AI can be used for good rather than just efficiency or ease.

Because AI is so new, there is no precedent for how to use it, no universal code of ethics that constrains its limits. While most AI applications certainly aim to make life easier, there is a growing push to consider other consequences of its use as well. Instead of just asking can we do this, we also need to ask should we do this. This outlook will help us make sense of how AI will change not only specific tasks but society as a whole. The UK code is one national push in this direction, and it will be interesting to see how other countries follow.
