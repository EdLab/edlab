First iPad Reviews Out

Ting Yuan

April 1, 2010

Check out the first iPad reviews by David Pogue and Walt Mossberg.

Pogue says it's no laptop replacement, but as a giant iPod touch, it's a huge success. Mossberg says he enjoys doing most tasks on it much more than on his laptop, and considers it 80% a laptop replacement. Pogue hates the keyboard, Mossberg likes it.
