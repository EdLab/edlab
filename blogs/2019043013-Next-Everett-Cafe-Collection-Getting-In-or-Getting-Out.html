Next Everett Cafe Collection: Getting In or Getting Out

Jennifer

April 30, 2019

Suggestions and book titles welcome, and thank you for reading this proposal!



Our next Everett Cafe book display (June-July) is on college admissions, framing the context of Operation Varsity Blues, the latest scandal stemming from corporate greed and the unfortunate pressure experienced by many to get into the "best" schools, if not "good" ones — at whatever the cost. Suffice to say, higher education, including test prep, has turned into Big Business. Cheating on tests, lying on applications, and bribing officials unjustly serves many people, but most of all the students themselves.


Does where you attend college determine your success in life? Can we even get out of this societal madness? Our intent is to restore good common sense and purpose in the college admissions process, while revealing the intrinsic promise of higher education.
