Finals Are Here, but iPads Are Gone?

Cinthia.Fabian

April 25, 2018

1524671942

A reimagined Library experience has been brewing on the fifth floor of Russell Hall — and it’s just about ready to make its formal debut! But before we roll out any changes, we here at the Library want to ensure that the transition process go as smoothly as possible. In the coming weeks, you can look forward to a simplified Library website, a more intuitive room booking system, and a fresh mobile addition to our collection of tools and resources. Before we get there, though, we’re putting up the metaphorical “Under Construction” sign as we make the necessary updates to our online and room booking systems. [embed]https://giphy.com/gifs/giatec-dogs-construction-3ov9jRPMChw9ZzVlUk[/embed] Here’s what you can expect in the coming weeks:

  • We’re going old school. For the remainder of the spring semester, your room reservations will be posted via paper rather than iPads.
  • Mark your calendars: You’ll be able to reserve rooms up until May 9th. After that, we'll be shutting down our current room booking system to make way for the new.
  • Don’t worry about finding a seat in the Library come finals. As of May 10th, all group rooms will become open study space. Come one, come all!
Thank you for your patience as we implement these changes. Stay tuned for more updates on the soon-to-be improved and expanded Library ecosystem and, as always, please feel free to reach out to us for a helping hand during the tumultuous final weeks of the semester. Best of luck during finals!
