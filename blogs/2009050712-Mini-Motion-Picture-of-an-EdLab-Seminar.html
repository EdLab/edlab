Mini-Motion-Picture of an EdLab Seminar

Arianna Choi

May 7, 2009

Following an experiment with Animoto earlier this week, I decided to try what it would be like to document a real EdLab seminar. The 30-sec animated photo/(text) video turned out to be more like a promotional video than a digital documentation of participant observation. For example, I uploaded EdLab logo hoping that the Animoto's patent-pending Cinematic Artificial Intelligence technology would place it with my text, EdLab Seminar, but it didn't. Instead, the system put it at the very end, which is not so bad, but I can see a potential draw back for storytellers because users do not have full control over how photographs and texts will be synthesized. It is exciting, anyway, to empower non-professionals to produce an animated motion video. Hope you enjoy.
