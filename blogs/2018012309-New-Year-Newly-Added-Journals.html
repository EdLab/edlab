New Year, Newly Added Journals

Julie.Schaeffer42250

January 23, 2018

New Year means new additions to our journal subscriptions! These journals are accessible now - Literacy Research and Instruction, Research in Dance Education, Journal of Multilingual & Multicultural Development, and Sport, Education & Society. If you are looking for a specific journal and what years our subscriptions covers, just visit the Online Resources page and search for the title. Literacy Research and Instruction This journal is the publishing arm of the Association of Literacy Educators & Researchers with a focus on articles dealing with research and instruction in reading education and allied literacy fields.   Research in Dance Education This journal covers a diverse array of topics from all stages of dance education, from pre-school and up, to discussion of topics like special needs, researching dance, creativity, and more.   Journal of Multilingual and Multicultural Development This is a cross-disciplinary journal that covers a range of topics on sociology and social psychology of language, language and cultural politics, policy, planning and practice.   Sport, Education and Society This journal covers the intersection between pedagogy, physical education, and society. Topics include the pedagogy of play, formal and informal education, equity and identity, and sports in the public sphere.
