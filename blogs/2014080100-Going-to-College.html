Going to College

rendy

August 1, 2014

This week has been one of the longest week for the past two weeks. I had to decide which college to attend out of the Bloomfield and New York Institution of Technology (NYIT). Everything seemed set for me to join NYIT but after speaking with the financial aid and finding out there had made a mistake in my financial aid package overturned the situation. Luckily, I received a better offer from Bloomfield College. After speaking with admissions at Bloomfield, I had found out that the last day for me to take a placement test was on Wednesday. Even if I didn't have enough time to review, I did well, especially in the math section. I wanted thank Hui Soo and George for giving me the opportunity to work at EdLab and thank all EdLab members for accepting me as one of them. Even if my major is still undecided, I have an idea on what I want to study.
