High School Tech Entrepreneurs

Rebecca Hyams

July 3, 2012

There was an article this morning in the New York Times about a student-run club in Palo Alto, California that centered around the creation and development of primarily tech entrepreneurship. They invite guest speakers who have launched their own start-ups (sound familiar?) and work together to flesh out ideas. Seeing as how today we're discussing STEM education in the lab, do you think this has a place? Not only is this technology/engineering, but business as well. Also, do you think this type of club could do what it does if it wasn't based in a well-to-do neighborhood located in the heart of Silicon Valley?
