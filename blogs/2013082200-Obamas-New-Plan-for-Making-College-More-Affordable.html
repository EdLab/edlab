Obama's New Plan for Making College More Affordable

Christopher Zamora

August 22, 2013

Check out this article of President Obama's plans to make college more affordable. Today, the average tuition at four year colleges has tripled over the past three decades as on average, students are left with $26,000 in debt upon graduation. Although President Obama hasn't quite flushed out his proposals, the focus will be on middle class families. I wonder what proposals he will make and if they will get approved. I find it surprising that the tuition at four year colleges has tripled and thus leaving many students in debt. What are your thoughts on President Obama's plans to make colleges more affordable? Is he correct in just focusing on the middle class? Which proposals make sense to make college more affordable?
