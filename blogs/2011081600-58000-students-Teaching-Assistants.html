58,000 students = ? Teaching Assistants

HSC

August 16, 2011

A Stanford University Artificial Intelligence course being offered by Peter Norvig and Sebastian Thrun has resulted in 58,000 students registering worldwide. The courses are only available on a non-credit basis but students will be evaluated and receive a certificate. Some interesting features of the course: - It will include both streaming Internet video and interactive technologies for quizzes and grading. - It will use a system running on the Amazon cloud to grade students. - It will use Google moderator service instead of office hours. - They will attempt to personalize exams. Learn more here. This course, which is one of three experimental courses being offered at Stanford, is a potential game changer in the education domain. Is anyone in the EdLab interested in registering for the course so that we can learn more about the future of online learning?
