YouTube as an Educational Tool

Youssef Ballo

November 13, 2015

Video has become a very powerful tool for educators. More and more schools are integrating free video platforms into their classrooms to enhance student learning. Recognizing the increasing impact of video-on-demand in education, YouTube is aggregating their educational content. Education videos are one of the most viewed categories on YouTube. Sal Khan, one of the most influential people in education, rose to fame after he shot a video to tutor his young cousin in math. His technique landed millions in grants from the Gates Foundation and Google. YouTube's educational channels are successful because the videos combine an element of entertainment that students may not receive in classrooms. According to a former teacher, “if you’re not getting people engaged, you’re not going to reach a big audience.” YouTube has now gathered educational channels into YouTube EDU. Students can learn from YouTube EDU on their own time.
