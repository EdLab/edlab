Textagram?!

Kate Meersschaert

April 27, 2012

Instagram, newly flush with cash, just launched a new Twitter-based service... just kidding! It was actually programmer Josh Helfferich (@Digeratii)! Helfferich, a Pittsburgh native and recent CA transplant who created this "lightweight" text-only version of Instagram aptly called, "Text Only Instagram." Like the original incarnation, this version is as simple and straightforward as its name, providing text-based descriptions of Instagram photos AND like its originator, was just (as of about an hour ago) bought by Facebook. Will you try this new "Insta"-incarnation? What are the benefits of a text-based photo sharing app? Could visually impaired users enjoy descriptions of these photos through screen readers? What additional uses can you think of?
