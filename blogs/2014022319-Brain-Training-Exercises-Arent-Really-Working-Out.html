Brain Training Exercises Aren't Really Working Out

Brian.Sweeting

February 23, 2014


Researchers are discovering that popular brain training apps may not actually have any benefits for improving cognition, intelligence, memory, or attention. Frequent users of apps like Lumosity may get better at playing games, but not at warding off the detrimental mental effects of aging.
