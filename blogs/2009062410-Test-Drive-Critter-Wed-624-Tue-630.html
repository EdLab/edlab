Test Drive Critter (Wed 6/24 — Tue 6/30)

Arianna Choi

June 24, 2009

Team Critter would like to kindly ask everyone at EdLab to test drive our prototype. It will take about 25 minutes if you're visiting Critter for the first time. Please begin when you are tagged by a colleague. Once you're finished with testing and completing our online survey, then place your headshot photo sticker to the friendly animal that was given to you when you were tagged. Finally, tag someone else! Say hello to someone whom you haven't met yet, yay. We will have a prize for the team with the longest chain of tags by the end of the test drive. No one person can test drive more than once. The best strategy for winning is trying to participate and making sure you are tagging others quickly and encouraging them to participate and keep the chain going. We also have embedded a feedback tool inside of Critter that you can use to write short comments as you use it. We encourage you to save your feedback for the usability survey above, but if you encounter bugs or other issues that you want to report immediately, then go ahead and click the "Write Feedback" tab to give us feedback. Testing Instructions Link to Critter 1. Log in with your unique pre-registered username Username: your first name followed by last name initial (e.g. ariannac, angelinaj, bradp, and etc.) Password: testcritter 2. Try the following features - Read the contents under Help tab - Write new comments - Read other peoples' comments - Recommend comments - Recognize highly recommended comments - Create a new group on Critter - Initiate discussions with other groups and users - Browse videos - Upload* videos *Currently, Critter only supports video files in “.flv” format. 3. Complete our online usability testing survey Thank you, Arianna, Calli, Faisal, Frederik, Rebekah and Zhou
