Parents as Classroom Partners

csd2126

May 15, 2017


ClassTag is a free tool to help teachers more easily schedule classroom activities, share important announcements, and communicate with parents.

Pros:

The appeal of ClassTag is to help busy teachers who don’t have much time to communicate information on their many students to all the parents in their classroom community. Similar to other classroom management tools, ClassTag asks teachers to create a profile, add the contact information for parents, and send notifications to their classroom contact list. The interface for organizing classroom activities is simple and intuitive even for less tech-savvy educators. ClassTag allows teachers to send various forms of communication to parent partners such as homework, event updates, pictures, announcements, and direct communications all through push and email notifications.

Cons:

When compared with the often crumpled up paper notices students forget in their knapsacks, ClassTag seems like a better alternative. However, a tech savvy teacher would be able to find most of the functionality offered by ClassTag in other pieces of software they may already be using to organize their classrooms. Indeed, many of the features available courtesy of this platform could be achieved by organizing an email inbox or by taking the time to manage an email newsletter.

Our Takeaway:

ClassTag brings a lot of features together that exist across other services, but providing parents with more information on their child’s learning is an interesting approach provided both parents and teachers take the time to leverage the features of this interesting tool.
