What's Your Fantasy Smart Phone Features?

Oumar Soumahoro

March 4, 2013

For many years now, giant smartphone companies have been trying to find the perfect match to consumers' demands and desires, but as time goes by and new technologies appear, once top ranked smartphones become old very very quickly. In this article from CNN, an assembly at the Mobile World Congress revealed the cutting-edge advances coming soon to the world of smartphones. But talking about upcoming features on the smartphone world, I wonder how it would be if we were allowed to create our own features for our smartphones. What do you think? What features are you hoping to see on new and upcoming smartphones?
