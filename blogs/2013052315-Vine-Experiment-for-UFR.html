Vine Experiment for UFR

Kate Meersschaert

May 23, 2013

After last-night's Young to Publishing Twitter workshop (we didn't cover Vine, I just downloaded it while they were reviewing how to set-up a Twitter account)... I was inspired to try Vine as a more multi-media-powered Understanding Fiscal Responsibility promo! What do you think?! Potential for other projects? Check-out my original blog post on the announcement re: Vine! How? You have 6 seconds to record a video using their app... it is edited by you as you create it. For ex, you hold down your finger on the interface to record and can stop and record anything along that six-second range. Creating a modern flip book or stop animation loop! Very easy to install, create and share! Sadly, though, not Android friendly (YET!)! UFR "My @UFRCurriculum just Arrived!" (Stretching it a bit... but I did just discover the unopened box and extra curriculum!) :)
