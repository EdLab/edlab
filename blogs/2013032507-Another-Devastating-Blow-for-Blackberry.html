Another Devastating Blow for Blackberry

Khalil Abubakar

March 25, 2013

The US Department of Defense (DoD) has decided to leave all Blackberry devices and switch to Apple. According to Electronista, the DoD left for Apple because the release of the Blackberry 10 has rendered an already existing 470,000 Blackberry devices obsolete. Due to cost measures, the DoD chose not to upgrade to Blackberry 10 and has decided to purchase 120,000 iPads, 100,000 iPad minis, 200,000 iPod Touches and 210,000 iPhones. Not only the DoD, the National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) also announced it will be switching to Apple. When companies were asked what made Apple devices special, they replied the new Blackberry 10 OS can run on only Blackberry 10 devices only whilst the Apple new iOS 6 can run on iPhones as old as the 3GS and iPads as old as the iPad 2. So with Blackberry not considered by companies and organizations, which usually makes purchases in bulk and the new BB10 not doing well in sales, what do you think should be Blackberry's next move to save the company ?
