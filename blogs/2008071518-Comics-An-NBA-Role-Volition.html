Comics: An NBA Role Volition

Ian Toledo

July 15, 2008

In response to Hui Soo's previous post about comics luring young athletes into certain universities, this CBR article reports that 2007 No. 1 draft pick of the Portland Trailblazers Greg Oden has teamed up with Viz Media to promote literacy via Japanese comics. Copies of manga will be donated to Ogden's charity, Oregon Mentors. One of the titles donated will be Japan's hugely popular SLAM DUNK manga. These will also feature a stat profile of an NBA player with their special move, starting with Lebron James' own slam dunk. Now athletes are luring youth into reading through comics. Ironic, man.
