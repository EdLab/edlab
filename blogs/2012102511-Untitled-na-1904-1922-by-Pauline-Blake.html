Untitled (n/a, 1904-1922), by Pauline Blake

Walter Hoyle

October 25, 2012

Pauline Blake studied under Arthur Wesley Dow. From Teachers College records, we know that during the summer of 1926 she "made lithographic drawings and painted at Provincetown, Mass" and in January of 1922 she was appointed an instructor "in design and allied subjects" at the Minneapolis Institute of the Arts.
