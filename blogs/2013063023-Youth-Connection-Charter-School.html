Youth Connection Charter School

abagigah92

June 30, 2013

Chicago is known for having some of the toughest neighborhoods in the country. The chance of having a great and successful education for many children is very slim due to many outside influences in the neighborhood. Last June, Chicago Public School districts were on the verge of setting a new record with a graduation rate of more than 60 percent for students earning diploma in 2012. Although Chicago is 20 percent lower than the overall national rate, the city has mad incredible efforts to limit dropouts and raise graduation rates. This is mainly because of charter schools and other alternative programs that help students in their studies. The Youth Connection Charter School is a system of charter schools around the city with 22 schools located in neighborhoods populated with the city's impoverished South and West sides.
