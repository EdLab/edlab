Technology Based Education for Inmates

AbdulMalik

March 21, 2016

The main goal of the corrections facility is to avoid an increase in repeat offenders. Criminology experts indicate that crimes shape the way society functions and that there is no way to eradicate criminal activities completely. Edovo is a technology based educational platform aimed at reducing the number of repeat offenders by giving inmates the chance to acquire education. This program can reduce recidivism by 43%. Through this program, incarcerated individuals will be able to learn about therapy and other health related issues in addition to learning tools for the GED. This program emphasizes the idea that inmates have an inherent sense of hope and they should be given a chance to redeem themselves. Perhaps the best part of the educational platform is that, taxpayer money is not required to fund the program.
