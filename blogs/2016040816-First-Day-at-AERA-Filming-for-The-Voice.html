First Day at AERA: Filming for The Voice

JHeltz

April 8, 2016

Greetings from DC! Andrew, Lauren, and I are perched here on the 3rd floor of the impressive Walter E. Washington Convention Center where AERA, now in its 100th year, is in full swing! Since arriving last night via Amtrak we've been conducting interviews for The Voice with AERA attendees. These are professors and education researchers from all over the country taking about e classrooms, racially charged disciplinary issues, and even suspected scandal within the world of higher ed. This season of The Voice is sure to shock and amaze! We'll keep you posted!
