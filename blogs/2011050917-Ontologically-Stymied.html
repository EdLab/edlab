Ontologically Stymied

Ajish George

May 9, 2011

Trying to find existing efforts for putting together an ontology of education research, I came across the Ontologies for Education project. Thinking my work had been done for me, I dug in, only to find this ontological mire. It is in fact an Ontology to inform the use of Ontologies in Education, or as they put it, an Ontological Web Portal for Educational Ontologies. I then proceed to vanish in a puff of logic.
