Guglielmo Marconi

Rebekah Wallin

July 16, 2012

Guglielmo Marconi was an Italian inventor who transmitted the first wireless radio signal across the Atlantic in 1901. He helped to create the business of wireless communication that paved the way for today's radio, cell phones, and satellite transmissions. Learn more about Marconi in a brief documentary (narrated by Walter Cronkite) on Vialogues.
