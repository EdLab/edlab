NY Edtech Entrepreneurs Meetup 04.30.12

Kate Meersschaert

April 23, 2012

Private! Thanks to Michelle's great heads-up, I subscribed to receive updates from this Edtech-focused MeetUp group. Their upcoming "edupreneur"-focused mash-up event on April 30th looks especially interesting and I plan to attend to gather ideas & contacts for LaunchPad! If you want to join me, feel-free to sign-up here (there is a $5 fee). Speakers include: James Shelton III, Assistant Deputy Secretary for Innovation and Improvement, Department of Education Ellen Winn, Senior Vice President, 50CAN Steven Hodas, Founder, Contnu and Founder/ CEO of Noodle Education Lyel Resner, Fellow at the Reynolds Program in Social Entrepreneurship at NYU, and working on Startup Box South Bronx (SB2) Jordan Garbis, Interim COO, PerformIT at Kaplan Ventures Jordan Goldman, Founder, Unigo Jalak Jobanputra, Managing Director, RTP Ventures Rhena Jasey, primary grades educator, The Equity Project Marisa Kaplan, educator and education consultant, EdGeeks Jerry Crisci, Technology Director, Scarsdale Public Schools
