After Ed 2.0: Refining the "Pitch" Process

bhughes

February 3, 2009

After Ed TV is evolving. Recognizing that this is still a work-in-progress, I invite everyone in the lab to download and read our current project plan (below). In summary, the video team will be working to make many of these changes in the coming weeks and months, and we appreciate feedback in one or all of the areas outlined in the doc (and anything else you may think of)! We are also looking for help and collaboration in all these areas. Also, one very important component of our evolution will be to solicit ideas for videos and video series in new ways. Please see the second document for a sample "Video Proposal Form." Try using it! As a goal for changing our editorial process, we are hoping to have pitches for the spring term submitted by Monday, February 16th. For now, that means sending them to me, Erin, or the aftered at tc dot columbia dot edu. For now, these pitches may just come from the lab itself, but in the near future we may be opening this process up to anyone at TC or beyond... *Note that I've updated the Video Proposal Form (on Tuesday, 2/3, 5pm) (Please keep private.)
