First Annual SiNYA's

JHeltz

December 17, 2015

Every Wednesday upon publication to NLT, the Seen in NY web series celebrates innovative approaches to education and learning around the state. But the first time in recorded history, we're going to celebrate (or at least recognize) the series itself with a fun awards ceremony. The highs, the lows, the success moments, the haunting regrets. We'll remember it all--and with your help! Please join us on Friday, December 18th from 11:00 AM to 11:30 AM in the seminar space for the First Annual SiNY Awards (SiNYA's). Don your neon yellow (if you like) and we'll see you there!
