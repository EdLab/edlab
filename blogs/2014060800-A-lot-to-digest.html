A lot to digest.

Luming Nie

June 8, 2014

Finished my first week of internship at EdLab. :)
A lot to digest -- not only just the general structure of current projects, but also various detailed techniques. For this week, I spent a lot of time reading documentations and play with simplest cases to prepare.
My work will be start from making unit test, building robust test modules for current projects. Although this is a brand new field to me, I feel confident to complete all the tasks with the instruction of two nice mentor here.
This week's free food seminar and group lunch are restful and joyful. Btw, our new programmers and interns here got brand new Mac Pro as the work stations, really cool! :)
Cannot wait to push my code at the beginning of next week.
