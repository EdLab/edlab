Infographic: How College Students Really Use Mobile Tech

Kate Meersschaert

December 4, 2012

With the recent launch of New Learning Times I thought I would share this inforgraphic featuring the mobile usage and habits of college students. Any surprises? Takeaways? I really like that the graphic opens with a "basic" overview of usage and then moves-into more specific data. Interesting to note for NLT: 65% read articles on mobile news sites & apps 84% use this tech to access "breaking" news This "recency" focus seems esp. apropos to NLT, as we publish 3x's a day!
