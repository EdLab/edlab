Project Proposal: EdLab Feedback Widget

Kate Meersschaert

June 7, 2012

Private please! Crazy Idea! What if... we created our own integrated feedback widget that could replace our current InfoInformer-based system and service all our tools & apps? I was inspired by a meeting with Rebecca when we discussed how the whole library could get involved and provide feedback on all EdLab tools. Vision... Here's what I imagine... a patron, TC student, Edlabber, user has feedback regarding one of the growing list of tools and apps we have developed here at the lab. Instead of feeding this feedback (sorry!) to an outside party, why don't we have a widget that could live on all of our sites (including the blog and TCR!) and pull feedback to one dedicated spot and then display it for us in our DataDashboard?
