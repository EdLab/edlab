Introducing Vialogues Simple Embed

zhou

April 16, 2015

------- UPDATE 4/19 ------- Twitter has approved our video embed request. So in addition to FB you can also embed a premium Vialogues video on Twitter by simply pasting an URL! -------------------------------- About three weeks ago I asked if we would like to have a Vialogues player app on Facebook so that we can embed premium Vialogues videos in FB. Thanks a lot to many of you who gave valuable feedbacks to my blogpost. With the support from Hui Soo and Pranav we finally made this happen. The feature we are introducing today is even more interesting than a FB app alone â€” it actually makes it possible to embed premium Vialogues videos on many social media websites/apps that support Open Graph metatags (e.g. Facebook, LinkedIn, Slack). Twitter has its own media embed rules similar to Open Graph, so we also made it work with Twitter (see notes below). For other social media websites (e.g. Google+, Pinterest), the video thumbnail will be embedded rather than the video because they only support image embed tags. How do we utilize this new feature? It's simple. To share a Vialogues video on Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn, simply copy the Vialogues play URL (NOT the embed code), and paste it into the textbox. You will probably see a preview automatically generated below the textbox. Then you can submit it and the video will be posted. Please note that only premium Vialogues videos are eligible for simple embed. To know whether a vialogue is premium, you can go to the embed tab in the Vialogues Play page. A premium video will have the option of "Embedding video without discussion". So how about non-premium videos? Well, non-premium videos cannot be embedded externally right now but their thumbnail images will be embedded with a short synopsis on the social media websites. Instead, an image link is presented so that the user will be taken to the Vialogues website to watch the video. Also, we are waiting for the approval from Twitter for video embed. Right now image embed will work on Twitter. Once they approve our request the video will be embedded. Please let me know if you have any feedback or find any bugs with this feature. Thanks.
