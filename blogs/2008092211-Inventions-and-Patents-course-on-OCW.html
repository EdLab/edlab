"Inventions and Patents" course on OCW

Stephanie Hunt

September 22, 2008

While digging around in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science course files on MIT's Open CourseWare site, I stumbled across course 6.901, called Inventions and Patents. The course is 3 years old at this point, but by all means still relevant reading for anyone interested in issues surrounding invention, innovation, and the ways that ideas are protected and shared under US law.
