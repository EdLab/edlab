Announcing the first....

Kate Meersschaert

October 18, 2012

Private! EdLab EdTech Talk podcast!!! I conducted this podcast (saved here in PocketKnowledge) with Marc from Urtak! Urtak presented this seminar recently at EdLab and sat for this podcast a few weeks later. The main goals of this project are... -To support & learn from the local edupreneurship community. -Gather insights that we can eventually share through the Launchpad platform. -Collect audio resources that will potentially be a part of an mSchool course on Entrepreneurship in the Ed space. The subjects of these podcasts are... -Founders that we meet at outreach events -Ed stakeholders and luminaries -Certain guests who visit the EdLab Please add your feedback in the comments so that we can make these audio resources the most relevant to people who are invested in the future of educational technology and learning! I look-forward to your thoughts!
