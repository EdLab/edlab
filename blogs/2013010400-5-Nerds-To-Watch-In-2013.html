5 Nerds To Watch In 2013

Daniel Vergara

January 4, 2013

I am not sure how many of you have heard about the conference held by Gina Bianchini, founder of Mightybell, which talks about the most interesting five technologies that people should look out for in the New Year. She mentions a Ted conference including Regina Dugan where she stated that nerds would be key in the future and as such people should keep track on certain “nerds” because they are the future of technology. Here is the list of nerds to look out for in the coming years:

  1. Regina Dugan
  2. Babak Parviz
  3. Andreas Raptopoulos
  4. Gina Bianchini
  5. Eddy Cue
