NYC Digital Humanities and Performing Arts Meetup

Leanora Lange

June 9, 2012

For all digital humanists and performing arts enthusiasts out there, and especially for those who might name themselves by both of these worthy titles, there is a Digital Humanities and Performing Arts Meetup just for you taking place this coming Thursday, June 14, 2012. It features presentations of two very intriguing projects: Linked Jazz and Journey On. I can personally vouch for the Linked Jazz project, as I have had the privilege of being Prof. Pattuelli's research assistant since the beginning of the summer. I won't give away the details, but there will be demonstrations of crowdsourcing and visualization tools, which are very much worth seeing! Plus you get the exclusive chance to give us feedback to improve these tools. Shea Elmore's presentation on Journey On, an interactive performance streamed live from one space to an audience in another, sounds quite intriguing as well. Here are the details: Digital Humanities and Performing Arts Meetup June 14, 2012 6:30 - 8:30 pm Barnard College 3009 Broadway, New York, NY 10027 Sign up here to attend. Learn more about the Digital Humanities and Performing Arts Meetup: http://www.meetup.com/NYC-Digital-Humanities-Performing-Arts-Meetup-Group/
