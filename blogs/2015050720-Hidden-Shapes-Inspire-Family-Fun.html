Hidden Shapes Inspire Family Fun

jvazquez

May 7, 2015


Shapes are sneaky. They could be hiding anywhere! What does it take to find them? Perceptive kids, patient parents, and an engaging environment.

A shape hunt, popularized by PBS Parents, is like a scavenger hunt and I Spy rolled into one. Kids race each other to find the circles, rhombuses, and pentagons hidden in plain sight. Recognizing shapes in the context of everyday surroundings is a great way to orient kids to geometry (without them even realizing it!). It also supports pattern recognition and allows kids to practice their vocabulary as they describe their findings.
