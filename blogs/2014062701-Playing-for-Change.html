Playing for Change

Carmel Addae

June 27, 2014

I came across an attention-grabbing Vialogue about Playing for Change (PFC), a foundation that brings music education to impoverished communities in developing countries. PFC has established nine music schools and programs thus far and plans to build more. The great thing about PFC schools is that it's entirely free for all students. They have provided music and educational opportunities to 700 children in six different countries. Here is a list of some schools that PFC has created.

  • Ntonga Music School, South Africa
  • Bizung School of Music & Dance, Ghana
  • Mitrata Music Program, Nepal
  • Star School Music & Sports Program, Rwanda
  • Udayapur Music Program, Nepal
  • Ecole De Musique De Kirina, Mali
