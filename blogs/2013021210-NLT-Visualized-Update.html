NLT Visualized Update

Janice Joo

February 12, 2013

Please keep private: Hello team, Here's the scoop on NLT Visualized. We have a new process in place that we've been transitioning to since the new year. The team consists of Laura S, Fred, Hui Soo, Demetri, and me. The weekly creative process We meet weekly for 30-45 mins on Mondays at 2:15pm. In preparation for the meeting, we review all the NLT High Fives from the last 7 days, and pitch stories that might be suitable for visualization. Demetri and I often take an early stab at sketching our stories before the meeting to expedite the ideation process and to get more meaningful feedback that directly affects the creative process (drawing!). For me, I devote my Monday mornings almost entirely to doing my NLT reading and subsequent sketching/pitching to prepare for the team meeting. It sticks! We are happy to say that we are now moving towards a more stable weekly production cycle of 4 pitch+sketches into the Evaluator system by Monday/Tuesday afternoon, with the hopes of 50% of the sketches being approved by the "NLT Review Board" (Gary). There is often some back and forth, incorporating Gary's feedback, and consulting with team members again throughout the week to craft the best visual story. We hope this process will result in 1-2 final pieces per week. Here's a sneak peak at the sketches the team submitted this week. We already know which 2 are approved for second drafts. Stay tuned...
