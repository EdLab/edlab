Jo's Idea got me thinking

Anthony Cocciolo

February 4, 2009

I've started using this app today called LoseIt, which is an iPhone app that helps you meet your weight loss goals. I was motivated to try it from Jo's app idea from today's seminar. I must say, it is very well done. I never liked the idea of calorie counting, but this makes it really easy to do (it does all the counting for you). I now know that I can eat 633 more calories today (assuming that I burn 464 calories tonight running). I set it up so that I can lose 2 lbs a week for the next 2 weeks. This is just in time for my trip with my ridiculously fit and toned friends to Vieques!
