Google Apps Challenging Business

abagigah92

December 27, 2012

A new report shows the advancement Google has made these past years. It has taking Google some years to reach its apex but it is now one of the top competitors in developing business software and in doing so, it is giving Microsoft a great deal of competition. The development of Google Apps, which consists of application for writing and collaboration, has sky rocketed Google's consumer base.

Google has been promoting the idea of an all cloud base for six years mainly targeting small business and technology startups. The use of Google Apps by larger enterprises and governmental departments such as the Department of Interior (with approximately 90,000 workers) showcases the vision and competitiveness of Google in its effort to expand and reach out for more cloud users. In a recent study, Google generated $37.9 billion in revenue including a billion solely from the use and expansion of Google Apps.
