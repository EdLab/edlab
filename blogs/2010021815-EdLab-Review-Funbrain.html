EdLab Review: Funbrain

Chandler Kang

February 18, 2010

teAchnology Tags: game, archives, comics, movie
http://www.funbrain.com/ Overall: 1.81 Design score: 1.86 out of 5 Pedagogy score: 1.75 out of 5 [The Good] The site focuses on learning by having fun activities. Therefore, many cartoon and images were used, which lead to children's easy access to the webpage and have them ready to next step for deeper learning. Also, each game is pre-set based on user (kid)'s grade level and sex. Based on that, game contents and interface vary. [The Bad] Because of its good character, the site does not fully provide in-depth learning environment. Also, there is no interaction between users. [The Bottom Line] Good quality of graphic uses and various fun activities enable children to easily approach learning task. Read Full Review
