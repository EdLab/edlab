BlackBerry 10 vs. iPhone 5

Francisco.Mendoza

February 21, 2013

The smartphone wars between Apple and Research in Motion (RIM), makers of BlackBerry, was all but done as Apple's trademark iPhone generated more sales than BlackBerry's models in recent years. A few weeks ago, RIM rebranded itself solely as BlackBerry and announced their latest smartphone, the BlackBerry 10, which it saw as the direct competitor to the latest iPhone, 5. According to this piece from Gadget Masters, the BlackBerry 10 has a bigger screen and a faster browser than the iPhone 5, among others. In my opinion, as an Apple user, I believe that the iPhone is better at everything and there is a good chance a BlackBerry 10 holder will tell you the exact opposite. With the new BlackBerry overtake the iPhone 5's place in the smartphone market? Which one is better in your opinion?
