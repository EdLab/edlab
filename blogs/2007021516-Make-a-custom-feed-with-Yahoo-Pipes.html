Make a custom feed with Yahoo Pipes

Jamison Judd

February 15, 2007

The new Yahoo! Pipes allows anyone to create a custom feed. The intuitive interface allows anyone with a little bit of programming experience to mash-up data from searches and RSS feeds and then filter them. One example ia a pipe that grabs headlines from the New York Times and finds photos on Flickr with matching keywords. This could be very useful for academics trying to keep up on all the developments related to their field. If we could get RSS feeds for all the journals and other reliable sources of information, it would eliminate much of the time spent searching for up-to-date information. Yahoo! Pipes
