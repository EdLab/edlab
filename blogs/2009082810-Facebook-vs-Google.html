Facebook vs. Google

Jeannie Crowley

August 28, 2009

Earlier this year, Facebook rejected Google's investment attempt and aligned itself with Microsoft. There has been a growing rivalry (at least on Facebook's side) ever since. The people at Facebook think Facebook will soon replace Google and be the search engine leader. The Facebook team believes searching based upon social networks and personal preferences will be a welcome alternative to Google's efficient but impersonal methods. Do you believe Facebook is a legitimate rival or will it become an object of ridicule like other social networking sites? This YouTube video shows what many users think of the popularity cycle of social networks. How long will it be until we call Facebook "the new Friendster"?
