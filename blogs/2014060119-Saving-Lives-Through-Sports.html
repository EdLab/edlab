Saving Lives Through Sports

AbdulMalik

June 1, 2014

Sports fans around the world have a lot of influence on the teams they support. Without fans, sports won't be as exciting. Fans are usually very emotional and passionate about the progress of the teams they support. This article sheds light on how Sport Club Recife, a soccer team in Brazil, has come up with an initiative they believe can help save many lives. The team has launched a campaign that encourage fans to agree to donate their organs after they die in order to ensure that their love for the club will continue to exist in another person. Over 66,000 people have signed up for the donor card. For the fans, this initiative helps them save lives while showing their love for the club. Some of the more famous and glamorous soccer clubs in Europe have expressed interest in starting a similar initiative.

For most people (myself included), supporting a sporting club is like being a member of a family. The fans of almost every team expect a lot from the players and the administration and vice versa. In this case, to donate an organ that saves a life is perhaps the best way to show your passion for the sport and club and also a way to give back to society.
