Please add your thoughts... EdLab EdTech Talks?!

Kate Meersschaert

November 14, 2011

Please keep private! Hi Team EdLab! I am hoping to develop along with the team, a new series of audio/video interviews with leaders and thinkers in the EdTech entrepreneurship & start-up world. This series is tentatively titled, EdLab EdTech Talks and could be featured as vialogues and then embedded both on the blog, tweeted and eventually featured on the New Learning Times platform. Here & embedded below is a vialogue I created to test this concept and gather feedback from the team. The name is definitely a work in progress, but based on other group's similar series listed below, it seems important that the name stay consistent and clearly express what the content involves and who was responsible for creation. Examples of popular Tech-focused video-based programs include (there are very few that are EdTech-focused!): Stanford's eCorner O'Reilly Media's Web Squared Series TechCrunch's Founder Stories Potential Goals of the EdLab EdTech Talks Could Include: 1) To create an additional forum to feature entrepreneurially-focused content via the EdLab blog, Vialogues & eventually the New Learning Times platform. 2) To create content that might be interesting to the EdTech world, as there are many Tech-focused interview series, but few that are EdTech-focused. 3) Create resources that might be helpful for our LaunchPad39B initiative. 4) As the vialogues are shorter than our traditional Seminar long-shot these interviews could be more easily shared and tweeted. Also, they could be created using webcast, screencasting, PowerPoint-generated movies to illustrate audio interviews and more. Please feel free to watch the vialogue embedded below and provide feedback on the potential for this new series, add your ideas for content, naming etc. I look forward to your thoughts and hopefully generating new materials together for LaunchPad and NLT!
