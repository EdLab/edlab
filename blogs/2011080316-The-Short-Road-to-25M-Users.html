The Short Road to 25M Users

Kate Meersschaert

August 3, 2011

Mashable featured the following graphs by ComScore today exploring the initial adoption rate and user behavior of Google+. Google+ had a meteoric rise, surpassing both FaceBook, MySpace & Twitter in speed of user adoption reaching 25M within the first 5 months of launch. How engaged are Google+ users as compared to the other 3 platforms? FaceBook still rules the social media realm! Do you think Google+ will become a victim of a fast start?
