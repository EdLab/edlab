French Horn Hip Hop

Alexandra Lederman

May 25, 2015


Question: How does one turn a French horn piece into hip hop? Answer: Through the Building Beats digital music production class, featuring guest Sarah Willis, a classically trained French hornist!

Sarah Willis spent a day at the Carnegie Hall Weill Music Institute with Building Beats, a non-profit organization teaching underserved youth life and management skills through digital music production. Sarah both shares her life as a traditional instrumentalist with this group of teens and teaches them how to play the French horn. Later the teens sample the French horn (and her voice) into original songs and beats. The end results are surprising and showcase the students’ creativity and innovative techniques.

Besides the obvious education factor, what benefits could be had from a classically trained instrumentalists collaborating in a digital music production class? Tell us about how you’ve repurposed sounds on Vialogues.
