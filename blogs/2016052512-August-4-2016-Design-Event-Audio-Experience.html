August 4, 2016 Design Event Audio Experience

HSC

May 25, 2016

Post your group's soundtrack ideas in this blog post. Thanks. == On August 4, 2016, the EdLab and the Office of the President will host a Design Event to build a network schools, educators, and researchers to support the selection and assessment of learning technologies. Here is a tentative outline of activities we have scheduled for the Design Event: I. Sign-In / Badge Pick Up (10am) II. Exhibit Tour - Four exhibits related to the Design Event theme will be set up in the Learning Theater (10-10:15am) III. Event Start (!0:15am) IV. Welcome / Problem Statement (10:20) V. Pair Icebreaker Activity and Share Out (10:20-10:45) VI. Small Group Case Study Analysis - In small groups users will visit four different exhibit spaces that highlight examples of collaborations between teachers, researchers, and developers. (10:45-11:30) VII. Whole Group Brainstorming (11:30-12pm) VIII. Lunch / Small Group Prototyping Phase I (12-12:30pm) IX. Small Group Prototyping Phase 2 (12:30pm-1:30pm) X. Report Out (1:30-2pm) Your task in small group is to come up with a soundtrack for the entire Design Event. Be prepared to share your soundtrack.
