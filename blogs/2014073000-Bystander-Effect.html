Bystander Effect

Henry Adjei

July 30, 2014

Bystander Effect occurs in a situation in which individuals do not give help to a victim when there are people present. The greater the number of people, the less likely people will help. This video is part of the video I wish my teacher can see project and it has helped me a lot to understand the different ways in which bystander effect occurs. This video is about this guy who abuse her girlfriend in public and people sit there and watch him abuse her. Some people ignored her and some reacted to the situation. We always encounter this kind of situations and we think is not part of our business so why should we intrude. How would you react to this situation if you where suppose to be there?
