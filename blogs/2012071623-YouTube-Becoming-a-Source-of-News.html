YouTube Becoming a Source of News

Mingoro Fanny

July 16, 2012

The platform for which people receive or read about the news has ballooned in recent years, mainly due to social sites such as Facebook and Twitter. According to this piece, YouTube has become a major platform for people to view the news. This is the result of a study conducted by the Pew Research Center. The report notes that though TV remains the predominant platform for viewers when it comes to the news, YouTube is fast becoming another source for people who want to watch the latest news. In today's day and age where everyone essentially serves as a journalist, the study notes that there is a balance between user-generated videos and those from news organizations such as NBC. Though user generated videos has become a large source of the videos on YouTube, there is also the question of authenticity as those content doesn't come with the name or brand associated with TV heavyweights such as CNN or ABC.
