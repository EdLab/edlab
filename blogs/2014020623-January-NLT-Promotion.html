January NLT Promotion

rendy

February 6, 2014

This past month, the DigiQ team also participated in the broader NLT promotion on Google Plus (G+). It was nice to participate in this endeavor because it allowed me to learn about social media promotion and the effort that goes into it. Before I was engaged in the NLT push, I didn't know much about G+. I had never used it and didn't know it had a lot so many interesting aspects such as the number of various communities. I also never came across Data Dashboard and didn't know how the proper information to put in to generate the kind of data we were looking to collect. In the beginning, it was a little confusing as it was my first time planning a promotional campaign. Promoting NLT everyday made it easier for me to create and post the campaigns. It was also very good to see all the results of my efforts and look forward to utilizing it to promote my set of STEM related Vialogue videos.
