Proposal for a New NLT Content Stream: "Gaming the System"

Kate Meersschaert

March 9, 2012

Private! As game dynamics and conventions continue to become more and more ubiquitous in the ed world... from badges in online discussion boards to quest-based brick & mortar schools, it seems like this might be a worthy content stream to consider for the New Learning Times? The stream tentatively titled, "Gaming the System" could cover the following: -New ed-focused games. -EdTech that integrates game dynamics well or not. -Best practice... the do's & don't's of adding these elements. -Reviews of the newest literature in the field. -How are online learning platforms integrating these elements? -Highlight instructors/professors who are using game dynamics in the classroom in new and exciting ways. Do you feel this would be a worthy content stream for NLT? If so, do you like the name "Gaming the System?" Is there enough content for a dedicated stream or should "gaming" be added as an element to an existed stream like EdLab Review? Thank you for your thoughts!
