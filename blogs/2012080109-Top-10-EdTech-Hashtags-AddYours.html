Top 10 EdTech Hashtags... #AddYours!

Kate Meersschaert

August 1, 2012

Do you Tweet for work? If so... what hash tags do you most often use to ensure that your ed-focused chat can be easily found using the ubiquitous #? While researching this for my own EdLab Twitter optimization quest (Teachers College Record & Vialogues accounts) I came-across this run-down of the top ten Twitter #' for edtech (check-out the link for the complete list of ed-focused #'s!): 1- #edtech 2- #edchat 3- #Web20 5- #mlearning (mSchool!?) 6- #edapps / #edapp 7- #appsforkids 8- #ipaded 9- #slide2learn 10- #highered What hash tags would you add? Thanks to Greg... I always try to always use "educational technology," instead of edtech in blog posts, as this term ranks higher in search results, but on Twitter, brevity seems to win-out!
