Urban Planning

abagigah92

July 24, 2015

Urban Planning and Urban Studies is a profession and study that works to improve the welfare of people and their communities by creating more convenient, equitable, healthful, efficient places for present and future generations to achieve a level of sustainability. Planning allows leaders, businesses, and citizens to collaborate and play an important role in creating communities that will improve the lives of the people. Good planning helps create communities that offer better choices and services for people to achieve a level of sustainability. Planning helps cities to plan ahead into the future to handle disaster and to maintain a productive society. It helps them find the right balance of new development and essential services, environmental protection, and new innovations that will improve the well being of the city.
