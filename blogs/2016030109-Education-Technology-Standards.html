Education Technology Standards

Youssef Ballo

March 1, 2016

Technology has improved since the last update of the plan in 2010. Mobile devices and relatively reasonable high-speed Internet access have served as platforms for many learning technologies. The growing digital divide in the access to these advanced technologies is also an area of concern. Technology standards is different from education standards because education standard is a specific capability and grade level. Technology standards creates a platform for the development of a tool. Technology standards provide assistance when it is useful to pointlessly complex features. In education this mean developing specific text-to-speech that would function across educational software, this would make teachers training easier and improves experience of teachers and students.
