Trends in Ed, 3.26.09

jvazquez

March 26, 2009

The elevator-pitch ready lecture e-Learners may have a lot more time on their hands now with the advent of the micro-lecture-- presentations that normally last 50+ minutes but have been condensed down to a one to three minute podcast (click here for more details). Students are asked to complete relevant assignments, discussions and practical learning activities in conjunction with watching the lecture. Proponents point to the fact that it supports an active learning environment. It also gives students the opportunity to take as much time as they need to absorb the info (there's so much less to absorb!). I've never taken an online or distance course but this format is definitely intriguing.
