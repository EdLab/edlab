Give and Take - Chs. 5 & 6

gnatriello

May 25, 2013

Keep Private Continuing through the book, a few questions and items from Chapters 5 and 6. Chapter 5 — The Power of Powerless Communication What are the major dimensions of a communication style that will lead to influence? Identify an example of a communication you have been engaged in (as provider or recipient) that exhibited the “power of powerless communication.” Identify an example of a communication you have been engaged in (as provider or recipient) that did not exhibit the “power of powerless communication.” Chapter 6 — The Art of Motivation Maintenance What are the key factors that seem to sustain givers and even energize them? In light of these factors, which of our current practices (if any) are energizing for givers? What new practices would you recommend to create an environment that sustains givers?
