NetPosse v0.1 is released. Try to find your suggested network!

Kyoho Satsumi

August 13, 2009

Faisal and I release NetPosse v0.1. NetPosse is a web tool for creating high-quality networks with people quickly and easily. Our initial target of this application is Teachers college's students, alumni, faculty and staff. Before we launch this application to TC people, we want Edlab people to use this web application and hear your opinion. People can use this application easily. 1)Here you can register your User ID and password. http://160.39.76.229/netposse/new/web_login.php For user ID(=email), you can use fake one, but it should be like email address (Example: xxx@test.com). 2)Go to "About me" menu. Please input your information. I ask you to input "real" information here, because without real information you cannot see how good/bad our suggestion is. 3)After input your information, go to "My Suggested Network" menu. We have already input over 25 professors from TC programs of anthropology, applied statistics, art education, cognitive studies, education policy studies, music education. You can see top 10 matched people here, and we ask you to evaluate our suggestion by clicking radio button and submission. If you want to input me some professors' information (to see how you match them), please let me know. We are welcome your comments or suggestions about NetPosse. You can reply by this blog comment or e-mail to kyoho.satsumi at gmail.com. PS. I am not good at cool web design, so if someone helps me to make this site fancier, please let me know!
