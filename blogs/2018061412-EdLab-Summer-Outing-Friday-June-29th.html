EdLab Summer Outing - Friday, June 29th

christophergu

June 14, 2018

* PRIVATE *


Hi All,


Our EdLab Summer Outing will be on Friday, June 29th in Brooklyn!


We will be starting with breakfast at Junior’s restaurant in Downtown Brooklyn at 9:30 AM. Please meet us directly at the restaurant.


http://www.juniorscheesecake.com/content/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Brooklyn-Breakfast-04.26.17.pdf


The address for Junior’s 365 Flatbush Avenue Ext. The closest subway is the Dekalb Avenue stop on the B, Q, and R; and the Nevins Street stop on the 2, 3, 4, and 5. 


https://www.google.com/maps/place/365+Flatbush+Ave+Ext,+Brooklyn,+NY+11201/@40.689985,-73.9835992,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x89c25bb35f9b6c3f:0x609dd5ef03122e3f!8m2!3d40.689985!4d-73.9814105


After we finish breakfast, we will be traveling to the Brooklyn Museum, which is currently featuring the “David Bowie is” exhibit. We will be a the Brooklyn Museum no later than 11:45, as we have timed entrances for the special exhibit at 12:00 and 12:15. 


https://www.brooklynmuseum.org/


The address for the Brooklyn Museum is 200 Eastern Parkway. The closest subway stop is the Eastern Parkway / Brooklyn Museum 2 and 3 subway stop.


https://www.google.com/maps/place/200+Eastern+Pkwy,+Brooklyn,+NY+11238/@40.6712062,-73.9658193,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x89c25b0a45bce6bb:0x5bf020f45a685ee6!8m2!3d40.6712062!4d-73.9636306


After the exhibit, the rest of the day is free for you to explore the rest of the Brooklyn Museum. Prospect Park and the Brooklyn Botanical Garden are also nearby. 


If you are interested in joining us for the EdLab Summer Outing, please RSVP by next Tuesday, June 19th using the link below:


https://goo.gl/forms/KxPoCvFlJUjKzpgb2 


Please let me know if you have any questions. Looking forward to seeing you there!


Best, Chris
