Acumen Fund...Funds Leadership Courses

Kate Meersschaert

February 4, 2013

Check-out this series of leadership courses from Acumen fund! (Note: check-out the infographics featured on their site... they visualize the funds 10 years and big successes. Great idea!) Is anyone interested in possibly joining me for this free six week "after hours" leadership course? The goal for participation is to gain insight for LaunchPad 1.0, generate a possible New Learning Times (NLT) Sector piece and blog about the experience for the lab. The course is estimated to involve about 1-2 hours of time a week. I still need to assess whether or not this course makes sense to participate in but I thought I would pass-it-along! The last two courses look really interesting as they are focused on "design thinking" and "adaptive leadership." Learn more about the course from their site-based description:

Acumen Leadership Essentials is a 6-week course that will introduce you to the “Acumen Way” of leadership. We will challenge you with lessons from Acumen Fund's 10+ years of experience investing in companies that change the way the world tackles poverty. The course is free but you must register as a group (min: 2 people; max: 6). We created +acumen to give people a way to “add Acumen” to their lives. We want to share what Acumen Fund is learning — our knowledge and insights, and perhaps more importantly, the values and principles underpinning our approach — with more people around the world because the work of creating a world beyond poverty needs all of us. Take what you learn at +acumen and apply it in your own way. Innovation could come from anyone, and we believe that the world truly needs you to lead.
