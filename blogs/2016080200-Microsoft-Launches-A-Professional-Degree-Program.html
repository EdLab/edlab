Microsoft Launches A Professional Degree Program

ABARRY

August 2, 2016

Technology has been one of the most important factors in our daily lives. From requesting an Uber to simply manipulating our cell phones. All these technologies handed to us might appear easy from our perspectives, but it takes a set of skills and knowledge to establish them. That being said, Microsoft wants more data scientists in the job market. However, they strongly recommend young individuals to gain their knowledge starting completely afresh, while mid-career professionals need to ameliorate their past experiences. Microsoft has launched a range of courses in data science through edX.org. The data science program is the first offering Microsoft Professional Degree, a curriculum designed to improve professionals experiences in the data science field. In addition, the Microsoft course consists of nine classes and a final project. Giving how desirable the IT field is, this is a great opportunity for young individuals who are seeking to obtain and develop significant tech skills.
