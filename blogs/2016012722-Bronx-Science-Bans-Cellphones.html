Bronx Science Bans Cellphones

AbdulMalik

January 27, 2016

It is evident that technology has become part of our daily lives. With technology we are able to interact with people without paying attention to distance. Perhaps high school students in New York were excited about being able to use their cellphones in school after Mayor de Blasio lifted the long-held ban. However, this change has not come without controversy. Students have to share the wifi connection of the school with teachers. Educators need the school's bandwidth to be able to access the internet to teach their lessons. Bronx Science has developed a strategy that will help block students from using the small amount of bandwidth that is available for instructors. It is important for students to have their cellphones in case of emergencies or other academically related functions. As a result, the Board of Education should increase the broadband capacity of schools to enable them to meet the demands of students and teachers.
