Monthly Music Hackathon

EdLab Studios

October 4, 2015


Where can a technologist who plays tabla, or an industrial designer who loves data, or a Ph.D. student who is also a singer-songwriter, go to practice their craft and work on their side projects, for free, with some regularity? Monthly Music Hackathon solves precisely these problems. It's a monthly hackathon organized by a team of musicians, tinkerers and technologists where people can go, enjoy free food, drink and thematic talks, and then hack on a side project all day long. While many hackathons tend to exist for the sake of networking or prototyping one-off projects, Monthly Music Hackathon seeks to create a space where regulars can return to a community of like-minded people and consistently work on one--or a series--of projects in a relaxed, stimulating environment.
