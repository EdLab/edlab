New Facebook Password Retrieving Method

Khalil Abubakar

May 8, 2013

Most Facebook users are automatically logged into our accounts on a mobile device and tend to forget our passwords when we are logged out, whether accidentally or otherwise. To solve this problem, Facebook now allows its users to select five trusted friends and different security codes are sent to them. At least three of the security codes are needed to retrieve your account.The introduction of this new password retrieving method is really helpful and users will be able to access their account even when it has been hacked.
