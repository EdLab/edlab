Rugrat Race: More on Red-Shirting

Fred Rossoff

March 7, 2012

60 Minutes just ran a piece on red-shirting (vialogue below), a practice previously discussed on this blog, in which they are fairly skeptical of the idea. Malcolm Gladwell makes an appearance and offers a lucid and concise summary of the issue. And here's an opinion piece from the New York Times, where the authors argue that the advantages of redshirting, such as they are, fade out fairly quickly, while the disadvantages (including behavioral problems and declining performance) are there to stay.
