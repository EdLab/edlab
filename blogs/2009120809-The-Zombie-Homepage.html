The Zombie Homepage

Jeannie Crowley

December 8, 2009

Apparently there is a growing concern that dead websites are spawning zombies. As a former student of The University of Florida, I was pleased to see UF's IT department was the first, and probably only, university department to create (and post on the official website) a zombie response plan. For those of you who need more details about the death (and ill-conceived attempts at reanimation) of a website, please refer to the Tales from Redesign Land blog. Having personally creating several dead sites, I believe I will take the necessary measures to protect myself from the inevitable attack.
