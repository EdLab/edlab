Kill Math

Josh Lopez

May 24, 2011

Ever wonder why mathematical notations are the way they are? Probably not, but it seems Bret Victor gave it a lot of thought when he began his Kill Math project. The idea here is that mathematical symbols are a necessity for writing equations and formulas out by hand, but with newer technology, a more accessible way of representing the underlying mathematics of a system could (should?) be developed. He has a pretty interesting take on how to represent variables and graph functions and a pretty interesting method of solving geometry problems. It looks like the project is still in its early stages, but I really hope to see more of this soon.
