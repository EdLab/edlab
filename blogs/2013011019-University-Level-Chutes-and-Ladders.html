University Level Chutes and Ladders

Janice Joo

January 10, 2013


According to the World University Rankings, the US and UK still dominate the top 10 spots, but experts predict that trends are shifting and Eastern universities are on the rise. Universities are ranked according to 13 performance indicators, which fall under five categories. Of those categories, teaching, research, and citation hold the greatest weight in the final score, with 30% each. Other categories include industry income (2.5%) and international outlook (7.5%).
