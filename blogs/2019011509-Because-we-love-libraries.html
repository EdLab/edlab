Because we love libraries...

Christine.Odenath

January 15, 2019

This American Life recently produced a podcast on libraries! The official title is "The Room of Requirement" - and yes, that's a HP reference.  The podcast highlights a variety of library spaces around the country and reminds us of the library's dynamic purpose. All the stories are pretty heartwarming and inspiring, which is very in-brand for This American Life but feels worth noting for new listeners. 


You can listen via the linked podcast title above, on iTunes, Spotify, or wherever you get your podcasts.
