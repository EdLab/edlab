What's Your Attitude Toward Change?

Oumar Soumahoro

July 8, 2015

As Maya Angelou once said “If one is lucky, a solitary fantasy can turn into a million realities.” Luck might be a key factor into turning one’s fantasy into reality; however, having the right plan could save a lot of time and efforts. As stated in the video, the odds against someone making change in his or her life are 9 to 1; regardless of the situation he or she is in. In other words, change could be considered as one of the most unwelcome episodes in a person’s life. I found this Ted Talk to be very useful, as it describes a couple of steps anyone could follow in order to facilitate transition in his/her life and beat the odds established by prior research. What would be your first step toward change? Please join the conversation below and share your thoughts on the subject.
