Marcel Saucet

Nantwi

August 23, 2017


Dr. Marcel Saucet is the founder and CEO of LCA Learning, an organization engaged in a host of learning activities. His most recent book, Street Marketing: The Future of Guerrilla Marketing and Buzz, was named the USA Best Book Award in Business & Advertising category. He is currently a visiting professor at UC San Diego School of Law.

How did your education and previous professional experience shape your current work at LCA Learning?

I have always wanted to learn, create, and share. While working on my doctoral thesis, I learned many things about marketing and innovation, and I created a few new innovative concepts. That led me to found my own company "LCA Consulting" in order to share my discoveries with others.

How do you hope your work at LCA Learning will change the way parents and students approach the college application process?

By using facial recognition to detect when a student is not focused, and by changing questions of the exam to make sure that all skills are well assimilated, NESTOR will help students whose parents may not be able to help them and, by doing so, I feel it will change the college application process into a more fair-minded system.

What broad trends do you think will have the most impact on learning in the years ahead?

What else are you working on in addition to LCA Learning?

As I mentioned earlier, my goal is to "learn, create, and share." I have written books and researched articles about Brand psychoanalysis and I founded LCA Consulting to share my discoveries with others. I also did research about innovation in marketing. My book "Street Marketing: the Future of Guerrilla Marketing and Buzz" won the 2015 Best Book Award in the business category, and I created my own advertising agency, "Street Marketing." I also created SM&B, a teaching organization based in Dubai, with world class accreditations. Our students earn their degrees from highly qualified professors, and teachers.
