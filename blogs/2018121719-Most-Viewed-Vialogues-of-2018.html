Most Viewed Vialogues of 2018

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2018


Throughout the year, these vialogues have highlighted new ways of learning, teaching, and creating in both educational and real-world environments. Here’s a collection of our most popular features this year.

1. A School Built on Suprise and Delight
Eminence Independent Schools in Eminence, Kentucky is reinventing their schools, from the furniture to the curriculum to the hallways themselves, to encourage surprise and delight.

2. What is Blockchain and Why is it Important?
Institute of the Future provides a simple and accessible overview of Blockchain technology, its current uses, and its potential in the future.

3. Learning Through Hip Hop With Flocabulary
The Director of Production and the VP of Learning at Flocabulary discuss how their platform contributes to a culturally responsive classroom through representation, social and emotional learning, and student voice.

4. VR Helps Seniors Explore the World Beyond the Assisted Living Center
Visual demonstrates how their VR technology is helping senior citizens reclaim their sense of adventure and wonder even as they lose their mobility.

5. Spatial Intelligence with Dan Ness, Steve Farenga, and Salvatore Garofalo
Researchers at St. John’s University and City University of New York at Queens College discuss issues of spatial cognition and spatial relations in spaces for teaching and learning.
