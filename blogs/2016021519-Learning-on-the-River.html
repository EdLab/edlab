Learning on the River

Jenny Shen

February 15, 2016


Obviously you can learn about science in the classroom, online, and at museums, but what about a river? Reconnecting to our Waterways is an initiative that is designed to prompt people to think about everything from the flora and fauna of the area to the ways in which to engineer paddles that move the water upstream.
