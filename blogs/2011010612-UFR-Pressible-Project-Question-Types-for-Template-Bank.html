UFR Pressible Project: Question Types for Template Bank?

Jessica Blum

January 6, 2011

After today's D&R meeting (thank you to our wonderful guest-presenters, Patrick & Eric!), our team was challenged to develop 10 question "types" (with corresponding examples) to help provide teachers with template options for creating posts. For example, what type of questions might teachers want to ask students in their classes (short answer, multiple choice, true/false, opinion polls, etc.)? How would the teacher pose these questions, and what should student responses look like? Chingfu, Hui Soo and I will be generating ideas and examples and sharing them in the comments below, and we invite (implore?) you to add your suggestions as well. All help is appreciated, but we'll need your ideas by Monday evening so we can bring our feedback to the next D&R meeting. Thanks in advance for any and all ideas!
