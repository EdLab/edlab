Social Media And Education

SambaDou

September 27, 2016

Social media and higher education don’t often go hand in hand. The assumption is that social media’s fun and sometimes unruly style doesn’t fit the rigor and intensity required for success for in higher ed. Snapchat, more than any other social media, perhaps captures this contrast. The app is especially popular with teenagers. However, there is a surprisingly high number of higher ed institutions using the app in various forms. Most institutions see the platform as a great way to recruit students, enhance their brand and increase community engagement. With over 100 million daily users, the audience is certainly there for higher ed.
