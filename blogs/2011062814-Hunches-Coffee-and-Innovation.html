Hunches, Coffee & Innovation

Kate Meersschaert

June 28, 2011

Please view the following Vialogue, How Coffee Houses Led to the Enlightenment, and share here how you feel EdLab encourages Eureka! moments through our culture of collaboration and innovation. How does EdLab encourage the "liquid network" in which great ideas bump-up against each other and agitate one another to create more energy and scaffold our thoughts? For example, Google allows 12% flex time for personal ideation or "hunch" time. The last example in the vialogue explains how GPS was developed by "hunches" & "needs" connecting to create something new. This is an amazing example of the value of open platforms for innovation! What open platforms have we developed that encourage these "needs" and "hunches" uniting to create something new and game-changing? "Chance favors the connected mind!" - Steven Johnson
