D&R- Vialoguin'

jvazquez

November 20, 2012

Hey D&R! As you all know, one of the streams on NLT is Vialogues. With this stream, we highlight and describe a vialogue we feel embodies the spirit of these crazy new learning times. Sometimes, though, while the content of the video is STELLAR, the comments are severely lacking. This is where you come in! Today, we'll all be taking this opportunity to watch and discuss pre-approved vialogues that may be featured in an upcoming NLT article. Your mission, if you choose to accept it (hahaha who am I kidding? You have no choice!), is to create captivating comments and engaging debates on these pre-approved vialogues. I've separated you all into 2 groups that will each attack one list of vialogues. Find your name below and get to chattin'! Also, after this meeting, if you come across any interesting videos that you think would make a great "Vialogues on NLT" article, post the link to the vialogue (or just embed it) as a comment to this post. Of course, this isn't necessary: If you create quality, NLT-relevant vialogues, I PROMISE you I will find them myself and I will pitch them.
