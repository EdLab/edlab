Online Credit Courses @ $150

Ashish Mehta

April 18, 2013

Today, I received an e-mail newsletter from â€˜Udacity', an online learning platform that offers transferable college credit courses. The e-mail consisted of four online courses that'll help students gain entry-level credits when they apply to American universities for higher education. A few months ago, when Sebastian Thrun, Udacity's founder, announced that masters degrees will be available for $100, it seemed like a far fetched goal but with the introduction of online summer courses in affiliation with San Jose State University, the path to $100 masters degree seems to have narrowed down. The following credit courses, if successful, are likely to give rise to a large number of other online entry-level credit courses, helping students who wish to enroll themselves in degree programs in the near future. Each of the summer courses below (affiliated to San Jose State University) will cost $150:

  • Intro to programming
  • Intro to Psychology
  • Elementary Statistics
  • College Algebra
