The World Is Flat:Updated and Expanded

Frank Webster

April 20, 2006

Thomas Friedman has published an updated edition of his best-seller that was first published just a year ago.

Hear him discuss the new forms of publishing, including ipod and wiki, and his new adivce to parents: What do I tell my kid?

He emphasizes: (1) the new middle jobs for the new middle class; (2) the education needed for those jobs; and (3) uses Georgia Tech as an example of where to get that education.

He also promotes "Green" as the new red, white and blue.
