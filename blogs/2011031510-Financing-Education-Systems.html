Financing Education Systems

Alexander Munson

March 15, 2011

Title: Financing Education Systems Authors: Baker, B, Green, P, & Richards, C. Publisher: Prentice Hall (September 24, 2007) Check it out: LB2825 .B25 2008 From the publisher: This book provides a comprehensive overview of the interdisciplinary field of school finance, while establishing a firm understanding of the relationship between school finance systems to their broader economic, political and sociological context. 1). Organization: This book is organized around (a) context, (b) equity, (c) adequacy and (d) productivity and efficiency. 2). Research Base: This book is based on the best available and most up-to-date empirical research by leading scholars across the various fields related to school finance policy. 3).Simulation Activities: This book includes numerous spreadsheet simulation and data analysis activities.  The authors have developed user friendly simulations with thorough documentation regarding the use and underlying assumptions of the simulation. 4).Companion Website: Includes up-to-date, downloadable versions of all chapter simulations.
