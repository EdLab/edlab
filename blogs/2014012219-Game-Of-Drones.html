Game Of Drones

Brian.Sweeting

January 22, 2014


Last month the Federal Aviation Administration granted airspace to Rutgers, The University of Maryland, and Virginia Tech (among several other institutions) to test unmanned aircraft for educational opportunities. As more universities rush to experiment with drones in engineering, agriculture, conservation, geography, and journalism, the game is on to determine who will be the first to claim new territory and new breakthroughs in learning.
