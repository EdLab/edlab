Vialogues November Usage Report

Nikita.Bogdanov

December 13, 2018

The November usage report for Vialogues is now live. The big takeaways are as follows:


  1. Engagement: Overall, Vialogues usage has decreased in November of this year as compared to November of last. The decreases are modest but seem to be larger than would result from random variation. We should look into use and engagement in more detail, perhaps deploying surveys or using fillable entries on upload or login to learn more about visitors’ experiences.
  2. Referral: The proportion of referrals as compared with direct entries has increased for November of this year. Referrals account for about half of all Vialogues traffic and many direct users to specific videos, as these videos are either a part of a lesson or related to the original site. It may be worthwhile to look into partnering with sites to increase referrals.
  3. Layout: Users generally leave the site after visiting just several pages. Re-organizing site layout and employing recommendation algorithms might keep users engaging for longer.


Check out the linked report for more detail, and as always, comment below or let me know if you have any questions.
