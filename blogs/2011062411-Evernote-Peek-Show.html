Evernote "Peek" Show!

Kate Meersschaert

June 24, 2011

Evernote just introduced a new flashcard-like app for Apple's second gen iPad, Peek. Studying has always been a less than pleasurable, so any new tool that adds some semblance of a "game layer" seems like a great idea... or at least a good start to moving the studying needle in a more interesting & engaging direction. Peek relies on the iPad 2's magnetic "fridge-style" cover, and you literally test yourself by ever-so-slightly lifting the cover to "peek" at the right answer after venturing a guess. You can auto-generate questions from the titles of notes in your Evernote library. Supposedly there are hacks already in the works to make the original iPad work in a similar manner... Evernote has also recently paired with StudyBlue.com , a device agnostic flashcard generation group, to create online flashcards for non-ipad 2 users.
