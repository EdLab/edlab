Parting is such sweet sorrow. Thank you EdLab!

Diana Diroy

July 28, 2016

Last week the video team ventured off to the Adriondack Mountains to shoot the lumberjack school at Paul Smith’s College. It was quite a bittersweet trip, because it actually was my last Seen in New York shoot! You may or may not know, but tomorrow is my last day at EdLab :(...... I can’t believe its been nearly 4 years since I walked into those EdLab doors . Since then, I have met some incredibly talented and brilliant people, and I’ve learned so much during my time here. This has seriously been an amazing experience, and I got to work with the best team. From shooting in places like the Grand Canyon to Coney Island’s Sideshow School, I have had the opportunity to meet individuals doing some amazing and inspiring things in the world of education. EdLab is such a special place. It has truly been a pleasure. Thank you, I love you guys very much. I have a lot more to say, but let’s save it for when I see you ;) KEEP IN TOUCH!!!!
