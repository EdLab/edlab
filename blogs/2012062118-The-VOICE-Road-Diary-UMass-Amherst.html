The VOICE Road Diary: UMass Amherst

Kate Meersschaert

June 21, 2012

We made it to UMass around 3:00 after about two hours on the road and quickly realized that our original destination, Hill Hall, would not work: No AC! So we secured a new spot at Gordon Hall (see pics below). Gordon houses both the Political Economy Research Institute and the Center for Public Policy and Administration (CPPA). Kathryn McDermott, our interviewee, has dual appointments with both the School of Ed and CPPA. The interview with Professor McDermott was a special challenge as the atrium space where we held the interview was public and as such, noise was a constant factor Ariel and Josh had to work with. Also, the lighting in the atrium was natural and provided by massive skylights, which posed unique challenges and perhaps will provide an especially beautiful finished product. Note: If you plan to travel to UMass... arrange for guest access to the campus WiFi! There is no way to access this without pre-approval through a faculty member.
