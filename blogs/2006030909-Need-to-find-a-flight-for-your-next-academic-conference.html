Need to find a flight for your next academic conference?

Anna Beard

March 9, 2006

Last week I discovered kayak.com. This is the best online travel booking site I've used to date. Kayak allows users to search a number of budget airlines the other online booking sites do not. Plus, it allows users to enter extremely specific search criteria (such as, "I want to depart between 6:42am and 9:13am on any airline but ..."). Not to sound too commericial, but the sky is the limit with kayak!
