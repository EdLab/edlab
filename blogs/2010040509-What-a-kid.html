What a kid

Arianna Choi

April 5, 2010

Twelve-year-old Adora Svitak is an exceptional speaker, a blogger, and since age seven she has been "a prolific short story writer." After speaking at TED Long Beach, she decided to organize her own TEDx event in Redmond, WA. Interesting and impressive. How would you like to have a kid like her live next door to you?
