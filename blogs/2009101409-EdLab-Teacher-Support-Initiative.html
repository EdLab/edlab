EdLab Teacher Support Initiative

Jessica Blum

October 14, 2009

A number of recent — and not so recent — developments suggest that NOW is a great time for EdLab/TC to contribute to the teacher preparation/support initiatives underway nationwide. Given Secretary Duncan's upcoming talk, as well as the recent grant to support TC's Urban Teaching Residents Program, I don't think it's an overstatement to say that we are poised on the brink of an important opportunity. While I have some strong opinions about the potential shapes and directions of such a resource (please feel free to explore my emerging thinking in this draft proposal), I am extremely interested to learn what others find MOST ESSENTIAL for new (and veteran) teacher support, and I eagerly welcome ideas about how to best utilize/integrate technology in this project. In other news, Hui Soo recently pointed out these three teacher support courses already on Moodle (in the Lifelong Learning section, from previous initiatives), and it would be really helpful to hear what you think of them (in particular, do you have any feedback about the delivery interface?). FYI: the enrollment key for “The Inspirational Teacher” is “coveycovey.” If you have strong opinions about this project (and I hope you do!), interest in collaborating, or stories/feedback to share, please post a comment or stop by my station. I really look forward to learning from you and your experiences.
