Silicon Alley Posts More Edtech Jobs than the Valley?

Kate Meersschaert

June 18, 2014

Edsurge recently released a report exploring the job market of various edtech hubs in the hopes of sussing out which area is "top" in the field as far as job growth. Using their own job boards as their data source the group has (not surprisingly?) confirmed that Silicon Alley & Valley top the list. With NYC edging-out CA for most postings, but CA winning for size of region represented (?). What is your take on the data? This is especially interesting to our work with LaunchPad sussing-out the elements that differentiate and influence edtech development on both coasts (look-out for the mSchool course that includes this comparison soon!).
