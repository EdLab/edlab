Biopsychology

Christopher Zamora

April 7, 2013

One of the classes I truly enjoy in my school, is Biopsychology. What is Biopsychology? Well it's the study of how emotions, thoughts and behaviors are affected by the brain, the nervous system and neurotransmitters in human and non-human animals. In this class, we did several experiments that test our emotions, thoughts and behaviors. One experiment we did was called "Tasty Visions" in which we had different color drinks and one of the drinks was food dye to a different color. (All of the students had to taste every color drink.) The color was purple. Many students in my class were fooled and thought that the taste was grape, but turns out the flavor was fruit punch. With this experiment we learned that taste can be influenced by the color of food and/or drink. So has anyone had this class? If so, did you enjoy it?
