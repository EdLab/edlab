Hacking Education Round 2

Kate Meersschaert

July 1, 2011

This summer (most likely in August) Baltimore will play host to a two-day Education Hack Day. The focus of this hack-fest is on "building tools to help teachers and schools." For more info, check-out this site and submit your email for more details. Also, if Baltimore isn't in your summer travel plans, you can visit this page to add to an "teacher wish-list" for hacks and if you are more dev-focused, this one, to discuss potential hacks. Also, Edu Hack Day is also coming to NYC in September! Add your email to this siteto receive an invite. Check-out Kevin's recent post to explore a NYC-based Edu Hack day at General Assembly this past June.
