The Ideal Learning Environment

jvazquez

December 16, 2013


If there is such a thing as an academic treat, then educators at the University of Melbourne have created one for their students. They have transformed learning into an experience by designing an assortment of spaces that cater to students’ and teachers’ every educational need.

In designing these spaces, designers and educators looked to create flexible environments that were conducive to collaboration. This means multiple circular tables, chairs with wheels, and comfortable furniture to read on, for starters. Special attention was also given to re-locatable classrooms, so that the classes utilizing these would consider them less a makeshift space and more a maker space. Above all, the designers wanted to create environments that allowed students to take an active lead in their learning practices by taking advantage of the technological innovations at their disposal.
