Second Look

Anthony Cocciolo

May 4, 2007

I thought I would share my class project for Prof. Kinzer's course on the Possibilities of Virtual Worlds. The name of the project is Second Look, which is a research platform for Second Life. Between the the NED project that Matthew is leading up, and the smarter survey tool that Nabeel is heading up, surveys have been on my mind! To use Second Look, drop the Second Look plaque onto your space in Second Life, and click on it to begin designing the survey. After you have designed your survey, users can click the plaque and begin taking the survey. The owner of the survey can click on it to view the participant results.
