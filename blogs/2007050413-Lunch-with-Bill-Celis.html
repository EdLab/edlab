Lunch with Bill Celis

Julia Solomonson

May 4, 2007

Jeff and I were taken to lunch today by Bill Celis, a professor of journalism at USC's Annenberg School of Communication. Celis wrote this commentary for the Record, and is interested in working further with the Record and the TC Channel. He might make a good seminar speaker at the Edlab when he comes to NY again. He is teaching a course on education reporting this fall, is the author of Battle Rock: The Struggle Over a One-Room School in America's Vanishing West and has another book forthcoming with the SUNY Press.
