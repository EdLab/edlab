Ingenuity Beyond Technology

Jenny Abamu

December 20, 2016


Experiments in transformational learning go beyond the creation of new classroom apps. These choice vialogues highlight tech-less innovation:

1. Leveraging the power of hip hop therapy
Straight outta the Bronx counselor, Ian Levy, helps students heal through hip-hop.

2. Art, Assimilation, and Asylum
Canadian NGO creates a safe space for refugees to learn English through art.

3. Turning Comics Into Academics
Marvel and Comic Con are now part of an American subculture, and educators are using comics to offer new educational expression.

4. Survivor: The Journey Filipino Students Make to School
Filipino students often travel miles from island to island, through rough terrain to get to school. However, now there’s a better way with the creation of ‘school bus boats.’

5. The Girls Who Are Breaking Norms in Egypt
The Maadi STEM School in Egypt is fighting hard for girls education with an emphasis on science and technology.
