Leaving the EdLab

Anthony Cocciolo

May 14, 2009

I just wanted to share that I will be leaving the EdLab at the end of summer and will be joining the faculty of the School of Information and Library Science at the Pratt Institute in Manhattan. There I will be focusing on research and teaching, particularly in the area of emerging information and communications technologies in education, museums and libraries. It has been a wonderful 7 years at Teachers College (5 of which were at the EdLab). Thank you to everyone I have worked with over the years, especially those I have worked the most closely with, particularly Gary, Brian, Hui Soo, Jeff, Maureen, Jennifer, Frank, Christine and Pravin. I am very excited to be joining the Pratt. Some interesting things about Pratt SILS is that it is the only ALA accredited program in Manhattan and is the oldest, continually accredited ALA program in the US. Some of the interesting things going on at Pratt SILS is they just started a Dual-Degree, MSLIS & MFA Digital Arts program. There are also a number of study-abroad programs, including ones in London and Florence. Sound good? Here's the Admissions website :-)
