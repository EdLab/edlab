Workshops at Educational Data Mining Conference

Alvaro.Ortiz-Vazquez

June 16, 2017

Next week I will be departing to Wuhan, China to attend the international Educational Data Mining (EDM) conference at the Normal University of Central China. There will be 3 days of conference in which I will present our very own study of Quuppa data titled, Cluster Analysis of Real Time Location Data- An Application of Gaussian Mixture Models


For the 4th day in Wuhan I have registered to attend workshops led by researchers who are participating in both the EDM conference and a later conference called the Artificial Intelligence in Education Conference. There are many interesting topics being covered and there may be many which would be relevant or useful to the EdLab. Some of them include:


  • Graph-based Educational Data Mining
