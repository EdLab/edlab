Pundit v 2.0 is Ready for Internal Testing!

Kate Meersschaert

November 11, 2011

Pundit v 2.0 is ready for internal testing! Please explore the tool before our grand-unveiling of the new features to the world middle of next week. Visit the dev site for now. Listed below are the shiny new features for you to explore in this newest Pundit iteration: 1) Social media integration: Twitter and Facebook "like" and "tweet" buttons for each section of a course listed on the course pages. 2) Export Favorites to Email: send your list of favorites from your starred favorites page to the email address associated with your account and/or a friend or advisor's. Please note that the email will be from an Ednode account for now, as we are awaiting a dedicated Pundit email address, so please only send initial emails to yourself to avoid confusion. 3) Help video integration: Screencast-based help videos of both an overview of Pundit features and the benefits of logging-in will be featured on the homepage, in the help section listed in the footer and featured throughout the site in a sidebar. 4) Spring 2012 Courses: Coming soon! This update is still in-progress and more involved than we had initially anticipated. We hope to have all Spring 2012 courses in the system and all courses correctly sorted by midweek. Feel free to add your initial thoughts on these new features, report bugs and generally share promotional ideas and thoughts in this post! Thanks for your help with initial testing & MANY THANKS to the hard-work of the Pundit Development & Design team aka Pranav, Stephen & Basak!
