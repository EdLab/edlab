Learning Analytics Seminar: Learning Analytics for Generating Intelligent Information in Heterogeneous Educational Enviroment

Sharon Hsiao

July 15, 2013

Learning Analytics Seminar Series Title: Learning Analytics for Generating Intelligent Information in Heterogeneous Educational Environments July 22, 2pm, Thompson Hall 229 Speaker: Pedro MuÃ±oz-Merino Abstract: Nowadays, there is an overload of data in different learning systems. Although many low level data is usually present, it is not always easy for teachers, students and other stakeholders to use and interpret this data and reach useful conclusions for the learning process. In heterogeneous educational environments, different platforms provide different contexts and functionality, which condition the type of data that can be collected and the information about the learning process that can be inferred. This intelligent information should be useful and presented to teachers and students in a clear and precise way, stating clearly the exact meaning of the information, e.g. with visualizations or recommendations. This talk exposes different examples of generation of relevant information for the learning process from heterogeneous educational systems. Specifically, different works will be presented for generating useful and intelligent information from low level data for platforms such as Google Course Builder, Khan Academy, a competition system, or a distributed environment in a course. Some relevant information that can be inferred in each case will be presented, as well as the differences of possible information depending on the educational platform. Moreover, some possible visualizations and recommendations will be shown, as well as the way to process the low level data to obtain intelligent information and the different meaning depending on the type of processing. A complete case study at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid will be presented using the Khan Academy platform. This case study took place in year 2012 for a course in Physics. Results of this study will be shown based on the generated intelligent information. There will be a reflection about the connection of the intelligent information with the learning process and how to take advantage of it. Bio: Pedro J.
