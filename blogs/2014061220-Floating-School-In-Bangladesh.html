Floating School In Bangladesh

Carmen James

June 12, 2014


"We are the ground zero of climate change" a man shares in the video above, the rising waters visible behind him. The next shot is of a farmer who says he has lost 40 acres of land in three years. The reality of rising water levels threatens the existence of communities in Bangladesh. It has destroyed farms, homes, and schools.

One visionary architect imagines a new possibility for a school: one that floats, fully outfitted with technology, to the children. Learn more about this moving and impressive design in the trailer for the documentary Easy Like Water. What do you think of the design? What other adaptations might we have to make to schools in the future?
