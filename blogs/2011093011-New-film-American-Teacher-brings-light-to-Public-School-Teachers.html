New film "American Teacher" brings light to Public School Teachers

Julia Martin

September 30, 2011

Democracy Now introduces this new film, American Teacher. Find links below to other coverage of this movie. It's an effort from Matt Damon, who's a known supporter of public educators, and Dave Eggers, who co-authored a similarly themed book. It's showing in New York this week if anyone wants to go.
