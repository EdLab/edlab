Rumblings...

Molly Riordan

February 9, 2010

We've been hearing rumblings from various people. Not scary, thunderous rumblings; more like hungry stomachs. People are hungry for two things: customization options especially for the header, and search functionality. As I mentioned earlier, we're adding a cool feature that populates header-image boxes with images from the site. That will be a first step toward a customized header. Instead of choosing the image, though, the images are chosen because you've chosen them previously! It follows with the whole Pressible philosophy. Search is a horse of a different color, but no less important. We're thinking we'll bump this into the 1.1 release. Obviously we're not going to reinvent the wheel. We'll just use a wheel to get people where they want to go.
