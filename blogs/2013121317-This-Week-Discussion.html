Bachir Diarrassouba

December 13, 2013

This week we discussed the proposal regarding the collection of college professor email. We decided to put this on hold, since we're heading into the end of the semester. We plan on carrying on this proposal on the beginning of next year. This week Eylsee recruited one new user from school, his friend was interested in the Young Arts and Master class so he signed up to the site. Ahmed recruited two new users from Hunter College and had them sign up to Vialogues. Ahmed collected emails from Professors that taught Economics in CUNY. He sent out 50 emails and two professors replied to his emails. Reindorf posted three videos and recruited one new user from his building. She was interested in one of his videos that discussed STEM students. Bismark recruited three new users from his church and posted four videos this week.
