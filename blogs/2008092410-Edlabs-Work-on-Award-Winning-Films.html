Edlab's Work on Award Winning Films

Nantwi

September 24, 2008

John Adams' sweep of major Emmy categories last Sunday means that the EdLab, through its collaboration with HBO, has worked on the following award winning films. Way to go EdLab!!! John Adams (2008): Emmy for Outstanding Miniseries Emmy for Outstanding Writing for a Miniseries or Emmy for Movie Outstanding Lead Actor in a Miniseries or Movie Emmy for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Miniseries or Emmy for Movie Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Miniseries or Movie Emmy for Outstanding Art Direction for a Miniseries or Movie Emmy for Outstanding Casting for a Miniseries, Movie or a Special Emmy for Outstanding Cinematography For A Miniseries or Movie (Episode 2, Independence) Emmy for Outstanding Costumes for a Miniseries, Movie or a Special Emmy for Outstanding Prosthetic Makeup for a Series, Miniseries, Movie or a Special Emmy for Outstanding Sound Editing for a Miniseries, Movie or a Special (Episode 3, Don't Tread On Me) Emmy for Outstanding Sound Mixing for a Miniseries or a Movie (Episode 3, Don't Tread On Me) Emmy for Outstanding Special Visual Effects for a Miniseries, Movie or a Special Recount: Emmy for Best Made-for-TV Movie Emmy for Directing for a Miniseries, Movie or a Dramatic Special Emmy for Single-Camera Picture Editing for a Miniseries or Movie Ghosts of Abu Ghraib (2007): Emmy for Outstanding Non-Fiction Special Iron Jawed Angels (2004): Golden Globe for Best Actress in a Supporting Role (Series, Mini-Series or Television Movie) When the Levees Broke (2007): Emmy for Exceptional Merit in Nonfiction Filmmaking Emmy for Outstanding Directing for Nonfiction Emmy Programming Outstanding Picture Editing for Nonfiction Programming
