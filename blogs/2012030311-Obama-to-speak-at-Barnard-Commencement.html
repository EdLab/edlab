Obama to speak at Barnard Commencement

Manav Malhotra

March 3, 2012

private President Obama has agreed to give this year's commencement address at Barnard. Between President Obama, Secretary Clinton, Mayor Bloomberg, and Sheryl Sandberg, Barnard seems to have a the best Class Day Speaker Getter at the University (though the White House offered President Obama's services this year). The College and SEAS haven't been too bad, just eclipsed. The only thing I can remember about our class day speaker and his speech is that he was a partner at Goldman Sachs.
