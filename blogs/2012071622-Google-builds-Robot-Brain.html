Google builds Robot Brain

Costa Lales

July 16, 2012

The human brain is the center of the human nervous system. It executes functions such as self-control, planning, reasoning, and abstract thought. The brain is the center of cognitive thought, its size and abilities is what separates us from animals. However, all of this will soon change because Google is creating an artificial brain made out of 16,000 computers processors. This is in order to create a brain style neuron network. After building this robotic brain, Google set the computer brain down in front of an internet connection and fed it multiple images from YouTube videos to see what it will learn. What did this artificial brain learn from these multiple videos on YouTube? It learned about cats. The idea was for the computer brain to be able to learn on its own when it's presented with a YouTube video through a process called machine learning. The machine was fed random thumbnails extracted from 10 million videos on YouTube and after searching through the thumbnail the computer brain was able to determine what a cat was. Dr. Dean, one of the Google designers, said “We never told it during the training, ‘This is a cat,' “It basically invented the concept of a cat. We probably have other ones that are side views of cats.” So guys do you think this is a super intelligent brain or do you think it's just a machine that identifies cats? I think this is a start in making computers that are able to learn about their surroundings.
