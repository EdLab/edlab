Blogging for Dollars, Viewer Content and TC Channel

Frank Webster

July 21, 2007

This BusinessWeek feature story points out that: (1) A blog that relies heavily on reader contributions can be a runaway success; and (2) Once the number of hits roll in, advertising dollars follow: Bloggers Bring in the Big Bucks: "How a personal obsession can turn into a popular favorite and maybe even a full-time job...." Can any or this be applied to the TC Channel? Imagine a segment every week with content from viewers who contribute their own ed news reports. Fox News is already doing it with uReport. What corporations would be most likely to advertise on TC Channel? (Maybe HGSE would take out an ad. LOL!) What would be the ethical issues?
