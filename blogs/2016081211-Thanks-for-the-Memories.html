Thanks for the Memories

Joshua Johnson

August 12, 2016

I’ve never lived in a big city before. In fact, I’ve never lived in a city at all. Then I moved to Manhattan. It was my New York adventure. I rented a basement apartment in Times Square, crushed a cockroach in my shoe, and learned that if you wait for the white “walk” symbol, you’ll never get anywhere on time. Oh, and I spent nine weeks as a Theater Production Intern at EdLab — a job I will never forget. How could I? Even if I wanted to forget, I have two EdLab sweatshirts, a notebook, a T-shirt, and two mugs reminding me of this funky, colorful office on the 5th floor of the TC library. So trust me. I will not forget. Thank you, EdLab. Thank you for accepting me, learning my name, telling me your stories, wearing my silly Xenotopia masks, and making my New York summer adventure so enjoyable. Hopefully I’ll see you all again soon. Thanks for the memories.
