EdLab Research Updates

Sharon Hsiao

January 16, 2013

We are very pleased to announce that we got four papers and a poster accepted for the 6th Annual International Symposium for Emerging Technologies for Online Learning. This is an exciting news, and just in time for the NLT launch celebration. We look forward to promoting the systems and exchanging ideas at the conference. The research team will schedule a development and research meeting sometime in March before the conference and share the idea, study and results with you. Congratulations to all of us :) Data Depository: A Tracking System to Provide Business Intelligence and Learning Analytics for Educational Web Applications Survey Sidekick: Teaching Scientifically-Sound Survey Design Through Embedded Learning Resources proposal Effective Coordination Strategies for Video Discussion in Collaborative Learning New Learning Times: Learning about Learning on the Go Connecting Teacher Bloggers: Unleashing The Educational Power of Wordpress
