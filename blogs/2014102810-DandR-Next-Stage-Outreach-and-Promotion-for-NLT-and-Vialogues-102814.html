D&R: Next Stage Outreach & Promotion for NLT & Vialogues, 10.28.14

Kate Meersschaert

October 28, 2014

Hi Everyone! Please find your group's activities listed below! Please also record your ideas in the comments before our group share-out. You will have about 20 minutes to complete this activity. Excited for your brilliant thoughts and ideas! Group #1: Read this article Group #1 Actiivity: Devise 3 social media campaigns for NLT and 3 for vialogues. (You can use Twitter, FB & or G+) What is the goal of these campaigns? What might a tweet or FB post be comprised of? Who is your target audience? How long will the campaign run? Is there a "#" associated? How will we assess success?

Group #2: Read this article Group #2 Activity: Draft a plan for an in-person event here at EdLab. Who should attend? What will they do? What is the promotional goal of the event? How will we assess success?

Group # 3: Read this article Group #3 Activity: How might we best outreach to teachers here in NYC? At TC? Please share 10 actionable ideas for reaching this group of potential users/readers both in NYC and here at TC. How would you promote this event and what messaging would you use in promotional material? How will you assess success?

Group #4: Read this article Group #4 Activity: Brainstorm 5 new ways to share NLT content via a newsletter (theme, design etc.). Then 5 ideas for Vialogues newsletter themes (Bullying was this mo's ex.). How will you assess the success of these newsletters? Who do you most hope to reach for each one? Thank you!!!
