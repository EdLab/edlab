Virtual Reality Meets Vialogues

saraehardman

May 31, 2016

In light of my most recent blog post, it's only fitting that I should be working on two vialogues articles about virtual reality. The particular headset featured in both of these videos is the HTC Vive, a revolutionary form of VR that allows users to interact with their computer-generated surroundings. The people who created the HTC Vive think it will transform education and storytelling and develop user empathy. If you're interested in this technology, check out the vialogues below. I think they're definitely worth a few minutes of your time! And, if you do watch them, please leave a comment.
