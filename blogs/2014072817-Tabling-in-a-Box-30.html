Tabling in a Box 3.0

Kate Meersschaert

July 28, 2014

Hi Everyone! For the past few months I have played around with the idea of a "tabling in a box" outreach experience that would help us to solicit interest in our work (and in Vialogues.com, in particular). To do this I devised a plan involving "ballot boxes" that passers-by can insert their business cards into to receive more information about our work (even TC students have business cards w/ their program info). This new evolution of our previous in-person "tabling" efforts will potentially solve the following issues and help us meet our goal of deeper engagement:

  • People don't want to take the time to engage with a human, as they are often in a rush on their way to or from class.
  • It would be valuable to gather contact information via business cards so that we may personalize our follow-up email to invite engagement based on role or affiliation.
  • We want to recruit users who WANT to use our tools, not people who we flag-down and ask to sign-up.
  • This effort requires "0" team hours to maintain, however, the follow-up email will require team lift (Mason).
Please add your thoughts on this self-service tabling model below! Also, please find images of the current prototype as well as the past mock-up! (Thanks for the production assistance Gonzalo!) Would you be enticed to leave your card? Why or why not? V.1 V.2 V.3
