Interactive Transcripts + HTML5 Video Player Demo For Vialogues

zhou

March 13, 2015

--- KEEP PRIVATE --- On April 14, we will have a D&R workshop to gain hands-on experiences on two HTML5 players we are considering as the backbone of the next-gen Vialogues Player. The two candidates are Flowplayer 5 and JWPlayer. Flowplayer 5 is the latest HTML5 version of the Flash Flowplayer, which is a component of the current Vialogues Player. The advantage of Flowplayer 5 is it's open source, and a lot less expensive than JWPlayer. JWPlayer is the world's most popular web video player. It has more features, larger user community, and wider choices of plugins. Of course it's a lot more expensive than Flowplayer 5. But it also has better customer support which is included in the annual subscription fee. Below is a brief comparison of the two candidates: Prior to the D&R, if any of you feel interested, please test the demos in the Vialogues dev site: JWPlayer Demo: http://vlg-cloud.tc-library.org/vialogues/play3/12 Flowplayer 5 Demo: http://vlg-cloud.tc-library.org/vialogues/play2/12 These are rough demo implementations so there might be bugs (for example, YouTube and Vimeo don't work yet for the demos. You have to upload your own video). But I have tried to make most typical Vialogues functionalities work. In the JWPlayer demo, you will find there is a new tab below the player called TRANSCRIPTS. This is to show what Interactive Transcripts look like in our next-gen Vialogues Player. I only implemented interactive transcripts for JWPlayer because it has a ready-made tutorial. Flowplayer 5 supports interactive transcripts as well. Please tell me how well you like the two players and the interactive transcripts feature. Also, you can suggest what other features you would like to experience in April 14 D&R workshop. I'll see if I have time to put up a quick demo. Thank you for your time!
