Possible partnership/collaboration ideas re: Watson/IBM Research & EdLab/TC: -Quantify research already created at TC and find-out if there are overlaps or holes in our expertise and then conduct additional research with IBM based on these findings. -Use Watson to farm-out "friction points" involved in matching pre-service teachers with opportunities. -Use Watson to ID areas of need in NYC schools, match these needs to TC/IBM capabilities and partner. -Use Watson as a "resident teacher" on the 4th floor Learning Theater & test interaction with educators & stakeholders here at TC.
