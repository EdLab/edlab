Thank you EdLab!!!!!!

Kate Meersschaert

March 31, 2015

***Private! I can't even begin to express how much the EdLab has meant to me over the past four years! There are so many reasons that this team and our work is special and unique. I will try to sum-up some of the MANY reasons I have loved working here over the years:

  • People. The team at EdLab is made-up of some of the kindest, smartest people I have ever met.
  • Work. We endeavor to always stay true to our mission of nosing-out, developing and celebrating truly innovative learning opportunities.
  • Workplace learning. It's baked-into every interaction and experience.
  • Space. Truly flexible and responsive to everyone and everything.
  • Leadership. Open-door and open-hearted the leadership of EdLab truly cares about the team and our work.
  • Humility. Everyone pulls their weight and sweeps the floors.
There is so much more to say, but, I will only leave you with this simple thought... thank you for being SUCH an important and formative part of my life! I can't wait to see where everyone and all our pipeline projects end-up over the next four years! #TeamEdLabForever!!!!
