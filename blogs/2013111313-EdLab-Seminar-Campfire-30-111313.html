EdLab Seminar: Campfire 3.0, 11.13.13

Kate Meersschaert

November 13, 2013

Group: Dilcia, Claire, Diana & Kate Lesson & Case Study: Our group decided to take Thornburg's "campfire" concept further and want to use our lesson to teach multimedia. Our lesson would focus on using the space to show the trajectory of a story from inciting incident to resolution. This lesson would also integrate documentary storytelling & filmmaking elements. Space Details: The floors will be integrated as on the holodeck. We also propose transparent (glass that can become opaque with titanium dioxide infused, charged elements) "caves" suspended in the corners or sides using the columns. We also feel inexpensive LED lights (hooked up to the HUE app) could be used to change the mood using colors when the screens are not in use as projectors.
