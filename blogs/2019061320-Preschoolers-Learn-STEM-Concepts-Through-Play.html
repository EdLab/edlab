Preschoolers Learn STEM Concepts Through Play

Melanie.Hering

June 13, 2019


For children not even old enough to attend a full day of school, many might think teaching science and technology concepts is far too advanced for their young minds to handle. Now, though, a new program in a New Orleans preschool is proving otherwise.

Educate New Orleans is integrating science, technology, engineering, and math concepts into their students' learning by encouraging play and discovery targeted at growth in these subjects. After extensively training teachers on how best to incorporate and support student-centered STEM learning, the program provides classrooms with tools, from digital microscopes to slime-making kits, for hands-on learning activities. Students who have completed the program all meet or exceed national kindergarten readiness standards both academically and in social-emotional development, proving that their methods might merit widespread adoption.
