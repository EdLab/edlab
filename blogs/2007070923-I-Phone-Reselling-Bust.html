I-Phone Reselling Bust

Minwoo You

July 9, 2007

I-Phone , the biggest hype in modern era of wireless communication, opened up to the public on Friday, June 29 on Apple Stores throughout the nation. People waited in line for days to get their hands on these magnificent pieces of geniuses and many left the store with smiles of pure bliss at the thought of showing off their new gadget to their sphere of Apple loving friends. However, some went further and went off into a business venture of hopeful success. Like many have done for the Playstation 3, Xbox 360, and Nintendo Wii, people bought extra I-Phones to sell on Ebay; eagerly wishing that apple doesn't meet demand. However, their endeavor turned to be an utter failure. The “resellers” of Apple I-Phones has failed to sell their phones with good profit. Some say, that it is even difficult to sell the I-phones with a mere profit of $100, compared to the Playstation 3's that some “resellers” sold with a profit as much as $600. I-Phone reselling has been proved to be a Bust! However, Apple resellers might have another chance at “striking it rich” later this year because Apple plans to launch a smaller “Nano” version of the I-Phone soon.
