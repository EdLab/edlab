Smarter Batteries

Daniel Vergara

January 9, 2013

One of the constant complaints from smartphone users is the length of their battery life. Well there could be a solution on the way. This piece from GOOD notes that though cellphones have and continue to improve, the one thing that has yet to change about them is the battery life. A chemistry professor at Colorado State University, Amy Prieto, has designed a rechargeable battery with twice the energy level of the average smartphone battery. Aptly called the Prieto Battery, it essentially eliminates all harmful chemicals that the old ones use to contain and charges faster. Please check out the video below on the Prieto Battery.
