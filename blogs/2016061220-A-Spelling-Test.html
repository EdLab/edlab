A+ Spelling Test

dinosossi2

June 12, 2016


The assessment era is here and if there isn’t already a test for your learning, there soon will be. Endless testing begs us to answer a simple question: how do we put the fun back in learning, especially for rote exercises like puzzling over the basics of spelling? A+ Spelling Test is an iPad app that helps adults quickly create quizzes for their kids to work toward word mastery.

Pros:

A+ Spelling Test allows parents and teachers to create customized spelling quizzes on an iPad with any words they want. Adults can also easily grade and monitor their child's progress courtesy of the app’s testing function. The app reduces the burden on struggling young wordsmiths by giving them tailored literacy activities and provides concerned teachers and parents with immediate feedback on their kids' spelling progress.

A+ Spelling Test is really easy to use. Just come up with a catchy title to your test, type in a set of desired words (with correct spelling, of course), record audio for these words, hit the test button, and you are good to go. You are now in the quiz creating biz.

Cons:

Although A+ Spelling Test allows users to integrate text with audio, the finished product isn’t much different than those old school, in-class spelling quizzes, where the teacher stood at the front of the class and read from a word list. As such, although this app is technically multimedia in nature, it’s on the stripped-down end of the spectrum.

Our Takeaway:

A+ Spelling Test is a good way for parents and teachers to take some of the pressure from high-stakes in-class spelling quizzes off of anxious learners by replicating testing conditions in the comfort of their own homes. It can replace the harsh tones of the all-knowing sage on the stage with someone more relaxed, familial, and able to adopt amusing accents, if they have that kind of voiceover talent.
