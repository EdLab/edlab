Research on Labs

HSC

November 29, 2017

Thank you for your help investigating research groups, skunks works, innovation centers, and other organizational units focused on transformation. 


Please post resources about your “lab” in the comments section below. 


Additionally, please write words/phrases that best describe the “organization” on the post-it pads. Your words/phrases might describe: 


- How the unit is organized


- The culture of the organization


- The mission


- The decision-making process


- Its relationship with higher ed, industry, and the broader society


If time permits, please create a “Quick Stats Sheet” for your “lab.” Some types of information you might include are: 


- Date Launched

- Founder(s)

- Location

- # of Staff

- Budget

- Funding Sources

- Partners 

- Did you know?

- Products

- Website
