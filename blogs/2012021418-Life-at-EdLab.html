Life at EdLab

Kate Meersschaert

February 14, 2012

Private! I was inspired by this "Life at Google" Google+ page to ask, what would a "Life at EdLab" page look like and contain? Google includes photos, videos and the following description "about" life at Google: "We're continually looking for great people to help grow our team. Add us to your circles to join the conversation on our job opportunities, offices, culture and life at Google." What would we include as photos, videos and an "about" section? Would we want prospective team members to add us to their circles? Why would this be beneficial? What specific purposes could an EdLab presence on Google+ serve? How would this differ from a Facebook page or active Twitter account? I have come-up with the following benefits of creating an EdLab Google+ page about life here at the lab: 1) To generate awareness for why the culture of the lab is unique and fun! 2) Recruitment of both staff & interns. 3) As a fun exercise for the team to ensure and explore whether or not the EdLab's culture and "image" are inline with our mission. 4) To inadvertently solicit feedback and comments from the "outside." 5) Increase our social media presence. Please add your ideas and thoughts to the comments below! Photos of our red booths & umbrellas would definitely draw at least a few potential candidates! ;)
