EdLab Review: MindSnacks

Lu Wang

February 1, 2012

MindSnacks is a game app designed for foreign language learners. Through playing games, learners will be able to command essential vocabulary, reading, listening, and conversational skills. It includes beginner and intermediate levels, which will be completed by finishing 24 fun challenges. By a simple click of each item (word or phrase) of your level, you will become a mastered through the lesson! Review lessons are available for learners who need repeated practices. Pros: Learning a foreign language can be boring, but one of the biggest advantages of MindSnacks is to help learners dip into a new language by playing interesting games. MindSnacks provides learning applications of a variety of foreign languages and ESL learning. SAT subjects are also set to be added to the MindSnacks learning toolkit. Lessons of different levels are designed by top-notch Ivy League instructors, which outshines the application among similar products. In order to maximize memorization of learners, MindSnacks offers personalized learning algorithms and a growing library of images is designed to serve as a visual learning aid. Cons: It is a great app for learning vocabulary, yet there lacks interactive conversational practices. Actually this has been a challenge for such language learning products. However, integrating the natural language user interface can probably solve the problem.
