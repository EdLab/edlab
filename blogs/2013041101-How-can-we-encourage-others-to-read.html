How can we encourage others to read?

Francisco.Mendoza

April 11, 2013

Not so long ago I was asked " what is the last book you've read? " I didn't know how to respond. Wishing I didn't remember the last book I read from reading so much, but instead I haven't read in so long that the question stunned me. Reading isn't bad and when I do read I enjoy it. A big problem is finding something I like or finding interest in books, and if it is not required for class then chances are I wont read it. I asked this question to students in my writing class and the faces they made, were just like mine when I was first asked. What can people do to encourage others to read? What books would you recommend? I like mystery books, and also sequels that way there is something to look forward when the first book is finished. Also could there be a social website where it is only on books? ( sharing a book you've read, recommending one, sharing thoughts on certain chapters, etc. ) It'll be nice if there was and if there's not it'll be a great idea to making one.
