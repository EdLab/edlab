MedTech Brings Portability to Blood Pressure Testing

Ryan.Allen

July 2, 2018


Many people are not aware of their risk for hypertension related to heart disease. While blood pressure tests are available at doctorsâ€™ offices or pharmacies, the equipment is still inaccessible to some parts of the world. Researchers have now created a smartphone device that can effectively measure blood pressure, even without a doctor or bulky equipment.

Smartphones already contain most of the technology needed for blood pressure tests. With just a small, cost-effective case with a few sensors, people can now test and track their blood pressure from anywhere. While not quite as accurate as the standard arm cuff method, 90% of participants were still able to successfully test their blood pressure with the same accuracy as the acceptable finger cuff method.
