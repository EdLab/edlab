Lawsuit against school district for asking high school grad to share valedictorian

Julia Martin

July 27, 2011

I'm aware that my posts have become sort of like the Education Gossip column on our little ol' blog, but have I got another juice nugget for you. I was sent this link earlier today, which had me snoop around to find this article. Apparently though young Kymberly Wimberly had the highest GPA in her class (which she earned while also raising a baby, good work Kymberly!) but her school district appointed a co-valedictorian, who was white, and who had a lower GPA. According to this article , the school district has a history of discrimination against their African-American students. It will be interesting to see how this plays out.
