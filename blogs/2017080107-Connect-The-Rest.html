Connect The Rest

Evans.Asamoah

August 1, 2017

Certain things have become essential in order for us to function or keep evolving as a human race. One of these essential items is high-speed Internet. It has dramatically changed every facet of our lives from work education, communication, and defense systems, among countless others. Unfortunately, many rural areas across the country still lack access to high-speed internet. It is estimated that over 23.4 million people in rural areas across the country lack access to broadband. This has led Microsoft to announce a new endeavor that will allow individuals in rural areas to connect to the Internet.

 

How will Microsoft do this? It plans to use a technology often dubbed TV white space, which runs on a wireless spectrum to transmit broadband data. The total cost could average $10 billion. As someone who has lived in a developing country for a significant where there was little to no broadband access at all, I can attest to the struggles that rural Americans face.
