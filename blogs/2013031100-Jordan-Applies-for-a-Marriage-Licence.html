Jordan Applies for a Marriage Licence

Francisco.Mendoza

March 11, 2013

Michael Jordan, who recently turned 50 years old, has applied to a marriage licence in Florida. He is going to marry Yvette Prieto, a Cuban-American model and this will be Jordan's second marriage. Jordan is a role model for many with all the great things that he has already accomplished. With six NBA championships and the many records that he has set he is considered to be one of the greats of all time. As a fan of Michael Jordan I wish the best for his marriage. It shows that it is never to late to do anything and seconds chances exist, well at least for him it did. I am a fan of his sneakers and I enjoy watching old clips of him playing I wish I was able to see him play when he was on his prime. What do you guys this of his second marriage? Will it be more exciting if he was younger, or is this a right age to still marry for the one in the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame? This article shares more on his next wedding.
