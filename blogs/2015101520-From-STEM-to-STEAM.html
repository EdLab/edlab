From STEM to STEAM

Jenny Shen

October 15, 2015


Roots + Wings School of Art and Design in North Carolina is part of a larger movement to include the letter ‘A’ in the oft-used term STEM. Through its Innovation Hour program, Roots + Wings is bringing art and design concepts directly into the classroom.

Innovation Hour seeks to connect art with Common Core standards in order to promote modern skills like innovation and collaboration. Roots + Wings’ teaching artists enter the classroom with their art curriculum so that students can learn through these creative projects, but the content goes beyond just art to include science, reading, and writing as well.
