Firefox Inspiration for EdLab Cross-promotion

Kate Meersschaert

January 27, 2012

Private! Hi Team EdLab! After our recent efforts and inquiry into the world of cross-promoting our apps & tools and developing new points-of-entry, I thought I would share the team at Mozilla Firefox's subtle take. When you open a new Firefox browser window you will be given one of the following three messages below the search bar: "Are you on Facebook? Hey, so are we! 'Like' us to stay on top of the latest." "Enhance, customize and make Firefox your own with add-ons. Find out how." "Download Firefox on your Android tablet and put the power of the Web right in your hands. Get it now." I wonder where a similar message could appear for on the EdLab blog? Under Pundit or Ednode's search bar? Any additional team thoughts? I like that the messages are subtle only a hyperlink to navigate to more info on the promoted features and change often enough to hold user interest.
