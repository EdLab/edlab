PK Marketing

Gonzalo Obelleiro

October 11, 2006

Some thoughts by Dan, Dennis, Linda and Gonzalo: -Marketing efforts should reflect a cohesive image of PK -Towards that end, all efforts whould be coordinated (advertisement, person-to-person outreach, etc.) -It seems that certain great features of PK are not being mentioned in our current communications. -Marketing should not only target potential users, but also remind current users of how great the product they already use is. -PK is a high investment product, yet some of our marketing strategies tret it as a low investment one. -To correct this problem we could... a) Give Phil a British accent b) Create different Phil-identities: SHakespeare Phil (English ed Program.), Hairy Philosopher Phil (Phil and Ed Program), etc. -Viral ad campaign? -We need more definition in our marketing efforts: purpose of Phil, purpose of structure, etc.
