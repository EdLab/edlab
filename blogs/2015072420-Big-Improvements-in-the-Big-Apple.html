Big Improvements in the Big Apple

idrissa10

July 24, 2015

Fewer students are required to attend summer school this year. This is a huge improvement when one consider that 26,000 students in the past five years attended summer school. That is about 8.3 percent of all students. Another big improvement that’s taking place in New York City education is the percentage of students who are failing and repeating grades is on the decline. This improvement is credited to mayor Bill De Blasio. The mayor’s appointment is said to be responsible for the city’s educational system moving upwards. The tentative budget plan he proposed focuses on donating money to failing schools and helping them rise back to success. However, I believe it is because of student ambition. Students are now filled with the ambition to do better in their respective schools.Some jobs now require more than a high school diploma for one to even be considered. Job competition and students wanting to live better lives i believe is the catalyst to students success. Not to take anything away from mayor De Blasio, but the students are the one’s working their socks off in the classroom.
