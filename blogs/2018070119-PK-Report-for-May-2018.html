PK Report for May 2018

Nantwi

July 1, 2018

Here are some of the highlights from PocketKnowledge for May 2018:


-There were 18 new registered users. Most were TC students (5) and staff (9), including library staff.


-410 files were uploaded, most were PDFs (221) and images (177). 


-There were a total of 1,094 sessions. Google and the library website served as the top two sources of all sessions. 


-The top five most downloaded titles were:


Unit Diary of an India


PubMed Search Histories


ORLD 5057 Thy and Prac of Adult Learning


The Things They Carried Unit Plans


Teachers College Logos 


Please let me know if there are any other interesting stats from PK you are interested in for June's report.
