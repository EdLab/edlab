PUNDIT II prototype is ready to use, have a try!

Pengfei Li

June 27, 2011

I am very glad to tell everyone that the prototype of PUNDIT, the PUNDIT II, is ready to use now. PUNDIT is a course recommendation system designed for students at TC to help them choose courses. For more information about PUNDIT, click here. If you are interested in the project, please have a try! We will discuss PUNDIT II on the D&R weekly meeting on June 30th. We will be very glad to hear your feedback. To use the PUNDIT II, we need to go through the following steps: 1. connect to Holland (The VPN) 2. register the Ednode account in sandbox4, (remember the username, it is very important) and fill in your personal information. If you already have Ednode account, then you just need to login and refill your personal information. The website is: http://sandbox4-edlab.tc.columbia.edu/tln/ketaki/ 3. go to the home page of PUNDIT II The website is: http://sandbox1-edlab.tc.columbia.edu/pundit/pengfei/home/ 4. type in your username, change date, or type in the course search terms and press go! 5. wait for a moment, the result will show below. 6. click in each courses to see the course details We are not using the database of Ednode because this is only a prototype. We use the data on sandbox4, so we have to refill the profile. Sorry for the extra work. If you find a bug, or you have any suggestions, please feel free to write it down. Your support could make it better and better. On Thursday's D&R meeting we will discuss the functionality and UI design of PUNDIT. Looking forward to your active participation. I just noticed that only developers could connect to sandbox4. Maybe we can do in this way: fill the Ednode profile through the computer which could connect to sandbox4, then you can try the pundit through that computer or during Thursday's D&R meeting. Sorry for the late notice and have fun with PUNDIT
