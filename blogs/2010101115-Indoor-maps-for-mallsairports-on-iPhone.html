Indoor maps for malls/airports on iPhone

pramnab1

October 11, 2010

Mobile phone maps have guide people through streets and alleys around the globe. But when those people step into a sprawling building, they can get lost. Companies like Mindsmack(FastMAll) have started creating maps for malls/airports so that people can find their way inside the buildings. Read the full article by theNYTimes. I wonder if it would be useful to make an iPhone version of TCRF, so we can get a lead on in the education sector and have something to showcase? Looking forward to hearing your opinions on this. By the way for people who are not familiar with TCRF it's like Teachers college maps and you can visit here from your computer or cellphone.
