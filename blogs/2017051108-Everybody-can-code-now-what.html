Everybody can code, now what?

ryanwu

May 11, 2017

Who says designer always start their work from hand drawing? Airbnb’s design team just released an Open Source project which enables designers to "Code" their design and render to Sketch App (Modern Digital Design Tool). 




I'm really excited to see this and I think designers will be able to pull in REAL data into their designs in the near future. And yes, iIn the future, programming/coding will become just like math


Everybody knows it but only a few people know how to do it right. 

We see the NYPD using Big Data to predict where crime will hit next and update their patrol routes accordingly, and Russia just created a Gun-wielding robot terminator through the power of AI. 


So what's Next?
