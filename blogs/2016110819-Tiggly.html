Tiggly

EdLabTeam

November 8, 2016


Tiggly is a line of toys and apps that encourages learning amongst preschoolers through a combination of physical and digitial play as well as parental involvement. In the fall of 2013, co-founder/chief learning officer, Azadeh Jamalian along with Tiggly founder Phyl Georgiou visited the EdLab seminar to share their products and the learning philosophy behind them.

Check out the talk and the related discussion on on Vialogues.
