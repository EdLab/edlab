Six New Tech Devices to Debut in 2015

Khalil Abubakar

December 30, 2014

A partnership with INC has help predict innovations to be made to tech devices in 2015. These changes are very significant and doesn't follow the same path as other upgrades for example Iphone 4s is not a big leap from its predecessor iPhone 4. Apps like the new Adobe Photoshop Web will have more editing tools and will be able to run on a web browser, making it location friendly. Also the Apple iPhone Air will be the thinnest and lightest phone in the market, it will have a longer battery life and will charge fully in a matter of minutes. Microsoft Surface Phone will replace the Windows Phones model, its has a very long battery life, a computer chip making it super fast and the phone will be able to run desktop apps. Belkin WeMo Maker is a 3d smart robot which has a sensor and other tools. These robots easily connects with the internet to help monitor your home. It might be able to clean and turn off appliances. Google Material Design will replace boring themes to lively ones with a virtual paper feel and texture. This feature in 2015 will improve the look of apps like Gmail. Toyota Mirai which will debut in mid-2015, runs on hydrogen fuel with is good for 300 miles per fill and it does not emit harmful gasses into the ecosystem. It will be able to monitor traffic with its cameras and watch-out for pedestrians.
