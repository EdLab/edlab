Cellphones in Schools?

Francisco.Mendoza

July 21, 2013

Have you ever taken your cellphone to school? Are you from the age where cellphones didn't yet exist? Some parents and teachers wish that cell phones are banned from school, but then again there are some who are in favor of their children taking cellphones to schools. In my opinion, cellphones should be allowed in school though there should be a penalty to the student who is caught with a cellphone during class time, especially if it is not class related. For example, to save paper, students can take notes on his or her phone. In case of an emergency, it'll be easier to get in touch with someone by having a cellphone. These are some of the reasons why I am in favor of cellphones being allowed in school. Share your thoughts and opinions in the Vialogue I've posted.
