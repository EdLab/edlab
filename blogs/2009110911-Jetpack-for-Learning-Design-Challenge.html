Jetpack for Learning Design Challenge

Arianna Choi

November 9, 2009

A call for innovative Firefox add-ons that turn the open Web into a rich learning environment! Participation is open to individuals and teams, but the site says, "we especially encourage submissions from inter-disciplinary groups (designers, educators, and software developers)." What's in it for us if we entered? >>> The ten most promising concepts will be invited to a face-to-face Jetpack for Learning Design Camp that will take place just before the South by Southwest (SXSW) Interactive 2010 in Austin, Texas and also be invited to attend it. >>> During the Design Camp, participants will get hands-on support and help to complete the process of turning the prototypes into fully-functional Firefox add-ons. >>> Each of the Design Camp participants will receive payment or reimbursement for travel, lodging, food, and miscellaneous expenses, and registration for SXSW Interactive, all approximately valued at $4,000. The submission for mockups is due on November 27th. Any challengers from our lab? Read the brief and more details here.
