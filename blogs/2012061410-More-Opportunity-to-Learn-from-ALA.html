More Opportunity to Learn from ALA

Rebecca Hyams

June 14, 2012

On Monday Greg posted about the webinars that ALA is offering as a virtual conference during their annual meeting in Anaheim in a few weeks. The virtual conference itself is not free, but there will be one free session on Friday, June 29th at 2pm EST. The session, the TechSource Webinar will summarize the trends and ideas in technology that were presented over the course of the ALA conference with distinguished panelists and a Q&A session to follow.
