Letter Battle

Youssef Ballo

June 23, 2014

At Troy Middle School in upstate New York, students are one of the first in the region to learn spelling by using a new educational game. The school approved the use of Letter Battle to give students the opportunity to learn. According to a teacher at the school students were having trouble understanding verbal instructions. He started using the Letter Battle in the classwork to help students better understand verbal lessons. The principle of Letter Battle is that a student has stolen letters in a word. The players' goal is to collect those letters and make words out of them. As a reward, players receive points, and feedback.
