A Modern Addition to an Old Neighborhood

Carmen James

December 15, 2014


This video begins with intriguing photos of the Bowery. As the narrator tells us, 100 years ago the Bowery was the roughest neighborhood in town with the "Bowery Boys" street gang terrorizing the area. The Bowery was also a place that housed many artists, including Maya Lin. Now it is an art center, gentrified, with upscale storesâ€¦and home of the New Museum. As the we learn from the video, the New Museum challenges the very concept of what it means to be a museum, with artists roaming partially-naked through the hallways, visitors painting on the second floor, and a life-size replica of the interior of a soviet spaceship on the fifth floor. Pioneering in uncharted areas of art and culture, the museum has created a laboratory of its own called NEW INC.

NEW INC is the first museum-led incubator and it aims, broadly speaking, to leverage the resources of people with a diversity of backgrounds to design sustainable solutions. The incubator offers shared workspace, professional development, and opportunities at the intersection of art, technology, and design including demo nights, hackathons, and symposiums. For more on NEW INC, read our prior coverage.
