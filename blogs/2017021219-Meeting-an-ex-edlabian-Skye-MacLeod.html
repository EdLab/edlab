Meeting an ex-edlabian Skye MacLeod

February 12, 2017

Skye was the first to recognize me and we started talking about our journey at EdLab and after. Anyone interested in visiting Jersey, Liberty Science Center would highly recommend for adults and kids :). Also if you plan in advance and want to meet do a shout out at pranav.garg007@gmail.com.
