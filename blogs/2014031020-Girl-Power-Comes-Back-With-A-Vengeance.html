Girl Power Comes Back With A Vengeance

Carmen James

March 10, 2014


Debbie Sterling, a Stanford engineer and founder of GoldieBlox, decided girls need more toys to build, create and practice design and construction, the foundations of engineering. GoldieBlox are more than just toys. The set includes an interactive book series and a construction kit that stars Goldie, the kid inventor who loves to build. Debbie, during her initial research, discovered that girls are initially drawn to reading while boys are drawn to building. Debbie drew on girls’ natural inclination to read and combined verbal and spatial reasoning by creating an interactive story about Goldie that allows girls to build as they progress through the narrative.

Watch the amazing inventions a trio of girls built in this promotional video for GoldieBlox, the engineering toys for girls. Levers, slides, pullies, and simple machines only begin to describe the incredible domino-effect experience these girls put together. If you’re curious for more of the philosophy behind Debbie’s invention, watch her TedX talk.
