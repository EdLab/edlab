Keep Private Continuing through the book, a few questions and items from Chapters 3 and 4. Chapter 3 - The Ripple Effect What are the lessons regarding collaboration that can be derived from Grant's view of Givers? Which of the individuals discussed in the chapter do you most identify with? What are some concrete indicators of "expedition behavior" in our own work? Does anything in this chapter address the issue posed by Brian in the blog entry on Chapter 1 and 2? Chapter 4 - Finding the Diamond in the Rough How does the advice that might be drawn from Grant's discussion of finding or developing talent align with what you have observed about the recruitment and hiring practices we typically use? How would you respond to the claim that it takes more time to make a diamond than to find one? Of all the individuals mentioned in this chapter, who is closest to your own approach to helping those around you develop?
