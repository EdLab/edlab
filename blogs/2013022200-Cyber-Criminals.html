Cyber Criminals

Daniel Vergara

February 22, 2013

The amount of cyber criminals has been increased in recent years. I came across this article that talks about the dangers on information mobile devices and other electronics contain. Apparently there are new tricks cyber criminals have been using lately that might just put your bank information at risk. According to the article, some cyber criminals have developed mobile sites that could seem like a legitimate banking site but really tricks people into submitting their personal information. With this in mind, I would be more careful when it comes to filling out this type of information anywhere, especially on my phone. Mobile devices have become the most targeted now for being the easiest place to trick people into giving away their information. It is just awful to know that this has been going on and that people are at risk for falling to these traps. I feel that most people are unaware that such things are happening, and even if they did, I doubt that this could ever be stop. Awareness might help people avoid these traps. What are your thoughts on these cyber criminals?
