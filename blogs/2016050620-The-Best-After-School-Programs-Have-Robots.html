The Best After School Programs Have Robots

Rebecca Beeson

May 6, 2016


The Digital Core is a project from the Homewood STEAM Summer Camp in Pittsburgh that serves students from lower socio economic demographics. It brings together technologists, educators, and librarians to mentor students in programs developed with STEAM curriculum.

Initiatives like this are essential for guaranteeing students access to the same resources available in more tech-equipped schools. The program offers students the chance to learn coding, study engineering, experiment creatively with robots, and take chances and make mistakes.
