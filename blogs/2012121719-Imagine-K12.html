With a strong belief that technology is transforming K-12 education, Imagine K12 aims to fish high-quality, innovative educational technologies that can benefit K-12 students out of this high-tech wave. As a for-profit enterprise, Imagine K12 looks to create a reciprocal platform that connects passionate educational entrepreneurs and technical know-how to identify a common vision and help them achieve their goals. Imagine K12 provides a three-month accelerator program for startup teams, who are mentored by seasoned professionals with extensive entrepreneurial experience, insightful vision on the future of the education industry, and knowledge of the educational marketplace, regionally and nationally. Imagine K12 aims to cultivate great ideas disruptive to the current education system.

The Future of Edtech in Palo Alto
Imagine K12 fills a market gap by providing a professional accelerator program specifically for education-focused startups. Attuned to trends in education technology and recognizing the potential of the internet to transform society, Imagine K12 works to provide resources to web-based companies aiming to improve the quality of K-12 education. One of the strengths of Imagine K12 is the ability to provide various kinds of resources a startup would need. For example, Imagine K12 has a professional team of experts who help new companies with business strategy, product design, and technical and recruiting advice through in-person office hours. It also has an extensive network of educators with whom startup companies can build relationships for further development of their business. In this sense, Imagine K12 functions not only as an incubator for startup companies, but also as a curator that keeps educators informed of the hands-on technologies that can be implemented to address existing problems in the educational system. Based in Palo Alto, Imagine K12 has ready access to the diverse resources that have made the Bay Area an environment for innovation.

An Engine for Change
Imagine K12 works to turn great ideas into reality and help disruptive innovations grow. Realizing that there are unique challenges for ventures to survive in the US education market, Imagine K12 adopts a tech-focused, edmarket-driven, down-to-earth approach that efficiently helps bring ideas to life, gets startups funded, and leads new education-focused companies to take their first step on the road to success. Imagine K12 is an important gateway for technology-focused innovations in K-12 education.
