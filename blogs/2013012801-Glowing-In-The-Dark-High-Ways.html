Glowing In The Dark High Ways

Oumar Soumahoro

January 28, 2013

I've created the Vialogues below in which a Dutch designer creates a system for highways to be safe without road lights. Using a photo luminescent paint, lens on highways will glow in the night after the paint absorbed the sun energy during day time. Under low temperature or bad weather, the lens are designed to glow in order to warn drivers of possible dangers while driving, and electric cars can be charged automatically as well. This innovation will save drivers from certain accidents, minimize the use of carbon dioxide and save electricity. What do you think of this innovation?
