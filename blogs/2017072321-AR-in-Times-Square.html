AR in Times Square

xk2120

July 23, 2017

The new iOS 11 (pre-released to apple developers) and Xcode beta bring AR to our life incredibly easy! I went to an AR workshop last week and followed the tutorial online, and made my simple AR games within one hour. I grew a virtual tulip farm in Times Square this weekend (oh, I also made Spiderman come to NYC).






I highly recommend you check out the ARkit for potential projects. Please talk with me if you have a great idea for AR applications in education as well.
