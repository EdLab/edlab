EVENT RECAP: Arts in Education Roundtable

Mabel Zhuang

May 22, 2013

Janice and I went to an awesome event last night hosted at the Apple Store in SoHo. (Side note: The store has such a great space on the 2nd floor. The EdTech community should definitely utilize that space more often!) It was refreshing to hear from a panel of teachers, all of whom have been integrating technology into the arts in a very unique and innovative way.Some examples include: 1. Dance teacher, Barry Blumenfeld, uses a board very similar to a smart board that enables students to display and share the choreography they have written to the rest of the class. 2. Theatre teacher and actress, Leah Reddy, created fake Twitter accounts for the characters in The Crucible to demonstrate the gossip occurring in that story in a way that is more relatable to students today. Panelists include: Christopher Amos - Carnegie Hall Barry Blumenfeld - Dance Teacher Jaymes Dec - The Makery and Marymount School Kevin Lopez - Soundview Academy Leah Reddy - Roundabout Theatre Company The event was hosted by Roundtable, a community of organization working to improve, advance, and advocate for arts education. They have monthly events as well as an annual conference so keep your eyes and ears open! Tweets about "#edlabrepresents"
