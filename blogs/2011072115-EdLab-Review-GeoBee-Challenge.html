EdLab Review: GeoBee Challenge

Skanda Amarnath

July 21, 2011

Recently I blogged about NAEP geography scores and the relationship between rote memorization and drawing connections in geography education. GeoBee Challenge, from the people at National Geographic and the National Geographic Bee is app that offers a suite of geography quizzes combined to form a 15 level challenge. Pros: The set of questions is constantly changing each day and the question set is large enough that if you play multiple times in the same day, you should not come across to many repeated questions. The app makes good use of touch features on the iPhone to zoom in features to make certain quizzes easily possible. For instance, for many of the quizzes, you have to touch a point on the map when asked to locate a city. The closer you point to the city, the more points you receive (kind of a rip from the Traveler IQ Quiz application on Facebook but far more adeptly executed with the help of zoom in and touch features).
