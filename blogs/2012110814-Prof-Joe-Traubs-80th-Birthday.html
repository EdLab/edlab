Prof. Joe Traub's 80th Birthday

Sharath Gururaj

November 8, 2012

Tomorrow is Professor Joe Traub's 80th birthday. Prof. Traub started the computer science department in Columbia and is well known for the Jenkins traub algorithm for numerically estimating roots of a polynomial. There is a series of programs in his honor tomorrow featuring many distinguished people in theoretical computer science like Richard Karp, well known for showing that many day to day problems are as hard as the million dollar problem. David Shaw (the founder of D.E.Shaw and an early professor of CS at Columbia) will also give a talk. If you're free during this time, I would definitely recommend attending it. The program is all day long at the Davis Auditorium. Details can be found here: http://www.cs.columbia.edu/symposium/november-9/#schedule
