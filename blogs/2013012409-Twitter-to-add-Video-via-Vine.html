Twitter to add Video via Vine?

Kate Meersschaert

January 24, 2013

In news that sounds like the "cheezeburger lolcats"... Twitter's whale now asks, "I can haz videos?" Mashable.com announced (in this blog post) that Twitter will most likely add the capability to share 6 second video clips via the Vine platform. Do you feel this potential new capability adds value to the microblogging platform? Why or why not? What might this mean for our video discussion platform, Vialogues?
