Who Taught Them This?

gnatriello

July 5, 2007

In All the News That's Fit to Print Out Jonathan Dee discusses the work of the teenagers and twenty-somethings who devote major effort to growing and maintaining Wikipedia. After describing the devotion of the Wikipedians to the values of the project, he concludes by noting their commitment to policing bias and asks "Who taught them this?" and then adds "It's a mystery, but they are teaching it to one another." This is an interesting question. Are those working on the Wikipedia project being taught values? Or are individuals with certain values being attracted to the project?
