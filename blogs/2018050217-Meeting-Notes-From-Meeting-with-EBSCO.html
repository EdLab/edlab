Meeting Notes From Meeting with EBSCO

HSC

May 2, 2018

Ideas from the EBSCO team:


- Provide perpetual access to content that libraries have "purchased" in the past. 


- Sell access to TCR archival content to libraries. 


- Make TCR a part of database subscriptions. 


- Move TCR to open access and charge authors for publishing. 


- Communicate the super low cost of TCR (cost per page basis). 


My thoughts during the meeting:


- Sell access to to future TCR content. 


- Advertise individual subscriptions on the Institutional site (http://tcrecord.org/library/). 


- Decrease the number of years libraries have access to (currently libraries have access to the last ten years of content). 


- Follow up with libraries that did not renew their institutional subscription. 


- Identify a donor(s) to underwrite the full cost of making TCR fully open. 


- Move to online only access for libraries.
