So...what are good study habits?

Michelle Delateur

September 8, 2010

The New York Times posted an interesting article today about what "good study habits" really are. It turns out that many adages and advice about studying, such as staying in one location or studying one topic for a long time, may not actually work best. College students that switched locations or studied multiple topics at once seemed to have better retention in the studies discussed in the article. Despite the research and various studies, one conclusion holds true: Not every technique works best for everyone. We are all different types of learners whose techniques for retention differ. But it is interesting that despite our research and those various types of learning, school environments are still catered to a one location - topic intensive model.
