Engaging Reluctant Readers on Google+

Brian.Sweeting

December 11, 2013

*Private* After a few weeks of comparing NLT campaign efforts among different social media platforms, I've noticed that Google+ consistently results in a steady stream of visits and signups, more so than Facebook and LinkedIn. Yet the audience on Google+ is vocal about their opposition to signing in to read articles. Below is a recent interaction I had after sharing this VisualizED. (Despite the few vocal opponents, according to Simple Counts, the campaign resulted in 151 mobile visits and 22 signups). How can we best communicate the sign in to circumspect readers?
