Critter Seminar Video: Feedback?

Skye MacLeod

July 20, 2009

If you haven't had a chance to take a look at the latest EdLab Vlog on the Critter Seminar, here it is: It was an interesting event to capture, and one that was quite enjoyable (and at times very difficult) to edit down to 2 1/2min! It's a (somewhat documentary) format that I am still working on improving. The main difficulty that I wrestled with was trying to include enough information of the event without going overboard. It is sometimes a challenging balance! I am looking forward to hearing your impressions of the video: was it engaging? What did you like/dislike? How might videos like these be useful to the lab/beyond? How might we structure filming seminars like this in the future? Thanks for your input!
