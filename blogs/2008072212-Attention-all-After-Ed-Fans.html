Attention all After Ed Fans!

Joachim Castellano

July 22, 2008

Some recent developments at AfterEd.tv! Please join the After Ed Facebook Group AfterEd.tv Fans ! Our numbers are growing at an alarming rate, members doubling in less than a week! Our goal is to break triple digits by the end of the month, so everyone please tell all your Facebook Friends to become fans. And if you're not on Facebook, join just so you can become a fan of After Ed. Right now our Facebook group isn't so active. I encourage you to enliven our Facebook group with posts, polls, pictures, anything! After Ed interns are buzzing along, creating some great media experiments! I encourage you to check out the After Ed blog. From interviews to podcasts, Alex, Stan, Evan, and James are busy producing content. After Ed media is more sharable as well. In each of our blog posts near the title you'll notice a button that says 'share.' We've inserted the 'add this' badges button which will hopefully spread our content far and wide to various blog aggregating sites. So go ahead and start digging our content now!
