UAE-TCCU Ideation and Design Event -Day 3 Afternoon Activity Pilot

HSC

January 24, 2018





PART I

In your small groups, design a study to deepen and advance our understanding of some aspect of learning and/or teaching in the future. 


1. What are your areas of inquiry? For example: 

a. Authentic assessment

b. adaptive learning

c. Virtual Reality/Augmented Reality


2. What are your research questions?


3. What does the data look like? 


4. What people and skills are necessary to conduct the study?


5. How will the findings be used to improve the education sector?
