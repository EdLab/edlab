Overheard Audio Tour and Documentation

Molly Riordan

September 9, 2009

At long last, the aural element of Overheard arrives! The Overheard Audio Tour is now online and on-campus: download the tour at the TC iTunes U site, grab a map from the exhibit on the third floor, and take the tour! It features personal stories and long-forgotten histories of TC you'll be surprised to hear. We are in the process of getting all of the exhibit documentation on the web, but check out the Flickr set for photos of the exhibit and Pocket Knowledge for the files and source material that make up the exhibit.
