Education Technology, An Investment to Make?

Oumar Soumahoro

June 4, 2015

The edtech market continues to boom as schools and administrators continue to find ways to integrate technology into the classroom and student learning. In 2013, spending on technology was estimated at $13 billion. This figure is expected to soar in the coming years. All this sounds like great news for education as it means there is a lot of tools and application for students, parents and administrators to choose from. Despite the broad interest and financial investments in educational technology, are stakeholders receiving bang for buck? This piece from Fortune analyzes whether the edtech market leads to better student outcomes.
