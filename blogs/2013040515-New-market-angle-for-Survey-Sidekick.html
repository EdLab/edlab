New market angle for Survey Sidekick

Janice Joo

April 5, 2013

*Private* Not just for product managers: I went to a datavis panel discussion last night, where I learned about how datavis has intersected with citizen science. It's cool on so many levels... Speakers were from Four Square, Public Laboratory, and CartoDB (VIzzuality)... and they all talked about how citizens are contributing to the "bigger picture". SSK is designed to support the practice of conducting informed scientific survey research online... (oh I like how I phrased that...). Citizen scientists certainly have a need for that. I think we should find a way to tap into existing citizen scientist audiences (perhaps through the speakers from last night... though I had to leave early and could not introduce myself personally). We could perhaps suggest a citizen science panel discussion where an SSK advocate could speak about how SSK has been used for citizen science... this is where an SSK API could be interesting in our near future... so that all the data from SSK citizen research can be used to visualize information! **EXCITING!** More for the future of our marketing... In addition to getting insight from organizations that conduct large-scale survey research, we should aim to get citizen use-case stories in the future too. This is a timely revelation in light of yesterday's PM meeting where the SSK team sought for some new outreach solutions. Something for the SSK team to think about...
