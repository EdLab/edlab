Saildrones Conduct Autonomous Research on the High Seas

Rebecca.Sullivan

September 3, 2018


Saildrones are autonomous, wind and solar-powered vessels with the potential to capture data from previously inaccessible areas of the ocean. Their sustainable energy allows them to voyage almost indefinitely, sending information about the atmosphere, the ocean, and animal life back to scientists on land.

Saildrone research allows scientists and engineers to understand and make predictions about climate change and its effect on ocean life. The drones sail from California and Australia, exploring nearby waters as well as difficult-to-reach recesses of the Antarctic seas.
