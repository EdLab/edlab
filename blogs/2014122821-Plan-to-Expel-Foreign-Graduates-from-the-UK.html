Plan to Expel Foreign Graduates from the UK

AbdulMalik

December 28, 2014

In most countries, international students form an integral part of the educational system. Considering the population of foreign graduates in the UK, most people criticized the Home Secretary of UK's idea of sending all foreign graduates back to their native countries. Most of those most likely to be sent back home are mainly science researchers and engineering students. Several people have argued that this plan will cause more harm than good to the country and its economy. This plan will deprive foreign graduates the ability to work in the UK after receiving their degrees there.

I believe this plan should not be implemented. Foreign students have a positive impact on the vitality and economy of the country. The growth and importance of science and technology makes it important to allow foreign science students to stay in the UK after graduating. Education is an essential sector of the economy and the UK government should take all necessary steps to ensure this plan does not go through.
