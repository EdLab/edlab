Project Space Templates

Jeannie Crowley

February 23, 2010

I've been tweaking some of the templates on the project space. These changes are intended to help spark some discussion during different phases of a project. The potential category additions are outlined below. I welcome any suggestions to help make the project space templates more effective in guiding the development of new projects.

  • Project Lead: Who will be responsible for moving the project forward, maintaining a cohesive vision for the project and organizing the team members?
  • Team Roles: How will work be divided among the team members to prevent redundant work?
  • Data Collection and Research: What data is of interest in this project? How will it be collected (by the system or externally)? What are some research goals of the project?
  • Revenue: What are some potential sources of revenue that can be built into the design of this project? How will the project sustain itself financially? Hint: "We'll sell it to school districts" hasn't proven to be a workable model.
  • EdLab Mission:How does this project advance the goals of the EdLab?
