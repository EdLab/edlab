Clay Shirky on Disrupting Higher Ed (Again)

Nara Kasbergen

January 8, 2013

A couple of months back I posted an article that Clay Shirky, one of my mentors in grad school, wrote about the trends he sees in higher ed in relation to MOOCs. My brief write-up and a link to the article can be found here. Clay was recently part of a video dialogue on the site BloggingHeads.tv where he discusses this same topic. The first part is similar to his previous article, but then he goes on to relate his ideas to some of his "classic Clay Shirky" concepts that are discussed in his books Cognitive Surplus and Here Comes Everybody (which are great and highly relevant reads, if you haven't read them already). You can check out the video here.
