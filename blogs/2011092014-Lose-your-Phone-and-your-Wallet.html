Lose your Phone... & your Wallet!

Kate Meersschaert

September 20, 2011

Now both your wallet and your phone can be lost or stolen, at the same time! Google has announced there long-awaited phone-based "wallet." Really an evolution of the "tap-to-pay" phenomena like blink, morphed into a phone based method of payment. The details are scarce, but check-out the intro from Google embedded in the vialogue below and this piece by AllThingsD.com. Do you want to carry your method of payment on your phone? Does this really eliminate the need for a wallet? I would argue no. You still need to carry an id, until the day that all forms of identification are digital or embedded subcutaneously, this seems like a purely symbolic gesture.
