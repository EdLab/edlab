Movie Recommendation

zhou

October 25, 2012

I watched a great movie today from Netflix and thought many of you might be interested. The movie is called Something Ventured and was released in 2011. I'm not sure how well EdLabbers are familiar with and interested in the history and big figures of venture capitalism but I knew next to nothing until I watched this movie. It gives a rough picture of the history of VC from the 1960's when Bill Draper, etc., founded the first few VC companies to the next two decades when Don Valentine, Arthur Rock, Tom Perkins etc. helped found some of today's influential high-tech companies such as Intel, Apple, Cisco, Genentech, and so on. The whole movie is fast-paced and light-hearted. Pretty amusing to watch although the music is kinda weird. But hey who cares. The last scene of the movie is what these great venture capitalists think are the areas venture capitalism will prosper in the next few decades.
