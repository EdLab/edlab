Library Site and App Updates, September 2018

jvazquez

October 2, 2018

September showed up with the receipts to prove its been our most active month since the launch.


I'm trying something new this month by putting the site and app update all within the same post so we can begin to draw comparisons.

We more than doubled our traffic this past month on both the site and app. That’s a lot of love!


Interestingly, though they only accounted for 31% of total traffic, on-campus visitors took their time on the site. On average, they spent 10 minutes and 44 seconds per visit while off-campus visitors had comparatively speedy tours, spending an average of 3 minutes and 58 seconds on the site.

Of the total site searches, 24% were filtered by category. Here’s what people preferred to see:

  • Books: 31%

  • Articles: 28%

  • Everything: 23%

  • Journals: 11%

  • Knowledge Base: 3%

  • Website: 4%


Only 11% of total site searches went on to look for “More Results.” Not surprisingly, the categories most sought after were Articles and Books.

  • For people who filtered by Articles, 99% went on for more article results

  • For people who filtered by Books, 48% went on for more book results
