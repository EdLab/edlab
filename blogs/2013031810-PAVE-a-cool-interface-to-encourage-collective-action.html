PAVE - a cool interface to encourage collective action

Laura Scheiber

March 18, 2013

*KEEP PRIVATE* Working collaboratively with others on a mutual goal can potentially lead to more effective action versus working alone. So making connections with folks with similar interests and goals in mind is important in today's networked society. A friend recently pointed me to a really cool site called Pave that aims to foster relationships/social supports around individuals who have a goal in mind. Seems like a good social platform.
