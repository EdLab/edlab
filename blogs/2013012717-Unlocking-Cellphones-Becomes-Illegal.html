Unlocking Cellphones Becomes Illegal

Daniel Vergara

January 27, 2013

I am not sure how many of you have heard the recent news but unlocking your phone is now illegal. Unlocking a cellphone is when you have a cellphone without a carrier. It basically provides you the option of choosing a phone that a certain carrier does not have and unlocking it so that you could use the phone with the carrier. I've never done this before nor was I interested but apparently many people have. The aforementioned article essentially notes that people who unlock phones this would not be affected in anyway by this new law. As it is now, many cellphone carries can unlock your phone for a price. Also you can buy an unlock phone but you would have to pay full price without accepting the contract. It does not seem like this new law will change much as your phone can still be unlocked. What are some ways in which this law could be potentially enforced?
