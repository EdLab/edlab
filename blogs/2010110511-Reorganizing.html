Reorganizing

Angela Lee

November 5, 2010

Hello, Continuing my last post about a healthy workspace in Trends In Ed... After the ALE design event, I realized there was a lot of potential to do a redesign of the EdLab space. Little things such as unused tables being moved to the back rooms, organizing the back room as meeting rooms/storages, or taking out the old book shelves and covering them with clothes can make a big difference to the whole atmosphere of the space; this will definitely make the set up process of future events easier and more efficient. I will go ahead and start organizing the kitchen area today and if you want to help out feel free to stop by!
