Making All Knowledge Computational

Stephen Asunka

April 27, 2010

Stephen Wolfram, creator of Mathematica, demonstrates his Wolfram|Alpha Computational Knowledge Engine - a quest to make ALL knowledge computational i.e. able to be searched, processed and manipulated using ordinary language and symbols. Insightful but also mind-boggling.
