Five Organizations That Remind Us Adults Are Learners Too

Brian.Sweeting

December 21, 2016


When looking at the crowded learning sector, it's easy to get lost in all the K-12 apps and forget that learning continues after formal schooling ends. These five organizations are helping adults learn how to be better entrepreneurs, executives, governors, and people.

1. Defy Ventures
Defy Ventures is an organization dedicated to helping people with criminal histories create their own startups and businesses.

2. GovLab
GovLab examines the ways technology can help institutions work collaboratively and openly to solve pervasive public problems.

3. 80,000 Hours
This organization is leading young adults into high-impact jobs through targeted online career counseling.

4. June Labs
This edtech company enables collaboration between technology companies and educators to create better tools and resources for learners.

5. NY Edtech Accelerator
An accelerator program sponsored by The NYU Steinhardt School of Culture, Education, and Human Development and StartEd Companies, Inc. awards finalists upwards of $170,000 each along with business, technology, and education mentors, and coaches to help them build successful businesses.
