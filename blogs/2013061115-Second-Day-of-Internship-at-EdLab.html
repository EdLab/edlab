Second Day of Internship at EdLab

Ariel Han

June 11, 2013

Hello everyone! This is my second day at work at EdLab and I'm so excited to be here! I'm working as Data Visualization intern to create visuals for our data. I like info graphics with narratives and contextual messages. I would like to share an example of infographic that I like - this one is about gamification. For me, infographics are a great way to convey data in an efficient way and it creates a context, which makes it more memorable. A little bit more about myself. My undergraduate degree is in Fine arts, ITCT and Industrial Design, and a Masters degree in Entertainment Technology. Throughout my life, I've been very interested in education and my passion is all about making Edutainment/ Gamification happen. I believe there will be more fun and new ways to help learning in the future. While studying for my master's degree at Carnegie Mellon University, I worked on a project at the Children's Museum of Pittsburgh. At this project, I was the UI/3D designer to make an interactive media that taught 5 year old children the basic concept of programming. I believe there will be more fun and new ways to help learning experience in the future. I went through the EdLab resources yesterday, especially Vialogues and NLT. It has so many great resources and information about current education issues and was really fun to read through. At EdLab, I'm hoping to participate in very exciting projects going on here. Stay tuned for more news!
