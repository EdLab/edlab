Inspiring a Love of Music Through Tech

Rebecca.Sullivan

March 14, 2019


"We have combined all we love about music with all we depend upon online and on mobile, to create what we call the magnetic practice app – to make practicing music fun and rewarding from the very first day and all the way to the stage." (Tonara)

Making Practice Fun from Day One

The creators of Tonara know that the first weeks and months of practicing an instrument are hardly rewarding. Kids’ dreams of jamming with their favorite band or faultlessly performing a piano concerto can quickly fade amid the clanging and screeching of misplayed chords and endless, halting renditions of "Three Blind Mice." Tonara’s team also knows that the more kids practice the better they become, and the more satisfaction they feel. So they created an interactive app that provides game-like encouragement and uses artificial intelligence to give students real-time feedback as they practice. Young musicians level-up as they log practice minutes, and receive tips on how to improve their performance.

A New Kind of Music Teaching

Tonara offers music teachers a new way to manage and communicate with their students. The teacher sends practice pieces to students through the app, and can include images of sheet music, recordings, time goals, and written instructions. Teachers receive notifications when students practice, and can communicate with them through the app’s message board. The activity dashboard shows how long each student spends playing per week, offering teachers key insights between music sessions.

Tonara endeavors to utilize the latest technology to jumpstart kids’ lifelong love of music, "a joy that will serve their soul for years to come."
