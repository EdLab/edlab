The Apollo Theater

EdLab Studios

May 27, 2014


In the Apollo Theater Saturday Workshop Series, stage production professionals shed light (and props and sound) on what it takes to put on a showâ€”from behind the velvet curtain. During this 12-week workshop series, teens learn technical stage production, the behind-the-scenes stuff of live theater: audio, lighting, set design and prop creation. The program connects teens to technical arts careers through one of the great cultural icons of the city. A tradition of mentoring and support for the next generation of dreamers lives on at the Apollo, as seasoned stage-hands, designers, and production staff guide teens through the ins-and-outs of their work, their world, and how to put together everything they have learned in their own show, live at the Apollo Theater.
