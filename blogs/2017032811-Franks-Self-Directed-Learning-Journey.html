Frank's Self-Directed Learning Journey

HSC

March 28, 2017

{{unknown}}
{{unknown}}
Frank Obeng, who I have known for over two years, recently decided to enroll in three self-directed learning opportunities to better prepare himself for the next stage of his life. The first is Organic Chemistry. The second is Introduction to Family Engagement in Education. The third is Leading Ambitious Teaching and Learning.  These courses are asynchronous and have no instructor presence. Frank intends to complete all the assignments and tests even though none will be graded. Frank is going to share his experiences on the Institute for Self-Directed Learning website. Here are some questions I will have for Frank as he documents his journey: - What did you learn? How did you learn it? - What, if any, are the social dimensions of the two "courses"? - How did motivation change over time? - How would you improve the experience?
