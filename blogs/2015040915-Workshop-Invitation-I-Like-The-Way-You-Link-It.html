Workshop Invitation: I Like The Way You Link It

Brian.Sweeting

April 9, 2015

*Private*

Do you privately wonder when your article or post has one link too many? When are you helping readers and when are you drowning them in a vast sea of meaningless blue? How long should your anchor text be and should a link open in a new tab? What are you even doing with your life?

Come to a workshop focused on creating guidelines for user-friendly linking etiquette for our publications. This is geared toward NLT authors, but everyone is welcome to attend.
