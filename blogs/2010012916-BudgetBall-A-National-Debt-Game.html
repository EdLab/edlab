BudgetBall: A National Debt Game

chingfu

January 29, 2010

Using games to motivate learning has been advocated widely by scholars and increasingly becomes a trend in schools (e.g. Q2L). While most of the innovations focus on digital and video games, it is especially interesting to see BudgetBall,an innovative sport that “highlights the relationships between debt, savings, interest, and taxes”, adopts a different approach.
