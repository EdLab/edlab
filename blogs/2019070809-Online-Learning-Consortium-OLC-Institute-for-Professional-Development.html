Back in 2001, I proposed the Teachers College Distance Learning Project office offer a similar set of learning opportunities when TC was in the early stages of online education so it's not surprising that OLC and organizations (un)like it are offering low cost workshops and certificates in the area of online teaching, instructional design, or blended learning. However, I do have a lot of questions:


  • What is the demand for these courses? Who is taking these courses?
  • How do these courses impact pedagogy and ultimately student learning?
  • What impact does certification have on employment opportunities and salary?
  • What are the opportunities for Teachers College to lead in this domain?

There was one course that did stand out to me. Can you guess which one?
