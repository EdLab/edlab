Hi! My name is Laura

Laura Scheiber

July 17, 2012

Hello, My name is Laura Scheiber and I just joined the EdLab team. I will be working on New Learning Times and very much look forward to meeting you! If you have any ideas that you think would be a great match for NLT, or if you are interested in being involved in some capacity, feel free to contact me. I am all ears and would love to hear your ideas!
