The Electric Run

Brian.Sweeting

May 1, 2015

*Private* For those who like running and all of the lights, The Electric Run is happening in Brooklyn on Saturday 9/5/15. The nighttime 5K course is at the Gateway National Recreation Area (in Deep Brooklyn) and moves through six glowing courselands: rainforest, candy lane, under the sea, rainbow road, powerhouse, and mystery land. And of course it all wraps up with a finish line glowstick dance party. Tickets are on presale now for $29.50. I've created an EdLab team for the Run/Walk/Dance/Cartwheel/Party Wave at 8:15pm.
