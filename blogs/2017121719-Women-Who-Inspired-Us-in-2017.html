Women Who Inspired Us in 2017

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2017


Whether telling stories or reinventing the soccer ball, these women share how they are positively impacting learning. Re-read their interviews for insights into the future of technology and learning.

1. Jessica O. Matthews
Jessica O. Matthews is the CEO and founder of Uncharted Play, a company that makes energy-generated play products that power communities worldwide.

2. Susanna Pollack
As president of the Games for Change festival, Susanna Pollack is advocating for the power of games as drivers of social impact.

3. Sehreen Noor Ali
Sehreen Noor Ali, the head of business development and corporate partnerships for Kaplan's New Economy Skills Training program, is helping tech companies stay competitive.

4. Micaela Blei
Micaela Blei is helping young people find their voices and tell their stories through the Moth Education Program.

5. Hannan Dee
Hannah Dee founded a conference for women computer science students seeking career advice from other successful women in tech.
