EdLab World Cup Madness

Ryan.Allen

June 13, 2018

There are a lot of soccer (football) fans here it EdLab and we have been anxiously awaiting the World Cup for four years. Well, the wait is over and the matches begin tomorrow! While the US (or some other favorite teams) didn't make the cut this year, we can still have a lot of fun watching and rooting for other teams/ players in the global tournament.


To make it a bit more interesting, George and I are starting a World Cup Tournament Pick'em Challenge. It's free to enter and there are no prizes. But the winner will receive the glory of victory, while we will ostracize the loser (just kidding). Do you want to enter? It's easy, just follow these steps: 


1. Click on the link to Fox Sports, the official host of the cup, https://www.foxsports.com/soccer/fifa-world-cup/tournament-pickem 

2. Sign up/ in on Fox Sports 

3. Fill out a bracket

4. Print it out

5. Give it to George or me before 11 AM tomorrow (June 14)   


We will hang them somewhere in the EdLab and keep track of the scores over the next few weeks. Don't worry if you don't know much about soccer. Everyone is welcome to join in the fun. Just pick your favorite national cuisine or something. Good luck pickin!
