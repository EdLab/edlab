Pundit "Speed-dating" Event?!

Kate Meersschaert

February 27, 2012

Private! Hi team EdLab! I would love your thoughts regarding a new marketing event for Pundit! I imagine this event to be structured much like "speed dating," in which prospective dates have only minutes to get to know one-another before moving on to the next "offering." I imagine this model as a potentially fun, slightly off-kilter marketing event for Pundit and a way for TC students to discover new & existent courses and meet the correspondent instructors! Read on for more details and feel free to offer feedback, questions and additional ideas in the comments. The pitch... Do you want to meet different courses, but are just too busy with work and your existent course-load? The team that brought you Pundit has the perfect solution! Speed-dating for courses! Sign-up on Ednode to receive an exclusive invite to this "course dating" event and you just might find true course love*! The details... Each department would be invited to send a representative (professor, GA, past student) to speak about new or existent course offerings. Students would then sign-up to be matched by departmental interest and get 3 minutes with each representative to learn more about their course or offering. The goal of the evening event would be to come-away with new courses you want to take. *course love |korsËˆluv| verb [ trans. ] to fall-head-over heels for your coursework, because it fuels your curiosity, sparks your passion for learning and gets you that much closer to your goals.
