A Tiny Toy Teaches Kids Coding

saraehardman

August 3, 2016


Teachers around the country are introducing Sphero SPRK, a robotic toy that teaches coding, to their classrooms. In this Vialogue, one schoolteacher shares his experience building a curriculum around Sphero. Each student is given their own tiny robot, and the teacher creates challenging coding lessons around them every week.

What the teacher likes most about Sphero is the fact that it fuses learning and play; his students have fun while practicing the basics of programming. He mentions that the lessons children learn overlap with math, science, and even art. For these reasons, the teacher in this video claims that Sphero has "changed the way kids see their own education."
