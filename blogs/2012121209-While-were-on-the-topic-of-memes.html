While we're on the topic of memes...

Nara Kasbergen

December 12, 2012

***Keep Private One of my favorite LOLcats of all time is this guy: He was featured on I Can Has Cheezburger in February of 2009 (back when that was still 'the' central place for memes), so he was still on my mind when in March of 2009 I came across something that inspired me to make my own version: Backstory for this photo: It was taken in Monterey, California, which I was visiting with my then-boyfriend during a spring break road trip we took around California in 2009. I have to admit it's somewhat staged; we saw the restaurant from the car one morning while we were on our way to the Monterey Bay Aquarium (having dated a Korean, I've learned to recognize my name in hangeul -- they are, in fact, the only Korean characters I can recognize on sight), but since it looked like we wouldn't easily be able to find a place to park the car even long enough to take a picture and, more importantly, we were running late for penguin feeding time, we decided to go to the aquarium first and then go back to the restaurant to take a picture (and try their food) later. Which explains why I'm holding a penguin (a souvenir from the aquarium) in the photo. The penguin's name is Steve.
