The Revolution of Email is Coming?

Ting Yuan

October 13, 2009

Google Wave (see a previous post) released an updated version with photo and video embedding functions at the end of last month. The new open-source Google application -- open to 100,000 users for testing on a wider scale -- which a Googler describes as "magical" in the video below -- promises to offer a new way for people to interact and work on the web.

Google Wave blends e-mail, instant messaging, social networks, and workplace collaboration. by CNET.
Google is granting access of Wave to 100,000 users, although more than a million people clamored to be in the test group.

Interesting Update: A Google Wave invite was found for sale on eBay, and had attracted 19 bids, pushing the price up to $70, according to Mashable. If you want to try this new version, please feel free to let me know. =)
