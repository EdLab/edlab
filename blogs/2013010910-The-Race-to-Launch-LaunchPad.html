The Race to Launch LaunchPad...

Janice Joo

January 9, 2013

The Sloan Consortium is coming out with their first Launch Pad initiative which offers support to entrepreneurs in the EdTech space by offering free and full conference registration, a specialized workshop just for them, with guests from venture capitalists to higher ed. A partnership with EdSurge will give each startup a spotlight in their newsletter (publicity!)... and in their own words, Sloan-C's Launch Pad offers, "[t]he priceless opportunity to speak with the world's leading online educators and learn how to improve [one's] product to meet their teaching needs." What do you think? Has Sloan-C figured out how to support edupreneurs? Will this format work? Will they draw the right crowd, or any crowd, if it at all? This sounds like a learning opportunity for EdLab as we think seriously about how to make the right offerings with our own Launch Pad... Share your thoughts!
