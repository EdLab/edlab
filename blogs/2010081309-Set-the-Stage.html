Set the Stage!

Julia Martin

August 13, 2010

Title: Set the stage! : Teaching Italian through theater Editors: Nicoletta Marini-Maio and Colleen Ryan-Scheutz. Publisher: New Haven : Yale University Press, c2010. CHECK IT OUT:  PC1066 .S44 2010 From the Publisher: Set the Stage! is a collection of essays on teaching Italian language, literature, and culture through theater.  From theoretical background to course models, this book provides all the resources that teachers and students need to incorporate the rich and abundant Italian theater tradition into the curriculum. … About the Editors: Nicoletta Marini-Maio is assistant professor of Italian at Dickinson College. Colleen Ryan-Scheutz is associate professor of Italian and director of Italian language instruction at Indiana University. Related Books by Editors: This year, Marini-Maio’s book "Dramatic Interactions: Teaching the Foreign Language, Culture, and Literature through Theater" (forthcoming in 2010)  will be published and Ryan-Scheutz is the author of Sex, the Self, and the Sacred: Women in the Cinema of Pier Paolo Pasolini (University of Toronto, 2007) Google Books Preview Scenario Review
