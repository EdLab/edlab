Most Viewed Profiles of 2017

Brian.Sweeting

December 12, 2017


We've learned a lot from leaders of edtech organizations this year. Here are the most viewed profiles worthy of a re-read.

1. Vaibhav Vashisht
Vaibhav Vashisht is co-founder of Acadly, an edtech platform that integrates an audience response system and a classroom-focused chat platform with class management tools.

2. Jessica Lindl
Jessica Lindl is the Global Head of Education at Unity Technologies with a vision of democratizing creativity for all learners.

3. Dan Hughes
Dan Hughes is President of Learning Machine, a blockchain credentialing company.

4. Sanjaya Mishra
Sanjaya Mishra is an Education Specialist who develops technology-enabled solutions to increase the quality of and access to educational opportunities.

5. Livio Bolzon
Livio Bolzon is a co-founder of Codemoji, a coding platform that encourages students to focus on building intuitive, relatable emojis.
