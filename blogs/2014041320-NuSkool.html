NuSkool

chingfu

April 13, 2014


Nuskool is a lesson platform that curates and develops lesson plans for grades 6-12 in math, English, science and social studies using online videos that reference popular entertainment media such as music, films, and games. Educators can search lesson plans based on grade level, subject area, and entertainment genres, and will find relevant popular media recommendations along with suggested class activities in each lesson plan. Teachers might be surprised to see that there are so many teachable moments in our daily entertainment.

Pros:

Each lesson plan consists of four major components: media, description, procedure, and assessment. This simple structure makes the lessons easy to follow, adopt or adapt to each unique classroom. The wide variety of entertainment genres used in NuSkool, including films, television programs, video games, social networks, sports, and popular culture, are very helpful because teachers can easily find lesson materials that cater to the diverse interests of students in their classrooms.

Cons:

There are many creative lessons on NuSkool, but currently these lessons do not provide guidelines for how they can be connected to popular curriculum standards like the Common Core. In addition, NuSkool lesson plans seem to offer supplemental activities for learning traditional subject matter, but the platform does not provide a curriculum structure for its lesson resources. Although serving as a standalone curriculum tool may not be the goal of NuSkool, it might be helpful to have a curriculum map that draws connections between NuSkool lessons and samples of school curricula so that it can be more easily adopted by teachers.

Our Takeaway:

Entertainment media is at the center of many teenagers’ daily lives, and using interesting and relevant segments from these sources to teach school subjects could potentially enhance student engagement. NuSkool identifies starting points for teachers and provides useful supporting resources. Through use of this system, teachers can make good matches between these creative lessons and their school’s curriculum and may promote engagement in lesson topics through entertainment media.

The Bottom Line:

NuSkool captures teachable moments on entertainment media for learning about grade 6-12 school subjects.
