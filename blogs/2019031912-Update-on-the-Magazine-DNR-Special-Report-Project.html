Update on the Magazine DNR: Special Report Project

Rob.Crawford

March 19, 2019

Thank you to everyone who participated in the EdLab magazine DNR in the fall, and I am happy to say that a new project has come out of that discussion.


Last week Hui Soo, Brian Sweeting, and I met to discuss the magazine concept we brainstormed in the DNR, and that conversation developed into one we think will work even better: a thematic special report. While this idea has been floating around, the conversation around the magazine helped to crystalize it, and we are now going to make it happen.


Published as an ebook, the special report will combine materials from TCR and NLT, for the first time. We can offer the report for sale, and can also distribute it to consulting clients, potential clients, and others, to demonstrate our expertise in a given area. Based on the success of the first report, we may issue additional reports on other subjects.


Ideas for the first report include: "AR/VR and Learning," "Social Emotional Learning," and "The Future of Learning." The publishing team is currently creating prototypes for a DNR next month, which will help in shaping the final publication. Thanks again, and we look forward to sharing our ideas for this new project.
