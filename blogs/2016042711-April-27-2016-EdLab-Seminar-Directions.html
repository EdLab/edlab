April 27, 2016 EdLab Seminar Directions

HSC

April 27, 2016

PART I We will start the seminar today by rating participant comments on 4 different Vialogues. In your groups, log into Vialogues and watch your group’s assigned vialogue. Then individually rate the relevance of each comment to the content in the video. Afterwards, discuss the ratings you assigned to the comments. If time permits, talk about what changes we might make to Vialogues to improve the quality of user discussions.
