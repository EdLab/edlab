A Call for NLT Edlab Review Videos

Demetri Lales

January 10, 2013

**Private Please While looking for edtechs to review for NLT I found that EdSurge has produce short one and an a half minute video reviews on a few notable edtech products. From watching the video it seems to be done from a teacher's perspective and is a bit structured. One interesting section of these videos is when the teacher talks about a "wishlist" for the edtech product in question. Below is the vialogue of one of these reviews. Feel free to critique this review video and don't be afraid to be too harsh. It may give us some great ideas for creating our own edtech review videos. For the Edlab Review column of NLT I believe the most natural next step in its evolution is to include a short review video that can bring the review to life and express the main points of the review in a short amount of time. Since NLT is a mobile publication we would need to emphasize on keeping the video short and to the important points of the review. There are several examples of short review videos that we can look to for inspirations. Although there would need to be a long discussion on whether or not to include Edlab Review videos it might be nice to talk about ways we can create one. There are many questions to think about when creating these types of videos.

  • How would it be structured?
  • Would there be a person in the video or would it be narrated with screenshots?
  • What would be the point of difference of our review video, our "special sauce"?
