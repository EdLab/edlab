Trends in Ed, 1.30.09

jvazquez

January 30, 2009

Happy FRIDAY! Cheers to a good weekend, as well. On to the new stories and innovations for the day. Some cool new products: Academic Earth Academic Earth gives access to “thousands of video lectures from the world's top scholars.” “Top scholars” seems to be defined as professors and lecturers from the nation's most prestigious universities. You'll find lectures from Walter Lewin, Bill Bradley, and other notables here. Unigo Unigo is a social network site that provides reviews for college students, by college students. Users can meet current students and get answers to questions that would normally go unasked. The site even caters to international students who are wondering about the North American college experience. Mi vida loca The BBC launched a free, fun, and interactive language course in Spanish. It teaches students using a thrilling story of mystery and intrigue, set in Madrid, Spain. The lessons are grouped into 22 10-minutes episodes. Watch the trailer after the jump!
