PLAAY Brings AI Tech to Little League and High School Sports

Ryan.Allen

September 20, 2018


"PLAAY uses artificial intelligence (AI) to create personalized highlight videos. Any footage you provide from one or multiple teams will be converted into a personalized highlight reel that is delivered right to your smartphone or desktop." (PLAAY)

Perfect for Little League or High School Sports

Little league and high school sports are a big part of American education. Every year, millions of kids gear up for games on the gridiron, hardwood, or the multitude of other surfaces where sports are played . Parents, coaches, and athletes often film these games to preserve the memories, learn from in-game mistakes, and celebrate success. PLAAY promises to make this video highlight process easier for teams and individuals using AI software. Users can upload game film into PLAAY and it will auto-generate personalized player or team highlight videos.

The software that PLAAY utilizes works better with more footage and diverse angles. So, this might require more buy-in from people at the games, such as parents really considering filming technique and location rather than just focusing on the action. PLAAY won’t turn game footage filmed on an iPhone up in the bleachers into an award-winning 30 for 30.

PLAAY also hopes its services will help student athletes get noticed by colleges. Making a good highlight tape is a difficult task, as seen from the sea of terrible attempts going unwatched on YouTube. Students or parents need to have video editing software, the skills to use the software, and the time to skim through hours of video. PLAAY makes AI do the hard work, generating a video that users can easily tweak with photos, title cards, or other effects.

Old School Cool

While PLAAY’s main service captures current school-aged kids’ sports, they also promise that their software can work with old video. Old films must first be digitized, which may scare away some luddites, and then the AI will process a personalized vintage highlight tape. People can relive their glory days with a new twist, although this service still cannot magically fix damaged, grainy footage from the past.
