TC's History of Innovation

Janet Jang

April 23, 2012

At Edlab's event during the Academic Festival this weekend, there were so many different apps and products that people could touch, look at, and experiment with. One of them was the History of Innovation iPad Magazine that Daniel and I created specifically for the event. For those who weren't able to take a look at it, here it is! The magazine highlights the past accomplishments of TC alumni and professors who pioneered technological or innovative movements during their time. All of the content and inspiration for each decade was discovered through our very own app, PocketKnowledge. We hope that this magazine will strengthen the spirit of EdLab and Teachers College, knowing that we come from an amazing background of innovative and passionate people.
