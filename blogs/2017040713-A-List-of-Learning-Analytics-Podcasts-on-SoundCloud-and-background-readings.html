A List of Learning Analytics Podcasts on SoundCloud and background readings

xk2120

April 7, 2017

The following podcasts are created by Professor Lang and his students in Learning Analytics program at Teachers College, Columbia University. Every podcast begins with educational news, followed by the discussion of weekly paper readings. 

Podcast 7 - Do Professors Matter? (spoiler alert - they don't)
Introduction:
Jon & Ben discuss the top 10 reasons Professors don't matter!
News stories discussed: Data Visualization Map Reveals Educational Attainment Levels in Your Neighborhood, EdSurge, March 14, 2017

discussion paper: 

  • Tomkin, J. H., & Charlevoix, D. (2014). Do Professors Matter?: Using an A/B Test to Evaluate the Impact of Instructor Involvement on MOOC Student Outcomes. In Proceedings of the first ACM Learning@Scale Conference (pp. 71–78). Atlanta, GA: ACM.

Podcast 6 - Courting Controversy
introduction:
Abbas, Mel & Xiang stir the pot of controversy talking neural nets, MOOCs, research labeling and more!

discussion paper: 

  • Tang, S., Peterson, J. C., & Pardos, Z. A. (2016). Deep Neural Networks and How They Apply to Sequential Education Data. In Proceedings of the Third (2016) ACM Conference on Learning @ Scale (pp. 321–324). Edinburgh, Scotland, UK: ACM.
