PART II: NLT DIGITAL OUTREACH

While the above information illustrated our impact in the Twitter-sphere, the second part of this report highlights the connection between our digital outreach and our New Learning Times readership. I’ll highlight both the impact of social media and the NLT newsletter. 

 

First, some context on the newsletter: The NLT newsletter is sent out every Thursday at 2PM via email. The articles featured in the newsletter are sourced from those published sometime between the previous Thursday at 5PM and the current Thursday at 12PM. A total of five articles are featured each week, one from each of the following streams: EdLab Review, NL Sector, Profiles, Research Digest, and Vialogues.


HOW MUCH NLT TRAFFIC CAN BE ATTRIBUTED TO DIGITAL OUTREACH? 

About 8.2% of total traffic to NLT was driven by our digital outreach, which includes email, Twitter, and Facebook. According to Google Analytics, a total of 502 sessions* were initiated via these channels. Here’s a more specific breakdown:
