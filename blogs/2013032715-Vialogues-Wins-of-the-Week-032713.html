Vialogues Wins of the Week: 03.27.13

Kate Meersschaert

March 27, 2013

Private!*** Hi Everyone! Once again, Jo and I would like to share the week's "V-Wins" & updates with you! Usage Stats: New Users: 217 Vialogues Created: 94 Total Visits: 2,147 Twitter followers: 430 Community engagement & anecdotal praise of the platform: Jo was interviewing a candidate and when asked what EdLab projects she was interested in, she said she knew about Vialogues, but not because of her own research about EdLab. Instead, she mentioned that her professor (@NYU) introduced it to the class as an effective edtech tool that facilitates learning around video! Also, Alex Selkirk shared an anecdotal account that when he shared the vialogues resources we created for Parlor when pitching their work to universities... many groups then became as interested in Vialogues & not jus Parlor! ;) Alex shared this insight at Demo Night 2 and I plan to follow-up with him regarding the specific universities he referenced.
