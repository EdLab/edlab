Confessions of a 'belated' adopter

Arianna Choi

February 11, 2009

I swear I'm very open to trying new things. I'm neither allergic to a certain type of food nor have strong enough of political or religious beliefs to abstain from reading/watching/doing anything. BUT one thing that I've been reluctant to try is acculturating myself to 'keep up' with new web tools. I'm making a confession right here that the amount of new web tools out there is SCARY! To add more stress, some tools are less successfully marketed than others, new tools are developed all the time and each tool has a different usage. So there was my problem and here is a solution = a starting point for reference. It was one of those manic clicking of links after links which led me to find this helpful one-page guide to...organizing the list of today's hot web 2.0 tools. Thank you Brian Solis, thank you, thank you.
