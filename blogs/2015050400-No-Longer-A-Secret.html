No Longer A Secret

Evans Frimpong

May 4, 2015

This news by Secret, an app that allows users to anonymously share information with those in their circle, to close up shop has sent reverberations through Silicon Valley. Secret's rapid ascent and demise highlights the sometimes-unpredictable nature of this current tech boom. When should a tech business decide to close up shop? Lack of funding? Lack of users?
