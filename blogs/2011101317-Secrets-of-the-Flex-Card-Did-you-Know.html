Secrets of the Flex Card: Did you Know???

Shevawn Battiste

October 13, 2011

Why can't I use my 20 free print quota in the TC Library? Why does the TC Library operate on a separate system from the CU Libraries? Why can't I use the funds I deposited onto my student account with Columbia in the TC Library to print? These are common questions that we often encounter that we ourselves struggle to answer.  Sure, Here in the TC Library, we often encounter students who are frustrated about the fact that we don't honor the 20 free print quota that everyone receives at the main campus libraries.  We totally understand their frustration.  I mean... how confusing is that? While I'm not writing to provide you with an explanation for TC's separate-but-equal arrangement with Columbia, I am hoping that the information I have to share will provide you with some comfort. Have you ever thought to yourself, "Why on earth should I go through the trouble of depositing money onto my flex account to print in the TC Library, when I have already purchased CUIT Printing Dollars?"  Or, "Why should I pay $5 to purchase a flex card when I only have 2 pages to print?" Well, I have the scoop for YOU!  Did you know that you can actually use any flex account funds for purchases other than printing and photocopying? http://library.columbia.edu/indiv/socwk/services.html
