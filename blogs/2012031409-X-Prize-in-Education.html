X-Prize in Education

Fred Rossoff

March 14, 2012

Peter Diamandis, who's been offering multi-million dollar prizes for innovative work in fields like spaceflight and medecine, has decided to do the same for education. Unfortunately, he can't think of what the prize should be for. You can see some other prizes his foundation has or is offering on the X-prize foundation's wikipedia page, including $10 million for a medical diagnostic device. Any ideas on what the contest should be?
