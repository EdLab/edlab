SpaceX and the Future of Space Travel

Duncan Asiedu

July 15, 2012

While browsing the official 60 minutes app for Google Chrome, I stumbled upon a documentary about the aerospace company SpaceX. SpaceX was founded in 2002 by entrepreneur Elon Musk, who is the co-founder of Paypal as well as the founder and CEO of Tesla Motors. The company recently made news when their Dragon spacecraft successfully visited the International Space Station, making SpaceX the first private company to achieve such a feat. Musk's long term vision with his company is to help with the commercialization of space travel. In fact, he has personally stated that he hopes to put a man on Mars in the next 10-20 years.
