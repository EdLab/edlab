The VOICE Shoot @ U of MD

Kate Meersschaert

January 20, 2012

Hi Team EdLab! I am reporting from The U. of Maryland School of Education. We are here to shoot videos for The VOICE of contributors to a special issue of Teachers College Record focused on "Teaching Quality." Read The intro to this special issue here. Patricia A. Alexander from the U of MD is the first interviewee and her session starts by Dr. Alexander explaining the meaning of epistemology in the Ed world... Some of the goals of the paper she co-authored include exploring what "justifications" educators hold for their beliefs about "truths" & the philosophical origins of big ideas in education. She breaks-down individual vs societal beliefs about knowledge in 2 focus areas for mathematics & reading. Dr. Alexander also explores the 4-year High Quality Teaching study.
