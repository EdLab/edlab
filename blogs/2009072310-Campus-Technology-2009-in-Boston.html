Campus Technology 2009 in Boston

Arianna Choi

July 23, 2009

Pranav recently proposed that we start a shared doc to create a‘pub list' (no worries if you don't get it, I just made that up.) This pub list would compile a list of journals and conferences that are of EdLabbers interest. It's like a media list that PR executives treasure, so that they can readily pitch their stories to different media outlets or more specifically target certain journalists. Anyway, here's one addition: Campus Technology 2009 Has anyone heard of it? Or better yet, going? It's $$$ but founders of Mendeley will be there to host a breakout session:

TH37: Creating the Social Research Tools of the Future Victor Henning, Founder and Director, Mendeley Ltd., and Jan Reichelt, Co-Founder and Director, Mendeley Ltd. This session travels to the year 2019. Using the “Collective Headline Mindmapping” exercise, attendees will collect and discuss the social research tools that made news in campus technology in the past 10 years. What became possible, what happened, what do we wish didn't happen? Attendees will then contrast the results with a social research tool developed during the session.
