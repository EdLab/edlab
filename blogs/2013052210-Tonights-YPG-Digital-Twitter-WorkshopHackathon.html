Tonight's YPG Digital Twitter Workshop/Hackathon

Kate Meersschaert

May 22, 2013

Mabel found & blogged re: tonight's exciting event sponsored by YPG (Young to Publishing Group). I thought I would provide more details before we attend. Here is the event description and agenda for the evening:

Whether you're a Twitter novice or a master with a Klout score through the roof, YPG's Twitter Hackathon will give you the tools to tweet with the best of them. Speaking of the best of them, Associate Marketing Director of Atavist Books Ryan Chapman will be on hand to answer questions and give feedback about how to use Twitter to meet your needs. An early session dedicated to total beginners who want to learn the basics will be followed by a more in-depth session exploring tips and tools to broadcast your (or your publisher's) message and generate discussion. 6:30pm — Introduction and account creation (Beginner) 7pm — Who to follow, lists, hashtags, and engagement (Beginner — Intermediate) 7:25pm — Pro tips and tricks (Intermediate) 7:30pm — In Conversation with Ryan Chapman of The Atavist PLUS! Best practices and a project to hack into.
I am excited to attend with Mabel and report-out on the blog soon! #ypgdigital
