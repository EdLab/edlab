Sacrifice 10 Friends on Facebook, Get a Free Whopper

HSC

January 9, 2009

I'm posting this for all the avid Facebook users and Whopper fans in the EdLab. Burger King has developed a Facebook application called Whopper Sacrifice. Here is the deal: Delete 10 people from your Facebook Friends List and receive a coupon for a free Whopper. Most of you know I am not on "The Facebook" so I can't participate in the promotion. But I look forward to hearing reports back from people who eliminate their "fair-weather web friendships." So far, over 102,00 people have been sacrificed (meaning over 1,020 free Whopper coupons are out there). Someone should create a Facebook app that allows people to donate their coupons to food pantries like City Harvest. Download the app here.
