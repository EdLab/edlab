“I Want to Move Out in the Daylight!" by Warren Zanes

HSC

August 14, 2011

Warren Zanes, executive director of Steve Van Zandt's Rock and Roll Forever Foundation (R&RFF) and our primary collaborator on the R&RFF Project, was recently featured in the NJ Star Ledger for various projects he is working on. Some of the highlights from the article: - He's working with Steven Van Zandt to develop a program to teach rock history to high school and middle school students. - He's writing an authorized biography of Tom Petty... - He conducted some of the interviews for the Martin Scorsese-directed documentary, “George Harrison: Living in the Material World,” which debuts on HBO in October. - He's also a singer-songwriter-guitarist whose third solo album, “I Want to Move Out in the Daylight!,” came out July 26.
