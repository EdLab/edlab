Free Survey and Poll Creation Tools

Kate Meersschaert

June 1, 2012

I was just notified through a LinkedIn "EdTech Startups" Group message, of this exhaustive listing of "free and easy" survey and poll-creation tools targeted at educators. This might be a great list for the EdLab to explore as we evolve the next evolution of SurveySidekick! In addition, the list itself was hosted on the site, "Educational Technology and Mobile Learning," which is dedicated to straight-forward edtech reviews and listings of resources. Also, a great site to explore as the EdLab moves-forward with "project BLT!" Finally, LinkedIn's Groups feature would be a great place to receive feedback and create awareness for all our new apps!
