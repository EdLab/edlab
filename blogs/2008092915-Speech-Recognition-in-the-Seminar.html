Speech Recognition in the Seminar

Anthony Cocciolo

September 29, 2008

I thought I would try running this past week's EdLab seminar through Meety to see if it was capturing the meeting live, what external content it would have generated. By and large, the content was not terribly great, but there were some hilarious generations, such as:

  • How can I become an independently wealthy artist? And some more sober findings:
  • TeacherNet, Creating a learning environment for the twenty-first century
  • Library Camp NYC
  • Electronic School Online: A new study shows the effectiveness -- and the limitations -- of school technology
  • Supporting Different Learning Styles in an Online Learning Environment: Does it Really Matter in the Long Run?
  • Cyberinfrastructure, Data, and Libraries, Part 1: A Cyberinfrastructure Primer for Librarians There were also some real-odd balls (not sure how it picked these up):
  • Lisa Loeb: The Way It Really Is You can find all the quacky content by logging in and selecting "EdLab Seminars"
