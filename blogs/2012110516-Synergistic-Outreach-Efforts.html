Synergistic Outreach Efforts?

Janice Joo

November 5, 2012

I got an email... and the subject title was: "Eventbrite and SurveyMonkey Team Up to Simplify Event Feedback" If you don't know already, Eventbrite claims to give you "all the online tools you need to bring people together for an event and sell tickets." And SurveyMonkey is a free online survey and questionnaire tool. And together, they have partnered to provide an integrated feedback system! Here are the benefits of such a collaboration: - Creating new values for the user (value = simplified feedback collection) - Sharing user audiences... which means more users for both parties Let's bring this idea back to EdLab... are there any other services within Teachers College (TC) (be it research- or academic-related, or maybe neither) that SurveySidekick could partner up with to provide an EVEN better experience for our users? Is there an EventBrite equivalent at TC? Should we consider partnering with Eventbrite ourselves? What do you think?
