Girls in Computer Science: Breaking Down Stereotypes

Laura Scheiber

June 12, 2013


While computer science has traditionally been a male-dominated field, in this video women share their experiences of studying and working in computer science. By addressing a number of misconceptions, such as the notion that computer science is an isolated and boring endeavor when in fact it often is a collaborative and creative field, the women describe why more people should get into computer science.

With a focus on incorporating more females into the mix, the participants offer opinions on why women traditionally have been a minority in computer science. They also highlight the appeal of programming.

Why do you think girls have traditionally shied away from computer science, and what do you think would be effective in shifting this imbalance? Add your thoughts to the conversation on Vialogues.
