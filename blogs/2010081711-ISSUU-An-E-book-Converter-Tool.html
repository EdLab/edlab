ISSUU: An E-book Converter Tool

Ting Yuan

August 17, 2010

For EdLab developers who are interested in creating future e-book tools: I enjoyed using ISSUU very much which I used for converting WORD, PDF, slides, and comics I created via Comic Life to an online embeddable book with flipping pages. It is a convenient converter tool (which is also used by lots of magazines and newspapers). You can simply select a document which is uploaded into your ISSUU database, and the converting process takes a few mins.
