Wiki World's Fair this Weekend!

Kate Meersschaert

July 2, 2012

Unfortunately I will be out of town, but I thought I would pass-along this NYC-based opportunity to explore the world of Wikipedia and perhaps even information literacy. If you go, please write about it on the blog and share pictures!
