Docdel Research - 1

Pranay Dharmale

December 7, 2010

I have been researching with Ankit about the Docdel System. Our primary focus is the different research questions which could be answered using the data being stored in the existing Docdel System. Also, we are trying to find the different research questions which we want the next version of the system to answer. Docdel is very rich system in terms of the data it collects. With the existing data we can answer questions such as -

  • What are the most popular resources requested?
  • What are the most popular sources (like TCR, Harvard Education Review etc.) requested?
  • What is the course-wise distribution of the resources being requested?
  • Courses making most and the least use of the system.
  • What are the Courses not using Docdel at all ?
    • We can also use the Docdel data to find related readings for a course and then can suggest the same to the user. What are other interesting research questions that we might want the Docdel System to record for us? I'll be posting the data analysis of the system soon. Stay tuned.
