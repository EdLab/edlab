Need Help Creating Your Digital Portfolio?

Adrienne Garber

February 11, 2010

In light of all of the displays and other media the EdLab created for the SECTtOR Design Event this week, it begs the question, how should we be archiving the SECTtOR design pieces and using them to create a digital portfolio for the EdLab? The Dwyer Center, in conjunction with the Harlem Arts Alliance, is hosting a timely workshop next week for new and established artists and will focus on techniques for creating digital images of your work in order to develop your digital portfolio for presentation via web-based and other technologies. Emphasis will be placed on proper preparation of work for photography. Those of us interested in the staging and archiving of EdLab design work (or even your personal work) should attend! Details:

  • Date: Monday February 15th
  • Time: 6:30pm — 8:30pm
  • Location: Dwyer Center, 258 St. Nicholas Avenue, entrance at 123rd Street between St. Nicholas Avenue and Frederick Douglass Boulevard
  • Cost: Admission is $25
  • RSVP: Register online at www.harlemaa.org, call (347) 735 — 4280 or email info@harlemaa.org
