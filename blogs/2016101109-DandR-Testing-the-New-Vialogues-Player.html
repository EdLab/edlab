D&R: Testing the New Vialogues Player

jvazquez

October 11, 2016

Our older Vialogues player served us well ok these past few years but since Flash has become so 2000-and-late, it's become necessary to make some HTML5-friendly updates so that it continues to play nicely across all browsers. We've designed and built a new player to meet the demands of the new world order but now we need your help to test out what we've got to make sure we're ready to roll it out. You've been separated into 5 groups: Android, iPads, Macs, PCs, and iPhone. You'll be testing from this site: https://dev-nlt.tc-library.org/. You can test the Vialogues player on either the Vialogues or SiNY streams unless otherwise specified in the testing doc. First, run through the entire testing doc individually.

  • For PC and Mac testers, divvy up browser duty (so one person will take Firefox, one Chrome, one Safari, and one Internet Explorer (if applicable))
  • Make notes liberally!
  • To test social media sharing: Take a screenshot of the posted result. You can delete this from your timelines immediately.
  • We will collect everyone's sheets at the end of the D&R.
Then, compare your results as a group Finally, we'll have a large group discussion of your first impressions, general thoughts, and findings. You can similarly add all of that to this post, if you are so inclined.
