Strike a Pose: Increasing Perceptions of Ourselves in 2 minutes

Karen Bryner

September 3, 2013

I've been thinking about this TED talk pretty much non-stop since I watched it over the weekend. I'm fascinated by it on several different levels. Amy Cuddy flips around the well-established concept that our body language influences what others think about us and argues that our body language also influences what we think about ourselves. In other words, if we maintain particular body positions or poses for two minutes, our levels of self-confidence, for example, increase or decrease accordingly. Is my self opinion or sense of power that plastic? On the other hand, I do like the idea of "fake it until you become it," as she puts it.
