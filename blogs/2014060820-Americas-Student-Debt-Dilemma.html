America's Student Debt Dilemma

AbdulMalik

June 8, 2014

Student loans are probably one of the biggest problems that college students stress about. This article explores how college students, including student athletes, are affected by America's "student debt dilemma." The article further explains that, even the fortunate students athletes who receive full scholarships end up taking additional loans to cover housing and other expenses. On the other hand, some college athletes only receive either partial scholarships or no scholarships at all. In this case, student athletes who have partial or no scholarship are in the same category as students who do not receive any aid or scholarship. It is necessary for student athletes to be educated about student loans and the repayment process during the recruiting process.

This article is very interesting in the sense that there are so many student athletes (myself included) that could relate to this. It is not too late for student athletes to ask questions about their financial package. The recruiting process for most colleges simply overlooks the financial aspect of an athletes' education that could negatively affect the student.
