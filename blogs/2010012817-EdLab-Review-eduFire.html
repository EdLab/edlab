EdLab Review: eduFire

Arianna Choi

January 28, 2010

eduFire Tags: live video, business, language, exam prep, private tutoring www.edufire.com Price: free classes, pay per class rates, and $29 per month for unlimited classes Overall 2.7 Design 2.6 Pedagogy 2.8 [The Good] Adobe Acrobat Connect Pro, the delivery platform for all eduFire classes, supports real-time voice, video, slide presentation, screen share, whiteboarding, and private/group chat - all of which are very intuitive therefore easy to use. www.eduFire.com is also well-designed for easy search, registration, and communication with other members of the eduFire community. [The Bad] Once the online synchronous class begins, everyone is confined to what the instructor provides within that particular learning space. Unless, the student decides to not give full attention to the class and do other things, what you see is what you get. [The Bottom Line] The e-learning platform scores high points and the same goes for features that support social networking within edufire.com. This leaves the rest on instructor's teaching skills and appropriate educational resources. So, find a great teacher - wherever they are on this planet - and learn via eduFire. Recommended uses - Learn foreign languages from native speakers residing in that country. - Receive private tutoring without having to consider the teacher's or your travel cost. - Teach a course yourself to a worldwide audience; if you get good reviews from your students, you will have more students which leads to more monetary rewards as well.
