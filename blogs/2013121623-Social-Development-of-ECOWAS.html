Social Development of ECOWAS

abagigah92

December 16, 2013

UNESCO is holding a series of forums in Accra, Ghana to help construct national policies for West Africa's regional bloc ECOWAS. This forum is an opportunity for all stakeholders to discuss the social and economic development in the region. The forum provides a platform for leaders to sit and discuss policy issues and other opportunities that will help grow the economy of the region. One of the major issues that was discussed at the forum concerned “The Social Inclusion Challenges of Environmental Change” which consist of the MOST Programme and Social Inclusion”. These programs are set to implement global environment changes and create social transformations by relating the processes of environmental change in West African societies including climate change, biodiversity loss and freshwater scarcity.
