Liberia Rebuilds Education System

abagigah92

April 27, 2015

The civil war in Liberia destroyed much countries educational infrastructure and school system. This left thousands of kids out of the classrooms and in the streets for over a decade. During the war and for the most part after the war, parents feared letting their kids outside.Schools were destroyed and committed teachers had no place to teach. This difficult conditions resulted in schools closing down. It was impossible to manage an educational system in a war zone. This resulted in the kidnapping of young kids and turning them into child soldiers. Liberia has been progressing since the end of the war and with the help from the World Bank, Liberia is now able to set up schools and programs that will develop both teachers and students. The main goal is to improve education system, which requires additional schools, rehabilitation of schools that were destroyed and better trained teachers.
