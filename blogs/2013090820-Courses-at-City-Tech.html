Courses at City Tech

Bachir Diarrassouba

September 8, 2013

During my first two weeks of New York City College Technology, I've been learning basic topics in various classes. I'm currently majoring in Communications, though from there I plan on branching off and pursuing a degree in marketing. The one thing I've noticed during the course over the first several weeks is the importance of taking several different classes as it helps balance out my overall schedule and also helps those who are yet undecided about a major finally pick one based on a core class. So far, Introduction to Psychology has been one of favorite classes as it allows me to see how people think and react. As a future marketing major, a course like this will allow me to see how to push products out in the world and what to expect from potential consumers. It can even help me know how to better promote a product like Vialogues. It is vital to be able to study the way people operate, how they think and what they think about. Such information like this is important when you are trying to introduce a product or service to the world.
