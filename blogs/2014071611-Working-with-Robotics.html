Working with Robotics

Kafoumba Doumbia

July 16, 2014

Our nation is facing a challenge encouraging more students to engage in STEM. However, some schools, such as Cloonan Middle School in Stamford, Connecticut, are doing their best change this situation. Cloonan Middle School has partnered with West Point to help provide them with tools for a robotic workshop. The workshop was held to engage students in the learning of STEM and teach them how to program a robot. I personally think that this is a great program for middle schoolers and we should think about spreading such ideas nationwide. This program will not only help those students pass their classes, and prepare them for future educational challenges, it will help them become problem solvers of the next generation.
