Digital Health Platform Delivers Evidence-based Happiness Solutions

DebraLee

December 3, 2017


"Happiness is a complex state of mind. We understand that. But we can teach you skills that can profoundly change the way you see the world" (Happify Health)

Happiness is a State of Mind

Just as Aristotle believed true happiness only comes from a life with meaning, Happify is convinced that holistic wellbeing comes from positive psychology, mindfulness, and cognitive behavioral therapy, served up in an evidence-based app. In an increasingly stressful and complex world, Happify, a New York-based startup, makes over 60 digital programs and tools available to the stressed and downright miserable 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The aim is to motivate users who want to address their emotional needs, which can range from general well-being to chronic health conditions including depression, anxiety, chronic pain, and insomnia.

Road Test Happify

The activities on Happify’s digital platform are derived from interventions that have demonstrated effectiveness in peer-reviewed research and include preventative programs like "Happiness Starts with Self-Knowledge" and "Get Unstuck." To build skills for greater emotional wellbeing, tracks like "Uncover Your Potential Through Mindfulness", and "Mindfulness at Work" offer guided meditation. And if you’re battling mild to moderate depression or anxiety you can try "Conquer Your Negative Thoughts and Overcome Anxiety" or "The Superstress Solution." There is also support for the emotional health challenges that come with the strain of living with a chronic condition. If you’re skeptical, Happify’s qualified team of research scientists and health practitioners invite you take a free 10-day trial, which is worth the experience even if you don’t suffer from any of the morbidities they treat; the downtime alone is, well, a very pleasant interruption to the day.
