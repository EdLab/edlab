How the HoloLens Helps Designers

saraehardman

March 13, 2017


The HoloLens has proven itself useful in medical education, and now it has extended its reach into building design and architecture. Designers at the University of Cambridge are using the HoloLens to plan and build buildings. The mixed reality device allows designers to view their blueprints as 3D visualizations so they can explore their structures digitally before breaking ground. This gives them a tool to more easily spot problems in their plans and to finesse their designs.

Not quite augmented reality, not quite virtual reality, the HoloLens is a mixed reality device, which means that users can view holographic images superimposed on the world around them. In architecture, this could give designers the ability to leave behind the 2D blueprints and instead walk around a digital representation of their buildings as they would appear in real life.
