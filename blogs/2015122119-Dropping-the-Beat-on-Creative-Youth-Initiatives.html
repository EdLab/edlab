Dropping the Beat on Creative Youth Initiatives

Jenny Shen

December 21, 2015


Five years ago, Wesley Thomas wanted to utilize his DJing profession to create a DJ school. This marked the beginning of Aspired Education CIC, a community-based enterprise that seeks to provide creative outlets and workshops for young people in the Birmingham area of the U.K.

Aspired Education CIC offers workshops in everything from DJing and dance to music production and drama, and through mentorships and courses, students can flex their creative muscles and build their confidence in their work.
