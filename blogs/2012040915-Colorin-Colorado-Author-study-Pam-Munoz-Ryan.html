Colorín Colorado...: Author study, Pam Muñoz Ryan

Veronica Garza

April 9, 2012

About the Author: Born and raised in California and encouraged by her college professor, Pam am Muñoz Ryan is the author of many children's and young adult books. Before becoming a writer, she worked as an early childhood teacher and after school coordinator.
