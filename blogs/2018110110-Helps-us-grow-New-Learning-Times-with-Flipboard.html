Helps us grow New Learning Times with Flipboard

Toland.Lawrence

November 1, 2018

As a part of our effort to grow the reach of New Learning Times, we’ve recently decided to add our content into Flipboard.


What’s that?


It’s a distribution platform that curates content from all over the world for users based on their interests. Basically, a user lists the topics they are interested in and swipes through various articles presented by Flipboard. It’s basically Tinder for news.


Why Flipboard?


We don’t just want to increase website visits. We want to connect with more of the people who are actively interested in education technology. Because Flipboard curates information based on each user’s unique interests, we increase the odds that our content is reaching the right people.


We want to put some effort into growing our presence on the platform to see if it could have an impact. That’s where you can help!


One way that NLT can increase its popularity on Flipboard is by receiving more  likes and comments on its “flipped” (posted) content. It also helps if people “flip” our content into their “magazines” (the digital equivalent of scrapbooks filled with article clippings).


So if you should accept it, we have a short mission for you.


First you need to sign up for Flipboard.


  1. Go to https://flipboard.com.

  2. Click Sign up.

  3. Enter your name, email, and password.

  4. Select a topic you are interested in.


Then, you are authorized for your mission:


  1. Once you sign up, you will land on your Flipboard homepage. Click the “What is your passion?” link to add additional topics. We’d recommend Technology or Education.

  2. Click the edit pencil.



  1. Select “Create Magazine.” Name your personal magazine, click next, and click out of the window.

  2. Now, use the search to find New Learning Times.

  3. You’ll land on our homepage. Please follow us.

  4. Scroll down to our articles. Hover over and click the red plus button to flip an article. Do that for 4 more articles.



  1. Like 10 articles and add comments to 5




    Thanks for your support and don’t hesitate to reach out to anyone on the publishing team if you have questions.
