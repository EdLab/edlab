Vialogues Monthly Report (August 2019)

Christine.Odenath

September 10, 2019


In case the image doesn't load, you can view the report as a google doc here, or download a PDF version of the report from the research Rhizr. I've also provided some highlights:

  • 22.3K video plays
  • 2.3K new comments
  • 272 video uploads
  • 16.3K unique visitors
  • 489 new users
  • Referral Sources: 2.5k direct; 3.2K via search; 14.6k via websites


And key takeaways:

  • Total visits this summer (Jun-Aug) fell 6.5% below visits last summer. August ‘19 visits were down 11% compared to Aug. ‘18. 
  • 17% of visits this August were referrals from tcrecord.org, presumably from The Voice homepage features.
  • Toward the end of August, both daily visits and new user sign-ups saw a notable uptick. Increased .edu email sign-ups came predominantly from Syracuse and Universidad Casa Grande (Ecuador). Increased new users were also the likely culprit for an increase in in direct visits, which grew 200% between 8/25 and 8/28). 
  • On 8/14 and 8/15, 65% of all new user sign ups came from Osceola County schools (FL). 76% of new sign ups on 8/16 came from Canada’s Montebello Unified School District.
