What is the flavor of the mobile-media in education debate?

Doug Beacom

February 16, 2009

I think if we're putting energy and resources into making mobile apps for education we should also be actively debating the role of mobile media in educational settings. Enter today's story in the Times: Industry Pitching Cellphones as Teaching Tool You can read My comment to this article and I'd be interested in continuing this conversation on this blog or in the EdLab. As one commenter points out, pilot studies almost always have positive outcomes, usually dependent on WHO has funded the pilot. In this case, it is Qualcom, who also funds the High Tech High village in San Diego, the location for our 'GlobalizEd: Design Principles' video published last week.
