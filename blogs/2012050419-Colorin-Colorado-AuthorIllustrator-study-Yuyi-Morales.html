Colorín Colorado...: Author/Illustrator study, Yuyi Morales

Veronica Garza

May 4, 2012

About the Author: Yuyi is of Mexican descent and immigrated to the United States in 1994.  She began drawing at a young age and was always inspired by the storytelling that took place during her childhood.
