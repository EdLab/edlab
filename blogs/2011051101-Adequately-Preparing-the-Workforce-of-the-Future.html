Adequately Preparing the Workforce of the Future?

Amelia Grohman

May 11, 2011

I don't know if everyone caught the infographic posted by Good on Monday about the dramatic decline of per-capita jobs for workers without college degrees since 1973, which is a pretty good advertisement for collaborator University of Phoenix. It made me think about how one might create an infographic covering the much thornier issues that the US Department of Ed and Justice Departments have been wrestling with: the net impact, positive and negative, of for-profit colleges and how they should be regulated, prosecuted or given better incentives to refrain from predatory lending and truly prepare students for the workforce.
