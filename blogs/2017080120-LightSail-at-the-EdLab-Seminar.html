LightSail at the EdLab Seminar

EdLab Studios

August 1, 2017


LightSail strives to "turn students into readers." Through the integration of this adaptive literacy solution in the balanced literacy curriculum, students across all grades increase their reading level, reading time, and comprehension skills. In July of 2017 Lauren Bochicchio Beaver visited the EdLab to offer a demo of the application and share how LightSail fits into current reading programs.
