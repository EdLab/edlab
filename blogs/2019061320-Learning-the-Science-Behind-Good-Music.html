Learning the Science Behind Good Music

Toland.Lawrence

June 13, 2019


Musical Instruments, Sound, Perception, and Creativity is an interdisciplinary course offered at Princeton University that teaches students about sound through many lenses, including physics, math, cognition, perception, and interface design, among others. The course provides learning through lectures, precepts, labs, and talks by visiting artists so that students are exposed to the material in many ways. In one lab, students even create their own musical instrument using a PVC pipe. They also engage in discussions on the creative process that goes into creating great music. By engaging with the science of music in this way, students gain a new appreciation for what music does and are inspired to engage with it in new ways.
