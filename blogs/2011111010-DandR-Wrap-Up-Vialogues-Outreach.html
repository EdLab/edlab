D&R-Wrap Up: Vialogues Outreach

Skanda Amarnath

November 10, 2011

*KEEP PRIVATE* I received a lot of awesome feedback from everyone regarding how I plan on marketing Vialogues to major educational video content providers such as Khan Academy and Justice with Michael Sandel. Some of the suggestions: Only include short comments that can easily be read while watching the video for the Vialogues that will be viewed by said providers. It is important to highlight misconceptions, a problem that can be more easily addressed in Vialogues than in a typical Youtube video. The importance of getting feedback from providers. Making clear that multiple discussions/Vialogues can be created for a single video. For Justice videos, ensure that the discussion does not stray away from the discussion questions. Perhaps only allow the user to respond to prompted questions. You should be able to see the results of polls once you submit a response. For Khan Academy Videos, give the moderator the power to remove incorrect answers to questions. I might throw some others up here as well later in th day. Let's hope we can hit a home run with at least one of these content providers!
