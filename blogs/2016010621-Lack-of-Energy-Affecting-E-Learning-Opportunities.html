Lack of Energy Affecting E-Learning Opportunities

AbdulMalik

January 6, 2016

The problems facing education in the developing world is still enormous despite the progress over the past 15 years. Schools are overcrowded due, there is a dearth of trained teachers and there is a great lack of access to essential materials such as books. As a result of these challenges, several measures, such as using technology, have been put in place. This is comes with a major problem: chronic power outages. The constant power outages hinders the rapid development of digital education in places such as sub-Sahara Africa. The advancement in technology has made learning easily and freely accessible to anyone anywhere. However, a lack of access to electricity means large portions of students are left out of the technological loop.
