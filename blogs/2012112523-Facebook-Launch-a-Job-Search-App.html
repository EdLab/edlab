Facebook Launch a Job Search App

abagigah92

November 25, 2012

The first thing that springs to mind when people think of social networking is Facebook. The same is true for LinkedIn when people think of professional networking. Facebook has recently jumped in the space of professional networking by launching a job search app. The idea to launch the app started in last October through a partnership with the U.S Department of Labor and other non-for profit labor organizations. With the launch, Facebook could well be on their way to their coronation as kings of professional networking instead of Linkedln. Since Facebook's announcement, Linkedln's share, usually robust, has dropped slightly, further fueling anxiety among its shareholders about Facebook's app. The jobs app by includes listings from services such as Monster, a move that doesn't sit too well with Monster's investors as it is essentially helping Facebook in a domain that it also competes. In its press release for the app, Facebook is hoping that its social media connections will inspire success in professional networking with the help of friends, families and communities on Facebook. With the Job Search App available, Facebook can now be the next step towards finding a job or career.
