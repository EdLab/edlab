New Vialogues Release is out!

fanwar

March 2, 2011

The Vialogues team is proud to announce the release of an updated vialogue site with new features and many bug fixes. Included in this release are: - Poll comments & analytics - Discussions about YouTube videos - Permissions improvements, including the ability to give access to a vialogue based on secret links. We also have a specially featured video on the homepage giving an introduction to what the tool is for newcomers. We're looking forward to people using these and other features in a variety of educational settings. Thanks to the entire Vialogues team in getting this release out: Pranav, Stephen, Yudan, Megha, Joann, Hui Soo, Zhou, Angela, Yana, Gonzalo, Josh and others. Enjoy and keep the feedback coming!
