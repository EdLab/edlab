Meme-ified Intentions

Jenny Shen

October 15, 2015


With many edtech conversations now focusing on how to bring social media into schools to support learning, teachers are more frequently incorporating not just Twitter, but also Instagram and Vine. But with platforms like Vine driving attention to viral rather than informational content, is this really the most beneficial way to integrate social media? Vine videos frequently feature physical comedy and slapstick humor, people tripping and cats falling off tables—things that quickly and easily become internet memes in a matter of hours. Even though efforts to use Vine in the classroom may be well-intentioned and educational, is it the best platform to engage students through social media?
