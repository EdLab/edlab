Bilingual Education In NYC Schools

AbdulMalik

April 6, 2016

New York City brings together people who share extremely diverse cultures and backgrounds. It is thus probably beneficial to set up all aspects of our environment to meet the needs of this cultural fusion. Language is a huge part of culture and it should not be a barrier for education in New York City. There is a new program that offers classes in foreign languages. The languages include but are not limited to French, Arabic and Creole. Students whose first language is not English will be able to learn the traditional courses like math and science in their native languages. In order to ensure that students are able to communicate outside the classroom, there will receive tutoring in English. I believe this program will help develop students (especially non-English speaking immigrants) academically without language being a barrier to their education and progress.
