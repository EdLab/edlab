An Online Game about Hurricane Katrina and Possible Youth Civic Engagement

chingfu

January 4, 2009

Check out this online game about Hurricane Katrina. The game is created by a team of Global Kids Youth Leaders at a Brooklyn high school in parternership with Gamepill Inc. The game "recognizes local heroes that emerged during the disaster while educating its players about the essentials of disaster readiness." This project illustrates a new way of youth civic participation by having young students learn and explore social issues through media (online game) production. It would be interesting to know more about what role these students play in building the game and what they learn from this project.
