Vialogues Wins of the Week: 05.08.13

Kate Meersschaert

May 8, 2013

***Private! Hi Everyone! Once again, Jo and I would like to share the week's "V-Wins", updates and ideas with you! Usage stats: New Users: 131 Total Visits: 2,433 Vialogues Created: 116 Community engagement: As we mentioned in our last "wins" posting Megha and Kate presented select EdLab tools to a small group of founders at Socratic Labs and the head of the cohort, Ash, officially announced that they plan to use Vialogues to receive feedback on their filmed pitches. He sent the following message via email:

For Socratic Labs, I'd like to start using your tools more in our own process. For example, we could use Vialogues to help our founders perfect their pitches and crowdsource feedback. We'll try it out with the cohort and see what we come up with! Lastly, we'll send along additional events/ideas for your Edtech Takes.
