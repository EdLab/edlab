What's Your Major/Concentration Worth?

HSC

August 3, 2007

Watch this commentary by Stephen Colbert on variable tuition costs for different college majors. In particular, I appreciated Colbert's classification of all knowledge into three tiers: 1) Marketable (e.g., business) 2) Non-Marketable (e.g., history) 3) You know this is killing your parents! (e.g., classics) Where do you think he would put "education?" I found this clip entertaining, particularly in light of some of our conversations at the last EdLab seminar (e.g., What should be the mission of the EdLab/Library?). Maybe one of the After Ed shows will tackle this issue in the coming weeks.
