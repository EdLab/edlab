Learning 2.0 Conference, Free to Attend :)

Sharon Hsiao

August 13, 2012

Learning 2.0 Conference, a worldwide virtual event and free to attend, August 20 - 24, 2012. It's an event held in conjunction with Connected Educator Month. The conference schedule and links for each virtual session will be posted during the week before the conference begins. You may check the accepted presentations and schedule keynotes. There's a panel on Technology and Librarians too.
