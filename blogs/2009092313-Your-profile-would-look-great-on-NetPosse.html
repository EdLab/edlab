Your profile would look great on NetPosse!

Jessica Blum

September 23, 2009

Chandler, Ting, Jess and I have been tinkering with NetPosse, EdLab's academic networking prototype, and have added profiles for many TC faculty members. In order to best test and improve the efficiency of the recommendation system, we are asking all EdLabbers to please create a profile and let us know what you think! You can sign up here to find out which TC professors' interests and experiences align most closely with your own. FYI: For whatever it's worth, my #1 NetPosse suggested contact was Gary Natriello! Maybe we should try to touch base . . . . As this is currently an internal library experiment, please keep this post in-house only. Thanks!
