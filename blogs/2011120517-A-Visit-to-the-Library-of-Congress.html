A Visit to the Library of Congress

Demetri Lales

December 5, 2011

Hello everyone! Recently I took a trip down to Washington D.C. for the NCSS Conference, and while I was there decided to swing by the Library of Congress. The Library of Congress is the world's largest library and it consists of three buildings: the Jefferson Building, the Adams Building, and the Madison Building. During my trip I only had time to stroll into the Jefferson Building and take a look around.
