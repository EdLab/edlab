What Gets Rewarded Gets Repeated

mason0

January 7, 2015

...online, listening = reading. That old school program from my childhood was right, so deeply fundamentally right. Reading. Reading Is Fundamental.
That's a quote from this pearl courtesy Jeff Atwood, co-founder of inquiry community Stack Exchange and bloguer extraordinaire at Coding Horror. He also built Discourse, an open-source discussion platform. A couple other keys from the post might be summarized like this: In a site or an app, any number a user can change becomes a target. This is gamification at its most minimal, its most animal. If there is only one such target, hitting that target becomes the purpose of the site. Atwood offers some ideas to encourage good conversation behavior online. Interesting stuff to think about with respect to the growth of Vialogues, and online education in general.
