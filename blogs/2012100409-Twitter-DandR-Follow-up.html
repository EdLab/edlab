Twitter D&R Follow-up...

Kate Meersschaert

October 4, 2012

In light of my recent Twitter D&R I thought I would share this cautionary Twitter tale from Mashable. It definitely pays to remember the Twitter Golden Rule... never post anything on Twitter about someone, even a president, that you wouldn't want your boss, grandmother, or the person in question to read! Wow... EPIC Twitter work account fail. Do you think this could be a good lesson to share with High School students? If so why? If not, why not? If you do... how and in what context? Should Twitter be a part of new media literacy classes and a part of learning how to be smart digital citizens? Sadly this person will have a VERY hard time finding a new job...
