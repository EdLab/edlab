NCDP Ideation Seminar

jvazquez

November 3, 2010

** KEEP PRIVATE ** To all groups participating in today's seminar: 1) Review the following NCDP resources: - Basic Crisis and Emergency Risk Communication for Public Health Workers (Hui Soo will come by and provide login credentials). - The NCDP Preparedness Wizard 2) Redesign them as self-directed learning modules (no human instruction or feedback). The following elements must be included for the Basic Crisis and Emergency Risk Communication for Public Health Workers course: a. Pre-Test b. Post-Test 3. Generate a list of ideas for NCDP videos the EdLab should produce for public health professionals (front line and administrative staff). Be sure to describe the following for each video: a. The context in which the videos will be published (e.g., NCDP course) b. The educational content of the video c. The form of the vide (e.g., animation)
