Losing Language Speaking Skills

rendy

November 10, 2013

I've decided to write about languages one last time before I switched to a new topic. I'm sure we've all heard people talk about how they use to speak a language perfectly and now only speak it intermittently or not at all. World-renowned Professor FranÃ§ois Grosjean notes that you start forgetting a language when you do not speak it often or in some cases, don't speak it at all. Most people that forget a language try to avoid any phrase with the word they've forgotten or tend to shorten the conversation. I speak five different languages (Twi, Italian, Spanish, Japanese and English) and sometimes when speaking one language, I tend to forget some words and end up avoiding the word or substitute it in a different language. At times, it comes out without even realizing and other times, I just forget completely. Not using some of these languages makes them fade in my head slowly. Have you lost touch with a language you once spoke fluently?
