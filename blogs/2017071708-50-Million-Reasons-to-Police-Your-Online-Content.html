50 Million Reasons to Police Your Online Content

dinosossi2

July 17, 2017


In response to fake news flooding the U.S. and France during their past presidential elections, the German parliament recently passed a law that imposes up to a 50 million Euro fine if social media platforms inadequately remove posts that incite hate or are defamation that are deemed to be criminal.

Time to hire some fact checkers, my social media platform friends.

As you might imagine, freedom of speech laws are stricter in jurisdictions like Germany that have an unfortunate history with expression that targets vulnerable populations.

Although social media platforms have shown willingness to begin to start self-policing themselves recently, market impulses compel them to be more open with letting whoever post whatever whenever from every type of device.
