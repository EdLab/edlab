Why Is Mathematics So Important ?

Kafoumba Doumbia

January 12, 2014

Mathematics is one of the most important subjects as it provides several vital skills for all learners. Some of these skills include: the ability to identify and analyze patterns, logic and critical thinking, and ability to see relationships and problem solving. All of us use mathematics in our daily lives. When we use money in transactions, we are using math. You need to know math to pay bills, buy food and other essentials. Mathematics is involved in almost everything that shapes our society from cellphones, cars, computers, bridges and many other things. I personally love mathematics and I think we should push students to also have an interest in it. We have become a society heavily reliant on technology, which is in itself a form or an extension of mathematics. I understand that Math can be very challenging and requires a lot of focus and determination, but we can make it possible for all students by providing the necessary support.
