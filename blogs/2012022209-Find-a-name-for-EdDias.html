Find a name for EdDias!

Alex Park

February 22, 2012

(Private) Good morning EdLabbers! This morning we are trying to find a new, catchier name for EdDias, our new discussion board tool. Here is the elevator pitch for those of you unfamiliar with the tool: EdDais is an intelligent discussion forum for both classroom and online learning. Users can create a discussion, keep it public or restrict it to a group of people. They can share their discussion or comments on social networking sites such as Twitter/Facebook, get analytics on a particular discussion as well as see their overall analytics by year, semester or month. Users can get recommendations to similar questions on the forum and can also embed the discussion on external sites such as their blog or learning system. Further, EdDais would also feature education related questions answered by experts in the lab or likewise in the industry. However, "EdDias" is a lot harder to say than you might think, and it's less catchy than we would like. If you need some inspiration, here is the handy dandy Web 2.0 Name Generator. So, what do you say? Have your say on the name for this tool!
