Read a (f%$#) book?!?

Joachim Castellano

August 9, 2007

Read a (f%$#) book?!? That's what this music video preaches, in addition to drinking water, buying land, and wearing deodorant. While its refreshing to see a rap video with a positive message, it's sad that this video retains other questionable rap video conventions (excessive cursing, shaking butts) for its delivery mechanism. Clearly, the producers of this video have a specific audience in mind. I doubt they would watch it packaged in inoffensive, radio-friendly 'Big Willie Stlye' (Will Smith) rap. Still, I wish they could have developed the rap video genre with new modes of positive expression, while retaining its street authenticity. It's a step in the right direction, but not a complete advancement forward. Watch with caution! http://youtube.com/watch?v=rN2VqFPNS8w
