DIY Workshop on Early Product Design @ General Assembly

Kate Meersschaert

August 22, 2011

This Thursday's event at General Assembly seems like it will be interesting and helpful for anyone who has or is designing an EdLab product/app/tool. Here is a bit more about the speaker from General Assembly's site: San is the founder/CEO of Easel, the company behind the ShowMe app. He also helps organize the NYC Lean Startup Meetup and the Columbia Venture Community, and holds a BA in Music. He's a self-taught UX designer and front-end developer, and is obsessed with learning from people who are better at stuff than he is. He blogs at sanonstartups.com. Why I HOPE this workshop will be worthwhile for EdLab: 1) San will have tips for taking ideas for new apps & products to production both quickly & with little initial investment 2) He created ShowMe & recently partnered with the Princeton Review 3) San is involved with the "Lean Startup" world 4) Active within the Columbia Venture community 5) He is a true self-taught DIY'er Hope you can join me for this event! Please let me know if you plan to attend and we can travel together from EdLab.
