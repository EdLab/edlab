How Music Affects Our Brain

Carmel Addae

May 30, 2014

It is really charming how the things people do for entertainment truly benefits them in the long run, albeit in different aspects. The least important things in life essentially have a meaning later in life. Common music, a combination of vocals and instrumental sounds, bring harmony and emotions to help our brain. It advances creativity, our motor and reasoning skills, visual attention and supports us to exercise by competing with our brain to help override signals of fatigue.
