Diddy Starts a Charter School

ABARRY

March 28, 2016

When was the last time you heard someone in the media or government say â€œWe must support and help entrepreneurs because they support our economy"? Probably not. Entrepreneurs or business owners are often viewed as self-centered and money hungry. However, it seems that today's entrepreneurs are focused on philanthropic endeavors. Mark Zuckerberg, Bill Gates and Warren Buffet have donated a substantial amount of their lifetime earnings to various causes including healthcare, education and development. Rapper and hip hop mogul Sean "Diddy" Combs recently invested in a new charter school in Harlem. Combs began working towards the launch of the school in 2011, having spent years searching for the best education environment. He finally decided to open the school in his childhood neighborhood in Harlem. According to officials, the charter school is a free, public school that will serve grades 6-12. Are entrepreneurs starting schools and getting involved good for education?
