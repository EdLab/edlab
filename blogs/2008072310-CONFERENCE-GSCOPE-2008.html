CONFERENCE GSCOPE 2008

Stephanie Burdick

July 23, 2008

Dear EdLab: Teachers College Graduate Students of Philosophy and Education are hosting this year's Graduate Student Conference of Philosophy and Education Oct. 3-5, 2008 right here, third floor of the library. EdLab has already helped this conference greatly by assisting with technology for the conference! The Graduate Students in Philosophy and Education invite all graduate students at TC to attend the conference, I think you'll find it very interdisciplinary and relevant to a lot of the questions we ask ourselves all the time at the EdLab. In particular, I would like to invite interested EdLab graduate students to chair or respond to a session at the conference. We have one session on Education and Technology and we are searching for a knowledgeable respondent for that session as well as a chair. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me. Thanks, Stephanie
