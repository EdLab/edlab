Google Hangout Team Test

Kate Meersschaert

December 19, 2012

Thank you to the team for quickly (& humorously!!!) testing Google's Hangout feature! I know some have you have used this videoconferencing (is that the most 3.0 term?) option before (Janice & Megha!), but I wanted to fully assess if it is a viable option to replace Skype. My quick takeaways... -Fast set-up and "entry" -Audio integration was seamless -A bit blurred-but no speech "lags" as with Skype -Overall seems useful, intuitive -Less "buggy" than Skype -Since we are already enmeshed in the Google world it seems a logical extension -Free! Note: Please be sure the plug-in is installed on your computer... if you want to get-in on the next quick Hangout test! Finally, please add possible uses for Google Hangouts below... My Possible Uses Include: -Meet/check-in with colleagues who are overseas -"interview" NLT Profilees -Screenshare re: an issue or idea/feature with teammates (lets test this functionality next time!) Ps. the virtual "costumes" are a fun element... but I wonder how often you could use these for serious meetings? Great way to lighten the mood... or totally loose credibility! ;)
