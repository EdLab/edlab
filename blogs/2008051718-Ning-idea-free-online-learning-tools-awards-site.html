Ning idea: "free online learning tools" awards site

bhughes

May 17, 2008

I was just reviewing the great set of resources Hui Soo and the EdLab team created for the Teaching the Levees Professional Development site, and I am surprised (sort of) that there are no awards for this kind of resource -- teacher professional development resources that are available online for free. OK, so I'm not entirely surprised. (Does anyone know of any award that would kind of count?) Short of finding a pre-existing place to nominate this set of resources and bring it to the attention of teachers everywhere, I am thinking there is an opportunity here to create a Ning (or some kind of social site) where a group of self-selected members could nominate and vote on the best free educational resources. With all the Oscars, Emmys, Tonys, Webbys, and so on, how could there not be a place for those of us dedicating our lives to education to gather and cluck about our great achievements? â€” A reason for us to dust off our finest dresses and tuxes... What do you think? And how could we go about finding, building, or installing the necessary nominating/voting/rating tools?
