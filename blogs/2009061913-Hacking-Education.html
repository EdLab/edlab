Hacking Education

Frank Webster

June 19, 2009

The 6-10-09 issue of American Libraries Online links to a Time article: How Twitter Will Change the Way We Live by Steven Johnson Friday, Jun. 05, 2009. Jouhnson uses an event called Hacking Education held here in New York in March as an example of how Twitter could create a real-time shadow conversation that spread out of the conference and into the world.
