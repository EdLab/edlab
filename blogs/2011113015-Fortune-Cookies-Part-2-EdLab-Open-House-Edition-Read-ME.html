Fortune Cookies Part 2: EdLab Open House Edition! Read ME!

Kate Meersschaert

November 30, 2011

Private! Hi team EdLab!* Thank you again to all of the Pundit fortune cookie slogan contest participants! Through your hard-work and brain-power, Pundit has a whole collection of witty, fun fortune cookies ready for delivery & stealth "drops" throughout TC in the coming weeks! Fast-forward to the upcoming EdLab Holiday Party on Dec. 14th... Lets harness the collective EdLab super powers once again to develop amazing fortunes to include in custom cookies to be enjoyed at this festive open house. The goal of the cookies will be to promote the lab, our work/projects and careers. Details... We get 15 different fortunes for this custom cookie order and again, in addition to our URL & logo on the back (or front depending on your perspective), we get up to 111 characters (including spaces) for the message. Also, please weigh-in here on whether or not you think we should have URL's & correspondent fortunes specific to each EdLab project and focus or stay general and link to the EdLab home page? To get the collective creative juices flowing here are a few ideas for fortunes: EdLab General Promo: EdLab much lately? On the road of life you definitely want EdLab on your side! Vision, design and technology for the education sector are in your cards! Q: What is diverse, innovative and ready to rethink education in the 21st century? A: EdLab! Learn to say "Educational innovation for the next century": Ed-LAB! Career Focus: You will find an enlightened, education-focused career at EdLab. Flip me over to find the career of your dreams! You have an innovative, collaborative future in store. A career in ed-focused research, design, and development is closer than you think. Who will help you re-imagine schooling for the 21st century? Specific Project Focus: Pressible: A better blog experience is in your future! Vialogues: Got meaningful dialogue around videos? Your future is paved with meaningful, annotated conversation around video! EdNode: A better study buddy is in your cards! Q: What is better than, yet inspired by an online dating site? A: EnNode! Pundit: May the course be with you! (or we can skip Pundit & just use some existent cookies?!) Research Broker(BETA): Want help navigating a sea of research? More meaningful research is in your near future... *Note: If you don't contribute ideas for fortunes you will be expected to perform a solo skating routine at the holiday party!
