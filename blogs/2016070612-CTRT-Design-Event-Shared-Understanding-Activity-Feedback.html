CTRT Design Event Shared Understanding Activity Feedback

HSC

July 6, 2016

* KEEP PRIVATE Objective: i. The purpose of the Design Event will be to generate innovative ideas to facilitate the establishment of “a network of school and university partners that will support the selection and assessment of learning technologies by serving as sites for testing their effectiveness and advising developers on use and value.“ ii. The Design Event itself will also function as an exemplar activity to advance TCEdTech, a new initiative to “assist start-up technology ventures to create the best products for educational institutions by providing them with data on the effectiveness of their new apps and other learning tools.” In your small groups, compare and contrast the resources below: AltSchool EDesign Lab NYC iZone Schools League of Innovative Schools International Positive Education Network Institute of Play | Quest 2 Learn hastac 1) Which characteristics of the organizations/networks do you think are useful for TC to consider as it builds a network? 2) What changes can be made to each of the organizations/networks to make them more “virtuous?” 3) Put on your “learner/student” hat. How does your assessment of each of the organizations/networks change when you analyze these organizations/networks from this perspective?
