ViaBlog Exclusive: Melanie Hibbert on the Future of Video in the Classroom

Kate Meersschaert

June 29, 2011

Here is the first exclusive podcast-based interview for the Vialogues Blog on Pressible: Melanie Hibbert, Doctoral Candidate in the Instructional Technology and Media program at Teachers College, Columbia University, shares her thoughts on the future of video in the classroom. Questions we explore in this informal interview include: -What is the future of video in schools? -Now that FlipVideo is no longer, will SmartPhones take-over? -What is the role of "Pro-sumer" (as opposed to consumer) tools in the classroom? -Will Vialogues help make video more interactive? -How can you make student feedback more valuable utilizing video?
