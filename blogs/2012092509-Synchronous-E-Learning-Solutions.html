Synchronous E-Learning Solutions

zhou

September 25, 2012

DOWNLOAD SLIDES Hello D&R: Today Jing, Yudan, and I are glad to talk about synchronous e-learning solutions. We will first overview and compare asynchronous and synchronous e-learning, and discuss why we should be focusing on synchronous e-learning solutions today. Then we will demonstrate the top two synchronous e-learning technologies - Cisco WebEx and Adobe Connect. Finally a group activity will be held to brainstorm how we might utilize these technologies in our projects. Please be seated in four groups. In the demo activity, please refer to the following instructions: Group 1 and 2: WebEx Every one can join with their computer, iPhone, iPad, or Android phone (up to 25 devices). Please download the Adobe Connect Mobile app to your mobile device prior to the group activity. Go to http://www.webex.com/, click "Attend a Meeting" on the top right corner then enter meeting number 193 159 341 and join. Group 3 and 4: Adobe Connect Every one can join with their computer, iPhone, iPad, or Android phone (up to 25 devices). Please download the Adobe Connect Mobile app to your mobile device prior to the group activity. The URL of the meeting is http:// meet46214144.adobeconnect.co m/dr0925.
