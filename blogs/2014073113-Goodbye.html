Goodbye :)

Sharon Hsiao

July 31, 2014

Hi everyone, Today is my last day in EdLab. I figure I share a bit of my thoughts. I joined EdLab about 3 years ago, year 2011. My internship cut short due to the hurricane irene at the end of the summer. So I didn't have a chance to say proper goodbye to EdLabbers by then, but I knew I'd be back. With support and a bit luck, I did return after I finished my doctoral degree, and become the first postdoc in EdLab. Thank you, Gary & Hui Soo. I'm very grateful to have the opportunity to collaborate with a bunch of creative minds here in EdLab. You guys give me a lot support and mostly the inspiration. I thank all of you. I will continue working on learning detection, visual analytics, & several relevant domains. I just establish my lab in ASU over the past weekend, it's called, CSI for Learning (Computing Systems & Informatics for Learning) . Here's my new affiliation and website. I recently rebuild my website by bootstrap, I learn the framework from our D&R too. Parting is such sweet sorrow, that I shall say bye till it is morrow. (quote from Romeo&Juliet) Miss you all. Take care & do good work :) Sharon
