An Earthy Problem

Minwoo You

June 25, 2007

Problems have been evident as long as I can remember. “Earth Mother is getting angry”, is how this article from the Concord Monitor Online describes it to us. Global Warming has been the heart of and the frontline of the environmentalist movements but nothing much has been done about it. More is being done by Native Americans, who are a minority of only 1% of America, using money earned from their Casinos to come to aid the earth. Thunderbull, a Lakota Sioux from the Article says "Think of the people who will die in the cleansing of Earth Mother, all around the world. Think of their spirits." I don't think many people realize the effects of Global Warming in the future ahead of us. In Al Gore's: An Inconvenient Truth, there is a Cartoon that explains comically what our government has been doing to help save the planet. If we don't stop using fossil fuels and if we cannot develop new energy sources for our future generations, we truly might have to reenact the cartoon.
