Staff Printer Work Around

HSC

May 2, 2017

Do you have the Sierra OS on computer?


Are you connecting to the Internet via WiFi? (named "TC") 


If you answered "yes" to both questions, you've probably noticed you are no longer able to print to the staff printers on the 1st and 5th floors. The reason is because the of the "upgrades" to the TC Network. 


Here is the work around: 


1) Go to System Preferences

2) Click on Printers & Scanners

3) Add a Printer (click the + sign)

4) Select "IP"

5) Input 10.60.3.213 (5th floor) or 10.60.3.210 (1st floor) in the "Address" field 

6) From the "Protocol" drop down menu, select Line Printer Daemon

7) Leave "Queue" blank

8) Enter a name in the "Name" field

9) Leave "Location" blank

10) Leave "Generic PostScript Printer" in the "Use" drop down


TC CIS is working to solve this problem.
