Love Guitar But Hate Practicing? Let The Robots Help

jvazquez

July 2, 2014


Finnish company Ovelin combines all things awesome—robots, animation, bright colors, and music education—to provide a unique learning experience within its popular app, GuitarBots. It presents an authentic way to learn an instrument on a browser or mobile device without having to buy any extra equipment. All you need is a guitar and a goal and you are on your way.

The easiest part of learning an instrument lies within the first few moments of the first lesson when learners are full of determination and eagerness. The hardest part? Returning to it day after day, sore fingers and all, to practice. The driving force behind the creation of the app was in figuring out how to motivate learners (especially beginners) to keep getting better. This video demonstrates the components Ovelin hopes are engaging enough to keep learners coming back and gives a taste of the exciting features yet to come.
