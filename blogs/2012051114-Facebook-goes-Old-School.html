Facebook goes "Old School"

Kate Meersschaert

May 11, 2012

A return to the early days: Facebook is going old school...literally. The social network recently announced a return to their roots through their new service restricted to educational institutions, Groups for Schools. As in early days of "the book," a user can only log-in via a valid ".edu" email address. What is unique? Once logged-in users can find-out what is happening at their University, in their class and within clubs. Additionally, & perhaps most compellingly, there is the option to share and store files. It seems that in addition to returning to their original user base, students, the behemoth social network is also creating a bit of collegiate rivalry for DropBox.
