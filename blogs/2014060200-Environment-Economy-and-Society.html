Environment, Economy and Society

abagigah92

June 2, 2014

The global economy is very much dependent on society and the labor market as well as key social systems that keep it running. Both the economy and society are dependent on the environment that provides them with the raw materials, food, clean air and water. Increasingly climate change, water scarcity, pollution, health issues and others impact these interrelationships. We need to recognize that our current business model will need to adapt to work within the environmental systems that secures the natural factors that keep both the economy and society running. Our growing population is a big issue relating to the environment; consumption is higher than ever before. More and more goods and services are being produced by our businesses, in turn depleting resources whilst increasing pollution.

Sustainability is a good process to go about change in the economy, the sooner we react, the faster the transition to a better environment that will benefit both the economy and society. By looking at sustainability strategically we can prepare for the future using a long-term vision to shape decision making whilst also generating short-term benefits.
