Count-down to Demo Night II!

Kate Meersschaert

March 20, 2013

Private!*** Happy Spring Team EdLab! I am excited to report that EdLab Demo Night II is officially happening tomorrow night from 7-9 on the second floor! Please attend, ask great questions and enjoy the evening! Also, please be sure to think about ways we can learn from events like this and promote our work! The promotional focus of the event will be NLT and gathering NLT sign-ups... we will have a sign-up table set-up, so please be sure to guide attendees there as you see fit. Also, please be sure to ask any guests that you plan to bring to register on our Eventbrite page: http://edlabdemonight.eventbrite.com/ (feel-free to share this link!) As always, thank you for helping to make the EdLab such a wonderful place to work and an exciting group and collection of work to promote and share! Please add any last-minute questions regarding the event in the comments below!
