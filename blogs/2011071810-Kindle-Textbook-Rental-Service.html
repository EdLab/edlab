Kindle Textbook Rental Service

Ankit Ranka

July 18, 2011

Amazon is now allowing students to rent textbooks according to this techcrunch article. After the rental expires students will be able to keep their notes and highlights online. This is a great step from Amazon. But as much as like Kindle to read books, my experience with Kindle have not been really good in terms of reading textbooks or research papers. One of the reason is that Its difficult to take notes with such a small keyboard and to highlight you have to move cursor down to the particular line. Also, the page turns are too slow in kindle to refer back and forth. And the lack of a good browser makes it difficult to look up definitions and go more in depth into a topic. There have been rumors about Amazon coming up with a ipad like device. May be that will provide a better textbook reading experience.
