zhou

October 25, 2016

In today's D&R, Chingfu and I will lead a discussion on Alexa - a voice service provided by Amazon. The presentation is available at the following link: https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1D8DAPBP-_18SzgH9pw12l-2b9Xrdq-cXfw83-V3qPqY/edit?usp=sharing Group activity: Each group come up with 3 ideas about what Alexa skills would benefit the TC Library and/or EdLab to improve our functionality, services, research, branding, etc. Example scenarios:

  • Study room
  • Collaboration
  • Book checkout
  • Workstation
  • EdLab work space
  • EdLab seminar
  • Demo night
  • EdLab apps (e.g. library website, Vialogues, NLT, etc.)
Please post your group ideas as comments to this tread. Thank you.
