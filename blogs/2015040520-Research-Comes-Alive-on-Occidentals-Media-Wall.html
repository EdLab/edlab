Research Comes Alive on Occidental's Media Wall

Nantwi

April 5, 2015


The increase in learning applications means students and educators now have an array of resources at their disposal. This has led many universities to reimagine how technology can change the physical landscape of their campuses. This is the case at Occidental College, a small liberal arts college in Los Angeles. The schoolâ€™s Global Crossroads project is changing the way in which students, researchers, and faculty create and showcase their work and research.

The Global Crossroads project consists of a web platform and a wall display. The web platform allows students to create content based on their academic work and research. The wall display then highlights the student-created content to members of the Occidental community and visitors. The hope is that the web platform and wall display will imbue students with a collaborative spirit that goes beyond the classroom.
