Call for Participants- Teachers College Social Issue Media Festival

Adrienne Garber

February 21, 2010

Have something to say about a social issue using video, audio podcast, or other multimedia outlet? Are you using digital artifacts to encourage others to take action? Get recognized! Teachers College, Columbia University is hosting its first annual Social Issue Media Festival (SIMF) in the Spring 2010. A jury of 10 members of the Columbia community will select a collection of 12-14 films that will be screened at the festival (date TBD). Details: - Open to anyone in the Columbia community. - Deadline for submissions Monday, March 22, 2010. - Films should be between 2-3 minutes in length. - All entries are eligible for jury awards. - Entry form and guidelines available at tcmediafestival.wordpress.com For more information, email SIMF at TCMediaFestival[at]gmail[dot]com.
