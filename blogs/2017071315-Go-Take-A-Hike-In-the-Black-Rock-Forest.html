Go Take A Hike!! (In the Black Rock Forest)

christophergu

July 13, 2017

Greetings EdLabbers!

As mentioned at the seminar yesterday, we are planning our summer outing this year to the Black Rock Forest Consortium in Orange County, NY. We will have the opportunity to learn about the Consortium's work in biodiversity, science research, and conservation. In addition to learning about their educational programs, we will also have the opportunity to hike through part of the Black Rock Forest. 


You can learn more about the Black Rock Forest Consortium here:


http://blackrockforest.org 

This day trip will be on Friday, August 25th. We plan to depart TC in the morning and we will travel up to the Black Rock Forest via chartered bus. 


If you are interested in participating, please RSVP using the link below, by next Wednesday, July 19th:


https://edlab.tc.columbia.edu/events/EdLab-Seminar-Staff-Development-at-Black-Rock-Forest-Library-Staff-Only--SJ2N26mSb
