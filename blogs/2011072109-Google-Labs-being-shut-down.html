Google Labs being shut down

Megha Agarwala

July 21, 2011


Google is shutting down Google Labs, "a platform that allowed users to interact and give feedback on experimental products produced by Googlers in their 20 Percent Time". Shutting down labs, will not shut down "one day a week Googlers working on things not a part of their job". It remains interesting to see how the life cycle of 20% projects will change as a result of Google Labs shutdown and how will it tie into their other products. Also what does this symbolize for Google? Greater control over their innovations, being more systematic or dimnishing confidence in putting something that could be crappy?
