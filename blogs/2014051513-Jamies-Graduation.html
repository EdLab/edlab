Jamie's Graduation

LukeMalone

May 15, 2014

*Keep Private* One week ago, Jamie led us on her last Seen in NY shoot at "Sideshow School" in Coney Island. It was appropriate there that they offered her part of their graduation ceremony in which one lays on a bed of nails and has a cinder block smashed on their stomach. Jamie didn't even flinch. This was a perfect example of how she has taken us all over the city and fit in seamlessly in whatever crazy (or not so crazy) environment we were in. We all wish Jamie luck in her post-TC life and will miss her much!
