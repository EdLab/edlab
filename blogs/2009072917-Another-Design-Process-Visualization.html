Another Design Process Visualization

Stephanie Hunt

July 29, 2009

Jeff mentioned this graphic in a comment to a different post a while back. I've been meaning to dedicate an actual blog post to it, as a continuation of my "Visualizing the Design Process" series. This one is from Taco Lab. Of all the design process graphics I've blogged about so far, I think this one feels the most accurate to my own experience. See that big huge messy tangle in the design/build blob? How it takes up lots of ink and space and energy in an otherwise sensible process diagram? It really does usually feel that way.
