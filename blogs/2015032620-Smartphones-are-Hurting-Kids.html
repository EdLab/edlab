Smartphones are Hurting Kids

Brian.Sweeting

March 26, 2015


There are many reasons why smartphones aren’t great for kids: they’re distracting, they inhibit social skills, and they discourage communication in anything but emojis :-/. Add to the list: they are causing physical harm. A new study on playground injuries reveals that scrapes and bruises are increasing due to distracted, smartphone-addicted parents.
