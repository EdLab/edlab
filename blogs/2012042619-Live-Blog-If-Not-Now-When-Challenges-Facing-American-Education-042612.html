Live Blog: If Not Now, When?: Challenges Facing American Education 04.26.12

Kate Meersschaert

April 26, 2012

If Not Now, When?: Challenges Facing American Education When: 7-9PM @ General Assembly Who: Dr. Adrian Sannier, SVP of Learning Technologies at Pearson Intro... Thoughts on how ridiculous it is to try to get funding for ed tech... Barbie & the military have $ to spend. Talking about his past history & how he ended-up @ ASU under President Crow. What makes a university great has traditionally been based on WHO they exclude... the president of ASU wants to change that: "...measured not by who we exclude, but who we include." First University to use Google Apps. Main Talk Takeaways: 1) Mining big data to identify insight will save us... teaching as if the ed field were a cottage industry will kill us. 2) The future of online learning will more closely resemble a turn-key "Xbox" or video-game-based learning experience: personalized, adaptive & tracked. 3) Whoever gets to scale first, controls the game & the conversation... we are two orders of magnitude away from teaching everyone on the planet.
