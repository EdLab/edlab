The Debate of Positivity Ratio

Sharon Hsiao

September 2, 2013

How many of you have read the book, "Positivity"? Here's the updated study to trash the 3:1 positivity ratio. Basically, the new research is meant to argue that the psychological and social sciences demand more conceptual rigor by proving the nonlinear dynamics of the mathematical model, which is definitely true and should be applied to all disciplines. The problem is not that Fredrickson is a bad research psychologist. The problem is that she's one of the best. the research is summarized here.
