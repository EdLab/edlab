CISCO Ed Tech Study - Ed Tech is beginning to work!

Linda Bloom

September 28, 2006

CISCO has come out with a study that has reviewed how and if educational technology is being used in schools. The study reviews training, support, implementation and assessment and finds that, when implemented carefully and monitored adequately that educational technology is beginning to show effectiveness in the schools across the nation. The study also discusses that up to now, educators may have "miscalculated" the difficulty on the effective ways to integrate and implement technology in the classrooms and curriculum; and while "promises" have been made little was delivered on the overall return of initial investments. The report is encouraging, it notes that more needs to be done to integrate technology in order to reach academic potentials. If you haven't read a CICSO study before I would take the time to review this one. CISCO studies are usually interesting as they demonstrate how corporations have partnered with academic environments to support and develop products for learning development.
