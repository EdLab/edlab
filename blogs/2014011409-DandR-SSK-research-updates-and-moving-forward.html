D&R - SSK research updates and moving forward

Sharon Hsiao

January 14, 2014

***private please*** Research updates: - Learning resources: classroom study revealed a dynamic non-linear reading behavior - Survey design: based on survey structure, layout and questions, found evidence that aligned with literature on survey design principles: 1)Begin with simple questions 2) Finishing one topic before raising a new topic 3)Gradual difficulty increase Moving forward: a) What are good quality surveys? b) Make inferences on learning c) Better sampling Activity: 1. Demographic survey https://surveysidekick.com/surveys/take?public_code=MCYGMNVXS5 2. Design a survey based on the following given scenario:

There are approximately 1800 schools and 75,000 teachers in New York City. You want to understand how teachers in New York City stay current in education.
- Please create 5~8 different types of questions to figure out how teachers stay current in education.
- Determine the required sample size with a sampling error of < 5%.
