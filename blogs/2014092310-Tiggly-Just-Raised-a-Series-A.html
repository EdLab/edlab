Tiggly Just Raised a "Series A"

Kate Meersschaert

September 23, 2014

EdLab seminar, GroundBREAKERS interview and Demo Night alums, Tiggly, have raised their "Series A" funding and will likely (if you ask me) be acquired by a major toy company. Rather than seek funding from traditional VC's the group accepted an offer from 75+ y/o German toy maker Habermaass GmBH (read more in this TechCrunch article) the makers of HABA toys. Congrats Azi and the whole Tiggly team!
