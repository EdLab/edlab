Meetup: Future of Education #3: Trends, Challenges, and Opportunities in EdTech

Kate Meersschaert

October 14, 2013

Join me for Future of Education #3: Trends, Challenges, and Opportunities in EdTech! Hosted by Be Social Change at Knewton's offices (see details below), it should be a wonderful opportunity to both learn more about the field and network with peers. Plus, it is always a treat to visit the Knewton offices! Event details: Thursday, October 24, 2013 6:45 PM Knewton 100 Fifth Ave. (at 15th St.) 8th Floor, New York, NY $10 (Please check with Hui Soo before registering via the Eventbrite link) Description from Meetup Page:

The Future of Education: Trends, Challenges, and Opportunities in EdTech As one of the largest and fastest-growing sectors, Education Technology is receiving an increasing amount of attention from entrepreneurs, educators, students, and investors. From massive open online courses (MOOCs) to adaptive learning platforms to individualized assessment, education is reaching a tipping point. ... This panel will explore the current trends, opportunities, and challenges in this rapidly growing sector through the eyes of leaders working at the forefront of education technology. We'll hear from a diverse group of experts who are thinking deeply about how technology can improve the way we learn and how we teach the valuable skills needed for a changing global workforce. {Drinks and snacks will be provided!} 6:30-7:00pm Doors open, Arrive 7:00-8:30pm Activity, Panel Discussion, Q&A 8:30-9:00pm Networking
If you can join and have checked-in with Hui Soo, please add your +1 below!
