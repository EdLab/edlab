Improving Critical Thinking Skills

Kafoumba Doumbia

December 29, 2014

A lot of educators and policymakers feel few of today's students are gaining critical thinking skills in their education. It is an important element that is missing in our education system. In this op-ed, the author makes a case for improving critical thinking for students (and even parents), especially as it pertains to health. How do we improve students' critical thinking skills?
