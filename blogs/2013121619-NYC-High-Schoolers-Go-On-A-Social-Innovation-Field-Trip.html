NYC High Schoolers Go On A Social Innovation Field Trip

jvazquez

December 16, 2013


Avenues bills itself as a "new school of thought." It aims to prepare students for a culturally connected future by teaching them to understand other societies, speak languages fluently, and appreciate othersâ€™ histories.

To get students to understand the ways in which they can make a difference in the global community, Avenues arranged for a set of field trips to 3 notable social innovation units across New York City. Each organization hosted a group of students for a day. During this time, the students learned about the work and culture of the organizations. As an added bonus, they participated in ideation sessions, which allowed them to experience the design process firsthand.
