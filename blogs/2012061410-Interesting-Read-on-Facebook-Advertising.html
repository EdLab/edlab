Interesting Read on Facebook Advertising

Rebecca Hyams

June 14, 2012

Last night I was looking through the day's posts on Fark.com and noticed a link to a blog post about Facebook marketing. Because we've been talking a lot about marketing and promoting our products over the last few days, especially in our Tuesday meeting that I gave the post a read. The post, Joseph Perla Was Wrong About Facebook Advertising, is a response to another blog post that buying ads on Facebook to advertise a product yields an insufficient return on investment. Samuel Scott argues that while outbound advertising on Facebook (buying ads) tends to be ineffective, using inbound marketing can be quite effective if used properly. I realize this ties in with a lot of what we've discussed already, but I feel it's always good to see outside opinions.
