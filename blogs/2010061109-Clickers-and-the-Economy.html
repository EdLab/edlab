Clickers and the Economy

Jeannie Crowley

June 11, 2010

According to Nature News, hand-held response devices ("clickers") are gaining in popularity due to the economic downturn. Clickers allow professors to collect responses from large numbers of students and to have those responses automatically graded. Kevin Owens, a spokesman for the a clicker making company, says the increase in popularity is due to the crunch in university budgets.
