D&R: EdTech Toys Demo

Meredith Powers

August 5, 2014

*private* Today's D&R is all about our new collection of EdTech toys. Get ready to test out some of the products and share your thoughts on the experience! We want to hear your feedback about the toys themselves, and also on the demo experience as a whole. We've prepared some questions to get you started, but please don't feel limited to them. Yue will be taking some video footage during the D&R, and we will also have a mini-recorder set up for the rest of the day if you'd like to share your thoughts in the "feedback booth." Finally, we are also doing a brief MakerBot demo for those of you who haven't gotten a chance to see it in action. Please use the poll to vote for the object you would like to see printed during today's D&R. (Link here if the embedded poll doesn't work for you.)
