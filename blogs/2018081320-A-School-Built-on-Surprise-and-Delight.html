A School Built on Surprise and Delight

saraehardman

August 13, 2018


Eminence Independent Schools in Eminence, Kentucky unveiled a new educational initiative to reinvent their schools. Instead of focusing on testing or specific subject matter, Eminence school leaders decided to focus on two oft-ignored characteristics: surprise and delight. "It’s taking anything you normally would do and then trying to crank it up to something that’s awesome," said Superintendent Buddy Berry of the initiative. It’s making the entire school, from the furniture to the curriculum to the hallways themselves, into an unexpected and fun experience.

To reinvent their school under the principles of surprise and delight, Eminence school leaders took a workshop at the Disney Institute for Creativity and Innovation to learn how to infuse everyday spaces with a feeling of discovery and exploration. They want students to leave each day feeling excited to return the next. And so far, they do. Teachers have also had a positive response to the changes, feeling free to experiment with fun in different ways in their classes.
