Is Wikipedia in Trouble ?

Abdulrahman Sanda

January 28, 2016

Wikipedia, a well known research tool used for random searches has been compromised due to lack of authentic sources and the decline of dedicated editors. Many individuals often use wikipedia to look up terms/ideologies. The information produced by Wikipedia is often trusted. No one really knows if the documents produced by this site is real because "large proportion of articles contain some sort of warning that they are incomplete, poorly written or inadequately researched". Wikipedia has aroused lots of curiosities around the world due to its negligence in producing reliable sources. The article "Why Wikipedia is in Trouble," provides insight on why the use of Wikipedia is gradually becoming an act that can't be purged because of its capabilities. The problem Wikipedia faces is its inability to gather occasional editors. How can Wikipedia collaborate to make it an authentic tool for proper research ?
