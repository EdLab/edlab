Robotics Competition Part II

EdLab Studios

May 2, 2015


The 15th annual First Robotics Competition kicks off at the Javits Center with cheering fans, blaring anthems, and a parade of massive robots being wheeled toward their first match. At the announcer’s command the metal competitors begin their 10 seconds of pre-programmed activity before each team’s driver takes over and carefully directs the machines to gather totes, stack bins, and remove brightly colored pool noodles from the course. On this global stage, it’s all about seeing how your ideas stack up against those of your peers in celebration of NYC First’s mission of inspiration and recognition of science and technology.
