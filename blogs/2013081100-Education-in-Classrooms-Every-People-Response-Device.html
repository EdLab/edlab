Education in Classrooms: Every People Response Device

Christopher Zamora

August 11, 2013

So this week I've been mainly uploading videos on Vialogues about technology in classrooms. I had videos of new technology that are used in today's classrooms as you rarely see textbooks and pencils. You see technology devices and computers, but in this video I didn't like the technology they used. This device prevented students from engaging with the teacher and students used this device mainly to answer the questions the teacher gave. I think in school you must learn to communicate, among others and this device prevented it by not giving the students the chance to communicate with everyone else. What are your thoughts on this device? Is it any good? Would you recommend it? Join the discussion.
