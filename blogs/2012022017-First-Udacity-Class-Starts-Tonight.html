First Udacity Class Starts Tonight!

Kate Meersschaert

February 20, 2012

I am excited to delve-into Sebastian Thrun's newest online course offering, CS 101: Building a Search Engine, on his proprietary platform, Udacity. As Fred and Megha mentioned here on the blog, this new class is Thrun's next move (sans Stanford) after the phenomenal success of his Intro to Artificial Intelligence course offered this past fall. I will keep you posted on my experiences and relevant take-aways as I learn to build a search engine... perhaps not as advanced as DuckDuckGo's featured below, but at least I will thoroughly appreciate what goes into search creation!
