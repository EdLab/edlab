The Most Interesting Adult Learning Apps in the World

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2017


The latest innovations in education aren’t just benefiting kids. People of all ages can now find useful apps to continue learning, gain new skills, or just forget about the stresses of the real world. Here are our favorite apps for lifelong learners.

1. Vanido
Have you ever wanted to become a karaoke superstar? Now is your chance with Vanido, a free voice and singing training app. You’ll be hitting those high notes in Don’t Stop Believing in no time.

2. GuidiGO
GuidiGO is perfect for two different kinds of people: storytellers and travelers. It allows storytellers to build hands-on learning excursions and VR explorations. For the travelers out there, download this app to experience interactive tours of museums, landmarks, or other guided programming.

3. Walden
Lovers of Henry David Thoreau will not want to miss this video game that transports you to a digital Walden Pond. Find serenity and inspiration in this tribute to a life so connected to literature and nature.

4. Curiosity
Are you tired of losing to those smarmy know-it-alls during trivia nights? Now, it’s your turn to be one of those smarmy know-it-alls with the help of Curiosity. This app curates the most interesting information from the internet and distributes it to you daily.

5. Short Trip
With financial, career, and family pressures, being an adult is tough. Short Trip will pull you into a beautiful hand-drawn world for a relaxing tram ride in an idyllic countryside: no rules, no objects, and no student loan interest.
