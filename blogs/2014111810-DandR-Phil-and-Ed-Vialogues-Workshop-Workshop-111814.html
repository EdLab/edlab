D&R: Phil & Ed Vialogues Workshop (Workshop) 11.18.14

Kate Meersschaert

November 18, 2014

Listed below are the group activities for this D&R as well as all support resources. After today's D&R please add your thoughts and questions to the comments below! Activity #1: 1) Carmen will create a vialogues with the group for you to comment on (Sir Ken Robinson) 2) Insert one comment or question Activity #2: 1) We will explore the following Use Cases: a) The Voice: The Penetration of Techtonic... b) GREEN SCHOOL c) Justice (Sandel) 2) Develop an ideal use case for using vialogues to teach or learn w/ one of these vialogues. (Who will use the vialogue, how will they use it & what is the desired outcome?) Add your top suggestion to the comments below! Activity #3: 1) Create a vialogue 2) Pose 2 questions 3) Define case in which this Vialogue would be used ((add this to the description section of your vialogue) 4) Partner with a neighbor and comment on each-other's vialogues Thank you for your time and feedback! We are excited to officially test-out this workshop on Thursday! Kate, Carmen, Jo & Mason
