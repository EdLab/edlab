Home Journey 6.2.2009: Multi-Optional Web Map of Shanghai

Ting Yuan

June 5, 2009

When I was searching for several art exhibition places in Shanghai the other day, I found the Shanghai web map pretty interesting and convenient compared with the web maps I have seen before. In addition to traditional two-dimension maps, most of us are very familiar with the Google Map, which is three dimensional with real street views. However, this Shanghai web map offers something new. Here are its three options: 1. Simulation-Version 3D Map 2. Traditional 2D Map 3. Google-Earth-Like Map Each of the three maps can be switched to each other. I feel the first option is very helpful, as it combines some features of Google Map and traditional 2D maps, and not only does it provide general views of buildings (in a simulation/game mode) but also gives names of streets in a clear way. Furthermore, given that buses are important public transportation in Shanghai which are driven faster and come more frequently than the U.S. counterparts, it is just convenient to see all the stops marked on the map.
