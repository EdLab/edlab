NLT Launch!

Rebecca Beck

January 22, 2013

* Keep Private Please! I had the privilege of promoting NLT and Thursday's NLT Launch Celebration this afternoon on the first floor. I love sharing what we do with the TC community and students seemed genuinely excited about the event and New Learning Times. As expected I received many comments about the free food & drinks, but I was also asked some good questions that touched upon the story of NLT. I wanted to share some of these questions here on the blog so that we can be prepared for them on Thursday. These were the most common questions I received: 1) How is NLT different from Education Week or other education publications out there? 2) Why is NLT relevant for TC students? 3) How can TC students get involved and can TC students write for NLT? 4) What is the EdLab?
