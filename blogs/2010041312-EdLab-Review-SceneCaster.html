EdLab Review: SceneCaster

Chandler Kang

April 13, 2010

SceneCaster is a 3D social media application and online community where anyone can visualize and share their ideas while make those ideas real in the process. It allows you to create your own 3D spaces and share them with your Facebook and other social networking friends.
