Let Android Help You! Android Training to Help Developers Create Better Apps!

Demetri Lales

December 16, 2011

Let Android show you the way to a better app! The Google team has just launched their Android Training site! This will be a free site that will allow people to create better apps for the Andriod market. The site has several lessons with plans to add more to expand and refine the existing classes. The training site also has a community forum for peer to peer help and discussions about developing apps, and other resources. This is a very interesting move by the Google team, and a creative way to inspire more people of all ages, to make better and more awesome applications. This can lead to more kids developing apps like the boy Janet Jang previously blogged about. Check out the training site! See if Android can help improve your app building skills!
