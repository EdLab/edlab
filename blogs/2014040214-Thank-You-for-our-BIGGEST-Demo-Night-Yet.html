Thank You for our BIGGEST Demo Night Yet!

Kate Meersschaert

April 2, 2014

***Private! Thank you to the whole EdLab family for making our fourth EdLab Demo Night our BIGGEST yet! A special thank you to everyone from the team in attendance, those who helped behind the scenes and everyone who helped with pre-production of the event! (Also, a special thank you to Brian S. & Laura for working with me on the FRONT (row) line!) Thank you to Diana, Luke & Edwin for the beautiful photos included below! Initial takeaways: -115 guests out of 170 confirmed as attending -Overall the demos went-off without a hitch -We need to use the mics next time (more people = less ability for the back rows to hear) -We had a great mix of new faces and an assortment of "friends of EdLab" -The badge ribbons were a hit! Check-out the tweet below - including one from an attendee showing-off her collection of ribbons! -People seemed refreshed that presenting company's were focused on writing and content - not just #STEM! Please add your feedback, favorite moments, photos & takeaways below! Tweets about "#edlabdemos"
