Do You Want to Know What's Our Users' First Move After Logging in Vialogues?

Sharon Hsiao

February 22, 2013

Here's my first attempt of behavioral analysis on Vialogues. I gathered data from the data depository (after 2012/09/28~ til today). I was wondering what people do on Vialogues after they've logged in and then what? Do we use our screen space efficiently? etc. So the numbers say...

  1. 47% of people stay on the navigational bar after they've logged in.
  2. If we look at the navigational bar, not so much people checked the "favorites" right away. They didn't seem to care about the request after logging in. They either create, explore or look at their profile page. This is a good indication that people know where their profiles are, they are clicking on it. We can possibly put eye catching promotions there or fancy stuff there, etc.,
  3. There are a lot of "play" action right after they've logged in and this is a good sign. They got referral links from anywhere, and directly watched the video.
  4. Another point. Once users log in, they don't click on the featured videos that much (or at all). The numbers there are relatively low but the videos are taking quite a lot of screen space.
  5. There are some other interesting stories. I'll dig them out....(on plane, maybe)
