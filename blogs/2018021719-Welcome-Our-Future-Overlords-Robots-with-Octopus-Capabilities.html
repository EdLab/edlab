Welcome Our Future Overlords: Robots with Octopus Capabilities

Ryan.Allen

February 17, 2018


Octopuses are brilliant and fascinating creatures that have otherworldly transformation abilities. These animals can change color and shape in incredible ways to suit their surroundings. To keep humans one step ahead of the natural world, scientists are now developing soft robots with abilities inspired directly from these cephalopods.

Researchers have designed a rubber skin filled with a fiber mesh that can expand or contract in various shapes and even textures. The materials can roll up and reshape thousands of times without breaking down. While still in the early stages, this skin will soon be tested for further soft robotic development.

The natural world can offer a wide range of inspirations to make sure humans stay on top. What are some other animals that we should be mimicking for current innovations? Add your voice to the discussion on Vialogues
