July 27, 2015

**PRIVATE** Meet QuietMaster®. QuietMaster® (please don't forget the proud copyright tag) is one of the most advanced technologies not only in the field of air conditioning, but the field of applied science in general. Being invented and patented in the late 1960s, QuietMaster® was the most quiet machine on the planet, delivering as little decibel as a breath of a sleepy child. Also, hidden features innovated solely for the QMaster® include:

  • A shock-proof hydraulic system that can withstand a free fall from a 10-story building (an equivalent of a two football teams tackling) to ensure no external shock can compromise its ability to operate silently.
  • An ability to self-destruct when the patented QMaster® feature has expired (smoke and sparks may be expected).
