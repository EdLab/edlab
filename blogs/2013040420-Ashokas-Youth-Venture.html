Ashoka’s Youth Venture

Laura Scheiber

April 4, 2013


How do we encourage today’s youth to engage in social change? Ashoka’s Youth Venture is an international youth leadership community aimed at fostering the next generation of social changemakers. The goal of the program is to encourage youth to "improve their communities now and throughout their lives...Youth Venture inspires and supports teams of young people to launch and lead their own ‘Ventures’…"

As part of the program, participants engage in workshops, have access to fundraising tools, and are connected with adult mentors as well as a global network of other aspiring changemakers. Currently there are over 5,000 Venture Teams in 17 countries.

This video focuses on the first local community panel sponsored by the San Francisco Bay Area Youth Venture Program. With keynote speaker Matt Flannery, KIVA co-founder, and a showcase of the community-based projects that have evolved out of the youth’s involvement in the leadership program, it is hard not to feel inspired.
