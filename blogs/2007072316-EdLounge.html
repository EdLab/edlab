EdLounge

Joachim Castellano

July 23, 2007

Presenting EdLounge - an informal gathering at the EdLab on Fridays at 3:00 p.m. At EdLounge, we will premiere the new AfterEd episodes of the week. The channel team hopes to gather useful feedback from these viewing sessions. Please do come to enjoy the free food, drinks, music, and video games. It's the end of the week! You need to take a break and relax! So on Fridays at 3:00, everyone is invited to room 506. I'm certain that socializing (after a drink or two) will encourage creativity and innovation.
