Replace Relevance: Help EdLab Review

LauraCostello

August 29, 2012

(Please keep private) For the launch of NLT, we've been creating a hearty store of fresh EdLab Reviews and have noticed in our writing the dwindling appropriateness of the "EdLab Relevance" section. As a working fix, we have shortened it to just "relevance" but that does not really do it justice either. The ER team and I have been writing this section to describe the place of the tool in the new and developing landscape of education, to highlight particular features that have relevance to the NLT lens, and to speculate about the tool's future potential. We need a name that will set up the section clearly and accurately and we need your help! Below are some selected ideas, comment to let us know which one you like best or suggest some of your own! Future Possibilities Place & Potential Context & Connection (or play on the pro/con and do a “potential & connection”) NLT Angle The Pitch Direction
