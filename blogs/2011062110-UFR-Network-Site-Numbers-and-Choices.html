UFR Network Site: Numbers & Choices

chingfu

June 21, 2011

Here is my pilot UFR Network Teacher site, Numbers and Choices. Currently it is a series of brief blog posts that aim to help students connect their understanding of personal and family finance to U.S. national budget. In an ideal class environment where students have access to computers/laptops, I'll save all these posts as draft and publish one at a time as the class proceeds. Thus, I suggest you browse the post at the bottom first, and move upwards as you go through all posts. I need to think more about how to present these resources in a less ideal class environment where computers are not available for every student. This design experience also suggests that we should think about/focus more on differentiating UFR Network experiences from popular content management systems or open courseware. You are welcome to join our D&R meeting at 11:00 am today to explore other great UFR Network sites (explore here) and give us feedback on these efforts.
