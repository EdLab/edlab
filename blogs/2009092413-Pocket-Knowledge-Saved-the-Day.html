Pocket Knowledge Saved the Day!

Jessica Blum

September 24, 2009

Yesterday, my year-long relationship with my workstation laptop crashed and burned. Not literally, thank goodness, but viral pop-ups and adware ravaged my poor machine -- jeopardizing more than a year's worth of EdLab research, mock-ups, draft proposals, and project iterations! Luckily, PocketKnowledge was there to save the day! When I couldn't save the full contents to disk (or email them in any efficient manner), Stephen showed me how to upload the entirety of My Documents to PK (thanks, Stephen!), and now they are safely housed on my new desktop, ready to go as if nothing had happened. Phew. Just a friendly warning for all of us working on susceptible PCs . . . and an earnest plug for Pocket Knowledge! PS - Now I understand why the little pocket character looks so triumphant. Well deserved, well deserved.
