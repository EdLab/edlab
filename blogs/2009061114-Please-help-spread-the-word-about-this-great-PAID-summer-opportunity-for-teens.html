Please help spread the word about this great (PAID) summer opportunity for teens

Stephanie Hunt

June 11, 2009

Do you know a teen who's curious to learn more about art, technology, and/or social issues? Please point them towards the Eyebeam Digital Day Camp info. This is a great way to spend the month of July, and participants are paid $20/day for attending! Do note that applications are due June 18, and that students must attend a NYC public high school to participate.
