Levees Rough Cut

bhughes

March 26, 2007

At last, a rough edit of the Levees video. As most of you know, part of our grant for the Levees project was to create video material for the website that promotes the curriculum. Our goal in making this video was to touch on themes of teaching and learning about the Levees without being overly didactic about the way the curriculum can be implemented. We want to inspire teachers! (please read more) There are problems with this edit, not the least of which is that details such as the score, transitions (effects), and titles (names) are still in-progress. Basically our goal here is to get FEEDBACK about the overall construction of the video. Does the story make sense? What doesn't make sense? What did you want to know more about? When? Critique away! (But be gentle with us... we can only do so much at this point to shoot new footage and rearrange the existing stuff!) Please use the timer when possible (e.g., "at 3:14 I didn't know what the person was talking about"). Feedback we get today or tomorrow will help us continue to shape the project.
