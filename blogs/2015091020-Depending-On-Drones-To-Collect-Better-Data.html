Depending On Drones To Collect Better Data

Rebecca Beeson

September 10, 2015


Drones might be a little unreliable when it comes to delivering pizzas and transporting any package greater than a few pounds, but what about a drone that drops some knowledge? Even though they may not be ready to deliver an emergency espresso, drones could be used to quickly and efficiently gather and distribute data in emergency situations. After a flood, during a political crisis, or en route to difficult-to-reach destinations, drones may be more efficient at taking photos, capturing video messages, and collecting data than anyone with a smartphone. After the cool factor of one hour Amazon drone delivery wears off, it's time to re-imagine the role of these robots as collectors and distributors of new information.
