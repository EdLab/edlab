Never Read the Comments

Panisuan Chasinga

July 21, 2015

I've found this blog post by Sarah Adler, a 24-year-old, Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of a startup that just raised $2 million. She studied Journalism and Religious Studies in college, and has the last look any engineer would expect from his CTO. This is the Facebook comment she posted on her blog to show comments from engineers about her as CTO (or at least someone like her). She also gave an example of the great Larry Wall, the creator of Perl, who studied chemistry and music in college, to support her point that only 27% on college students went on to excel in what's related to their majors (which is probably why Google stops considering degrees from their job candidates entirely). Read the entire blog post here. The part where she was in the train coding was priceless!
