Product Feedback from the Academic Festival

Manav Malhotra

April 23, 2012

private please Hey Everyone, After an awesome Academic Festival, I just wanted to share some of the comments I got about Research Broker. Most of the feedback was about how great the idea was and how they can't wait to see what kind of companies will create postings. A couple of people asked whether there would be companies outside of education as well. I told them that would likely be the case in the future, but RB will initially focus on ed. We had a sign-in issue when someone tried to create an account, but we hadn't thoroughly bug-tested beforehand so we're doing that now. It was very positive overall and I think confirms our idea that getting interesting opportunities up there is the most important next step. What kind of feedback did everyone else get on the concept and implementation of our products? Or any other general feedback we should consider?
