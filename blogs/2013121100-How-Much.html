How Much!?

Elysee Dougan

December 11, 2013

Inflation affects everyone, especially for-profit business. These rising prices are making it difficult for some businesses to compete, especially if the competitor has lower prices for their goods. When setting a price for a good or service, it is important to make sure the price is reasonable for both the business and costumer. Setting prices too low can see the company financially suffer, but also setting the price very high will force most costumers to shop elsewhere. Those likely to continue shopping at a store with high prices for their goods tend to be very loyal to the store or brand. Loyal costumers play a big role in the financial success of businesses and companies. Apple and Gillette are two major companies with a loyal following among customers. For instance, Schick, Gillette's primary competitor, does not have such a loyal following. Gillette built too big of a reputation with a loyal costumer base for Schick.
