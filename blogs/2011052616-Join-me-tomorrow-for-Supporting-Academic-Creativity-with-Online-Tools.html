Join me tomorrow for "Supporting Academic Creativity with Online Tools"

bhughes

May 26, 2011

Just FYI, tomorrow I'll be giving a short talk (one third of a group session, "Tech Panel I") on Pressible as a "social learning tool" in the service of "academic creativity." Also at the session: Peter Beaugard will be presenting on Using Graphic Design Methods & Interactive Tools to Encourage Play in Online Learning. Nate Dern will be presenting on Crowdsourcing Creativity: The Amazon Studios Experiment. Then we'll open it up to discussion. Join us at 10:45am in Horace Mann 150. Though registration for the conference is $100, I think I could sneak the whole EdLab into this one session. :) Also see the conference's "meta-site" at: http://create.pressible.org/ Conference details: Creativity, Play, and the Imagination Across the Disciplines Conference http://blogs.tc.columbia.edu/creativityconference2011/ May 26-28th, 2011 (Thursday - Saturday)
