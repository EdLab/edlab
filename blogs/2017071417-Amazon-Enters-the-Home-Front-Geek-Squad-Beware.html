Amazon Enters the Home Front. Geek Squad Beware!

dinosossi2

July 14, 2017


First it was Amazon Bookstores.


Then it was Alexa.


Then it was the acquisition of Whole Foods.

Now it’s this. 


Amazon has started hiring people to act as its private Geek Squad to enter homes.

Why this newest incursion on the physical space?

Smart homes are the newest front in the digital realm.

And being able to quickly and capably enter homes of all types can be a whole new market.

Obviously there are potential sales for more Alexa devices by entering domiciles.

Also, internal studies show that there are fewer returns of devices.

Ultimately, being in the information and data business, Amazon can collect even more intimate data about your home life than they do already.

As a cost to convenience through a digitally enabled home, be prepared to give even more of your data away…
