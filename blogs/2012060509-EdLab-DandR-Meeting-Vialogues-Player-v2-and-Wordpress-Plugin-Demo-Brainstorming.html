EdLab D&R Meeting: Vialogues Player v.2 & Wordpress Plugin Demo + Brainstorming

zhou

June 5, 2012

* Private Please * Megha and I today will be demoing the work progress of our long-waited Vialogues Player v.2. We will also demo the Vialogues Plugin For Wordpress as a utility of our two grant projects (Rock & Roll Forever and YoungArts). In addition, we will reveal a public secret namely the Vialogues API which had existed from the first day of Vialogues' existence but only a few of us are aware of it. Finally, groups will discuss the potential application, promotion, and business model of the new Vialogues Player and Wordpress plugin via the support of the Vialogues API. The following questions will be discussed:

  • Will it be useful to implement a Vialogues API with full features support (video + discussions)?
  • Who might want to use the Vialogues API?
  • What features should we provide?
  • Business models?
