Article recommendation system

Ankit Ranka

November 6, 2009

I am working with pranay to develop an article recommendation system for TCR and NSSE. We are in the initial stage of the project. The main idea behind the system is to provide personalized recommendations to the users of TCR and NSSE. But the system will be generalized enough so that we can integrate it with social networking platforms such as elgg or netPosse. We are done with designing the generalized database for the system. The system will take into account the articles that user visited in the past and also the behavior of the similar users. We will be indexing all the TCR and NSSE articles and that index will be used to make recommendations. But we have to figure out the specific details about the algorithm.
