Flipping+Vialogues D&R Meeting

Demetri Lales

October 11, 2011

Hello Everyone! The links below are the Vialogues we will be watching and discussing during the D&R Meeting. Activity 1: https://vialogues.com/vialogues/play/1684 Activity 2: https://vialogues.com/vialogues/play/1685 Activity 3: https://vialogues.com/vialogues/play/1682 At the end of the Meeting please 1) Write a 2-3 sentences introduction to Vialogues, how would you best describe Vialogues to a teacher who has never used it before? How would you describe the best features of Vialogues? 2) Also can you think of any new features we can add to Vialogues to better help these teachers? Any other feedback on how to promote Vialogues would be greatly appreciated! For those who are interested in the Flipping movement below are some resources you can refer to, as well as a link to an info-graph on the subject. http://vodcasting.ning.com/ http://www.flippedclassroom.com/ http://www.fi.ncsu.edu/project/fizz/press https://bitly.com/bundles/tsc/a Info-Graphic on Flipping: http://joindotme.files.wordpress.com/2011/09/flipped-classroom.jpg Thank you.
