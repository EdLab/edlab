iFake In Walmart?

Oumar Soumahoro

March 25, 2013

You would expect to have a fake deal when you make a purchase from an ambulant seller than you will from a big retailer such as Walmart. According to this article, Susane Nassise walked out of a Walmart in Broxton, Massachusetts with what she thought was an iPod, until she opened up the box, and after checking the item at close angle noticed that it wasn't what she expected it to be, not the original iPod. This story has happened in a variety of states and in other big retail stores such as Best Buy, where consumers have been sold iPods very different from the one Apple has in its stores. The best way to avoid being a victim of this scam would be to either check the box in which your product comes in before you walk out of the store or make sure you get an iPod from the traditional store to guarantee a legit transaction. Should such purchasing mistakes happen in giant big retail stores such as Walmart and Best Buy? What do you think?
