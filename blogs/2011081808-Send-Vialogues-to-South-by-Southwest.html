Send Vialogues to South by Southwest

gnatriello

August 18, 2011

We need everyone's help to send Vialogues to South by Southwest. SXSW uses an online voting and commenting system as part of the process for selecting sessions to appear on the program. We have submitted a proposal for Vialogues for the SWSX Interactive Program, and we need lots of comments and votes to move us forward. You can get to the Vialogues page in the SXSW Panel Picker at: http://panelpicker.sxsw.com/ideas/view/13595 You will need to register on the system, but the process is simple. The system will be open until September 2 so vote early and vote often, and tell your friends and colleagues to do the same. The SWSX program committee encourages this kind of shameless self-promotion and sees generating an audience online as proof of the value of the proposed session.
