These 3D Printed Autonomous Boats Could Revolutionize City Planning and Health

Melanie.Hering

August 6, 2018


While most of the world is obsessing over autonomous cars, why not consider the autonomous boat? As demonstrated by researchers at MIT, self-driving boats can be a godsend for cities like Bangkok, Venice, and Amsterdam that rely on their waterways to carry both people and products to and fro.

The model developed by MIT’s Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Lab has high maneuverability and precise control, and can conveniently and rapidly be 3D printed. While the first thought behind this invention is to take a load off of infrastructure services, these boats could also be equipped with environmental sensors to monitor a city’s waters and help us learn about urban and human health.

What other opportunities for learning do you think autonomous boats could provide? Do you think they will be practical additions to commercial boats already on these waterways, or will they be a threat to them? Add your voice to the discussion on Vialogues.
