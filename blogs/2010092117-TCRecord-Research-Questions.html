TCRecord Research Questions

Megha Agarwala

September 21, 2010

DataDashboard is currently collecting some data from TCRecord, which is helping us in writing the yearly website usage reports and carrying out analysis on that data. There are many other interesting research questions that we can explore and implement so that DataDashboard starts collecting data related to those questions as well. Some of these questions are: 1. What is the click-through percentage (number of clicks made) for articles, book reviews, etc? 2. How much time are people spending on the website? 3. Are they using the search feature embedded in the website? If so, how are they using it? - Number of queries they are making? - Number of terms in the query? - Time spent on search? 4. How is the behavior of registered users different from non-registered users? 5. How is the behavior different based on geographic area? What are your thoughts on this? Do you have any interesting research questions? Any feedback on the above mentioned questions would also be very valuable.
