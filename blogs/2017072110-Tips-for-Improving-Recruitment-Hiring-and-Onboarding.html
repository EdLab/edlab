Tips for Improving Recruitment, Hiring, and Onboarding

gnatriello

July 21, 2017



As we continue to refine and improve our approach to recruiting, hiring, and onboarding, we might want to pay attention to the issues highlighted in a recent Baseline article on the recruiting and onboarding process and candidate preferences.  The article cites a Career Builder Survey that identifies common gaps in the process. This is a good checklist for examining our process of recruiting and on boarding.  The recent redesign of this process led by George addressed many of the issues noted in the article and survey, but there are additional steps we can take to make our process even better.  It would be great to hear from recently hired staff about the hiring experience. Post comments here or let George know directly.
