Parents Fear Student-Teacher Texting

Meredith Powers

July 24, 2014


Despite evidence confirming that one-on-one texting between teachers and students fosters supportive relationships, many parents remain uneasy—sometimes devastatingly so—with their children having direct communication via text with teachers. Will parents’ perceptions of the risks continue to outweigh any potential rewards?
