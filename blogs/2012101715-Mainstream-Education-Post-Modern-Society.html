Mainstream Education + Post-Modern Society = ?

Kate Meersschaert

October 17, 2012

This Forbes.com article from the CEO of Curriki, is an informative look at the clash of modern life and an outdated education system. The article points-out that the following three trends are dominant in the transformation of our out-dated schools into more modern institutions... 1) Devices (mobile etc.) in ed settings 2) The Common Core Standards 3) OER (Open Educational Resources) Do you agree? Are these the three saving-graces of the education system? If not, why not? What is missing? What should be removed from the list?
