Going Viral

HSC

January 17, 2008

In light of the AfterEd TV Launch discussion at our most recent seminar, I wanted to share two articles with everyone. The first, "Six Strategies for Building Viral Content" provides a framework for creating social media. The second article contains 37 viral marketing ideas. The fact that the AfterEd team has already employed so many of these practices speaks volumes about their work. My favorites are: #2: Create a time spectacle: create content non-stop over a designated period of time (8hrs, 24hrs?). #29: Show readers how to construct a cheap object that will prove useful to them. #23: Predict what your niche will look like in 5, or 10, or 50 years. #24: Create a list of feeds you believe everyone interested in your topic should be subscribed to.
