With Snapchat Update, Student Attention & Teacher Patience Fade Fast

Brian.Sweeting

May 7, 2014


Even in classrooms where mobile phones are a welcomed learning tool, last week’s social network update caused students and teachers alike to snap.

Widespread panic broke out amongst teens who absolutely needed to update their Snapchat app for a revolutionary feature that allowed them to... chat. One science teacher in Kansas not only had to confiscate phones, but found a student hiding under a desk. "In 16 years of teaching I can’t think of anything that has ever disrupted my classroom more than today’s @snapchat update," she now famously tweeted.

For educators who incorporate apps in their lessons, taking phones away is a waste of time and further disrupts learning, and expecting students to exercise self-restraint seems unlikely. Perhaps Snapchat could help a teacher out and keep updates from showing up until the weekend, because this problem isn’t going to disappear on its own.
