EdLab Halloween Party Postmortem!

Kate Meersschaert

November 1, 2011

Featured below are a few pictures and thoughts from EdLab Halloween 2011! I hope one of the main results of the event is that, in addition to being on-hand to answer questions about the "mysterious 5th floor" for TC students & staff we were able to generate interest in the lab's work, our tools and our open jobs! Enjoy these pics & please share other shots you might have taken and your thoughts here! We will have the official photos from the party photo booth soon as well! Mystery paper clip pumpkin appears... Jo is the best DJ! The lab is set... pizza at the ready! Tech graveyard... what a hit! Sega Genesis lives! Ednode has her big moment with a moustache! Remy the Vialogues cat shines in red satin bows! Pundit pontificates panache! Megha gets behind the camera... Pam & Patrick are haunted! These jobs are sweet! Julia & Basak are going to renovate the lab! It just isn't a party until the preschool arrives! A big thanks to everyone who helped Jo, Janet, Megha & I pull-off this party!
