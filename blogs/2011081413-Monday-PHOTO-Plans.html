Monday PHOTO Plans

bhughes

August 14, 2011

*keep private* Hi EdLabbers, tomorrow (Monday) Steven Van Zandt is visiting the lab. I think you knew that. But what you might not have known is that we'll be take a group portrait with him around 3:30pm. So, here's the full plan, so you can plan your time accordingly to be in the picture: - He'll arrive around 3pm, and take a quick tour of the lab. This is a good time to be working as usual. Wait, no: remember he is assessing our group as a current and future financial partner -- work vibrantly. - He'll meet with a small group led by Gary for about 30 minutes in 505. - Sometime between 3:30-4pm he'll emerge into the seminar space, where Yana will begin taking photos. Think wedding photos: we'll arrange a few with the project leads (SVZ, Warren, Hui Soo, Gary) and THEN assemble the whole group. Hence, once you see some photos being taken, place be ready to join. After the group photo, he might sign a few autographs, so if you're a big SVZ fan, be ready for the cue . . . - Once the photo and social stuff is done (15 minutes?), we'll whisk SVZ off to the other end of campus to do a video interview. Then we'll let him go back into the wild. Thanks! Casual dress is fine, and feel free to wear your EdLab Shirt. See you tomorrow!
