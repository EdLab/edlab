#Internagram

Mable Yiu

June 20, 2013

It's official! The EdLab interns have our very own Instagram account @EdLabTC! I think it took a while for Hui Soo to warm up to the idea, but I think he really likes it now! If you have a personal Instagram account, please go ahead and follow us! Also, I will email everyone soon with the log-in details. We were thinking of having it be more of the interns' job to post photos and manage the account, but of course, everyone, please feel free to sign into the account and snap some pictures! Not only can it help recruit people, but it's a great way share some of our experiences here at EdLab! More info about our Instagram account and ways you can get involved will be discussed at the next D&R meeting!
