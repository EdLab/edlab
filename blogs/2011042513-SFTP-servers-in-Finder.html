SFTP servers in Finder

Josh Anderson

April 25, 2011

Hey guys, Just thought I'd share a little app for mac osx. MacFusion let's you open an STFP server inside Finder, and, it gives all your other apps access to the server. Kinda groovy, huh? A few of us spend a lot of time in the sandbox so I thought this might be helpful. I know I'm going to be using it. :)
