How to Add the EdLab Logo to your Signature on Email

Daniel Um

June 29, 2012

NOTE: Please leave this post as 'private only' for EdLab —— Question: Why should I care to add an email signature, or to add a logo? There are many reasons why you should set up an 'automatic' signature file in your email preferences, and good reasons to add a logo too. It instantly raises the level of your communications, both internally and externally. By adding a branded value area at the bottom of your messaging, in the form of your email signature, you enhance visibility of EdLab and that of yourself. It's also a marketing tool (for both) that leaves a residual effect in recipients' minds. It's a win—win. I thought that it might be good to share the steps, as not everyone might know exactly how to set up their email signature: You'll have a CHOICE of a STATIC image, or an Animated GIF image. Now, the STEPS... 1. In the TOP RIGHT Corner, LOCATE this Icon, then CLICK:

2. LOCATE Settings, then CLICK

3. In SETTINGS window, LOCATE Signature Area

4. LOCATE This Icon, but don't click it (uncheck 'No signature', if selected)

5. SET UP the TEXT portion of your Signature first, or if you have a text Signature already, just CLICK where you'd like to have the LOGO appear. Then CLICK the 'Mountain' Icon.
