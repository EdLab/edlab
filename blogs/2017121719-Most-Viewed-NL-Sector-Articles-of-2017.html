Most Viewed NL Sector Articles of 2017

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2017


This year the most viewed articles on the learning sector are focused on using technology to increase accessibility. Revisit how these tools are leveling the playing field.

1. Did Someone Say Field Frip?
Frip is rethinking the future of field trips with an online planning platform that makes experiential learning more accessible for everyone.

2. The YouTube of World Class Coaching
This collection of coaching videos is improving access to premium athletic insight and knowledge.

3. Surf the Blood Stream With Virtual Reality
The Body VR is moving virtual reality technology from the entertainment sector into the classroom with an app that lets scientists virtually cruise through the bloodstream and build DNA parts.

4. Building Women Representation in Tech Fields
Girl Develop It is a non-profit teaching software development skills to women with diverse backgrounds, helping them improve their careers and build confidence.

5. Art, Anywhere
The Artsy tool is making all of the world's art more accessible to anyone with an internet connection.
