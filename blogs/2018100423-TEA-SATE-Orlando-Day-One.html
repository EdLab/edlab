TEA SATE Orlando, Day One

jvazquez

October 4, 2018

Greetings from SeaWorld!



Cinthia, Kim, Angela, and I are attending the 2018 TEA SATE conference. 

Each of the talks correspond to one of the themes of the four pillars of the Themed Events Association (TEA): Storytelling, Architecture, Technology, and Experience SATE). Today’s talks focused on Experience and Storytelling.  



We are slated to share our takeaways at an upcoming seminar so I won't reveal too much  now, so consider the following a teaser for what is to come. 


Here are some paraphrases from the day:

The new KPIs to measure the success of  experience design: task (can people do what they set out to do), social (is there opportunity for people to engage with each other), discovery (can people with no clear goal wander aimlessly), entertainment (does it offer an escape), aspirational (can people do something they’ve always wanted to do).

-Thomas Ito
Principal and Global Lifestyle Sector Leader, Gensler


I’m not interested in creating instagramable moments, I want to create instagramable journeys. 

-Joseph Lucius
Global Director of Events, Tom Ford Beauty

If fashion makes a statement, costumes tell a story. 

-Virginia Chu
Managing Director and Founder, Limelight Costumes

(On working collaboratively across groups:) A lot of designers feel left out of the process, especially the storytelling process. 

-Colette Piceau
Creative Director/Writer, It Ain’t Shakespeare, Inc
