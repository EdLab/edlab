Video Team Visits Arizona

Jihii Jolly

February 12, 2014

-private- The video team took a trip to Arizona last week to shoot some Voice episodes from this issue of TC Record which has some fascinating pieces in it! We hit Tucson (where we tried some really great Pho near the University of Arizona), drove to Phoenix (and set a record of 9 minutes for setting up studio at ASU) and then headed to the Grand Canyon to film an innovative distance learning program they've got going on there.
