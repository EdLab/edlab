Survey Sidekick Development Session

Adam Mitchinson

August 21, 2008

The Development & Research team met today to discuss the future of Survey Sidekick, the Edlab's very own online survey tool. The tool is envisioned as being both practical and educational, teaching individuals how to best conduct surveys as they simultaneously create them. It is intended to provide the capacity to create highly functioning surveys but also give insight into best practices in online survey methodology. Todays session began with a discussion of existing online survey tools, such as Survey Monkey and TC's very own Survey Wizard, focusing on the fact that whilst these tools are highly functional, their quality is dependent on the creators knowledge of the survey process. The group discussed the challenge of incorporating this functionality into Survey Sidekick while providing the educational understanding which will help users create more reliable and accurate surveys. As the project moves into the future the group will begin to develop educational content for the tool and decide on the most beneficial ways to present this information. There is a lot of brainstorming to be done and so ideas and feedback of any type are welcome!
