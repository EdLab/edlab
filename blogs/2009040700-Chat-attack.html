Chat attack

jvazquez

April 7, 2009

Time is a precious commodity lately but if you have any to spare, I have just the thing for you, my friends: Omegle. Just go to http://omegle.com and start chatting with a random stranger from anywhere in the world. It's like a throwback to the chatrooms of the mid-late nineties. For the most part, the conversations I've had so far are really short and fun and, for some reason, 3 of the 5 I spoke to tonight were from Brazil. For someone like me who grooves on getting to know people, this site is pretty interesting. Check it out but only if you can afford to spend the time! It will draw you in for sure...
