Trends in Ed, 08.18.09

Arianna Choi

August 18, 2009

Star Teacher in Classroom AND on a Reality Show This star teacher is a real celebrity. The news came out last week as the school commissioners in Philadelphia received a letter from Mayor Michael Nutter supporting A&E channel's new reality TV show “Teach,” which will follow actor Tony Danza co-teaching a 10th grade English class. A pilot episode was already shot and while the producers aren't sure when the show will air, Danza is attending teacher training with other first-year teachers in the district. School officials will vote tomorrow on whether to allow at least 13 episodes of “Teach” to be shot at Northeast High School. Related: “Yo, Teach! Tony Danza poised to head back to class,” The Washington Post
