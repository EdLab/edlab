Your Permanent Record 7: Student Stories Near & Far

Jessica Blum

October 25, 2010

Your Permanent Record #7 presents the stories and experiences of 5 international high school students: Ahmed, Jeff, Idrissa, Duncan and Eric. Now studying in New York, the five students share the ups and downs of moving to – and learning in – a brand new country. The episode also features a cameo commentary by EdLab super-coach, George!
