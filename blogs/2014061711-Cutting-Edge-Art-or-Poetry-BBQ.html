Cutting Edge Art | Poetry BBQ

Brian.Sweeting

June 17, 2014

*Private* Just wanted to share two events I'm going to this week in case you're interested in attending as well: Arthur I. Miller, Emeritus Professor of History and Philosophy of Science at University College London, will discuss his new book, Colliding Worlds: How Cutting-Edge Science is Redefining Contemporary Art (available on Amazon) Wed, June 18th @ 6pm, CUNY Graduate Center Poetry Genius is throwing their annual End-of-the-Year BBQ this Thursday at their HQ in Williamsburg. Warning: they have a ridiculously nice rooftop with gorgeous views of Manhattan.

(Background: Jeremy Dean, Chief Education of Education at Genius, has been a seminar speaker, interviewee on Groundbreakers, and we've reviewed Rap Genius on NLT.)
