One-Way Wall for Sound

Kate Meersschaert

February 21, 2014

In continuing to think (dream) of potential technologies to employ on the 4th floor, I found this interesting design in Bloomberg Businessweek for a "one-way wall for sound." Basically, this device projects sound waves through a wall, but prevents sound waves from penetrating in the opposite direction. Perfect for creating permeable "walled-off" space within a large open multi-use plan. Do you feel this technology might have use on the 4th floor? In the classroom?
