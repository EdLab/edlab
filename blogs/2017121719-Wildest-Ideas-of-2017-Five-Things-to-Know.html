Wildest Ideas of 2017: Five Things to Know

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2017


Winston Churchill said, "Without tradition, art is a flock of sheep without a shepherd. Without innovation it is a corpse." In 2017 we scoured the web for the most innovative videos on learning. Here are five goodies you may have missed:

1. You’ll Have to Travel Below to Find Earth’s Biggest Musical Instrument.
This vialogue shows how Earth’s natural elements, like stalactites, can create the most sublime and beautiful sounds in the world.

2. Spy Creatures Collect Animal Footage by Making Friends.
Filmmaker John Downer’s ingenious spy cameras bring us a previously unavailable in-depth understanding of the inner-lives of animals.

3. The Gender AI
Artificial intelligence has made it possible to bring an indisputable evidence-base to the lesser presence of women in films, which have consistently favored men for decades.

4. Let This 11-year-old Create Your Password.
For a small fee, this eleven-year-old creates uncrackable passwords by rolling dice and using the numbers to find corresponding words in the diceware dictionary.

5. AI Robot Takes the Burden Out of End-of-Life Conversations.
While most people would prefer to die at home, 78% of Americans don’t have a plan in place that directly expresses their end-of-life wishes. This bot helps overcome the conversational barriers.
