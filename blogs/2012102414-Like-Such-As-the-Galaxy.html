Like, Such As, the Galaxy

jvazquez

October 24, 2012

Why do we have so much trouble locating things on maps?? In another blow to Americans' scientific street cred, researchers at the University of Michigan found that 43% of adults aged 37-40 could not correctly identify a picture of a galaxy as our own Milky Way. ...Could you? Interestingly enough, the study found that "those in the know about the Milky Way were slightly less likely than the other participants to agree that "the size and complexity of the universe proves the greatness of God's creation" (45 percent versus 51 percent, respectively)." For more on this space case, check out LiveScience.
