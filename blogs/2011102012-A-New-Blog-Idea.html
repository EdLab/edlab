A New Blog Idea

Fred Rossoff

October 20, 2011

Hui-Soo has suggested a new topic to be added to the regular blog rotation, and I thought I'd run it up the flagpost and see who salutes. The idea is basically to regularly offer concise but thorough explanations of educational/pedagogical theories. For instance, in a recent Schoolyard Brawl discussion, Skanda blindsided me by discussing "Constructivism", a concept with which I had no familiarity. This new blog series would help myself, and possibly others who don't have much knowledge of that side of the educational world, gain a little more literacy in this domain. Now, I'm ready to go it alone, but if anyone else has the courage to join me in this expedition, I'd be glad to have them on my team. The working title is “Education Unpacked,” but we're taking suggestions on that front as well. (keep private)
