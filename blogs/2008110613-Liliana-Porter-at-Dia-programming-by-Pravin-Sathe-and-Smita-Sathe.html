Liliana Porter at Dia, programming by Pravin Sathe and Smita Sathe

Pravin Sathe

November 6, 2008

Tonight is the opening for a new piece at the Dia Art Foundation by the wonderful Argentine artist Liliana Porter. Her work is very whimsical and her first online work is titled Rehearsal. My mother and I did the flash programming and were super stoked to not only work with her but on the piece. I started working on web projects with Dia while working with Tony Oursler. He, along with Stephen Vitello and Constance De Jong did the first web project (and CD-ROM) called Fantastic Prayers. I took a bit of a hiatus and was recently asked to work with them again. I brought my mom along (she's a master programmer) and we have now completed three projects in the web series: Rosa Barba's Vertiginous Mapping, Barbara Bloom's Half Full/Half Empty and Liliana Porter's Rehearsal.
