What about badges?

Janice Joo

September 4, 2012


Mozilla's Open Badge Initiative has inspired a multitude of organizations, companies, and even higher education institutions to experiment with issuing badges. Badges are digital records of not only technical and/or academic credentials, but also skills such as leadership and collaboration. Could badges ever stand alone as the "better" alternative to degrees and certificates? If the average annual earnings of a person with a Bachelors degree is $65k and the average annual earnings of a person with a certificate is $42k, what might a badge be worth?
