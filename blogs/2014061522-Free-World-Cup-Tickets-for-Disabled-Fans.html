Free World Cup Tickets for Disabled Fans

AbdulMalik

June 15, 2014

In most societies, disabled people are deprived of the opportunities to participate in sports. Most disabled people who enjoy sports would have loved to be given the chance to participate in a sport or support and cheer on a team just like any ordinary fan will. At the ongoing World Cup in Brazil, the local organizing committee is putting measures in place to ensure that disabled people can also participate in the World Cup.

According to this article, the committee intends to donate 32,000 free tickets to disabled fans for matches. Perhaps this is a good way to reassure disabled fans around the world that they form an integral part of society. As influential as the World Cup has been, it will not be too surprising if other organizers of international events adopt this campaign of getting disabled people involved in the festivities.
