Hack For A Good Cause (Or Because will.i.am Told You To)

jvazquez

September 5, 2013


In various cities across America on the weekend of June 1-2 of this year, a beautiful thing happened. Technologists, software developers, designers and entrepreneurs joined forces to take part in the National Day of Civic Hacking. Sponsored by Intel, the event aimed to use "publicly-released data, code and technology to solve challenges relevant to our neighborhoods, our cities, our states and our country." Among several contributors to the event was will.i.am’s nonprofit, i.am.angel Foundation.

will.i.am has been an outspoken ally in the movement to build excitement around coding and developing. While the above video is only 40 seconds long, it’s enough time for the rapper and mogul to get his message across: Find a group and make some apps!

Working in multidisciplinary teams within a fixed amount of time has proven to be the kind of pressure cooker environment that brings great ideas to life (see Startup Weekend EDU). When the next National Day of Civic Hacking comes around, will you be one of the participants? Share your thoughts on Vialogues. In the meantime, Kanye, Britney, Jay-Z, Dr. Dre—in the words of will.i.am himself, let’s get it poppin’!
