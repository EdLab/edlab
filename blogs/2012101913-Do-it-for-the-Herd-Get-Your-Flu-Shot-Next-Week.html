Do it for the Herd: Get Your Flu Shot Next Week!

LauraCostello

October 19, 2012

(please keep private) As the daughter of a nurse, I feel honor bound to remind you of the free flu shots offered annually in Everett Lounge. Here's the inf, as listed on the CU Health Services page: Date: Tuesday, October 23 Time: 2:00 - 6:00 p.m. Location: Zankel Hall, Everett Lounge Last year they were notably quick and efficient! They swipe your card, a friendly medical professional does the deed, and you're on the way to seasonal flu-immunity! If most of us get vaccinated, everyone in the lab is less likely to get sick. Go team!
