D&R July 21

zhou

July 21, 2015

Sorry about the technical difficulties in the D&R guys. I checked the server logs and it seems the bottleneck is not fully on the streaming server because we hardly reached half of its capability at the peak time. I think the technical barrier is on either the TC network connectivity (because a dozen of us are streaming videos at the same time) or the Meteor.com free app hosting. As discussed with Ryan, I could also send the instructor's video stream to a content distribution network (CDN) which will provide a more robust access point for the video stream. Anyway, my a few takeaway from the D&R:

  • The interaction allowed in the technology determines the pedagogical model in distance learning.
  • Test out and stress test the technology before it is being used for production.
  • Provide alternatives to your main technology in case it fails at the critical moment.
Video streaming is my side project but I will keeping thinking and developing the application when I am free. If you have any ideas or thoughts you are welcome to grab me and talk. Thank you for your patience in today's D&R!
