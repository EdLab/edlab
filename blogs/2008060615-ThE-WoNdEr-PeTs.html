ThE WoNdEr PeTs

Greg Hallman

June 6, 2008

Hello, everyone. There is a small production house in town called Little Airplane Productions. They created a kid's show called Wonder Pets. I think it's an excellent show. Little Airplane offers group tours of their facilities and production space. I've been wanting to see their digs for awhile. So if you're interested in joining the group tour let me know . They charge $10 per person. Located down near South Street Seaport. http://www.littleairplane.com/
