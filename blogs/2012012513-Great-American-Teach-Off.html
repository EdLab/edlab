Great American Teach Off!

Janet Jang

January 25, 2012

Do you know any innovative and passionate 7-12 teachers you would like to nominate? Here's the K-6 winner from the last contest. I think it's wonderful that public school teachers are being recognized for their hard work. One running theme from the contest entries from last time was how many of them paid out of their own pocket to bring in tools and supplies that they believed would be beneficial to the students that the school's budget couldn't cover. There aren't many other professions out there where self-sacrifice is the norm. Teachers say it is part of the "calling". However, it makes me wonder where the line is between it just being part of the job versus a reflection of negligence in this country for funding education.
