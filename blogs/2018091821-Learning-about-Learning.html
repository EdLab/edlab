Learning about Learning

HSC

September 18, 2018

At the D&R today I shared that it might be useful for EdLab/Library staff members to become more familiar with some of the literature on learning (Patrick echoed a similar thought). 


Here are some resources on learning, broadly defined, to help us get started:


Natriello, G. (2007). Imagining, seeking, inventing: the future of learning and the emerging discovery networks. Learning Inquiry, 1(1), 7-18.


Natriello, G. (2015). Networked learning. Pp. 337-348 in Handbook of Educational Psychology, APA Division 15.  New York: Routledge.
