Behind the Scene of Mindful Lights

veronicablack

January 27, 2016

After the install Mindful Lights in the 3rd floor gallery we were asked to think about the future use of the structure in the Learning Theater. 


Thinking about the controls. I was talking to someone a few weeks ago and we were playing around with ideas for the MakeyMakey. Now I'm not sure if the MakeyMakey would work for this but it's something try... maybe. Inspired by BIG the movie,

what about having a "keyboard/piano" that related to the lights and sounds. Not piano or 8-bit sounds more like soothing noses, OMs, bells, wind, water. When triggered/note played a light would come on and fade along with a sound.
