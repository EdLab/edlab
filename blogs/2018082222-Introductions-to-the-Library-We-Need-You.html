Introduction(s) to the Library - We Need You

bhughes

August 22, 2018

Hi all EdLabbers. We need you. We have three upcoming events to welcome new TC students to campus: next Wednesday (8/29, 12-1pm) and Tuesday, September 4th (2-3pm, 3-4pm). The event will follow the same format every time, and YOU are at the heart of it.


UPDATE, 8/24: Kim will join the D&R meeting on Tuesday to review the plan for the event. Everyone is invited!


What the general plan? 


1) We'll meet in the theater. (15 minutes before the start of the event, please.) You will assemble a name tag for yourself.


2) 10 minutes before the event, doors open. Students will gather in the reception area (or extended area for larger groups). We will join them.


3) After 15 minutes (5 min past the hour), Gary will welcome students to the event.


4) Glass doors up, light go up. We all enter the theater.


5) In the theater: Music plays. Videos loop silently. Food & drink is on hand (Weds = pizza; Tues = coffee & cookies). We all talk to the students about the library, what we do, and answer questions.


6) After 40 minutes, librarians will gather interested students in small groups for tours of floors 1 -3.


That's it!


Please add these dates to your calendar.


In advance of the events, please think about your "elevator pitch" for how your work at EdLab connects to student life. The environment will feature a set of videos that highlight:


- The Library's education program

- New Learning Times

- Teachers College Record

- EdLab Seminars

- The Learning Theater

- and more.


I hope to see you there!
