Thank you Amphetamobile, Biblionasium & Late Night Labs!

Kate Meersschaert

December 5, 2012

A big EdLab thank you to the companies who participated in last-night's inaugural demo night! Presenters included: Dr. SiNae Pitts & Amphetamobile Marjan Ghara & Biblionasium Ovi Jacob and Harris Goodman of Late Night Labs Event Takeaways... The presenters all brought energy and passion for both their products and the field of educational entrepreneurship to the event. The 100-strong crowd was an interesting mix of "edupreneurs", educators, press, a few VC's and even an ex-NYC public schools Chancellor. The Q&A flow was exciting as each presenter only had 15 minutes for both their demo and to answer audience questions. Audience members lined-up at a mic stand to ask their questions and managed to ask both challenging and thought-provoking inquiries. The networking portion of the night took-place on the 5th floor of the Gottesman Libraries in the EdLab space and was a great opportunity for all attendees to meet and share resources, food & drink! Overall, the feedback was overwhelmingly positive and everyone asked when we would hold our next event! Stay tuned... Please be sure to follow the startups who presented: @amphetamobile @BiblioNasium @LateNiteLab And check-out the demo night Twitter conversation: #EdLabDemos & @EdLabTC Here's to another successful demo event soon!
