Trends in Ed, 10.01.09

jvazquez

October 1, 2009

The newest LMS kid on the block: Fedena Fedena hails itself as the first, “open source school/campus management system.” In fact, it's so new that its website is still being constructed as we speak! It is a little difficult to parse out any specific details given the lack of content, but I gathered that their mission is to, “utilize the power of technology to simplify the process of education.” One cool feature I noticed was that it can communicate with users via SMS and email. Its developers seem very enthusiastic and helpful (check out the Fedena community board). It will be released to the public in mid-October but the golden bug-free version is expected to come out in January 2010. (I hope that bug-free version recognizes the bug in that logo...) I am really curious as to whether it will, in fact, harness the power of technology in its features. If it does, it could have some serious(ly cool) implications on the future of the LMS.
