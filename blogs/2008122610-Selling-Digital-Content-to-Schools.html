Selling Digital Content to Schools

gnatriello

December 26, 2008

Those of use interested in getting new ideas and products into schools, particularly in digital form, might learn something from the efforts of Discovery. It seems that Discovery Education is now selling $2000 subscriptions to about half of the 120k schools in the US. The subscriptions provide access to the digital content of Discovery Channel and related properties.
