Learning a New Language Takes Dedication

rendy

October 31, 2013

Learning a new language takes some level of dedication and excitement to keep going. Some people lose their excitement and dedication dwindles especially if the language they are learning is difficult. The vialogue below features Tim Ferris, who had some difficulties learning new languages but stayed dedicated until he was able to master the language. This was especially true when he went to Japan and had to learn kanji. As true for any learner, all you need is someone to support and sustain you until the end.
