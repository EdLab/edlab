Trends in Ed, 9. 15. 09: Are Colleges Ready for Personal Branding Courses?

Ting Yuan

September 15, 2009

The term of “personal branding” has become increasingly known to young job seekers and school applicants. Nowadays, universities and companies are not interested in looking at a ten-page-long personal profile (only if you are as well-known as Michael Owen). Instead, they ask for web links or videos about you to examine your information online.

Advocates of personal branding line up and argue for the indispensable need of personal branding courses offered by higher education institutions.
