More STEM Teachers the Solution?

rendy

June 10, 2014

President Obama announced the construction of 100,000 new STEM teachers by 2020 in his State of the Union address a few years ago. The decline of STEM is all the rage in education these years. Articles about our nation needing STEM workers such as Tapping America's Potential and Rising Above the Gathering Storm argue that the US is not competing globally with regards to STEM education. The creation of the new teacher jobs will hopefully lead to an increase in STEM interest and participation by students.
