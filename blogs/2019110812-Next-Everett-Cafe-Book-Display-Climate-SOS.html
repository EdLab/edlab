Next Everett Cafe Book Display: Climate SOS

Jennifer

November 8, 2019

Hi, All. Here is what we're thinking of for the next Everett Cafe book display. Thoughts, comments, suggestions most welcome! We're looking to launch in early December and keep the exhibit up until mid-January.


Title: Climate SOS


Pointing to “untold suffering due to the climate crisis", 11,000 scientists from 153 nations recently endorsed a major statement on the ill health of our planet which was published on the 40th anniversary of the first world climate conference and in the journal BioScience. We are urged: unless there are major changes to global society, we face a climate emergency that threatens not only our natural ecosystems, but our humanity.


On display through mid-January, Climate SOS features compelling books that share our concern for our planet and desire to understand the magnitude of the problem. While critical issues include energy consumption, population stabilization, nature conservation, diet, and world GDP growth, we embrace a common narrative, with a call to educators, citizens, and policy makers.
