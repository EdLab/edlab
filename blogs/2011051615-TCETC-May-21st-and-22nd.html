TCETC: May 21st and 22nd

Melanie Hibbert

May 16, 2011

TCETC 2011, the Teachers College Educational Technology Conference is scheduled for this weekend (Saturday, May 21st and Sunday, May 22nd). There are two full days of activities planned with presenters from across TC, as well as from twelve other universities. Topics include building literacies, social media, technology policy and school integration, video, virtual worlds, and many other education + technology related subjects. We have a mobile application with the conference schedule and proceedings. There will be three presentations on EdLab projects, including EdNode, Vialogues, and The Voice. It should be a fun and interesting conference, so please come!
