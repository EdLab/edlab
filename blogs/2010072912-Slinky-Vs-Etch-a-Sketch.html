Slinky Vs Etch a Sketch

Michelle Delateur

July 29, 2010

Want to revisit your childhood? Have a personal story about why the slinky was your favorite toy? Couldn't get the Etch a Sketch out of your hands? Well you're in luck! The two toy companies have introduced a friendly competition: The Slinky Vs Etch A Sketch Film Festival! Yup - You read that right. The rules are simple: Submit a video describing which toy is your favorite and why. You could win lots of money. Now the reason I am bringing this up on the EdLab blog is not because I think Etch a Sketch is awesome or I spent hours trying to get a slinky to crawl down the staircase as a kid. What is interesting to me is that these toys have lasted throughout time and technological advances (although Etch a Sketch could definitely be a precursor to the iPad) to remain whimsical toys that spark creativity. You can still buy both in the toy store. You can still breathe a sigh of relief when you give a kid in a car an Etch a sketch. Sometimes it pays to be a fan of the classics ; ) Literally.
