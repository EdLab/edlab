Memories... of the 1st EdLab Jazz Band!

Kate Meersschaert

June 7, 2012

Hi everyone! I thought I would share some of the only "proof" (thanks to Pranav!) of the inaugural EdLab Jazz band! Thanks to Janice and her beautiful voice/fearless leadership... we all got a taste for spur-of-the-moment musical creation! I think Prof. Sawyer would be proud... but, perhaps not Mr. Mingus! ;) Seriously, thank you all for taking the plunge with us and here's to many more opportunities to "riff" together! Janice & Jing leading the charge! I think I need a "snapping" tutor! Why is Brian hiding? Allen "bongering"!
