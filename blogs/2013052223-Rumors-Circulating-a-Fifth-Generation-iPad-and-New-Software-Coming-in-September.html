Rumors Circulating a Fifth Generation iPad and New Software Coming in September

Khalil Abubakar

May 22, 2013

Weeks ahead of the 2013 Worldwide Developers Conference, there are rumors that Apple will release a new iPad along with the new MacBook. Additionally, there is also the rumor it will unveil its new mobile operating system IOS 7, which has led many to believe a new iPhone could be on its way very soon. The controversial fifth generation iPad to be released will have a 2,048 x 1,536 Retina display, the same as the one used in the 4th-generation model, except the glass display will be 0.05 thinner than the 4th generation iPad and a lot lighter according to industry sources to Digitimes, a well known IT publication. This report might be true because it is said that 100% of Digitimes' Apple product predictions has been accurate.
