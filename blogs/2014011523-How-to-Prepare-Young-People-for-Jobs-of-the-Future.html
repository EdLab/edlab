How to Prepare Young People for Jobs of the Future

Henry Adjei

January 15, 2014

College graduates are increasingly finding it difficult to land a job after graduation. Those fortunate enough to have a job are working in areas unrelated to their academic training in college. One popular sentiment that employers continue to lament is the lack of skills new graduates are bringing to the workforce. This perceived lack of skills is one of the reasons why recent graduates are finding a hard time landing a job. In today's super competitive and knowledge driven economy, it is essential for students to graduate with a set of skills that will help them land a job after graduation. This article highlights ten skills that can benefit new and soon to be graduates. There were several takeaways for me from this article. First, I need to be ambitious and resourceful and have a passion in everything I do. I must also be interested in learning more in whatever I do while also researching and seeking new ideas and skills. And for me to be resourceful, I need to think positive about most things and that will help me solve every issue
