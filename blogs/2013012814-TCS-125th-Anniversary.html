TC'S 125th Anniversary!

Rebecca Beck

January 28, 2013

Keep Private Please* I went to a meeting today about the 125th Kick Off event on Thursday. I wanted to let you know a little bit about the event. The event starts at 4:30 with a welcome saxophone performance. At 4:45 the college is hoping to gather all of the staff at TC and as many students as possible to sing 'Happy Birthday' to Teachers College and to join President Susan Fuhrman for birthday cake. I hope you can join us. From 4:30 - 7:30 there will music, food, drinks and various tables set up around the first floor of the college. These tables offer photo booths, raffles, and give-aways. I will be at a table promoting NLT and the EdLab tools. I will be encouraging people to sign up for NLT on our ipads. I will be handing out buttons and mints and answering any questions that people have. I am excited for the event, but I did want to invite anyone who is interested to join me for all or part of the kick off event. If you are interested in being at the table downstairs with me, please let me know! If you have any further questions, I will see if I can find some answers!
