Flying above the Rooftops

Carmen James

September 16, 2014

EdLab's intrepid Seen in New York (SiNY) team shared last week a video of the New York Trapeze school. Watching the video you feel the strain of the arm muscles, the woosh of air, the yelp of the beginner toppling to the net. In fact, watching the video reminded me of when I tried my hand on the trapeze at the very same school. . The video is also a reminder that there are countless corners where education is happening, formal schools and speciality schools, non-schools and even the city street.
