MOOC2Degree Initiative Raises Credit Profile

abagigah92

March 4, 2013

Moocs have become very popular recently as it continues to expand the opportunity of occurring knowledge; it is a great time in the Moocs world as they are widely being accepted for credits in colleges and university. Moocs are being offered by many universities in around the world and allows students all over the world to part take in different academic courses offered online. Last week, through an innovative effort, many public universities working with Academic Partnerships have opened up courses as Moocs. The courses being offered allows students to earn credit if enrolled as part of the full degree program. The approach these universities bring is to allow Moocs courses to be on the same platform and structure as courses offered in the universities. Randy Best, Founder and Chairman of Academic Partnerships, is very pleased about this set up and feels that this new initiative has elevated Moocs in a higher level. Moreover, the structure allows for higher rate of student completing the course. In additions, it allows a lower cost of attaining a degree and also gives those around the world who are unable to pay for higher education an opportunity to part take in this wonderful movement in the Moocs world
