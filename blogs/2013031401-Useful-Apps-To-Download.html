Useful Apps To Download

Daniel Vergara

March 14, 2013

I came across this article that discussed the 20 apps that people might find interesting and useful to have on their mobile devices. Out of the 20 apps I found, five particularly grabbed my attention. Two of these apps dealt with typing in a touch screen phone. I know many times, it can get annoying because you might misspell certain words because your fingers are too big or you just not use to typing on that screen. Well these two different apps were created to make typing on a touch screen mobile device way easier. One of the apps' main focus is to be able to do a spell check, where it will very accurately predict what you attempted to type even if the word is completely misspelled. The other app focuses more on being able to type faster and easier without having to lift your finger. Here is the list of the five apps that seem to have caught my attention the most:

  1. Haze
  2. SwiftKey
  3. CamMe
  4. Find.Eat.Drink.
  5. Flesky
