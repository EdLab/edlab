Non-Disruptive Technology in Education

gnatriello

October 6, 2006

I am not sure that we need more examples of technology being used to maintain the status quo, but here's another one in the form of COW or the "curriculum on wheels" developed by George Bush's brother Neil. The COW displays lively videos to aid in the memorization of facts useful in passing government-mandated tests. Where does one raise funds for such work? Where is the destructive power of capitalism when we need it?
