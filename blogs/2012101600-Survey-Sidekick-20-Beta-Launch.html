Survey Sidekick 2.0 Beta Launch

Manav Malhotra

October 16, 2012

The team of Janice, George, Demetri, Daniel, Jing, and me would like to invite you to the beta release of Survey Sidekick 2.0! We are going to be inviting researchers to the tool in the coming weeks as well. Getting a fresh set of eyes on the tool should help us test the features as well as some of our assumptions about the UI. It'll also allow us to check our ability to scale and fix bugs with limited impact. Thanks to Luke, Danielle, and Jennifer for their video work, Pranav and Yudan for dealing with our constant harassment, Joanna Fu for her help with the tutorials and design, and everyone in the lab for your brains throughout the design and development process. Play around with it and let us know what you think!
