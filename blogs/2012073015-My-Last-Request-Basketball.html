My Last Request: Basketball!?

Scott Streiner

July 30, 2012

Hey guys, So as you may already know, this week is my last week at EdLab (the summer flew by!). I'm doing the D&R meeting tomorrow to talk about what I did this summer, and to share my general EdLab experience with everyone. But the proper way for me to end this internship is with one final session of pick-up basketball at Riverside Park. So for those of you who are interested, I just wanted to give you a heads up! If a majority of you cannot play tomorrow, we could change it to Wednesday. Either way, basketball needs to happen this week, it's only fitting.
