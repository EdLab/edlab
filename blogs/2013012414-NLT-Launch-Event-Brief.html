NLT Launch Event Brief

bhughes

January 24, 2013

(private) NEWEST UPDATE: Just a note... when signing people up, their username must contain no spaces and their password must be at least 6 characters. (Otherwise there will just be an error.) UPDATE: newlearningtimes.com works! People can sign up on either URL, but this is the one to promote. Hi Everyone! Ready to show off NLT? I hope so! Here's what you need to know about our 4-5:30p event today: - At 3:30p, Aimee will be handing out iPod Touches to EdLab staff (20 of them, so please share). This is your informal calling card for the event (they're green!). Please use it to demonstrate NLT to visitors. NOTE: Please log into the iPods (or your own devices) using your own NLT account and stay logged in -- visitors should be directed to the iPads on the event tables to create an account. (This is particularly important because the nlt.tc.columbia.edu is the default on the iPads, and there is currently a bug on the newlearningtimes.com site that blocks the sign up process.) - At 3:55p, please read this blog post one last time for last-minute news (i.e., if newlearningtimes.com works for signing up, we want everyone to know! Otherwise you'll be coaching folks using their own devices to go to nlt.tc.columbia.edu) - Please use your knowledge of NLT to help our community understand why we think it's important... and help them sign up. This is the primary purpose of this event! Don't forget your best ideas! Please offer a mint to folks who actually sign up! They'll be by the iPads in limited numbers... - The secondary purpose of this event is to celebrate everyone's hard work on NLT. Just sayin'. Thanks for helping to spread the word everyone. Go NLT!
