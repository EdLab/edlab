Google Hits Pause on Glass

Kate Meersschaert

January 15, 2015

(Image: via blouinnews.com) Production of Google Glass has been halted. While the Google stresses that this is merely a "pause" in production while they focus on redesigning the wearable tech, however, some may SPECulate (couldn't resist!) that this is the end to what amounts to an innovative experiment. For more read this BBC piece. What are your thoughts? Would you consider this recent turn of events a failure on the part of Google? Or, is this an exciting sign of even more innovation to come? I tend to skew towards the latter and can't wait for the next evolution! Contacts?!
