The Democracy Kit

Pablo Oliva

April 25, 2007

Well, as you know we are Juan and Pablo, two high school apprentices at the EdLab, and this is the last project that we have done, it's about the “Democracy Kit.” The idea of this clip is to show what a Democracy Kit is and how it works. The Democracy Kit was basically used to show people in the street different parts of Spike Lee and Al Gore's documentary, and to catch their reactions to these short and shocking video clips. After we showed them the clips, the interviewers (us) asked the interviewees questions about democracy, how it was related to these clips, and what were their reactions to the images.
