Who Is Eligible To Know Your SSN?

Bismark Appiah

March 31, 2013

It is not every company or institute that we should provide our Social Security Number (SSN) to without thinking about the consequences. We all, as citizens and residents of this nation, know the importance of keeping our SSN private. The SSN, which every legal resident has, is the key to his or her personal kingdom. Every citizen's SSN pertains all the necessary information about that particular person. The worst that can happen is when hackers get hold of someone's SSN; they can use it to purchase a house, cars, or even commit a crime, which will go on records for a long period. This article from Yahoo focuses on five places people should never give out their SSN because it can cause a disaster in the end.
