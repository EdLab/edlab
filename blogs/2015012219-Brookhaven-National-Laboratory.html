Brookhaven National Laboratory

EdLab Studios

January 22, 2015


Funded primarily by the U.S. Department of Energy, Brookhaven National Laboratory is large-scale research facility comprised of nearly 3,000 scientists, engineers and support staff as well as over 4,000 visiting researchers per year. Sprawling over 5,321 acres in the middle of Long Island, its facilities are a unique educational resource for students across the island and in New York City, who have the opportunity to visit, take courses, and participate in research on site. A key goal of its educational programming is for exposure to the work of real scientists and contemporary research questions to inform and inspire students' own future careers. After all, there's nothing quite like learning about solar energy by touring the largest solar photovoltaic power plant in the Eastern United States with the experts who run it.
