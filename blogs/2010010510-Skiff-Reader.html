Skiff Reader

Ting Yuan

January 5, 2010

Skiff, an e-book reader developed by Hearst and Sprint, had its debut at CES-PC World yesterday (though the price has not been released yet). It is the thinnest e-book reader so far, which further adds to an increasing list of companies vying for a share of the digital book market:

  • Amazon Kindle
  • Sony Reader
  • Barnes & Noble Nook
  • Plastic Logic Que
  • Spring Design Alex
  • LG's solar eReader
