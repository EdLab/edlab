EdLounge Update: 4:00 today with a surprise!

Naoko Kiyosawa

August 3, 2007

We've got the Wii!! Reason for Wii hunting? That is for EdLounge! (and educational research, of course). Please visit the front of the EdLab this time at 4:00 p.m. not 3:00. Let's have fun! Do you know how hard it is to own a Wii? I remember last Christmas when it had hit the market. Since so many people still seek it, you might barely get it. Let me tell you about my experience purchasing the game console this morning for the EdLab. I got there at 9:00 am, but I was about the 50th customer. (=_=;) Then I had to wait for the cashiers for 1 hour. Here are some tips to get it. -Go to Nintendo World before 9:00 am on Friday, which is the date the store gets stock. -Bring a thick book so that you don't waste your time. -Don't bring any kids. They will be bored before you get it. The other floor opens at 10:00 am.
