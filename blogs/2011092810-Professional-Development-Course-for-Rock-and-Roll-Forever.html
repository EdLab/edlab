Professional Development Course for Rock & Roll Forever

Kate Meersschaert

September 28, 2011

Private! Nothing gets in the way of adopting/adapting a "Rock" curriculum like feeling out-of-the-loop or "old" when confronted with a roomful of tech-saavy, music-forward young people. I know firsthand, the benefit of having a readily available, efficient clearinghouse of resources at your fingertips so that you may get quickly get up-to-speed on a topic, in this case, rock music. To help facilitate this comfort level Jo and I are working on a course for Moodle that will help educators who are engaged with the Rock & Roll Forever Foundation curriculum, Rock & Roll: An American Story. The main goals of this course are to 1) as illustrated above, to help educators feel comfortable with the material 2) create a concise easy-to-use clearinghouse of resources, tools, articles etc., thoughts around potential issues, etc. 3) To provide a forum for discussion around the materials. Once we have the site and video assets finished or near completion, we will also create tutorials to help educators to use these resources (screencasts etc.). Please visit this Moodle course and provide feedback to us as comments on this blog post. Your thoughts are valuable as we near completion of all deliverables! Thanks!
