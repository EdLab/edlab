Disability website

Jessica Pang

April 17, 2008

I feel like I am flooding the blog space here. But let me just share one, no two more things. BBC has a website that focuses on disability. They have done a great job educating people in a humorous way. There are podcasts, blogs, even comics. It definitely gives us inspirations on how to tackle sensitive issues like this one. As I am looking at comics today, I found one that's about a fictional librarian named Rex Libris. Intro: "with the aid of an ancient god who lives beneath the library branch, Rex travels to the farthest reaches of the galaxy in search of overdue books. He must confront incredible foes, such as powerful alien warlords who refuse to pay their late fees" Click here for the first nine page of the comics on how Rex defeats the undead sammuri who wants to borrow but don't have a library card.
