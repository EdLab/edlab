simple data mining technique to detect user behaviors in online discussion forums

Sharon Hsiao

July 7, 2011

"Modeling students' activity in online discussion forum: a strategy based on time series and agglomerative hierarchical clustering" 2011 EDM. This paper presents a strategy based on characterizing students' activity in time series and grouping similar students through an agglomerative hierarchical clustering algorithm. Time series allow representing different kinds of activity, such as writing and reading. Authors use dendrograms to display the data by exploring the hierarchical tree structure and show robust natural clusters. I think the method of clustering is easy and worth of doing quick-and-dirty groupings for understanding latent communities in the forum or any online discussion places. Maybe we can replicate the same method for analyzing Vialogues. Vialogues, in a sense is like an online discussion forum, but not quite. There are various moderating conditions. It might be interesting to see the dendrograms, whether we'd find the structures or not.
