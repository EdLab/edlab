UFR Additional Resource: The Secret Millionaires Club

Nantwi

November 10, 2010

The Secret Millionaires Club is a web based animation series that teaches young people valuable financial lessons. The series features an animated Warren Buffet as a mentor to a group of children aspiring to create their own businesses. He advises them on everything from the need to save and the importance of investing. Buffet bought his first stock at 13 and is clearly the right person to advice children about their finances. The show is aimed primarily at teaching young people basic financial skills and as such it serves as an additional resource for the Understanding Fiscal Responsibility (UFR) project. The animation in the Secret Millionaires Club could also serve as an added inspiration as we think of video resources to accompany promotional efforts around UFR and especially in light of Gary's blog post about the ease with which we can create animated videos.
