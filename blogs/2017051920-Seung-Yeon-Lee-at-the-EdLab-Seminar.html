Seung Yeon Lee at the EdLab Seminar

EdLab Studios

May 19, 2017


Seung Yeon Lee joined the EdLab Seminar in March 2017 to talk about her recent work and study at UC Berkeley in psychometric models and its relevance to the education sector. She hopes that her work can help improve lectures and customize lessons in its application and formative assessment.

Watch the full seminar on Vialogues.
