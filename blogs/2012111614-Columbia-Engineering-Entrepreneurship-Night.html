Columbia Engineering Entrepreneurship Night!

Kate Meersschaert

November 16, 2012

Hi Everyone! Please join me for this exciting Entrepreneurship Night hosted by Columbia's Engineering Department! Follow this EventBrite link for more details and to register (there is a fee, so please clear this with Hui Soo first!)... to get a student discount be sure to select Teachers College and add your UNI at the end of the registration process! This should be a blockbuster event... full of both discussion re: "the Alley" vs "Valley", demos from up & coming startups and opportunities to learn more from one of Steve Jobs advisers... Bill Campbell!

Join Columbia Engineering and the Columbia Alumni Association (CAA) for Columbia Engineering Entrepreneurship Night. Arguably among the most influential technology entrepreneurs over the last decade, Bill Campbell '62CC, '64TC and Ben Horowitz '88CC will discuss the new NYC tech boom, how it is driving the East Coast startup ecosystem, and how NYC entrepreneurship is giving Silicon Valley a run for their money. Click here to see bios of our two speakers. Immediately following the keynote program, stick around for technology demos by some of the brightest young Columbia entrepreneurs on the East Coast.
Hope you can join me and help document the evening for our vialogues collection and live blog/tweets to support future scholartship around entrepreneurship for mSchool & LaunchPad! #EdLabRepresents!
