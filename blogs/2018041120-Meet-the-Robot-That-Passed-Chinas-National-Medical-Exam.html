Meet the Robot That Passed China’s National Medical Exam

Ryan.Allen

April 11, 2018


Researchers from Tsinghua University in China built an artificial intelligence (AI)-powered robot that successfully passed the Chinese national medical licensing examination. The team of researchers programmed Xiaoyi with textbooks and other resources in preparation for the exam. She scored a 456 out of 600, 96 points higher than the passing limit.

These robots are not meant to completely replace human doctors. Instead, the researchers hope that Xiaoyis can be used in hospitals and clinics as additional resources for medical professionals. The team also contends that with the help of AI, diagnoses can become more efficient and accurate.
