BusinessWeek's <i>The 10 Commandments of Web Design</i>

Frank Webster

June 30, 2008

The 10 Commandments of Web Design BusinessWeek brought together a panel of web designers to winkle out the rules of the moment for good web design and nominate sites as the best and worse examples. I was impressed with the article's emphasis on the transitory nautre of such rules. Flash fans will be happy to know that its use has been mitigated. Flash once led the list of Web Pages That Suck. I was also impressed that in three cases, the same web site was nominated as both The Best and The Worst. So much for the judges -- how did America vote? More entertaining, topical and even more opinionated is Web Pages That Suck: Learn Good Design by Looking at Bad Design. Quite a few university web sites make WPTS's list of nonprofits.
