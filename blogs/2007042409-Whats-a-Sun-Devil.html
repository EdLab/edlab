What's a Sun Devil?

HSC

April 24, 2007

Professor Natriello's trip to Arizona State University (ASU) next week motivated me to do some research on the institution. Here is a list of some of the more interesting initiatives/activities: A New American University: Specifically, read about their Design Imperatives. The ASU Decision Theater: Watch how the the Decision Theater is being used to plan the city of Phoenix or an example Decision Theater demonstration about possible solar energy sources in Arizona on this page The Innovation Space: View their model of innovation. KAET-TV: Check out Horizonte, a news program that helps educate audiences about issues of particular interest to the Latino community. Virginia G. Piper Center for Creative Writing: View their Distinguished Visiting Writers Series. For those of us who work in the EdLab, these activities should sound quite familiar. It would be great to invite some of the individuals behind these groups to the EdLab Seminar. BTW, a Sun Devil is the mascot for ASU.
