Glimpses of the Learning Theater - Learning Analytics Orientation

gnatriello

December 27, 2016

Early in the fall 2016 semester The Learning Theater was the site of the Learning Analytics Program orientation session.  The goal was to introduce the learning analytics students to the Learning Theater as a site for research.  We concentrated activities in the north and central sections of the theater and used the south sections for equipment and control consoles.   [caption id="attachment_146" align="alignnone" width="602"] The north side of the theater was set up for food and mingling space.[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_148" align="alignnone" width="591"] The large display was used to illustrate the data tracking capabilities available in the Learning Theater.[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_147" align="alignnone" width="587"] The center bays were configured for student work groups.[/caption]
