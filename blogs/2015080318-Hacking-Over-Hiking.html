Hacking Over Hiking?

idrissa10

August 3, 2015

When one thinks of camp, they think of trees, large mountains, and annoying mosquitos. The increase use of technology in our everyday lives has led to a host of camps dedicated to hacking. One such camp is Gen Cyber Camp. The camp is free of charge and it is run by the National Security Agency (NSA). The goal of the N.S.A is to generate interest in computers and educate students on hacking. Additionally, the camp is also a recruitment tool for the N.S.A as it provides them with access to future hackers. The program houses about 1,400 students and half of them are females. There are 43 camps so far. The camp wants to expand to 200 by 2020. Check out the story and tell me what you guys think of the camp!
