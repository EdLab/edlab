Launching In 3...2...1...Action

Elysee Dougan

January 9, 2014

Some inventions usually wow people: the first car in the late 1800s, the Wright Brothers and the airplane in the early 1900s and the first man on the moon in the mid 1900s. All these ideas were deemed impossible because it never crossed a lot of peoples mind. Now in the 2000's, we've combined brains with technology to probe Mars, and that idea is being taken a step further. Recently, a non-profit team from Netherlands created an awesome project named Mars One. The idea of the project is to send four humans to Mars to spend the rest of their life there by 2023-25. Every two years after, another group will be launched creating a civilization on the surface of Mars. This project will be filmed and documented as a reality show before launching. The money from the show will be reinvested into the project. One of the project members claim the show will be more popular than Big Brother. This project, if successful, will definitely wow me.
