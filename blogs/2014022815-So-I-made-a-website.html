So I made a website...

Dana Haugh

February 28, 2014

Hi all! For one of my classes last semester, I was given the task of creating a library-related website. Since I noticed that many of our students and faculty have trouble locating (every)thing on our site, I decided to make a "Student's Guide to the Gottesman Libraries" website that showcases all of the most requested links. It's a pretty simple site (I used Dreamweaver and Photoshop!) but I think it gets the job done. Let me know what you think/ways to improve/general feedback! It can be accessed here.
