Hope for the Subway System?

gnatriello

July 1, 2017



Will the 1 billion additional dollars promised by Governor Cuomo change life for subway riders anytime soon? According to this piece in Wired the outlook is not promising. There are various explanations for the plight of the subway from insufficient funding to poor organization, but perhaps the most provocative one is lack of vision.  What is your vision for the future of the subway system or the future of transportation in the region more generally? Can you imagine something as transformative for our time as the original opening of the subway was for New Yorkers in 1904?
