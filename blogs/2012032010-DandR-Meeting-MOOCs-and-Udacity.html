D&R Meeting: MOOCs & Udacity

LauraCostello

March 20, 2012

(please keep private) Link to Presentation Thanks for participating in our intensive exploration of Udacity! To draw connections to developing products like mSchool and EDais, please answer a question or questions below: What do see as the strengths or weaknesses of the Udacity platform and content? Have MOOC's and courses made platforms like moodle and blackboard redundant? Are you familiar with any similar platforms? How do they compare in terms of community building, business model, and content? How does the Udacity model build community? How can we apply strategies from this platform to projects like mSchool and Edais? Compare the business models and advertising for an MOOC, like Udacity, and an MOC like Straighterline. What lessons can we draw for a free or freemium site or features? How important is the content or “celebrity factor” of a MOOC or MOC? How can we replicate the hype in an mSchool setting?
