Positive Economics

abagigah92

October 28, 2014

Positive economics is a division of economics that sort to describe what is happening in reality. This type of economics focuses on the facts and cause relationship in an economical behavior. Positive economic statements predicts how people behave and make decision. Positive economics stands in contrast to normative economics, which uses value judgments.Positive Coase theorem states that parties negotiates towards socially desirable outcome if transactions are low, people in disputes are able to bargain towards mutually beneficial solution.Positive Hobbes theorem is based on the books "Leviathan" by Thomas Hobbes. "Leviathan" explains that people are adversarial and require the government to prevent destructive disputes. Positive Hobbes Theorem states that people are not able to reach bargains leading to mutually beneficial outcomes.
