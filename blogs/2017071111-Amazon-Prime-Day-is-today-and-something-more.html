Amazon Prime Day is today and something more

ryanwu

July 11, 2017


July 16, 2017, is important to me, not only because it’s the Game of Thrones Season 7 release, but also it is my two year anniversary at EdLab.


And I know it will be very different for me in my third year because Lingxiao will be leaving us for a great future. She has been with me every day that I have been here at the Lab and it won't be the same without her here. 




Lingxiao joined EdLab in 2015 as a QA tester. I was fortunate to see her grow over the years into an even stronger developer. She has always been a valuable member of our team. 


Most of us who have worked with Lingxiao have found her to be a very professional and hard-working, but also very personable. 

2015 - Rhizr


2016 - Learning Theater Website


2016-17 - The New EdLab Website



Lingxiao's contributions have greatly benefitted this organization and, undeniably, she will be deeply missed here. It is definitely our loss and someone else’s gain. 


Please join us this Friday in wishing Lingxiao the very best of luck and the greatest of success in all her future endeavors. 


Time: July 14, 2017, 1:30 PM
