New Platform Takes Us One Step Closer to a Paperless Classroom

Jenny Abamu

August 21, 2016


With big textbook designers moving from paper to tablets, the idea of a paperless classroom is becoming a reality. A new platform called DocentEDU is pushing that growth forward with their app and browser extension, challenging the traditional paper worksheet.

Pros:

DocentEDU allows instructors to change any ordinary online text into an interactive learning experience. Through the platform, teachers can add comprehension, open-ended, or multiple choice questions to any document, transforming it into an interactive article that students can access and respond to from the comfort of their own devices. In addition, teachers can insert Youtube videos or images into documents, changing static text into a multimedia experience. Though users primarily seem to focus product application on literacy comprehension assignments, the platform can be used for science, math, and virtually any topic on the internet.

Product installation is simple and requires only a few clicks to get started. Assignment customization is versatile and allows tasks to be created weeks in advance or an hour before class starts. Though the app is not free, the cost of a subscription promises to be cheaper than the cost of printing worksheets.

Cons:

The product is not exactly intuitive and therefore might require some training for teachers and students before they can fully implement it into daily lessons. Also, without a relatively fast internet connection, the lag that students and teachers might experience when accessing or creating tasks can be discouraging.

Our Takeaway:

DocentEDU is a powerful addition to the 21st century classroom. Both its user friendly interface and versatile design simplify and expand teaching opportunities around the range of content available online. This platform promises to be something that enhances literacy assignments and online educational experiences. For those looking to do away with endless worksheets, this could be the answer.
