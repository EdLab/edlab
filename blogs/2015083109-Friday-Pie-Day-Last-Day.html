Friday, Pie Day, Last Day

Meredith Powers

August 31, 2015

Hey EdLab, It's that time again—another end of summer, another goodbye blog post. Though I've only been here a little over a year, I've learned so much already and I've enjoyed my time here immensely. Thank you all for being such wonderful colleagues and friends—you've all made coming in every day so enjoyable. I'm excited to start a new chapter at Brooklyn Public Library, but I'm also going to miss all of you! Thanks for letting me be a part of EdLab for the past year. I'm no coffee whiz like Beckie, so you're on your own for your morning dose of caffeine, but I'll definitely bring in a final pie (or two!) so we can all share a sweet treat together on Friday. Please stay in touch! I'm not going to the ends of the earth—just Brooklyn. And now I don't want to say goodbye... so let's just say so long.
