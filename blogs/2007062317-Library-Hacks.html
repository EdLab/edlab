Library Hacks

Anthony Cocciolo

June 23, 2007

I came across some fun library stuff from Life Hacker. One particular cool tool is called Book Burro, which comes up when you are looking to buy a book. When looing at a book, it shows the local libraries it is at, as well as how much it costs from various retailers. For example, the print screen shows a book that is available at CU, NYU, and the prices at various retailers.
