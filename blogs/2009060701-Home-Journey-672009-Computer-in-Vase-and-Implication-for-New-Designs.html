Home Journey 6.7.2009: Computer in Vase and Implication for New Designs

Ting Yuan

June 7, 2009

I run into ESC's new design about putting computer devices into a Chinese blue-flower porcelain work. This innovative and beautiful idea drives me to think about the bronze exhibitions I have viewed at Shanghai Museum, the most famous art institute for bronze collection in China: Is it possible that in the future we will see computers having our personalized formats, e.g., porcelain, bronze, our own designs? Courtesy to Shanghai Museum Wine Container, 1100-1300 BC Wine Container, 1100-1300 BC Wine Container, 1100 BC My next question is: Regarding IP ownership, who will own the IP rights of design if we use old porcelain/bronze works or some others' works to design such kind of "new" computer or new electronic devices?
