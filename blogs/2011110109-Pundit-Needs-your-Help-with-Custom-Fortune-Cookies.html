Pundit Needs your Help... with Custom Fortune Cookies!

Kate Meersschaert

November 1, 2011

Private! Hi Team EdLab! Pundit needs your help! He is trying to think of witty, effective messages to have printed for custom fortune cookies. It is his hope that these cookies with Pundit-themed messages & the Pundit URL will inspire potential users to visit the site and learn more about his magical powers through this unconventional, disruptive & yummy promotional effort. Who doesn't take the time to read their fortune cookie message? Pundit Needs: 1) your wittiest, most catchy and under word messages to include, see my attempts listed below & feel-free to modify & offer feedback on these! 2) Any inspiration re: where these cookies could be placed & handed-out to best promote Pundit. The overall goal of this promo is to raise awareness for, generate interest in and most of all get people to engage with and USE the tool! Current messages... we get 15 messages, each message can be up-to 3 lines and we are limited to 37 characters per line for up to 111 characters including spaces (the URL should be able to fit on the back with the logo - Bitly?). 1) A fulfilling class is in your future. But first, you have to visit a greenish blue creature. 2) Your future is looking more course-full. 3) For yummy course recommendations enjoy Pundit. 4) Your future is full of possibility - if you choose the right courses! 5) Don't underestimate the power of intelligent course search & recommendation! 6) An amazing schedule of courses is closer than you think! 7) Make true course-search love happen! 8) Your course-related dreams will come true! 9) I see beautiful courses in your future! 10) Life without intelligent course search and recommendation… is boring! 11) A mysterious blue, one-eyed creature is looking-out for your academic future! 12) You will meet a mysterious blue creature… don't worry he just wants to help you find amazing courses! Current Ideas for placement of cookies... -Bowl in student services -Distribute strategically in cafeteria... oops my fortune cookies dropped on the table! -During promotional events for Ednode/Pundit we will have bowls -Replace the lollipops in the bowl featured at the entrance to EdLab for guests
