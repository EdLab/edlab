July 14, 2014

***keep private*** After deploying around noon on Friday, here are some stats on the July missive. For context, I've also included stats for the Vialogues Version 1.2 Release message we sent back in April after the big upgrade, as well as last week's NLT Newsletter. Stats are from equivalent time periods, from send to +3 days. NLT newsletter subscription is currently right around 1900, and Vialogues is around 14000. Plenty to think about for our newslettercraft in general, so we'll see if we can't apply some lessons learned in August. Thank you to everyone who helps put these letters together, whether in content or in execution. I believe these stats show your efforts are not in vain. ***Data corrected 7/15, 8:35 AM*** Apologies for breaking links for a few hours, but I found some errors that needed correction.
