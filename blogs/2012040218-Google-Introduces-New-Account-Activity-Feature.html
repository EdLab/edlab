Google Introduces New Account Activity Feature

Kate Meersschaert

April 2, 2012

Google has made analytics more personal... Google now offers users the option to opt-into an "Account Activity" feature that provides "at-a-glance" tracking of your behavior and settings across Google apps. Data is tracked by date in a scaled-down "Google-analytics-like" dashboard and includes most used browsers, your top email contacts and mini bar-graphs that represent spikes in email activity by day. Once you opt-in Google will send you a monthly email-based summary of your personal data. Read this blog post from Google for more. EdLab Relevance As we think about new ways to visualize data across our current and future projects and apps it is helpful to explore innovative, simple ways that personal and site- based information is represented. Now that we are all our own unique " start-ups of one" shouldn't we begin to look more closely at our own flows of data.
