Millions of American Children Sleep on the Floor

abagigah92

December 3, 2012

Sleep is one of the most important things to mankind as we humans spend one third of their lives sleeping, a clear evidence of the importance of sleep. While surfing the net, I came across an article discussing the issue of foster children and sleep in the United States. Foster children in this country are invisible and about 1.8 million children nationwide either sleep on the floor or sleep on a crowded bed with their siblings. Deprived of many privileges with absolutely no blames; these children are often sent out to shelters or with grandparents who cannot provide beds for them. Moreover, with charity groups and shelters running out of money, finding a place to sleep or having a bed is hard to find for these children. The article serves as awareness to reach to readers about local programs that are in support in raising funds to get millions of children in the United States comfortable beds to sleep. By donating to non-profit organizers and local programs on this issue, these children can have comfort and affection of warm bed they can sleep in with love and care.
