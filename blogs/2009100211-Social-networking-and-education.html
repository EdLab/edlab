Social networking and education

Ellen Qian

October 2, 2009

K12 It's really outstanding and provide a great platform for parents and children. Xiaonei The first version of this website is exactly the same as Facebook with the only difference being the language. But now,everyone in China uses this website instead of Facebook because there are too much annoying applications on Facebook. Furthermore, the game "happy farm", which makes it possible for users to run a farm and steal vegetables and eggs from other people's farm, is very popular on the site. Myspace I've heard that more people use MySpace than Facebook.I like MySpace better because it has less advertisement and better GUI. Blog Blogs are becoming very popular among large segments of society. Some people have even gotten famous because of their blogs but social network systems are better than blogs mainly because,in my mind, they provide a platform to people who want to contact their old friends and classmates and know what they are doing now. Facebook Too many applications and functions is sometimes not a good thing. However,this website is the most popular social networking website in the world. So just get an account and wait for your friends to add you:). Combining social network with an educational platform is an exciting prospect.Imagination is infinite while technology is just a method to implement it.
