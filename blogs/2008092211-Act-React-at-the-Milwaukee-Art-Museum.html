Act React at the Milwaukee Art Museum

Stephanie Hunt

September 22, 2008

I'm so excited to see this new interactive art exhibition, Act React, coming to the Milwaukee Art Museum. Curated by George Fifield, the exhibition features work by some of the "flagship" artists working with technology. Camille Utterback, Scott Snibbe, and Janet Cardiff are showing work, but I think I'm most excited about Brian Knep's piece, Healing Pool. He's patching together six ceiling-mounted projectors and three cameras to create a living floor. As you walk across the surface, rips open and reconnect in a continuous healing cycle. Oh, and the size of the interactive surface?: 20 x 30 feet. Act React opens October 4, 2008 - definitely check it out if you find yourself in Milwaukee!
