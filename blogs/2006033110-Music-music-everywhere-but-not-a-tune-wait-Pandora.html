Music music everywhere, but not a tune ... wait, Pandora!

Anna Beard

March 31, 2006

Do you love Coldplay?  The Beatles?  Cibo Matto?  Bach?

Do you repeatedly listen to the same 50 songs of the 3000 mp3s currently on your computer without a second thought?

It's time to branch out and find that song, like "The Speed of Sound", "Help!", "SciFi Wasabi", and "Cello Suite in C", but NOT!

Try Pandora (http://www.pandora.com).  This is a site where you can type in the artists and songs you love and it will compile a playlist for you of the same and similar stuff suited to your individual music tastes.

Enjoy marching to the beat of a similar sounding, yet new drum!
