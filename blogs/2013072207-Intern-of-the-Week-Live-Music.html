Intern of the Week Live Music

Haymar Lim

July 22, 2013

First of all, congratulations to last week's intern of the week Brian. You worked hard and deserved the celebration we had on Friday. Speaking of the celebration we had on Friday, there was something that was brought to my attention. I was informed by George that there are musicians in the EdLab. Now, as cool as the video Kate brought in for the celebration was, how much cooler would it be if we had live music played by people at the EdLab? I know we like to emphasize STEM here, but let's remember that the arts are really important too! I know being first is hard and it would be rude for me to just throw the idea out there without contributing, so I volunteer myself for this Friday. I'll bring in my guitar and sing a song or two for you. The following week, hopefully one if our other musicians will keep it up. Any objections?
