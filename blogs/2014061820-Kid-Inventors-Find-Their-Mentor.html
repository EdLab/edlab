Kid Inventors Find Their Mentor

jvazquez

June 18, 2014


Bugs Tan is a decorated inventor, having won the National Innovation Award of his native Malaysia in 2007. Brimming with creative wisdom, his latest venture is quite possibly his most meaningful yet. Uncle Bugs, as he is more familiarly known, wants to help Malaysian children think more holistically by strengthening their right-brain capacities. He created an "academy" (more accurately, an after school program) that enables them to let loose their inner Thomas Edisons.

Packed with brainstorming sheets, 3D printers for prototyping, and even IP lawyers, Uncle Bugs Inventor Academy is no joke. In fact, Uncle Bugs may come off goofy, but he is completely serious about his students' educations.

What wacky inventions could Uncle Bugs have helped your childhood self bring to market? Automatic bed-maker? Squirt gun machine gun? Invisible ink that actually worked? Tell us in the comments of the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
