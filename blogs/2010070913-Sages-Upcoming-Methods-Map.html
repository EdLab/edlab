Sage's Upcoming Methods Map

Ting Yuan

July 9, 2010

SAGE recently reported its upcoming SAGE Research Methods Online, which is a new development about SAGE's "Methods Map". It is a visual search "map" linking different methods and authors, and is actually connected to over 100,000 pages of content of SAGE books and journals. It shows relationships between methods and further guides users to related content to assist with searches.
