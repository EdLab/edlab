Games for Learning Summit

Youssef Ballo

May 28, 2015

“Video games are essentially complex systems that students can learn to navigate very quickly”. Since the beginning of the 21st century, the educational community has required something different from the traditional model. The Games for Learning Summit is a one day event during the 12th annual Games for Change Festival. This event allows educators and policymakers to learn strategies for developing video games for education and how they can connect devices in the classroom. This project also helped student develop media literacy, soft skills like collaboration, and technical skills.
