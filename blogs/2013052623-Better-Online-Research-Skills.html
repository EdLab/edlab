Better Online Research Skills

abagigah92

May 26, 2013

Teaching students better online research skills is a new project by an elementary school teacher, Sara Shaw. Shaw, a fifth grade teacher, has seen many flaws and errors and misused or misunderstood research by students. This is mainly due to students trusting their Google search results without going the extra steps of verifying the credibility of that source. The internet provides students with loads of information but many do not have the digital literacy skills to go through the information. Moreover, a lot of online information is opinions that students usually mistake as facts. Shaw is working on a project that will help students clarify between good and bad sources. The project will also teach processes to determine site credibility in order to trust the information that site is sharing. In order to be a savvy researcher, students should check for credibility, authenticity, using the right search engine, using updates information and understanding the background of what they are researching.
