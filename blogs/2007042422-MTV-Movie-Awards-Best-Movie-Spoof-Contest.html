MTV Movie Awards "Best Movie Spoof" Contest

HSC

April 24, 2007

MTV is sponsoring a contest for aspiring film-makers. The contest allows individuals to submit an original movie short parodying selected films from the past year. Does anyone in the EdLab have an interest in creating/submitting a five-minute clip? The final day to submit is May 21st. The five finalists will be flown to Los Angeles to attend the 2007 MTV Movie Awards and will receive a point and shoot video camera. The Winner will be invited on stage to accept the first-ever "Best Movie Spoof" Award during the show's live broadcast on Sunday, June 3rd.
