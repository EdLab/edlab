The TC Art Collections

ZKL

May 22, 2019

Today for the Design Team update Seminar, one set of teams will be creating a unique exhibit for the Offit Gallery on the 3rd floor from the Teachers College Art Collections.


The Collections to draw from will be:

  • The Castellon Collection﻿﻿
  • The Dow Collection
  • The Ziegfeld Collection
  • The TC Historical Portraits


Each exhibit of archival art will have a theme, a comprehensive layout and customized features to allow the best experience for patrons of the Gottesman Libraries.
