NYTs Article... Possible Ideas for our Learning Discussion Platform.

Kate Meersschaert

January 11, 2012

Private! In light of a discussion tool in the works by Megha, I thought I would share this NYT article/debate with the team... In addition to important thoughts on the future of technology in the classroom, this piece contains a few potentially interesting ways to structure an online discussion. For example, there is a text-based introduction to the debate topic created by the moderator, a list of the debate participants with both their picture, position and a sample point made in the debate. If you click through to the next page, along with the article, there is a classic linear discussion embedded below. Although the discussion interface seems rudimentary, and overly vertical/linear (see screenshot below), the tab system could be interesting to explore. These tabs are set-up as, "All, NYT Pick" and "Reader Pick." It would be interesting to explore this structure for our platform and modify these tabs to include, "Instructor Pick, Student Pick" etc.
