WDYL?

Alex Park

June 28, 2011

WDYL? Oh, I'm sorry. You're not cool enough to know what that stands for. (joking!) WDYL stands for What do you love? It is a new site-service-Idon'tknowwhatitis that Google released recently. My first thoughts: Interesting. Google's decided to show off its products sort of all iat once with WDYL. At the same time, what real useful purpose does this serve? Yes, it combines a lot of Google's services to show you an aggregated search result, but I found it quite fickle and not practical to use. Verdict? I think Google had way too many bored developers and told them to make something that was marginally cool. And this is it. Side note: It seems that we (collectively) have broken the blog interface. Good job, soldiers.
