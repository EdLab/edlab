Communicating as a Leader

Oumar Soumahoro

July 19, 2015

I am almost sure we all have at one point had to take on a leadership role in some capacity. Being a leader at any level, whether it’s a JV basketball team captain or CEO of a Fortune 500 company requires an ability to effectively communicate. An ability to effectively communicate can resolve many issues almost immediately and also prevent it from exacerbating into something far worse. For instance, a CEO’s ability to communicate with his stockholders, the press and general public during a major crisis could ultimately determine the very future of that company. Forbes, as ever, advice for leaders on how to “secretly” communicate with their employees. What is your communication “secrets” when in a leadership role?
