Ideas for Let Freedom Swing Marketing Campaign

HSC

October 13, 2010

The EdLab (specifically Patrick, Gonzalo, Chingfu, George, Eric, Joann, Eric, and Brian) recently launched the Jazz at Lincoln Center and involved the Documentary Group (the actual videos), the TC Social Studies Department (i.e., curriculum development) and Teachers College Press (i.e., printing/distribution). In your groups, please come up with at least 10 ideas for marketing the Let Freedom Swing Project. The target audience is high school teachers and/or individuals in other educational institutions. At least five of the ideas should be actionable immediately with current EdLab staff.
