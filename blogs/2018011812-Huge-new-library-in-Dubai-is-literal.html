Huge new library in Dubai is literal

ZKL

January 18, 2018

Happened to notice this short article on the new library being constructed in Dubai.

The form is based on an open book resting on a Rehl (a traditional lectern for holding the Qur'an).

It will house over four million books and have interaction zones, but so far no theater :).
