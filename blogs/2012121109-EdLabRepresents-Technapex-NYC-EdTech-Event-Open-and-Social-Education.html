#EdLabRepresents @ Technapex NYC EdTech Event: Open & Social Education

Kate Meersschaert

December 11, 2012

Tweets about "#edlabrepresents" Tweets about "#edlabrepresents @EdLabTC" Last night Janice, Laura, Laura, Demetri and I traveled downtown to @WixLounge for a night of #edtech discussion and networking! The event was hosted by TriplePoint PR and their edtech-focused blog, Technapex. Takeaways... -The "photo" booth upon check-in was great! I just wish that they sent you the pics as a fun souvenir instead of using them as a backdrop before the speakers began. The costumes were brilliant! -Sign-in was easy, with pre-printed nametags and an analogue list (iPad sign-in is often too slow!). -The moderator was masterful, fast and directive. The audience clearly appreciated her skills! -The panel was an interesting mix ranging-from EdLab EdTech Take Alum Nihal P. to Eric Ma an engineer from Skillshare. -The WixLounge space was awkward for a panel discussion, as there was a sizable amount of wasted space near the entrance and everyone was crowded-into a space in the back to watch & listen to the panel. -There were probably about 70-100 people there... however, the event invites went-out over a month ago and attendees received multiple reminders... I was surprised that their weren't more people in attendance, but it was the perfect # for the tight space! -Overall a great experience! I just wish I could have stayed later for the rest of the panel! Any thoughts from my #edlabrepresents crew?!
