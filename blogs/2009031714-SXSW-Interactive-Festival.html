SXSW Interactive Festival

Adam Mitchinson

March 17, 2009

This past weekend was the SXSW (South by Southwest) Interactive Festival which “celebrates the creativity and passion behind the coolest new media technologies”. As always at these types of things there are some really great ideas on show. The BBC have some great coverage of some of the festival highlights ranging from the more traditional, including a demonstration of the Microsoft Surface (following the news that the next generation is in the works), to the more obscure, introducing the Hat Game new craze hitting the streets, billed as reinvigorating the classic game of tag by adding a technological twist! Check it out, get inspired!!
