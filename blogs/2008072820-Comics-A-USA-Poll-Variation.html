Comics: A U.S.A. Poll Variation?

Ian Toledo

July 28, 2008

According to this New York Times article Senator Barack Obama and Senator John McCain will have biographies made about them in comic book form for the public to read. Here's a CBR article that gives a more thorough behind the scenes look. According to Hui Soo's post about comics it has been proven that they can lure great athletes to certain universities. In my previous post an NBA player is using the medium to encourage children to read. Will these President comics swing the vote? Or is it just a ploy to take our money? Or both? Since I bought the Pope John Paul II comic back in the 80s, I guess I should get these. But what I really want is a Randy Pausch comic. R.I.P.
