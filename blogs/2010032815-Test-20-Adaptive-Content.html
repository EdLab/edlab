Test 2.0 - Adaptive Content

gnatriello

March 28, 2010

The tools currently available through e-books are just scratching the surface of what will be possible as the format continues to evolve along with software and hardware. As a case in point, consider Elliot Van Buskirk's account of Text 2.0, a system that uses eye-tracking software and an e-book with a reader-facing camera to capture eye movements and adapt the presentation of the text to reader behavior. For a quick overview of the system, take a look at this video presentation.
