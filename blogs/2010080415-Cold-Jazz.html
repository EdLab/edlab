Cold Jazz

Kwahiwi Edwards

August 4, 2010

While researching additional online resources for the Let Freedom Swing project, I came across this paper on a jazz tour that consisted of Dizzy Gillespie, Benny Goodman, Louis Armstrong and Duke Ellington during the Cold War. The tour was meant to influence democracy and weaken the spread of communism during the Cold War. The tour was month-long and took the music legends to the United Kingdom, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Sweden, and Denmark.
