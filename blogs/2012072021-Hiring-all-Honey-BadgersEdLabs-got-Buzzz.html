Hiring all Honey Badgers...EdLab's got Buzzz!

Michelle Lee

July 20, 2012

Calling all passionate coders and technologists - EdLab is hiring software developers and solutions architects! In the spirit of describing the right spirit and "can do" attitude that we're looking for, I came across a hilarious but insightful article on Forbes that described one startup's hiring approach to find folks with the same chutzpah as the infamous Honey Badger as narrated by "Randall" - an Internet meme that made the rounds a year and a half ago. While I'm not sure I want newly hired EdLabbers to start biting off snake heads in anticipation of launching their products, I do agree that we're looking for folks with the courage and perseverance to develop insanely great educational technologies. What "animal" do you think represents EdLab's culture or the current education system? What do you think of the honey badger?
