The EdLab Elevator... Speech

Kate Meersschaert

June 21, 2011

One of our elevators may be broken... but, our EdLab elevator "speech" can be amazing! Many of us have talked about the importance of having an "elevator speech" ready for events, meet-ups and conferences that we attend on behalf of the lab, in this spirit, let's create one together! Or at least, get the dialogue rolling in the direction of how we can best describe the work we do at EdLAb to strangers and even fellow TC'ers. Perhaps rehearse a few versions of your "speech" based on the following variables: 1) Context - is the event more tech, educational or a VC-focused? 2) Audience - is the listener informed about the issues we focus on at the lab? Deeply invested in education? New media? 3) Time - do you have 10 minutes or 30 seconds.
