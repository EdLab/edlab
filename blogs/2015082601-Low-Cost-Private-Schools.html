Low Cost Private Schools

abagigah92

August 26, 2015

There is a growing trend of the private sector playing a major role in education in schools, especially in countries with poor education systems. This will help develop and prepare students for the work force, which in turn will help develop the economy. In major cities such as Lagos, Nigeria, the government fails to provide proper free public education. This is one of the major reasons why private schools are bigger and better at the primary level, especially in countries with weak public education systems. The Ken Ade Private School is one of the leading private schools offering low-cost education to students in Lagos. The fee per term is around $35 dollars. The private companies, with their financial power, can help improve education by continuing to open small low cost schools. This trend is growing in Nigeria and it should be adopted by private companies around the world looking to provide better education for the youth.
