Palin did not specify a list of books to ban

Gus Andrews

September 8, 2008

Because I've seen a number of people repeating rumors about Sarah Palin providing a list of books to ban, I just wanted to pass along the message that while Palin did suggest to the Wasilla librarian that she might be asked to ban books, she did NOT provide the list which has been circulating on the Internet. In fact, a number of the books on the list being sent around hadn't even been published when Palin allegedly made the request. No matter how we feel, political affiliations should not get in the way of the facts. I just don't want to see anyone else forwarding rumors and muddying the truth.
