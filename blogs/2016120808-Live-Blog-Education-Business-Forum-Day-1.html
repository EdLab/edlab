Live Blog: Education Business Forum Day 1

HSC

December 8, 2016

George and I will be live blogging at the Education Business Forum today. Jenny has requested the following: for the education business conference it would be nice to know some stats on the edtech field: -Country leading in edtech development - market saturation - percent of companies that fail - percent of companies that go beyond 5 years - typical developer mistakes - how do they decide what to count as edtech and what does not? Also some ethical questions of access and profit balances, as this is normally a social-entrepreneurship venture Dino added: More on a personal note, I am curious about the edtech funding in NYC v. Silicon Valley or even globally. Obviously there is more VC funding in California but we may do better specifically concerning edtech. On a similar note, I am curious about the motivation for participating in edtech. On its face, although there is money in edtech, it just doesn't seem as potentially lucrative as tech investing in general. This seems to build off of Jenny's Q regarding the ethics of the field or tech in general. Finally, I wonder if there will be a lag in terms of investing between now and January 20th, 2017 (or longer) depending on the new administration's political goals... We will do our best to capture our learning on this blog post.
