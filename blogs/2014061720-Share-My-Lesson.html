Share My Lesson

Fred Rossoff

June 17, 2014


Share My Lesson is a teacher-to-teacher lesson sharing platform. In Share My Lesson, a teacher community uploads, downloads, and reviews primary and secondary school lesson plans. In addition, the website provides its own native lessons to ensure full coverage of educational topics.

Pros:

Although the meat of the website is in the actual lessons, the creators have provided a variety of supporting tools to improve their accessibility and use. A robust reviewing community and discussion forum aids teachers in locating the appropriate resources, or finding help when none are available.

Cons:

The website layout and navigation aren’t particularly intuitive, and the reviewing system, like those in many other online communities, is often unreliable.

Our Takeaway:

A website providing new, tested classroom lesson plans (and resources), as well as a community capable of providing informed feedback on lessons you’ve already developed can certainly up your teaching game. However, there’s a lot of competition in this space, and the extra bells and whistles provided by Share My Lesson might not be enough to differentiate the site from others providing similar services.

The Bottom Line:

Everything depends on whether Share My Lesson can build a community.
