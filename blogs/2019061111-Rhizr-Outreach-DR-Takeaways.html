Rhizr Outreach D+R Takeaways

Cinthia.Fabian

June 11, 2019

Thank you to everyone who participated in today’s D&R! I appreciate all your insights/illustrations/imagined esoteric interests. 


Below you may find a list of your responses to the question “Given both your learning preferences and past learning experiences, what challenges might you face when teaching yourself something new?” 





Our team will keep your responses in mind as we a) craft promotional messaging that speaks directly to the concerns and anxieties of those engaged in self-directed learning and b) model rhizrs that perhaps alleviate or resolve some of the obstacles ya’ll pointed out. 


Some of the messaging frameworks that were pitched include: 

  • Are you a single father of twins trying to reconcile your newfound interest in knitting with your sense of masculinity? Through this cutesy Instagram ad, we will show you how rhizr is an ideal space for working your sh*t out! 
  • “Ask random questions, receive random answers!” > Rhizr is an inclusive platform where people can ask and learn about anything, sans embarrassment or shame. 
  • “What is happiness?” > More than just a place where learners can pick up a specific or tangible skill, Rhizr is also a one-stop-shop for users seeking to consolidate information on some of life’s biggest questions. 
  • Mind map tool or knowledge sharing platform? Why not have both? > A visual tool with a community emphasis, Rhizr’s flexibility allows it to fulfill a wide range of functions simultaneously. 


Honestly, I enjoyed hearing 'em all. Can't wait to start getting the (your!) word(s) out there.
