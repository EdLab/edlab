Learning in Second Life

Anthony Cocciolo

October 3, 2006

As you all may know, I have been keeping my eye on Second Life as a space for education. For those of you who participated in our Summer 2006 course on the Sociology of Online Learning will recognize both the Harvard and NMC space. Here is an ad for a class this term being offered by Harvard Law School: The disappointment of course is that they are using the space to watch video lectures and hold discussions. They allude to other "possibilities", but it is unclear if this course will tap any of these. The desire to fly out of the lecture hall and do something else was strongly exhibited by the students in our course, so I hope they have more planned then simply sitting in the lecture hall.
