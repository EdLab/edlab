Understanding Socio-Technical Phenomena in a Web 2.0 Era

Anthony Cocciolo

September 29, 2008

I think danah boyd does a nice job discussing the relationship between socio-technical factors and Web 2.0 at at talk at her new home at Microsoft Research New England. I tried to cover some of this ground in my talk to CCTE, but from the questions I got there are probably clearer ways to talk about this. Some of this problably has to do with methods- ethnographic methods (rather than more computational methods) in analyzing data can help "bring home" a point.
