Authors of tcrecords.org- Post 1

Megha Agarwala

November 22, 2010

Authors who have contributed to tcrecords have been associated with schools only in the United States and Canada. This is interesting in an amusing manner. There are 21 different schools in Canada and 60 different schools in United States where authors of tcrecords have been associated with. I broke down the schools in US by the state regions to see from which states authors of tcrecords have been associated with. From the 50 states in US, I found that: Authors of tcr have not been associated with any school in 15 of the states. We can say that tcr has not seen publications from any school in these states: Alaska, Hawaii, Idaho, Kentucky, Louisiana, Missouri, Mississippi, Montana, North Dakota, New Jersey, New Mexico, Nevada, Oregon, South Dakota, West Virginia. These states can be seen labeled in the map.

From tcrecords
There have been authors from schools in the rest of the 35 states (states shown colored in the map). Further, from amongst these 35 states, most number of schools associated with authors of tcr have been from California and Massachusetts, followed by Illinois and Pennsylvania. These states have been labeled in capital letters on the map.
