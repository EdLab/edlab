Learning Beyond the Classroom

Jenny Abamu

December 21, 2016


This year we reviewed a lot of research on the push to move learning from traditional classrooms to online and virtual spaces. Here are some of our favorite studies on what the evolution of digital learning looks like.

1. What is it Like to Teach Virtual Students?
Teacher training has received an upgrade from virtual reality, but is it just as effective? This is at the top of the list as it combines the use of fascinating technology with concrete analysis.

2. Disadvantages in the Virtual World Classroom Might Not Be So Simple
Do gamers finally have an educational edge over others? Some researchers explored this hypothesis as more content is moving into the virtual realm.

3. What Will Make Professors Start Valuing Online Education?
This study on faculty perspectives on online teaching might explain the quality gap that many observe. This research made our list for its fascinating results, showing the possible clashes among faculty, student, and administrator perspectives on distance learning.

4. Your Online Class is Missing These Things
Researchers say your success in an online class can be predicted. Take a look at the indicators they explore in the study that is shaping the future of online formative assessment.

5. This is Why No One is Finishing that Online course
Too many of us know people who have started and never completed online courses. This study gets to the nitty gritty and breaks down the barriers that are keeping students from completing courses.
