Social Media Mavericks

jvazquez

December 7, 2009

The challenge: Be the first team to find 8 red weather balloons scattered across the US by using social networks. The result: A group at MIT won the challenge, hosted by Darpa, within 8 hours. The moral: With enough contacts and plenty of incentives, a once arduous task can be a cakewalk. Ancillary moral: We should do this! I smell "great PR opportunity" written all over it, especially in relation to the EdVenture competition. We could drop symbols of educational entrepreneurship (I don't know... giant red apples with dollar signs? Strategically placed tv/radio/internet/print ads?) all across the Northeast/ed schools/NYC and have people report their findings on our EdLab Venture Competition website. As long as it doesn't turn out like the ATHF marketing fiasco of 2007, I think it could be pretty effective in spreading the word of the competition! Read about the Dapra Network Challenge here
