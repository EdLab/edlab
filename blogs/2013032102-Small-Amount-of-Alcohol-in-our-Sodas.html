Small Amount of Alcohol in our Sodas?

Khalil Abubakar

March 21, 2013

Soda, known for containing unhealthy amount sugar now has traces of small amount of alcohol in it according to French researchers. This has raised some concerns about the health and religious issues, especially Muslims. According to the article, Michel Pepin, scientific director of Coca-Cola France states “alcohol comes from the process of making coke according to its secret recipe”. A French Magazine, 60 Million Consumers, published a result saying the beverages contain 0.001 percent alcohol, which amounts to 10 mg in every liter. Ten beverages which contain traces of alcohol include Coca-Cola, Coca-Cola Class Light, Coke Zero, and Pepsi Cola. Dr. Hatem Soliman, Specialist Critical Care Medicine at Al Zahra Private Hospital states that although the amount of alcohol might be very small, it may increase or decrease the effect of drugs taken like blood thinners and antibiotics. The health hazards of this sugary beverage can impact diabetic patients and even in healthy people who are predisposed to obesity and osteoporosis, among other conditions.
