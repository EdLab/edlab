A Fun Way to Teach Your Machine to Learn

DebraLee

January 8, 2018


To make creating artificial neural networks easy to understand, Google developed Teachable Machine, a web tool that lets you create simple programs using just your webcam.

In this vialogue, Google Creative Lab steps you through how to train your machine to recognize faces and objects through pattern recognition. The software uses three basic classes (a cat, a dog, and a rabbit) and shows you how to record an action, such as waving, making a crazy face, or raising a hand, that your machine can interpret as a command. You can also use your microphone to record a voice response.
