New IRB rules

Julia Martin

July 26, 2011

Per my very trustworthy Professor: Ever wanted to post a survey to a listserv only to be told that you will need approval from an Institutional Review Board (IRB)? Well, it seems that is about to change. As reported in today's New York Times, the Department of Health and Human Services proposed changes to the research protocol involving human subjects. The proposal includes changes to the requirement for informed consent recognizing the changing nature of research methods in digital environments and overall it sounds like a step in the right direction.
