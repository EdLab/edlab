The averages for the past two months are: November Desktop 1:53 Mobile 0:30 October Desktop 1:31 Mobile 0:30 Visit length and post length are measures of engagement, but 7 minutes shouldn't necessarily be the goal; a good article can't be determined by length. Also, aside from a few long Profiles, you'd have to be reading VERY slowly to spend 7 minutes on the average NLT article.

What it does mean is that it's worth writing however much you really need. Don't feel constrained by presumed short attention spans. If you put in the effort, so will your audience. It's just math.
