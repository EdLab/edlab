Visit to University of Phoenix

gnatriello

December 9, 2012

The second part of the trip was a visit to the University of Phoenix where we discussed online programs and had a chance to see their recently renovated classroom of the future. Each side of the classroom has a set of display screens that students can access to to display some of their work and share it with classmates. The displays at the front of the room rely on short throw projectors mounted side-by-side. The display can be either a single image spanning all three projectors or a separate image from each projector. The tables in the room feature flip-tops that can also serve as writing surfaces. For me the most intriguing technology in the room was the walls the can be changed from transparent glass to opaque white. These can also serve as writing surfaces. The team from Phoenix provided some useful insights into the use of the classroom since its launch in January 2012.
