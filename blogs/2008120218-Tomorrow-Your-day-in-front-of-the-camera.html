Tomorrow: Your day in front of the camera

Stephanie Hunt

December 2, 2008

Heads up! Tomorrow Josh will be coming around to conduct brief video interviews with as many of you as possible. The material from these interviews will make up some videos that we will present during the Open House/Holiday Party on December 17th. I'd appreciate it if you could try to phrase your questions in a way that anyone could “get,” so that the video interviews can be used to introduce EdLab to new audiences. (No acronyms!) Prepare for a healthy mix of serious and goofy. Questions posed to you might include...

  • What is your name and how did you arrive at the lab?
  • What are you working on now?
  • What's a weird skill that's helped you in your work with EdLab?
  • What do you think the EdLab will be working on 10/20 years from now?
  • Any ideas for an EdLab secret handshake?
  • If EdLab had a special dance, what dance move would have to be part of it?
  • Nerdy team-specific questions:
    • For the design team: What's your favorite font?
    • For the video team: What's your favorite video length?
    • For the publishing team: What's your favorite magazine?
    • For the research team: What's your favorite research method?
    • For the development team: What's your favorite programming language?
    • For the new learning team: What's your favorite game?
