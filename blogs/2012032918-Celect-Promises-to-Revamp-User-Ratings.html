Celect Promises to Revamp User Ratings

Kate Meersschaert

March 29, 2012

What is Celect? After reading this New York Magazine piece on user-generated rating systems, I was inspired to try-out MIT professor, Devavrat Shaw's newest entry into the realm... Celect, described on the app's site as, "a comparison based polling, or group decision, tool that provides an alternative to ratings (e.g. five stars) or yes/no based voting." The "problem" and a potential solution... After creating and testing my first "Celect" poll, I found the interface and experience to be underwhelming... I am left unsure how this will revolutionize and help solve a growing problem with user ratings described in the aforementioned NY Mag piece as, "This democritization of reviewing tends to produce aggregate scores that reveal nothing much at all." Your turn! Create a Celect poll for yourself or try this one I created and let me know in the comments if you think there is potential to revolutionize ratings here?
