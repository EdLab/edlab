Development and Research-- 5/31/2017 - Vialogues 2.0 Internal Testing

zhou

May 30, 2017

The goal of this D&R is to test internally the upcoming release of Vialogues 2.0 featuring synchronous commenting. The development team is about 70% done with the implementation work as of today. We'd like to give the D&R a preview of the new release and collect feedback.


Development website: https://dev-vlg2.tc-library.org


This site uses the dev login system so if you can't login with your EdLab account please register a new one.


Today we are focusing on the following aspects:


  • Macro design (site structure, page layout)
  • UX (User Experience) / LX (Learning Experience) with asynchronous + synchronous commenting
  • Creating and discussing vialogues


We are NOT focusing on the following:


  • Micro design (colors, fonts, etc.)
  • Functionality
  • Bugs
  • Other pages/activities except creating and discussing vialogues


Below is an outline of today's group activity:


  1. Each group will spend 15 min to create 2 vialogues and discuss them within the group. The vialogues can be uploaded, YouTube, or Vimeo. Due to the time limit, please choose a video that is not too long (ideally no more than 5 min).
  2. Each individual will then spend 10 min to discuss at least 2 vialogues created by other groups.
  3. Each group will then discuss internally to share their experiences in creating and discussing the vialogues (10 min). Topics of discussion can include but are not limited to: issues or suggestions; potential use cases of the new Vialogues that combine asynchronous and synchronous discussions; future directions.
  4. Each group will then report and share their discussions.
