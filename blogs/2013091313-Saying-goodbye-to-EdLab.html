Saying goodbye to EdLab

Josh Lopez

September 13, 2013

For those of you who don't already know, Today is my last day at EdLab. I didn't want to make a big deal out of it which is why there was no big announcement (thanks to Pranav and Hui Soo for respecting my wishes there). This parting is amiable; I just felt it was time for me to move on. After what I hope is a short service on jury duty I'll be flinging myself into the NYC edtech startup world. As Hui Soo says, the world of edtech in New York is a small one. Also I live in the neighborhood. So I'll likely run into some of you again. Working at EdLab has been a really great experience and I'm glad to have worked with everyone here. To those of you who feel we never got to know each other as well as we should, I'm sorry, but I'm probably not going all that far away so maybe we'll still get the chance.
