Google Enters the Realm of Massive Open Online Course

Kate Meersschaert

July 16, 2012

Google has launched its first Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) on "Power Searching." Check-it-out and sign-up here (Admission seems to be "rolling" with all activities ending on July 23rd!). The self-directed, online course is made-up of six 50-minute classes that include video resources, group "Hangouts", a final exam and correspondent printable certificate of completion. Are you intrigued by Google's entry into the MOOC universe?
