Science With a Dash of Humor

DebraLee

December 12, 2017


As a child, Bathambile Mpofu dreamt of studying medicine, but her high school science education failed her and when she got to university she had to settle for chemistry. Today she is a scientist, innovator, entrepreneur, and co-founder of Nkazimulo Applied Sciences, a firm in Durban, South Africa that manufactures science kits for high school students who want to improve their science understanding and performance.

In this video Bathambile Mpofu delights school children with her science experiments that she delivers with a dash of humor and an intent to show that science is cool, not scary. Each demonstration links science in the classroom with a wow factor, which also puts the science in a real life context. Bathambile Mpofu encourages learners to ask questions and find out how things are made, how they work, and how they can be improved. She wants to change the learning landscape so that more children pursue science careers, innovate, and make their lives and the world better through science.
