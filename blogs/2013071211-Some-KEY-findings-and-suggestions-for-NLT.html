Some KEY findings & suggestions for NLT

Sharon Hsiao

July 12, 2013

*keep private please I look at Non-EdLab user behavior of NLT (Shuguang & I will share more findings in 2 weeks D&R) Just give you a little flavor now, so we can think about it and discuss further.

83% of Non-EdLabers click on most-view-articles. 52% of them click most-share-articles. 1/3 of NON-EdLab users only read 1 article.
These are big proportion of their actions, indicating, they are exploring this site. So, what we could do, is to enhance: 1. "findability" (good search) 2. visibility for exploration (longer lists for popular articles & most share, visualizations) 3. 1&2 for mobile too. (in fact, a big portion of mobile users get to NLT thru direct links, but got bounced off by login. maybe, a 75% of an article for free, or some strategies to at least get them stay a little longer will increase mobile readers' usage) of course, we are moving on the recommender service, that will increase chances for users to stay, to spice up the exploration. But, currently, i think, findability & visibility are more essential for early stage!
