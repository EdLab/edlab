Teaching with Zombies

Walter Hoyle

April 5, 2011

McPherson College is crawling with zombies and the only cure is…information literacy? Library Journal reports that McPherson’s Miller Library has created a graphic novel zombie apocalypse adventure to demonstrate library services and strategies for incoming students. I love action librarians! You can take a look at the finished product here: Library of the Living Dead The artwork is great and the story is exciting. The real library info seems to be in a little guide at the back (after the zombie part), but I'm for anything to tart up library instruction! Way to market, McPherson!
