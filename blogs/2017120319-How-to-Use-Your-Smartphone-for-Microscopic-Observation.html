How to Use Your Smartphone for Microscopic Observation

DebraLee

December 3, 2017


Thanks to breakthroughs in phone technology, iADAPTER offers budding scientists an affordable way to convert traditional optical microscope images to digital using a smartphone. This vialogue demos how easy it is to take a professional micrograph with a smartphone on student or vintage microscopes. It even works with a telescope. The micrographs can be shared in classrooms on a screen, or projected to a wall for live video play.

In the beta phase of development, iADAPTER collaborated with MOMALA, which used its technology to detect the presence of malaria parasites on a regular slide in the field in Africa. Researchers were able to deliver high-quality diagnoses in a low resource environment. Could this simplistic technology improve STEM learning by enabling teachers to create their own content using images shot through a microscope? Join the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
