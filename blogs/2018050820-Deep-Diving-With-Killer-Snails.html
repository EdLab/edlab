Deep Diving With Killer Snails

EdLabStudios

May 8, 2018


Elementary and middle school teachers are often struggling to find ways to foster a true interest in science in their classrooms. The answer? Killer snails! Not only are killer snails a fascinating predator from the deep but it is also the name of a brand new creator of science games that inspires a love of science in users of all ages. Killer Snails was developed for teachers by teachers and offers both card games and virtual reality experiences. In April, 2018 two members of the Killer Snails team, Jessica Ochoa Hendrix and Lindsay Portnoy, shared their product and an exciting VR demo at the EdLab seminar. Watch the full seminar on Vialogues.

Excerpts from the discussion:
