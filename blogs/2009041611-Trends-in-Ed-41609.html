Trends in Ed, 4.16.09

Arianna Choi

April 16, 2009

Learn Freely for Free FreshBrain is a technology exploration platform for teens. It is hosting a free virtual summer technology camp that teaches youth how to build a Facebook app, create a YouTube movie or 3D video game among other things that might help define coolness. 3 Simple Techniques to Guide Your Learner's Attention The author of the Rapid E-Learning Blog shares three simple techniques to keep that engagement going between you, the teacher or presenter, and the learners(s): 1. Show them the big picture and let them see everything in context. 2. Point out those parts of the screen that are important. 3. Only show them the information as you get to it. Theses recommendations are adopted very well in prezi.com. Jo, who is making a presentation today at AERA, has done her presentation practice using this tool. In line with today's theme, zero cost, the basic version of the prezi tool is also free. Utah, the First State to offer iPhone Apps Earlier this week, Utah.gov announced the release of two free iPhone applications, Utah.Gov and Utah Professional License Look Up. Features include: Agency Lookup: find agencies by keyword search. Displays contact information including phone number, address, and Web site address. Service Lookup: search for or browse over 1,000 award-winning Citizen, Business, and Government-to-Government services. Utah News: the latest news and announcements from Utah.Gov.
