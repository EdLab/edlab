Mixing Physical and Digital Experiences (at Sea)

gnatriello

June 5, 2017



Carnival Cruise Lines is introducing a new shipwide technology platform to provide a more personalized and tech enabled experience for passengers.  The new system placed sensors and video screens throughout the ship. Passengers are issued personal medallions that allow the system to know where they are and customize the immediate environment based on their preferences.  Notably, the system also allows passengers to decline data sharing and personalization if they prefer.


Mixing physical and digital experiences offers a number of opportunities for us to rethink and improve the experience for library patrons.  If you think about the entire experience of the library patron in Russell Hall, where do you think we could use digital applications to enhance the learning environment?
