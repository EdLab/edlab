Meetup! The Future of Education: New Models for 21st Century Learning

Kate Meersschaert

January 9, 2013

*Join me on January 23rd for the Meetup: "The Future of Education: New Models for 21st Century Learning". This meetup is sponsored by the Meetup group Be Social Change and Centre for Social Innovation. *Note: this meetup is $20 to attend - please check-in with Hui Soo before registering! The event will feature the following speakers: Kane Sarhan, Co-founder, Enstitute. Enstitute is reintroducing a project-based and apprenticeship-style learning model. Juliette LaMontagne, Founder & Director, Breaker. Breaker is a project-based model focused on design-process learning & social innovation. Brad Hargreaves, Founding Partner, General Assembly. GA is a global network of campuses providing education in technology, business, & design. Abigail Besdin, Head of Education Team, Skillshare. Skillshare is a global learning community encouraging anyone to learn and teach skills. Location: CSI Pop-Up Space 601 West 26th Street Suite #360, New York, NY Time: 6:30-9:00 Cost: $20 Register after Speaking with Hui Soo: http://futureofeducation1.eventbrite.com/
