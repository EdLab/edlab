15-Year-Olds Cry for Help

Bismark Appiah

June 8, 2015

One of the most beautiful things in this world is to see a group of people coming together to raise awareness for a good cause. I was so enthused when I came across this piece. A group of 15-year-olds across the globe came together to bring attention to an issue that impacts the education of children around the world: poverty. Poverty has been one of the many reasons why children from developing nations often don’t enroll in school. Children can’t attend school on an empty stomach. Likewise, children who live in broken homes can’t afford to go to school. The purpose of the “Action/2015” is to call upon world leaders to take immediate action towards hunger, violence against women and children, and poverty. If world leaders are willing to heed to the crying voices of 15-year-olds across the globe, I believe the world will be better place. The action by these young people demonstrates their hunger for not just education but quality education.
