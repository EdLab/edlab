Ground Control to Major Palm

csd2126

March 26, 2017


Get a first-hand view of the International Space Station (ISS) Vegetable Production System in this new app from NASA. NASA Science Investigations: Plant Growth introduces players to the engineering design process of growing plants in space.

Pros:

Science Investigations: Plant Growth provides an extensive tutorial to orient users to their role in the game. The simulationâ€™s first-person perspective makes you feel like you're in space as you spin, flip, and float through the ISS. Players have several options for exploration within the app, which allows them to explore the game at their own pace. The digital badging for each mission allows teachers to create instruction around its modules or to assess students progress through the game. The most beneficial aspect of the app is the first-hand view of conducting science in space.

Cons:

While this app provides explicit instructions, it has a significant amount of text which may be difficult for young users. The navigation requires several gestures, which may also be difficult for some students to use. However, navigation can also be accomplished through the use of a map depiction of the ISS.

Our Takeaway:

Science Investigations: Plant Growth provides a lot of great information for students about plant life in space. It is fun to play, has different modes that could engage learners, and provides helpful hints to make the large amounts of information more understandable.
