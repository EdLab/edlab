How to Live With AI

DebraLee

November 14, 2017


In these times of fake news and alternative facts, itâ€™s not surprising that so many of us suffer confusion and anxiety about the role AI will play in the future. In this vialogue, the Future of Life Institute works with author and physicist Max Tegmark to separate AI myths from AI facts.

Tegmark, who recently published a book on the topic, Life 3.0: Being Human in the Age of Artificial Intelligence, argues that artificial intelligence is simply a particular kind of information processing performed by reacting, and we should be thinking about the societal implications of what we create and how we might design and build AI systems that reflect and respect our hopes and values.

Tegmark invites skeptics to experience a machine trumping them by downloading a state-of-the-art chess engine. Though you can try to beat the AI, Tegmark says "you never will." Challenge accepted?
