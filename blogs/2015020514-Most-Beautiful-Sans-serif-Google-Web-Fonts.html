Most Beautiful Sans-serif Google Web Fonts

Panisuan Chasinga

February 5, 2015

Fonts alone can set a great visual experience for the users. Sans-serifs, or fonts "without" serifs (referred to as Gothic typefaces by some) are used to achieve a modern, clear, bold and concise visual statement. Here is a small list of sans-serif fonts from Google's most popular web fonts.

  • Open sans
  • Lato
  • Ubuntu
  • PT Sans + PT Serif
  • Droid Sans
    • While looking more "ancient" than sans-serifs, serif typefaces aka "Roman" shouldn't be neglected. They are still widely used today, for instance, in books and literature documents like resume, to convey authenticity and warmth to the document or website.
