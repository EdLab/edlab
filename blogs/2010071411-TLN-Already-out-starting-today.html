TLN - Already out starting today...

Jason Mariasis

July 14, 2010

To all TLN members, once again - Nathaniel shared a new and critical piece of news with me. Unfortunately for us, the company NIXTY launched today the TLN network we're in the process of creating. I've had the chance to experiment with their interface, and yes, it's just like the one we're planning. While continuing to create the entire TLN network is, in my opinion, no longer an option (oh capitalism and competitive markets...), I think we're presented with a potential opportunity to consult for NIXTY. Their architecture and layout is basically the same as ours, but they're lacking some of the social/interactive features. Additionally, I think we've thought through some of the more complicated concepts of the network more fully than they have, and they could benefit from our knowledge. Perhaps we should contact them soon (like this week or next). Other options include: -Continue with creating the TLN (though I don't see why if the product already exists and we would have to spend a significant amount of time just to reach the point that NIXTY has, unless we think that Columbia can market the system more effectively) -Increase our development resources and quickly (within 2-3 months) copy NIXTY's site while incorporating our more thought-through ideas -Work with NIXTY to develop modules for their system (different than consulting) -Drop the project altogether Check out the system for yourselves and then let's discuss how we should proceed.
