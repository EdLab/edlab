America's Future College Cost

Khalil Abubakar

July 25, 2013

This video is about how college tuition at public, and private is destined to increase if current trends are not solved by 2034. According to the Vialogue below, by 2034 college costs for Ivy League schools might be almost half a million. This shows the that Ivy League schools in the future might be impossible for poor or middle class students to attend. Additionally, the income levels of most American families have not increased more than one percent since 1987. If the government or other organizations don't look for certain ways to make education cheaper and more affordable, I think college will only be affordable for the rich, which will significantly enhance the already income inequality mobility in America.
