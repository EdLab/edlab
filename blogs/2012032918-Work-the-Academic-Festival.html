Work the Academic Festival!

jvazquez

March 29, 2012

Our plans are quickly solidifying as we get set to rock the Academic Festival this year. We'll be occupying the 2nd and 5th floors of the library and will be delivering copious amounts of information regarding our various products, curriculum projects, and services to eager alumni (and prospective/current students') ears. With over 1,000 guests scheduled to flock to TC's campus for the event, it promises to be a good time. In order to make it a GREAT time, though, we need your help. We are looking for people to help us staff this event! The festival will take place on Saturday, April 21st from 8:30am-6:30pm. If you're interested in helping, contact your supervisor and let them know that you'd like to work a morning shift, afternoon shift, or that you want to be there for the whole shebang! For details on exactly what you're signing up for, you'll have to attend a mandatory staff meeting on Thursday, April 19th (time TBD). Sign up now, ask questions later! Ha. But I promise, it'll be fun. And easy! Who doesn't love a festival?? Hope to see y'all there!
