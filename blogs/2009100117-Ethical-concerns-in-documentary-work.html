Ethical concerns in documentary work

Gus Andrews

October 1, 2009

The Center for Social Media recently produced a report on the ethical problems documentary filmmakers face in their work. It's apparently gotten a lot of press, including a writeup in the New York Times and a supplementary interview on NPR's On The Media. Interesting food for thought. Seems to me this might be interesting reading/reflecting material for us all at AfterEd (she said, remembering the piece she did on term paper mills in particular)!
