Clever: The New Login Layer for Education Apps

Panisuan Chasinga

April 10, 2015

Clever, a thriving edtech startup founded in 2012 by three Harvard graduates, has raised a total amount of $43M in funding to become the go-to login layer for education apps. Clever claims 1 out of 5 K-12 schools in the US are using their service, and that's ten of thousands of them. The competitive edge lies in the fact that Facebook has a bad name among the schools. It is often blocked. Google is all about acquiring users' data and then selling them customized ads. In K-12 settings, there is no trust in both. Learn more about the service here.
