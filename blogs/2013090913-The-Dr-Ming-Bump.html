The Dr. Ming Bump

Kate Meersschaert

September 9, 2013

Private! Recent NLT Profilee Dr. Vivienne Ming featured her recent Profile piece on her site (see image below). To what effect? Glad you asked, in the time period from 08/21/13-09/09/13 the posting has been the #4 referrer to NLT (w/ 31+ visits). What can we learn from this? Well, for one, inviting participants and subjects to share their NLT pieces across their network can lead to a nice boost in traffic. Also, it is interesting to note that Dr. Ming did not question our sign-up process. Once. Thanks Dr. Ming! Note: I love that we are listed in her "Recent News" section above O Magazine and all her other impressive press pieces!
