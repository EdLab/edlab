Cherry Blossom Season

Clare O'Dowd

April 3, 2013

April is cherry blossom season! The Brooklyn Botanic Garden has a great map to show you when their cherry blossom trees are blooming and has a program celebrating Japanese culture at the end of the month. The New York Botanical Garden also has cherry blossoms, including those in Cherry Valley near the Peggy Rockefeller Rose Garden.
