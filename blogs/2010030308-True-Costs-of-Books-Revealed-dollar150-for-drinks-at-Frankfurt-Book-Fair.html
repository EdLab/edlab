True Costs of Books Revealed - $1.50 for drinks at Frankfurt Book Fair

gnatriello

March 3, 2010

Now, Michael Kinsley has weighed in on the debate over the cost of books and ebooks. He provides a list of all the things paid for with the $26 average book price for traditional print books, including $1.50 for drinks at Frankfurt. Let's not just be observers of this radical transition because at some point it will hit higher education. So, what's our equivalent of the $1.50 for drinks? And what should we be doing about it?
