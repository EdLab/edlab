Fantastico! for experimenting with web-based tools

bhughes

March 7, 2007

I had a cafe conversation with Paul Acquaro today, and he mentioned Fantastico as an interesting solution for deploying websites. It's clear that the College needs a way of keeping pace with people's desires for experimenting with new web tools. Our response has been to moderate their desires with a dose of reality... but what if we could respond with a sandbox approach? I can imagine the library or another tech-based group at the College supporting these efforts in a broad and sustained way. Groups working with open-source software could dedicate personnel (students?) to installation and set-up, and the school partner could assist. Then, if/when the tool takes off and becomes an integral part of a group's work, it could be put through a development process and formally installed on a school-supported server.
