Group Discussion

Bachir Diarrassouba

November 18, 2013

The Vialogues meeting last week was a predictive meeting. Each person posted at least two videos that were related to their theme. Each person discussed their personal approach on recruiting new users as well as the number of new users they received. Bachir, Ahmed and Reindorf used the same approach which is personally talking to people. They collected a total of three new users. Bismarck has a new approach where he's to the TC mailing address and emailing various professors and asking them to sign up to Vialogues he recruited two new users last week. Francisco talked to his Criminal Justice Professor about Vialogues and asked his professor to spread the word; as a result Francisco recruited four new users. We also talked about a proposal for a marketing plan for recruiting new users. We proposed talking to teachers and children about Vialogues and allowing them to implement it in their school for a short period of time and then seeing the outcome of that.We thought that by discussing the sight to educators, it would be easier for them to promote Vialogues because of their connection with other school officials.
