VISUAMUSIO

Ting Yuan

October 9, 2014


Designed by WOW, Inc., a company based in Japan, VISUAMUSIO is an app for iPad and iPhone that helps users to create music and visually transcribe it into animations. The unique gameplay gives users an experience of creating visual-music films via animated shapes and sounds that draws connections between the arts and geometry. From simple compositions of geometric shapes and synthetic sounds to relatively complex algorithmic visual symphonies, VISUAMUSIO provides ample composing possibilities to users of all ages.

Pros:

With a clean, friendly design, VISUAMUSIO allows users to make meaning of different music notes by selecting shapes in multiple colors. Each choice of shape and color represents a unique music note. Users place shapes onto four strings to start composing. Users learn to understand music elements through the aesthetic experience of visually designing their creations. Each composition can be saved and shared through the app’s online library in the form of hyperlinks or via social networking sites.

Cons:

The app is offered at a cost of $3.99. Currently it only provides three shape choices; more options might need to be considered for future development. The space for composing is unlimited, but the app only shows one section at a time and lacks the flexibility to zoom in and out to adjust the complete visual design.

Our Takeaway:

VISUAMUSIO is a creative music visualization app for hands-on digital interactive play. It is suitable not only for children’s independent play at home but also for students’ collaborative exploration in arts programs at school.

The Bottom Line:

VISUAMUSIO is an animated music visualization app for K-12 children and beyond.
