EdLab Review: iKnowthat.com

Chandler Kang

March 25, 2010

iKnowthat.com Tags: interactive, dynamic, social learning, online multimedia game
http://www.iknowthat.com Overall: 3.29 Design score: 3.08 out of 5 Pedagogy score: 3.50 out of 5 [The Good] There are abundant contents and subject matters. Even constructed tasks are embedded and permeated into activities that the website provided. It makes students learn with fun. For example, learning task comes in the middle of game or adventure activities. This makes student users do learning tasks in "flow." [The Bad] Learning paths in the website were preprogrammed, therefore it is hard to expect personalized instructions depending on learners' activities in the website. [The Bottom Line] It basically adopts a game mode, but learning materials are well constructed, so students user can easily focus on learning tasks. Also, "parent info" in each activity provides a specified learning goal via the activity. Read Full Review
