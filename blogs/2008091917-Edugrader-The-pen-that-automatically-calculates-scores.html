Edugrader: The pen that automatically calculates scores

Stephen Asunka

September 19, 2008

Edugrader is a combination of an ink pen and an electronic grading tool that teachers can use to mark paper-based assignments and tests by writing comments, while keeping track of the final score. It works this way: You program the pen for the highest possible score. You then grade the work by pressing an error button each time there's a mistake, or whenever you want to deduct a mark (you can also add bonus points). When you are done, the pen will display the final score in percent. It will also show number of points awarded, the number of points deducted, and maximum points possible. Great! but isn't this a case of moving forward to the past?
