Good People - Chap. 3 - The Foundation: Truth

HSC

June 19, 2017

This chapter read a little like a “Yoda rant” but it’s hard to argue with the substance: 


“Truth is our foundation, and humility, self-awareness, and integrity are the mind-set, practice, and action values of truth” (p. 35). 


1. Be humble (have a mind-set of humility ) because it facilitates learning and openness, which are required to do ambitious work. 


2. Practice self-awareness (the practice).  It will help you to be honest about your intentions and actions, and enable you to form better relationships with others.


3. Act with integrity (the action). Conduct yourself in a way that is consistent with your principles and values.


What can we do as an organization to foster these values of truth? 


For me the most intriguing part of the chapter was the quote by Lao Tzu right at the beginning:


At the center of your being 

you have the answer; 

You know who you are

and you know what you want (p. p41). 


Like "goodness," knowing who you are and what you want is a process, not an endpoint. Humility, self-awareness, and integrity are all required values for self-exploration and improvement.
