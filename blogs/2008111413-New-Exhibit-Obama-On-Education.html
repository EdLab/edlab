New Exhibit: Obama On Education

Stephanie Hunt

November 14, 2008

Hopefully many of you have already seen the "Where They Stand" display, on view in the second floor reading room of Gottesman Libraries. I've posted some images of the display (both the print graphics and installation shots) on the EdLab Flickr page. Well, as soon as Barack Obama received his 270th electoral vote on November 5th, the design team got to work updating "Where They Stand." The display now has a new name: "Obama On Education." The updates were installed this Monday (November 10), and include more and newer information on Obama's proposed education policy, as well as new infographics and historical quotes from past presidents. VOICE YOUR IDEAS THROUGH THE OBAMAEDU BLOG We'd also like the newly revised display to include your thoughts about the issues and Obama's proposed solutions. With this in mind, I've created the Obama On Education blog. We will select comments from those submitted on the blog to print and include in the display. The display will be up through February 2009. Please help us to make this an active sounding board reflecting the views and ideas of the TC community.
