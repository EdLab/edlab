Paperless library

Chandler Kang

May 20, 2010

Library is gone paperless! This is not a totally new idea, but now it seems to be becoming a general trend, since Teachers College moved all bound periodicals to the sub-basement and started substituting it to e-files a couple years ago. Now Stanford has joined TC. Stanford University has moved toward the creation of its "bookless library." Read article here. This article reminds me of two things. One is EdLab's ebook research, which is still ongoing. I wonder if there are different readability issues depending on a subject matter. The other is our second floor renovation plans. We can also set an e-station in the second floor which provides e-book readers like Kindle and test it for efficiency of teaching and learning.
