Design Thinking

EdLab Studios

March 24, 2013


One may not typically think of teachers as designers, but the design agency, IDEO, certainly does. On this professional development day at Riverdale Country School, teachers are learning about the mindset and process that is known as Design Thinking. As an approach to problem solving that is human-centered, optimistic, and often experimental, Design Thinking encourages teachers to tackle numerous problems in their own classrooms through a process of ideating and prototyping. During this professional development crash course, IDEO is introducing their "Design Thinking for Educators Toolkit" that provides suggestions and guidelines for teachers who are looking for solutions in their classrooms and beyond.
