The Lottery

Duncan Asiedu

April 8, 2011

While browsing through Hulu, I happened to stumble across a documentary entitled The Lottery. This documentary profiled the lives of several Harlem residents, hopeful of gaining their child admission into the Harlem Success Academy. In doing so, the documentary also goes on to detail the greater controversy that surrounds the concept of charter schools and efforts to quell its advancement. The Harlem Success Academy is used as a specific example in which the documentary chronicles its fight to expand amongst opposition from certain groups. In regards to the charter school's admission method, some people are appalled by the thought that a child's access to a quality education should come down to a lottery system. Furthermore, the documentary illustrates the faults associated with the nation's educational system particularly that of inner cities. Watch the documentary below and give your thoughts.
