Dating in 1949: Rated G

HSC

June 4, 2011

Need advice on dating? Check out the vialogue below. It addresses essential questions like: - How to choose a date? - How do you ask for a date? - How to say goodnight? The video was produced in 1949 by Coronet Films. But in all seriousness, videos like this can be used to teach about American cultural norms and values. For educators who are teaching about post-war (WWII) America or youth culture, videos like this provide another way for students to engage in the material. And don't worry about needing parents' permission for showing this. The video is SFW (suitable for work).
