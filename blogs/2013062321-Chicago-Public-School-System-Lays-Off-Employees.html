Chicago Public School System Lays Off Employees

Bismark Appiah

June 23, 2013

I was very shock when I saw the title of this article. I have heard of teachers and staff members losing their jobs but not to this extent. In Chicago public schools, 850 employees have been laid off due to budget cuts. The Chicago Board of Education made this decision after realizing that there was a $1 billion deficit in the coming fiscal year. The board has concluded that by laying off this amount of employees, they will be able to offset $52 million in administrative and operational cuts. Among the employees who were laid off, 550 of them were teachers, while the remaining 300 employees were teacher assistants, bus aides, and custodians. Although this looks very bad in the short run, employees who have been laid off will be able to reapply for other jobs, which is the only benefit they will get from this unexpected layoff.
