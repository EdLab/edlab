List of Relevant Conferences for EdLabbers

Sharon Hsiao

August 9, 2012

Most of the conferences listed below are organized by ACM, IEEE, AAAI and are ordered by the paper submission deadlines. Of course there are still several relevant ones out there but these are the ones I found particularly focused and aligned with the EdLab's project goals. A side note, in a big conference, like CSCL, there's usually a Demo session. It's very suitable to promote projects and stir discussions in the communities. - LAK '13: International Learning Analytics & Knowledge Conference - CSCL '13: International conference on Computer Supported Collaborative Learning - Ed-MEdia 2013: World Conference on Educational Media and Technology - AIED: Artificial Intelligence in Education Conference - EDM: International Conference on Educational Data Mining - JCDL '13: Joint Conference on Digital Libraries - UMAP: The 21th conference on User Modeling, Adaptation and Personalization - Hypertext and Social Media - FIE '13: The 43rd Annual Frontiers in Education (FIE) Conference
