Edit Jam II Update: Success!

bhughes

March 3, 2007

I want to give a big congratulations to everyone who helped make yesterday's Edit Jam a fantastic, whirlwind media event. All the participants — especially the high school students and invited artists — had an education-themed experience probably unlike any they have ever had before. That's pretty cool. Now we have to figure out what it all means and create a 20-minute video. The first step will be gathering all the materials that were created at the event and starting the editing process. We have a storyboard that guided the event-planning process, but now we have to see what the outcomes add to its larger themes. (read more) I would like to ask anyone in EdLab interested in sifting through audio, video, and photo media to participate... Basically, the work will be watching unedited footage for salient clips, and making notes (this is usually referred to as "logging" the video). The production team will write up notes that will guide this process, and set up some workstations. We'll start this week!
