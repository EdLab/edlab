Keeping Quiet? The Parameters of Online Speech

dinosossi2

July 31, 2017


New studies that have been examining online speech speak (!) to the great tensions that normally inform all freedom of expression cases.

The balance between expressing yourself freely versus the harms that they cause.

The Pew Research Institute found that nearly one-third of Americans know about the issue of online harassment while 62% consider it a major problem.

The study also reveals that respondents believe that platforms should be held responsible.

However, given that expression online transcends local barriers and even international, finding the source of the harassment that could be protected by VPNs or other dark web-inspired protocols makes assigning culpability extremely difficult.

Awareness of these types of issues is a first step.

But finding an adequate solution is a much bigger one.
