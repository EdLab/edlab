Help mSchool: Give Us Online Learning!

LauraCostello

January 15, 2013

The mSchool Child Abuse Prevention team has been working to create a course for the future mandated reporters of Teachers College and greater New York State! All teachers in the state and many other professionals are required to pass a 2 hour child abuse prevention seminar as part of their license process. At this point, Teachers College students route from our site to an external website to complete this training and the mSchool team's goal is to bring this certification to TC and perhaps make the process a bit more interesting and meaningful. We recently obtained certification as a provider of this course from the Division of Professional Education at the State Education Department. We (primarily Lu :) also created an engaging set of video lectures, scenarios, quizzes, and external content connections for the course. We're ready to roll...but we're not sure where we should go. The course is currently hosted on Moodle, but we need more control over timing because the certificate requires 2 hours of in class time. We're looking for an online course platform that:

  • is able to keep time for us
  • can award a certificate at the end of the process
  • automates the "grading" process to ensure that users that fail to complete the content/2 hours do not receive certificates
  • is able to accept payment from students
Any ideas? We're pretty stumped and would appreciate any help you could give us! Thanks in advance for your amazing collective brain powers!
