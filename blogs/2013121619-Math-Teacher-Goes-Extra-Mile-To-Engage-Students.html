Math Teacher Goes Extra Mile To Engage Students

jvazquez

December 16, 2013


Matthew Weathers, Assistant Professor of Math at Biola University, has a flair for capturing his students’ attention. He’s been playing hilarious April Fools’ jokes on them for years now. The video above features one from 2011, as an intro to teaching complex and imaginary numbers.

It’s hard to say how many hours Professor Weathers puts into creating these interactive videos, but he usually collaborates with students from the film department. His students herald him for making a normally boring subject more interesting. As most educators will tell you, this is often half the battle.

What’s the most creative thing you’ve done to engage an audience? Tell us in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
