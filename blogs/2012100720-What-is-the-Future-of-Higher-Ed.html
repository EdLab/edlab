What is the Future of Higher Ed?

jvazquez

October 7, 2012


In the year 2020, academia is no longer the gatekeeper for education. And so begins a 10-minute-long journey through the future of higher education, as envisioned by the people behind EPIC 2020. Grounded in the belief that this decade will engender major change within the higher education system, EPIC 2020 shows us what the future holds.
