Women's Voices, Women's Vision Event Invite/Call for video blog people!

Jenny Taira

April 11, 2008

Just wanted to send an invite to anyone who might be interested, my chamber music group, Musique a la Mode will be doing an art show/concert entitled, "Women's Voices, Women's Vision", celebrating women in the arts this Sunday, April 13th at 7:30 PM @ St. Mark's Church-in-the-Bowery, 131 E. 10th Street (at 2nd Ave). The program features a world premiere of Margaret Anne Schedel's "Albedo .12" for bass and amplifier, as well as music by women composers from the baroque through the present. Visual Artists to be featured include Guerrilla Girls On Tour, Olek, Ai Chen LIn, Mare Vaccaro, Ayakoh Furukawa, Linda Byrne, Erin Partridge, Lydia Conklin, Coralina Meyer, Ula Einstein, Barbara Roux, Laura Napier, and Natasha Turvey. Additionally, we are looking for anyone who might be interested in taking some video footage/informal interviews of guests at the event for a future blog post. We are still pretty much a start-up organization so do not have much of a budget, but some compensation is possible and TBD. If any of you are interested, please shoot me an email ASAP. Tickets are $10/5 (for students and seniors) and can be purchased at http://www.brownpapertickets.com/event/30797. Tickets will also be sold at the door beginning at 7:00 PM. Flyer is attached.
