Kerbal Space Program

Youssef Ballo

May 28, 2014

Since its inception, it seems video games are obsessed with space travel. For instance, SpaceWar was one of the first games ever made to modern science fiction journeys. One of the new space-based video games making the waves is Kerbal, a game where players create and manage their own space program. Kerbal has become one of the top five best-selling titles. The game has notably crossed over into the scientific community with scientists and members of the space industry (i.e. astronauts) expressing interest in the game.

At a time when there is less emphasis on space exploration in our schools and by the federal government, what role can games such as Kerbal play in encouraging those students who have an interest in space exploration?
