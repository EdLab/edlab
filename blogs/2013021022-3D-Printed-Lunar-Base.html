3D Printed Lunar Base

Daniel Vergara

February 10, 2013

After a long time discussing a moon base, they've recently come up with a blueprint for creating one. I watched the video below and was amazed how they are using 3D printing. They are basically planning on sending robots that would use the moon's soil to create a solid form that would protect humans from radiation. Most of the stuff that would be needed to create it would be found in the moon already. I posted a previous blog about an inflatable moon base and was curious to know which idea would be greater. Both ideas seem interesting mostly because of the fact that people would be able to live on the moon. But which moon base would people feel more towards creating?
