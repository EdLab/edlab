Morningside Park Dog Run

EdLab Studios

March 31, 2015


Since dog first walked with man some 14,000 years ago, the ways in which our furry companions learn best has remained relatively unchanged. As Upper Paleolithic period cave drawings and settlement excavations suggest, early domesticated dogs acquired knowledge through integrated social play in natural environments. The Morningside Park Dog Run, located just beyond the gates at 115th street, draws in educators and dog owners from all walks of life hoping to educate their pets with these historically proven methods.
