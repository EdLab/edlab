Importance of Technical and Vocational Schools

abagigah92

April 8, 2015

Unemployment is a major issue affecting young people across the globe. Even young people with college degree often find it difficult to land a job. The situation is no different in my homeland. Though the standard of education in Ghana has improved in recent years, there is still a shortage of vocational and technical training opportunities. Most of the focus in the education sector has been towards academic subjects. In a country like Ghana, where most of the youth cannot afford a university education, it is important that the government focus on technical and vocational training. The success of every economy is based on employment competitiveness. The skill of the labor force is essential to economic growth for any country. An improvement in the technical and vocational field will provide employment for the youth, generate taxable income for the government and improve the country's overall economy.
