Event Recap: #SharkTankEDU Principals Edition

Kate Meersschaert

May 30, 2014

Last night Mason, Carmen, Laura and I ventured down to ThoughtWorks offices on Madison Avenue for Innovate NYC's (iZone) next installment of their #SharkTankEDU series. This time the focus was on principals; unlike the last iZone event (here is my blog post recap) which featured a panel of parent sharks. Takeaways: (more in Twitter form from the team below) -The panel was clearly invested in asking challenging questions and it made for a more lively and engaging audience experience -Preeti did a great job both stepping-in and stepping-back as a facilitator (her app.-based timer reminded me a bit of Laura's during demo night) -It was a great testament to our work building resources and a network to support entrepreneurship, that we knew and had interviewed/participated in a seminar from/hosted at demo night, a large portion of those in attendance -I am excited to play around with new demo night/panel hybrid ideas for a fall EdLab event -Microphone management is challenging for everyone -Have candy and mints for the panel -A good spread of food includes charcuterie, veggies and fruit plus wine and beer (EdLab has this down pat!) -Giving a shout-out to institutions like EdLab who are in the audience is classy and breeds goodwill between orgs -Having and using a # for events is a great way to organize and share the conversation and generate buzz... "But don't take my word for it:" Tweets about "#Sharktankedu"
