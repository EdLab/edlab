EdLab Review (LMS): udutu

Chandler Kang

February 24, 2010

udutu Tags: online course authoring, learning solution
http://www.udutu.com/ Overall: 2.65 Design score: 3.2 out of 5 Pedagogy score: 2.1 out of 5 [The Good] The company has many tools and functions for instructors to use. Users can create as many courses as possible and set all the functionalities they wish to have for each course. [The Bad] Platforms and functions are not intuitive and there is no instant feedback in the course making process. Since course interface between developers (instructors) and learners are different, it is hard to imagine how this course will look to learners who have yet to fully familiarize themselves with the site. Also, on the main page, they advertise it as free, but at the very end when you publish your course, you will realize it is NOT a free. [The Bottom Line] You can publish your course through Facebook and one of the benefits from that is the potential to receive attention from a large and diverse group of people. Also, Facebook is a fast and efficient platform to advertise the course due to the amount of users. Read Full Review
