Learning Theater Opening!

gnatriello

September 29, 2017


At long last the official opening of the Smith Learning Theater has been scheduled for October!


In the coming week there will be previews of the opening event - "AN OUT-OF-THIS-WORLD INTRODUCTION TO THE LEARNING THEATER."


We hope that EdLab alumni and friends will be able to join us for one of these previews.  Just go to the Learning Theater events page to get your ticket to a preview.  Only those with tickets will be admitted, and the doors to the theater will close promptly at show time so be sure to arrive on time.


During the official opening week of October 9th there will additional offerings of our opening event, and this event will be offered throughout the fall semester.


Finally, there will be an opening reception on Wednesday, October 11th at 6 PM to celebrate the completion of this project. 


Thanks to everyone who helped to make the Learning Theater a reality for our current and future students!
