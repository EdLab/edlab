Medium's Newsletter is Simple & (Possibly) Effective

Kate Meersschaert

September 13, 2013

I know we have casually talked as a group about long-form writing site, Medium, and I thought I would share their newsletter with the team. Founded recently by one of the Twitter founders (Ev Williams @Ev) and created as an antidote to the "Tweet" mentality of communicating in quick sound-bites, Medium hopes to take blogging up a notch in both quality and dissemination. As a subscriber I receive their weekly newsletter of "top posts" and find both the simplicity and listing by popularity to be interesting. See screenshots below. What are your thoughts on the lack of imagery (or colors?)? Headlines catchy? Engaging?
