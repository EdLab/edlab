D&R Meeting: Feedback on UFR Teacher Activities

Janice Joo

February 5, 2013

Week 5: George Nantwi & Demetri Lales You will need the following: Since their last D&R meeting in December, the UFR team has created 4 activities for their teacher development course here. Please visit the link, and in your assigned groups, please help the UFR team by providing feedback on the following questions:

  1. How do you feel about the length of the lesson?
  2. What did you like about it?
  3. What didn't you like about it? What could be improved?
  4. Was it easy to grasp the main idea of the lesson? Were the activities easy to understand?
  5. What additional multimedia resources can be added to make the lesson more engaging?
Please feel free to leave some additional feedback too!
