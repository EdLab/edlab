Puppet Talk

Skye MacLeod

May 23, 2007

During the channel meeting yesterday I pitched the Puppet Talk segment as having two Educational Theorists (Dewey and Skinner) as the anchors of the show. They would initially be puppet reviewers for the channel (and perhaps introduce segments) then move on to interviewing special guests in a manner much like Crossfire. Upon discussing the benefits and drawbacks of producing such a show, we decided that having two old male puppets might not be the most interesting and/or engaging pair. So, I devised a list of possibilities I thought were interesting: Dewey vs Chomsky Skinner vs Grace Dodge Dewey vs Horace Mann Chomsky vs Piaget Skinner vs Steve Jobs Britney Spears vs Skinner Marvin the grad student vs Chomsky/Skinner Old Dewey vs Young Dewey If you have ideas of puppet possibilities, please post them. Also, I have attached a mockup of the set.
