Please Help Promote NLT in Obvious & Not-so Places...

Kate Meersschaert

September 4, 2013

Private! In an effort to support our NLT promotion efforts I wanted to share a case study. I am a member of the NY Edtech Women's group. The group has recently transitioned to Google+ to share news with one another. I am testing strategically sharing relevant articles with the group (see image below). Can you please also share articles in relevant forums, group listservs (be a bit prudent with these!) and across your social networks? Regardless, please start to think about were and how you discover new articles, interesting pieces to share on social media etc. I will lead our next D&R on NLT promotion and it would be great if you could take a moment to assess what you think of as successful social media promotion. For example: What are the most effective ways to promote our NLT content on Facebook? Twitter? LinkedIn? Groups on Google+? I look-forward to sharing more next Tuesday! Feel-free to add questions and comments below that we can address in our meeting together.
