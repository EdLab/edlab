D&R for Vialogues Closed Captions and Share Central beta

zhou

September 17, 2013

Hi Everyone! You can download today's presentation click here. For group activities: Group 1,3,5: Test uploading closed captions to Vialogues test server (http://vlg-cloud.tc-library.org/). To obtain CC, you can utilize YouTube if you own a video that has spoken languages because YouTube will automatically create transcriptions for your video. You can download both the MP4 and caption file (in .vtt format) from the Video Manager. For detailed instructions please see in the presentation file. Group 2, 4, 6: Try the Share Central and discuss how you would like to improve the design. Please draw out: * Your revised design of each tab. * Your minimal share options on the embedded player. Thank you!
