Paralyzed Woman Controls Robot Arm With Brain

Francisco.Mendoza

December 26, 2012

Technology's rapid advancement amazes me more and more everyday. Recently, a paralyzed women was able to feed herself with a chocolate bar using a robotic arm controlled by her brain. She is now able to move any item she wishes to with this new creation. The 53-year old woman from Pittsburgh is paralyzed from the neck down but she can move objects with her robotic arm at a speed comparable to able-bodied people. The development has scientists predicting that technology will continue growing and reach a point where it can be used to bypass nerve damage and reawaken paralyzed muscles. Do you think it could be possible in the near future for a paralyzed person to be able to walk again on their own after this new invention with hand movements?
