Vialogues Wins of the Week, 10.30.13

Kate Meersschaert

October 30, 2013

Private! Hi Everyone! Once again, Jo and I would like to share the week(s) "V-Wins", updates and ideas with you! Usage stats: New users: 67 # Vialogues: 59 Total visits: 5,656 Vialogues Sightings on the World Wild Web: Sonoma School of Education featured Vialogues as part of their "Tech Tips and Tools" series. Of it they say, "Wondering how to make a digital video more interactive? Vialogues is your answer." Vialogues user, Karen Fasimpaur, plugs it on the K12online conference keynote page. Scroll down to her comment (it's right under the comment of "Moving at the Speed of Creativity" blogger, Wes Fryer!) Elementary and middle school educator, Judy Mutzari, put together this nice prezo on how other teachers at her [Hebrew] school can use Vialogues [PPT]. ----Interesting to note that the one and only con she listed was that we require users to login with an email address to comment; she was concerned that this would be an issue with younger users.
