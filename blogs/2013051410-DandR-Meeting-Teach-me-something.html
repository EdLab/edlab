D&R Meeting: "Teach me something"

Janice Joo

May 14, 2013

Week 18: Dr. Sharon Hsiao You will need the following: EdLab is a learning organization, and as such, we should find ourselves sharing knowledge and ultimately teaching each other various processes. Today, we will get an opportunity to dust off our teaching skills and give feedback on one another's styles. If you walk out of today's D&R being a little more aware of your own teaching and learning styles, then you've met the goal! Activity 1. Pair up and teach something (absolutely anything) to your partner in 3 minutes. Your partner should observe, jot down notes, draw it out, take photos, etc. to visualize what and how s/he's teaching 2. Get feedback from your partner... then switch! (Your partner now teaches, and you will observe.)
