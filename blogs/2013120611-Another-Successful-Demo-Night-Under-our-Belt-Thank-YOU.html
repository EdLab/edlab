Another Successful Demo Night Under our Belt... Thank YOU!

Kate Meersschaert

December 6, 2013

Private! Event Recap EdLab's third demo night went-off without a hitch last-night! Thanks to the hard-work of EdLab team members (and Brian S.'s masterful co-presenting skills) the event was a success (happy, engaged visitors, no technical glitches etc.)! We had about 65 attendees (with comments like "what a packed house") and this felt like a nice number for the fifth floor space! The presenters did a masterful job of staying within a 5 minute(ish) range while fully engaging the audience and the mic-less self-moderated q&a seemed to go smoothly. I was impressed by the variety of roles represented by attendees and heard many guest comment that they were happy it wasn't the usual "edtech crowd." Finally, the food was yummy and plentiful, with special attention paid to both beer selection and cheese presentation. ;) Twitter-fest! Listed below is the Twitter feed from #EdLabDemos, however, I noticed that many people tweeting did not use this or misspelled some part of our # or @ so be sure to check some of the team's "power" Twitter accounts for more!
