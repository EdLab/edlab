Faster Forward! It's Time for EdLab Running Club!

LauraCostello

March 26, 2015

**please keep private** Get ready for it!! Our tentative schedule for this season is Monday/Thursday, departing the lab at 5:30pm. New members are very welcome! Our one and only rule is that nobody gets left behind, so there's a place on the team for everyone: never-runs, speed walkers, wannabes, walk walkers, speedy speed boys, run/walkers, run-for-the-bussers, haven't-run-since-the-last-ERC-runners, and (my people!) slow runners. We'll sweeten the pot with prizes too: After you log your first run, you get a sweet EdLab tech tee so you can #edlabrepresent in the park with us all summer! We also have a standing post-run popsicle tradition when it gets a little warmer. We kick off in one week (Thursday, April 2nd) so dust off your sneaks and get psyched! Let me know if you want to be added to the listserve!
