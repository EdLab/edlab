Using Technology to Engage Students in Environmental Learning

Laura Scheiber

June 10, 2013


In light of todayâ€™s environmental concerns, fostering a sense of social responsibility and ecofriendly ethos is more important than ever. The question is how, considering many kids would prefer to play video games than frolic in nature.

Educators are using digital devices in creative ways to engage students in environmental learning. This video shows students using technology to explore their local environment and collect data to contribute to environmental science knowledge. Among other tools, students utilize tablets, cameras, and data collection software to engage in STEM-related subjects and environmental learning. The learning process is particularly meaningful because the data that students gather is then utilized to address community environmental needs.
