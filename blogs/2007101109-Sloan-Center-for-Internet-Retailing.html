Sloan Center for Internet Retailing

Anthony Cocciolo

October 11, 2007

I'd like to share an R&D center that I think is doing really interesting work. I first bumped into the Sloan Center for Internet Retailing when the director emailed me for information on Second Look, a project I am developing that is a research platform for Second Life. I then bumped into the Sloan Center when I read this New York Times article about their new project, the eLab exchange, which is a "set of prediction markets that allows consumers to judge the likelihood of future events in the online retailing space and give their opinions about the best (or worst) aspects of online shopping." I then bumped into the Sloan Center for the class I am taking at CBS, where my professor recommended that I consult some of their research articles for my class project I am completing on Second Life. If you are interested in behavior on the Internet, check-out their site. BTW- they are now located at my undergraduate institution- UCR (go Highlanders!)
