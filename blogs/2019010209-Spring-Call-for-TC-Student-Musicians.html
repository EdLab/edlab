Spring Call for TC Student Musicians!

Jennifer

January 2, 2019

Come showcase your talents this Spring at the Everett Cafe! We sponsor live music by TC students during peak hours, Mondays through Thursdays, between 4 and 7pm. Performances are scheduled each week for an hour, and for all to enjoy, whether attendees gather informally on the stairwell or listen over coffee. Acoustic instrumentalists and vocalists performing classical, folk, jazz, R&B, bluegrass, traditional, pop, and/or world music are welcome! We take solos, duets, and trios, so please bring an accompanist or two to add some spice and liven up the sound. With thanks to a wonderful pool of talented musicians, here are examples of performances this past Fall. Scroll through the rhizr by clicking on the "next root" and also be sure to check out last year's performances, too. If you'd like to participate in the Everett Cafe Music Program, please contact us as soon as possible via online support with your details, including a short bio and music sample if you have one. Spots fill fast, so don't delay and let us get you on the schedule! The sound of Spring is in the air...
