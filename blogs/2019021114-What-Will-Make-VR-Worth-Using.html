What Will Make VR Worth Using?

Melanie.Hering

February 11, 2019

VR is a showy piece of digital technology promising a future of immersive experiences, both educational and entertaining. Yet, it's been around for nearly two decades and is still disappointingly expensive, awkward, isolating, and sometimes straight-up unimpressive. Why is this? I've been thinking about this as I try to find and test VR experiences on the HTC Vive we have here at EdLab, because while I have had maybe two spectacular VR experiences, the majority of what I've tried have elicited a shrug emoji from me. While I've seen AR improve at a pretty drastic pace, VR seems to be lagging behind. Tech Insider produced a video on why VR is struggling and it really resonated with me.
