Librarians at Comic Con!

raltvater

October 2, 2018

If you're going to New York Comic Con this year, be sure to stop by the Pop-Up Library hosted by the American Library Association's Graphic Novels and Comics Roundtable. I'll be volunteering at this booth for all four days, and I'm proud to announce that we'll be hosting all kinds of events ranging from readers' advisory to book signings, story times to crafting sessions. There will also be a browsable collection available on-site.


The outreach provided by the ALA bridges gaps between those who are new to the world of comics and a wealth of wonderful titles. It also starts conversations with library professionals about the importance of graphic novels and comics in libraries, information literacy, and society as a whole.


Hope to see you there!
