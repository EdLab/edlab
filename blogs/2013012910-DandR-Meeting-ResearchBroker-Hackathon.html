D&R Meeting: ResearchBroker Hackathon

Janice Joo

January 29, 2013

Week 4: Megha Agarwala and Janice Joo In line with EdLab's vision to support edtech entrepreneurs to not just do good, but do awesome in the space, ResearchBroker was developed to connect startups and researchers via an online platform. We believe a "hackathon" event will catalyze the mission behind the product. What do you think? Please provide feedback on the following questions: 1. List the pros and cons of the event format that you select. 2. How do we select startups?- Criteria 3. Where could we find researchers AND get them excited about this? Columbia University? 4. Audience? (Other than startups and researchers?) 5. Who should be the judges? 6. Sponsorship? 7. Cross promotion that makes the most sense 8. When is a realistic time to do it and WHY?
