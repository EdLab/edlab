A Partner for Edit Jams?

bhughes

August 29, 2007

Anyone interested in the future of Edit Jam should read this draft paper, The Future of Learning Institutions in a Digital Age. HASTAC appears to be a group with a mission near to EdLab's heart, and they are partnering with the MacArthur Foundation to offer grants through the Digital Media and Learning Competition. It seems to be a great opportunity to write a proposal and enter it into the competition, and especially with Edit Jam in mind. We could potentially get funding to do a year's worth (or more) of events to bring technology and learning to K-12 students. Ideas?
