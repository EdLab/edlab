Teachley at the EdLab Seminar

EdLab Studios

December 20, 2016


Better data and research create better apps. Better apps improve learning experiences for teachers and students alike. In the winter of 2015, Kara Carpenter discussed Teachley's alternative approach to rote learning which employs a unique blend of instruction, learning tools, feedback, and support within a series of fun, child-friendly games.
