The Need for Educational Reform in the Philippines

Bismark Appiah

August 7, 2013

I always feel blessed that I have gotten the opportunity to live in a well developed country like the U.S. The ample amount of opportunities in this country is what motivates people to succeed. Additionally, it is the reason why people strive to come to this country. This is the reason why I am determined to achieve all my goals and become the kind of person I wish to be in the future. In this vialogues video, children in the Philippines are struggling to obtain a better education because of the lack of resources and less government intervention. Some of these kids are just nine and ten years old, however, they are the ones that put food on top of their family's table at the end of the day. It is very sad that some of these children have to go through such a harsh living. This is the reason why we need to raise awareness to get people to help these children who are yearning to earn a better eduction and live a better life.
