Is Samsung Galaxy S4 Worth Buying?

Oumar Soumahoro

March 18, 2013

Samsung Galaxy S3 was the big sensation in the market during the summer of 2012 as it had better sales in the market than Apple's iPhone 4S. And once again, the Korean giant is ready to jump back in the market with its next big product, the Samsung Galaxy S4. ABC news gives us a first look at the upcoming phone scheduled to be in available to several carriers very soon. The Galaxy S4 is more like the Galaxy S3 except that certain features, like "eye scroll', "eye pause" and motion control have been added. Also, the phone has a wider screen which makes it more sharp. Is the screen resolution and a couple of more changes worth changing your phone to the Galaxy S4? What do you think?
