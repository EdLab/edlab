Quest to Learn

EdLab Studios

February 10, 2013


The Institute of Play is an organization that focuses on the design and implementation of games in learning. Their first project was the design and implementation of an innovative New York City public school, called Quest to Learn. At Q2L, teachers work with game designers to assess knowledge and to provide more engaging educational opportunities to students. Join the conversation at Vialogues.
