Future Technology Associates charged with fraud

Julia Martin

October 3, 2011

The Gossip Column comes close to home, and gets a bit serious. The company, Future Technology Associates, was granted a no bid contract by the city of New York for $250,000 for consultations though the company had no record. Against their contract, they created shell companies and subcontracted the work to Turkish companies. Over the last 5 and a half years, they've been given $74 million dollars. There is currently an investigation being carried out.
