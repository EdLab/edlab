No shelf required!

Yodit Kebede

December 15, 2010

In preparation for launching a new Patron Driven Acquisitions (PDA) service for electronic books in 2011, the library, in cooperation with leading e-book provider E-brary, is presenting a webinar to explain how this model of acquisitions works. All library staff members are encouraged to attend this webinar session and provide us with feedback. This new service will provide full access to patrons, and have the selected e-titles automatically added to our library's permanent collection, in real-time, based on usage.
