The Dutch re-take Manhattan

Anthony Cocciolo

January 6, 2009

Ever wish you had EU papers? I sure did. Well, according to a professor from Russia, he predicts that the USA will disintegrate around the summer of 2010. The crazy thing is the WSJ picked up the story, and I guess the Russians are really starting to dig it. Fun to think about though. Sarah Palin won't have to look out her window to see Russia- she will be in it! I wonder if I will need a passport to visits my parents in Alabama within the Republic of Texas. Oh, you have to love the Russians. Probably one of those examples of reading too much into geography. If things went down, I'd put Tennessee and Kentucky with Texas (and probably the Carolinas and W Virginia). And the South would NEVER be under the influence of the Mexicans (the Professor is clearly missing the... antagonistic relationship between the South and Mexico- they still think the Confederacy is coming back- really). I think I'd give NH and VT to the Canadians too- they would like that- we don't need the EU getting too big. The EU should take Florida too- beautiful beaches.
