NLT just underwent a huge change. You won't believe what happens next.

Brian.Sweeting

June 3, 2014

*Private* A few weeks ago you may have noticed that short article descriptions, or teasers, were added under article titles on NLT. We're hoping these will give just enough information to entice (but not bait) our readers. They can be: Short: (<65 characters) Simple: (incomplete sentences are A-ok!) Sweet: (aww) Intriguing: (because remember this?) Problem is: we have many older articles that are teaser-less. We need your help writing catchy tag lines for these stories! If you are or have been an author on NLT, please take a look at this doc and try your hand at writing some (as many or as few as the spirit moves you) teasers!
