The Tablet

Patrick Carey

January 5, 2010

"I think The Tablet is nothing short of Apple's re-conception of personal computing." This is a quote from John Gruber of Daring Fireball that ends this piece on the Apple Tablet. Gruber follows and knows Apple about as well as a non Apple insider can. His view of the Tablet is that it will not be an e-reader, mainly because Apple is not in the business of dedicated pieces of hardware, but instead a new tool for personal computing. If you read, or even skim, Gruber's post about the Tablet I wonder what you may think about the role of the e-reader in the future of online publishing? It looks like we will know exactly what the Tablet is and does by the end of the month, so let's have some EdLab speculation.
