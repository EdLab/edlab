Enter the Code

Nantwi

April 26, 2015


The rise of employment opportunities in technology jobs is changing the way techies are trained. Gone are the days when a bachelor's or advanced degree from a university was the usual route to a job in technology, especially software development. For-profit institutions have been springing up across the country to fill the growing interest. Organizations like the Flatiron School in New York City offer rapid and intense training for students who want to find a career in software development.

Admissions to the Flatiron School are very competitive even though students do not need prior coding experience. The result is a pool of eager and willing students ready to explore new educational and career opportunities.
