Using Web 2.0 in the Classroom

Kevin M. Gong

July 18, 2011

I recently stumbled across Ann Michaelsen's blog, titled Teaching English Using Web 2.0. A Norwegian high school teacher and administrator, Ann blogs about Web 2.0 use in classrooms, attends education conferences, outlines possible curriculums incorporating TED Talks, and even encourages her students to blog. We need more teachers like Ann who are incorporating social media and ICT into their classrooms. Make sure to take a look at her blog.
