D&R 2017/01/17: Elasticsearch (EdLab Super Search)

zhou

January 17, 2017

Today Xiang and I will give an update about the D&R's latest work on Elasticsearch (tentative name: EdLab Super Search). Below are the bullet points we will be covering.

Why do we conduct this project?

  • Need to better understand the relations of our data across all products
  • Need to better understand the user experience across all products
  • Research how to enhance the user experience by providing cross-domain, contextualized, and personalized learning resources

How EdLab Super Search can help us?

  • Data ingestion
  • Data reorganization and mapping
  • Data discovery and visualization
  • Data-driven decision making on how to elevate our products and services
