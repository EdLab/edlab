Goodbye & Thank You

Rebecca Beck

January 30, 2014

Private Please* Tomorrow will be my last day here at the EdLab and the library. It's hard for me to believe that I have been here 2 and a half years, I am so appreciative for my time here. I have learned so much and met so many smart, interesting and creative people. The EdLab and the Gottesman Libraries is such a special place - there has never been never a dull moment as my days were filled with service interactions, teamwork, seminars, exciting design activities, sharing our tools with the TC community and so much more. To me what is so special about the EdLab and the library is how everyone genuinely supports one another. Thank you for making my experience here a great one! I'll be at Am Caf tomorrow at 5 if you want to join me for a farewell drink!
