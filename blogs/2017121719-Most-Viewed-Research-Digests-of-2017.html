Most Viewed Research Digests of 2017

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2017


Throughout the year, our writers highlight the latest research on innovations in technology and learning. Brush up on the latest findings from 2017:

1. Early Math and Literacy Learning With Devices
Researchers ran an experiment with mobile learning integration in classrooms and found that math scores and literacy skills can be boosted through the use of this technology.

2. Effects of Social Media Usage on Academic Performance
No surprises in this research from Hong Kong: gaming, social media, and multitasking can hurt GPAs.

3. Remixing Storytelling
This two-year digital storytelling experiment illustrates how engaging students in the creative process can boost learning outcomes.

4. What Makes Teachers Ready to Introduce Mobile Devices into the Classroom?
Results from this large-scale survey show that age and experience can be key mitigating factors for teacher preparedness and confidence in integrating technology into the classroom.

5. Cognitive Training Gives Hope to Those with Dementia
Groundbreaking research suggests that cognitive training games can be employed to help fight dementia, giving hope to those who have been touched in some way by this debilitating disease.
