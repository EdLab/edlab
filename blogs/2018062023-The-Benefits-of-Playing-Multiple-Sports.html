The Benefits of Playing Multiple Sports

Abdoulie.waggeh

June 20, 2018

I recently read this article that talks about the benefits of playing more than two sports for teens. Two-sport athletes were found to be more likely to report having good grades and graduate college. I can relate to some parts of the article. I currently play college soccer. In high school, I played varsity soccer and basketball. Playing two sports really helped me develop certain skills especially time management. Some people might think it is not healthy to play two sports due to a risk of injury, lack of downtime, and enjoying being a teenager. However, I disagree because playing two sports always kept me busy during the school year, both in the classroom and on the field.


Playing two sports motivated me in the classroom as well. When I woke up in the morning, I had a schedule and a goal to archive before the end of the day and through this goal, I always completed my assignments on time, attend practice and go home. It is a routine that I became used to. Like everything else, once you get the hang of it, it becomes easier. Therefore, I encourage teens to try and play more than one sport if possible.
