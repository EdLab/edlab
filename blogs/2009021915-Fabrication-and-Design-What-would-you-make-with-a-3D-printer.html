Fabrication and Design: What would you make with a 3D printer?

Stephanie Hunt

February 19, 2009

So I've been catching up on 3D printer technology in an attempt to psych myself up about the cool educational opportunities that this technology would open up. I've found people all over the web basically saying that "...in X years, every classroom will have a 3D printer..." They seem to jump to the conclusion that if a 3D printer is in the classroom, then it will be used in fantastic ways. So I'm here asking you: What are those ways? If you had your very own 3D printer, what would you make? What types of things do you think kids would enjoy making? How can you imagine incorporating this type of fabrication device into the curriculum?
