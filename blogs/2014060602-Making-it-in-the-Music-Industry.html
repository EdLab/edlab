Making it in the Music Industry

Carmel Addae

June 6, 2014

It is extremely difficult to be successful in the music Industry. In addition to talent and ability, you also have to know the right people and make connections. There are heaps of talents all over the world, yet if you are not assisted by a person in the know about the music Industry, you can become very successful. Most people say that to make it into the music industry you need luck. Social media is a great tool for upcoming musicians, who may not have the connections, to showcase their talent. I came across an article that specifically talked about the four key factors needed to succeed in the music industry.

  1. Talented individual.
  2. Motivated to succeed.
  3. In need of good Marketing Knowledge.
  4. LUCK.
