For the Love of Marketing

Elysee Dougan

September 23, 2013

I am currently focusing on marketing and advertising as my college major as I find it to be a very interesting concept/profession. Many people in business and other areas suffer the weight of trying to market goods or services without having a proper knowledge of the steps to marketing. The most basic marketing step is the comprehension of the meaning of the word itself. Marketing is the action or process taken to publicize any product or services to your intended market. Be aware that such process includes research and advertising. Advertising in itself is an important step in marketing any product especially the platform(s) you use. The Internet has become a major marketing utility for many small and big companies. There are many businesses that took this initiative and grew into giants over other marketers adding their own ingredients (strategies) to the mix.
