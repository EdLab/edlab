Magical Microbes Makes Microbiology Manageable for Kids

Ryan.Allen

April 28, 2018


"It’s a microbe’s world, and we just live in it. Our innovative STEM kits leverage student creativity and ingenuity while exposing them to the magical world of microbes." (Magical Microbes)

STEM Experiments

Magical Microbes is a STEM-focused toy and experiment startup. Their products teach kids about the tiny but powerful world of microbes through interactive, hands-on activities. The founders hope that their learning activities will inspire students to think about science as part of the world around them, not just something that belongs in a lab.

Currently there are only three kits from Magical Microbes, each one reasonably priced, ranging from $25 to $65. They are all quite different and individually impressive:

  • The MudWatt kit introduces kids to bioenergy. They can make their own biofuel battery just by using soil found in the backyard.
  • The Dough Lab baking kit illustrates the gassy power of yeast. Students will learn how bread is actually made and will also get to eat their experiments.
  • BioGlo is an aquarium filled with bioluminescent microbes that absorb sunlight. Whenever kids shake the structure, the creatures emit a fascinating blue glow.

Educator resources.

The kits also come at a discount for block classroom purchase and are suitable for class science experiments. The company even has free curricula available on its website in both English and Spanish. Teachers can scale these project kits to their entire classrooms. There is also a community support page for those having trouble with any of the experiments.
