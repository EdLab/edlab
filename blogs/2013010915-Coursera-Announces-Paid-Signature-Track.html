Coursera Announces Paid "Signature Track"

Kate Meersschaert

January 9, 2013

Further proving that 2013 will be the year of the "monetized MOOC" or in my mind... MOOC back-lash, Coursera announces new paid certification. This new initiative is classified as a "Signature Track" and involves creating a more detailed profile (seems they should be paying YOU for that!). Do you feel that this extra step to verify that learners are actually learning will give MOOCs more credibility on the credentialing market? If you were an employer or institution, would you care? Why or why not? Perhaps Coursera could change their tagline from: "Take the World's Best Courses, Online, For Free" to "Take the World's Best Courses, Online, For a Fee."
