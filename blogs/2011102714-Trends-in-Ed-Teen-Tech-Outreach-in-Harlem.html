Trends in Ed: Teen Tech Outreach in Harlem

LauraCostello

October 27, 2011

The Hamilton Grange branch of the New York Public Library has recently opened a new tech-heavy teen floor. Teen outreach with technology in libraries is nothing new, but this Harlem branch offers community space in the form of a performance area and media viewing stations and plenty of computer access. Teen spaces have been a hot trend in libraries built or updated since the mid 2000's and teen services have evolved in an interesting and often controversial way.
