Best Time to Post On Twitter for Clickthroughs

Megha Agarwala

October 24, 2012

A study by Bit.ly Results: 1) Post early in the afternoon between 1-3 pm. Unless it is breaking news avoid tweeting important content after 8pm, specifically don't bother posting after 3 pm on Fridays, it seems that twitter doesn't work on weekends. 2) Twitter gets the most traffic 9am to 3pm, Monday-Thursday, tweeting when there is more traffic means more clicks, although it also means more competition, so optimize your posts considering the competition.
