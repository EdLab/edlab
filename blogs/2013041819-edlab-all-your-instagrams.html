#edlab all your instagrams

Janice Joo

April 18, 2013

Hey everyone! Let's start tagging all of our instagrams accordingly. #edlab seemed to be the most popular tag, followed by #edlabdemos. This way, we can have all EdLab-related photos in one place, perhaps even find some original material for NLT, and most important, we can keep each other in the loop. Looking for the best widget... SnapWidget seems to be the most customizable. We could either give the widget a permanent home on our blog (hm, unlikely) OR we could just post a new widget after a special EdLab event to show all the social media photos in one place.
