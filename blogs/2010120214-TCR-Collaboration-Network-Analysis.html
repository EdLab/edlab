TCR - Collaboration Network Analysis

Ankit Ranka

December 2, 2010

We plan to do a collaboration network study of Teachers College Record (TCR) based on this paper. As explained in the paper, we will make a collaboration network of all the authors in TCR. In this network, nodes will be authors and two authors will be connected in the network if they published a paper together in the past. Since there can be multiple publication between the same set of authors, the graph will be a weighed graph where edge weights will represent how closely authors collaborated over years. We plan to calculate following statistics from this network - Mean papers per author Mean authors per paper Collaborators per author Size of giant component (this shows overall connectivity of the graph) Second largest component Mean distance between authors Clustering Coefficient (how closely clustered are the author) We also plan to do a study of topics that evolved over the years as mentioned in this blog post. Stay tuned for more exciting statistics and results we come up with for TCR.
