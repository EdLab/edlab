3D Printshow

EdLab Studios

June 21, 2015


Years ago the concept of 3D Printing was something out of science fiction; a manufacturing and technological feat that would remain as exorbitant and inaccessible as an at-home nuclear reactor. Today, industrial additive manufacturing, desktop 3D printers, and 3D scanning technology are an integral part of education, business, and design in part because of events like 3D Printshow. Launched in 2012 as the first 3D printing-specific event in the world, today 3D Printshow brings the general public face-to-face with the latest in manufacturing developments and opportunities across Madrid, Berlin, London, Paris, Dubai, Amsterdam, Barcelona, California, and NYC. As part of 3D Print Week in New York City, founder Kerry Hogarth and her team welcomed students, artists, fashion designers, architects, makers, engineers, and dreamers to walk the four showroom floors and mingle with the biggest names and hottest tech in the 3D Printing community.
