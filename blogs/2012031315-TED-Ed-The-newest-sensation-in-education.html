TED-Ed: The newest sensation in education

jvazquez

March 13, 2012

The world's favorite big idea platform, TED, announced its newest video-based endeavor yesterday: TED-Ed. While TED's tagline is "Ideas worth sharing," the newest affiliation claims the similar, "Lessons worth sharing." This initiative pairs educators with animators to create stimulating lessons that are designed to incite the curiosity and imagination of students. Educators are encouraged to submit their best lessons to TED-Ed and will receive help in paring it down to less than 10 minutes. From here, the educator can make an audio recording of her lesson and send it off to the animation team. TED-Ed has put out calls for people to nominate educators, suggest a lesson, or nominate animators.
