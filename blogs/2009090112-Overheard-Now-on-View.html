Overheard Now on View!!

Molly Riordan

September 1, 2009

Overheard: An Insider's History of Teachers College is now on view in the 3rd floor Offit Gallery. Overheard was inspired by the TC101 challenge in which the Design Team sought to expose the untold tales and hidden histories of this institution from its inception until today. We culled the archives, ransacked the basement stacks, and collected oral histories from some of TC's prominent faculty and former students to orient new students and the existing college community alike to a different side of TC. Overheard features some distinct design elements that we're particularly excited about, allowing viewers to contribute their own stories. We invite YOU, EdLabbers, to go down, check it out, and be the first to write on the walls. I can tell you, it's quite satisfying. A special shout out to our recently-departed D-Team manager-extraordinaire Stephanie who led us toward the successful completion of this exhibit. We're thrilled with Overheard, and would love to hear your feedback. Stay tuned for more info about the Overheard audio tour and documentation of this project!
