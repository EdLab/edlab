Are Students Ditching Technology As a Major?

Kafoumba Doumbia

July 28, 2013

Most students, especially some high school graduates, fear science and technology careers due to the challenging situation it might lead them. Engineering is hard, however not impossible to pursue as a career. I believe that there is nothing easy in life. It's better to go through all the challenges and struggles while you are in college and be better off later in real life. Most students who graduate from college with a science or engineering degree will probably have thousands of loans but their contributions to society and the world can't be underestimated. Due to the scientists and engineers, we now have access to alot of information and we live longer than we did 20 years ago, so why not compensate them for their efforts and motivate others to follow the same path?
