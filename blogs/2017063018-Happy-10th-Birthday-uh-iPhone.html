Happy 10th Birthday, uh, iPhone?!?

dinosossi2

June 30, 2017


Are you 1? Are you 2?


Happy 10th birthday iPhone! It’s hard to believe that it has already been a full decade since the iPhone made its auspicious debut.


When a technology has the staying power and impact of the iPhone, it becomes an underlying assumption for your life and for others. Even for those who grew up before the advent of this iconic technology, isn’t it hard to imagine not having a smartphone in our lives?


Texting rather than phone. Finding any a map to your destination. Shooting a photo at any time. And, of course, my personal favorite: desperately looking for a place to charge your phone. Oh the splendor...


The affordances of technology become the predominant narrative. The very real negatives are pushed aside. And with something as dominant as the iPhone, there have been negatives.


Printed maps? Tucked away.


Pocket Kodak? 


Even a simple compact mirror? Too vain.


Maybe most insidious, technology is constantly in our presence wherever we go. To be unplugged is to not exist. I text, therefore I am.
