Knowledge base enhancements

Jamison Judd

April 1, 2007

Following our flurry of TSI project presentations, Dan and I spent the better part of last week updating the KnowledgeBase to address concerns that were raised. A summary of the new features is included below. We would really appreciate your feedback on the changes.

  • Adding articles/attachments
    Because most of the documents we had from library support were in Microsoft Word format, the KB has been modified to automatically convert Word files attached to a document to full readable text within the article (including images!). The full text of articles and attachments are also searchable.
  • Public and private folders and articles
    Each article and folder can now be set to either public mode (viewable by anyone) or private mode (viewable by library staff who have logged in). Making a folder private will automatically hide all of its contents.
  • Cleaner user interface
    We've streamlined the administrative interface, making it much easier and faster to add and edit articles. By removing extraneous fields, and including automatic Word conversion, articles can be added in as little as 30 seconds.
  • Predictive help suggestions
    To help harness the power of the KB, the support form shown at the meeting has been updated to offer live help suggestions (via AJAX) as you type in your question. This should further reduce the number of support requests for information that is easily accessible via the KB.
