EdLab D&R Meeting: mSchool Online Teaching Program Group Activity Notes

chingfu

November 5, 2014

Hi Everyone, Thank you for your great ideas and contributions at the D&R yesterday. For documentation, below are the notes from our group activities. Group Activity: We are designing an online teaching program here at mSchool to prepare the next generation of online educators. If you you are going to enroll in this mSchool program to learn about online teaching and course design (exclusive online, no face-to-face sessions) on a subject of your expertise, what kinds of learning experiences and training would you like to have? Some areas for your consideration:

  • course topics
  • modes of interactions
  • types of assignments that would be meaningful to you
  • forms of assessment
  • anything else
