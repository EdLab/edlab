2011 DML Trip Report #8

gnatriello

March 4, 2011

So how many would finally show up for our workshop? That is what we pondered as the start time drew near. We made contingency plans for all levels from 2 to 50 with elaborate calculations for each part of the workshop - intros, groups, teams, reporting. Thousands of calculations were run, and there were ultimately tens of thousands of scenarios that could evolve. In the end we had 11 people participating, and we broke them into pairs to discuss the roles that would need to be involved in an adaptive learning ecology. For the groups and teams exercises we used two groups. Those in attendance were terrific participants and seemed fully involved in the activities. It was a great opportunity to meet some new people who might become involved in our future projects.
