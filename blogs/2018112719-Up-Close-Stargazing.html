Up-Close Stargazing

Porsha.Monroe

November 27, 2018


Star Walk – Explore the Sky may seem like a simple stargazing app at first glance, but it is so much more. An extensive interactive astronomy guide, it stimulates curiosity about the galaxy all around us.

Upon opening the app, an ethereal soundscape begins to play as users navigate real-time positions of the entire solar system, including meteor showers, nebulas, stars, comets, deep sky circles, satellites, and more. Using GPS and motion tracking technology, device sensors pinpoint the accurate positioning of objects from any vantage point in space according to the location and movement of a mobile device.

Pros:

What sets Star Walk apart from other stargazing apps is its use of AR technology, and the breadth of its content, going beyond what many stargazing apps typically offer. With over 200,000 celestial bodies to explore, users can zoom in on objects to take a closer look at key visual elements in 3D, and then read to learn more about those objects as well. Students, science instructors, amateur stargazers, astronomy specialists, and astrology enthusiasts will appreciate the depth of detail available in Star Walk.

Cons:

The downside of Star Walk is the limited access it provides for unpaid use. While the app is free to download, many of its most exciting features are only available through an in-app subscription, for a one-time charge of $9.99, monthly charges of $0.99, or $0.42 a month for a yearly subscription. It can be quite frustrating to encounter a paywall when you’re curious to learn more about an interesting deep space object you’ve just stumbled across.

Our Takeaway:

As I explored the app, I found myself completely absorbed in the act of navigating through the night sky. Star Walk is both fun to use and educational, and with a beautifully polished interface, it boasts impressive visual features to boot. Allowing users to investigate celestial bodies at such a detailed level brings ease and intimacy to the process of learning about our solar system.
