President Obama on Immigration

Francisco.Mendoza

January 30, 2013

President Barack Obama recently gave a speech on immigration reform and his new plans on changing the immigrations laws. According to this article from News Works, President Obama informed illegal immigrants it might take time for them to become citizens but that there is an actual path to getting there. The President's speech comes on the heels of an election in which there was a strong Hispanic turnout in his favor. In addition to the speech, several senators including New Jersey's Bob Menendez are also laying out their plans for immigration reform. The President's plan was, as expected, met with some skepticism from some Congressmen. Republican Lou Barletta argued that a path to citizenship (or “amnesty” as he calls it) would come at an economic loss for the nation as it tries to recover from the recession. The President has called on Congress to propose a bill on immigration reform. Immigration looks set to dominate the president's second term agenda.
