Vote for your favorite Vialogues Tee! (+ add your ideas!)

Kate Meersschaert

March 1, 2012

Private! Jo led the Vialogues marketing team through two inspiring marketing meetings, with the goal of creating new promotional ideas for the platform. One of the initiatives I took-on was to help create a Vialogues tee-shirt. I envision the tee-shirt functioning in the following ways: 1) Vialogues "brand" awareness (@ gym, meetups, EdLab events etc.) 2) As VIP swag at events/presentations. 3) Mandatory for all interns 1X a week (kidding... sort-of!)! Some additional ideas for these tees from the group include: creating a word-scramble with the letters in "Vialgues," starting with a solid red tee w/white and/or black lettering, creating a fun cartoon around Remy The Vialogues Cat inspired by this cartoon-based design and finally, a tee inspired by Luke's comment on this vialogue. Here are my initial designs - please add your vote for a favorite and additional ideas in the comments below!
