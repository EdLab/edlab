Event: EDesign Lab Showcase & Meetup

Kate Meersschaert

June 12, 2013

Hi Everyone! Here is a potentially valuable event hosted by EDesign Lab (Read the New Learning Times article on EDesign Lab here) @ Alley NYC: Details: Date: Saturday June 15th from 2 to 5PM Location: AlleyNYC (500 7th Ave., 17th Floor, New York) RSVP here. There will be snacks! From EDesign Lab: This year's Labbers have been hard at work in our second Design Cycle. We'll have demo stations so you can try out our 3 new prototypes, and brief conversational presentations: -“Round Robin”: A web app facilitates the peer review process in writing -“Breadcrumbs”: An ipad app for reflecting on problem solving -“Because” - A web-app a real collaborative analysis of cause and effect. There will be ample opportunity for Q&A, and for you to share your thoughts/ideas. Equally, if not more important to what we make is our EDesign Lab community, so we invite you to be a part of it.
