GZA + Rap Genius + Prof. Chris Emdin = Science Genius!

Kate Meersschaert

December 13, 2012

Truly Real... Last-night's press launch of Science Genius "Battles" was truly epic. It stood-out as a unique event in that both young learners, rap legends and leaders in the science community came-together and got personal. Stories ranging from the Executive Producer of Neil deGrasse Tyson's popular "Star Talk" radio show, herself a talented scientist, sharing personal episodes that led to her love of nature, to GZA rapping re: his love of the subject. Teachers College Professor Chris Emdin (check-out this article on Emdin in New Learning Times) did a masterful job emceeing the event and even rapping himself. Overall, a truly inspired, inspiring and STEM-tastic event! For a re-cap check-out the #TCBATTLES Twitter feed embedded below. Rap Genius Connection Science Genius is a project funded by Rap Genius, who is in-turn a company invested in by Marc Andreesen and his crew.
