Talking Rapper, Making Rhymes Line-by-Line

dinosossi2

July 10, 2016


Talking Rapper is an interactive, social mobile app that introduces kids to the art of rapping.

Pros:

Talking Rapper is a neat and inviting app. Although I’m not very musical and a little afraid of the rigors of matching rhymes between lines, Talking Rapper’s avatar repeats what you speak into your iPad. By seeing someone else spitting your lines, you are slightly removed, feel a little less exposed, and become more apt to experiment.

This app automatically mixes in beatboxing and record scratching to give music to words. The background sounds place your words within a richer soundscape, keeping them from being spoken into a vacuum.

Cons:

Being a fun, open-ended app, Talking Rapper could be less useful for kids who need guidance. Although the musically inclined will love this app, those who are non-musical, shy, or just not good at expressing themselves orally may find this app frustrating. Similarly, the open-ended nature of this app may keep it from helping kids learn formal curriculum; more regimented learning seems to go against its underlying free wheeling, unstructured nature.

Our Takeaway:

Talking Rapper is a fun way to learn to express yourself musically and could also be a great tool to build young musicians' confidence. But for users who need guidance in their play or who just aren’t interested in music, this app might be put down faster than a mic drop.
