PocketKnowledge Report--June 2019

Nantwi

August 4, 2019

Here are some of the highlights from PocketKnowledge for June 2019:


-There were 86 new registered users. Most were non-Columbia affiliated (68). TC students (11), faculty (1) and staff (3), including library staff, along with Columbia affiliates (3) make up the rest.


-417 files were uploaded, 813 items were downloaded, and 1,560 tags were created


-There were a total of 5,180 sessions.


-The most downloaded title was The Absolutely True Diary of a Part-Time Indian. The item contains two files and was downloaded a total of 25 times. The item are lesson plans based on the book of the same name that has garnered controversy due to its graphic content. This might account for the interest in the item in PK.


Please let me know if there are any other interesting stats from PK you are interested in for July's report.
