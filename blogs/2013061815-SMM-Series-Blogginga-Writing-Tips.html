Hey everyone! Today's post on the SMM Series will be on Writing Tips for Bloggers. Yesterday, Brian S. showed me this useful blog that talks a lot about tips and tricks for social media and blogging things called Reviewz ‘n' Tips (thanks, Brian!). Blogging is one of the topics in my course, and I thought these writing tips from this blog would be great to use for blogging as well as for writing articles in general. You guys can definitely use these tips when writing for anything related to NLT, or even papers in school. Just to sum up the points, here are the main tips I took from it: 1. Don't write if you don't really have anything to say 2. Writing well comes with practice! 3. The more you read, the easier it will be to write 4. Don't worry too much about the length of the article 5. Learn and use new words 6. Take time between writing and editing 7. Read out loud (quietly) when editing 8. Listen to criticism and learn from it They may sound very simple, but I think most people overlook these rules! For myself, I find that Rule #7 works much better than you would expect, and I have yet to try Rule #6! What rules do you use/are going to try?
