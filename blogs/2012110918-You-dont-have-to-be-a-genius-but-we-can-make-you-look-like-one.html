"You don't have to be a genius, but we can make you look like one"

Janice Joo

November 9, 2012

Ideas from the market: SoGoSurvey.com I've been spending some time on Quora to find opportunities to talk about ResearchBroker and SurveySidekick. And in my stroll through Quora's site, I found a survey tool called SoGoSurvey.com. It's definitely not a streamlined interface like SurveySidekick, but let's be resourceful, and turn this fateful encounter into a learning experience. What can we learn from SogoSurvey, based on their demo video? 1. Intelligent reminders SoGoSurvey lets users control WHO gets the reminder, i.e. those who have not yet responded to the survey, vs. everyone with an invitation to the survey. As for SSK, the reminder is global to all survey invitees. How important is this feature to survey designers? 2. Visualized respondent analytics, even over years SurveySidekick plans to develop a visualized dashboard for users in future iterations... I just thought I'd throw this in here, and use this opportunity to dream about the future SurveySidekick. 3. Visualized reports SurveySidekick currently offers free download of raw .csv files. Would it be useful for SurveySidekick users to be able to create charts, etc, through SurveySidekick too? OR, here's an idea: SSK could teach its users how to transform their raw data into beautiful reports. We could have a special documentation section (or an mSchool course!) on data visualization alone. It could include links to online javascript chart software such as infogr.am or jqplot.... However great I think this idea is, the question always comes down to whether our end-user would find any of this useful... hm... ... SoGoSurvey's demo video neared its end, and the glass was still half-full for me... until they made the following statement as part of their closing remarks (screenshots below), "You don't have to be a genius... but we can make you look like one."
