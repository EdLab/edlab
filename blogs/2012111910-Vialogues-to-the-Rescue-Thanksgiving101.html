Vialogues to the Rescue! #Thanksgiving101

Kate Meersschaert

November 19, 2012

Here's a Vialogues experiment... lets use the platform to learn how to make a complete Thanksgiving meal! When I was in college in Arizona, I couldn't fly home to PA and lived in a dorm with a kitchen. Soooo I innocently decided to cook Thanksgiving dinner for all of the "geographically undesirable" souls left-behind by expensive airfare. Listed below is the result... and a possible intervention for those left in a similar situation this Thanksgiving! Step One: (This dates me...) Wait for Martha Stewart's cooking show to air on dorm TV. Watch. Take notes. Step Two: Call mom. Step Three: Bike to grocery store and stock-up. What's marjoram? Onion vs Shallots? Step Four: Start to cook. Repeat steps 2-3. Step Five: Attempt to carve a turkey with a pairing knife and serve pumpkin pie with a spoon. Sigh. SOLUTION! Vialogues to the rescue! I am in the process of creating a series of Vialogues that will teach you how to make a complete Thanksgiving feast! Please feel-free to join-in the Turkey Day fun and find your favorite recipes broken-down to add. Please just be sure they are beginner friendly... and add #Thanksgiving101!
