Critter 0.52 Released - Comment stats, replying to comments and more

fanwar

May 26, 2010

** Keep private ** We've just released another iteration of Critter: http://critter.tc-library.org/ Here are some key features/improvements for this version: 1) The ability to reply to other people's comments has been added. 2) Access lists for videos and discussions has been improved, with a smoother UI. 3) Comment statistics have been incorporated, so users in a discussion can see which parts of a video are most commented. 4) We've added support for retaining video quality when it is uploaded, so high quality videos remain high quality videos after they are processed by the Critter upload system. 5) We've fixed other minor bugs and issues people have been reporting. We are eager to find a few partners both inside of TC and outside to work with us to use Critter on a trial basis. If you have ideas of groups or organizations that we can reach out to, please do get in touch with me.
