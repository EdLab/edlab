A Farewell to EdLab

Andrew Jarcho

May 29, 2015

-- please keep as private; thank you -- Dear EdLab, You are a wonderful group of people. It has been a real pleasure and an inspiration to work with you and among you for the past year. I wish you all good fortune, happiness, and success in every endeavor -- now and in the future. On my last day at EdLab there were people to whom I didn't get to say goodbye, and this I very much regret. If anyone wants to get in touch, please feel free to email me at andrew.jarcho@gmail.com, friend me as Andrew Jarcho on FaceBook, or link up with me on LinkedIn. With great affection, Andy J.
