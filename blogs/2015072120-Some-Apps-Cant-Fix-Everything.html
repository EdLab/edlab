Some Apps Can't Fix Everything

Rebecca Rubenstein

July 21, 2015


The Crazy Gears app encourages kids to get into a mechanical engineering mindset with colorful gear shapes that guide them through the process of fixing a bike, but can the experience of a touch screen really simulate the learning that takes place by getting your hands dirty? It may be a step above a paper manual, but is it better than old-fashioned hands-on learning?
