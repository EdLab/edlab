InstaEDU

Demetri Lales

July 18, 2013


InstaEDU connects learners with tutors and facilitates study sessions online. Users can scroll through a list of tutors who teach a diverse set of traditional school subjects. The long list of tutors is augmented with information to help learners choose the right tutor for them. This set of information includes a thumbs up and down rating system, background information, user reviews, sample explanations, and a list of teaching subjects. Learners message tutors to schedule time for a study session that takes place on the site through a webcam. Before users commit to a study session they can view useful data such as the likelihood of response and expected response time. The online study space includes video and text chat, a whiteboard to draw out explanations, and a collaborative text editing space.

Pros:

The site gives learners access to useful information that can help them make better decisions when choosing a tutor. The most interesting parts of the tutor profile are the sample explanations. Users are able to view exactly how each tutor answered real student queries in the past. This can give learners a good preview of how the tutors will help them in a study session. Tutors are reviewed by the students they have tutored in the past which can give prospective students a better idea of how well they teach.

Cons:

A subscription is required for full access to tutors and the online study space, which may not be affordable for some learners.

Our Takeaway:

InstaEDU streamlines the tutoring process by providing an easy way to find the right tutor and hosting the study session in one online platform. The subjects taught on the platform are not limited to traditional K-12 school subjects and also include some higher education subjects like engineering, programming, and astrophysics. With more students-per-teacher in traditional and online courses, unique methods for individual learning will be in demand for learners who want a full grasp of a subject. It will be interesting to see whether tutors on the site diversify to teach subjects that are outside the realm of traditional schooling such as social media marketing.

The Bottom Line:

InstaEDU allows you to find the perfect tutor.
