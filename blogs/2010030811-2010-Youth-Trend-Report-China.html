2010 Youth Trend Report: China

Ting Yuan

March 8, 2010

mobileYouth has partnered with enovate, which just opened a new office in Shanghai, in developing the latest 2010 Global Youth Trends Report about China. As reported, by 2015, there will be 500 million people under age 30 in China—roughly the population of the entire European Union. The report is written based on data collected from the two organizations' close interactions (direct market research, social media participation, trend analyses) in China with China's emerging consumer (15-30 years old) segment. The report highlights key trends affecting China's emerging consumer class in the following areas: * Technology Trends * Social Trends * Regional Trends * Media Trends * Marketing Trends

Read the full report here.

The report includes three key emerging Chinese consumer trends:
1. Youth Still Crave International Brands but Want It Their Way – Will This Be Exported Back Out of China? 2. Online and Offline Interactions Amongst Chinese Youth Expanding While Also Fragmenting. 3. Multi-versatile and Integrated Companies, Technology and Media.
