Social Class Differences in Access to Higher Education in China

Ting Yuan

November 3, 2009

Prof. Weiyi Wang is currently a visiting professor from Fujian Normal University invited by the Center on Chinese Education of Teachers College. He gave a speech this afternoon at TC about his recent study entitled Social Class Differences in Access to Higher Education in China. This study investigated the social classes among over 11,000 freshman students in 51 colleges and universities in China. It concludes with interesting findings regarding the variation of ten social classes among college students.
