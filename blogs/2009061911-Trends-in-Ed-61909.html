Trends in Ed 6.19.09

Calli Kosch

June 19, 2009

People to People Ambassador Program People to People, a travel ambassador program dedicated to promoting cultural understanding and peace, is a means of providing youth with first-hand international awareness. Even with the current downturn in the economy, this program has received an array of applicants for its Summer 2009 trips to places like Australia, China, and the United Kingdom. A recent article sites parents' commitment to an "investment" in their children's futures as the key to steady enrollment in the People to People Students Ambassadors Program, available for students in grades 5-12. Aside from the value of such an experience in an increasingly globalized world, the application itself proves a learning experience in that it requires materials commonly necessary for college applications. The FAQ page explains that each applicant must submit 3 letters of recommendation and be interviewed. Some students even submit a video for a video contest, documenting their intentions and motivations for participating in the program: All in all, their mission is to "achieve peace through understanding."
