Right to Dream Academy

abagigah92

October 31, 2013

Right to Dream is a youth development program in Ghana that combines soccer with education. Education is seen by many experts as one of the ways to improve lives for those in developing nations. Soccer is also seen by some as their way out of poverty though the number who actually makes it as professional are very few. Right to Dream's platform offers both as youth in the program receive a very good education and those who are talented enough do end up playing professional soccer upon completion. The members who do not make it as professional soccer players end up with soccer scholarships to study in Europe or America. The long term vision is to offer underprivileged children who are drawn from a background of extreme poverty the opportunity to reach their true potential in life through sports and education and ensure that these children are motivated and empowered to make meaningful, lifelong contributions to their communities.
