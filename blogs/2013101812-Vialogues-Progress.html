Vialogues Progress

Bismark Appiah

October 18, 2013

Part of my job here at the EdLab is to promote Vialogues, which is recruiting users to sign up and engage in a conversation around the videos I am creating or any other video. On Vialogues, the set of videos that I have decided to create focuses on raising awareness about countries struggling to provide education for their citizens or developing a good a educational system. After the last meeting, the Digitization group had a meeting with Hui Soo and George and this has been my progress with Vialogues so far. I have been able to recruit six people since then and posted about five videos. Initially, my intentions were to recruit active members who would comment on these videos all the time because the gist of Vialogues is to have people sharing their thoughts and views about videos they are watching. However, I have come to realize that I have to do my utmost best and not focus specifically on recruiting active members only.
