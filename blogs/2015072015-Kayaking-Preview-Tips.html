Kayaking Preview + Tips

christophergu

July 20, 2015

* PRIVATE * This past Friday, Laura, Kim, Mason, and I checked out the Skyline Kayak Tour at Manhattan Kayak Company. It was an incredible experience and a great workout as well! Here are some tips from our excursion: - When you first arrive at MKC, there is a changing area with lockers for you to store your personal belongings. - It is ideal to wear shorts and short-sleeve clothing as you will get wet in the open-faced kayaks. - It is also highly recommended to bring a change of clothing. - Also, don't forget your sunscreen, hats, sunglasses, etc. You will be on the water during the midday heat. - Before you get on the water, the guide will give a brief 10 minute orientation on how to use the paddle to maneuver the kayak. Pay close attention here!! - Once you get settled in the kayak, you will have an opportunity to practice your paddling for about 10-15 minutes before you start sailing into the Hudson as a group. - This is a beginner-level class, but it can still be a bit challenging if you are not comfortable navigating in the water.
