A Facebook Flavored Favor!

Kate Meersschaert

October 17, 2014

Private!!!! Hi Everyone! I have a semi huge favor to ask of you! Can you please invite friends with relevant ties to the education/learning world to "like" our NLT page on Facebook? I realize this may seem like an imposition, but, I also know that we create highly relevant, interesting content everyday that educators in your networks may appreciate. (You don't have to invite everyone!*) Hopefully, your ed contacts already have accounts and receive our newsletter, BUT, lets test whether or not people comment on, provide suggested content or simply "like" our content on Facebook! Previously, the NLT Facebook page was set so that only friends of friends could "like" content... I have now set us free and allowed anyone to "like" and comment on posts! Lets see what happens! Thank you for your help as we tighten-up and evolve our social media presence! *
