The Fiercest Poem About Teaching You’ll Ever Hear

jvazquez

January 28, 2014


Taylor Mali is a proud teacher and a talented poet. In this powerful spoken word, he poignantly articulates an answer to the kind of question that would tie the tongues of most others put in his position. He enters a zone of perfect eloquence as he weaves through tales of interactions with students. The entity of "Mr. Mali" comes through loud and clear: his acerbity may strike a chord of fear in your childhood self, but ultimately you know he’d be the kind of teacher you remember fondly.

Mali exudes the kind of energy that can only stem from a deep-rooted passion. He crafts a story so masterfully that every character is seen in vivid display, even though they pass by in an instant. He is the teacher you wish you had (or wish you could have again). From where does his passion spring? How can we bottle it and save it for future generations of teachers?
