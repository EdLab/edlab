Teachers Gear Up for Science Standards

abagigah92

May 29, 2013

Math and Science continues to be the hardest subjects for students in our public schools and the students in the United States are falling behind when compared to other countries. Teachers have been making great efforts to improve the vision of science education and bring it to the classrooms. This can be done by improving the standards and expectations. The new standard asks teachers to have depth in understanding the subject and applying knowledge through the practice of science inquiry. Interactive learning and applying theories to real life through investigations and activities are many ways teachers are improving science education from grades K to 12. The Next Generation standard finalized in April, was developed through a coalition of 26 states. The standard lays the groundwork to help teachers' through training and professional development opportunities.
