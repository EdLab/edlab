Teenage Mutant Ninja Robots!

Ryan.Allen

December 12, 2017


Nunchucks are an ancient weapon that can take years to master. Often associated with Michelangelo from the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, we may soon start identifying them with robots. A team of researchers recently built a robotic arm that has mastered this weapon.

The team first programed a flow chart of how to perform a complex nunchucks move. Next, a human performed the trick for the robot. Using motion capture alongside the other data, the mechanical arm was able to emulate the complex move through practice.
