AERA the Jo way

jvazquez

April 16, 2009

Presentation day! I Admittedly, I was more nervous than I wanted to be, but the room was small enough so I figured not many people were expected to come. To my surprise, the room filled up rather well the closer we got to curtain time. I think the SIG was interesting to a lot of people (it was "Use of video, television, technology, and print media in schools and impacts on students"). Although I was a big ball of nerves, I said everything I wanted to without looking at my notes, so I am going to call this one a success :) I received some feedback: a few suggestions for further research and a few comments. If we decide to continue to develop this research topic, I think it would be a good idea to incorporate their suggestions. The other presenters on my panel were all great. One presenter made me a fan of HBO's "The Wire." Another made me want to start creating my own works of cinematic art. AERA is a great networking event. I've made some new friends here. Maybe it's the palm trees and the weather... but I'm loving the friendliness :) AERA 2009: mission complete.
