What Kind of jobs will be Available When You Graduate?

rendy

November 20, 2013

According to a recent study released by the global outplacement firm the global outplacement firm Challenger, Gray & Christmas, Inc., employment will rise during the years 2018-2025. There are a number of occupations expected to increase in salary and visibility during this period. Some of these are: Big Data, Physical Therapy, Teachers, Medical Technicians, Sales & Marketing, and Research & Development. The highest percentage of projected growth is Physical Therapy with 46% and a median salary of $52,160 and the lowest is Research & Development with 9% projected growth and a median salary of $80,580. In the middle, they have Big Data (31%, $77,080), Medical Technicians (44%, $65,860), Teachers (17%, $53, 400), and Sales & Marketing (41%, $60,300). I wonder what effect this projection will have on current and soon to be college students in terms of choosing a major.
