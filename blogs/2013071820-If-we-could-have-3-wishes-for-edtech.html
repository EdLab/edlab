If we could have 3 wishes for edtech...

Demetri Lales

July 18, 2013


In recent years, startups have made efforts to bring new technologies to the education sector, but many startups still struggle to find the golden ticket into the classroom. Stanford researcher Vanessa Vega offers some guidance in the form of three "simple wishes" for education technologies that offer frequent personalized feedback, allow students to create their own media, and connect the classroom to the outside world.
