Edlab Review: ScienceMadeSimple

Chandler Kang

February 12, 2010

sciencemadesimple Tags: science, project, experiment, video http://www.sciencemadesimple.com Overall: 2.31 Design score: 1.86 out of 5 Pedagogy score: 2.75 out of 5 [The Good] The site help teacher or parents guide students start science project and approach to it systematically. Therefore, it helps students discover scientific concept or principles by themselves. [The Bad] Once they are selling the product, they did not uncover the product itself, but to support it, they uploaded many articles that are related to phenomenon that students curious about. Also, it is a stand alone platform so that it is lack of collaborative chance with peer. [The Bottom Line] Research is getting expanded to be interdisciplinary and there are more to be considered. Knowledge is burgeoning from interaction with other, too. Therefore, this stand alone platform learning tool is appropriate only for dealing with a simple aspect or a phenomenon. Read Full Review
