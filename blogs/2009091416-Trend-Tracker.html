Trend Tracker

Jeannie Crowley

September 14, 2009

The Trend Tracker tracks and graphs tweet trends from Twitter. The tool allows you to view up and coming trends as new keywords begin to gain in frequency. One very cool feature is the trend map that shows you how trends are emerging over time across the globe. While this application is limited to Twitter, it does have the potential for broader use.
