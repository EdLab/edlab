UFR in NCSS Conference Update

Demetri Lales

December 2, 2011

Hello from Washington D.C. everybody! George and I are taking a little break so we can blog and tweet about our progress in the NCSS Conference. We returned to the convention center at 8:30 this morning to continue promoting the UFR website. The first task was to help Professor Marri, Scott and Michael with their presentation on the UFR site by giving out handouts, and passing along our invitation cards! There was a huge turnout at the presentation with over 80 people present. There was more people then expected so the twittering, fliers, invite cards must have been very effective. Many people were interested in what the presenters had to say and in the site and curriculum itself, so I believe our this weekend will be a big success. Of course our work is not done yet, after this blog posting George and I, the UFR guys, will be promoting UFR all around the Washington D.C. area. We will also be tweeting more of the posters and one-liners whenever we can, and keep the twitter active even after this weekend since it seems to be very effective in increasing our following. We have gain some more followers some of them are companies like EDU Online Company, and McGrawHill Education. This will be it for now but expect a full update later tonight, including a story about our adventure with Occupy Washington.
