Data Driven Research Group at Edlab

Megha Agarwala

January 10, 2012

Moving on with our efforts around data driven research at Edlab, Manav, JoJo, HuiSoo and I recently met to discuss the Edlab research agenda and form an exclusive data center group at Edlab. We figured there could be the following categories for us to consider when analyzing data: 1) User Interaction (Type of interaction: What are users doing) 2) User Experience (Quality of interaction: Why are users doing what they are doing) 4) Learning (Do our systems help in learning) 2) Strategy (Long term goals and strategies to collect data) We imagine the data center group at Edlab to drive the following things. 1. Clarify what data are we currently collecting for each Edlab App. 2. Create Research Questions on what additional data we should collect. 3. Put together a list of publications we should target for 2012. 4. Create resources on educating people on data analysis, data research, tools to conduct data research etc. If you have any other ideas on what the data group should do or if you would like to contribute to the group in any way, please let us know!
