Grockit Founders Launch Learnist

Kate Meersschaert

May 23, 2012

Pinterest for learning... The founders of social test prep site Grockit have announced an invite-only version of their new venture... Learnist. Learnist will combine social curation with a niche-focus on learning and knowledge acquisition. Topics featured on their bold, Pinterest-esque splash page include: travel, fitness and social science among many others. Initial thoughts... The initial invite process is a bit daunting with a rather "explicit" caution featured on the invite request pop-up message. However, despite this initial sign-up experience, I am excited to see whether or not Learnist lives up to their claim to be the "Future of Learning"! Education could use more real-world solutions that are both idealistic and realistic and Learnist just might be both!
