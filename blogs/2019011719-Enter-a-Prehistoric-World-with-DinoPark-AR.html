Enter a Prehistoric World with DinoPark AR+

Porsha.Monroe

January 17, 2019


Upon opening the app, users enter the gates to DinoPark, a 3D mixed-reality island that transports users to a prehistoric Jurassic world. Dinosaurs roam around open fields as users navigate through an interactive map of the island, discovering various prehistoric creatures that exist there. While navigating, users place prehistoric creatures on any surface in front of them, scaling them down to become miniatures or scaling them up to mimic life-sized recreations. Using a tracking function, users walk around while watching dinosaurs perform unique actions, or they can view details of a dinosaur's skeleton. Interactive information tags allow users to learn about their most notable features.

Pros:

DinoPark AR+ brings T-Rexes, stegosauruses and more back to life in augmented 3D, while the island escape concept also piques interest in exploring a Jurassic world.

Cons:

The learning content included in the information tags felt a bit superficial, not covering much beyond the basic facts. DinoPark is marketed as an education app, so I would expect that its learning content would be more in-depth. With the addition of detailed facts about the evolution and extinction of each creature, DinoPark could be an engaging complement for younger students studying the prehistoric era.

Our Takeaway:

For a free AR app that brings pretty realistic renderings of dinosaurs to your mobile devices, DinoPark is a fun experience for users of all ages.
