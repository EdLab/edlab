Ziegfeld Collection of International Children's Art

veronicarodriguez

May 2, 2013

The Ziegfeld Collection of International Children's Art is an absolute knockout collection of drawings! It is nearly impossible to select a highlight or two (or three!) to share but I will try my best. The Collection was started by TC professor Edwin Ziegfeld in the 1950s and grew to contain around 350 works of art made by children aged 10 to 18. The countries represented range from Portugal, Germany, South Africa and more. There are so many wonderful images to share from this collection, none of which feel forced or self-conscious which is evident in the confident mark making each artist set out to create with.
