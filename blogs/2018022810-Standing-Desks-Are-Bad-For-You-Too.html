Standing Desks Are Bad For You, Too

jvazquez

February 28, 2018

According to research recently published in the journal Ergonomics, standing for prolonged periods of time may have negative consequences on your health and productivity. 


In the study, 20 participants withstood 2-straight hours of "standing computer work". Researchers found that during this time, participants experienced physical discomfort in the form of muscle fatigue and lower limb swelling. 


But it's not all bad news. While reaction time and mental state worsened, creative problem-solving abilities improved.


Here's the article. 


So, sitting all day isn't great for your health and it looks like standing isn't much better. But what's the alternative? Researchers suggest taking frequent, short walks throughout the day.
