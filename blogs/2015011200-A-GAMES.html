A-GAMES

Youssef Ballo

January 12, 2015

Video games bring new tasks in the education field and also positive potential to its entertainment value. Video games represent few techniques that may be available to the classroom teachers. A-GAMES (Analyzing Games for Assessment in Math, ELA/Social Studies, and Science) is a collaboration between New York University and the University of Michigan. A-GAMES believes that games are very important because it impacts the learning experience and the relationship between teachers and students. Formative assessment is a term use when it comes to games in the classroom. The goal of formative assessment is to measure progress through instruction. According to a professor, “Formative assessment is thought of as one of the most important classroom practices to support student learning.”
