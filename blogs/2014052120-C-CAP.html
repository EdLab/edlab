C-CAP

EdLab Studios

May 21, 2014


Home Economics. The name evokes a different time in the public school system. A time when perhaps a cooking class could be valued as an important life skill. But times have changed. Home Economics has all but disappeared and students are left with one less offering in the public system. C-CAP (Careers through Culinary Arts Program) has partnered with schools and teachers to address this gap. C-CAP selects interested students to be placed in great restaurants as interns to successful chefs in order to gain experience in the restaurant industry. Students are also matched to scholarships based on a two hour cooking competition. With experienced chefs and judges providing valuable feedback and assessment to these aspiring chefs, C-CAP is providing futures to students that would otherwise be unimaginable.
