NLT Multimedia: Treemaps to Navigate NLT Archive

Janice Joo

June 13, 2012

Inspired by ted.com... LF: Feedback.... What do you think about navigating old NLT archives through a visual interface (i.e. treemap mockup below) according to some simple search parameters. "Treemapping is a method for displaying hierarchical data by using nested rectangles. [wiki]" The parameters in the mockup are "time" (2012 to today) and "Best" (which could mean "most views", or "most likes"). Would a visual interface such as this promote users to engage, or would you rather have a search bar where you would type in keywords? Or both?
