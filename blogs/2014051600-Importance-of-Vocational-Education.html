Importance of Vocational Education

Jeffrey Hado

May 16, 2014

Vocational education has been on the decline in recent years. The opposite is happening in countries like Finland. According to Tony Wagner, author of The Global Achievement Gap and creator of Innovators, 45% of students choose to move on to a technical field and not an academic field after completing their basic education in Finland, which has a highly successful educational system. The decrease in vocational training in America comes at a time when many job openings are in the vocational field. One of the recommendations to increase vocational education is provide students with the opportunity to be trained in different skills. It should become part of the curriculum like other subject areas such as math and science. Should we focus more on vocational schools? What are some pros and cons of vocational education?
