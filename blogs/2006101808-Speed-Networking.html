Worth Chapter Meeting - October 2006 Date: October 18, 2006 at 11:30 am Speaker: Patrick Sweeney CEO of ODIN Technologies http://www.odintechnologies.com/ Author of "RFID for Dummies" http://www.dummies.com/WileyCDA/DummiesArticle/id-2988.html Meeting Theme: RFID Implementation at the US Department of Defense (DOD) RFID Tribe Meeting Agenda: 11:30 Registration and Networking Pick up your name badge at the registration desk. Visit with RFID Tribe members and guests. Trade business cards and make connections. 11:50 Lunch Eat lunch and enjoy informal discussion with RFID industry participants. 12:10 Welcome and Introductions - Mark Johnson, President, RFID Tribe 12:15 RFID Implementation at the US Department of Defense (DOD) Patrick Sweeney, CEO of ODIN Technologies 1:00 Collaboration Time (Team up with others) Let Tribe members know of your needs and to discover member needs - partnership development, funding sources, technical expertise or connections with key players. Make a 30 second announcement to let the group know your needs and hear the group's announcements. Then find those with whom you want to work. Meet exactly who you want to meet. Speed networking at its best! Great way to make connections with those who have a similar interest, to uncover opportunities and to create deal flow. 1:20 Adjourn
