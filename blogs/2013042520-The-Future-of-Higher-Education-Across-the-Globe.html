The Future of Higher Education Across the Globe

Laura Scheiber

April 25, 2013


It’s an exciting time for higher education, according to Professor Caroline McMillen of the University of Newcastle in Australia. She predicts that universities will form partnerships across the globe on an unprecedented scale in order to maximize their capacities to solve large global challenges. She also believes that new teaching technologies are being embraced by higher education institutions, which encourages learning outside of the traditional classroom. She highlights some of these trends as they impact the future development of the University of Newcastle.

Do you agree or disagree with Professor McMillen’s assertions? Let the world know your thoughts on Vialogues.
