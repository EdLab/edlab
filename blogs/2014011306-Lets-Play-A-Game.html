Lets Play A Game

Elysee Dougan

January 13, 2014

There is a lot of controversy when it comes to how video gaming affects the human mind. This controversy has led to experiments on avid video gamers. The results of the experiences show both a positive and negative effect on the mind. For the positive aspect of gaming on the mind, cooperation and teamwork was seen among gamers who play multi-player games, as well as having a higher level of perseverance and a strategic mindset. As for the negative influence of gaming on the human mind, the subjects who play alone showed a deal of isolation and a lack of social skills. An increase in aggravation and academic decline has also been recorded in these people, as well as an increase in attention problems. More positive and negative results here.
