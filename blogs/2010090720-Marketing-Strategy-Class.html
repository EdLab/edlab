Marketing Strategy Class

pramnab1

September 7, 2010

The four P's of Marketing - Product: the product is selling - Place : whether it's online - Price : the price at which a product is selling - Promotion : let the consumers know about direct marketing, the new marketing strategy - Customer value (Product) - Cost to the customer (Price): Shipping charges, - Convenience for the buyer (Place) - Communication Positioning and Targeting Position is - Differentiation from Competitors - Value proposition- what do you offer? - Product Dimensions - What do you do? Targeting is - Demographic Segments

  • Gender
  • Age
  • Income
  • Education
  • Location
  • Marital Status
  • Culture
- Audience Philip Kotlet - if you nail positioning and targeting, the rest falls into place needs, requirements, psychology - how consumers think - know your audience, people and the target
