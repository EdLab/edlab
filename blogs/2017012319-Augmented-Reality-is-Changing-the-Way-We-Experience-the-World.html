Augmented Reality is Changing the Way We Experience the World

saraehardman

January 23, 2017


Augmented reality is changing how we experience technology, and John Hanke, CEO of augmented reality (AR) company Niantic, thinks that it will change the way we experience the world. Hanke mentions that until recently, using technology such as computers or TVs was a stationary activity. He says that technology used to require us to be tethered to a screen, but AR lets us reimagine that.

AR can come in many different forms, including audio cues, popup videos, and smart watches. It allows users to experience an overlay of information over the real world, creating a digital enhancement of reality. Niantic has created two successful AR apps so far, including Pokémon Go and Field Trip. Pokémon Go, in particular, highlighted AR’s potential to foster collaboration and communication among people as they experienced a digital world together.

How can AR change how we experience the world? Do you think it has greater potential to foster or weaken relationships? What learning potential does AR carry with it? Answer these big questions and more on Vialogues.
