Digital Learning Series Event at GA

Kate Meersschaert

September 26, 2011

I highly suggest this Digital Learning series co-sponsored by Startl & General Assembly! I attended the last event and learned so much from the group of ed tech-focused attendees and most of all, from the quick, 5-minute pitches of the presenters. Many of these start-ups had either just presented seminars here at EdLab (LearnBop, Re-Vinyl, Hoot.Me etc.) or will present in the near future (Socrative) after being recruited from this last event. Despite the slight redundancy, it was informative to see how these new companies vary their presentation based on audience and how they present in a short-form pitch-like setting as opposed to our hour-long seminar format. Highly recommend for anyone who wants to refine EdLab product pitches or is curious about new ed tech start-ups! This event is at General Assembly on 10/5 from 7-9 and I would sign-up ASAP as tickets are $10 and the last event was standing-room only. Here is the link to sign-up through EventBrite. I will definitely attend and will most-likely eat somewhere quickly before-hand if you want to join me! Some of the presenters will include: -Chalkable -Desmos -Flat World Knowledge -Knewton -Schoolbinder Hope you can join me!
