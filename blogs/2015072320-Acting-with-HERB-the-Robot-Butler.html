Acting with HERB, the Robot Butler

EdLab Studios

July 23, 2015


Can a robot learn to act on stage with human actors? At Carnegie Mellon University, the School of Drama has teamed with The Robotics Institute to build HERB, the Robot Butler, who stars in the play, "Sure Thing."

The actors explain that they seek "to find a truth onstage," while considering their impulse-based work. Acting, based on human interaction, meets new challenges when actors consider who they are next to HERB. Scientists working on the project allow the larger questions to be asked about people and humanity.

"I believe a man is what he is . . . a person is what he is," says HERB, as part of the play. The lab team considers movement, emotion, and expression when working with the robot onstage.
