TC R&W Project 8.14.2009: Closing Celebration for the Teaching of Writing

Ting Yuan

August 17, 2009

About 1,600 teachers from 38 states and 11 countries have gathered at Teachers College to attend the recent two weeks' summer institute on Teaching of Writing and Teaching of Reading. This week, the focus has shifted from writing to reading. Part of the week-long "training" at the writing institute was to ask teachers to envision themselves as writers. Every attendee was required to come up with an adult writing piece about a story related to their lives. Last Friday, I was honored to read my one-page piece, together with six other teachers, before the audience at the closing writing celebration. My piece was about a moment I picked from the story of my grandma, who ran away from her family because of an arranged marriage in the 1940s in China. You can read it here if interested. Thanks Josh for proofreading it for me before I "tremblingly" walked up to the stage.
