When open courses come marching in...

Ting Yuan

March 24, 2009

"Machine Learning" fans at the EdLab now can attend the same course taught at Stanford! Academic Earth is now offering free video courses, with support from Berkeley, Harvard, MIT, Princeton, Stanford, and Yale. Similar to but a bit different from TED and Hulu, video lectures at Academic Earth seem more organized with syllabuses and series of videos in 17 subjects (however, no course in Education yet). Starcraft and Competitive Gaming (session 1), a super hot course newly offered at Berkeley this spring (which Xuyang posted before): Introduction to Game Theory (session 1), offered at Yale (rated as A+): Its mission statement says: As more and more high quality educational content becomes available online for free, we ask ourselves, what are the real barriers to achieving a world class education? At Academic Earth, we are working to identify these barriers and find innovative ways to use technology to increase the ease of learning... All the courses on the site can be rated by viewers, regarding each clip or the series as a whole. Enjoy!
