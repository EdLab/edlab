Shared AR experiences are perfect for teaching Physics

Iyob.Gebremariam

November 26, 2018

Apple released shared AR experiences a while back along with their ARKit2. Here's a video they released to demo the featureÂ 


https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gZhQCVSvq5E


It's powerful. And it's supposed to work peer-to-peer meaning no need for a central server to organize the shared experiences.


I think the feature can be a great way to demo and interact with some cool physics. Here's a website that lets you play around with how gravity works with different masses and velocities. Shared AR can do something similar in a classroom, in 3D, in realtime and in a collaborative setting.
