Research/Tech Workshops at Columbia

Ting Yuan

March 25, 2009

Yesterday, I attended a workshop introducing a qualitative analysis software (NVivo) at the Butler Library and found it very helpful. I would strongly recommend it to those (like myself) who didn't know about this free resource before. The rest of the spring workshops include basic and advanced use of Photoshop, Firefox's citation function, and tools for textual analysis. A full spring schedule can be viewed here.
