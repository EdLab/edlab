Notes for Library Appy Hour (a D&amp;R production)

jvazquez

November 7, 2017

Thanks for helping us think about the possibilities of our library app!


Here are just some of the areas ripe for re-imagining.


Support

  • Fact: Patrons want on-demand help; librarians aren’t available 24/7

  • Consideration: Possible integration across our network of services and tools


Space:

  • Fact: It’s awkward to kick someone out of a room you reserved

  • Consideration: Think beyond reserving a study room


Readings and Requests

  • Fact: Professors aren’t keen on the idea of reserving on a mobile device

  • Consideration: Think about the entire DocDel system and all of the ways in which people interact with it


Wildcard

Pick any of the above or make like Fleetwood Mac and go your own way!


For those of you reading this who weren't at our meeting but would still like to participate, let us know if you have any ideas on the above. Even better, what other exclusive experiences would you like to see on the app? Give us those sweet, sweet ideas!
