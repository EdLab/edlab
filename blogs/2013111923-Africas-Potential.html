Africa's Potential

abagigah92

November 19, 2013

The potential of economic development in Africa is very big. With the right amount of investment and stability, opportunities for trade and development will increase and lead to greater economical growth. The video below is about the need for infrastructure investment in southern Africa. The goal is to achieve economic potential. Economic growth in the region remains hampered by poor infrastructure and links between country markets. Africa has enough natural resources to become an economic force in this century. According to researchers, these resources are helping raise the profile of the continent. Africa is also the fastest growing continent in terms of economic potential. Most African nations have seen a 6% annual increase in GDP. Urbanization and stability has been the driving force of Africa's growing economy. Coups and authoritarian regimes are declining and trade is flourishing. Cell phones are also becoming a big factor due to its cheap cost and access.
