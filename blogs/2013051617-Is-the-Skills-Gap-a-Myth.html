Is the "Skills Gap" a Myth?

Kate Meersschaert

May 16, 2013

This recent Bloomberg Businessweek article blew my mind for three reasons. 1) the "skills gap" isn't EVERYTHING in the race to defeat still pervasive unemployment (we need to create MORE JOBS not just more job-ready applicants) 2) The importance of math and science are starting to take a back-seat as compared to social skills. 3) The "cognitive content" of college grad's work has been on the decline since 2000 and is further reason that the "skills gap" might not be as big of an issue as we had once thought.
