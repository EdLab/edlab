TC in Panorama!

Adam Mitchinson

September 24, 2008

Today Eric, Jo and I were informally brainstorming about the TC Experience project ("TC the Ride"), and the development of a program to help new students become acclimatized to Teachers College. One idea we had was to create an interactive map of the college containing real photos of the places every student needs to visit. We started thinking, wouldn't it be cool if this map allowed a 360 view of places so every new student could know their way around before even setting foot in the building or even arriving in New York? There are many websites which contain great panoramic photography of some of the most beautiful places on earth! Move the cursor enough on these photos and it even starts to feel like a "ride"! What would the Edlab look like in Panorama? From my weak effort you can see its easier said than done but you get the idea (see if you can find the hidden 'hotspots' or tags)! Are there any more technically savvy people out there who can do better or know of software that allow even the most amateurish (me?) to do it?
