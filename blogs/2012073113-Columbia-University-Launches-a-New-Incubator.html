Columbia University Launches a New Incubator

Kate Meersschaert

July 31, 2012

Graduates of Columbia University's MBA program have a new place to call "home" after graduation, Columbia Business Lab. Officially launched this past Wednesday, the lab, which is located downtown, currently hosts an inaugural class of 22 ventures. The brainchild of Columbia Business School's Deans Advisory Committee, the lab features co-working space and is funded by Columbia Business School. Both the Lang Entrepreneurship Center and a group of mentors, faculty and alumni provide additional guidance and resources. Learn more in this Inc. piece.
