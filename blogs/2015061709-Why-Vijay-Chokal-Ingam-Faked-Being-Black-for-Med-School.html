Why Vijay Chokal-Ingam Faked Being Black for Med School

Patrick Williamson

June 17, 2015

In light of the racial discussion revolving around Rachel Dolezal, here is an interesting story of another individual who has strategically used his racial ambiguity to his advantage. While his commitment to the Black experience and level of identity with the Black community is to a much shallower extent than Dolezal, the question arises about where the line should be drawn in the acceptance of the transracial identity.
