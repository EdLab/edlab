Genre Watch: TMZ

gnatriello

June 27, 2011

As we consider the genres that are emerging in online environments, it might be worth adding TMZ to the list. The TV show that emerged from the TMZ website has generated a significant audience, and as this piece in the NYT suggests, the secret is less in the topic of celebrity and more in the format of the show which assembles the website producers in what the article notes is a newsroom without news. The strategy of using a work group as characters on a show about their work is worth considering as we think about ways to communicate the work of the EdLab. I suspect that it is more difficult than it appears.
