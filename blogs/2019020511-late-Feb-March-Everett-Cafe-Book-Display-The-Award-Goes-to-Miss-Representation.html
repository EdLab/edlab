late Feb-March Everett Cafe Book Display: The Award Goes to Miss Representation

Jennifer

February 5, 2019

Hi, All.  Here's our proposal for the next Everett Cafe book display, late February through March. Comments, suggestions, book titles most welcome! Thank you for reading.

Title:  The Award Goes to Miss Representation for a Cabinet of Controversies

The 91st Academy Awards ceremony, presented by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences in Hollywood, California, has not been without controversy, from the lack of a host, to the nominations of the best films of 2018. In taking a look at the history of the Oscars, we see that awards, like cinematic productions, may involve false or misleading pretexts and contexts  to sway opinion in favor of certain people, ideas, or notions. Some may argue that misrepresentation, while unethical in and of itself, is rightly exercised through freedom of speech and artistic license. Also, that whether something is true or partially true is up for interpretation…

This display features books about awards in film and media, with examples drawn from the Hollywood industry. It asks us to confront controversial issues with a critical lens so that we can better understand the filmmaker’s intent and meaning and develop more educated opinions about pressing societal matters.
