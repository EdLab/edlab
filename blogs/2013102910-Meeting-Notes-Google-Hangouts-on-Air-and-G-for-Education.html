Meeting Notes: Google Hangouts on Air & G+ for Education

Kate Meersschaert

October 29, 2013

Private! Here are my unedited notes from this morning's meeting @ Google re: Google Hangouts on Air & G+ for education - I will go back & add links + more detail once I am back in the office. Google Partners Meeting Edu & Makers Steve Grove head of G+ partnerships from mountain view Speakers: Journeyman founder adafruit MoMA Ed STEMConnect Steve g re challenges of YouTube's relevance to Ed Challenges of anonymous usernames (shirky issue) Relevance of comments on YouTube How to bring social layer? G+ seen as social evolution to YouTube One unified G+ experience Importance of beautiful multimedia to feed the "interest graph" (GIFs etc) Three G+ trends: 1) Giving audience an inside view (behind the scenes content) = hangout jumping etc. (personal connection) exclusivity & debuts of content = poster session intros & breaking albums - virtual field trips 2) collaborate on class= MIT connected .Creativity MOOC - community "sandbox" Crowd source ideas for maker projects Nat goes #greatnature photo collaborations 3) search & discovery: right panel insights, #'s integrated into search, "flip the card" exploration Adafruit: 20M Weekly show & tell on hangouts Circuit playground kids show Ask an engineer Ppl join communities for: Social status, scoop & scale Set tone ---> post type of content we want to receive back ----> release content as a heartbeat Dubspot: Music school supported by G+ StemConnector: Google+HOA=webinar 2.0 JourneyMan - scaling teaching through G+: Profile w founder: Patrick McConlogue Role of trusting users & their content Wants to build a mentorship environment Desire to mentor & learn remotely MoMA Art Hangs Using Q&A for inquiry-based education Google+ Hangouts on Air 6 actual phone calls in three years (much like Edlab) Explore idea of conversations emerging in a lively setting Seamlessly take a convo & have it travel with you over time Features announced today: Launched 2 years ago 3 months to code G+ social layer with YouTube streaming First year - get it to work seamlessly across devices 2nd year - how can we make this more interactive? 3 step: Plan - promote & manage Trailer RSVP Share via social networks Control room (mute, adjust vol, kick-out participants) "Auto enhance" HD Q&A feature allows you to both ask & answer audience q's (up-vote & down-vote) - bookmark video for documentation later - time stamped annotation GoogleModerator
