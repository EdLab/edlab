Do you "Snow-fall" or "Jockey"?

Kate Meersschaert

November 15, 2013

As Nara shared, Snow Fall (here is her follow-up post re: "Firestorm") was a watershed moment in digital journalism. Integrating beautiful interactive elements with text, the block-buster, award-winning piece has set a new precedent for digital journalism. So much so that the term to "Snow-fall" is now thrown around as a verb in newsrooms. Another term, "to jockey" is now used after an additional ground-breaking piece of multimedia was released by the NYT's focused on the life of Jockeys. (Explore this piece here.) How do you feel this ground-breaking use of multimedia and interactive UE can be applied to NLT? How do we "Snow-fall?"
