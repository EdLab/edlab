Trend alert
We are losing people before they get to content pages. 

For example...
There were 52 hits on “See all library services” → which led to 23 hits on the first section, “Research Services” → and ultimately 12 content pages viewed. 

Similarly, 
There were 25 hits on “See all library resources” → which led to 13 hits on the first section, “Top resources”→ and then 0 content pages were viewed. 

This pattern was consistent for all sections and content pages: visits to pages were pretty much halved at each subsequent step of the journey.
