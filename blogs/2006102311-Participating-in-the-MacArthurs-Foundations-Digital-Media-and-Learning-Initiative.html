Participating in the MacArthurs Foundation's Digital Media and Learning Initiative?

Dennis Stevens

October 23, 2006

After Gary's post last week, I was checking out the details and assorted blog postings regarding the new MacArthur Foundation initiative "Building the Field of Digital Media and Learning" and ran across the following invitation to join their Knowledge Network:

"We are seeking other institutions to join our network. Selected institutions -- ranging from think tanks to TV networks -- will bring their expertise to bear on aggregating and highlighting the very best from their vital domains. Know a good candidate? Invite them to contact us."


Perhaps we should contact the Foundation, let them know what we are doing within the EdLab and see how we might participate in this dialogue?

Thoughts? Comments?
