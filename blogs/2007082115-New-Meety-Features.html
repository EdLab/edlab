New Meety Features

Anthony Cocciolo

August 21, 2007

New features have been incorporated into Meety to make it generally more useful to users. The first, as alluded to in an earlier post, is the ratings engine for collaboratively "promoting" or "burying" Meety-made or Meeters-made content. Content that receives a low score will be placed at the bottom of the list and hidden (however, you can click to see low-rated content). (see figure). Also clearly visible in the figure is the small speaker icon (), which allows you to click listen to where in the speech a reference came from. The speaker icon now also appears in the translation screen, as well as the search screen so you can always refer back to the audio to which the text refers. More Meety testing is underway...
