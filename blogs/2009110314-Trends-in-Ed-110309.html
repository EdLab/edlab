Trends in Ed, 11.03.09

Ting Yuan

November 3, 2009

The World's Deepest Bin? - A Practice of "The Fun Theory" FunTheory.com just issued an interesting clip about how to change people's behavior in a simple and fun way, which provides a fresh perspective to think of planning actions to bring behavioral changes. FunTheory.com is an organization dedicated to “the thought that something as simple as fun is the easiest way to change people's behavior for the better”. Call for Presentations, Papers, and Ideas Deadline: December 15, 2009. ICELW 2010 - The International Conference on E-learning in the Workplace June 9th-11th, 2010 Teachers College, Columbia University New York, NY ICELW is an international conference focused specifically on e-learning in the workplace. ICELW 2010 will incorporate an increased focus on demonstrations of successful e-learning, and on the application of new research ideas and research studies to practical workplace learning problems, including e-learning project management, usability studies, e-learning design, etc. Categories of topics can be viewed on the website.
