Resources about web video accessibility for education

zhou

November 13, 2014

--- keep private --- 3playmedia has a bunch of very useful resources about web video accessibility for education. I am thinking this could be useful for making Vialogues more accessible. Here are a few latest white papers and guidances they have (you can download the original ones from 3playmedia after free signup): 2015 Roadmap to Web Accessibility in Higher Education DIY Resources for Closed Captioning and Transcription Sources of Grants and Funding for Closed Captioning of Online Video Maybe we can work with the Office of Access and Services for Individuals with Disabilities to apply for the federal grant so as to get our NLT videos transcribed?
