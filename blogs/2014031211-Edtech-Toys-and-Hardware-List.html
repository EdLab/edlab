Edtech Toys and Hardware List

LauraCostello

March 12, 2014

(please, keep private) Our library has long collected curriculum "kits" that teachers can demo for use in the classroom. In the past these kits included books, worksheets, toys, and videos but today's version of a "kit" might include tablets, circuits, and robot-building tools. Along with Gary, I've started compiling a list of these kinds of edtech toys and hardware building kits but I need your help to make sure it's complete! Check the Google Doc to make sure your favorite hardware products are represented and please add any products that are missing! We're particularly looking for education-focused electronic products or tools that help teach 21st century skills or support teaching in the classroom of the future. Please respond if you have any questions and thanks for your help!
