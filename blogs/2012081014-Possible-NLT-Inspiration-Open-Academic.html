Possible NLT Inspiration: Open Academic

Kate Meersschaert

August 10, 2012

Here is an interesting source of new thought on edtech and issues surrounding higher-ed! This is a "sub-blog" within a site called OnlineColleges.net. Here are my thoughts as to why I think this content and blog design is intriguing for NLT: 1) Colors and design... I find the custom "icons" designating sections like "course offerings" are effective and enjoyable. 2) Compelling (even though it is most-likely "stock") Imagery as links to accompany each piece. These images are laid-out in a grid and provide the visual "hook" to excite me about the content. 3) Simple layout. Not cluttered or overwhelming. Now the negative... lack of "voice" variety - I wish there were more contributors of content! What are your thoughts on this site?
