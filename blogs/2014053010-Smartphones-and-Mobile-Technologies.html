Smartphones and Mobile Technologies

Kafoumba Doumbia

May 30, 2014

Technology has rapidly changed the way we communicate and socialize. Even though smarts phones and mobile technology devices have had great impact on the way we live by making communication affordable and easier, we still fail to note its negative effects. As we already know, the positive impact of mobile technologies are numerous and endless but the negatives are unnoticeable.

In the video below, the narrator addresses society's current issues due to our obsessive use of technological devices. It is very hard for people to even meet each other in person. Individuals would rather spend all day, week or month messaging each other than planning a visit to meet each other. Even in a social meeting, people have a hard time listening and looking at each other because they are too busy giving attention to whoever is texting them. Sometimes I wish we never had the knowledge of making devices that change the way we interact with each other. There are so many opportunities we miss. When we are alone, the first thing is to take out our cell phone and start messaging. Socializing with other people is in fact the best way to build a network that will serve you well in the long run, so look up from your mobile devices and socialize with each other.
