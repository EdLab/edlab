"Better Substitute Teachers"

Christopher Zamora

April 3, 2013

One thing that recently came to my mind is the lack of teaching substitute teachers are at my school. Whenever students hear that one of the teachers is absent, it's almost as if saying you have a free class that period. Substitute teachers at my school usually just sit and watch over us, and don't really teach us. Our teachers would usually leave work for us to do, but most of the time it doesn't get done since our substitute teachers just give work without much direction and don't leave room for us to ask questions or express concerns. Of course, some students take advantage and be unproductive during the class period. I feel that if my school had better substitute teachers, or at least have those teachers pushing students to do work rather than just sitting in the classroom and being unproductive as the students, then just maybe education and time isn't wasted in my school. I feel my high school needs much improvement, and improving substitute teachers would be a good place to start.
