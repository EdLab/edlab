Good People - Chap. 1 - Things good people do

ryanwu

June 8, 2017

When I saw the book tagline "The only leadership decision that really matters" I thought this is just another book for business leaders or entrepreneurs. But it's actually for any lifelong student of leadership.

So what exactly is leadership?

Quote from one of my favorite writer and inspirational speaker, Robin Shama. "Leadership is not about how much money you make or the clothes you wear. Leadership is a philosophy. It’s an attitude. It’s a state of mind. And it’s available to each one of us."


Following are five good people mantra and some sentences in the chapter about the idea of goodness and what "good people" always do.


Good people purposely and proactively put people first in their decion making

Never make anyone feel as if there isn’t room in the life boat. Always be ready and willing to help -- donate your time, run out for coffee. The true leaders understand that strengthening capacity at every level, in every person, is the way to win.


Goodness is a mandate to create more goodness in the world

Just like Tom Peters said, "Leaders don't create followers, they create more leaders". And the only way to be a leader is to lead yourself first. By using the best judgment in all situation and be the first one to act. Because your behavior reveals your beliefs.


And Anthony hopes he can inspire the readers to start a relationship of goodness with others and motivate people to be their own leader, a.k.a the good people. We will see more detail about these five mantras in the following part of the book.
