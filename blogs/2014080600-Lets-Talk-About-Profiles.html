Let's Talk About Profiles

Nantwi

August 6, 2014

I enjoyed this Good Magazine Q&A with artist and architect Olafur Eliasson. Some of you might remember Eliasson as one of the mentors from YoungArts MasterClass. The format of the interview focused on Eliasson's take on creativity (among other things) and his art and research studio in Berlin. The interview got me thinking about NLT Profiles and some potential changes we can make regarding the format. The Eliasson interview focused on his views regarding specific topics (e.g. creativity) and included images and selected quotes. Should we include more images (apart from the picture of the individual) and other multimedia elements in Profiles? Additionally, what are some questions we should consider adding (or removing)?
