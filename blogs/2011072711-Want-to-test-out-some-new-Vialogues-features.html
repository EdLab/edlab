Want to test out some new Vialogues features?

Josh Lopez

July 27, 2011

Two new features in Vialogues are just about ready to roll out, but we need your help to make sure they work as they should and make sense to users. First is a new interface for leaving comments on a Vialogue. The buttons have moved around and you can now add #hashtags in your comments just as you would in twitter. Second is the new search. When you do a search, you'll see results like always, but you can also see whether any vialogues have been #tagged with any of the words you searched for. There is also an advanced search box so you can get more specific results. If you have a minute, go over to the Vialogues Dev site (Your regular login should work) and try them out. Then let us know what you think.
