Hello Edlabbers ! Its Halloween again ! And this time, we want to celebrate it together with our very own 'Vialogues Remy', 'Ednode Octopus' and 'Pundit Monster'. The team here is organizing a Halloween party on Oct 31st , Monday from 3 pm to 6 pm. The event will be open to entire TC and Comp Science CU students. We are thinking of having different stations set up on the event. 1. Photobooth station: Get your halloween picture clicked against our super fun backdrops (backdrops currently in design). 2. Dress the characters: We will have cut outs for the three characters (Remy, Octopus and Monster) and scrap materials so people can dress them. 3. Technical graveyard: We'll have a graveyard alongwith predesigned graves. People will make graves of obsolete technologies, place them in the graveyard and allow them to *finally* rest in peace. 4. Career Oppourtunities Station : For learning about Edlab and Career Oppurtunities. We'll also have candies and Remy, Ednode and Pundit stickers on this table. Miscellaneous things in the event: - "Are you afraid of the dark" playing in the background - Snacks and Drinks (Bloody Fruit Punch)...haha - Tour of the spooky tower We will be doing Reverse trick or treat next Thursday (Oct 27) where we will be distributing candies and party invitations in TC. Flyers and eboard ads will also be put up next week. Further to get everyone excited, we have created a short promotional video which we will be sending out with the email invitation at the TC Portal. You lucky ones get a chance to see it first. Thanks to the entire team - Joann, Kate, Janet and Fred ! Feedback, Suggestions ? Bring it on !
