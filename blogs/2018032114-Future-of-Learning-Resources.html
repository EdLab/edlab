Future of Learning - Resources

gnatriello

March 21, 2018

As we consider development of a new platform for the archives it is useful to consider some forward leaning examples.




One such example is the National Archives of the UAE  In addition to managing the physical archives shown above and an archives exhibit center, the National Archives of the UAE also offer a VR interface.


In addition they offer a range of services to support broad use of the materials in the archives as part of a comprehensive program of education. For example, they offer new teachers an orientation program.




What kinds of services and experiences should we be considering as we re-think the archives?
