News Corp Buys Wireless

gnatriello

November 23, 2010

Chancellor Klein announced last week that he was leaving his role as head of the NYC schools to join News Corp. Now, it seems that Rupert Murdoch's News Corp is purchasing Wireless Generation for $360 million. What's the attraction of the education sector? Murdoch believes that education is a $500 billion sector waiting desperately to be transformed.
