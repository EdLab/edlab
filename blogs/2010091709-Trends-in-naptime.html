Trends in naptime

jvazquez

September 17, 2010

To everyone who knew of my original exhibit idea for the Adaptive Learning event, let the record show that I might have been on to something. I give you: The Dream Helmet. (While not nearly as invasive as what I had in mind, maybe my dream controlling technology wasn't just fodder for science fiction.) The Dream Helmet has been sharing its time in the spotlight with company nap rooms. Places like Nike, Google, and Ben & Jerry's offer their employees quiet rooms (and futuristic sleep pods) to recharge via "productivity naps." Research has shown that napping increases performance and mood. The prime time to take a nap is between 1pm and 3pm, as this is the time by which our bodies and minds have grown weary. What are your thoughts on the workplace napping trend? Google's "Energy Pod." If only George Costanza had one of these!
