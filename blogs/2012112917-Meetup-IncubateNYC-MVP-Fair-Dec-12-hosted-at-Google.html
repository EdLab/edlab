Meetup: IncubateNYC MVP Fair, Dec 12, hosted at Google

Janice Joo

November 29, 2012

Hey EdLab, Here's the next meetup that Kate and I will be going to... Anyone interested should sign up here! :) IncubateNYC MVP Fair (November Class)

IncubateNYC's November Bootcamp class will complete their five-week intensive program on Wednesday Dec. 12th. Our 17 amazing entrepreneurs will be unveiling their MVPs (minimally viable products) after working long nights and weeks, with no sleep, no food, only coffee. Come to Google and meet these up and coming entrepreneurs of the NYC Startup Community in a cool science fair style event. Hear their pitch, see their products in action and give your feedback. There will be drinks, amazing products demoed, good conversation and awesome connections made. See you there!!
