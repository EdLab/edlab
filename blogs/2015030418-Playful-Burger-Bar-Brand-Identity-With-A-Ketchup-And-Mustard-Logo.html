Playful Burger Bar Brand Identity With A ‘Ketchup And Mustard’ Logo

Panisuan Chasinga

March 4, 2015

Budapest-based graphic designer and visual artist MiklÃ³s Kiss has created a fun and eye-catching branding identity for new burger restaurant “Burger&Love”. Other than making a distinctive pattern out of colorful icons and illustrations, Kiss has also produced an attractive logo that looks like it has been made by squirting ketchup and mustard out of squeeze bottles. What's really neat about this is not only because it's a visual candy, but how cleverly the artist employ the activity so common in a burger shop--spilled ketchup and mustard--you almost want to interact with the paper and ketchup bottle.
