Sunny Crowdsourcing

Jeannie Crowley

September 21, 2009

It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia is utilizing crowdsourcing to generate buzz about its season five premier. Fans can use the trailer mash up to splice together clips from previous seasons, add music, and create an embeddable video they can share on Facebook, blogs, YouTube, etc. This ad campaign is a great example of how crowdsourcing can be really fun for the participants (as opposed to typical crowdsourcing campaigns that ask users to sift through and edit data).
