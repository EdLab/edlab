EdLab Review: Wisc-Online

Chandler Kang

April 20, 2010

Wisc-Online is a digital library of Web-based learning resources called "learning objects." You can create learning materials for learners or teachers who need teaching materials. The materials are made based on templates which are provided by Wisc-Online.com.
