Most Viral Teaching Moments of 2018

gnatriello

December 30, 2018


Students and teachers increasingly use social media to share their experiences in U.S. schools. Check out the most viral moments of 2018 in U.S. schools as collected by NPR. Although each of these tells us something about a particular school and the experience of students and teachers, my takeaway is that there is so much variability in public schooling in the United States that it is misleading to consider it a single system even in broad terms.
