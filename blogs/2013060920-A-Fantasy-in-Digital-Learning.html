A Fantasy in Digital Learning

jvazquez

June 9, 2013


Science fiction meets education when students find their learning match in smart mobile devices. From Pearson, the creator of the adaptive MyLab/Mastering series, comes this fanciful and imaginative look at the future of learning. Facilitated by interactive, mobile touch screen devices, the students, teachers, and parents in this video demonstrate fluid communication between real-life and online networks. How masterfully they transfer information from one device to another with the mere flick of a finger, and how seamlessly it all weaves together to create an epic, everlasting learning experience.

Could technology facilitate an "always learning" way of being? More than ever, it is the conduit of collaboration, creating pathways between students and opening up doors to a wealth of online resources. But should we take the message of an always-plugged-in reality with a grain of salt, or a sign of things to come?
