Games for Learning Institute to Host "Games for Learning Day" with Parsons The New School for Design

Adrienne Garber

April 19, 2010

The Games for Learning Institute is hosting the inaugural Games for Learning Day at the 7th Annual Games for Change Festival. The festival will take place on May 24 - 26, 2010 at Parsons, The New School for Design in New York City, with a special extended day at NYU (separate registration) on May 27th. Keynote speakers include the first-ever White House Chief Technology Officer Aneesh Chopra and retired Associate Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court the Honorable Sandra Day O'Connor. Of interest to the EdLab audience, in addition to the all-star roster of presenters and moderators, this year's festival will feature a day-long workshop which will focus on game design programs for youth. The youth-focused workshop day, entitled "Power of Design: Youth Making Social Issue Games" will include reviews of game design platforms for use in the classroom, game software demos, game design and STEM learning presentations, and networking opportunities for educators and after school program directors.
