Now, That's Data Visualization

gnatriello

June 16, 2010

With data visualization gaining ground as an essential element of analysis in a variety of fields, it's interesting to imagine where this movement might lead. We have looked at facilities at ASU and RPI as models for university based visualization centers. The Allosphere at UC Santa Barbara is the latest leading edge model of a data visualization facility. Might be an interesting new use for the tower!
