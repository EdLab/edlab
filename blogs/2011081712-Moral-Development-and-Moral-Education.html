Moral Development and Moral Education

Yoshi Nakazawa

August 17, 2011

Title: Moral Development and Moral Education Author: R. S. Peters Publisher: HarperCollins Publishers Ltd, 1981 About the Author: Peters was a professional philosopher who specialized in philosophical psychology and philosophy of education. He famously coined the term 'paradox of moral education', a treatment of which can be found in chapter two of the present volume.
