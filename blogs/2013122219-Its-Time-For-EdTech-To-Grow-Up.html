It’s Time For EdTech To Grow Up

Brian.Sweeting

December 22, 2013


A recent Forbes piece makes the case that it’s time for the education technology industry to grow up. Compared to an industry like healthcare that has seen increased funding and calls for reform, the fledging edtech industry has yet to receive the same attention. Time will tell if the advances in online learning and a rush of private money will encourage similar growth.
