School Competitiveness and Test Scores

Evans.Asamoah

May 10, 2017

 


The Programme for International Students Assessment (PISA), the group responsible for administering international exams, recently conducted a large-scale study where they looked at students’ social and emotional well-being. Over half a million students from 72 countries were included in the study. The test scores, anxiety, and motivation level of the students are highlighted in the graph below. Students who do not want to be the best in their class have lower levels of anxiety. Furthermore, these students perform relatively well. This is perhaps the result of a low level of anxiety as opposed to those students who want to top their class. These students normally have high levels of anxiety, which negatively affects their scores.
