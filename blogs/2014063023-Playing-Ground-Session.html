Playing Ground Session

Carmel Addae

June 30, 2014

Legendary producer Quincy Jones has launched a musical app that teaches users how to play the piano. Jones, who has worked with the likes of Celine Dion, Frank Sinatra, Michael Jackson and Aretha Franklin, feels the app will help children and adults learn how to read and play music. The app, Playing Ground Session, will act sort of like the Rosetta Stone of teaching music. I like this application because I feel like it makes it easy to learn how to play the piano and faster even though it looks really complicated and hard. This will help push interested users in learning more about other music in different countries. It can also have a huge influence on music education programs in schools across the country and the globe.
