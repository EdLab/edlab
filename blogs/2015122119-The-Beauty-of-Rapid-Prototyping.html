The Beauty of Rapid Prototyping

dinosossi2

December 21, 2015


How do we build the future through new technology? Maybe more importantly, how do we make "great" products that help us arrive at a better and more livable, future?

The people at Google X think they have the answerâ€”rapid prototyping. By creating processes that foster building quick mockups of ideas, they believe they have come up with more efficient, effective, and useful products.

In the secretive space of Google X, the dedicated engineers and scientists make things the old fashioned way. They embrace pie-in-the-sky thinking, knowing that their beloved project may not make it to market. What they do is creation for the sake of creation. They live in an all-day, every day, makerspace for adults. Who says you need to grow up?
