Smartphone Sensors

Daniel Vergara

January 30, 2013

I read this article and was shocked when I realized what they had to say about smartphone sensors. I am sure most of us lock your smartphone with either a pin or pattern. The aforementioned article shows that the sensors on a smartphone gather data on the movements that occur when they are in use; any movement as well as when they press down on the screen. As a result of the data they've gathered, it has become increasingly easier for the sensors to guess pins and patterns when phones are locked. Being aware of the capacity of these sensors, I wouldn't feel so secure now when I put a pin on my phone. If this sensor can gather information of my movements with my phone, it's only a matter of a few tries for it to be able to correctly guess my pin. How do you feel about this sensor on your smartphone?
