More School time, better learning?

Christopher Zamora

May 1, 2013

Many middle schools will now have classes that will end as late as 6pm starting this fall as part of the city's effort to extend the school day. This is in response to students falling off track during the middle grades. It's where the achievement gap really seems to grow, said City Council speaker Christine Quinn, who is seeking the mayoral seat soon to be vacated my Bloomberg. The city's strategy is to help first the struggling middle schools. New York is one of the first major cities to focus on longer day program in order to improve reading skills. I find very interesting the idea increasing school hours in middle school. I agree totally on increasing school hours, but school until 6pm? I think even that is too late. I feel teachers need to become better at teaching as well. When I was in middle school, the teachers were just so boring, and I lost attention quickly. What are your thoughts on this article ? Will increasing school hours help improve student performance?
