3-Line Online Learning Manifestos from Today's D&R Meeting

Kate Meersschaert

March 20, 2012

Writing a manifesto is a major undertaking, but of course, no challenge is too big for five minutes in EdLab Development & Research session! Please find your thoughts listed below... in no particular order or attribution. Feel-free to add to these in the comments! Here is the example we referenced. 1) Online learning is... 2) Online learning is not... 3) The future of online learning will be... Your D&R-festos of Online Learning: Online learning is to learn the way you want. Online learning is not easy... The future of online learning will be unprecedented. Online learning is fun, easy, easily marketable when its fun, easy & convenient. Online learning is not being conceptualized in useful ways. The future of online learning will get worse before it gets better. Online learning is more efficient than human interaction. Online learning is not restricted to your computer. The future of online learning will come at a cost... Online learning is for those who hunger for more knowledge. Online learning is the democratization of knowledge. Online learning is not limited by distance. The future of online learning will be... ??? Online learning is engaging and collaborative. Online learning is not just videos or just a teacher. The future of online learning will be individualized and hybrid. THE FUTURE OF ONLINE LEARNING IS UP TO US!!!!
