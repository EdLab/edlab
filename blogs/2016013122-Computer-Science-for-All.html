Computer Science for All

AbdulMalik

January 31, 2016

The field of education has experienced a lot of changes over the years due to technology. Some schools offer intensive courses in computer science for students to learn basic coding. Other schools do not focus on the computer science courses they offer. Considering the current state of the labor market and economy, it is very essential for students to learn basic computer skills because the world revolves around technology. This initiative by the Obama administration is a proposed bill to Congress which will allocate $4 billion to the education sector. These funds will be used to provide resources for students and educators. The proposal also emphasizes the need for computer literacy among students. The goal of the plan, as indicated by President Obama, is to ensure that we are constantly developing new forms of technology while familiarizing ourselves with the technology already in place.
