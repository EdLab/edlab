3D Scanning today! 2-2:30pm

ZKL

July 31, 2018

Our newest EdLab Artist Rosalie Yu is coming in today to test the lighting for the 3D scanning workshop being held in September.


The process is quick and easy (just a series of photographs taken in a circle around the subject who stands still) and results in an awesome 3D digital image! You don't need anything special except a pose and the ability to stand still.


Please help us out by coming to the Learning Theater at 2pm - the more the better. Shoot me an email zoe.logan@tc.columbia.edu and let me know you are interested so I have a basic count. You can keep your scan and use it for a 3D print or a VR/AR experiment!!
