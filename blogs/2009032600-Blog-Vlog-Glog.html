Blog - Vlog - Glog...?

Ting Yuan

March 26, 2009

What is Glog? The sphere of internet log after blog and vlog has witnessed the birth of this new generation. Glog is like a poster produced on internet which combines and goes beyond all the functions of blog and vlog, mixing graphics, photos, videos, music and text vividly in one "poster". All the functions can be lively-created, moved, and decorated by users flexibly. This new web 2.0 tool is of great educational value and can assist teachers in better teaching many subjects. For example, after viewing a John Adam video clip, students can leave reflections on the "poster" in various formats, or design their individual glogs to reflect on the clip. Compared with VoiceThread which can be used with elementary students, Glog may be more suitable for secondary students. Here is a Glog I just created for fun!!
