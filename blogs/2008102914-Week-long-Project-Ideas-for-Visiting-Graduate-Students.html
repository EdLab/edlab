Week-long Project Ideas for Visiting Graduate Students?

Pollyanna Rhee

October 29, 2008

The Information School at the University of Michigan has an Alternative Spring Break program for their graduate students placing them with organizations in New York to assist with information management projects and so they can get some additional work experience. Does anyone have any ideas for a project? Last year Columbia University Libraries hosted some interns. More information can be found at their website. If you have any suggestions or would be interested in having someone be a part of a project for a week, please respond--and include any preferred skills (programming, design, library services, etc.).
