Vialogues Paper at ICALT 2012

Megha Agarwala

March 2, 2012


Hi Everyone! Sharon, Hui Soo, Gary and I submitted a Vialogues paper to the 2012 IEEE International Conference on Advanced Learning Technologies, aka ICALT. We are very happy to announce that our paper titled "Vialogues: Videos and Dialogues based Social Learning Environment" has been accepted as a full paper to the conference (5 pages). The acceptance rate for full papers has been less than 20% this year. See a full list of all selected papers here.
