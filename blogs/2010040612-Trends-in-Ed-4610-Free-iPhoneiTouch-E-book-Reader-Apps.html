Trends in Ed, 4.6.10: Free iPhone/iTouch E-book Reader Apps

Ting Yuan

April 6, 2010

1. eReader of Children's book is under development by the International Children's Digital Library. The International Children's Digital Library brings a small part of its worldwide collection of children's books to the iPhone, with more promised in the future. 2. Along with the Barnes & Noble iPhone/iTouch Reader and Kindle iPhone/iTouch eReader, more applications are entering the mobile eReader market. The handheld features make those applications unique and competitive through the birth of the iPad. Amazon's Stanza -- Stanza is the best e-book reader for the iPhone, and my favorite given by the PC Magazine --includes the ability to slide finger across the screen to turn the page, which a real winner for those not quite prepared to turn their back on the paper book experience. 3. Wattpad 100, 000 + Books -- eReader from an online book community

What differentiates Wattpad from similar apps is the user-generated content. The system offers a publishing platform, for amateur or fan fiction writers.
