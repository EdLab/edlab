Songmark Is a Simple but Powerful Tool for Teaching or Learning Choreography

Ryan.Allen

June 18, 2018


SongMark is an app specifically for dance instructors, choreographers, or anyone learning dance. It was designed by a pair of dance instructors who were tired of constantly rewinding to replay a specific section of a song while teaching.

Pros:

This free app only has a few features, but they are instrumental in teaching or learning dance. Users can bookmark multiple locations in a song for simple playback right from the marked spots. Likewise, the loop function allows users to bookend the segment to create specific loops. For instance, if an instructor just wants to teach dance moves to a song’s chorus, they can simply press "mark" at the beginning and then press "loop" at the end; SongMark will then continuously loop that marked segment.

Songs can be uploaded directly from a phone’s library. The music can also be sped up or slowed down depending on the needs and skills of dancers. All of the features are easy and intuitive to use.

Cons:

SongMark is supposed to have the capabilities to download music directly from YouTube into the app. I tried this with several different songs, but I could never get the feature to work. SongMark should figure out how to integrate its functions with YouTube streaming.

Our Takeaway:

SongMark is a free and simple tool for dance instructors that makes teaching or learning much more efficient. No more constantly scrubbing! If you are interested in choreography, this is an app that you will want to try.
