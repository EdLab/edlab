Brainstorming on Recruiting Study Participants

Xiang.Liu

December 5, 2014

Thank you guys participating in the pilot study earlier this week. The initial results look pretty promising. Now it comes to the most difficult part of any study - recruiting participants.

We will talk a little more about this on Monday. But I want to put something up just to get it started.

The experiment plan is going to be similar to what you guys experienced on the D&R. However, I would like to increase the test length to 18 items from 9 items. The extra items will have a boost in test reliability and correct classification rate. So I budget the required time to roughly 90 minutes.

Ideally, the study participants would come from high school or college students. Any ideas on recruiting them?
