NLT Analytics, Nov 2018

Rob.Crawford

December 14, 2018



In November we had 4,589 visitors to NLT, almost half of whom (shown here in blue) arrived from Google Search. Another 30%, nearly, came through direct traffic, which means we don't have more information on that segment.


This leaves about 25% of our visitors, who arrived through a combination of referrals, our marketing campaigns, and other social media.


For referrals, in orange, the largest were Flipboard, with 38% of all referrals, and EdLab, with 28%.


The social segment, in yellow, is 67% Facebook, 21% LinkedIn, and 8% Twitter. This is separate from the traffic generated by our own social media accounts.


Marketing campaigns appear in light blue, and we can drill down for more detail. In the following, "E" represents the NLT Newsletter, "FB" is Facebook, and "T" is Twitter. (The specific NLT streams are also coded.)




Here we can see that, in November, 82% of the traffic from our marketing campaigns was due to the newsletter. About 8% was Twitter, and 5% Facebook.
