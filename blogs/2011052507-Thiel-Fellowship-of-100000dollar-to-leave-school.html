Thiel Fellowship of 100,000$ to leave school

Megha Agarwala

May 25, 2011

Thiel Foundation has announced a batch of 24 fellows who would be awarded 100,000$ over the course of two years, to pursue their entrepreneurial ideas. The only condition is that these students will have to drop out of college (or high school) to focus full-time on building a business. And if they should fail? As Mr Thiel told The Economist recently, "they can always go back to college."
