What's for Seminar?

Francisco.Mendoza

June 5, 2013

**Keep in private** It is always great to be part of our weekly seminars. The presentations are nice, the environment is as well, and last but not least the food. I can't promise that ill order something to anyone for next week, but I am willing to hear some suggestions from you guys. It can be pizza from CheBella, Food from Noche ( which we ordered today), Falafels, Roti Roll, Kitchenette, or subway subs. I'm not sure if we have ordered anything else besides that but if I have and forgot to mention it you can also mention it in the comments. Do you all have any new suggestions? Comment them below, but like I said I can't promise anything, I am just exploring other options if you have them.
