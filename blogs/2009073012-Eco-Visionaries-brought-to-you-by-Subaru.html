"Eco-Visionaries" brought to you by Subaru

Erin Murphy

July 30, 2009

In researching for our series on sustainability I came across this web video show on the Sundance Website, "Traction for Life." The show's producers travel around the US and interview people who are "making a difference" in terms of sustainability and green action. I think this series is interesting for 2 reasons. One being the brevity of the episodes. I havent watched one that was longer than a minute. Two, the series is sponsored by by Subaru. Subaru's website seems to be down but it would be interesting to read the company mission statement. Maybe a car company would be interested in funding our series! Also I was wondering what people thought of the 1 minute pieces. Are they just a tease? I'm a bit confused about the purpose of the episodes. I dont think they are trailers for larger stories but they seem to be tied into the Eco Trip series. What do Sundance and Subaru want the audience to take from this series?
