New Media in Education 2008 - Live from the Low Library, CU

Stephen Asunka

October 17, 2008

The Mew Media in Education 2008 conference is currently in session at the Low Library, Columbia Univ. It is organized by the Columbia Center for New Media Teaching and Learning (CNMTL). Day-long activities include demonstrations, presentations and discussions, all aimed at stimulating dialogue on pedagogical best practices and the future possibilities of an interconnected classroom.
