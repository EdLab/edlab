New Vialogues

Angela Lee

February 10, 2011

Hello All, Stay tuned for a series of upcoming videos on Vialogues! These videos would mostly consist of a television series- Open Mind. First broadcasted in May, 1956, Open Mind is still produced weekly by Richard D. Heffner, host, historian, and Professor of Communications and Public Policy at Rutgers University. The show consists of conversations with some of the most creative thinkers of the last half-century and is available to students, teachers, researchers, archivists, librarians, historians, journalists and anyone interested in history, biography, media, communications, news, and public affairs. We will be categorizing / hash tagging / traditional tagging these videos accordingly (once the Vialogues team figures out the organizing system, so please take this survey from Megha's post. Other than the Open Mind series, we are also looking for more appropriate videos for Vialogues, so if you have appropriate videos (of length and content) please upload them or consult with Megha and I. These videos will eventually be sent out to teachers, researchers, and students for the purpose of further discussion and Vialogues site traffic gain. Below are some previews of the upcoming series, and feel free to check them out and discuss on Vialogues! Please let us know if you find it interesting.
