Interesting Site/Concept

Jeff Frank

January 13, 2006

Richard A. Lanham, the author of The Economics of Attention: Style and Substance in the Age of Information and The Electronic Word: Democracy, Technology, and the Arts among many other titles, has a very interesting website called Rhetorica. I have not seen a personal/academic/publishing page like this before, but I find it compelling. Further, it is interesting that Lantham [and his wife] made the transition from very traditional academic roles [English at UCLA, Principal Editor at the UCLA Center for Medieval and Renaissance Studies] to embracing electronic publishing. I look forward to reading his work with an eye towards implications for the future of education/scholarly publishing.
