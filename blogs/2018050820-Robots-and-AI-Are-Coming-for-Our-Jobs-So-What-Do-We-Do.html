Robots and AI Are Coming for Our Jobs, So What Do We Do?

Ryan.Allen

May 8, 2018


Tech giants like Google, Amazon, and Baidu are rapidly expanding the capabilities of artificial intelligence (AI). In the near future, low-skill jobs such as bank tellers or receptionists will become replaced through AI advancements. Eventually, though, even high-skilled jobs like radiologists will be fully automated. How can we prepare for the future of AI career takeovers?

Kai-Fu Lee, CEO of Sinovation Ventures, doesnâ€™t warn of a grim future where no one has a job; instead, he says that we should retrain workers and focus on education in order to prepare for these changes. He contends that because machines are cold and lack compassion, we should emphasize careers that are more human-centered.

Lee argues that we need a change in values, stressing compassion as a key component for careers. What kind of educational changes do you think we need to prepare for this automated future? Add your voice to the discussion on Vialogues.
