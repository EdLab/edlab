LAB Experiences at The Rockefeller University

EdLab Studios

December 15, 2015


All students should have access to high quality science education and experiences no matter what. At least that's the philosophy at The Rockefeller University. As part of the university’s Science Outreach Initiative, Learning At the Bench (LAB) Experience classes invite middle and high school students from New York City and the greater metropolitan area to get their hands dirty in the Science Outreach and Education Laboratory under the guidance of practicing scientists and Ph.D. students currently pursuing their own research on the same campus. LAB Experience classes illuminate the link between the scientific research surrounding genetics and our understanding of human disease; bridging the gap between what happens in the lab and what happens in your doctor’s office. Students learn best practices and apply results of authentic experimentation to case studies; nurturing a burgeoning love of science in the most conducive of environments.
