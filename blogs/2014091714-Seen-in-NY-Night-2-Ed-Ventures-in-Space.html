**Keep Private** Hey all, I wanted to make a quick insider post that we have confirmed the next Seen in NY Night for October 2nd from 7-9pm, right here in the lab. If you have time to spare, please let Diana know (or comment below) that you're willing to volunteer for the event. I'll be posting a public, more official post next week but wanted to give you all the heads up right away.
