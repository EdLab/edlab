A "Primer" for Non-Education Writers...Maybe

Kate Meersschaert

November 20, 2012

I came-across this piece while searching for High Five leads this AM. While not learning-focused enough to qualify... I thought I would share it with the team. At first I hoped it would explain usually mystifying ed-related concepts or terms and instead, it seems to lean more towards a diatribe on why non-ed folks shouldn't write about education. It does however, include helpful reading assignments for well-known writers, courtesy of the author (who is a historian and award-winning teacher), that could be helpful or informative.
