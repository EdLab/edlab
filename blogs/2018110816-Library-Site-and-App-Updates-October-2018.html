Of the total site searches, 21% were filtered by category. Here’s what people preferred to see:

  • Books (23%)

Only 11% of total site searches went on to look for “More Results.” Not surprisingly, the categories most sought after were Articles and Books.

  • For people who filtered by Articles, 71% went on for more article results

  • For people who filtered by Books, 25% went on for more book results

Takeaways

  • On the website homepage, it might be worth considering adding “CLIO” and “Proquest” quicklinks, as these are consistently among the top-visited outlinks (not to mention, “Proquest” was the most searched term this month).

  • On the app, I wonder if the content pages are buried too deep to be browsable. People don’t seem to want to click beyond the front page of the “More” section. Or maybe they don’t need to, so they don’t. I’m looking forward to doing user testing to explore this one further!

  • As we think about future app developments, we may want to design with our on campus users in mind, since they seem to be the ones most readily adopting the app.
