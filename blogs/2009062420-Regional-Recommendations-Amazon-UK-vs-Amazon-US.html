Regional Recommendations: Amazon UK vs. Amazon US

Jeff Goldenson

June 24, 2009

So I was looking at a book (I highly recommend it) last night and I noticed something. On the UK Amazon site, the recommendations of other books I may like were different than the recommendations I got from the US site. And in fact, I liked the UK recommendations better!
