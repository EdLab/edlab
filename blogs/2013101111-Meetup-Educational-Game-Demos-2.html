Meetup: Educational Game Demos #2

Kate Meersschaert

October 11, 2013

Event Details: Wednesday, October 16, 2013 7:00 PM NYU Game Center 2 Metrotech Center, 8th Floor Brooklyn, ny 11201 Price: $7.00 per person Register here! (Please Note: $ is involved, so please check w/ Hui Soo first before registering) Event Description:

We'll have several great educational game demos (in the works or recently released) presented by local GBL innovators here to share their insights and open the floor to Q&A. Followed by networking with other designers, educators and enthusiasts in the game-based learning sphere.
Presenters will include one of my all-time favorites... Gamestar Mechanic! (check-out Demetri's Review here)
