Home Journey 5.29.2009: Revisit "The City of Heaven on Earth" in China

Ting Yuan

June 4, 2009

Hangzhou, known as "The City of Heaven on Earth" quoted from an old Chinese poem, is one of the most beautiful Chinese cities. It is about a 2 hour train ride from Shanghai, just like from NY to Philly. I used to visit there a lot during my college years in Shanghai, sitting beside its West Lake to feel the breeze whenever my friends and I wanted to spend a weekend outside of Shanghai. The Shanghai-Hangzhou train ticket today only costs $6 (it used to be $3).
