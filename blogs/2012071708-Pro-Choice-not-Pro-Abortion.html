Pro Choice, not Pro Abortion

Khalil Abubakar

July 17, 2012

One of the most debated issues during any presidential election is the abortion issue. Both Republicans and Democrats have strong stances on the issue and there seems to be little in the way of compromise. Additionally, abortion has not only become a political issue, it has become a social, religious and moral issue. President Obama has made it clear where he stands on the issue, similar to what he did with gay marriage several months ago. I am sure presidential candidate also has a direct stance on where he stands on the abortion issue.
