Using documentary video to explore the city

Erin Murphy

October 16, 2009

I discovered this interesting video site through the Gothamist. It's called Revel in New York and it features short videos about interesting and quirky New Yorkers. But it offers more than just video - the website lists the subjects favorite places in NYC. I think its a pretty interesting way to explore the city. It also works in reverse - you can find a place in NYC and see who recommended it and watch their video.
