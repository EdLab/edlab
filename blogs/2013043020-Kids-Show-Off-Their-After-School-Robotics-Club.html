Kids Show Off Their After-School Robotics Club

jvazquez

April 30, 2013


Even though the school day has ended, 4th and 5th graders of Smith Elementary in Spring, Texas are ready to keep learning. They are part of the after school robotics club, a place where they can learn about real-world problem solving, engineering, and teamwork. These students genuinely enjoy their time in the club. One student summed up the club in one word: "fun"!

The robotics club encourages kids to apply their knowledge of STEM subjects and allows them to gain the practical skills necessary to succeed in increasingly collaborative learning environments.
