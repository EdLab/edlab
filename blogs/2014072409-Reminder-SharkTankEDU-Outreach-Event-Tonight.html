Reminder: #SharkTankEDU Outreach Event Tonight!

Kate Meersschaert

July 24, 2014

(Image: By Simon_sees from Australia (Sea World Shark Tank Uploaded by russavia) [CC-BY-2.0 (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0)], via Wikimedia Commons) Join me and the EdLab outreach team for a night of lively discussion as startups pitch their ELL-focused products to a panel of ELL instructors. Hosted by the DOE and our friends at Innovate NYC, this event like all previous #SharkTankEDU events, should be really interesting and worthwhile. If you are still interested in attending, please let me know in the comments below and I will secure a place for you with the Innovate NYC team. Details: Thursday, July 24th from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM Thoughtworks 99 Madison Avenue, 15th Flr , New York, NY Go summer outreach team! Go!
