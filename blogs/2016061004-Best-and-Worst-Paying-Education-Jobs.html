Best and Worst Paying Education Jobs

abagigah92

June 10, 2016

Education professions, like many other professions, spend years in school trying to achieve the best degree possible in order to land the best job in the industry. Wages in the education sector ranges from $25,000-$100,000s annually. Forbes recently outlined the best and the worst paying jobs in education. Their list was compiled through the use of BLS Occupational, Employment and Wages data of 2015. Law teachers are the highest paid in education with a mean annual salary of $126,230. Some of the highest paid professions are in post secondary roles in health specialties, economics, engineering and agricultural science. On the other hand, the worst paying job is teacher's assistant, with a mean annual salary of $26,550. Preschool teachers, teacher's assistant and substitute teachers are also at the bottom of the pay scale.
