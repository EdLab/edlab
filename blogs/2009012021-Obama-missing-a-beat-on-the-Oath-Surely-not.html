Obama missing a beat on the Oath? Surely not!

Adam Mitchinson

January 20, 2009

Those of us who watched the inauguration (is there anyone who didnt?!) would have seen what seemed to be Obama stumbling on the Oath. A sign of early Presidential nerves? Definitely not! It was in fact the new Chief Justice who stumbled, pronouncing the words in the wrong order:



http://thecaucus.blogs.nytimes.com/2009/01/20/i-do-solemnly-swear/

http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/28753348/

And if you re-watch the ceremony, as was pointed out to me by a Professor of mine, it is very clear that Obama knew that Roberts' wording was out of order and he tried to get him to correct it. A good early sign of clarity-of-mind in a nervous situation! Afterwards, the new President definitely didn't miss a beat with his speech. I hope you all enjoyed the day, it was a great experience for an 'outsider'.
