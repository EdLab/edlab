Obtaining and Managing Patents to Create a Competitive Advantage

Adrienne Garber

February 22, 2010

What is intellectual property due diligence? Can developing a patent strategy add value to your research and/or product development initiatives? What is a "patent value story" and how can you use it to attract investors? The above-mentioned questions and more will be discussed during the latest installment of Columbia's Technology Management Seminar Series. Columbia Technology Ventures and the Columbia Engineering Alumni Association to host the upcoming lecture, "Patent Strategy for Emerging Technologies: Maximizing Business Value." Details:

  • Who: Presented by patent attorney Mark Catan, Esq. of Miles & Stockbridge P.C.
  • When: Wednesday March 3, 2010, 6:30 PM - 7:30 PM EST
  • Where: Schapiro Center, Interschool Lab, Room 750, Morningside Campus
  • Address: 530 West 120th Street, New York, NY 10027
    • The event is free. Must RSVP to attend. Register here.
