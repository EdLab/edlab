Datavis for Nonprofits...

Janice Joo

June 7, 2012

I participated in a webinar this afternoon called "Nonprofits & Data: Worth 1,000 Words - Visualizing Your Data". The slides can be downloaded here. (Thank you Kate for posting about it earlier!) The talk was intended for nonprofits, so here is briefly what I found most meaningful to us: Create tweetable chunks of information. Elaborate text is good, but infographics/ data visualizations can be passed along social media networks. Your message is more likely to spread. The main takeaway (in slides) ... GROUP GENIUS! I especially found it interesting that we need the right people to come up with the right stories... this is where all the past teachers' and administrators' insight will be extremely valuable in coming up with [visual] stories that our NLT readers will find compelling. Content for the D&R meeting I keep talking about is shaping up pretty fast...
