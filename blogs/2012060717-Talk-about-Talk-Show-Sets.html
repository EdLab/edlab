Talk about Talk Show Sets

Janice Joo

June 7, 2012

I've been invited to join Kate and Luke on their interviews with EdTech Talks :O. Super excited! You may have seen the latest one. The set is open for suggestions (so I am told)... So why don't we get the ball rolling with suggestions for the set, though it is stuck in a basement room for now... FYI there are 2 interviews scheduled for next week. What talk shows make you feel like you're in on the discussion? Letterman? The View? Rachael Ray (loft feel)? Anything from another country or outside of tech-related TV? What features would promote that grabbing effect? Furniture? Backdrop? Any sound effects? Any visual effects? EXAMPLES Two couches facing each other at angle. Camera angle is interesting: Back of interviewer and clear view of interviewee's expressions. PS this is a Canadian current events talk show (George Strombolopolous)... TV in the middle bearing a logo? Backdrop in natural lighting (perhaps outside even)... Black background with just backlighting/head fills, bearing only logo post production A desk in between guests? Candid closeup clips of guests smiling, with shaky camera
