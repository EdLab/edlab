#NYTechDay... Hey Girl, Ryan Will Be There*

Kate Meersschaert

April 22, 2013

(Image from @nynjpa & This #NYTechDay post) #NYTechDay is billed by organizers as the "world's largest startup expo... read their official description below. Hundreds of startups across a wide range of verticals (including #edtech) will have booths set-up at Pier 92 to share their work, network with VC's and potential employees. Lets get a team to #EdLabRepresent!!! Here are my goals for attendance: 1) Gain new outreach contacts 2) Learn re: the NY Tech community 3) Meet press contacts 4) Meet potential EdLab team members If you are interested please check with Hui Soo (during work hours!) and add your +1 below!

NY Tech Day is a massive science fair for entrepreneurs to exhibit their startups to thousands of consumers, investors, first adopters, job-seekers, major companies, press and media. The inaugural expo SOLD OUT New York's Lexington Armory with 200 startups and 4,500 attendees. The Second Annual NY Tech Day is poised to outstrip last year's success by growing bigger and bolder than ever. Don't miss out on the largest startup event in the world! Join us as a startup exhibitor or attendee April 25!
Details: 10am-5:30pm Pier 92 | New York City 711 12th Ave, New York, NY 10019 *Not really... but awesome "viral promo" pic @nynjpa!
