Rocket Man

dinosossi2

June 28, 2017




SpaceX, Elon Musk’s Tesla side project, launched not one, but two successful satellites into orbit this past weekend. This is yet another signal that the goal of successfully creating reusable rockets is something that could become a reality in the not-too-distant future. 


This space race is becoming even faster from competition from Blue Origin, a similar attempt at rocketry fronted by Mr. Amazon himself, Jeff Bezos. Blue Origin just announced the building of a rocket plant in Alabama.

 



On a similar note, successfully creating a viable business putting people in orbit could ensue.


But don’t get your jet packs ready just yet.


The current sticker price for a one-person trip outside earth’s atmosphere is a shocking $62 million.


Yep, income inequality rears its ugly head even in the far reaches of our universe.


I guess the George Jetsons in our midst will just have to settle for flying cars.
