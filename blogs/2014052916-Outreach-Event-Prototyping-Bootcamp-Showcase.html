Outreach Event: Prototyping Bootcamp Showcase

Brian.Sweeting

May 29, 2014

4.0 Schools and EDesign Labs are hosting: Prototyping Bootcamp Showcase Thursday, June 5th at 7pm ThoughtWorks 99 Madison Ave Four teams will prototype the products they've been developing:

  • CODR moves students from Scratch to real coding languages
  • POPQ connects students with careers
  • Bright(er) Ideas equips students with tools for project based learning
  • StoryBits guides students through story writing based on experiential learning
Not only will these teams share their work, but attendees will have an opportunity to use the products and ask questions. I went to a similar event last summer had a great time playing around with Breadcrumbs (read Laura's EdLab Review here).

Let me (or Kate) know if you're interested and we'll add you as a guest on our invites!
