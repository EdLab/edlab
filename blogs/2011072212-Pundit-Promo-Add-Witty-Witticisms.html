Pundit Promo! Add Witty Witticisms!

Kate Meersschaert

July 22, 2011

(PRIVATE!) As we rapidly approach the launch of Pundit (August 5th), I would love your thoughts on the following irreverent slogans to be featured on a series of three "teaser" postcards and posters. Here is a short description of Pundit... Pundit is a course search and recommendation tool. On a basic level, use Pundit to simplify your search for upcoming courses... complete a few additional steps, and Pundit will generate custom course recommendations based on your interests & background. My goals for these slogans are that they 1) are funny 2) eye catching & 3) get people to visit the site. Think OKCupid of course recommendation (have you checked-out the blog! Wow... supposedly, legitimate researchers really value their data!)! Feel free to add your thoughts on these slogans and add witticisms of your own... the winning entries will be featured in our promo campaign! Fame & fortune would surely follow... 1) I could never find that perfect class. Now perfect class and I are getting married. Thanks Pundit. 2) Some classes just don't get me. Now I feel appreciated & you laugh at my jokes! Thanks Pundit. 3) Lost in a sea of course choices? I was, until I discovered personalized course recommendation. Thanks Pundit.
