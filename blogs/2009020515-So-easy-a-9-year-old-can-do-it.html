So easy a 9 year old can do it.

Adam Mitchinson

February 5, 2009

So while we all bask in the relative sense of achievement at collectively coming up with ~50 mobile apps in yesterdays seminar, news is emerging that might just take the shine off our accomplishments. According to Reuters and other news sources, a nine year old (yeah 9!) has created a popular iphone app! The app, called Doodle Kids, allows users to draw on their screen in many colors and styles and then erase their work by shaking the device! The creator, Lim Ding Wen from Singapore, said he created the app for his little sisters aged 3 & 5. Couldn't they have created it for themselves?!
