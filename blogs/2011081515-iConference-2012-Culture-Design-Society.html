iConference 2012: Culture - Design - Society

Sharon Hsiao

August 15, 2011

iSchools are a collective of Information Schools dedicated to advancing the information field in the 21st Century. These schools, colleges, and departments have been newly created or are evolving from programs formerly focused on specific tracks such as information technology, library science, informatics, and information science. The study of information is interdisciplinary, fed by multiple diverse fields.
