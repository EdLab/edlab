Live Blogging Walkout

Nantwi

September 22, 2008

Last night, the curtain came down on the House That Ruth Built as the last official major league game was played at Yankee Stadium. Arguably the most heralded sports venue in North America, Yankee Stadium has a hosted a slew of events in addition to baseball. Boxing, papal vists, and rock concerts have all become part of the legendary ball park. Amy Nelson followed all the festivites at Yankee stadium during last night's game between the Yankees and Baltimore Orioles through a live blog. This Wednesday, the Office of Diversity and Community Affairs Diversity Film Series will broadcast Walkout, the true story of the Chicano students of East LA, who in 1968 staged several dramatic walkouts in their high schools to protest academic prejudice and dire school conditions. The film will be shown in Grace Dodge Hall, room 179 at from 1-3:30pm to be followed by a group discussion. I, along with the two interns, Malves and Zoraida, will be there to live blog the event but will welcome anyone who will be interested in joining us.
