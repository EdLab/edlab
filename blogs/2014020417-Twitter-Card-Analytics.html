Kate Meersschaert

February 4, 2014

In our endeavor to continue to make NewLearningTimes.com content more sharable and viral on Twitter, I am excited to discover the richer, more in-depth analytics made possible by Twitter Cards (Thanks for the heads-up Dr. Hsiao). Pranav & the engineering team implemented these cards for both @NLTTC & @Vialogues and it is fascinating to get this deeper dive into what is being shared on Twitter (when & by whom).
