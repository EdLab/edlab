High Schoolers Create Soft, Ingestible Robots You Actually Want to Eat

Melanie.Hering

January 10, 2019


The field of soft robotics often takes its inspiration from living things, from octopi to vines, but a group of high school students at the Haverford School was inspired by something altogether different: candy.

In considering how to create an ingestible robot, the team realized they could use gummy candy to make a bot that is flexible, dissolvable, and tasty. Using a gummy bear base, the students created an edible actuator that can be inflated and ingested for a variety of purposes. They have created gummy bots in a variety of sizes and models.
