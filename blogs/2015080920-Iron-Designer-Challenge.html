Iron Designer Challenge

EdLab Studios

August 9, 2015


"And today’s secret ingredient is…" kicks off the challenge on the Food Network’s "Iron Chef." Substitute "material" for "ingredient" and you’ve tuned into the chaos and creativity that is the Iron Designer Challenge . Modeled after the popular television program, the Urban Assembly School for Design and Construction’s annual event, now in its 6th year, puts teams’ ingenuity, speed, and adaptability to the test in a 3 hour building challenge that determines who is the true Iron Designer. Eight teams of SDC students join forces with a fleet of mentors; professionals currently working in the field as architects, engineers, and designers to take on this year’s all too relevant theme: water conservation. Together during a 6 week lead up, these diverse teams meet to strategize their approach to the topic, develop their backstory, and draw up blueprints while students gain insight into the kind of planning and professionalism they can expect when they enter the workforce one day. At the three hour mark, it’s hammers down and the panel of judges make their way to each life-sized structure for a formal presentation and demo of its functionality. Trophies for structural integrity, best spectacle, and most creative story will be awarded but one team will walk away with the coveted title of "Iron Designer Champion."
