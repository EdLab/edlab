Re-imagining NYC Dumpsters as Mobile Learning Spaces

Brian.Sweeting

October 16, 2013

The Atlantic Cities highlights a project aimed at turning the city's dumpsters into mobile learning laboratories. The vision for Inflato Dumpster:

Initially, we see this space as taking the form of a hacker workshop where simple, off-the-shelf components can be procured and combined to provide local residents with access to technology and a means for hands-on testing of public space concerns.
This is certainly less gross than that time NYC turned dumpsters into swimming pools.
