Basement Work

Carmel Addae

June 22, 2014

Last week, the DigiQ team worked on removing over 7,000 books from the basement and sub-basement as part of the college's efforts to digitize our archives. Most of the books we pulled out were part of the TCANA collection and master thesis. Overall, the process went very smoothly. We came up with a system that enabled us to work diligently and effective. We worked most of day last Saturday to remove most of the books and finished it on Monday morning. Once the books were removed, we stored it on the ground floor for the shipping company to send it for digitization. After the book removal, we worked on cleaning and making sure the basement and sub-basement rooms were in top condition as those rooms will be closed for the rest of the summer for cleaning. As part of this effort, we recorded all damages in the rooms so they can be repaired at a later date. Thanks to Hui Soo, Laura, George and the entire DigiQ for all their efforts in this endeavor.
