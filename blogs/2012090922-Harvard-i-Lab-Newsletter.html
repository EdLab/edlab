Harvard i-Lab Newsletter

Kate Meersschaert

September 9, 2012

Private! I thought I would share Harvard's i-Lab newsletter... visit this link to check-it-out. I have also attached a screenshot as well. What are your thoughts? Should we adopt a similar model for the EdLab? Why or why not? What are the strengths and weaknesses of the design and content? Personally, I find the design to be simple, intuitive and easy-to-read. Content-wise, it feels like a nice balance of upcoming events and news. If we were to create an EdLab newsletter, how often should we send-it to subscribers? To whom? What should we feature? Events, product/app news etc.? I look-forward to your thoughts and feedback!
