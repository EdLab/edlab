The Intrepid Sea, Air and Space Museum

EdLab Studios

March 10, 2013


The Intrepid Sea, Air and Space Museum (based on an aircraft carrier now permanently anchored in the Hudson River) is hosting Kids Week while school is not in session. One of the themes of the week is "Science Meets Science Fiction," and all of the talks, experiences, and play-stations of the day use this theme to explore the science behind - you got it - sea, air and space. Playful, interactive and out-of-the-box, this museum day uses pop culture, imagination and a healthy dose of nerdy-fun to explore and learn by considering the truths, fictions and possibilities of science as popularly imagined. From strolling Star Wars characters to talks on the give-and-take between cinematic and real-life science inspiration, this is a fun day of intrepid exploration for all.
