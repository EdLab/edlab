EzProxy November Usage Report

Nikita.Bogdanov

December 6, 2018

The November usage report for EzProxy is now live. Here are some of the basic findings:


  • 15,080 logins in total: 54% from on campus and 46% from off campus.
  • 2,222 unique UNIs logged, with an average of 3.1 logins per user.
  • 20% of users logged in once or twice, but some users logged in upwards of 70 times.
  • 85% of all logins from the state of New York.
  • 3.7% of off-campus logins from abroad, representing 40 countries.
  • 88 GB total downloaded: 55% of this off-campus and 45% on-campus.
  • ProQuest and OCLC had the most amount of data downloaded from them.
  • Because of insufficient data from previous months, comparisons are at this time impractical.


Check out the linked report for more detail, and as always, comment below or let me know if you have any questions.
