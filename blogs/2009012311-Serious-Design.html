Serious Design

Skye MacLeod

January 23, 2009

Check out this Ted talk with Paula Scher if you get a chance: She speaks about the importance of "serious play" in design as opposed to the frequent fate of so-called "good design," which she terms as "solemn." That is, when a design has been standardized and is in high demand, it changes from an innovative concept to something more tangible, concrete, or solemn. I find this concept very interesting as I approach (and re-approach) future episodes that involve animation or puppetry.
