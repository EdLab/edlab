CSI: Sixth Grade

Brian.Sweeting

March 12, 2014


Sixth graders in South Dakota are getting lessons in forensics thanks to a new education technology program aimed at familiarizing students with technical careers. And like the popularity of investigative dramas, these interactive crime labs may be preferred over more traditional programs. Middle school students seem to enjoy developing hands-on crime-solving skills, even if they are a little young to be using them.
