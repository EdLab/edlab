Pedaling It Forward

dinosossi2

March 13, 2016


When Anurudh Ganesan was fatally ill as a child in rural India, his grandmother carried him over 10 miles of rough terrain to a clinic to save his life. The vaccine he needed had been ruined because of improper storage in the extreme heat, but thankfully he fought through his illness and survived.

Vaccinations become useless if they are stored in extreme temperatures. Globally, 1.5 million die every year due to insufficient vaccines. Thankful for his own survival, Anurudh wanted to help others and develop a portable refrigeration system for vaccine transportation. With the help of his father, he created VAXXWAGON, which allows doctors to transport vaccines safely and effectively by bicycle.
