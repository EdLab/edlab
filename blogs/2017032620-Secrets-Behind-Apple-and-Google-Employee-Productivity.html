Secrets Behind Apple and Google Employee Productivity

zhou

March 26, 2017

According to this article Why Employees At Apple And Google Are More Productive, Apple and Google are the most productive companies not because of talent . These companies have three secret weapons:

1. Let the most talented people work together.

2. Shield their employees from institutional/organizational drags.

3. Engage the employees in awesome works with inspiring leaders.

This is great advice for us to follow to keep improving and making great things happen at EdLab.
