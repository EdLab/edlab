New Learning Times (NLT) Inspiration: Hybrid Pedagogy

Kate Meersschaert

August 13, 2012

Benefits of High 5 Research... While reading my trusty news sources for High Five leads (check-out Friday's thanks to Laura S.!) I came across this site, Hybrid Pedagogy: A Digital Journal of Teaching and Technology, as a link-through from Audrey Water's blog. At Michelle's suggestion I discovered Ms. Water's site/newsletter and assessed it here as possible NLT inspiration... check-out this interview with her on MOOCs (here are my thoughts). NLT Inspiration? I like the agile, blog-like format, active Twitter feed and overall feeling of minimal, yet edgy design. The content is interesting and often takes a more confrontational, questioning tone. The author(s) integrate Storify.com's embeddable, multimedia story lines in their posts and I really like this additional multimedia... read my review of Storify.com here What are your thoughts? What elements should we examine & evolve for NLT? Check-out their "Concordance of Digital Tools" for ALT/BLT inspiration as well...
