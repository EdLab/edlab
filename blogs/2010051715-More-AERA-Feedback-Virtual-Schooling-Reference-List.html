More AERA Feedback: Virtual Schooling Reference List

Ting Yuan

May 17, 2010

Michael K. Barbour, a professor at Wayne State University recently reviewed our AERA paper on K-12 virtual schooling. Professor Barbour has been doing research in virtual schooling for over ten years and shared some insights on our paper as well as the discussant's comments. Additionally, he suggested more readings to look at. The following is a list of the references he included. In the next a couple of weeks, we will try to include them into the paper and highlight some of the articles in the coming EdLab Research Digest as well.
