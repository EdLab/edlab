UAE to Construct Largest Mars Simulation on Earth

gnatriello

November 7, 2017



The United Arab Emirates has announced plans to construct the largest Mars simulation in the desert near Dubai.  This is part of a larger plan called Mars 2117 which aims to establish a city on Mars within 100 years.  The first step is sending an unmanned probe to Mars by 2021, a project being staffed by a team of more than 100 with an average age of 27 and comprised of 30% women.  The UAE is leveraging interest in this project to encourage science and technology learning among its students.
