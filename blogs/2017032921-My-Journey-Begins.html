Here are the classes: -Organic Chemistry 1 offered by MIT OPEN COURSEWARE "taught" by Dr. Sarah Tabacco and Prof. Barbara. - Introduction to Family Engagement in Education offered by Harvard through edX. The "instructor" is Karen L. Mapp. - Leading Ambitious Teaching and Learning offered by the University of Michigan through edX. This course is "taught" by Don Peurach. These classes are self-paced so I plan on posting my daily experiences on this Pressible site. Please let me know what you think in the comments section below.
