Introducing Chromebook

abagigah92

February 10, 2013

Google's Chromebook is been viewed as one of the best laptops in the market. With American classrooms fast transforming into the digital age, Google's Chromebook serves as a platform in that transition into distance learning as the laptop is easy and secure to use. Moreover, the laptop is built to favor the workload of students and teachers and comes with a built-in virus protection and stays up to date whenever there is an update. Chromebook has many positive features built in such as many of Google's products: Gmail, Drive and YouTubeEdu. Many other free and paid educational applications can be find in the Chrome web store. Chrome Instant is another feature that allows for a faster and better browsing experience. The laptop also back up files online so there are no worries of losing important documents. The difference between Chromebook and other laptops is that it has a cloud storage, speed, connectivity and upgrades built In.
