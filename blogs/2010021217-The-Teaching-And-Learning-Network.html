The Teaching And Learning Network

Weiqi Ma

February 12, 2010

Hi all, I am the new guy. Nice to work with all of you at EdLab. I am currently working on an E-learning system with Ellen, Ankit and Faisal. Our goal is to create a social online environment that supports learning and teaching. We have lots of great ideas about features we could have in our system. Some of the basic functionalities we have in mind includes: 1. Social Networking features such as "friending", profile pages, status updates, messaging. 2. Creating, Delivering and Joining Courses with a bunch of interesting tools like Knowledge Sharing System( something like Wiki), Blogs, Chat, Assessment Tools etc.. 3. What makes our project unique is EVERYONE is able to create courses and our system could even recommend courses to users. Also, Status updates and news feeds aggregate activity from courses as well as friends and groups. This is the general description of the TLN project we are working on. If you are interested in our project, here is some slides regarding to more details about our project. We would love any comment or idea on our project from you.
