New Chapter

Nara Kasbergen

March 3, 2014

** Private Please Most of you have already heard, but just in case you haven't, last Friday I accepted a position as a software engineer for NPR in DC, so I'll be leaving EdLab and New York City the weekend of March 22nd. Since the 21st is a holiday, my last day will most likely be Thursday, March 20th. I know some people might be going out of town for the long weekend, but I hope you'll join me for a farewell shindig at AmCaf on the 20th. I'll bring Louis, since he'll most likely be in town to help me move. Thanks for the memories! I'll miss you guys a lot, and please come and visit me in DC!
