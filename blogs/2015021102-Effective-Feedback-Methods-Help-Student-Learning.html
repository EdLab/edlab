Effective Feedback Methods Help Student Learning

Khalil Abubakar

February 11, 2015

Several studies have tried to explain how effective feedback, can enhance students ability to understand answers better. Based on 40 quality studies, three most effective explanation methods, based on computer learning software have been mentioned in this articlethis article. First method suggests computer systems should flag wrong answers and make students progress to a next question even if they get it wrong. Teachers should teach or further explain these answer later on. Also spoon-feeding answers to students is not an effective ways of learning. This method suggests software provide hints to help students learn the answer and math steps to show how to solve a question. Finally depending on the question's difficulty you may decide to give feed back during the test or later after the student have finished answering all questions.
