Laughing With Your Students

jvazquez

September 14, 2014


Galeet Cohen, a middle school teacher, has a funny way of keeping her class in order. How’s that, you ask? She makes them laugh!

Never missing an opportunity to chide a student or inject a little self-deprecating humor, Galeet has crafted a teaching persona that puts her kids at ease and on their toes. Even when they act up, she reacts in character with a logical course of action. This approach has earned her respect from countless students who are used to getting a "because I said so" response to a prescribed punishment like detention.

Galeet appreciates a good sense of humor. "Kids are funny!" she says. The way she says it, with the hint of a laugh cracking her voice, makes you wait in anticipation for a punchline. It’s easy to see how she could captivate her students.

Besides the obvious laughter factor, what benefits could be had from a wisecracking teacher? Tell us about the funniest teacher you’ve ever had in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
