Family Income Found to Play Larger Role in Educational Success

Kate Meersschaert

February 10, 2012

Recent findings highlighted in this New York Times article, show an ever widening gap in school performance between the rich and poor. Previous studies had focused on race, but this new finding shows a direct relationship between availability of financial resources and school performance. Stanford Professor Sean F.
