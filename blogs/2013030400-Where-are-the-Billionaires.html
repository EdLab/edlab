Where are the Billionaires?

Christopher Zamora

March 4, 2013

This article from CNN lists the cities with the most billionaires. According to the article, "the world has at least 1,453 billionaires, with about half of those residing in the U.S. and China, according to the Hurun Global Rich List." It seems appropriate that the two largest global economies will have the most billionaires. In terms of cities with the most billionaires, Moscow leads the charge with 76 billionaires and New York City comes in second with 70 and Hong Kong rounding out the top three list with 52. The most billionaires as in individuals are in Asia, and then North America. These facts about billionaires make me wonder, while despite our country having so much wealth, there are also many individuals and/or families who struggle and have so little money in the U.S. I feel that billionaires should use their money to try and help those in need in the country. They have enough money, why not waste it supporting the people who have very little money. What are your thoughts on this and does it surprise you that American has so many billionaires?
