Disrupting Poverty With The Girl Effect

DebraLee

April 20, 2018


"We're ambitious and optimistic. By 2020 our work will have 30 million active participants inspiring more than 350 million people to create a new normal for girls." (Girl Effect)

The Normals: Old and New

Girl Effect is about transformation, specifically from the old normal to the new normal, where the old normal is represented by 60% of girls in Pakistan who rarely or never feel confident speaking in the presence of boys and men, 84% of Ethiopian girls who do not attend secondary school, and 14% of Ghanaian girls aged 15-19 who are already mothers. The New Normal is Girl Effect’s unique approach to social norm change that uses 21st century technology like social media along with girl-centered community engagement to challenge individual and social barriers that hold girls and their communities back.

Technology Enabled Girls Connect

One of the ways Girl Effect achieves this is through Girls Connect, an immersive program for girls that anonymously answers questions about growing up in challenging circumstances without judgment. Girls dial a number and listen to pre-recorded inspiring, entertaining stories embedded with educational lessons: girls learning how to speak up, finding their own path in life, and learning to nurture meaningful relationships. The narratives cover multifaceted issues including violence, healthcare, personal finance and education. The program also connects girls to a 'role model': specially-trained call center operatives who can answer girls’ questions truthfully and talk through the stories to make them meaningful and relevant to their own lives.
