D&R: Re-reading New Learning Times

Brian.Sweeting

July 8, 2014

*Private* Hi Everyone, During today's D&R we'll be digging through the archives of NLT and thinking about how we think about all the content we've amassed. For the first activity, each group will search for five articles to recommend for their scenario. Try to include articles from a variety of streams. Please include your recommendations in the comments below. Group 1 An intern from a venture capital firm is doing research on companies to invest in related to higher learning. Group 2 A private math tutor is following up after an outreach event and is wondering if we have any recommendations on K-12 math tools. Group 3 You're writing an EdLab Review for a new learning management system and want to compare it to products we've already reviewed. Group 4 A teacher is making a case to her administration for incorporating video games in her classroom and is looking for supporting research. Group 5 TC is offering a TESOL certificate program and we want to provide the participants with additional resources for their students. Group 6 An MBA grad is networking for edtech jobs in NYC and wants to know about the most influential startup founders.
