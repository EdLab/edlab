Fix it and forget it? Forget it.

jvazquez

May 13, 2009

Can I interest anyone in some rancid office meats? An employee at an AT&T office in San Jose, CA was just trying to do a nice thing, something that is usually avoided at all costs: clean the office fridge. In doing so, she cleared away piles of rotted vegetables and decomposing meat and began to scrub, scrub, scrub, with a multitude of cleaning agents. Soon, the combined smell of the once-tasty food and the effects of the chemical-heavy disinfectants mixed together to create some monstrously noxious fumes, sending s e v e n people to the hospital, with an additional 28 needing medical attention. To underscore the amplitude of the situation even more, a hazmat team had to be called in and the entire building was evacuated. And what happened to the worker who was in the epicenter of the stench machine? Absolutely nothing. She suffers from allergies and never smelled a thing! Let that be a lesson to all of yous.
