A Conference on the Future in the Future

jvazquez

July 31, 2014

Fall is technically the future. Hot off the tails of yesterday's High 5 nugget on wearables comes the news of an entire conference centered on the subject of future tech. Behold: The Designers of Things Conference. If you fancy yourself a connoisseur of wearable tech, 3D printing, or the Internet of Things-- or if you just want to learn more about all of the above-- these might be your people. The conference will be held in the unsurprising city of San Francisco on Sept 23rd and 24th. Judging by the slotted presentations, they'll be very general, allowing people from various industries to pick up what the speakers are putting down and apply them however they want once they get back to work. I appreciate the refreshing tone of the site. It even has a "Justification Toolkit" in case someone needed to give their boss a reason to let them go (hint hint). Hopefully the conference itself will be just as entertaining. Sound like your cup of tea? What industry(s) do you think could most benefit from 3D printing, wearables, and IoT? (And how soon before those terms become the next "Web 2.0" and "MOOC"?)
