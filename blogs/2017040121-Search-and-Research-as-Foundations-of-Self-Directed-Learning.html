Search and Research as Foundations of Self-Directed Learning

gnatriello

April 1, 2017


In a lecture at UCI Dan Russell from Google discusses the future of learning and the importance of skills in research and search.  This overview of the talk highlights the importance of evolving literacies as foundational for self-directed learning.  Russell covers search strategies in his blog and he offers several courses in advanced searching. Although progress in the development of search tools promises to enhance self-directed learning opportunities, realizing such opportunities depends on learners acquiring the new skills needed to make good use of such tools.
