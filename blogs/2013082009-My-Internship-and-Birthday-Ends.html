My Internship and Birthday Ends

Roy Kim

August 20, 2013

Seminars, free food abound, running-club, basketball days, D and R sessions... all these in a singular week. How is it that EdLab has never failed to have that good humored intern-of-the-week celebration with ice-cream and popcorn barrels filled to the roof? When I think back to how laughably fun this summer was, I can't help but grin foolishly at anyone nearby. Before I came, I didn't know anything about programming. I didn't even know that Amazon had so many cloud services. The full time developers still took me under their lovely wings and today I am left with feelings of growth and appreciation. Thank you! By the same token of appreciation I want to say that Sakar is right: one of my most favorite parts of being at EdLab is what I learned from the summer interns as a whole. We became friends so quickly... Best of luck to all of you! I love working for EdLab.
