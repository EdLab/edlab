Hacker Tour Comes to a University Near You

Kate Meersschaert

October 18, 2012

Proving that "bus tours" as promotion are apparently not totally dead... the "Hacker Tour" is coming to a University near you. Housed in an airstream trailer and sponsored by a wide variety of startups and companies (from Codecademy to Etsy), the initiative hopes to attract young programers on college campuses. The goal of the initiative is not totally clear, although I think the companies hope to attract young talent right out of school and are participating in this promotion so that their "brand" will be top-of-mind. From their site:

Hacker Tour 2012 is an 8 week national bus tour designed to connect fast growing startups and tech companies to top computer science and engineering students across the country.
Do you think this is a cheap trick or a adds some value to campus life?
