2015 Year in Review

EdLab Studios

December 20, 2015


Keeping alive one’s childhood curiosity for learning and adventure can be difficult, especially in a city as bustling and career-oriented as New York. But hidden between the chaos of daily commutes and endless professional, personal and social obligations, is a rich world of learning, new ideas and quirky adventures waiting to be had. For three years, EdLab Studios has been covering the work of organizations and individuals pushing the boundaries of what it means to learn outside of the traditional school setting. This year was especially invigorating—from craft trades to unconventional art forms, from magical historical places to innovative community spaces, there is nothing one cannot learn in New York City.
