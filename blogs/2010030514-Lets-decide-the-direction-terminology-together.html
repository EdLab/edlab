Let's decide the direction terminology together!

Shaoqing Niu

March 5, 2010

I am currently working on the project, TC Routefinder, which is a Flash Internet application to help one navigate in Teachers College. We need to decide the direction terminology to tell users which way to go, and I am posting this blog to ask for your suggestions. Let's look at the picture below:
In this picture, users come to the current position from the previous one, and we want to tell them where to go next. So if the direction is relatively straight, left or right, it is easy to navigate. However, if the next position falls in Area 1-4, we are not sure what terminology to use. One suggestion is to use "turn slight left or right" for Area 1 and Area 2 and to use "turn sharp left or right" for Area 3 and Area 4. Another suggestion is to use an approximate angle. For example, we can tell patrons to "turn 45 degrees to your right" for Area 2 and to "turn 135 degrees to your left" for Area 3. So, Come on! What are your suggestions?
