Play Darfur is Dying

Nantwi

February 26, 2009

As part of our ongoing preparation for the March 4th seminar on Game Design, we are presenting a host of selected online and mini games that will allow everyone in the lab to play before the seminar. This will enable all of us to have a detailed and effective discussion. Today's featured game is Darfur is Dying, a social action game that highlights some of the many problems facing refugees in that region. Created by game designers at the University of Southern California as part of the Darfur Digital Activist Contest, Darfur is Dying is a narrative based simulation where the user plays a Darfurian in a refugee camp who must avoid the Janjaweed while trying to fetch water for refugees back at the camp. In refugee camps in and around Darfur, the possibility of an attack by the Janjaweed is always high and as such leaving the camps even to go a few feet to fetch water can be a terrifying experience. Darfur is Dying is a spiral game and as such players are urged to pass the game on to family, friends and colleagues alike. Hope you enjoy playing the game. Darfur is Dying
