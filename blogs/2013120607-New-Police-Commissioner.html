New Police Commissioner

Francisco.Mendoza

December 6, 2013

Bill Bratton was appointed by mayor elect Bill de Blasio as New York City's new police commissioner. When he was part of LAPD, there was a significant drop in crime there. Prior to his reign, Los Angeles was going through high crime rates. Bratton will bring something new to NYPD as new strategies and tactics will be put in place. I believe the city will have even lower crime rates as a result. He was able to do an excellent job in Los Angeles and believe he will continue to do so in New York. How do you think Bratton will do in New York? This article talks more about our new police commissioner.
