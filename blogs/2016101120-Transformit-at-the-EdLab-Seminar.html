Transformit at the EdLab Seminar

EdLab Studios

October 11, 2016


Founder Cindy Thompson of Transformit, shared the versatility and ingenuity behind the sculptural shapes and interior designs known as Transformit at the EdLab Seminar in January 2015. The EdLab will be using Transformit to create on-the-fly learning spaces in the Smith Learning Theater. Check out the talk and the related discussion on on Vialogues.

Excerpts from the discussion:

@10:23 LauraCostello: That art museum lit by natural light is such a cool idea. I like the thought of different weights of fabric being used to control the flow of natural sunlight. It couldn't work everywhere, but that's part of the charm.

@21:20 Mason: Lots of neat CAD options for flattening 3D models, probably not so much for the change from a sketch to a digital flat pattern. I wonder if Transformit ever considers change of medium using the same flat patterns (e.g. metal or plastic).
