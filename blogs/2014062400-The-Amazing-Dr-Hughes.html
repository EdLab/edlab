The Amazing Dr. Hughes

HSC

June 24, 2014




Brian showing off his art knowledge at the Atlas Institute, the location of our event.
Brian really wanted to walk across the water foundation exhibit below.

But he could not work up the courage to do it and walked away.

But to his credit, he came back after dinner to overcome his fears.

And he slowly walked across the fountain exhibit. And he made it across without getting wet. AMAZING.
