Opera face gestures

Xuyang Shi

April 1, 2009

The famous internet browser producer Opera recently released a new feature called "Face gesture" for its browser (Opera v10). Roberto Mateu, one of the Opera announcer said:"We recognize the future importance of touch interfaces, but we believe that there is another input device that is already present in most new computers and it's ready to enable a whole new way of user-interaction: the webcam". Using the web camera, the Opera v10 can capture the facial gesture of the user, then match with the sample gesture that registered before. After the facial gesture recognized, the Opera can take the corresponding action for the user. For more information, please check out this video.
