Longshot Problems and Solutions

LukeMalone

August 3, 2011

Wow. Thanks everyone. That went better than we expected with passing the headcam and mic around. Having said that, I'm just sure that some of you have an opinion on how to address this problem we're having with sound. In the summer, with the air conditioning on, we can't hear much of anything if we just use the typical sound setup so we need a seasonal solution. So some solutions are: A) pass the mic around like today. Keep in mind that if you're in the front of the room or within a few feet of a camera, you don't need the mic. The mic is just for the dead zone in the middle to the back of the room. B) Have multiple mics at a few tables (this would be difficult in post production so it's not our favorite choice). C) A mic stand in the center of the room where you have to ask your questions Town Hall style. D) We'll try anything once. And as far as the helmet cam goes, thanks for being good sports but we'll probably just assign that to one person per seminar...unless there's a better idea.
