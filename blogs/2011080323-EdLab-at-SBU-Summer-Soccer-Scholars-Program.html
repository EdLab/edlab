EdLab at SBU Summer Soccer Scholars Program

Nantwi

August 3, 2011

A few EdLabbers will be making our way down to Mott Haven HS tomorrow morning to do a Vialogues workshop for South Bronx United (SBU). In keeping with its educational (and athletic) goals, SBU started a month long summer program in which its members take a host of classes from journalism to photography in addition to physical conditioning and soccer practice. One of the workshops SBU members have been taking thus far is on emerging or new technologies in education and as such they asked if the EdLab was willing to do a workshop on one of our projects that fits that bill and Vialogues seemed appropriate since a few of them have been introduced to the tool. Jo and Kate will be doing the workshop while Erick and Ariel K. will film footage that will be used in a variety of ways to promote Vialogues. We will give you the full rundown of how it went later so keep posted. Learn more about SBU through the Vialogues below.
