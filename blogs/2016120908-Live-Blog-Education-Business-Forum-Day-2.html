Live Blog: Education Business Forum Day 2

HSC

December 9, 2016

First up: More 'Ed' for Your 'Tech': Growing your Value with Content Collaboration You're all about technology, you're scaled and secure, and you're ready for the 21st century, consumer-experience K-20 classroom. But are you ready with instructional content appropriate for the devices used in schools and postsecondary institutions? How do you produce or procure that content, with pedagogy that matches learner and institutional needs, while maximizing the possibilities of your platform? And how do you build value for your core-technology company with instructional content, while minimizing the investment? What form should these partnerships take? Moderator: Myron Cizdyn, Chief Executive Officer, The BLPS Group Panelists: Gail Lewis, Director of Product Strategy, myON Michele Molnar, Associate Editor, EdWeek Market Brief Randy Wilhelm, CEO and Co-Founder, Knovation This session is going to be all about content.
