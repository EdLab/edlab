D&R: Twitter and Facebook Analytics

Patrick Williamson

August 4, 2015

Put yourselves in the shoes of our patrons. Think about your experiences with Twitter; the types of links that you click on, the topics that appeal to you, etc. Each group is assigned a different goal - Group 1: Foster discussion and build user community - Group 2: Drive more traffic to NLT, EdLab and Vialogue sites - Group 3: Cultivate more active users and regain lost users - Group 4: Increase virality coefficient Activity 1: Think about the message that you want to send to our users. What were some of the things you guys first came up with? What aspect of Twitter are you prioritizing first? Content? Pictures? Wittiness? How do we increase followers? What are some things that can affect the traffic that a tweet gets? Time of year? Time of day? Content? What promotional ideas do you have for Facebook and Twitter posts to engage more with our following audience? Activity 2: Come up with a way to use Twitter that is outside of its traditional purpose of posting and sharing information to achieve your groups goal. Ex. Using Twitter as a promotional tool for trivia with prizes Activity 3: Watch this Vialogue https://vialogues.com/vialogues/play/23141/ and work as a team to create a catchy line that can be used for the 5pm tweet. Take into account the intended audience and the intended message.
