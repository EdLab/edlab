EdLab Summer Internship

Jeff Frank

November 4, 2009

Thank you for coming to the seminar today; it helped me think about directions for improving how we currently run our summer internship program. Summarizing the comments and questions that came up, we felt that we should:

  • Attend in-person recruiting fairs
  • Offer a stronger orientation programâ€“both on the first day, and on an ongoing basis
  • Offer interns the opportunity to present their work
  • Offer interns better opportunities for staying connected with the Lab
  • Give applicants a better sense of who we are and what we do
  • Think about different options for paying interns
  • Think about how we can create a more diverse team of interns
  • Think about offering a class/internship in the summer
  • Think about how we can increase the visibility of the Lab at the college
  • Think about how we can manage a year-round internship program
If you have other ideas now that the seminar is over, please post them here. Also, please see me if you are interested in working together on the internship program. [Please keep private]
