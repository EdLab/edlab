Glimpses of the Learning Theater - TC Board of Trustees October Meeting

gnatriello

December 26, 2016

In October 2016 the Learning Theater was the site of the fall meeting of the Teachers College Board of Trustees.  The theme of the meeting was the presidential election and the theater was designed to support the theme.   [caption id="attachment_128" align="alignnone" width="599"] The bookcase at the entrance featured books by Hillary Clinton on one side,[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_133" align="alignnone" width="600"] ...and books by Donald Trump on the other side.[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_134" align="alignnone" width="600"] Refreshments for the meeting were presented cafe style as shown on this view looking through the glass doors.[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_129" align="alignnone" width="600"] We used a combination of high top tables and stools to create the cafe zone. Note the collection of Learning Theater building materials just beyond the cafe.[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_131" align="alignnone" width="600"] We made good use of the east wall for life size video from the conventions with the democratic convention on the left or north side,[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_135" align="alignnone" width="600"] ...and the republican convention featured on the right or south side.[/caption]   [caption id="attachment_130" align="alignnone" width="600"] For the meeting area, we were able to us our newly arrived round tables designed by Matt Taylor and built in Australia.[/caption]   We created and curated a variety of materials to support the theme of the meeting in addition to those shown in the photos.  A more compete compilation of these materials is included in the rhizr created for the event.
