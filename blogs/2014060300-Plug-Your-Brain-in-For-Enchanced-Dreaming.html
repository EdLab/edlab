Plug Your Brain in For Enchanced Dreaming

Carmel Addae

June 3, 2014

Do we really need to remember and control every dream we have? Researchers have revealed that if mild electrical currents are applied to individual's scalps, they will be able to regain everything. Lucid dreaming is a type of dream in which the individual is aware of it; the dreamer might be able to control their actions and operate the dream's plot as well.
