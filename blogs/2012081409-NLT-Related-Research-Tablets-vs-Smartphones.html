NLT-Related Research: Tablets vs Smartphones

Kate Meersschaert

August 14, 2012

As we move-forward with our ground-breaking new digital publication devoted to the future of learning, New Learning Times, it is interesting to note research surrounding user preference regarding mobile devices. This report was recently released and uncovers a greater level of satisfaction for tablets over smartphones. It also appears that an almost equal number of men vs women own tablets, and Apple's iPad leads the pack. What additional data can we glean from these findings that might be relevant to NLT?
