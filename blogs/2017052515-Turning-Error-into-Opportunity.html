Turning Error into Opportunity

sklee

May 25, 2017

I came across this article on Creative Bloq about fun and unique 404 error pages. The creators of these websites took some extra time designing the 404 error pages so they're in line with the company's branding and spirit. Encountering an error page while searching for something is oftentimes a frustrating experience for users. By paying special attention to the design, however, these creators have turned errors into an opportunity to showcase their design capabilities and offer an entertaining experience to the users. Some of these pages let you play games, some of them are very pretty to look at, and others are humorous and make you laugh. Error pages are not something that users encounter all the time, and maybe it's one of the last things to be given attention to in a design/development process. But it is also a key moment that can impact the users' impressions and experiences.Â
