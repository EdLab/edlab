Pundit Fortune Cookie Contest & the winners are...

Kate Meersschaert

November 9, 2011

Private! After introducing and receiving many great submissions for the Pundit fortune cookie message promotional... Pundit has selected the following 6 winning messages! He couldn't narrow it down to 3... he told me that EdLab should have a splinter cell group that charges clients for naming and ideation. That Pundit, such a smart jellyfish/squid/cyclops/stingray/wise person! Here are the winners in random order: 1) Joanna: "For true course love, stop searching for your tall dark and handsome and meet your short, blue and round." 2) Fred: "As Confucius instructed his disciples: 'only choose college courses with the Pundit platform.'" 3) Joann: "[Grad] school is tough. Let Pundit help you stay the course." 4) Laura: "The path to course fulfillment lies before you." 5) Megha: "Excitement and intrigue follow in your next course search." 6) Janet: "We heard from our sources that you're searching for courses." Runners-up: 7) Joann: "The future is what you make of it. But Pundit can help." 8) Laura: "Your educational destiny is a click away." 9) Laura: "You have a date with course destiny." 10) Demetri: "Help me help you, pick the right courses!" 11) Zhou Zhou: "Tidnup, Dutnip, or Nutpin, Search and choose courses whatever way you like with Pundit." 12) Fred: "You will log on to a computer and use Pundit to choose your courses." Congratulations to the winners and runners-up! Pundit & I really appreciate your help making the fortune cookie promotional a success! As I detailed in this blog post the plan for distribution is focused for now on Student Services, throughout TC (oops cookies fell-out of my bag onto the cafeteria tables) and possibly even at local restaurants! Please share additional distribution ideas here! Thanks for playing & your prizes will be forthcoming! - Pundit
