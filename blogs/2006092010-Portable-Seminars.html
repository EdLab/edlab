Portable Seminars

Dan Mallinger

September 20, 2006

Ever walk out of our weekly seminars wishing, "Man, if I could just listen to that all over again... wouldn't that be sweet!" Good news sports fans: The EdLab seminars are now recorded by our friends at MDC and put into PocketKnowledge here. But the really good news? PK's RSS feed now doubles as a podcast feed to iTunes. So take your favorite media pocket and let iTunes know about it! Put it in your iPod and relive the whole seminar as you go for a jog! iTunes is a little slow to update certain fields, so our website has not been properly registered yet, and my test file is still listed. But give it a listen if you want, it's a demo a friend made.
