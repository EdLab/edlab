Performance Review Exhibit Schedule

gnatriello

March 14, 2018

To develop the products that will be featured in the 2018 performance review exhibit that we will launch in August, we will use the following schedule of activities and interim deliverables:


March - Team discussions and consensus on an overall strategy, including a determination of how individual products will be coordinated for a possible team product or product set. Team leaders will compile the results of this activity at the end of March.


April - Initial draft proposals - Each staff member prepares an initial draft of a proposal for a product to be featured in the exhibit and forwards it to their team leader by the end of April.


May - Proposal development - Each team member develops a rhizr for their proposal that includes text, rough sketches, initial gallery specs, etc. by the end of May.Â 


June - Proof of concept - Each staff member develops a proof of concept/prototype of their exhibit by the end of June.


July - Revisions - Team members revise and refine their exhibits by the end of July.


August - Installation - Exhibits are installed in the third floor gallery.
