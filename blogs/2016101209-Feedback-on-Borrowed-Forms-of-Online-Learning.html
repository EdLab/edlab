Feedback on Borrowed Forms of Online Learning

HSC

October 12, 2016

Students in Gary's class have been working in teams to create online learning experiences on four "borrowed" form of online learning: - Courses - Schools - Workplaces - Libraries At today's seminar we will examine the online learning opportunities created by the students in Gary's class.
