Education is a Right

Bismark Appiah

July 6, 2015

“Education is not a privilege of the rich and well-to-do; it is the inalienable right of every child.” This quote from this piece discusses the role of privatization of education and its impact on poor families. Privatization of education leads to inequality. Although it is widely held that private education provides quality education, it comes at an expensive cost. Not every family can afford private education. As a result, many private schools lack diversified system of learning and cultural aspect that is so essential today. One of the primary goals of providers of private education is profit. This is the reason why many people see private education as a business. Governments must step up to improve these challenges. They must invest more in public education to provide the best education for students.
