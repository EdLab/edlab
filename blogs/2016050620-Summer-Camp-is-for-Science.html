Summer Camp is for Science

Nantwi

May 6, 2016


For most students, summer is the end of a long school year and the beginning of lazy idle time and endless outdoor activities. School or anything remotely educational is usually the last thing on a student's mind; however, after a nationwide attention-shift to science and technology subjects, traditional camps have transformed into techy educational events that inspire more intellectual stimulation during the summer months.

The 92nd Street Y, a community center located in the heart of Manhattan’s East Side, is seizing on the tech craze to orient its summer camps. Whether it's identifying sedimentary rocks, explaining the mechanical parts of a kiln, or covering the basics of engineering through crafts, these camp counselors are instilling an appreciation for science without ever using the buzzword STEM.
