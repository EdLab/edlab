Ecuador and The United States of America

Carlos.Pena

April 1, 2017

Ecuador, the land of biodiversity, is the first place I called home. I was born in Guayaquil, a coastal city, with beautiful beaches and passionate people. Family is what is most important to us. Love is what drives our lives. We love, we live, and we laugh together for the sole purpose of enjoying life together. The U.S., is my second home. It gave me the opportunity to experience intellectual curiosity, embrace diversity, and drove me towards perseverance. The U.S. is an opportunity to build a better world, not only for us, but for society as a whole.
