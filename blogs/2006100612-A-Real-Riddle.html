A Real Riddle

Dan Mallinger

October 6, 2006

This is my favorite riddle of all time. Sadly, I've forgotten the real names, so I'll tell the story as it was once told to me (admittedly over drinks): The Kingdom of Dan is ruled by a Queen Cigarette. One day Queen Cigarette became upset by the amount of infidelity in Dan, particularly husbands cheating on wives (the wives don't cheat). To address this problem, Queen Cigarette declares that any woman in the kingdom who discovers that her husband is cheating must shoot him at 11:59pm that night. Additionally:

  1. At least one husband is cheating
  2. Every woman follows Queen Cigarette's commands exactly
  3. Every woman is an expert at solving logic problems
  4. The gunshots can be heard anywhere in the kingdom and one can tell exactly how many occured
On the first night there is silence. The second night is also silent. This continues until the 40th night until there is the sound of gunshots. How many husbands were shot and how do you know?
