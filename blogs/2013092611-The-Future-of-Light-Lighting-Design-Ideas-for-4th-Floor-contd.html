The Future of Light (Lighting Design Ideas for 4th Floor cont'd)

Brian.Sweeting

September 26, 2013

*private* In June we brainstormed different lighting options for the 4th floor renovation, and a recent PSFK post highlights new tech that corresponds to our idea of creating lighting that is customizable and interactive:

"In a trend we are calling Sound Syncing, PSFK Labs draws attention to lighting installations that convert audio cues into vibrant lighting displays, enabling people to experience the sound in entirely new ways."
A brilliant example of this is the mobile application Ambify, which syncs Philips Hue bulbs to react to music to create "dynamic in-room visuals in nearly infinite color combinations." After watching the videos below, do you think this lighting tech could contribute to a stimulating and interactive environment for the learning theatre?
