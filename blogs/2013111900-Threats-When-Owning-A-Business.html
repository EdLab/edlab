Threats When Owning A Business

Elysee Dougan

November 19, 2013

In my business class today, we talked about the different weaknesses and threats many business owners currently face. I was particularly struck with the threat of having to face a business with similar traits. We then analyzed the different methods in conquering that problem. One can change their company, continue throwing blows at one the competitor or one can look back at how the competitor came about and use their faults against them. The last idea was the one I would've chosen. If I look back at the problems a competitor has to face, I can come up with methods to better prepare myself if it comes my way. I can even use it to sabotage my competition. In the marketing and business world, one thing is certain: sometimes you have to get dirty.
