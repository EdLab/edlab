HTML5 vs. Native Apps: Who Will Win?

HSC

November 23, 2012

Check out this article from Business Insider about HTML5 and native apps. Long story short, Business Insider Intelligence thinks HTML5 will win in the end. In the short term, native apps have a major advantage when it comes to functionality and speed (and likely will for the foreseeable future). However, HTML5's has huge advantages when it comes to distribution, monetization, platform power, and network effects. Although these are significant advantages, I think all this will be moot if HTML5 does not offer a better experience for end users.
