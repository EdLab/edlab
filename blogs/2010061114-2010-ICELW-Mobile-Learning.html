2010 ICELW - Mobile Learning

Ting Yuan

June 11, 2010

Stephen and I are currently at a presentation entitled Mobile Communities in the Learning Context: A Possible Approach by faculty members (Presenter: Mr. Andrea Molinari) from the University of Trento, Trento, Italy. The university developed mobile applications to support the mobile teaching and learning for students and faculty members. The presenter talked about the idea of moving online communities to mobile communities. Applications include mobile quiz, mobile blog with picture uploading function from phone camera, and mobile social networking site, etc. There were different usage results received such as: while the mobile quiz was not, as expected, used much by faculty members, the mobile blogging ended up been very popular.
