Bloomberg Promotes Cigarette Display Ban

Francisco.Mendoza

March 25, 2013

Mayor Bloomberg is now banning tobacco products that are publicly displayed. He claims that if people can see that a store is selling cigarettes, they are more tempted to buy it. This is not the first time Bloomberg has banned cigarettes. He has made sure that cigarettes are not permitted in restaurants and bars. Many people can be against this, but from my point of view, I don't see why they should be. He is not making it illegal to sell cigarettes, he is just keeping it away as much as he can from temptation. In my opinion this is a good thing that the mayor is trying to do. I don't like the smell of smoke nor the effects it has on people so the farther it can be kept from me, the better. I believe people will be healthier with less smoke around. I have shared a video on Vialouges, please share your thoughts and comments about this ban on cigarette displays.
