"Check in" to boost educational opportunities in the community

Sharon Hsiao

August 2, 2011

Are you checking in anywhere at all? Foursquare, Yelp, Facebook, Twitter etc. Do you know check-ins could actually give a boost in education, not merely crowdsourcing for food, for fun or Please Rob Me. DonorsChoose.org recently hosted a Hacking Education Showcase to help solicit donations for classroom projects via foursquare check-ins by using the location-based mobile app's open API to come up with ways to accomplish some goals, especially in education sector.
