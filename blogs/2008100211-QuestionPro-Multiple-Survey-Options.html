QuestionPro - Multiple Survey Options

Ting Yuan

October 2, 2008

QuestionPro, a research-oriented survey site, may be a good reference for SurveySidekick. Compared with the widely-known SurveyMonkey, it provides free-registration users with a great variety of templates. And it charges much less for upgraded users than Digivey, which is another survey site offering multi-media uploading function. There are some other pretty good features in QuestionPro, e.g. multi-media uploading, multi-lingual, survey tracking, graph making, invitation of multi-users, etc. Wanna get a free trial ?
