My (not so final) Goodbye

Mable Yiu

August 21, 2013

This was my first internship, and I would not have it any other way. Everyone at EdLab was awesome, just so welcoming on our first day when we were all so nervous! I definitely learned a lot through the actual work I did, but I think the entire summer was the greatest learning experience for me. Being able to work with so many different people on different projects really taught me to be able to adapt to various situations and think quickly. Throughout the summer, I made so many friends, from my fellow interns, to the full-time, and of course (though towards the end), the "G's". We really are a family. I just wanted to give a special shout-out to the lovely Kate, Jo, and Laura C, for all their help. I certainly learned a lot about writing, social media, and outreach. Also, thank you Hui Soo for pushing me to step outside my comfort zone and for genuinely wanting the best for everyone. My internship here may be over, but this is not my final goodbye! I will definitely visit (especially for the free lunch, I mean, seminars) and will bring my friends...and probably Roy. And someday, I will organize that reunion. Thank you everyone for the wonderful experience, Mabes
