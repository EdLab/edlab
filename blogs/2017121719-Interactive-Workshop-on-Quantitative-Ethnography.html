Interactive Workshop on Quantitative Ethnography

EdLabStudios

December 17, 2017


David Williamson Shaffer is a professor at the University of Wisconsin-Madison in the Department of Educational Psychology. Following the publication of his book, Quantitative Ethnography in April of 2017, Shaffer visited EdLab to present two keys tools for modeling complex thinking and discuss the challenges and possibilities of applying quantitative methods to qualitative data. Watch the full seminar on Vialogues.
