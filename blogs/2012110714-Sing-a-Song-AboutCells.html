Sing a Song About...Cells!

Janice Joo

November 7, 2012

Imagine your classroom singing songs about the content you were teaching them! Klablab brings the songwriting and content-creation experience to classrooms by giving students the tools to communicate what they learn effectively... This reminds me of an early idea at EdLab, called STEMedia, a proposed platform for students to develop and teach concepts using relevant popular media. Klablab is also reminiscent of TEDed, but differs because Klablab includes students in the creation process. Check out one of the products of their work! The lyrics that hooked me: "The nucleus... like city hall... lalalala"
