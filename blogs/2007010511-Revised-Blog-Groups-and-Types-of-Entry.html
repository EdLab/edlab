Revised Blog "Groups" and "Types of Entry"

bhughes

January 5, 2007

I've revised the way you can classify your blog entries somewhat. After reviewing how people were using this feature, I streamlined it so that if you don't do anything, your blog will be "Blog News/General." (Too many things were "Design Event/Event" by default!) I also switched around the categories a bit. FYI: you can always choose multiple categories by holding down the command key for Mac/control key for Windows. I'm curious if TSI can add a list of these categories on the right side of the page under the current "recent" lists... We held off during implementation because we were still piloting the categories, but now it would seem to be a great way to browse our blog... especially for people who are new to the Lab. Happy Blogging in the New Year!
