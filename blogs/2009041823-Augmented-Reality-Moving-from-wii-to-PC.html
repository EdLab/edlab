Augmented Reality: Moving from wii to PC

zhou

April 18, 2009

I knew this website for a while but just had time to blog about it... Basically what you are doing is to print out a particular pattern on a piece of paper, then move the pattern into your webcam connected to the PC; an application on your PC will recognize the pattern and put some fictional content on top of the pattern. The augmented reality (AR) will be tied to the pattern so that you can do actions with/towards the AR. See an video example below: Now play it yourself: http://ge.ecomagination.com/smartgrid/#/augmented_reality This website even offer free Flash codes for AR, though I haven't figured it out how to use them. I imagine sticking a pattern on a ping-pong bat then play ping-pong with PC or even a friend through the webcam...
