Backing Up Google Books

Frank Webster

October 14, 2008

A group of major libraries that are participating in Google's Library Project are working together to create what amounts to a publicly-accessible backup of the digital library that Google is creating. The HathiTrust project includes 12 Midwestern university libraries and the 11 libraries of the University of California system. The Hathi Trust calls itself, "a bold idea with big plans." Hathi is Hindi for “elephant,” an animal that is said to never forget.
