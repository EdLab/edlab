Our green screen capabilities

Simon Doolittle

October 12, 2009

Hey all, Over the summer we started to work on a prototype of a new kind of green screen video, and the result of that work is below. This video is by no means complete -- it's really just a rough draft of a pilot episode for what could become a new series for TCR. Nonetheless, I wanted to share it because I think that this format may have potential as a format for a number of things that we do throughout the lab, and I wanted to show everyone. Could I ask you to take a look an let me know if and how this kind of video could be useful to any part of your work? I'm not so much even talking about the actual content of this video, but more the format: a person or people, and using a green screen to pair with them with various illustrations and animations of their ideas. cheers, Simon
