XMAS - A project you may be interested in

Stephanie Hunt

January 13, 2009

I was about to send this as a comment to Zhou Zhou in response to something he posted last month, but it seems like enough people would be interested that it's worth dedicating a fresh blog post to: MIT's Cross Media Annotation Project (XMAS) was originally developed as a tool to aid in the study and comparison of Shakespeare texts. This project is a few years old, and I'm not even sure whether it's still under development, but it's definitely still a nice one to know about if you're thinking about making use of video within online learning tools. From their website:

By combining on-line discussion and text editing with video sequences on DVD, XMAS makes possible a range of new teaching and learning techniques in which text, video and images can be studied, excerpted, and shared remotely in more flexible ways than in the past.
