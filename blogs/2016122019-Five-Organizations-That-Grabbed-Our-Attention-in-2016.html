Five Organizations That Grabbed Our Attention in 2016

Brian.Sweeting

December 20, 2016


This year we're encouraged by the creative new ways these organizations are changing how we learn and access resources.

1. E-180
Like the Tinder for meeting your next tutor, this app creates a spark between users by forging knowledge connections, one area of interest at a time.

2. AngelScholars
This crowdfunding platform for education could change the way students finance their learning goals.

3. Joyable
An app that delivers timely and effective mental health solutions through online personal coaching.

4. DreamUp
A startup that launches student science experiments into space is making space-based research more accessible for all.

5. Level Up Village
This organization is teaching tech skills to students through solving real life international problems.
