Pundit is alive!

Kate Meersschaert

August 15, 2011

Private! Team Pundit is thrilled to announce that the alpha version of Pundit is ready for a test drive! Please add your feedback, report bugs and make comments through our Idea.Informer feedback widget located to the left of all Pundit screens. Also, please feel free to visit our TeamBox project to see what is in the works for release 2.0 & 3.0. What is Pundit? Pundit is an intelligent course recommendation and search tool. The v. 1.0 release will employ an innovative combination of data retrieval and data mining to recommend courses to users based on a match between their EdNode profile, TC course catalogue and a database of related readings. This unique combination of search results that includes both courses & related readings, paired with personalized recommendations, based on a users EdNode profile, sets Pundit apart from other course recommendations tools and allows for an unprecedented degree of both personalization and increased efficiency, both important benefits for busy modern learners. Check-out our "Help" section listed in the footer of the site for more info about the project and info on how to use the Pundit site. We are thrilled that Pundit has come this far in just under a month! This speedy turn-around of our initial vision would not be possible without the following superstar team members... Ankit Ranka , Stephen Pratt, Josh Lopez, Pengfei Li, Yudan Li, Pranav Garg, Gonzalo Obelleiro, & Basak Haznedaroglu! We will make final changes based on your feedback & internal testing - so please comment away & look-out for the official announcement of the Beta test for the greater TC community! Go Team Pundit!
