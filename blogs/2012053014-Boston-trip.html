Boston trip

Michelle Lee

May 30, 2012

All, Hui Soo and I will be traveling up to Boston Thursday and Friday as part of an exploratory phase to determine how to best consider future excubation and partnership activities for EdLab. Here is a list of companies/groups we will meet: - Harvard iLab - MIT Ventures - NextView Ventures - Mass Challenge - Highland Capital - Harvard Business Review Publishing Group If anyone has questions or thoughts you'd like us to consider as relates to how we can better involve EdLab activities with venture capital, innovation labs and accelerator programs, please let us know. As you know, we are embarking on exciting activities as relates to LaunchPad and NLT with Kate heading up a Demo Night and NLT launching in Fall. It will be great for everyone to think about how we can increase EdLab's profile with the broader edutech and education community and how we can partner with other organizations who can promote EdLab's mission.
