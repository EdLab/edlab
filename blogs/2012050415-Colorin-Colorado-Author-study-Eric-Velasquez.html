Colorín Colorado...: Author study, Eric Velasquez

Veronica Garza

May 4, 2012

About the Author:

Eric is a native new yorker of Afro-Puertorican heritage.  He grew up in Harlem and uses much of his childhood memories as centerpieces for his books.  He earned his BFA from the School of Visual Arts in 1983.
