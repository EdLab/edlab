Colorín Colorado...: Author study, Alma Flor Ada

Veronica Garza

April 21, 2012

About the Author: Of  Cuban descent, Alma Flor Ada is the author of numerous children's books and adult literature in both English and Spanish.  Most themes draw from her own or students' experiences as Latinos in both the United States and Latin America.
