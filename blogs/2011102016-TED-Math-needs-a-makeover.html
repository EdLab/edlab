TED: Math needs a makeover

Demetri Lales

October 20, 2011

I want to share a video with you guys on a talk given by Dan Meyer on how math classes today do not let students strengthen their problem solving skills. A typical math problem in a textbook provides students with plenty of information to solve it: all the student has to do is plug the info into a formula. This doesn't teach students how to solve problems, only how to get the answer to that specific question. Dan Meyer goes into detail on that point and shows us his approach to constructing a math problem that will really make students think.
