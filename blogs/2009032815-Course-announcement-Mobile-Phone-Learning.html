Course announcement: Mobile Phone Learning

Nabeel Ahmad

March 28, 2009

A colleague and I are teaching a course this summer on mobile learning and this topic parallels with a lot of the research coming out of the EdLab on mobile devices. The title is Mobile Phone Learning - An Introduction. Given the increased attention to the use of mobile devices in education at the EdLab, this can be a good opportunity to learn more about this from a variety of aspects. Course details: Syllabus - there is a lengthy reference list at the end. CRN: 23008 MSTU 5510A Section: 002 Credits: 1-3 Dates: May 21 - July 1 (Summer A) Day/time: Tue/Thu 2p-405p Instructors: Dominic Mentor, Nabeel Ahmad
