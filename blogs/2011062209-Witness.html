Witness

Angela Lee

June 22, 2011

With Peter Gabriel's recent come back at the United Center concert I thought of his human right compagine WITNESS.org . My senior thesis in Parsons was to come up with a system/product which would empower and prevent child trafficking in South East Asia. I did quite a bit of research on this well organized NGO, non profit compagine. The idea is to use the power of images and videos to expose the injustice in the society. The videos on witness.org have all been professionally edited and it would be a good idea to feature some of their videos on Vialogues to be further used as outreach materials to groups such as the UN or social studies teachers.
