Vialogues Wins of the Week: 05.09.14

Kate Meersschaert

May 9, 2014

***Private! Please find our weekly (in this case 2 weeks!) Vialogues.com "wins" and developments below! As always, please let us know if you find any additional vialogues mentions on the interwebs! ;) Stats: Users - 146 Vialogues - 111 Visits - 4,228 Holy Toledo! (I'm sorry...) We landed on University of Toledo's LibGuide for "Instructional Video Tools." We are listed among many other edtech resources for faculty. We can always count on the flipped followers Another great flipped learning slideshow from Vialogues user and learning technologist, Nik Peachey (go to slide #9 for our small moment in his presentation). At almost 3,000 views for a presentation on "The challenges of teaching in a continuously developing world," he is doing well for himself (and for us)!
