D&R: NLT Top 100 Users & Personas!

Kate Meersschaert

March 10, 2015

Hi Everyone! I am excited to share this research re: our top 100 NewLearningTimes.com users ("user" globally signifies reader) with you! Please find our original download of the CAS data here (special thanks to Mason for pulling this for me and assisting w/ standardization!). Please note that this data is now a few months old. We added and standardized categories on this doc including:

  • Social media profile (Most often, LinkedIn or Twitter profile)
  • Industry
  • Position
  • Connection to Kate on LinkedIn (1st, 2nd, 3rd degree contact etc.)
  • Institution (this was often "Null*")
  • Maximum education achieved
    • (*Note: "Null" data was either not self-reported or discoverable via social media) Also, here is my presentation for today's D&R presentation! Group work (please report-out as a group in the comments below!):
    • Any surprises re: users?
    • What additional CAS info should we collect?
    • What would you imagine the main goal of each of our top 4 user personas to be?
    • What messaging would you use to market NLT as a whole to each of these 4 groups?
    • What NLT content would you target to each group? (specific articles, streams)
