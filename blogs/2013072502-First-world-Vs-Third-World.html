First world Vs Third World

Jeffrey Hado

July 25, 2013

All countries, regardless of their economic power, face some sort of social, educational and political problems. However, there is a huge difference between the extent of the problems between developed (first world) and developing (third world) countries. Third world countries face much bigger problems. Many organizations have been put in place to help the poor around the world but nearly a billion people in the world today still suffer from hunger. Primary school enrolment in African countries is among the lowest in the world. Due to lack of basic health care, millions of children die every year from treatable diseases and ailments. First world countries have an abundance of resources for such problems, which leads many to sometimes take things for granted.
