Never Gonna Give You Up...

HSC

April 3, 2008

Earlier today I heard Greg (a fellow teacher) singing "Never Gonna Give You Up" by Rick Astley. So I asked him, "Why do you have this particular tune in your head?" Greg explained to me an Internet meme known as "Rickrolling". So I did some research and I was amazed to learn how popular this practice had become. For example, this particular Rickroll video has been viewed over 9 million times. In fact, it has become so popular that the 80s British popstar is experiencing a career resurgence (For 10 fun facts on Rick Astley, click here). No offense to Rick Astley, but how did this happen? Is there something, anything that the EdLab can learn from this phenomenon?
