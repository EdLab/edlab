Library App Update, August 2018

jvazquez

September 12, 2018

Time to uncock your eyebrows, here are the stats from last month’s app usage.

Overview

Downloads this month: 59 (49 iPhone |10 Android) +44% from last month

Total downloads to date: 239 (196 iPhone | 43 Android)

Total visits: 162 (49% on campus | 51% off campus) +46% from last month

Returning visits: 127 (47%on campus | 53% off campus) +9% from last month


Though slight, it’s the first time in months that off-campus usage has surpassed on campus.
