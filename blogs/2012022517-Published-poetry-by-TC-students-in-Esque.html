Published poetry by TC students in Esque

Anne Hays

February 25, 2012

Recently, it came to my attention that not one, but two, Teachers College students have poems published in the most recent Esque Magazine, and so I thought I would bring this to everyone’s attention.  Esque is a literary journal founded by two poets, Amy King & Ana Božičević, and their webdesign skills are something to behold.  Check out the site, here. This particular issue covers the theme “manifesto,” and who doesn’t love a good manifesto? The two TC students in question are Maya Pindyck, whose visual poem “Flew to Town” (it appears in the form of a mini book) is HERE. And Maryam Alikhani, whose poem “Celastrus Scandens” appears HERE. Do you know of other literary TC students with publications out?  Let us know!
