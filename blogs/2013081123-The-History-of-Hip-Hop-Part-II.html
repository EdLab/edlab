The History of Hip Hop: Part II

Carmel Addae

August 11, 2013

This blog is a follow-up to my previous blog about my series on the cultural, and social influence of hip hop. Hip Hop is not just about the music as there are other features that comes along with it. Hip Hop was the creator of core elements known in music today such as break dancing, MC-ing (recognized as the art of rapping), graffiti art also identified as the aerosol art, and DJ-ing: the cutting, mixing and scratching of a record. Hip Hop is known for being an underground urban movement; a way one can express their life through music. The Vialogue talks about what the true meaning of Hip Hop is really about.
