WikiEd

Kate Meersschaert

April 20, 2012

Guided by Wikis... As a part of their "outreach" initiatives, Wikimedia recently launched a Global Education Program. This new, global project grew-out-of learning from their national Public Policy Initiative, which was piloted in higher ed institutions throughout the country during the 2010-11 academic year. The Details... Students are led both by their instructor, who chooses which articles to assign for their classes to edit, and are also assisted by specially trained Wikipedia Ambassadors. The Ambassador provides extra help with social editing best-practices both online and in-class and functions almost like a specialized teaching assistant. Is Social Editing the future of Ed? Do you feel wikipedia even belongs in education? Would you become a Wiki Campus Ambassador? Want to assign wiki articles to your class to edit? If so, how would you define the learning outcomes and goals of assigning these articles to your classes? What topics and subject-areas is this most conducive to?
