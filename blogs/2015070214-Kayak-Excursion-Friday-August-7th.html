Kayak Excursion - Friday, August 7th

christophergu

July 2, 2015

Hi EdLabbers! As Gary mentioned in the seminar yesterday, we will be having a kayak trip on Friday, August 7th. We will be participating in the Skyline Tour, a guided beginner-level tour, hosted by the Manhattan Kayak Company. MKC is located at Pier 84 Boathouse (555 12th Avenue, at West 44th Street). The event will begin at 12:30 PM, so the group will most likely leave from EdLab at 11:30 AM. A catered lunch will also be served at the Pier 84 lounge deck. Our team will most likely be divided into two groups, with one group kayaking first and the other having lunch first. (Please feel free to come for the lunch as well, if you are not interested in going out on the water.) The whole event should last between 2.5 and 3 hours. Please RSVP as soon as possible, using the link below, if you are interested in attending the event: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/19vFHNnnF8FFXAQaZa_VX4K3HEg-OSR-d0yUWFqsjWM8/viewform And of course, please feel free to teach out if you have any questions. Best, Chris
