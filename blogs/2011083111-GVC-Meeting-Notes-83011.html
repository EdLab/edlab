GVC Meeting Notes (8.30.11)

LukeMalone

August 31, 2011

The GVC (Gottesman Video Collective) has been holding meetings as that defined group once a week for almost a year and half now. Recently, we've restructured our style and, as part of that, will come these post meeting notes in hopes that it will provide more transparency and communication. Feel free to comment, use the links for specific comments or join us at any given week. We meet in the back at 10:00 on Tuesdays. 8.30.11

  • Any video that we produce comes with the struggle of finding the right (free) music. Ariel created a post giving us a plethora of free music resources. She walked us through a lot of the features and benefits of these sites. It would be great if you have any resources to add and please do so as a comment to that posting!
  • We discussed the latest cut of the UFR Technology in the Classroom video (because it is a rough cut, we're keeping it private unless you request access)
  • Being fairly happy with how the longshots are turning out lately, we came up with a standard rotation of a 3 camera shoot and wireless mic situation that will be the foundation for the longshots. There is still plenty of room to experiment with these but the setup from the most recent longshot will be the standard. Please continue passing the mic around if you're not sitting in the front or right next to a camera. It's working out great!
  • Phil shared his latest graphic work for both the Rock and Roll videos and for the Strage Prize video. These are in an unsharable form right now, but will be unveiled soon!
  • Besides that, we talked a lot about our restructuring goals in general.
