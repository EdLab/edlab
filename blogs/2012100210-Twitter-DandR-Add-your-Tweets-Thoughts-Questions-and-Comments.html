Twitter D&R: Add your Tweets, Thoughts, Questions & Comments!

Kate Meersschaert

October 2, 2012

Hi Everyone! Thank you for participating in today's Twitter "best practices" D&R! I hope you feel even more connected to Twitter as a tool to promote our work on your personal accounts! Here are the instructions for today's group activity: 1) Each group is assigned a different project... explore the website & Twitter feeds for each (if available!). 2) Develop unique 3 tweets for each project and post them on the blog! The focus of each tweet could be one of the following: Entertainment, information, gathering of data (what are you reading? How often do you read the news on your tablet? etc.) 3) Each tweet must contain the following: 1 @, 1 # and bitly links in place of any full URLs. (https://bitly.com/)
