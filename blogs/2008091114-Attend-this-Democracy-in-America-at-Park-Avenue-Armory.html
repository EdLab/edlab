Attend this: Democracy in America at Park Avenue Armory

Stephanie Hunt

September 11, 2008

New York is home to so many artists whose work uses technology to explore issues of freedom and democracy. If you're even remotely curious, then I strongly recommend this upcoming exhibition and speech series. Contributors include the Yes Men, Trevor Paglen, and many many other favorites. This fantastic lineup will be taking place at Park Avenue Armory from September 21 to 27. Definitely check this one out.
