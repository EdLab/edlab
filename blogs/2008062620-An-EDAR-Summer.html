An EDAR Summer

Chia-Ling Lee

June 26, 2008

What is EDAR? EDAR is the Edlab Digital Art Residency, which is our first attempt at an artist in residence program. The EdLab Design Team has talked about this idea and made plans, and now we're making it happen! Thanks for every one who works, supports, and participates in it. See this news posting for more information. We hope EDAR can bring interesting thoughts and art from the outside world into our community of educators. Through EDAR, artists' practices and works should helps us to expand our way of looking at the world where we live, work, and love. The most interesting thing about EDAR is that it is unlike other programs that provide an independent studio for each artist to develop art. EDAR artists work with other EdLab people in the same open space. We hope it will create a flow of conversation in our community. Performance artist, Daniel Iglesia had his first day at EdLab. His project for EDAR may use the library databases and 3D images to create visual experience through a screen. Please check our photo diary of EDAR!
