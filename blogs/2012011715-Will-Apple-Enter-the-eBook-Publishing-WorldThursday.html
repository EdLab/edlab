Will Apple Enter the eBook Publishing World...Thursday?

Kate Meersschaert

January 17, 2012

According to this blog post, Apple may announce on Thursday that it will debut a suite of tools to easily create eBooks. With the following slightly cryptic message, “Join us for an education announcement,” Apple might be aiming this powerful new suite of ePub tools at the textbook market. Currently Adobe & Quark have 1-click ePublishing options, but Apple may be able to take this even further with even more simplicity and speed. Additionally, these tools will add more efficiencies to the process of updating & selling of textbooks through the Apple platform. Also, MacObserver.com noted that Thursday's event might announce a partnership with McGraw Hill that would allow Apple to one-up Amazon in the race to add textbooks to eReaders. Thursday we should know whether these predictions and rumors are true and as MacObserver.com eloquently states, "we may be witnessing the beginnings of another Cupertino-led revolution!"
