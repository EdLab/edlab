ALE Exhibition

Gonzalo Obelleiro

September 8, 2010

Thank you for the feedback and stimulating conversation today. For the next week Andrew and I will be posting the progress of our infographics design and we look forward to our continuing conversation, face-to-face, virtual or mediated by marginal notes on the posters. We are looking for feedback of any kind, but particularly we want to identify the most relevant, most powerful data points, we want feedback on the effectiveness of our graphic interpretations of the info, and we want ideas for ways in which we can best organize this content for the purposes of the event.
