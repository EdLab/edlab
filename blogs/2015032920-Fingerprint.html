Fingerprint

Rachel Smiley

March 29, 2015


"The family market always evolves, from books and board games to smart phones and tablets, but the human experience will always remain social. We designed a way to transition this parent and kid engagement into the digital world. Our award-winning platform provides a place where kids and parents can discover and share new apps. Now, we can enable other businesses to break into the mobile market by helping them construct their own kid-friendly app network." (About Fingerprint)

Fun for the Whole Family
With over 800 unique apps to enhance children's education and entertainment experiences, Fingerprint curates a digital network aimed at pre- and early elementary school students. The Fingerprint social dashboard supports functionality for the whole family including progress and analytic reports for parents, multiple user profiles, and personalized recommendations from the app catalog. Fingerprintâ€™s applications combining education and entertainment have gained recognition from notable child and family organizations including the National Parenting Publications Awards and the TechWithKids.com Best Picks.

Platform for the Future
In order to increase the amount of mobile content available in the education sector, Fingerprint created a software development kit platform that other developers and brands can customize to reach local and international users. The network provides easy integration with Fingerprint, IT support, language customization, analytics, and the tools mentioned above for parents and kids. App creators can also use this platform as a starting point to expand their business as long as they adhere to the core mission of Fingerprint to be kid-friendly, educational, engaging, and provide a high-quality product to mobile users.

Apps in the Classroom
Digital tool curators and aggregators, like Fingerprint, can have a great impact on early development in children. For use at home or in the classroom, apps geared toward kids and learning can augment school curriculum and stimulate creativity, critical thinking, and exploration in young minds. By creating connections between developers and families, Fingerprint facilitates the development of educational technologies that respond to real educational needs.
