U.S. Slide in Top Countries for Business

Carmel Addae

January 9, 2015

Though the U.S. economy is showing signs of growth, it has been largely struggling to get back on their feet since the Great Recession. According to the Heritage Foundation's Index of Economic Freedom, the U.S. has acquired losses in economic freedom for the past seven years. There are many components that could be responsible for this such as the expansion of the government and the highly financed new principles in the health care sector. Additionally, the US's global stock has also taken a hit. It has slide down Forbes' list of top countries for business.
