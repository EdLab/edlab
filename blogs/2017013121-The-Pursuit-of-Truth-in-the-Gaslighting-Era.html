The Pursuit of Truth in the Gaslighting Era

gnatriello

January 31, 2017

As members of the College community dedicated to research and the pursuit of knowledge, we have a special interest and obligation to maintain an environment where truth is the foundation for individual and collective action. In light of our current national context of alternative facts, we would do well to review the major techniques of gaslighting, or the strategy of making victims question their reality.
