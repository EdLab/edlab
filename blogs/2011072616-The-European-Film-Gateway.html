The European Film Gateway

LauraCostello

July 26, 2011

Film buff? History fiend? Check the new EU-supported film portal which unites 400,000 digitized films, images, and texts from archives all across Europe. Highlights include French magic lantern slides, awesome German documentaries from the 60's, Marlene Dietrich, and Austrian film censorship cards from 1933. The content is amazing, the site could use a bit of work. Navigating is a challenge, moving as you must through languages, video platforms, and external websites for access to content and metadata. Nonetheless, it's an exciting step in EU archival collaboration and a boon to those who adore esoteric foreign language content.
