Interactive Visualization of the Forces that Helped Create the Internet

Kate Meersschaert

July 6, 2012

This visualization is beautiful and informative... Despite your thoughts on data viz, it is really worth exploring this interactive visualization of the forces and technologies that worked together to create the current Internet that we have come to know and at times take for granted. Note that the "colored flows" represent the interaction between web technologies and browsers in the first visualization. The second is a more classic line graph of both global internet traffic and usage. I have taken a screen capture from the site and embedded it below... Google I/O Debut! This visualization debuted at Google I/O and was created as a partnership between Vizzuality and Hyperakt. Check-it-out in action on the Google stage in Colorado! Potential for New Learning Times (NLT)? Do you feel that a highly interactive, multifaceted visualization like this could could be created to support NLT content? Perhaps as an expression of the history of technology in the classroom? What would be the value?
