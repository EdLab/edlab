H20

Anthony Cocciolo

November 4, 2005

I wanted to bring everyone's attention to this project at the Berkman Center at Harvard called H2O (a production version is running here). Some of the interesting aspects of H2O is the use of playlists (ala ITunes) to organize multimedia materials. The project manager for H2O, Molly Krause, is now a student at Teachers College in the CCTE program. She continues to work for the Berkman Center remotely. She may be a great person to invite in to talk about H2O (she is very friendly).
