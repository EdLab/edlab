Takeaways from Today's Data Viz D&R

Kate Meersschaert

March 6, 2012

Manav and I hope that this D&R session provided inspiration and insight for the team as we move-forward with Data Dashboard and consider using visualizations to promote our tools. As we were working on the presentation one of our major realizations was that clarifying WHAT data you will represent is almost as challenging and important as deciding HOW you will represent it... data visualization really is both an art and a science! Please try to answer the following in the comments below: -What are your thoughts from today's D&R re: Data Visualization & Infographic design? -Was this an easy task to accomplish? -What info was missing for you to create a more evolved visualization? -What data should we focus on and represent for the EdLab Data Dashboard? -As Hui Soo pointed-out, how can we use infographics and data visualizations to promote our work here at the lab? -What tools/apps would lend themselves most to this kind of representation? -How would we use these designs as a marketing campaign?
