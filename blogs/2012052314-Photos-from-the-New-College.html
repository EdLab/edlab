Photos from the New College

Walter Hoyle

May 23, 2012

I found these photos in a digital dusty corner while looking for inventory spreadsheets. They appear to be photos of the people and grounds of the New College Community in Canton, North Carolina during the 1930's. They seem to be part of the Thomas Alexander collection, some other papers and correspondence about the New College can be found in Pocket Knowledge. Captions come from JPEG file names.
