Social media as risk communication tools

Angela Lee

March 14, 2011

Over the weekend, a 8.9 scale earthquake hit Japan and many mobile networks and telephone landlines collapsed. This article explains how Facebook, Twitter, and Skype were used as important risk communication tools for the past three days. Charities are also using social media to drum up support relief efforts in Japan. While we give thanks to what we have, lets also pray for Japan to overcome this disaster soon.
