YoungArts/Masterclass Reaction Videos

Nantwi

March 21, 2012

As Luke and Brian presented at an EdLab Development & Research meeting several weeks ago, we want the professional development videos for YoungArts/Masterclass to take on a different feel and tone from that of UFR and Teaching the Levees. We are imagining videos in which an expert, student and teacher in each of the three related fields (math & science, humanities and visual & performing arts) of the study guide are reacting to a clip from one of the nine Masterclass episodes. Below is a video starring Manav as a physics expert reacting to a clip from Olafur Eliasson's episode. Please add any feedback, comments or questions you have about the video, and its direction. Additionally, please let me know if you know of any teachers or experts in the aforementioned fields who will be great for the set of reaction videos.
