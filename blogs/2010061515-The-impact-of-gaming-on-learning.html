The impact of gaming on learning

pramnab1

June 15, 2010

http://www.educationaldatamining.org/ - Conference proceedings - Journals What are the research questions addressed - How much dies gaming behavior impact learning in ITS? Hughely (Effectively zero learning when gaming. ) - Any novel predictors of gaming - Students gain on skills where they are relatively weaker. Student identity is important. Our gaming detector three criteria: - rapid guessing - rapid response - repeatedly bottom out hinting Methodology: Goal : Determine impact of gamin on learning - work at the microlevel - Starting point: knowledge tracing (Crobett & Anderson, 1995) It's a hidden markov model - observe student correct or not Initial knowledge - P(Initial knowledge | gaming = true) P(Initial knowledge | gaming =False) Data set - Math ASSISTment system - 106 skills (Ara of polygons, Venn diagram, Diviison, etc) - 343 Students (Mostly 12-14 years) Trainin - Fit data to augmented knowledge tracking How to infer: smoothing
