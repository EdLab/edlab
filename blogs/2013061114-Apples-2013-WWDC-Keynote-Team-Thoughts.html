Apple's 2013 WWDC Keynote... Team Thoughts?!

Kate Meersschaert

June 11, 2013

Inspired by a quick conversation with Daniel this morning... I thought I would create a blog post to share our thoughts around Apple's WWDC keynote yesterday. What are your thoughts regarding the new focus on streamlined, "literal" design (no more "skeuomorphism")? What was your favorite new release? Thoughts on iOS7? Was "education" and "learning" missing this year? Here are some of mine: -I am glad that "literal" design elements like fake bookshelves might be a thing of the past... the Readmill founder mentioned this during his EdLab seminar) -It will be a great addition to additional divisions... iOS7 looks smooth (a bit like the new Windows "Metro" design language) and the almost 3D depth of the homescreen and icons and background is fresh. -MacPro looks like "the object" from Space Odyssey that the monkeys worship... but I am hopeful that this new "vertical" approach will in-fact be better, faster and easier to integrate into a crowded workspace. -Will iTunes Radio kill Pandora? -iBookstore and Newsstand will be available on Mac and not just iOS devices... a boon for digital textbooks?
