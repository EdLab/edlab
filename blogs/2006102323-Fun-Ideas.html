Fun Ideas

Dan Mallinger

October 23, 2006

As we think of new Website [\w]'s (yes, that is a really bad computer science joke), it might be helpful to think about what other users are building for themselves, and what the community thinks. Mac users are voting on My Dream App, a contest to have Mac users choose the next app. Though probably more promotion than honest feedback, this contest does provide some interesting ideas. I really like "Blossom" the flower that feeds on productivity. I think it would make a great rating system. Instead of a numeric rating, imagine if your reviewing status (for example) was a growing flower. Not only does it feed your ego, it feeds your aesthetic needs. I think Mac designers/users have a special talent for hitting those two dimensions simultaneously.
