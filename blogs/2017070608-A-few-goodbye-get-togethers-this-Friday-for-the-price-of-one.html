A few goodbye get togethers this Friday for the price of one

dinosossi2

July 6, 2017


Howdy all!

I am ending my one-year post-doc in a couple of weeks. I'm off to teach at CU & NYU and returning to the Berkman Center at HLS in Cambridge, MA to produce media. Also, Hadley (customer service workat Makespace, another tech run in her theatre company's weekly show, and her birthday is Saturday!!!) and Adrian (a prodigal son returning to the Lone Star state) are leaving soon as well. A collective sad face emoji from one to all.

If you have time, we'll be having a little goodbye gathering at Arts & Crafts Pub this Friday July 7th (a.k.a. tomorrow) from 1:30 to 3:30 pm. It's located at 1135 Amsterdam Ave near 116th Street.

If you are busy, no worries. What's more important is that we have a few moments over the next couple of weeks to say a few parting words.

Regardless, hope you are well!

Dino
