More TCR Team Pics from our Alabama Adventure

Kate Meersschaert

June 27, 2014

Hi Everyone, Please find more pictures from our TCR team adventure to Alabama. Please feel-free to add comments and questions! Special thank you to Gary, Brian S., Chris & George for making this trip so smooth, productive and fun! Also, a very special thank you to Gary for his strong leadership and agile driving skills! The van was big and the Alabama roads were not well marked and EXTREMELY dark at night. Needless to say, it was a great trip!
