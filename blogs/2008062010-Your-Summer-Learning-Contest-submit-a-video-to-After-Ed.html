Your Summer Learning Contest (submit a video to After Ed!)

bhughes

June 20, 2008

The After Ed team needs your help advertising its video contest for 13-22 year-olds. You can help us by sending/posting the following info to your "ed" network (teachers, students, anyone!): About the AfterEd.TV video contest: Are you 13-22 years old with access to a video camera? Create a video that demonstrates innovative teaching or learning. Win $1000, a Future of Education Award, and get your video published on the After Ed Channel. More info: http://aftered.tv/index.php?q=node/202
