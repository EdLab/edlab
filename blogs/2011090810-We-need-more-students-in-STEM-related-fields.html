We need more students in STEM related fields!!

Demetri Lales

September 8, 2011

I've decided to take it upon myself to spread the good word of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics)! Many college students that I have talked to say that math and science are boring and difficult to understand, which is baffling to me. While I understand that math and science are difficult, it can be easy to understand with plenty of practice! I would like more students to have the same kind of enthusiasm I have with STEM classes, because we will definitely need more STEM majors in the future. The graphic below shows us why we need STEM majors, for what reason students choose STEM majors, and more. Check it out, and while you do I would like to ask if any other people on this site are interesting in making STEM more interesting for younger students?
