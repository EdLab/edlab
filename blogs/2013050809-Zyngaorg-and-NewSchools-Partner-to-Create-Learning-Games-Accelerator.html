Zynga.org & NewSchools Partner to Create Learning Games Accelerator

Kate Meersschaert

May 8, 2013

Entering the edtech accelerator race are two unlikely bedfellows... Zynga.org (the nonprofit arm of the game dev co.) and NewSchools Venture fund. Read more in this Yahoo finance piece that I discovered via EdSurge this AM. Do you feel this is yet another sign of an impending edtech bubble... or is this an exciting move in the right direction for learning and gamification fans? From the Yahoo article:

Zynga will encourage its employees to volunteer and collaborate with the companies participating in the accelerator's first cohort -- sharing their expertise in areas such as game design, testing, analytics, product marketing and distribution. In addition to access to the Zynga team, Zynga.org has agreed to commit $1 million in funding for the accelerator's first year.
