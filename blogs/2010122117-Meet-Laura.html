Meet Laura

Michelle Mcnamara

December 21, 2010

Continuing the get to know you series, today we get to learn a bit more about Laura.  She's been a fixture at Gottesman since February 2010, and lives in Columbia student housing with her boyfriend who's a doctoral student in the Economics program.

Are you a Teachers College student, and, if so, what are you studying?
I'm not a student at TC, but I'm working on my MLIS at the University of Wisconsin (online).
Where's your hometown?
Shoreview, MN
What did you want to be when you grew up?
A teacher actually...
What was your first job?
A Pizza Hut in Arden Hills, I applied as a joke but ended up working there for nearly two years.
What has been your worst job?
Pizza Hut.  I had to wear polyester (and a hat), people ordering pizza were often mean to me and I gained 8 pounds.
What has been your best job?
This job has been pretty good.
What do you remember about your first day of school?
I was not too impressed.
A book you strongly recommend?  The Game, by Neil Strauss.  I like that it's so surprising.  It's a very well-wrought story and the writing is great though the topic is fluff and/or extremely offensive.
A sport you enjoy playing (or watching)?  running!
TOP THREE....
...Best places to read?
I'm a big fan of Riverside Park, in the bath and on the train...but I read everywhere.
... Favorite authors or books?
I read a lot of memoirs, pop science, books written by journalists and literary fiction, but I'll read almost anything.
The weirdest thing I've ever done for money.... I used to be an Armenian folk dancer, but we only sometimes got paid...
