Learning New Skills this Summer

Stephanie Burdick

June 4, 2009

What Skill can you Teach? During yesterday's seminar my group focused a lot of our discussion on how we could learn a new skill and help the edlab out. It turned out that two of us (myself being one) wanted to learn how to create an interactive web page. Surprising as it may be, some of us in the lab aren't web-design savvy enough to do this yet. But then, I got to thinking....I can't make a great web page but I'm quickly becoming a 'google master' and I'm really quite adept at researching journals for relevant articles on a variety of subjects. So, the question "what new skill do you want to learn?" may best be answered by another question, "what skill could I teach?" And these question pairs could soon become teaching and learning pairs in the lab. So...Anyone want to teach me how to design my own web page? Anyone need to learn how to research secondary materials?
