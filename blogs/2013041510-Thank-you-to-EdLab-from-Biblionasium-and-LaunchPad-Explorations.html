Thank you to EdLab from Biblionasium & LaunchPad Explorations...

Kate Meersschaert

April 15, 2013

Private!*** LaunchPad Insights... As we continue to evolve our plans for LaunchPad it is heartening to receive messages from participants in our existent "edupreneurship" ecosystem who have derived special value from participation. One such message came from EdLab EdTech Take, Seminar and Demo Night 1 alum Marjan Gara the founder of Biblionasium.com. Visually, experience the trajectory of participation below (Kate heard marjan present at edtech outreach event ---> invited her to participate in EdTech Take interview ---> Invited to participate in EdLab Seminar ---> Invited to participate in Demo Night) that contributed to Biblionasium's selection for the U Penn Graduate School of Education's Milken Business Plan Competition (check-out additional co's for potential EdLab Reviews!)! Don't take my word for it, read Marjan's:

I am proud to share with you that a few days ago, I found out that BiblioNasium was selected as one of the Finalists in the Mike Milken Foundation-Penn GSE Competition, focused exclusively on education startups. Here is the announcement: http://nestcentral.org/ As one of our earlier supporters, I wanted to thank you again for the opportunities to present at EdLabs and your invaluable support of our project. I will keep you posted.
Future What are your thoughts on this existing process? Innovations? Growth potential? Other models? These are our initial "lightweight" LaunchPad initiatives and I look-forward to growing and evolving our edupreneurship-focused capabilities in the months to come... we are definitely establishing ourselves as leaders and players in the edtech world with both our own products and experiences like the ones listed below.
