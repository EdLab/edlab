Goal of the American Education System

abagigah92

September 2, 2013

A normal conversation when discussing issues with technologies in the classroom often include reform within the educational system. This deals both with the issue of Ed-Tech and Ed-Reform. Technologies within the classroom have various uses that increase the educational experience in a classroom; it is more suitable to the modernization of the learning experience, especially now that technologies are becoming common within the classroom. While technology in classroom deals with modernization of education, reform focuses on the learning experience itself. Questions such as how to teach and what to teach are very important to improve and develop a quality educational system. Reform also points out what is to be achieved from the educational system. In the United States educational system, students are under the influence of the educational institution until the age of 18. What are the goals and standards set before students reach the age of 18? Is the educational system trying to produce happier adults, a strong work force for the future, or active and well informed citizens?
