Video Producer/Video Editor/Media Content Developer

LukeMalone

May 11, 2012

EdLab is filling a wide range of positions on the video team. Videographers, video editors, and journalists with an expertise in documentation and a passion for education are all encouraged to apply. We are building a team for our many web-based video series. We seek enthusiastic candidates with strong video production and editing skills. We place a strong emphasis on collaboration but with the ability to operate individually, with knowledge of all aspects of video production. The incumbent will be expected to work with teams of videographers, educators, graphic designers and engineers to help define and complete projects that serve EdLab's mission. EdLab at the Gottesman Libraries envisions, designs, and pilots projects for an education sector that is attuned to the emerging post-industrial, globally-networked world. EdLab is organized in small flexible multi-disciplinary teams that collaborate to develop educational tools and resources for diverse learners. Below are more details for each available position. Video Producer:

  • Work with the EdLab team to document and produce interviews and lectures that occur onsite and around campus.
  • Familiarity with Final Cut and/or Adobe Premiere is required.
  • Familiarity with digital video cameras.
  • Working with the video team to convey meaningful content in creative and engaging ways.
  • Proactive and responsible.
  • Passionate about education.
Video Editor:
  • Edit short web videos for the different educational series which EdLab produces.
  • Experience documenting and storytelling in video.
  • Editing with Adobe Premiere.
  • Presenting multiple versions of cuts for feedback.
  • Proactive and responsible.
  • Passionate about education.
Media Content Developer:
  • Work with the video department and research team in order to identify current trends and innovations in education. Help with pre-production for video assignments by establishing relationships with organizations, scheduling shoots and conducting interviews.
  • Researching and discovering innovative educational opportunities and organizations.
  • Reaching out to organizations and individuals in order to conduct pre-interviews and coordinate filming days with the video team.
  • Conduct filmed interviews on location.
  • Proactive and responsible.
  • Passionate about education.
We are filling both part-time and Graduate Assistant positions. Please send a cover letter and your resume (including a link to your work or portfolio) to edlabjobs@tc.columbia.edu.
