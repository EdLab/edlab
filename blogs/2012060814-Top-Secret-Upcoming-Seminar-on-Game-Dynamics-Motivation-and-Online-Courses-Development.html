Top Secret: Upcoming Seminar on Game Dynamics, Motivation & Online Courses Development

Kate Meersschaert

June 8, 2012

Private Please! Hi Team EdLab! Details Alex Sarlin, a TC Alum, early Knewton team member and Scholastic producer is almost 100% confirmed to give a seminar later this summer (August?) This is particularly exciting because at that time he will be able to talk about an as-yet-released, new scholastic online learning initiative that he has been a part of. Please don't share this info yet as it is still being finalized! Your Turn: What are you curious about re: online courses, gamification, constructivist curriculum development and badge ecosystems?
