Optional Essay Written, NOT. Submit a Self-Produced Video.

Arianna Choi

February 23, 2010

New York Times' article, To Impress, Tufts Prospects Turn to YouTube, led me to shuffle through some of the videos published by high school seniors as a part of their application portfolio. Reportedly, about 1,000 of 15,000 Tufts University applicants this year submitted their optional essay 6B: post a 1-minute video that "says something about you." Everyone is on the selling/pitching side these days, and there are lots of new ways to reach out. (Recap: Jo's post on student-produced musical video on Why I Chose Yale and Rebekah's post on how social networking sites are changing the way high school athletes are being recruited by major colleges.) Check out my three favorites. They made me smile and go "awww" - let's see what reactions you have! 1. In My Shoes 2. Stop-motion essay by Max 3. Original Rap!
