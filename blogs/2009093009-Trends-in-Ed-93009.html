Trends in Ed 9.30.09

Jeannie Crowley

September 30, 2009

I have been obsessed lately with the new generation of tablets and e-readers on the market. It seems as if I'm not the only one. Gizmodo recently released images of Microsoft's plans for on-screen multitouch keyboards that will be part of the Courier tablet. Most screen-based keyboards (like the one used on the iPhone) require the user to look at the screen to type. Microsoft's new keyboard automatically aligns the keys with your hands. If you move your hand, the keyboard realigns to the new position. This new keyboard will make it much easier to take detailed notes when reading e-books and e-documents.
