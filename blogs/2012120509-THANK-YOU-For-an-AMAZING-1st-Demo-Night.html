THANK YOU!!!! For an AMAZING 1st Demo Night!

Kate Meersschaert

December 5, 2012

Private Please! Thank you team EdLab for making last-night's demo night a success! The feedback I received was unanimously good. It seems that EdLab is providing an important opportunity for the edtech community to connect in a highly credible setting. Here is some of the buzz I overheard: "you are providing a valuable resource" (from an edupreneur) "I was hesitant to ask my 'hard' question, but I thought, 'why not, this IS Columbia'" (Teacher & edupreneur) "You could only hold the demo portion of the night and I would still come." (edupreneur) "I love this space it looks like a warehouse..." (networker) "I want to put my desk right there & work here (pointing to a space in the seminar area near the window)." (edupreneur) "When is the next demo night? Next month?" (CTO) "How can I present a seminar or demo?" (after telling them that all presenters had to first conduct a seminar) "The energy of this event was so much better than others... and the timing of demos was impressive." (edupreneur) "Great job! I know how hard an event like this is and I really appreciated all the details and forethought." (organizer of another edtech Meetup) Overwhelmingly, the feedback was really amazing and it was thanks to your hard-work and support that this event went-off without a hitch or technical difficulty! You represented the EdLab with flair and energy. Thank you for tweeting on our #EdLabDemos tag and asking great questions! I want to extend my biggest thanks to my co-host Janice!!! And... to Hui Soo, Brian, Daniel and Gary for your hard-work, support and help making this event happen! Also, a special thank-you to Danielle for her beautiful design elements throughout the event! I want to thank the team that stepped-in and manned special elements of the event like check-in, Q&A, Timing and more... Jo, Rebecca, Laura C., Laura S. and Megha! Thank you to the services team who stepped-in to greet guests and direct traffic (Michelle, Jordyn and more!)! Finally, I want to thank George & his team, including Danny, Oumar, Francisco and more for your valuable help with set-up and event management. Excited for you to share your thoughts, feedback and takeaways (& pictures!) in the comments below!
