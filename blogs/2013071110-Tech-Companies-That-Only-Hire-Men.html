Tech Companies That Only Hire Men

Brian.Sweeting

July 11, 2013

Last week the BBC reported that girls are outperforming boys in science subjects in the UK, according to data from Pearson. Since then I've been searching for more examples of women excelling in tech and successful women encouraging increased participation in STEM areas. Despite many positive stories, organizations, and influencers that all point to a future where women are more equally represented in tech fields, there are still some discouraging reminders that men can foster exclusive environments, i.e. this Tumblr, Tech Companies That Only Hire Men, featuring job search language like:

  • ”The ideal candidate is the go-to-guy for GUI with a strong Java background."
  • ”He must have technical aptitude to drive definition of integration needs... ”
The authors of these job posts obviously assume that the only candidates who are qualified or applying are men, and it definitely wouldn't fly in other industries. In addition to changing this language, how do we change this mentality? In what specific ways can we foster more inclusive communities and environments?
