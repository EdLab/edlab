Videos I Wish: The Accounting Equation

Bismark Appiah

July 29, 2014

As part of EdLab's object to promote vialogues, they have introduced the Digi-Q team to a new project, which I see to be fantastic. I believe that this new project will help us build a networking relationship with our past and present professors. As it goes to demonstrate, how much we care about helping them to help their students. The gist of the project is to pick a course and create videos explaining concepts or topics that will be beneficial to students. Furthermore, these videos must be the type we wished to have known. Here is one video I have created, which focuses on Accounting. Please feel free to ask questions and also add your comments.
