D&Rchives!

Demetri Lales

March 27, 2012

Hey everyone! Today Laura and I will present D&R to discuss ways to properly represent the archival materials in the post-spreadsheet era. Join us on our journey to explore the different ways we can represent the archival materials of Teachers College and proclaim “death to the spreadsheets!” Please help us answer the following questions: How do we prevent “the spreadsheet effect” in our future designs? Is this even possible? What are your thoughts on our plans for the wiki and map specifically? Are we near the right track or can you think of other systems or strategies that would be more sustainable, efficient, or enduring? These representations will benefit us internally, how can we push and publicize our improved workflow and increased clarity? If you have any feedback or suggestions on what we have presented then please comment below. Thanks for your help and enjoy the meeting! **Update** Thanks to everyone for their feedback! We will be taking in your feedback, investigating further options, and keeping you updated on our discoveries and new solutions! We'll keep making improvements on how to get these collections properly organized and search-able. In the meantime here are some pictures of your feedback!
