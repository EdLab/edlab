Sheep Can Recognize Barack Obama and Emma Watson

Ryan.Allen

March 1, 2018


Humans do not simply look at individual characteristics of faces to recognize a person; instead, we rely on an integrated totality of features for recollection. Researchers have long understood this human characteristic of vision, but it has been less clear how other animals process facial impressions, especially in cross-species recall. A team of researchers has tested how sheep recognize human faces through a test using celebrities such as Barack Obama and Emma Watson.

The sheep were first trained to recognize the celebrities. Next, they needed to choose the correct face flashed on a screen in order to get a tasty reward. Researchers found that sheep were able to pass the test with around 80% accuracy and have facial recognition patterns remarkably similar to those of humans.

To show that the sheep were not simply memorizing the images, the team used other images of the celebrities at different angles. The sheep still passed the test with only a slight drop in accuracy, but a drop similar to humans in the same kinds of tests. The results prove that sheep have advanced facial recognition capabilities that were previously thought to be found only in humans and other primates.
