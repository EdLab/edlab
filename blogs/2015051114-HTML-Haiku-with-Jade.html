HTML Haiku with Jade

Panisuan Chasinga

May 11, 2015

Want to write expressive, beautiful Haiku of an HTML? Try Jade, a templating markup language that translate to HTML automatically and lets you write beautifully and naturally. Our development team has been using Jade for mSchool design and development for the past week. Since we are all qualified as HTML elites with years due to years of posting on this blog, I'd assume you all know what I mean when I say "HTML is ugly, hard to read, and will likely give you Carpel-tunnel Syndrome for typing". Jade is a nice little template language that's considered a jewel among the Node community. However, Jade can be used in any context since all it needs to compile to HTML is a little installation of its command line tool.
