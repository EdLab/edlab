Classroom Without Borders

AbdulMalik

May 18, 2015

Educators are constantly working on trying to improve education. In order to improve the learning experience, it is inevitable to consider the fact that education has no boundaries. The classroom of the future as predicted by many researches is believed to be one that would enable global peer feedback. The classroom of the future reveals the cultural aspect of education. The cultural aspect of students improves the learning experience of teachers and students. The classroom of the future is indeed the classroom we have now courtesy iPodia, a project by USC's Viterbi School of Engineering which connects students around the world to learn despite their cultural differences.
