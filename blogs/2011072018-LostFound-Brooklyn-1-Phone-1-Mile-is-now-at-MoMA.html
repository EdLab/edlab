Lost/Found Brooklyn: 1 Phone + 1 Mile is now at MoMA!

Kate Meersschaert

July 20, 2011

I want to share some great news with Team EdLab! Last week I dropped-off my book project, Lost/Found Brooklyn: 1 Phone + 1 Mile at MoMA Design & Books on 53rd street! Many of you might remember my EdLab seminar on the project this past March, but for those of you who weren't there or were not yet on board, the following is a brief summary of the project. My main goal for this "iphotography" project was to capture a fleeting moment in a rapidly changing urban neighborhood. I set specific rules for the work that included: no photos of people, I could only use my iPhone, I must stay within a mile of my apartment and I could not edit the photos beyond cropping & saturation. Setting these parameters really helped push the project forward to completion and helped me to clarify my unique point-of-difference for both marketing and explaining the work! Now, a hardcover version of the book is available at the MoMA Design and books for a limited time! I have included the cover image as well as, a few inside pages. Thank you for providing a platform for me to share this work and if anyone has any questions about the project please let me know!
