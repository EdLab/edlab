Survey Feature of the New Vialogues Release -#2 Respond

Yudan Li

February 25, 2011

A participant of a vialogue can make a response to the vialogue's surveys. Below is the snapshot of a sample multi-choice survey. For a multi-choice survey, respondents need to choose exactly one option. For a check-all-apply survey, respondents can choose any number of options. A respondent can only respond once to each of the surveys. A message will be given if he/she has already responded before. Like the old text comment, users can still post reply to surveys by clicking on the 'Comment' button.
