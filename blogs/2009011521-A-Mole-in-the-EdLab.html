A Mole in the EdLab

HSC

January 15, 2009

I just saw this article in the New York Times by Sara Rimer about TEAL (Technology Enhanced Active Learning) (Ninth most popular article on January 15). Most folks in the EdLab will remember that there was an After Ed Special Report on this not too long ago. This also reminded me of an article by Sam Dillon in the Times regarding DC Schools Chancellor Michelle Rhee that After Ed TV reported on. It seems like the New York Times education reporters could get most of their education stories from After Ed TV. I for one won't be surprised if the Times begin reporting on NY2NO (by Erin Murphy) or Mayoral Control (by Simon Doolittle) or Double Dutch. Afterall, as Joachim points out, the New York Times "imitated" Looseleaf report from After Ed TV as well. Kudos to After Ed TV for getting these great stories out. Perhaps someone on the team can do a special investigation on the number of After Ed stories appearing in the New York Times.
