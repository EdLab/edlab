The Diary of My Grandmother, Translated by My Mother

Alexandra Lederman

April 8, 2015

I am so proud and pleased to share with you a new book written by my grandmother and translated by my mother! Memory is Our Home is my grandmother's diary of life in Poland before, during, and after WWII- covering a span of thirty years. You can read an excerpt here!
