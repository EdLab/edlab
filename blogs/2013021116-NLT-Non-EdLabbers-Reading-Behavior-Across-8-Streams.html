NLT: Non-EdLabbers Reading Behavior Across 8 Streams

Sharon Hsiao

February 11, 2013

Data source: EdLab Data Depository (Nov 2012-Today) Non-Edlabbers' reading behaviors are plotted. X-axis is date, Y axis is time of the day. Some findings:

  1. High Five is the #1 stream for non-Edlabbers.
  2. Not surprisingly, no non-edlabber read articles during the New Year except seen in New York.
  3. Seen in New York appears to be three clusters for non-Edlabbers. I suspect there might be special videos that the NLT team promoted.
  4. Between 3pm-8pm are the favorite hours for reading.
See a bigger image here. Smoothed by mean based on all the data confirmed that High Five was mostly read between the aforementioned hours. It's difficult to see when non-Edlabbers read NLSector. Wide spread currently shows the non-Edlabbers can be reading literally anytime. See a bigger image here.
