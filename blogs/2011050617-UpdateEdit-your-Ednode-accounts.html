Update/Edit your Ednode accounts

Megha Agarwala

May 6, 2011

Hello everyone, Please take some time out to visit your ednode profile, paying special attention to the following: 1. Please upload a picture. If you already have a picture, but it is squeezed or doesn't look good, please upload it again. We have made some improvements to the image processing capabilities of ednode, so you should not see your image squeezed in or stretched out. Tip: Please try and upload a picture with your face at the center of the picture. (We currently do not support auto crop) 2. Please fill your profile, so that you can see your own keyword cloud and so that other people can learn about you, when they visit your profile. Making some parts of your profile public to everyone, is highly encouraged for the same reason as above. 3. Accept/Reject Connection requests: You might be having new requests under your connections page. Please approve/reject. 4. Search for new connections & explore your recommendations. Do you like ednode , not sure, questions, comments? Please feel free to leave your feedback on Ednode
