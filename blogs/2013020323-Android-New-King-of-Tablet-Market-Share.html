Android New King of Tablet Market Share

abagigah92

February 3, 2013

A recent report by IDC around the world on tablets shows that Androids tablets are outpacing iPads. In the report, Android is grabbing a big amount of the market while Apple's iPad and iPad mini dropped below 50% market share. The combine market shares coming from Amazon of Androids such as Samsung, Asus and others are well over 50%. Apple alone stands firm with 43% in shares of shipment. This shows that Apple is doing very well compared to other companies under the platform of Android but collectively Androids now holds a bigger share of the market. The iPad still remains the single bestselling tablet and in 2011 Apple held 90% of the tablet market. In order to properly compete with Apple, Google's plan is to allow anyone to make and sell Android devices. This makes the number of Android tablets larger and also much cheaper in the market than iPads. Google has been using the same strategy in the smart phone market for the past five years.
