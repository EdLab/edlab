Dear Great Teachers, Thanks for Teaching Us.

Amanda Stessen

September 19, 2011


For this back to school season, TBD is hosting ThanksForTeaching.Us, a 30 day campaign to recognize amazing teachers all over the world. Feel free to post about teachers who have inspired you or check out the letters for ideas in your own classroom, such as quoting Simpsons episodes to demonstrate the relevancy of pop culture literature. Perhaps a bit cheesy, but for those going through student teaching or taking night classes while teaching full time, here's a little inspiration to help get you through some of the challenges!
