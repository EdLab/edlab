Ipad App Development

gnatriello

March 22, 2010

With the Ipad coming in April, it is no surprise that developers are working to have applications ready. Flurry Analytics is tracking development work for the Ipad, and it appears that games are the leading area. Other popular areas for development work include entertainment, social networking, sports, and travel. And this piece in the NY Times discusses the race to have applications available as soon as possible.
