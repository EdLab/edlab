The Art of Minecraft Maps

csd2126

March 20, 2017


The video game Minecraft has become more than a game, it has become a tool for artistic expression. Minecraft comes with two modes of play: Survival Mode and Creative Mode. Creative Mode allows users to construct their own, often intricate, worlds. Creative map builders use Minecraft as a medium to recreate and invent elaborate architectural designs.

All of these beautiful maps are made in-game, with incredible amounts of detail. Competition among different Minecraft servers allows for this new industry to emerge. Design collectives have even formed to build a business of making maps in this virtual world.

Minecraft has a large and predominantly young user base. How can this popular game be leveraged as an educational tool? Tell us about the educational applications of Minecraft in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
