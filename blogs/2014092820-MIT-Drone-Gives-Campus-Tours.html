MIT Drone Gives Campus Tours

Carmen James

September 28, 2014


With students flocking to colleges across the country for campus tours in preparation for the next application deadlines, what is the future of the campus tour? Drone technology has helped re-imagine what a campus tour looks like. Beyond the wow-factor of having a drone lead the way, a drone can personalize the tour to the studentâ€™s needs and interests and can take the student to the places that will most excite them.

In this video, the drone SkyCall gives a seamless tour of the MIT campus. The video begins with the student calling the drone with his phone. SkyCall then navigates indoor and outdoor space and takes the visitor right to his destination. Not to be missed is the jab at Harvard, a competitor of MIT: The student taking the tour is a surprised and awed Harvard student.

How is the drone able to navigate the campus so well? What other resources and apps could be developed to support the drone tour? What else could drones be used for on campus? Add your thoughts in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
