A Taste Of Math

EdLab Studios

August 6, 2014


How much do prestige and price affect our perceptions of preference, especially when it comes to a bottle of wine? And can these partialities be predicted? Scientists turned sommeliers, Jeff Porter and Coco Krumme, host a night of wine tasting at The National Museum of Mathematics (MoMath) in order to prove that, while "in vino veritas" holds true, "in statistics veritas" is equally valid. With the help some hardy wine, cheese, and chocolate specimens, this real-time experiment explores the power of statistics and probability theory, models, and heuristics with delectable results!
