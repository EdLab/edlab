Torsh Inc. is Hiring Teacher Classroom Data Analyst

Megha Agarwala

July 26, 2012

Torsh Inc is an early stage Ed Tech Startup that has developed an innovative teacher instructional support platform that manages the capture, upload, storage, retrieval and management of user generated, long form, in-classroom video content. They are looking to conduct a study where they can capture short cycle classroom data via video and then analyze that video to generate insights into teacher and student interactions, activities and behavior, key things teachers and administrators would want to learn from such analysis in order to help them improve their practice and increase student achievement, how frequently would teachers want these insights and in what format would they want the information ?
