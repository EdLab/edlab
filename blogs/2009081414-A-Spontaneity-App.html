A Spontaneity App

Jeannie Crowley

August 14, 2009

OffBeat is a (not yet made) mobile app that gives New Yorkers just-in-time notifications of strange events nearby. I thought this app could be modified to include college-sponsored events and student-initiated events (such as small music shows). The idea is to have a tool that new and current students could use to become more involved in unpublicized community events. I have a rough proposal online and screen shots of what the interface could look like. I'd love to have some people jump in and give me feedback about this idea. In the image below the corner is tilted to indicate a map is hidden beneath the photo (accessible by a thumb swipe). Also, the idea is the skin for the app would be photos taken and shared by users while at an event.
