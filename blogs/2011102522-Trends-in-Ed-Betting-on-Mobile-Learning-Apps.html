Trends in Ed: Betting on Mobile Learning Apps

Skanda Amarnath

October 25, 2011

Techcrunch has the scoop on SmarTots raising $750,000 in seed funding. SmarTots is not a mobile learning application itself, but rather an analytics and recommendation tool for mobile learning apps. It's impressive that they have raised so much for what is essentially a meta-mobile learning app. If SmarTots succeeds, it will inevitably come as a result of a rise in mobile learning apps overall. There is something to be said for having a tool at hand to navigate the ocean of mobile education apps available and seeing which are most appropriate for a given child. Nevertheless, I'm not sure if the evidence shows a definite substitution towards mobile devices for online learning. The success of SmarTots will serve as a valuable bellwether for mobile learning app demand.
