Techno for Tots

Linda Bloom

August 1, 2006

This week the BBC is running a report, on their 'Click' program , that discusses a recent report out of Sheffield University (UK) that looks at how children (18 months to 6 years old) are introduced to the computer. The report called, Digital Beginnings reviews the dynamic relationship between young children, computers and their usage â€” you can download the report for free. The program, 'Click' also reports on a course that teaches young children how to use the computer and the software applications; and how the gaming industry is using technology to make children computer literate. If you haven't seen 'Click', I recommend that you watch the video of the program and see the latest trends in technology in the European and Asian markets. The program also identifies the social and cultural perception of technology and how technology continues to assimilate into daily activities.
