D&R Meeting: Advanced Google Analytics

Janice Joo

May 21, 2013

Week 19: Zhou Zhou You will need the following: Today we will dive deeper into the user experiences of our various WordPress Teacher Network projects in order to determine what data to mine from it -- and how. What do we want to know about our users on the Teacher Network? What interactions should we be collecting data on? The outcome of this brainstorming session will support Zhou and the team to build custom variables and events for advanced Google Analytics in the context of the Wordpress Teacher Network.
