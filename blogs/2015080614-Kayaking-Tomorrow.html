Kayaking Tomorrow!

kk2012

August 6, 2015

Hi all! I promised Chris that I would remind everyone to bring sunscreen tomorrow. Bring sunscreen! The event begins at 12:30pm. A few other reminders from Manhattan Kayak: Location: Pier 84 (W. 44th & 12th Ave). Coming from the street, the Intrepid will be on your left. On the right hand side is a restaurant called P.D. O'Hurleys. You will walk up the pier, away from the street, towards our boathouse which is on the water. What to bring: - Water friendly clothing (i.e. shorts, bathing suit, rash guard, flip-flops/crocs/water shoes) - Change of clothes for after (and a towel) - Sun protection - drinking water (it get's thirsty on the Hudson!) They have lockers to store personal items while there. They have locks to loan, however if anyone has their own which they prefer to use-they are welcome to bring them.
