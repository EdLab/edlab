Online Tool for Syllabuses

Abdulrahman Sanda

February 16, 2016

The first day of classes at in college consist of professors describing the course material and class expectations. Do we often ponder what happens to the syllabuses after the completion of the courses? College course syllabuses outline specific subject in a timely manner. Professors dissect a particular subject then find a way to turn it into course material. Syllabuses "structures the main activity of colleges and universities. And then, for the most part, they disappear". Fortunately intellectuals who are inquisitive about utilizing these syllabuses for research have developed an online tool that will enable people to "learn new things about teaching, publishing and intellectual history". The online tool is known as the" Syllabus Explorer". The " Open Syllabus Project" (based at the American Assembly at Columbia) helped create this online tool. The Syllabus Explorer demonstrates diversified strategies that can help can support teaching and innovative perspective on publishings.
