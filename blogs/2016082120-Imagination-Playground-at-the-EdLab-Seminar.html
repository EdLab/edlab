Imagination Playground at the EdLab Seminar

EdLab Studios

August 21, 2016


"The iconic blue playground system known as Imagination Playground, is available in over 70 countries to support the development of children ages 2-10 years old. On April 20th, 2016, IP CEO David Krishock challenged EdLab Seminar attendees to exercise their own imaginations with a set of big blocks. Check out the talk and the related discussion on on Vialogues.

Excerpts from the discussion

@01:00 chingfu: These pieces are big enough for and invite collaboration. Plus, as David mentioned, without collaboration building some of the big structures is not possible.

@08:30 hapj2011: "Independent play is important to the development of children." The UN recognizes the right of children to play. Play is a necessity.

@51:00 gnatriello: It will be interesting to develop play experiences both for children and for adults - and perhaps in combination at times.
