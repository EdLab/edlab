Vialogues Marketing Update

Kate Meersschaert

December 4, 2014

***Private!!! As we continue to focus on thoughtfully marketing both Vialogues and New Learning Times, I thought I would provide a quick update on recent Vialogues focused initiatives and new directions. Thanks to Mason's exceptional product team flow chart (more on this coming-soon!), we were able to work-out a relatively straight-forward product marketing process. This process will inform the inception of all future marketing initiatives and endeavors. In a very small nutshell... Audience → Goal → Activity → Metric While this may not seem ground-breaking, the attempt to rigorously quantify and assess our marketing initiatives is a new, important focus for the team! Recap of recent & upcoming initiatives... -DILA Awards Vialogues (explore the full collection here!) sent to winners (Preliminary 2 wk Results: 5 out of 6 educators created an account, replied to our comments and want to talk more with us, while 2 out of 5 administrators responded and will share the tool w/ their districts!) We will continue to schedule Google Hangouts with these winners to follow-up and gain insight into their needs and uses for Vialogues. -Mini workshop with Philosophy of Ed students here at TC (thanks, Carmen & Jo!) -Site visit to School of the Future to share Vialogues & visit Adam Kinory & Jim Chin (see this post!) -Upcoming workshops scheduled with pre/inservice teacher groups here at TC (stay-tuned!) -Also, the Office of Teacher Education is using Vialogues as the cornerstone of their Innovative Educator Program (see attachment below!) -Possible opportunities to share our work with the following interested groups: ScienceFriday Southern History Project Literacy Design Collaborative More to come soon!
