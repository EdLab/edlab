Social commenting for video

Jamison Judd

November 9, 2006

A new site called Mojiti allows users to take existing video sources on the web and annotate them with a text overlay (think pop-up video meets traditional captions). Although the site is in beta and the tools are currently a little difficult to use, this could be a fantastic distance education tool along the same lines as CTELL.
