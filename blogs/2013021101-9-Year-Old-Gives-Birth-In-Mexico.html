9 Year Old Gives Birth In Mexico

Francisco.Mendoza

February 11, 2013

"A nine year old gives birth in Jalisco, Mexico." It is not everyday that I hear things like this, but when I did, it really caught my attention and a lot of questions immediately come to mind. The nine year old girl goes by the name of Dafne, and according to her family, they did not know she was pregnant until she was about seven months. We do not know yet if she was raped or if this is a case of child sex abuse. Dafne parents don't have the chance to pay as much attention to her because they work most of the day. Dafne is one of eleven children, which I can imagine would make it a lot harder for her parents to simply focus on her. This article explains a few more details to this story. What are your thoughts on a nine year old giving birth?
