What is the EdLab-Part 1

Nantwi

June 24, 2010

EdLabbers views on what the EdLab is from today's seminar: I've been wondering myths. A team of software developers working to build the TC community by increasing accessibility to the library collection and the student body.-Harry from Pleasantville An avant-garde unit at the TC devoted to the future of education. EdLab includes a Development & Research Group, a Video Production Group & an academic journal of TC. EdLab focuses on its five missions such as the future of schooling of libraries EdLab develops tools and products aimed at advancing the quality and effectiveness of education in America-Peeptheflix The EdLab is a research and development unit post of the Gottesman Libraries at Teachers College. It seeks to advance education by leveraging technology-Michael Dudly EdLab develops new ways of teaching-Ronaldinho from Brazil EdLab is an organization in Teachers College which has the purpose of looking for new and innovative ways to improve education/learning-Eura An educated related think tank and creative team-Jenny From The Block It is a research based space where people explore new ways of teaching and learning. It strongly focuses on the use of technology in education-Cri A space where people in schools of education look to (re) energize their work-Art Blakely A fortunate confluence of money and space, with increasing amounts of executive oversight and very little vision for connectivity to the community.-Rosalind The EdLab is a necessary and vital component for the growth and development for the future of education. It is a place where creativity is expected and encouraged. It allows us to use all our skills to address the pressing issues of education-Free Lunch? The EdLab is a place for thinking about and creativity resources for education. It is part of the library @ TC so it also services that community-Burrito Kingpin EdLab is an experimental lab driven by innovations in education.-El Wingador The lab is a place to experiment with new ideas for building educational tools-Plant Life A group of people researching education as it pertains to the 21st century. Designing tools to meet the needs of the TC community and the larger supposed educational agenda. A place where ideas can be tested but sometimes don't see the end result-Andrakhones This is the place in which people work collaboratively and give opinions and suggestions to active progress.-Idontknow A think tank that strives to innovate or (inclusive) influence educational practices with technology.-Episteme The EdLab is an organization of people with diverse educational and career background who come together to drive innovation in education, particularly where it intersects with technology and media.-Jet Stanton The EdLab is a number of things: -It is a hub of technological innovation of the 5th floor of the Gottesman Library -It is a source of research an output -Teachers AID -Filming for educational resources -Thinker of the future in education & educational resources-Bafana Bafana An education research, development, and media lab which follows and attempts to participate in innovations in educational technology development. A relatively invisible but potentially great place for new ideas at TC. A place that doesn't have enough pens.-Francis Crazy (positively)-all the creative ideas can come out!-Slimshady EdLab is a multimedia laboratory at Teachers College that develops multimedia platforms, tools, video and software about education-related topics and ongoing events and projects at Gottesmans Library-“The Producer” Collaboration of research design, analysts, video editors, website creators working together to turn new ideas into actual working education systems-Nietzche The Edlab is a research and development unit at TC which focuses primarily on creating innovative and cutting edge technology for learning. It has five foundations, all of which I cant quite remember-Status A space, a mode of creativity and designing in and out of institutional contexts. An eclectic place that optimizes collective effort by intelligently drawing on everyone's different strengths-The Riddler
