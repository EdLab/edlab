A Special Homepage

Pravin Sathe

September 3, 2008

The homepage of Emory's alumni magazine is a welcome departure from the usual homepage stylings of publications around the web. I don't know if it's better than the usual but because it's different and it's definitely refreshing. I emailed this example to Jeff Frank, our beloved Managing Editor at TCR because I was wondering if this was something we might do for a special issue of TCR as we rethink the site. His suggestion, one I think is spot on, is this might work perfectly on a redesigned EdLab site (Teaching the Levees comes to mind) Either way, looking to bring in print design flourishes into the online design world is never a bad thing. Even if sometimes it doesn't completely work in the interactive environment. At least it starts a discussion about why all of the homepages we see for publishing sites are similar.
