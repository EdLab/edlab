How to Promote Woman Education in Third World Countries?

Oumar Soumahoro

June 8, 2015

Brigham Young once said "You educate a man; you educate a man. You Educate a woman; you educate a generation." However, women in third world countries found themselves being restrained the privilege of being educated, under the stereotype that set them aside as being the ones who take care of the house. Women make up for more than half the population of students that are out of school. Moreover, many young girls do not get the chance to complete first level mathematics course, before they are given out for marriage . This video elaborates more on the living gap that exist between young males and females in third world countries. How can education for young females be promoted in this countries?
