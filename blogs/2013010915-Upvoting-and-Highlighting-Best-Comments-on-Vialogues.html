Upvoting and Highlighting Best Comments on Vialogues...

Janice Joo

January 9, 2013

During today's seminar, I had the following idea... I can't remember what triggered this thought... but the entire seminar was about Learning Analytics. What do you think about incorporating "upvoting" features to Vialogues comments. Not only will this crowdsourcing method promote engagement, ownership and responsibility over content (curation a la "wiki" style)... BUT I can immediately see this feature helping Jo to identify the "best comments" for featured vialogues... Especially if we anticipate for Vialogues to start snowballing more and more users in the near future. Example screenshot from Quora.com Also, maybe a special feature for moderators could be to go into the comments and "highlight" an important comment in one of his/her vialogues. Maybe highlight an important comment for the class, or draw attention to an important poll which they are to answer, upon watching the video at that time-stamped moment.... Furthermore, maybe the highest "upvoted comment" can be visualized in conjunction to the video themunbails on the homepage... perhaps the comment's text would appear upon hovering over the video thumbnail. Not sure about the interaction behavior of our users on Vialogues, but these are just a couple thoughts I had about how to promote engagement and keep users on our site...
