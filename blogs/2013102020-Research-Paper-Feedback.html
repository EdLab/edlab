Research Paper Feedback

Francisco.Mendoza

October 20, 2013

In my criminal justice class, one of our assignments is to write a ten page paper by the end of the semester. We have to do research on a specific topic and present to the professor and class. Many of the students in my class and I have had bad experiences with police officers. This has changed their way of thinking and most of them have negative attitudes and thoughts toward cops. My point of view did not change, although I did have a bad experience. Many students have chosen to write about police corruption. We watched the video below in my class about the experiences an African American male could have had and that changed his point of view about cops. Have you had any bad experiences? If so do you look at police officers differently? I want to write about police heroes to change or try to get others to see my point of view. If there are any articles or books anyone can recommend, that would be great. I need at least three articles and two books as resources. Share your thoughts on the video below.
