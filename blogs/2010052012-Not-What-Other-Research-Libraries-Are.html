Not What Other Research Libraries Are...

Frank Webster

May 20, 2010

I was reading an article by Donald A. Barclay about the futility of trying to maintain large browseable book collections in academic research libraries (The Myth of Browsing: Academic library Space in the Age of Facebook). Barclay is the deputy university librarian at University of California. Merced. Merced's library and indeed it's entire campus was born the same year that Gottesman undertook its most recent renovation. The library's shocking brand was and still is: Not What Other Research Libraries Are, What They Will Be. Anything look familiar?
