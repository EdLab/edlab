Writing Buddy?

Arianna Choi

April 23, 2009

Brian's idea about a new word processor or writing tool got me thinking about what's out there now. Besides Turnitin and Writecheck which is centered around reporting or avoiding plagiarism, there are also Proofwriter and Whitesmoke which is very costly and are advertised as an excellent writing assistant. Well, are they? I was especially interested to find out that Whitesmoke's writing software launched a separate version just for creative writing. How helpful is it, I don't know yet. They have not yet come up with 'research writing' version. Has anyone tried the Whitesmoke series or like to share favorite writing tool?
