"The Ahmed Plan"

Elysee Dougan

November 22, 2013

This week I created three videos on Marketing and only managed to recruit one person to Vialogues via face-to-face. In our team meeting, we discussed new ways we can go about recruiting people. Our new plan proposed reaching out more to teachers, and not just any teachers, but more towards college professors. We see that trying to obtain K through twelve level teachers will bring forth more difficulties; things like having kids ask their parents for permission to create an account for website that may have little to do with their school work can be a factor. The good thing about this plan is that we're not only emailing teachers, we're emailing the teachers who are teaching or is in contact with our field of work or our topics, making them more inclined to join the growing Vialogues family. If anyone wants to add to this plan or have anything they want to say, feel free to write in the comment section. Thank you for reading.
