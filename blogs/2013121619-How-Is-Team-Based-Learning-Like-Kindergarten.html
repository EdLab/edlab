How Is Team-Based Learning Like Kindergarten?

jvazquez

December 16, 2013


Team-based learning (TBL) is often touted as an effective method of teaching and learning, but what do students actually think of it? The video above showcases a group of students who answer that question with tongues fully in cheeks.

Drawing comparisons to kindergarten, this "infomercial" for TBL calls into question many of its practices: creating group names, peer-reviews, and group quizzes. It also sheds doubt on the efficacy of learning through peers rather than trained professors.

The students featured here are in med school, but could their discontent breed in other fields as well?
