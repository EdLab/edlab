11 Year Old Creates New Facebook

Daniel Vergara

January 13, 2013

I posted the video below in Vialogues because I found the story very interesting. The story is about an 11 year-old whose parents did not allow him to log into Facebook because he apparently lied about his age to create an account and they didn't approve of his constant usage of this website and his interactions with strangers. And instead of trying to figure a way of getting back into Facebook, he created his own website for those 16 and younger. The site currently has 7,000 users but in time I have a feeling this would grow mostly because there are not many popular social sites just for young people to socialize. Hopefully you can all check the video and leave your thoughts in the Vialogue.
