Amphetamobile wins a PIA!

Janice Joo

January 28, 2013

We just heard some great news about Amphetamobile... Just to recap, Dr. SiNae Pitts, founder and CEO of Amphetamobile, recently presented at our EdLab Demo Night in December 2012. Since then, one of the apps she and her team developed for The World Bank just won a Publishing Innovation Award for Best App Reference. Congratulations!
