Looking at the World Through a Cardboard

idrissa10

July 13, 2015

Google Cardboard is the latest innovation by the search engine giant and it aims to help improve learning everywhere. Google Cardboard works with Google Earth to create a virtual environment for students (or anyone) to learn. This new technology is made entirely out of cardboard. It works by inserting your phone into the cardboard and wearing it as one would a pair of glasses. It then gives a 360 degree view of your surrounding and creates a 3D environment. As of right now, only Android users can benefit from this technology because an iOS version has not yet been created. I like this idea of creating a virtual environment. It will help students become more engaged as they are already comfortable with technology and represents teaching moments for educators as well.
