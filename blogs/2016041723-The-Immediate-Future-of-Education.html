The Immediate Future of Education

AbdulMalik

April 17, 2016

Technology's influence on modern day life is as such that we are not surprised to children pick up iPads more often than a book. Even though most people feel like technology hinders learning, this new initiative seems to give us more than one reason to believe in technology based learning only. Using the concept of a one room school house, children will be placed in mixed age classrooms. There will be no gym, cafeteria or even report cards. The reason for eliminating these traditional school rituals is for these students to spend the bulk of their time with the loads of technology they will be provided with in this house classroom setting. Cameras will be placed all over the room to track progress and the level of interaction. I believe this program will not only make teaching easier but also enable children to be innovative without any fear of making mistakes or failing.
