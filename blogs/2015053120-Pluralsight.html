Pluralsight

Dana Haugh

May 31, 2015


"We deliver world-class training that’s easy to comprehend and quick to learn. That’s the beauty of being taught by the best. Our course authors are an elite group of tech and creative professionals, innovators and leaders. You know who they are. As a subscriber, we connect you with authors through discussion boards for ongoing, real-time learning." (via Pluralsight site)

Not Another MOOC
Pluralsight is an online learning platform that publishes high-quality training courses for professionals in IT, creative design, and administration. Founded in 2004, Pluralsight started as a classroom-based training program for developers interested in expanding their knowledge and expertise in the growing field. Today, Pluralsight is entirely web-based and features over 700 expert instructors and over 3,500 courses focused on training technology professionals across the globe.

Pluralsight is not just another MOOC. This platform was created for hardcore coders and techies with a strong desire to expand their knowledge base and increase their skillset. As digital trends continue to evolve, tech professionals worldwide are expected to stay on top of the latest software and coding languages. More and more companies are seeking out individuals who can fill hybrid roles, blurring the line between positions like developer and designer. Recognizing this shift, Pluralsight created a way to help techies to stay relevant in this highly transformative industry.

Rich Content for the Real World
With over 3,500 existing courses and new content developed each day, Pluralsight is guaranteed to have the latest material for every learner. The company's comprehensive learning management system ensures subscribers are able to access their courses from anywhere, on any device. Users can track results, assess their work, learn from experts, and connect with peers, regardless of their location or time zone. Individual, business, and academic subscriptions are available, offering flexible and affordable options for every budget. Recently, Plursalsight acquired Digital-Tutors, an online learning library for digital creatives, and Code School, a platform for beginner and intermediate developers looking to expand their coding knowledge, ensuring their continuing reign as a top site for professional developers.
