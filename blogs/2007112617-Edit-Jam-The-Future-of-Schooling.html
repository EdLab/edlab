Edit Jam: The Future of Schooling

Skye MacLeod

November 26, 2007

Please join the video production team on December 14th from 5-8pm for a special Edit Jam, After>Ed style! The event will lead a group of talented up-and-coming high school video producers through the making of channel shows such as Speak Out, Out of the Box, Techknowledge, and P&S Review. Part After>Ed.tv outreach, part video production and graphic design workshop, part gigantic brainstorming activity, this Edit Jam is sure to get high school students actively thinking about becoming After>Ed contributors, while addressing the question of "What is the Future of Education?" To prepare the students for the event, I will be leading a series of workshops, starting this Friday, at International High School, KCC. The initial workshop will be a forum for students to explore the new After>Ed website, give feedback on existing shows, and suggest directions for future episodes. This workshop will serve as a pre-production activity to the main event on December 14th. During the Edit Jam, the same students will be deeply involved in the actual video production process of the shows they blogged about. We hope to tease out some interesting and engaging content to show in future lineups. The final post-event workshop will be for both parents and students and will serve as a focus group and screening of the student's video production. Stay tuned for some exciting and unique content on the After>Ed Channel, and we hope to see you at the Edit Jam!
