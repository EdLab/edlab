Public Libraries Add Multimedia Learning

abagigah92

September 9, 2013

Public libraries across the country are expanding their goals beyond loaner books and resources. Public libraries are now providing students with the opportunity to participate in digital learning. This enhancement in the library setup is to make students college and career ready. During the past two decades, libraries have added technology services because of a growing need to better prepare students for the 21st century, which have pushed these venerable institutions into new roles. Libraries are teaching tech driven workshops for middle and high school students and helping students bring their best practices into school classrooms and other institutions to share.
