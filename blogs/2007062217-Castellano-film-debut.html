Castellano film debut

Joachim Castellano

June 22, 2007

Next week there will be an exclusive NJ screening for a film which my brother Ken wrote and directed, and I recorded sound for. You all are welcome. If you can't make it there will be a NYC screening in the future. Please feel free to circulate the information below. ******** Close to Midnight is complete! And, before we turn our attention to the festival circuit and the steps beyond, we want to share the film with our friends and supporters: Upcoming Private Screenings: Wednesday, June 27, 2007 - 8PM Thursday, June 28, 2007 - 8PM Mountainside AMC Lowes Theater 1021 Route 22 East Mountainside, NJ 07092 Seats will be available prior to each screening from 7:15 PM to 8:00 PM. Theater doors open at 7:15 PM. Screening starts promptly at 8:00 PM. Donation: $10.00 (To help offset the cost of theater rental only. As is required by most festival submission guidelines, these are non-commercial/not-for-profit screenings and do not represent public theatrical premiers). If you wish to reserve a seat, please email info@closetomidnight.com with the names of the people attending and on which night. Blocks of seats will not be reserved without names. No more than 10 seats per group. Please, only reserve seats if you are committed to attending. Reservations cannot be guaranteed after 7:45 PM. For additional information about the film, please visit: http://closetomidnight.com A detailed CTM Information Packet can be downloaded here: http://closetomidnight.com/info/info.pdf For those friends not in the local area, we hope you can see the film at a festival near you soon! On behalf of the cast and crew of CTM: Thank you for your support and friendship throughout this process!
