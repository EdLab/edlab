Learning Theater Learning Experience: The Time Trek

jvazquez

November 13, 2013

Team: Ahmed, George, Fred, Jo Traveling on an automatic walkway will open up a world-- a familiar yet new world-- to curious learners. Beginning from the Big Bang and moving into just last week, the Time Trek will take learners on a journey through time, space, and history. As learners walk through this 3D, interactive experience, the timeline against the left wall will show the period in history while the images on the right side will show key developments of that period. At any point, learners can step off the walkway to touch the image wall in order to learn more about what they are seeing. Science and history educators will find this space highly useful. Here we are, 600 million years ago, when signs of the first dinosaur began to appear.
