Glimpses of the Learning Theater - The Wicked Problems Seminar

gnatriello

December 29, 2016

On November 30, 2016 we held the EdLab Seminar in the Learning Theater.  Our goal was to try out the Learning Theater as a seminar space while developing some materials that might be useful for thinking about systemic problems for the upCreation Experience.
