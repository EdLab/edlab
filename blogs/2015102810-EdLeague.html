EdLeague?

JHeltz

October 28, 2015

**Keep Private** Though Chris's endless supply of candy and the Harry Potter House challenges are stirring up some camaraderie in these dark days of 5th floor construction, I was thinking a little friendly competition and sports-related escapism could do us some good! In an effort to promote work culture, I would like to propose the EdLeague!--aka Fantasy Basketball. So, if you're interested (and have an amazing pun-tastic team name on the tip of your tongue) please say so here! Also, make mention of what day and time this coming week you'd prefer to draft. After that, I'll organize and launch and all you have to do is set your line ups! See you on the (imaginary) court!
