Missed Connection

Meredith Powers

December 19, 2014


For all the emphasis on integrating technology and education, many elementary schools across the country still lack sufficient broadband access, from Massachusetts to Mississippi to Washington state, and even the California Bay Area. The Internet’s potential to overcome distance barriers is invaluable, particularly for students in rural schools, the very ones that end have the least access.
