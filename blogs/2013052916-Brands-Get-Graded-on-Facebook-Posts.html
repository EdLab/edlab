Brands Get "Graded" on Facebook Posts

Kate Meersschaert

May 29, 2013

Facebook recently announced a new "grading" system for brand-generated newsfeed posts. Users can now like and then "rate" how interesting they find the content companies post. VERY interesting development in the world of crowd-curated digital content. What do you think? Will this impact our NLT Facebook account? Ads? Currently the platform is using these ratings internally to inform future newsfeed content decisions. However, imagine if these ratings as SproutSocial opines, were available to advertisers. From SproutSocial article:

After feedback has been collected, Facebook will then use these ratings to improve the content shown in News Feed in the future. Currently the company relies on an algorithm known as EdgeRank to deliver posts that are most likely to be engaged with. Asking fans directly what they find interesting is a great way to determine relevancy.
