EdLab EdTech Take Featured on Noodle.org Blog and Tumblr!

Kate Meersschaert

February 11, 2013

Noodle.org, the innovative college and course recommendation platform, who recently presented a seminar here at EdLab, featured their CEO, Joe Morgan's, "EdTech Take" on both the Noodle blog and Tumblr page. We created this series to help us to track and discover useful resources and tips for "edupreneurs" and hope that this vialogue from the growing #EdTechTake series will prove useful and interesting to ed-focused researchers and entrepreneurs near and far. What edupreneurs and researchers would you like us to feature? What questions would you like us to ask? Your feedback is always welcome and greatly appreciated!
