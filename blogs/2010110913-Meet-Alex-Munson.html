Meet Alex Munson

Michelle Mcnamara

November 9, 2010

Continuing the get to know you series, today we get to learn a bit more about Alex.  Alex is from Baltimore, and is a student in the mathematics education program.  You will probably recognize him as he's been working at the library for a year now, and lives on campus in Whittier.
What did you want to be when you grew up?
Astronomer.
What was your first job?
Waiter.
What has been your worst job?
Waiter.
What has been your best job?
This job has been pretty good.
What do you remember about your first day of school?
I was not too impressed.
Your favorite meal? steak. Are you a morning or night person? night. Your favorite dessert? tiramisu. Song you love no matter how many times you hear it: Got my mind set on you. A country you'd like to visit? Greece. A sport you enjoy playing (or watching)?  swimming for both.  Football to watch.
TOP THREE....
...Things to do at the library?
Surfing the internet.
...Best places to read?
Actually Butler is a good place to read  after I finish my daily swim and with a cup of coffee.
... Favorite authors or books?
Sir Arthur Conan Doyle: Sherlock Holmes.
The most amazing thing I've ever seen was.... the Milky Way.
Thanks Alex for sharing your responses!  If you would like to make like Proust,  you can answer the Proust questionnaire (and also compare your answers to celebrities’ responses) here or listen to Eric Cartman answer the questions via an NPR interview.  And, if you would like to know more about Alex, just ask the next time you see him!
