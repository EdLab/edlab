Marshmellow Challenge: A Design Workshop

Carmen James

April 11, 2014

There are so many ways to build collaboration in order to design better solutions to problems. Collaboration, in fact, often is the hardest part. This is a great workshop that can be easily replicated (though not easy to do). The workshop teaches teams to work more effectively together. The basic structure is teams of 4 have to build a tower with a marshmallows at top. They are given a limited selection of supplies. The findings? The workshop has been replicated across the nation from Fortune 500 CEOs to children. The group that is the best at the workshop is Kindergartners. The worst, recent business school grads.
