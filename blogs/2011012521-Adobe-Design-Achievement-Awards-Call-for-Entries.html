Adobe Design Achievement Awards - Call for Entries

Melanie Hibbert

January 25, 2011

The Adobe Design Achievement Awards celebrates achievement reflecting the convergence of technology, education, and the creative arts. Competition is free to enter and open to all students, faculty and staff of higher education institutions around the world. Categories include: Interactive Media: Browser-Based Design, Non-Browser Based Design, Application Development, Mobile Design, Game Design, Installation Design, Innovation in Interactive Media in Education Web and Mobile Analytics: Web Analytics and Mobile Application Analytics Video and Motion: Animation, Live Action, Motion Graphics, Innovation in Video and Motion in Education Traditional Media: Illustration, Packaging, Photography, Print Communications, Innovation in Traditional Media in Education Finalists will receive Adobe software and a trip to Taipei, Taiwan. Category winners also will receive a cash award of $3,000. The deadline is soon, though: Jan. 28!
