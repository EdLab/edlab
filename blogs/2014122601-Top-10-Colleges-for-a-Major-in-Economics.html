Top 10 Colleges for a Major in Economics

abagigah92

December 26, 2014

Classes in economics educates students in how businesses and governments use money and resources and how it might influence the market. Courses in economics ranges from in areas such as statistics, accounting, calculus, business economics, developmental economics, economic theory, microeconomics, macroeconomics, and money and banking. These courses are geared to introduce students to the impact economic theories have on money and the economy. It is always important as a high school student to know what schools are the best in the areas that you find interesting. Below is the list of the top 10 schools for getting an economic degree in the United States.
