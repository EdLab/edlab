EdSurge Introduces a New Site Design

Kate Meersschaert

August 28, 2012

EdSurge recently launched a new homepage! This is a great design from a "brand-awareness" and subscriber acquisition perspective as they clearly highlight their sponsors and their "sign-up" button. Also, the "news-feed" is interesting, fresh and I like the featured story slide-show. EdSurge has the unique challenge of developing a relevant stand-alone site as they are best known for their weekly newsletter. Regardless, the site is a definite improvement over the last design which had rather unclear navigation. What are your thoughts? I find the dominant feeling to be more of a landing or branding page, than an interactive site. As I mention above, this might be the whole point. Agree? Disagree?
