Research Consultation on Geography Education/Geography Literacy

Allen.Foresta

May 11, 2011

This consultation outline reviews promising resources for research on topics that include the nature of geography and geography education, the concept of geography literacy, and means of searching specific journals on the subject as well as the works of specific authors. Try free-text search "geograph* literacy" in WorldCat (109 results:  69 books, including dissertations; other media as well) Journal Articles ERIC:
  • in Advanced Search mode, try geograph* literacy alone, or search "geography" as Descriptor in conjunction with specific journals, i.e., by Journal Name (note:  ERIC covers the journals Journal of Geography and Journal of Geography in Higher Education).
  • or try geograph* as Descriptor in conjunction with specific authors (e.g., Stoltman, J. or Bednarz, S., or Schmidt, S. or Salinas, C.) as Author.
