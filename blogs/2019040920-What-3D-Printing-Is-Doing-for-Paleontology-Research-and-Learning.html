What 3D Printing Is Doing for Paleontology Research and Learning

Melanie.Hering

April 9, 2019


While the advent of 3D printing has led to important advancements in many fields, this technology has found particularly intriguing uses in paleontology. From the American Museum of Natural History to the movie Jurassic World, 3D-printed dinosaur bones have come to play an important role in research, education, and entertainment. have been utilizing 3D printing of dinosaur bones. Southwest Adventist Universityâ€™s Dinosaur Museum and Research Center has been involved not just in 3D printing facsimiles of their 20,000 bones, but in creating a digital archive of this growing collection. Even more impressive is the fact that this collection is available for members of the public to download.

This digital archive holds an expanse of data that can help researchers at the Center as well as other professional and amateur researchers located elsewhere who might not have access to any original bones. The center hopes that their resource will contribute to the work of citizen scientists everywhere.
