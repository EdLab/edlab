PocketKnowledge Entry in Wikipedia

Stephen Asunka

September 14, 2006

I have created a PocketKnowledge entry in Wikipedia. The initial post is fairly basic, but as an encyclopedia article, it has to be fairly exhaustive. Therefore we need to provide more content including the rationale, timeline and personalities behind the creation of PK, potential benefits, user statistics etc. We should therefore individually and collectively add the appropriate content and also strive to keep the information as current as possible. For the benefit of those who might not be aware, anyone can post or edit an article in Wikipedia once you have an account. Creating an account is fairly easy. Go to the Wikipedia.org main page, click on the Create Account link located at the upper-right corner of the browser window and provide your username, password and email. After submitting the page, you can then login and be offered the opportunity to modify content. To get to the PocketKnowledge page, you can either search for PocketKnowledge (the search function appears to be case sensitive - at least for now) or type http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PocketKnowledge in the address bar of the browser.
