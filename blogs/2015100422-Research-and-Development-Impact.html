Research & Development Impact

Bismark Appiah

October 4, 2015

Most companies these days recruit individuals who can bring new methods and ideas to the table. This article highlights the importance of research and development (R&D), which is the foundation of innovation. Companies that invest more in R&D show signs of growth and advancement. Likewise, investing in R&D can help boost the overall economy of a country: skilled workers will be employed and new products will be on the market for consumers.
