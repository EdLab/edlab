Will Chrome's New "Save to Drive" Extension Replace Evernote?

Kate Meersschaert

December 12, 2012

Check-out the new Chrome extension that lets you save web content to Google Drive. I am excited to test drive (sorry!) this new extension... as I seem to never use Evernote anymore. Sadly, the ground-breaking platform with the cute elephant-based logo and innovative founder just seems "too far" away from my usual searches and feels as if there are too many barriers to easy access. Perhaps this new extension will satisfy all my "clipping" needs?!
