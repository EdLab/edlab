The Esquire Augmented Reality Issue

Jeannie Crowley

November 4, 2009

In an attempt to compete with online news sources, Esquire magazine is releasing an augmented reality edition on November 9. According to an early review, the reader will hold up the cover to a Webcam, "and cover subject Robert Downey Jr. steps off the page in 3-D, offering a primer on Esquire's augmented reality issue while the cover copy flies off the cover behind him. Tilt the magazine and the on-screen animation moves in sync." I don't know exactly what this means or what it would look like, but I think we should get a copy for the lab just to play around with. The official site does not provide many details.
