Learn a Language in Three Months

rendy

October 27, 2013

Learning a new language is one of my primary interests and I never thought it could be possible for a person to learn a language in just three months. The Vialogue below shows a good method on learning new languages, particularly German, which I consider a difficult language. Everyone has their reasons on wanting to learn a new language. The narrator of the video shows us a different way to learn a language in a small amount of time, and gives us some advice: overall strategy, resources, and a game plan. Basically, find someone to speak with like a friend and a tutor to teach you the grammar.
