The Secret To Learning A Skill In 20 Hours

jvazquez

February 24, 2014


Throw away any excuse you held on to before! In this video, Jonathan Fields interviews author Josh Kaufman who gives concrete ways to become proficient in any skill in just 20 hours. This is no infomercial. Kauffman offers honest, practical, and candid ways to end the frustration and attrition associated with learning something new.

His 20-hour threshold makes Gladwellâ€™s 10,00-hour rule seem a bit superfluous. After all, the goal for most people when undergoing a new challenge is not to rank the best in the world at that skill, but rather to acquire a desired level of competency. Nothing can be more intimidating than having to live up to an ideal of mastery before even getting the basics of a skill down.

Josh is a champion for persistence when it comes to tackling a new skill. But the author of The Personal MBA Manifesto offers up plenty more advice in his detailed interview featured here.
