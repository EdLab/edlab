EdLab Participation in the JPMorgan Chase Corporate Challenge!

Joanna Fu

April 12, 2012

Hi, everyone! Pranav brought to our attention that the JPMorgan Chase Corporate Challenge 3.5 mile run is happening in Central Park at 7:30p on Wednesday, June 13 and Thursday, June 14. While race entries have filled up quickly and we will be on waitlist to participate, please let me know if you are interested in joining our team to represent the EdLab at the race! Team registration closes by 4/19 (and we are not allowed to add additional participants once the company team has been created) so please let me know ASAP or before 4/19. Thanks, and happy running!
