Advantage of Being a Gamer

Khalil Abubakar

June 12, 2013

Researchers from Duke University have concluded that a gamer can have an advantage over a non gamer in terms of the way the brain handles visual responses and both reacts and remembers visual scenes. The study consisted of 125 participants which were sought out from a large group who participated in a visual study and labelled themselves as intensive gamers or non gamers. Each of the 125 participants were asked to take part in a visual sensory memory task which consisted of flashing letters and arrows. Circular groups of letters were flashed on the screen in front of volunteers for a very short period of time and the arrow showed the location of the letters on the screen. The participants were later asked to name the letters and where the arrows were pointing. Gamers outperformed non gamers at the test in every interval. The study showed the gamers ability to visually focus on a task at hand and reject everything else. Also, the study proves gaming makes the mind make better visual decisions and remember scenes. This goes a long way to prove gaming does more good than harm depending on how you manage your time on it.
