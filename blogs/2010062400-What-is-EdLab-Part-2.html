What is EdLab Part 2

Nantwi

June 24, 2010

EdLab is an educational research lab which aims in pioneering in the field of educational technologies and strives for making better pedagogical tools to consist student learning-Fiat Palio From India A unit at TC that is charged with spearheading new innovations for the college and education sector.-Silver Surfer A research, design, development and publishing unit at Teachers College, Columbia University concerned with charting the future of education.-Allison from DC EdLab is a group of teachers, designers, programmers and researchers who work to create new technology that may be used by TC or the outside world-Sally EdLab is a group of multitalented people working towards serving the field of education and more specifically the needs of library and students, teachers and community. EdLab develops tools for online learning, and video based tools like Critter. Collaboration is an important piece of work at EdLab and every project is thoroughly discussed, collaborated among design, publishing, development and research groups-Red Mist From NY EdLab is an exploratory space of ideas and projects all involving the push of technology to further educational aspirations of individuals or organizations-HeMan from Orlando A confused project caught between student service, academic research and real-world production that fails to be a leader in all three. Also fails at being pragmatic-James T. Kirk The EdLab is a group of researchers, developers, publishers and designers that run a journal of educational research, conduct original research mainly at the intersection of education and technology, create services for the TC library and community, produce video and design.-Rob A creative space to think about and address educational issues-Paca A collaborative space where the future of education is realized and shaped through developing technologies-Marilyn Monroe A space for invention & collaboration-Lady Gaga The EdLab is a think tank-esque unit of TC that addresses current issues in education, finding technological solutions for problems in education or for emerging related markets-Max A place located on the fifth floor of the library at TC-George from Minneapolis An education/technology think tank-Olivia Benson A dynamic and interactive working space for people of diverse backgrounds to experiment and create new online/offline education services-ABC A research and development production that belongs to TC Gottesman Library where mission is to promote education innovations to schools, society and family.-Albert @ USTC EdLabs is a collective. EdLabs is a confluence of thoughts and actions.-Goro The EdLab is a group of researchers, designers, developers, publishers, videographers & all-around innovators who are committed to reshaping education, libraries & publishing through technology. (Kinda like an artists' enclave…without the enclave)-Chick Pea An innovator hub that fosters growth and creativity within the education sector-Madarne Bourier A place to figure stuff out, accidentally or intentionally, and then share the stuff with other people-Leonard from Ouaha Me plus a network of people who are committed to our mission-Jackie EdLab is an intellectual free space where novel ideas are born, nurtured, and released into the wild-Garee Natriyellow EdLab is the “engine room” of the Gottesman Ship. The Lab not only ensure that the “ship” is operating soundly, but also charts the course of the llibrary and College as a whole.-Lady Gaga
