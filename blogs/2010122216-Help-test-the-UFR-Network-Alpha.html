Help test the UFR Network Alpha!

Patrick Carey

December 22, 2010

We have launched an alpha of the UFR Network we hope to have teachers use in combination with the UFR curriculum. We need help testing the site and would love any help from folks at the Lab. If you are interested please leave a comment below and I will add you as an administrator to the site. We have a post on the site outlining the feedback we are looking for, which I have also included below. Thanks! Most importantly we want feedback on the system of post and responses.

  • How well do you think this works?
  • Can you see this being applicable in a HS classroom?
  • Is the system intuitive? Not intuitive?
We also need feedback about being a user.
  • Try creating and logging in as a fake user. Users can be created in the User section of the admin dashboard.
  • Use your fake user to respond to post.
How does the admin dashboard work? Our goal was to allow the teacher to quickly add users and get a sense of how the site is being used. It is broken down into weekly sections. If you click an author's name you will be taken to their page. This page contains a breakdown of their recent posts as well as their stats breakdown over time.
  • Is this helpful as a user?
  • Will students respond to this in your opinion?
These are the key areas that make up the site, but we also want any and all feedback. Feel free to leave feedback as posts or microposts on the site, or as comments on this blog post.
