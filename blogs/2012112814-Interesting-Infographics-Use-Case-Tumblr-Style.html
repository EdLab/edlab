Interesting Infographics Use Case... Tumblr Style

Kate Meersschaert

November 28, 2012

The Washington Post pulls all their proprietary infographics and cross-promotes them on a separate "graphics Tumblr". Could we adopt a similar approach for a specific New Learning Times (NLT) content stream? Teachers College Record (TCR)? What would this look-like? What purpose would it serve? Could it possibly feed traffic back to our home sites? Tumblr just made the top 10 list for social networks... should the EdLab make use of this platform? If so, how?
