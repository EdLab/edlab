Data Preservation on Science Friday

Kaitlin.Kehnemuyi

December 17, 2017

If you aren't here late or on the weekends you might not have had a chance to cross paths with Lauren, one of the Service Associates. This means you might not know about this awesome article/series she wrote for Science Friday. It went live last week. It's a three part series focused on data preservation and technology obsolescence. She got to interview Cory Doctrow and other great people fighting to save data.
