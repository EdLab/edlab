Survival School

EdLab Studios

April 14, 2015


As a population that has become reliant on technology to exist, we risk losing the ability to connect with nature and provide for ourselves. Particularly in emergency situations when disaster strikes, urbanites need the knowledge to help them survive. Mountain Scout Survival School founder, Shane "White Feather" Hobel, wants to put the power back in the peoplesâ€™ hands by teaching basic, ancestral skills that could mean the difference between life and death in the face of catastrophe. Taking place in the all too familiar Central Park, the course forces participants to stop and think differently about awareness, community, and the resources the city has to offer.
