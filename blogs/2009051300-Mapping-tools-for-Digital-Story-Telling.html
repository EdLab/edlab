Mapping tools for Digital Story Telling

Ting Yuan

May 13, 2009

A few EdLabbers (i.e., Doug, Arianna, and myself) are interested in making and investigating tools for digital story telling (DST). Here is an initial glog mapping current available tools for DST. Among the tools, Animoto and Dvolver are free online movie making tools, while JayCut is a free online video editing tool. Critter is under development. Please click on the glog to get to the respective links. Jaycut Dvolver is an easy-to-use animated movie maker, suitable for making simple comic like cartoon clips. The shortcoming of it is the limitation of choices of figures and scenes.
