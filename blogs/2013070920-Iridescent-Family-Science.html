Iridescent Family Science

EdLab Studios

July 9, 2013


Recognizing that parents are an integral part and real stakeholders in any child's education, Iridescent Family Science seeks to engage families in science learning together. Through a series of experiment-based design workshops using simple engineering principles, parents and children access science as a part of life, and a fun way to learn together. Family Science also gives parents insight into their children's lives as learners and thinkers, and gives children a chance to see their parents learning, struggling, and working things out alongside them; they can come to see each other as fellow explorers, as peers and as allies in science and education alike.
