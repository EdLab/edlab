Live-stream of #140edu Conference

Kate Meersschaert

July 31, 2012

#140edu... Happening Now If you were ever curious about the 4th annual #140edu conference check-out the live-stream throughout the next few days to get a feel for this conference which promises to, "Explore the state of now." My takeaways so far... If you do pop-in, what are your initial thoughts? So far, and I have only been listening in lieu of music for the past 30 minutes, is that it is just a bunch of random educators and administrators talking about why social media is cool... I hope the rest of the conference is more interesting! "Pop-in" & see for yourself!
