Killer Mike on Bill Maher - True Reason of Violence in America & Origins of Hip Hop

Patrick Williamson

July 1, 2015

Often times I hear critiques about rap and hip hop culture, as saying that it promotes violence and only perpetuates the problem. However, too frequently, this critique is taken from an objective point of view, failing to realize that rap music is a reflection of the realities and achievements of a subordinated community. Killer Mike explores the common fallacy that music is the catalyst of violence, while schooling Bill Maher and millions of viewers on the origin of the culture...All in less than 5 minutes.
