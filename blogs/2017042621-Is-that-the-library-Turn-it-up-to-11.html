Is that the library? Turn it up to 11

bhughes

April 26, 2017



I wanted to remind the 5th floor team that the 1st floor team started working longer days on Monday (until 1AM), and continues until Monday, May 8th! As you might suspect, finals puts extra pressure on many of our services. Here's a brief update about what happens at the end of Spring term:


  • We're open 118 hours/week.
  • More patrons! Russell Hall sees more than 3,000 entrances per day.
  • 1,000 books are returned! Hey, that one you checked out in September is finally due.
  • E-Resources are used more. Â I suspect. I didn't check. I bet we can use the rise in Support Requests as a good proxy for this statistic...
  • Patrons fight for library space. That's right, it's not finals until our patrons start fighting with each other. That's why we bring in Abby the Certified Dog Therapist. She reminds us of our common humanity. Because she's a happy dog.
  • I remember to check our Twitter. I reward happiness. I accept criticism.
  • The whole team keeps the CUIT team on their feet to keep our Pawprint printers running. The students like the printers.
  • Fines are paid. Or degrees aren't conferred.
  • Library rooms are booked for high-stakes, winner-takes-all dissertation defenses. Occasionally winners throw parties and spread confetti everywhere. We track them down and shame them into saying a personal "thank you" to our Facilities staff who have to clean it up.
  • Books and more books.


So, if the services team seem a little more harried than usual, it's because the pressure is on them to deliver happiness.


Perhaps you've heard tell that "the Library is at the heart of the TC experience?" For these two weeks, it's a heart undergoing a fast-paced sprint.
