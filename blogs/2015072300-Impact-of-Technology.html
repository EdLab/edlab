Impact of Technology

Evans Frimpong

July 23, 2015

Technology is drastically affecting many aspects of society in a positive way, especially education. Students have the internet to access countless amount of information for learning purposes.Technology has eliminated the need for physical interaction, with the introduction of video chat allowing students the option of a virtual study group. Younger students are exposed to computers through educational games and other multimedia forms. Furthermore, thanks to technology, most universities and colleges can now offer online classes for students.
