Games For Change

EdLab Studios

August 28, 2013


From mental health to education to peacekeeping, field-changing visionaries are tapping into the ability of games to engage, educate, and transform. This three-day festival, Games for Change, brings together everyone who believes in the transformative potential of games to share tips and stories, workshop, and hear from leading voices in the games-for-social-change industry. Special workshops that focus on education coexist with broader sessions focused on gaming's ability to change its participants for the better by connecting games to reality rather than by submitting to an escapist platform. As gaming enters mainstream education and pushes forward on the periphery, programmers as well as educational innovators have ambitious goals for gaming to foster social change.
