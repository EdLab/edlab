Global Nomad Group

Erin Murphy

January 5, 2009

The Global Nomad Group organizes video conferences with students across the world. I noticed they've gotten a lot of press recently. I was checking out their website and I saw they are sponsoring an Open Architecture Design Challenge - they are asking people to submit their ideas for a new classroom. They want students to team up with local architects and submit their ideas. I think it would be really interesting to follow a group of students or teachers who want to submit their designs. Does anyone know of any students or teachers who might be interested in doing this project? And would let me film them? http://www.openarchitecturenetwork.org/challenge
