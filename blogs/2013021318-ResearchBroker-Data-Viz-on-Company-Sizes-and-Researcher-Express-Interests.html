ResearchBroker Data Viz on Company Sizes and Researcher Express Interests

Sharon Hsiao

February 13, 2013

This is a density chart of how much researchers express their interests toward research projects. Small companies like Torch.Inc attracted a lot traffic. There are no clear patterns yet but there's a tendency by smaller companies to have more research projects (dense). Big companies have few projects, but once they do offer, they attract researchers' interests. For a larger chart, click here. Another bubble chart: Color groups the company sizes. The bigger the bubble indicates, the more interactions between researchers and companies. (Data is collected through ResearchBroker (RB) database, where researchers expressed their interests toward companies through RB). A larger image click here.
