DataGotham Event Tonight at Strata

Megha Agarwala

October 23, 2012

Its Data Week Oct 22-Oct 26 , and tonight there is a DataGotham event which will feature short talks from data scientists on topics like influence in social networks, statistics in sports and fashion, and the beauty of normalization. This event is from 7:30pm to 9pm at New York Hilton, Beekman + Sutter North room , 1335 6th Avenue, New York, NY 10019 The speakers for this event are as follows: Fred Benson, Data Engineer at Kickstarter Roberto Medri, Analytics at Etsy Matthew Healy, Data Visualization at Foursquare DJ Patil, Data Scientist in Residence at Greylock Partners Anna Smith, Data Scientist at Bitly Jake Porway, Data Scientist Max Shron, Data Strategy Consultant Brian Abelson, Data Analyst at Harmony Institute Joseph Turian, Ph.D consultant on data monetization, machine learning, NLP and business intelligence Michael Li, Data Scientist in operations research and financial engineering at Princeton University Sharon, Janice and I will be hopping in and out of the event, if anyone else is interested, please join us and register for the event here (Free). We'll also try liveblogging the event.
