YPR? Would Like Your Feedback!

Jessica Blum

May 12, 2010

Chingfu and I have been working on a teacher-support podcast project, Your Permanent Record? (YPR?), and would love your feedback on our first three episodes (keep an ear open for some star EdLab cameos!). In it's intention, YPR? aims to create a reflective and supportive audio space for educators, and hopes to report, consider, and investigate various topics relevant to K-12 classrooms. We're experimenting with different formats, and ultimately envision the show including a number of different styles: traditional talk-show format, featured guest interviews, participatory podcasting, and case discussion / analysis. We currently have three episodes ready for your review, and would like to invite you to listen, comment and critique as you may. To experience these episodes, you can listen on the players below, or visit our YPR? Podbean site for more information and episode context. Each podcast is about 25 minutes long, so feel free to listen while you work, snack, commute or . . . whatever! Thank you very, very much in advance for your feedback. We plan to put it to good use.
