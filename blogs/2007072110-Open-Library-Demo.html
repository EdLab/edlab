Open Library Demo

gnatriello

July 21, 2007

For those tracking the progress of efforts to make books available online, it is worth noting that the the open content alliance has launched a demo version of its open library. The library will eventually feature a catalog of every book ever published and a large collection of books in digital form. For an interesting review of this effort, see the comments of Ben Vershbow who considers both interface issues and the larger questions of who will own and control book resources -- Google or open projects like this one.
