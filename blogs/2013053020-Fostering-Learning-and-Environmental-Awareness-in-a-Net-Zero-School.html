Fostering Learning and Environmental Awareness in a "Net Zero" School

Laura Scheiber

May 30, 2013


Caring for Mother Earth is one of today’s most pressing societal issues. Luckily, new and innovative efforts to minimize our green footprint occur in many shapes and forms. Take Lady Bird Johnson Middle School in Texas, for example. This "net zero school" produces as much energy as it consumes through the use of sustainable energy. According to the school’s Assistant Superintendent, the school was intentionally designed to break from conventional school models in terms of its infrastructure, curricula and administrative policies. The school building serves as an authentic living science laboratory with interactive technologies in the hallways, and a pedagogical approach aimed at instilling a sense of passion in the students for learning.
