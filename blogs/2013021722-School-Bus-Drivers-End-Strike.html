School Bus Drivers End Strike

Francisco.Mendoza

February 17, 2013

After five long weeks on a strike on the streets of New York City, school bus drivers are heading back to work this week, making it easier for students to get to and from school. Five of the the city's mayoral Democratic candidates offered to look over the transportation school system. I believe the bus drivers have a right to fight over job security because they are the ones who take care of the children and make sure they are safe. They take them to school and bring them home without a problem. Mayor Bloomberg on Friday said "I urged the union leaders to end the strike and made clear that the City would not be held hostage ..Tonight, they agreed." Do you agree with the school bus drivers going on strike? Was it worth it? This article sheds more light on the strike.
