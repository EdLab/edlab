GuitarBots

Demetri Lales

July 26, 2013


GuitarBots is a site that employs the power of gaming to teach users how to play a guitar. The look and feel are similar to Guitar Hero but instead of using the plastic game guitar, players in this game use their own real electric or acoustic guitar. To progress through the game, aspiring guitarists play the chords that appear on the screen and try to match the rhythm of the lesson. GuitarBots uses the computer's built-in microphone to listen to a player’s notes and give instant feedback in realtime on the performance. The game can inform users when they are playing too fast or too slow. Users earn badges and other rewards when they successfully complete lessons which could encourage users to keep getting better.

Pros:

The game makes use of social functionalities to encourage players to play and practice more. Users can invite their friends from social networks to play and compare scores and stats. Friends are grouped on a leaderboard and can compare each other’s completion, motivation, and achievement percentages. The game is good practice for beginners and veteran guitarists alike. More experienced guitarists are able to take a skills test so they can start playing at levels that are challenging for them. The site also provides a library of Youtube instructional videos.

Cons:

Users need to sign up for a monthly subscription in order to play GuitarBots for an unlimited amount of time. Free users have a five minute play limit, which can be extended when users invite their friends to play for a daily total of an hour of free play.

Our Takeaway:

GuitarBots is unique in that users are learning to play a guitar by practicing on an actual guitar. The gaming elements of the site serve to encourage aspiring guitarists to practice playing for a longer period of time. While the site mainly focuses on practicing guitar, it does provide video lessons for extra help on chords and other techniques. It would be interesting to see similar teaching sites for other subjects to put more of an emphasis on practice rather than passive video lessons.

The Bottom Line:

GuitarBots teaches guitarists to become rock stars through gaming.
