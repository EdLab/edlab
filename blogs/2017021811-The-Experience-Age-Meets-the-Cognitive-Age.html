The Experience Age Meets the Cognitive Age

gnatriello

February 18, 2017

As we approach the development of the physical, digital, and human tool sets that will become the library, we have been exploring the blending of data, immersion, emotion, and craft as well as the combination of science and art. These elements have functioned as both inputs and outcomes of our processes. In this journey we have been drawn to boundary blurring projects. An interesting example of of such a project is described in a Rolling Stone article on the role of Watson technology in the creation of Alex da Kid's song, Not Easy.
