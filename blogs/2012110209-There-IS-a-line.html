There IS a line

Janice Joo

November 2, 2012

Image: Photo from Social News Daily Image: Photo from Mashable American Apparel and GAP capitalized on the effects of Hurricane Sandy and received much criticism for it. I don't think their market research was completely off; there were certainly some individuals on social media stating boredom. But the market research was misinterpreted. They tried too hard to be cool, clever, nonchalant, and forgot to be sensitive. Line crossed. Something to think about as we try to come up with our own clever marketing tactics for RB, SSK, Vialogues, NLT, and more...
