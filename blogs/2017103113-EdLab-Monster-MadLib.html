EdLab Monster MadLib

saraehardman

October 31, 2017

Thanks to everyone who participated in the publishing team's Halloween D&R today! Here is a copy of the MadLib story we created together. Enjoy the spooks and laughs!



Everyone at the EdLab was working late one snowy night to prepare for a big election.


George heard a loud "boo" from the learning theater. Everyone gathered in the kitchen to listen and finally after nothing more was heard Ryan W. volunteered to go down with George to investigate.


After a minute, Hui Soo tries to join HIS/HER colleagues, but when HE/SHE tries to leave the fifth floor, HE/SHE discovers a beautiful metal bar had been welded across the double doors preventing anyone from leaving. Hui Soo became hysterical. “We’re trapped!” Hui Soo screamed.


Another crackle was heard and cautiously everyone made their way back to the kitchen. On the way, Patrick threw open the window at the desk where the copy editor used to sit and killed out. HIS/HER screams could be heard all the way down to the courtyard where HE/SHE landed with a gargle.


Deciding that someone had to be the hero Cinthia grabbed a chair and said HE/SHE was going to go down and investigate.


Debra became upset and admitted that HE/SHE was secretly in love with Cinthia. “I never knew,” said Cinthia, “but this is bigger than both of us.”


Malik reminds everyone that the situation is dire. HE/SHE grabs a bowl for protection and runs down the stairs before anyone can stop HIM/HER. Before anyone else can follow, they hear a hot scream from the stairs and the remaining Edlabers slam the kitchen door shut and lock it. Now they are trapped on level 5.


All of a sudden they hear a boring moo coming from the video team and they run toward the pod. On the way, Ziyin pauses and claims to have seen a shadow of a zip code monster in the phone booth closest to the dining booths. Sara screams, dances, and faints. But nobody cares. Least of all Brian, who never liked Sara.


Just then, the lights above the dining tables start spitting, the air turns cold, and the windows sleep. With its neck raised high above its noisy head the zip code monster let out a blood-curdling cough and sinks its teeth into Sabarish and rips his hand from HIS/HER body.


“Awwww!” shouts Iyob, and hides behind SeungYeon. As the zip code monster lunges at SeungYeon, HE/SHE shouts: “But I never got to show off my sexy Superman costume!” The zip code monster bites SeungYeon in the arm and kills HIM/HER instantly.


Joann says, “Quick! Everyone to the phone booths!”  Xiang, Melanie, and Ryan A. huddle together and everyone else crams in. As the door is pulled shut the lights in EdLab go out and they are thrown into darkness. There is a collective intaking of breath and then, suddenly, the floor of the booth collapses and they tumble into the Learning Theater, where they hear a creepy version of Hotel California playing and see George and Ryan W. dancing the macarena. Their eyes are weirdly spicy.


Just as the Edlabers think they’ve found safety, the learning theater door is ripped off its hinges by the zip code monster. “Oh no, Gary is going to hate this so much!” says Cinthia. Suddenly Gary appears behind the monster and says “I do hate this. But I also love it!” Gary picks up a huge post-it note and throws it at the monster’s finger. “Eat my dog!” says Gary.


All of the surviving EdLabers link toes and shout "Yikes!" and charge at the zip code monster with the big glass door. Then they wrap the monster up in a projector and force it into a vat of boiling water. The monster lets out a bloodcurdling Wow! as it disintegrates, while all the Edlabbers cackle.


With a final groan, the monster disappears into the water. Silence descends upon the Learning Theater.


“Now what?” says Iyob.


“Now,” says Gary. “We feast.” Into the liquid, he mixed flour, butter, sugar, and mashed potatoes. “We are ready for another batch of Learning Theater cookies!”
