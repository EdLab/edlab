Don’t Pretend They’re Naked: A New Workshop Series on Effective Communications

Adrienne Garber

February 7, 2010

Teachers College alumnus Nabeel Ahmad will be leading an exciting workshop this week entitled "Don't Pretend They're Naked: A New Workshop Series on Effective Communications." Starting Friday, February 12th, this 4-session weekly workshop covers: (1) an introduction to effective communication; (2) delivery of a customized presentation; (3) review of your recorded presentation with constructive feedback; and (4) re-delivery of your polished presentation in the final session. RSVP is required with a commitment to attend all four sessions. Space is limited to 10 persons. RSVP by Wednesday, February 10th to library@tc.edu. Details: Location: The Gottesman Libraries, Russell 305 Date: Sessions are February 12, February 19, February 26, and March 5. Time: All sessions are 10:00-11:30 a.m.
