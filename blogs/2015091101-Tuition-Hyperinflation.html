Tuition Hyperinflation

abagigah92

September 11, 2015

Paying for higher education has always been a major issue in the United States. As the primaries get closer many presidential candidates are presenting their ideas on how to improve the system of paying for higher education. These politicians must first address the issues leading to hyperinflation of tuitions which results in a huge burden of paying student loans by students. Another issue is highlighting weather these institutions are really providing the students with a proper education that will secure them a job which will help them pay their debts. Many average schools turn to over price which leave their students with huge amount of debt after graduating because the student cannot afford to pay their loans and cover their living expenses at the same time. The government must put in policies that protect student against hyper inflation. Tuition have increased about 440% the last quarter century. This usually creates an economic imbalance if students are not able to pay for their debts after graduating. Higher education institutions needs to held accountable for these problems.
