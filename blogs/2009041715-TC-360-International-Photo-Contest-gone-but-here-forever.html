TC 360 International Photo Contest - gone but here forever

Molly Riordan

April 17, 2009

The Teachers College Office of International Services, with the help of EdLab's Media Design team, sponsored its second annual International Photo Contest in conjunction with International Week 2009. Teachers College community members were invited to submit photographs taken outside the United States. An expert panel judged entries on their artistic and technical merits, as well as their ability to inspire international awareness and engage in deeper cultural exchange, and chose nine finalists for the International Week display. The photos were on display in the Kasser Family Exhibition space on the 1st floor of the library until April 15, 2009. If you missed it, you can see the featured photos through Pocket Knowledge and check out pictures from the April 1st reception on Flickr.
