125th Anniversary: Call for Actors

Jennifer

March 7, 2013

Do you have a talent for acting that is going unused? Maybe you do and you don't even know it. Maybe you just want to give it a shot. For Teachers College's 125th, EdLab is producing a series of short (2 minute, slightly humorous video re-enactments of major events in Teachers College history. The films will be silent, and the acting commitment will be a very short, fun way to exercise a part of your brain that may remain unfulfilled by even the most creative of midterm papers. The first short video will be about the decision to make all school buses yellow. We will film on the 2nd floor of the Gottesman Libraries at 2pm on Tuesday, March 19th. Light refreshments will be provided. Fame guaranteed. If interested, please email jdr2147@tc.columbia.edu, Joe Riina-Ferrie, member of the Edlab video team.
