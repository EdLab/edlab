Would You Stick to a Low Paying Job?

Oumar Soumahoro

January 9, 2014

An increase in youth employment in the last decade has left many recent college graduates without job prospects and settling for whatever they find. Some might argue that part of the blame could be labeled at students who might have studied majors with low job prospects. Unemployment and the possibility of becoming successful without enrolling in college has led many to be have second thoughts about a college degree. As interest rates for loans continues to increase and students given a short amount of time to start paying them off, it might be very beneficial for future and current college students to take a good look at the most in-demand jobs. This will help them choose a major that will assure a job after graduation. This article highlights the most in-demand jobs on the market. Some of these jobs will always be open to new graduates. Others can be obtained without a college degree. This said, would you have chosen a low paying job from this list and avoid all the hassles of college?
