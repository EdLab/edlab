STEM Education in 5th to 12th Grade Schools

Kafoumba Doumbia

June 29, 2014

It is time that we start introducing more technology in our schools including engineering courses that can help our students better prepare for the future. According to the Vialogues below, students from low-income backgrounds are learning about the theoretical aspects of education and its implementation in real life. They are learning about skills such as communication, presentation and questioning; all skills essential to their future as students and professionals. This video should be a model to better our education system and better educate our children. The methods used by the teachers make students think logically and become problem solvers. The students are tasked with different problems that require critical thinking and the use of software to learn some engineering skills. By introducing technology and high-level math to students, educators are preparing them for college and eventual life as engineers, mathematicians, among others.
