Gender Equality Strategy for Sustainable Development

abagigah92

March 10, 2014

Development for girls and women around the world is very important for sustainable growth. Although women are very much involved in the global economy, the playing field is not equal. UNESCO will be hosting a meeting to review the work being done to support equality for women and girls around the world. The meeting presents an opportunity to reaffirm that women are equal and important to sustainable growth. The meeting is to highlight the urgency of empowering women and girls through access to education and a platform to speak and access media. March is usually a month dedicated to women rights and empowering young girls to dream big. More importantly, it is a time where the focus is on finding new platforms and ideas to help close the gender gap in terms of educational and economic opportunity. Though the rights of women and girls have increased in some societies, there is still a lot of work to be done to ensure they have opportunity to quality education, health and lifestyle. I will report on the notes from the meeting in a later blog.
