A Day in the Life of an NYCiSchool Student

Melanie Hibbert

December 11, 2009

I was fortunate to have the opportunity to follow around a student for a day at NYCiSchool, a new school located in SoHo that focuses on technology integration. Students use laptops in most classes, take online courses, and have close advisory relationships during their four years of high school. Here is a video of a "Day in the Life" of Emmi Perez, a 9th grader from the Bronx. It's pretty "cinema verite," definitely showing rather than telling; if anything, it demonstrates a typical day of a high schooler. (Password to view is: after ed).
