Delivering Happiness

gnatriello

May 31, 2010

On June 7 Tony Hsieh's book Delivering Happiness will be released. This should be of interest to us for several reasons. First, it discusses how to deliver a compelling customer experience. Second, it describes how to get employees engaged in the process of customer service. Third, this sounds like a good tale. Also of interest is the website for the book, particularly since we have from time to time been involved in helping authors develop sites for their books. This one seems worth studying for the lessons we might learn.
