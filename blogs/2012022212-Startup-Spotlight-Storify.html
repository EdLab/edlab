Startup Spotlight: Storify

Kate Meersschaert

February 22, 2012

The Details and Backstory Co-founder Xavier Damman, who previously launched Publitweet, believes that, "there are voices on social media that deserve to be published on main stream media," and this vision informed both his original startup and now Storify. Storify won the Startup Accelerator contest at South by Southwest in 2011 and recently launched a free iPad app that allows users to take their social-media storytelling on the road. Storify provides a platform for users to curate and create social media-based stories around a central theme or topic. Using their built-in editor, you can search for relevant social elements like tweets, videos, or status updates and drag them directly into your storyline. These social media-enhanced stories can then be embedded into any blog. Unique Point of Difference Simple-to-use social media content discovery and curation allows the citizen journalist or professional to easily create social stories of all kinds. The addition of an iPad app seems apt as people move further away from their physically tethered devices in order to meet the stories where they happen. EdLab Relevance and Takeaway As we consider content creation for our New Learning Times project, it is increasingly important to think of new ways to link together traditional "news" and new, more social/interactive media.
