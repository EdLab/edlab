Vialogues Mini Challenge

Brian.Sweeting

January 26, 2016

*Private* Today's D&R will serve as refresher for creating compelling and shareable Vialogues with engaging moderated discussions that can be featured on Rhizr, NLT, and the blog. Groups: 1. Robotics/ Artificial Intelligence 2. International Technology Education 3. Student Data 4. Wearable Technology 5. Computer Science (Learning, Teaching, Teacher Training)

Criteria for selecting a Vialogue (also available on the NLT Style Guide):

  • Timely
  • Short-ish (<5 mins is ideal)
  • Medium-high production value
  • NOT viral content (<10,000 views)
  • Choose a video that lends itself to discussion

Videos to avoid:

  • Anything that feels like (or is) a commercial
  • Videos that are of little or no substance
  • Topics or people too often covered in education (Sir Ken Robinson, MOOCs)
  • Pulling too often from the same [big] sources (i.e., Edutopia)
  • Content designed to teach about a subject (i.e., Kahn Academy videos, OCW)

Tips for moderating a discussion:

  • Avoid yes or no questions
  • Avoid questions that are TOO broad
  • Avoid re-stating or quoting moments from the Vialogue
  • Encourage users to add their own personal experiences and reflections
  • Take advantage of the multiple choice/poll feature
  • Hyperlink to additional resources when helpful
  • Don't ask leading questions
  • Keep in mind what you want users to learn

At the end of the activity, please embed your group's Vialogue (with discussion) in the comments below. Thanks everyone!
