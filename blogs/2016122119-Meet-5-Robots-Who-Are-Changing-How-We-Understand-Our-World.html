Meet 5 Robots Who Are Changing How We Understand Our World

Brian.Sweeting

December 21, 2016


Over the past year, the advancement of programmable, personalized, and humanoid robots has created exciting new learning opportunities. In the video discussions below, meet five robots who are changing the way we live, learn, and interact with the world.

1. Using a Social Robot for Student Learning
Tega, a fluffy red robot with a blue mohawk, looks like a character out of a Disney movie and is programmed by Android devices to socialize with students on simple tasks.

2. Root the Robot Makes Coding Accessible to Kids
Root the robot is a primer on coding fundamentals for kids and can be programmed to draw shapes and designs on paper.

3. How User-Created Social Robots Are Finding Work at the Library
The Bibli robot program in Colorado provides personalizable robots to help autistic students in the library.

4 . One Hospitalâ€™s Newest Staff Member is a Robot
Pepper is a humanoid robot who works at hospitals; it can show patients to their rooms, hold conversations in 19 languages, and offer bedside comfort.

5. Humanoid Robot to Explore the Great Blue
Ocean One, shaped like a scuba diver and controlled by humans on land, is exploring shipwrecks and dangerous deep sea dives that are inaccessible to human explorers.
