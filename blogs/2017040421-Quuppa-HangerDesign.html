Quuppa Hanger/Design

Kevin.Camacho

April 4, 2017

This past week, Alvaro, Chingfu and I have been working on a more effective to way to hang the Quuppa devices on the ceiling at the Learning Theater. The devices currently hang on a zip tie. We are working on finding a solution so that the devices can hang from a steel pipe. Alvaro and I have been using round bend u-bolts, screws, washers and other tools to get the Quuppa base to hang from the pole.

In our quest for other options and ultimately a solution, we ran into Zoey, Veronica and SK and asked for some suggestions. They introduced us to the 3D printer. Zoey taught Alvaro and I how to use Sketch to design our own models of Quuppa hangers to the 3D printer and hopefully produce a more efficient and effective product.

 On a personal level, my limited but fun learning experience working with the Quuppa system and the design team to come with a solution has reignited my love for design. I have yet to decide on a college major (taking mostly electives) and I am leaning towards something in design.
