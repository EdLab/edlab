NY EdTech Meetup: Parental Engagement in EdTech

Brian.Sweeting

September 4, 2013

NY EdTech Meetup is hosting an event on September 25th, Parental engagement required; an EdTech conversation outside the school at Microsoft about whether and how technology tools are giving parents insights into their kids' learning. The keynote presenters will be Alexandra Meis Co-Founder, Director of Community Partnerships, Kinvolved @Kinvolved Peter Bergman Associate Professor, Columbia Teachers College I'll be attending; let me know if you plan to RSVP!
