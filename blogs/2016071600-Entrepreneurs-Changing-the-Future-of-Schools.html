Entrepreneurs Changing the Future of Schools

ABARRY

July 16, 2016

We often think about business startups, making money and ownership when we read about entrepreneurs. However, Matt Candler, the founder and current CEO of 4.0 schools, is looking at entrepreneurship very differently. He is utilizing an entrepreneur mindset to innovate schools in the United States. 4.0 Schools was founded several years ago to mainly serve as a platform for technologists and entrepreneurs to create solutions for America's schools. Candler believes the best way to ameliorate schools and education is to work on the user rather than the entire school system. He refused to work with the typical system and preferred to work with entrepreneurs and education leaders to identify common problems and solve the problem collectively. His main goal is to help facilitate the startups of new schools, especially in New Orleans. With the help of 4.O schools, New Orleans has made great strides in its education system. Candler hopes to expand his vision all across the country.
