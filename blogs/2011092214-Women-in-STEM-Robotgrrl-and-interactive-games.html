Women in STEM: Robotgrrl and interactive games

Julia Martin

September 22, 2011

I just became aware of Robotgrrl through this article on Makezine. As you can see in the video, her robots are structurally held together by popsicle sticks, and if you click through to her About Page on her blog, you'll see she started creating robot with a simple kit at the age of 13! I know I'm always going on about this stuff, but like I said in my last Women in STEM, I don't want the idea that there are no women in STEM to somehow block out the really cool young women who ARE out there math, science and TECHING it up!
