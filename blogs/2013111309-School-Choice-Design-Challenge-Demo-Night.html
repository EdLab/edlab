School Choice Design Challenge Demo Night

Brian.Sweeting

November 13, 2013

*Private* Last night Kate and I attended a demo night sponsored by iZone's Innovate NYC Schools initiative at General Assembly. Six startups presented applications aimed at helping eighth grade students apply to NYC high schools. Ingeniously, the startups were judged by who else, but a panel of [surprisingly shrewd] students. Spoiler alert: the winner was Find the Best, a startup from California that collects information from companies and institutions and makes it easier to analyze. While there were other more glitchy apps in earlier stages of development, the entire design challenge was a positive example of how innovating with open data can help people make more informed decisions.
