Digital Destiny

gnatriello

December 21, 2006

I recently attended a seminar on the future of digital media on TVs, PCs, and Cell Phones. The seminar dealt with how these and other digital delivery platforms might be used in combination to reach audiences for various purposes. The presenter slides are now available for this seminar on Digital Destiny: Combining the Three Screen Experience for Maximum Customer Engagement. I have also attached my notes from the seminar. The idea of working across multiple digital platforms to have an impact on an audience has important implications for both the library and the college. How might we utilize these platforms and the interactions increasingly possible among them in a strategy to deliver powerful educational experiences?
