Screens Aren’t The Only Classroom Distraction

Brian.Sweeting

May 28, 2014


US maps, seasonal calendars, school rules, motivational posters… classroom décor has its purposes, but overdoing it can be a distraction. A study from Carnegie Mellon found that too much flair was over-stimulating and impaired learning for elementary school students. If teachers find themselves losing students’ eyes, they should keep in mind that phone screens might not be the only attention grabbers in class.
