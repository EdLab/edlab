Banned Books Week 2013

Clare O'Dowd

September 23, 2013

September 22-28 is Banned Books Week! If you're looking to celebrate by reading a banned or challenged book, you can check out 2012's top ten challenged books . You can also check out Banned Books Week's list of Banned Books that Shaped America. In recent news on this front, the school board of Randolph County, NC recently banned Ralph's Ellison's Invisible Man, which won the 1953 National Book Award for fiction and features regularly on lists of best English language novels. If you're looking for something to do in the city, you can go visit the Bookstore Cafe on September 30th at 7pm for Paradise Banned: Poetry Readings from Banned Books with Timothy Donnelly, Deborah Landau, and More Have a favorite banned book?
