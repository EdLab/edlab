Competition Ideas - A Better Coffee Cup

gnatriello

May 22, 2010

Betacup from the betacup on Vimeo.

We seem to have a continuing interest in holding competitions to spur innovation and creativity. A few years ago we sponsored a national competition for web video by K-12 students when we launched AfterEd. More recently, we have been developing plans for a business plan competition to encourage innovation in the education sector. And we recently awarded the first Strage Prize for faculty work that might be best represented in a web video. Throughout all of our efforts to develop these innovation competitions, it seems that we have never quite developed a format that we felt represented the EdLab and that itself made use of innovative processes. With these experiences in mind it is interesting to read this account of efforts to develop the competition for a better coffee cup.
