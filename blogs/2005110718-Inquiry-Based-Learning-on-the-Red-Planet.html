Inquiry-Based Learning on the Red Planet

AJ

November 7, 2005

Ever think about colonizing Mars? Yeah, I thought so. I call for yet another exploratory committee! My former high school was featured in media outlets for creating a competition challenging eighth graders... to think about something that NASA doesn't even consider in their pipeline. Two-hundred eighth graders entered the interdisciplinary contest, and completed a rigorous case study on Mars. Participants were required to: (1) describe colonizing the planet and model population growth; (2) set up a tiered-government structure and social policies; (3) institute current science, as well as conceive future technology to survive the inclement conditions, and finally; (4) create a fiscally-plausible timetable of events. Talk about interdisciplinary approaches! Linked is a 30-second clip highlighting the event.
