Is MindMixer the Discussion Board of the Future?

Kate Meersschaert

July 9, 2013

As this InformationWeek.com article explores, an urban planning professor recently embedded a "town hall" discussion board platform into her MOOC on Coursera (with a bit of code integration from Coursera). The chosen platform, MindMixer, has been traditionally used by municipalities to engage citizens around planning decisions, however, the application seems to have the following benefits for learning-focused discussions in online spaces: 1) Topics are open, but directed by facilitator (as the professor explains, this can be problematic) 2) The platform is highly social 3) Multimedia can be easily included and accessed Takeaways... I am excited to explore this platform further in the context of mSchool and EdLab inititiatives that might integrate a discussion board.
