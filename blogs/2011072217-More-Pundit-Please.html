More Pundit Please!

Kate Meersschaert

July 22, 2011

(Private!) Ok, I don't want you to get Pundit fatigue... but, could you put on your creative caps and help me brainstorm Pundit tag lines? This Tagline will immediately proceed the name... for example: Pundit. Your Personal Course Consultant. Here are some use case scenarios for the tool to consider when brainstorming: A. I overwhelmed by the current TC Course Catalogue. Why can't I just find things that I care about (e.g., by keyword, title, instructor, etc) B. There are too many darn courses at TC. I have no idea what I should take. I wish there was something like Netflix to recommend courses that might interest me. C. I don't like all the courses at TC. I wish they offered_________. Current ideas: Your Personal Course Shopper. Your Virtual Course Consultant. Courses Customized. Custom Course Selection. Smarter Course Selection. Courses Curated. Course Search Simplified. Course Search and Recommendation for Real Life. Intelligent Course Selection. Take Course Search to the Next Level. Discover the Course of your Dreams. Take the #$%^&! out of Course Selection. An Intern for Course Selection. Your Personal Course Picker has Arrived. Remember... we need to be engaging, fun and yet TC appropriate!
