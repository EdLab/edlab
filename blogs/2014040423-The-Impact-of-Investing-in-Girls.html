The Impact of Investing in Girls

Bismark Appiah

April 4, 2014

The significant impact that women bring to the community when they are educated is one of the reasons why under developed countries should allow girls to attend school. When women and girls are empowered through education, they can help the economy of their country. Additionally, empowering girls through education reduces the risk of early teenage pregnancy and most importantly, the high increase in rape cases and girls trafficking.
