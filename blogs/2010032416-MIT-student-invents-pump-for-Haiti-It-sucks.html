MIT student invents pump for Haiti: It sucks!

jvazquez

March 24, 2010

PhD candidate Danielle Zurovcik's invention is astounding in its mere simplicity. It acts in a similar way to the negative-pressure pumps found in hospitals by plunging out bacteria and waste from wounds while creating healthy blood flow, drastically reducing the recovery time for victims. But you won't find the expensive hospital price tag on this medical marvel... it only costs $3! Genius. Source: Popular Science
