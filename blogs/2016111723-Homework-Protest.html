Homework Protest

Derrick.Cordova

November 17, 2016

One thing most students, regardless of age, constantly complain about is homework. Like most things, we complain before eventually doing it. However, students and families in Spain are taking action about what they deem as too much homework. The Spanish Alliance of Parents' Associations has urged all parents across the country to observe a homework strike every weekend this month. A recent study by the World Health Organization found that 30% of Spanish 11-year-olds feel stressed by the amount of homework. This rate rises to 65% by age 15. The protest comes out of parental concerns that the homework load, often two or more hours a day, is a consequence of an old-fashioned system of learning in Spain's education system. Additionally, parents also complain that homework hours decrease family time.
