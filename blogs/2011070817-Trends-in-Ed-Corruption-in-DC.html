Trends in Ed: Corruption in DC

Fred Rossoff

July 8, 2011

Hot on the heels of the announcement that a significant amount of teachers and administrators in Atlanta had been caught “juking the stats” (as they say in The Wire) we hear that the U.S. Department of Education has joined a D.C. investigation into similar behavior in the D.C. school district. The whole thing was sparked by a U.S.A. Today investigation which found that in several flagship D.C. schools students were erasing wrong answers and switching to the correct answers in a statistically impossible fashion. It looks like the District has been fairly slow in investigating these irregularities, and may still be dragging its feet. Is there more to come? I bet!
