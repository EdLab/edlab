Poster Session 10:35am 04/11/2011

Ankit Ranka

April 11, 2011

After the great breakfast at a place in front of our hotel this morning, Jo and I headed towards our poster session (which we thought was on a Tuesday afternoon, surprise!). The poster session went really well and people were really interested in talking about the iPad reading apps and many people working in the field of iPad development and researchers trying to find ways to introduce iPads in learning programs showed their interest. We were also able to attract peoples attention to our poster due to innovative iPad format poster designed by Jo (an iposter!). Overall we had a great discussion with people who visited. And it made me think that this is the good time to venture into the ipad app development and research.
