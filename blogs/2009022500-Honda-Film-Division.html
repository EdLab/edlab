Honda Film Division?

HSC

February 25, 2009

Honda (yes, the Japanese car company) has launched a new site called “The Power of Dreams” to showcase advances and discoveries in the company though the "candid approach of the documentary film process." So far they have three shorts on the site: 1) Failure: The Secret to Success (8:19) 2) Kick out the Ladder (5:55) 3) Mobility 2088 (7:33) I am impressed by the approach Honda is taking to share what is going on the company (and in particular the third video which presents views on what transportation will look like 80 years from now). I know Stephen recently blogged about enhancing the EdLab entry on Wikipedia but I wonder if a parallel effort should be undertaken to create documentaries about the EdLab. Here are some titles that I think are worth expanding upon: 1) Collaborativity 2) N of 1 3) If You Aren't Failing, You Aren't Trying Hard Enough Anyone else have EdLab documentary ideas? Anyone interesting in trying to create one?
