@ArtsEdTechNYC and Music Notation

Brian.Sweeting

November 20, 2013

*Private* Yesterday Rebecca and I attended the @ArtsEdTechNYC Meetup at the Empire Hotel. This was a perfect opportunity to share our products (especially Vialogues and Research Broker) with new users. And because of the focus on arts, it drew a different crowd than the typical EdTech event. We spent some quality time talking to Sven AhlbÃ¤ck, a composer and folk musician from Sweden. He created Score Cleaner, an app that translates your recordings into musical notation. I'm looking forward to playing with this and pitching it for an EdLab Review. It's not so often that we come across educational music apps, and this seems like it has the potential not just to teach kids about reading and writing music, but to help them understand music theory and even cross-disciplinary concepts (especially in math and physics). Thanks for coming and helping with outreach, Rebecca! I like the way we played off each other in conversations. #YOLO
