Re-Cap of Meetup @ OMGPOP

Kate Meersschaert

November 16, 2012

Last-night Jo and I attended the InsideStartups.org Meetup at the OMGPOP offices. Featured startups included GetGlue.com, Boomset.com and Spotify.com. For more event information please visit this blog post I created in advance of tonight's affair. First, the space... While technically cool and full of the requisite technology and bright colors of a startup (OMGPOP is owned by Zynga.com), the space for demoing was cramped and unfortunate for an event of this size (see pic below). More than half of attendees had to stand... not fun after a super long day of work. However, the event organizers InsideStartups.org are always super gracious and accommodating regardless of the space! These events are definitely more about who you meet and what you learn, rather than where they are or what food and drinks they serve. Presenters While GetGlue.com and Boomset.com provided great overviews of their products, Spotify veered from a traditional demo and presented their development model. This stood-out as refreshingly unconventional (see live tweets below). Spotify dev teams work in "squads" and value self-starters and "natural leadership" that emerges when everyone communicates and feels empowered to make decisions that will benefit their project. #EdLabRepresents We chose this "#" to categorize and document our live tweets for outreach events.
