More Tech In The Classroom

Evans Frimpong

June 12, 2015

High schools across the nation are starting to bring more technology inside the classrooms. Students in more than 500 districts nationwide are learning with iPads or some sort of tablet. Many schools have placed textbooks on electronic device for an efficient and less physically daunting experience. A recent pilot program showed that students who used an Algebra 1 App outperformed their peers who used textbooks. It also provides low-income students with the opportunity to work with emerging technology.
