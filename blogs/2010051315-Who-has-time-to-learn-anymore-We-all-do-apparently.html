Who has time to learn anymore? We all do, apparently!

jvazquez

May 13, 2010

Learn skills fast and for free with these iPhone apps. This might be an interesting avenue for us to explore... rapid skill development via simple, easy-to-use (and understand) apps. Why would someone choose to download one of these apps instead of visiting one of the many sites on the subject? For one thing, I think the iPhone/iPad lends itself beautifully to visual examples. And, more obviously, these are all mobile. If I'm in a pinch and I need to get a tie tied but the closest I ever get is a mess of knots resembling a Gehry exhibition, I can rest assured knowing there is an app out there that'll teach me exactly what I need to know in less than 10 minutes. I appreciate that kind of efficiency!
