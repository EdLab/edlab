Prep for Next Week's D&R Meeting: EdLab Cross-Pollination

Kate Meersschaert

January 6, 2012

Private! Hi Team D&R! In anticipation of our D&R meeting this coming Tuesday, I wanted to enlist your thoughts and invite you to suggest synergies/collaborations/connections that could be developed between our existent and upcoming tools and projects for 2012. My hope is that we will have a chance to brainstorm these possibilities together in the context of capitalizing on the assets of the lab for promotion, marketing and to help explore cultivating an EdLab "umbrella" over all of our tools and platforms. I currently have the following as possible connections to debate together and would love your thoughts and additional ideas to cover in this exploratory idea session! The VOICE + Vialogues = Consistent EdLab video experience Pundit + mSchool = Should we include mSchool courses on Pundit? Vialogues + TC Record = Should we consider embedding vialogues on TCR for relevant content? Research Broker + TCR = Would it be beneficial to advertise RB where we usually solicit research? Pocket Knowledge + Ednode = Could users of PK share their pockets on Ednode? I look forward to exploring these "synergies" more fully together on Tuesday and thank you for your thoughts!
