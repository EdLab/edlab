How Do We Keep Educators Engaged?

Nantwi

August 2, 2013

According to a recent Gallup poll, our nation's K-12 educators lose engagement almost half a year into their roles. The poll, conducted last year, included 7,256 K-12 teachers. The poll categorized the educators as engaged (deeply involved in and enthusiastic about their work and actively contributing to their organization), not engaged (satisfied with their workplaces, but ... not emotionally connected to them), or actively disengaged. The news is not all bad for K-12 teachers as they tend to do well overall compared to other professions (see charts below). What activities or resources should be included in early professional development for educators to ensure they stay engaged for the duration of their first year and beyond?
