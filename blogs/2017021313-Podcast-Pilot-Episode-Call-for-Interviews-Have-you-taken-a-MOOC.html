Podcast Pilot Episode Call for Interviews: Have you taken a MOOC?

saraehardman

February 13, 2017

Exciting times lie ahead, fellow EdLabbers, for the publishing team is in the midst of prototyping a pilot episode for a new experiment in podcasting. And we might be interested in interviewing you. Our first episode will be on the much-discussed, highly-contested, fast and furious subject of MOOCs. We want to interview both people who have had unsuccessful, negative experiences and have dropped a course (roughly 98% of people who start a MOOC) and people who have had successful, positive experiences and completed one (because we know they're out there). We want to put you on air to ask how you chose the course, what platform you used, whether you had any interaction with other students, and what contributed to your success or failure. If you fall into one of the above categories, please leave a comment! We want to tell your story. Sincerely, The creators of the future This EdLab Life* *Not the actual name of the podcast; reflects only the author's creativity-lacking brainstorming session and overlistening to This American Life.
