Land Art in Support of Women Rights

abagigah92

January 6, 2013

A massive land art project by artist Jorge Rodriguez-Gerada in support of women's rights in Central America serves as a new innovative model in which an idea or movement can reach the public. Land arts have been very good as it continuous to market ideas to people in order to help generate interest or highlight a global issue. Spanning the length of two football fields, the portrait is the largest ever created in the Netherlands. The portrait was the face of an anonymous Central American activist that was made out of sand and soil. The piece was launched as a campaign on the issue of violence against women in Mexico and Central America. As the EdLab readies to launch several promotional efforts and initiatives to launch its newest New Learning Times (NLT), what are some ways in which we can think “outside the box” to come up with innovative and striking promotional campaigns?
