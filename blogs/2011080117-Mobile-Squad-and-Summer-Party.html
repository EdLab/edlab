Mobile Squad & Summer Party

bhughes

August 1, 2011

(keep private: EdLab eyes only!) Please join me for the first annual EdLab Mobile Squad this Thursday at 4pm (wearing your EdLab shirt)! We're going to take to the streets to learn more about people's mobile learning habits. Um . . . if you have any ideas about what to ask people, lets make a protocol together that we can distribute. I'm thinking we'll be in pairs or triads (Greenpeace-style) with 2-4 questions for each innocent passer-by. How does that sound? Let's aim to learn cool and unexpected stuff! Or at least confirm or suspicions. After an hour of that, we'll gather in the basement of the Amsterdam Cafe with EVERYONE in EdLab (5pm). And we'll raise a toast to our summer work and progress, and offer unsolicited advice to summer interns who will soon be leaving us.
