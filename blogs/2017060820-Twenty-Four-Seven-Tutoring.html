Twenty-Four Seven Tutoring

csd2126

June 8, 2017


While teachers may have a firm grip on students’ learning inside the classroom, Gradeslam offers a one-to-one chat-based tutoring platform to provide teachers with insights on how their students learn outside of class too.

The "Slam" Suite

Gradeslam is a suite of tools for schools, students, and teachers that aims to support students’ independent learning. With this platform, all learners can seek help through instant messaging a team of certified tutors. Gradeslam tutors provide students with contextualized feedback on where they have gone wrong and how to avoid the same mistakes in the future.

Teacher and Student Support

Gradeslam encourages students to seek help by contacting their team of educators who are available around the clock to provide feedback and guidance. While the platform emphasizes chat-based tutoring, there is also an extensive suite of tools for students. Student-focused resources include: an archive of previous learning sessions, subject-related answers to common questions, an online classroom where students can live chat with tutors, a digital whiteboard where tutors can directly comment on student work, and SMS tutoring support.

Features of the platform targeted at teachers provide different slices of information on student use of the system from learning trends to transcripts of student conversations. While these insights could help educators change in-class instruction, student privacy does not appear to be a priority as teachers can see students’ session archives. Finally, administrators have a unique data dashboard, which provides insights on school-wide trends and student successes. Gradeslam is an edtech approach to tutoring that distributes the cost and benefits of individualized learning across all students.
