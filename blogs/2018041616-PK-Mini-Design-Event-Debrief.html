PK Mini-Design Event Debrief

Cinthia.Fabian

April 16, 2018

Thank you again to everyone who participated in today's design event! Joann, Sabarish, and I really appreciate you bringing your virtualized, explorable, discoverable, and PocketChanged ideas to the table ;) 


As we continue to rethink our current archival system, we thought it might be helpful to share with everyone some of the resources that we used to plan today's event as well as a few key takeaways from today's ideation activities. 


Let us know if you have any thoughts or feedback — on Phil the Pocket, the future of archiving, or our presentation — in the comments below!
