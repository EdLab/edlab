Fantasy Smartphone

Daniel Vergara

March 18, 2013

I came across this article that talks about people's fantasy smartphone, and I thought it was interesting because I never really thought about my fantasy smartphone would look like. Of course, one of the main features would be long lasting battery power. I feel that it is very essential to have a phone that is able to go through most of the day without being charged even after frequent use. However, it would be very amazing if there was a phone with a holographic screen where you type, search things up and do pretty much all the same functions but in a better way. Of course it's something that is only seen in movies for now but one could imagine all the things that could be done in the future. What would your fantasy smartphone look like?
