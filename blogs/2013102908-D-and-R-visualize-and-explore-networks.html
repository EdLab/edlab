D & R - visualize & explore networks

Sharon Hsiao

October 29, 2013

Exploratory data analysis: Basic concepts of Networks, Graphs, and Social Network Analysis Activity: 1. Warm up - make use of Gephi to visualize your facebook network

  • download and install Gephi from here https://gephi.org/users/download/
  • download your facebook data http://snacourse.com/getnet
  • Layout: ForceAtlas2, Yifan Hu, Fruchterman Reingold
  • Statistics: Average degree, Modularity
  • Partition&Ranking: refresh
2. Explore Vialogues & NLT campaign data by network visualizations Please download one of the attachments from this blog post. 1. Create a new graph in your Gephi workspace. 2. Go to "Data Laboratory" tab page, import the csv file as graph "edge" tables. 3. Return to "Overview" tab page. You may begin with exploration. Try some layouts, run some statistics (degree centrality), observe sub-communities... What do you find from the exploratory analysis? How many sub-communities? What's the degree centality & what does it mean? 4. Go to "Preview" tab page, output one network image and share with us. Discussion: What network visualizations YOU think that might be interesting to our users? Please share in replying this blog.
