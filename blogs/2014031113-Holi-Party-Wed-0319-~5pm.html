Holi Party - Wed (03/19) ~5pm

pramnab1

March 11, 2014

Hey guys.. Its this time of the year when Holi [Festival of colors] is round the corner (ofificially on 17th March). In lieu we are organizing our own annual holi play next week on Wednesday (March 19th) after work ~5pm. Just a guideline please do not wear your best clothes if you want to play. If feasible just to avoid stares bring an extra pair of clothes in case you want to change. We'll have colors for everyone, sweets and food along with bhangra/bollywood music to transcend us into the mood. Event Details: Holi 2014..Color me! Time: 5 pm, March 19 Venue: Sakura Park (two blocks from EdLab so no cleaning hassles). I am also posting Ankit (an ex-colleague) post of pics what Holi is. http://edlab.tc.columbia.edu/index.php?q=node/3943 See you all there.
