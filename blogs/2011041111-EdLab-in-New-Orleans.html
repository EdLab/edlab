EdLab in New Orleans

Nantwi

April 11, 2011

The rest of the EdLab contingent arrived in New Orleans without incident yesterday morning. Since we were unable to check into our hotel until 4pm, we immediately went over to the Sheraton for a poster session. Most of the posters focused on learning opportunities in math and science though a few focused on social networking opportunities as well. From there, we headed to Bourbon Street to look for a place to eat. Most places lacked more than one vegetarian option and so our search led us to La Bayou where there were a few more vegetarian options. A full lunch wasn't enough for all of us so we went to CafÃ© Du Monde for desserts. It got a little messy but we were able to cope and spend the next 30 minutes touring the French Quarter with the jazz festival and a silver clad entertainer been the major highlights. Pranav purchased trolley tickets for all of us so we can get a better sense of New Orleans. The hour drive through New Orleans showed us the other parts of this beautiful city. We were finally able to check in to our hotel and then headed over to the TC Reception (where again there were a lack of vegetarian options). Pranav, Ankit and I ended the night at an Indian Restaurant a few blocks from our hotel.
