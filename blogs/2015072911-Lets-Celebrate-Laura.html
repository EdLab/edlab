Let's Celebrate Laura!

Dana Haugh

July 29, 2015

I don't know if you heard, but Ms. Laura Costello, Head of Materials, Library Hero, Face of EdLab, is ending her 5 year reign at Teachers College in pursuit of bigger and better things! And though she won't be too far away (THANK GOD), her presence here on the Materials team, and all of EdLab and the library, will be GREATLY missed. (PLEASE DON'T GO LAURA) In true EdLab fashion, we need to bid her a farewell worthy of the great legacy she will leave behind. And what better way to send her off than to celebrate an early happy hour at THE HEIGHTS! This lovely establishment is located on 111th and Broadway and features a rooftop deck, mediocre appetizers, heavy-handed margaritas, and a happy hour that starts at 1pm (I think). So let's wipe our tears away and send Laura off in style! Details as follows: WHERE: The Heights WHEN: Friday, July 31st TIME: 1pm See you there!
