Fear Not...Google Compose!

Kate Meersschaert

March 29, 2013

I was a bit skeptical when I received the pop-up from Google re: their new streamlined "Compose" experience for well, composing emails. However, despite the initial fear that accompanies changes to a well-worn tech process, I can whole-heartedly endorse this new "experience." Here is my informal review: "Compose" pop-out!: No longer takes-up the whole inbox space & instead is a "pop-out" window - particularly great, if like me, you are managing about a dozen tasks at once! Additional options... like "insert photo" Emoticon Heaven!Building on the "addiitonal options" icons... one is for Emoji/emticons and it leads to a MOTHERLODE! Creative, personalized "emo" are now a click away! Thanks Google (seriously... really... I will try to keep my use of these little gems to a minimum at work)! Overall: I really appreciate the subtle streamlining and greater "visibility" & context-reinforcement of the new pop-out email "compose" window! The lack of extraneous visible details as you write a message might greatly cut-down on common email missteps like mistaken "reply all" etc. - what are your thoughts?
