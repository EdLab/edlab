Making Intelligent games

pramnab1

June 15, 2010

If the question presented to the student is answered incorrectly then the game should be intuitive enough to give another question with a smaller degree of hardness if the question is hard or harder question if it was correctly answered. Also the different answers should be evaluated (to disambiguate whether the word entered by the student is incorrect or correct). say for e.g. if the student says hard work (arduous, etc) are all correct.
