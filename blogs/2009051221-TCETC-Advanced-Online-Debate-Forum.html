TCETC: Advanced Online Debate Forum

chingfu

May 12, 2009

Zhou, Jessica and I presented one of our current Development & Research projects, Advanced Online Discussion Forum, in TCETC last Sunday. Due to time constrain, regrettably, we did not get feedback from our audience. Since we would like to develop this online tool in the future, it would be very helpful to have your comments/critique/feedback on our current design. Below is our prototype and paper abstract. You can also view our presentation here . Abstract: Deliberation on public issues is an important aspect of democratic participation. Computer-mediated communication tools provide new opportunities and present new challenges for such essential discussion. This paper describes an advanced online deliberative forum that aims to ensure an equal and inclusive participation, facilitate co-construction of thought, enhance critical thinking, and promote self reflection in online discussions.
