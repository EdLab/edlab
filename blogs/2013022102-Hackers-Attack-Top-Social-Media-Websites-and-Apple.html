Hackers Attack Top Social Media Websites and Apple

Oumar Soumahoro

February 21, 2013

Hacking has become such a serious issue in modern times that the government are looking for new ways to eradicate it though doing so has forced hackers to be more creative and find new ways to hack into systems. This article from CNN sheds light on a recent hacking event encountered by Apple, Facebook, Twitter and other websites. The incident happened after employees of the aforementioned companies visited a developer website. The responsible hackers belong to an Eastern European gang of hackers that steals important information from giant companiess and sell on the underground market. The tactics, dubbed the "water hole" attack is similar to a lion waiting on speedy gazelles to slow down and have a drink. The hackers load viruses on sites they suspect attractive targets will visit and then wait without having a specific victim. iPhone Dev SDK, supposedly a forum for developers who work with Apple's mobile operating system, was one of the sites where the attack appeared.
