EdModo Con 2014

Kate Meersschaert

July 28, 2014

Join the outreach team for an experiment in "virtual" conferencing... EdModo Con 2014! The conference in described as follows on the event splash page:

EdmodoCon is a live, online event that brings together educators from around the world for a full day of professional development, where teachers share, inspire, and showcase the ways they use Edmodo and other digital tools in the classroom.
I have registered an EdLab "viewing party" for the 6th (please see me for more details!) so, if you are interested in joining is for a portion of the virtual event please let me know in the comments below. In the past, the EdLab team had found value in reaching-out to educators who are a part of EdModo's many communities of practice and this event should be a great way to both share our work and learn from the community! Also, it will be nice to test this format for future EdLab initiatives. Please explore the full conference schedule here.
