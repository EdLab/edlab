Trends in Ed, 4.20.10: DuckDuckGo & Google Bookmarks

Ting Yuan

April 20, 2010

Duck Duck Go, an alternative to Google Search? Duck Duck Go is a search engine which recognizes multiple meanings of words and helps to clarify the search. So, instead of Google's general search, Duck Duck Go helps to refine your search by providing brief informational summaries/categories related to the searched topics. The following is an example of searching "ducks". Duck Duck Go can be a great tool to use with younger students who might be overwhelmed by the huge search results from Google and might need help refining their internet search terms. Google Bookmarks Google Bookmarks seems like an easy and quick way to share information with groups of people. Similar to Google reader, you can make your resources public or shared with selected individuals. It might be a great tool not only for workplace but also for schools. Students can use Google Bookmarks for their collaborative class project together with Google Docs and Photos across their school year. The following is Google's intro video for Google Bookmarks:
