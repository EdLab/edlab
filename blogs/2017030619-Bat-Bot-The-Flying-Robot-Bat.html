Bat Bot: The Flying Robot Bat

csd2126

March 6, 2017


Researchers created a new robot called the Bat Bot to replicate the agility of bats as they fly. This robot is a fully autonomous, lightweight, flying robot. To replicate the wings of real bats, scientists focused on recreating the major joints bats use in flight. Covering these joints on Bat Bot is an ultra-thin membrane that is reshaped as the robot moves its legs.

The design of Bat Bot allows it to fly straight, dive, and turn much like a real bat. Learning to mimic its design may help scientists better understand how real bats change the surface of their wings to control how they fly.
