Vialogues OTE Workshop Recap

Kate Meersschaert

January 20, 2015

****Private! Today Jo and I presented a workshop in partnership with the Office for Teacher Education (OTE). The focus of the workshop was to get pre-service teachers excited about using Vialogues in their classrooms and for professional development. Here is our workshop presentation with a minute-by-minute flow of events and activities. Also, here, here & here are the handouts we shared at the beginning and mid-point of the workshop. We structured the workshop in the following way: 1) Lunch/Intros/Icebreaker 2) Intro Vialogues functionality 3) Vialogues use cases 4) Individual activity: Create a vialogue 5) Group discussion 6) Formative, Summative & Self-assessment use cases 7) Workshop ELA Lesson Plan 8) Group discussion 9) Last Q&A 10) Julia Yu introduction to EdTPA contest 11) Last comments & Thank you! Overall the event ran smoothly with 8 people in attendance. The group seemed genuinely excited to use the tool both in their teaching and pre-service teacher training. Jo even received a request for a digital version of the curriculum we shared! Speaking of follow-up... I will create a follow-up survey for the group that will be sent out soon. I am excited for more opportunities to workshop our workshop model! Special thanks to Jo for co-leading this effort and to Mason and Carmen for acting as "plants!"
