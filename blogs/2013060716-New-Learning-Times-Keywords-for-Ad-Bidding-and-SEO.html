New Learning Times Keywords for Ad Bidding & SEO

Kate Meersschaert

June 7, 2013

Private!!! Hi Everyone! As an appetizer to my upcoming D&R (June 18th) I thought I would share my progress creating a database of keywords for both ad bidding and SEO for New Learning Times. (See Hui Soo's recent post for more on SEO Optimization!) The Challenge... To emasse a database of keywords to use for both 1. Google, Facebook, LinkedIn & Twitter (when applicable in these cases) ads 2. NLT SEO and eventually to modify for content specific metakeywords for NLT articles and images. The process... 1) I used the Google AdWords Keyword Tool to generate lists of relevant terms based on searches like: Adaptive Learning Personalized Learning STEM Education Learning Innovation & many more... 2) I then filtered the results based on the 1. # of global searches (over 1K monthly - avg. 200K w/ those over 100M highlighted in green on spreadsheet) 2. competitiveness re: other bids (low-mid for first "ad bidding" list and high level of competition for SEO). 3) I then separated the results based on Ad Bidding & SEO (Note: NLT's current metakeywords are listed on the third sheet). End Goals: In the D&R we will collectively share our suggestions for the best keywords and discover which will be most effective to support the multiple goals of our work. (Both Ads, SEO & Metatags.) In the end, in addition to serving our ad & SEO needs, I hope the results of this study will inform the long-term additional goal of creating a database of metatags & keywords for each NLT content stream. Authors can then add these to each of their articles to increase discoverability and relevance. Your Turn! I look-forward to sharing more re: my initial exploration & findings in the D&R! In the meantime please check-out the growing database and feel free to add your suggested keywords to the last sheet entitled "! (Remember: this list is broad for now and will be edited-down!)
