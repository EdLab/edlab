Describing the EdLab (with Wordle) for the Open House

Stephanie Hunt

December 5, 2008

The design team is furiously cranking out wordle-based print material for the December 17 EdLab Open House. One of the things we're working on is a set of large posters that we will print and hang above each workgroup in the EdLab. These posters will help to orient visitors to the space and the various things we all work on here. The posters will contain clusters of words (see the design team's example below) that convey a sense of the group's focus to people who may very well be total strangers to the EdLab. I've included a few ideas after the break -- what words would you add/subtract to successfully communicate the work you do to Open House visitors?
