Everything is Miscellaneous by David Weinberger

Leanora Lange

May 15, 2012


If you want to know what we're learning in core classes at library science school these days, just take a look at this video. I won't say that it covers everything I learned in class is in the video, but I will say that everything in the video is something I learned in class. While Weinberger's thesis in this video may seem a little self-evident to us in 2012, let's remember it's been five years since he said this. If you're interested in what he's been up to since, check out his new book Too Big to Know (2012) on its blog.
