A Recent Study of 39 University Mobile Websites

Ting Yuan

July 29, 2010

Since MIT and Stanford launched their mobile sites in 2008, increasing higher edu institutions have been trying out a mobile version of university/library websites. A recent study of higher ed mobile sites was found on EDUCAUSE Quarterly. It examines the functions and services provided by 39 mobile websites of large research universities and their libraries in the United States and Canada. Results found that few functions on university mobile websites clearly addressed educational needs, highlighting an opportunity to provide more educational links and applications.
