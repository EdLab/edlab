50,000 plays!

bhughes

July 7, 2008

After Ed turned one year old today, crossing the 50,000 play mark during the past week... ending the year with exactly 51,283 plays. That's exciting stuff! Congrats to everyone who's put in a HUGE effort this year to make incredible content. It's great to have an archive — it's one of the most important assets for a web video channel to have. It gives us flexibility, and a deep playlist to showcase when we experiment with new site designs. We've also built an archive with great breadth — videos that explore many different types of content. This will also serve us well in the coming year, as we can experiment serving up videos to different audiences. This project will continue to bring visibility to the lab, the library, and the college. Let's keep going! (Can we get the next 50,000 plays in 3 months?) Ideas for us?
