Social Media, This Is Your Life For all you've ever wanted to know about the history of social media, check out this post. It details everything from the Bulletin Board Systems (BBSs) of the late 70s to the mess of tools we have today. It's interesting to see the evolving role that this media has played in our lives over the years. It has moved from being a secondary sitcom character to having its own spin off… and it looks like its Neilson ratings are through the roof! Sorry, I got carried away. The point is, in looking at the spectrum of tools that have existed and currently do exist, it gives pause to think about… what's next? What characteristics will new social media tools build upon?
