mSchool Inspiration... Versal

Kate Meersschaert

July 10, 2013

While searching the interwebs for High Five leadsa for New Learning Times I came-across an interesting interactive, online and crowd-sourced learning platform, Versal. Read the Mashable.com article on the platform here. Takeaways... 1) Simple, intuitive UI/site design 2) Crowd-sourced online learning is a fresh idea 3) No programming necessary, creators can access insertable widgets and other plug and play elements 4) "Think, Create & Share" seem like clear action items for an online course creation experience... again, very simple/straightforward call to action. What are your thoughts? Anything to learn from/apply to our own research into the future of online learning and mSchool?
