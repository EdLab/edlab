You Won't Believe This Amazing Secret To Viral Content

Brian.Sweeting

January 16, 2014

*Private* Building on yesterday's post-seminar conversation of Plutchik's wheel of emotions, is this FastCo.Design post on how audiences consume content that triggers emotional responses.

Part of what makes emotional content so susceptible to spreading is that emotions themselves are contagious. Researchers have long known that people can "catch" the emotions of someone around them, so to speak, through direct exposure to that person's expressions and tones and gestures. They also believe this process of emotional contagion can occur indirectly--say, by receiving a forwarded video clip or article.
We've had discussions before about guidelines for creating compelling titles for NLT. Do you think there are tactful ways to appeal to readers' emotions without resorting to schticky Buzzfeed formulas? Can you think of any particular NLT titles that made you want to read the article?
