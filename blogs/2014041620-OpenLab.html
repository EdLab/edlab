OpenLab

EdLab Studios

April 16, 2014


OpenLab is an online platform supporting teaching, learning, and intellectual and social life at the New York City College of Technology, or City Tech. Designed at and for City Tech by a team of faculty, students, and staff, OpenLab is a combined learning management system, social and professional network, news site, discussion forum, collaboration tool, and more. Through OpenLab, students and faculty can establish interdisciplinary connections, find collaborators or new interests, and converse around issues relating to the college. The name reflects the key principles: the "Lab" serves as a digital space to work together, in a potentially disconnected urban campus, and its "Open" source nature, along with the public-facing, blog-like sharing of courses, assignments, and projects, emphasizes the openness of the lab as a place for experimentation, innovation, and collaboration.
