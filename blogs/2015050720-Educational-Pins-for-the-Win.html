Educational Pins, for the Win?

Dana Haugh

May 7, 2015


Remember the days when teachers would pin creative ideas to the classroom corkboard, endeavoring to captivate and inspire young minds? Well they're still pinning, but the board has changed considerably. Teachers are ditching their buoyant bark tissues in favor of a more digital approach, relying on the popular social media site, Pinterest, to fulfill their creative appetite. And while this style of mind mapping is surely more organized (and eco-friendly), one has to wonder if the site is actually helping educators better their lessons or just distracting them with endless should-would-could ideas.
