Startup Spotlight: Qeyno Labs

Janice Joo

June 12, 2012

The Details and Back-story Qeyno Labs, cofounded by Kalimah Priforce, is a social learning platform for kids (aged 13 to 17 years old) that helps "make career day an everyday experience for kids." Currently, this is done through their video content channel that showcases people from multiple professions; however, Qeyno is creating a network of games and awards, which is next to launch. Unique Point of Difference Qeyno focuses on mentorship through video content (and eventually gaming content). Each content stream is its own project, responding to a specific need in the landscape of professions. For example, Stem4Girls is intended to awaken the potential that young girls possess in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics careers. It was here that I found out that the co-founder of FoodSpotting.com is an Asian woman, Alexa Andrzejewski!
