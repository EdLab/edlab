Survey Design: Elements of Both Content and Format

Ting Yuan

February 2, 2010

I happened to see the following teacher survey sheet at the Office of Accreditation and Assessment at Teachers College, and thought it was a good example of format design for survey questions. With over 100 questions included, this annual teacher exit survey (on one double-sided sheet) was designed in a very succinct format, which as introduced received a return rate of over 50% in many programs (e.g., Curriculum & Teaching and Art Education). This also inspires me to think that in designing a survey, and considering survey questions, one can draft a well-crafted invitation letter, and also want to carefully design the visual format of the survey to attract more participants.
