Where Can Our EdTech Talk Podcasts Live?

Janice Joo

January 4, 2013

Does anyone know of any places we can house our EdTech Talks, which are 10-15 minute conversations with edupreneurs and changemakers in education. They are currently being housed on PocketKnowledge, but we would like to stream the music online, perhaps even garner attention through an existing social community of listeners. SoundCloud is the crowd contender, but its "Learning" section is far smaller than PodBean's "Education" section. But quantity is not always quality... it's hard to get a clear sense of the content from the PodBean interface... not very friendly for exploration. Regardless... I'd like to suggest a different platform called MixCloud, which has its own "Education" section too. It offers unlimited uploads for free, whereas SoundCloud has a 2hr playlist max. I am not a fan of PodBean for its ad clutter, some of the code even seems to be broken... whereas MixCloud's ads follow more standard conventions. Though MixCloud doesn't have the same cool interface as SoundCloud, it still looks friendly enough to use. Check it out below:

MIT Press Podcast- Take Back the Center by Mit on Mixcloud

If you have any other suggestions for housing podcasts, let us know in the comments!
