The Magic School Bus

Victoria Lebron

March 31, 2011

When I was a child, one of my favorite book series were "The Magic School Bus" books. In each book kooky Ms. Frizzle would take her students on one amazing adventure after another. I still remember how much I loved it when Ms. Frizzle shrank the entire class - including the bus - to a microscopic size and they were all swallowed by Arnold (The Magic School Bus Inside the Human Body). Oh, how I wanted to be a member of Ms. Frizzle's class! I knew that I would be up to any challenge she put in front of me. This week was a lesson planning week for me in a few classes. When I started to think about how I could infuse literacy into my math lesson I realized that I could absolutely go back to my favorite Ms. Frizzle. Since the books range a wide amount of topics, they can be infused into many different subjects, which is great. Click here if you are curious about checking out the libraries selection of Magic School Bus books. What was your favorite book series when you were a kid? I'd love to know!
