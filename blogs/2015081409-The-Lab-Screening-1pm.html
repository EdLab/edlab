The Lab Screening @ 1pm

Palmer Taylor

August 14, 2015

Good morning, EdLab! Today is my last day here with you guys and I can't thank you enough for letting me spend the summer with you, allowing me to get to know all of you, and tolerating me filming you at awkward and at times entirely inappropriate moments. However, I don't really want to become a part of the growing trend of 'goodbye' blog posts. So, instead of a mushy goodbye I was hoping to leave EdLab with some smiles and laughter. At 1pm today in the seminar space, the EdLab Studios team will be screening 'The Lab', my short mockumentary about the EdLab intern experience. I personally invite YOU (yes you) to attend. It features yours truly, as well as all the other interns, and many full-time EdLabbers as well. While it received 5 out of 5 Rubber Ducks on GarysFilmBlog.com, other nonspecific critics have called it 'The EdLab Summer 2015 Intern Video that will go down in history', 'an unflinching look into the realities of being an intern in this day and age and EdLab', and 'yeah it was okay but please don't quote this and put it in the promo blog post." Come see yourself, your coworkers, and your interns on the big screen!
