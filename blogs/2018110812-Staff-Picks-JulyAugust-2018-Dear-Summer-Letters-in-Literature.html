Staff Picks July/August 2018 - Dear Summer: Letters in Literature

JasmineSK

November 8, 2018

Epistolary literature is most commonly identified as works written in the form of letters but has been expanded to include literature that is told through the use of documents. This style of writing is most commonly used in fiction but there also non-fiction works that adopt this format.
