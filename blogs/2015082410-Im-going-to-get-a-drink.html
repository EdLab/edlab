I'm going to get a drink

Santosh Kumar

August 24, 2015

** Keep private ** This friday, is going to be my last day at Edlab and it's been quite a ride! Steal from the best they say -- in that vein, I say I'm going to get a drink. Stuff I feel extremely proud about the work I've had the privilege of being a part of at Edlab: - Mschool: I think the Mschool project is in a good place and I'm glad to see a great team that's moving it forward. Shout out to: Ling Xiao, Pranav, Ryan, Panisuan, Jo & HuiSoo. - PK: Neha is killing it on PK and doing the grunt work of upgrading PHP, making the code base more robust etc. - Dev Ops awesomeness: I'm super happy to see Vagrant creeping it's way in, to more of our repos and making going through giant README's a thing of the past. The pie in the sky should be, run one command and you have a complete instance of the app up and running. You focus on the software that adds business value and less on the trivial stuff of how to bring it up. And I think we are getting there for a lot of the apps that we support. I now find myself in a point in life where I would like to spend more time with my (extended) family and would like my daughter to have the childhood that I had, growing up in India. I also think India (and other developing countries) is at a unique point in history of having ridiculous numbers in terms of mobile Internet usage and that serving as a hot-bed for some amazing innovation. This I would like to be a part of, in whatever small way I can be. I thank you all for letting me be part of this family and wish you awesomeness in your work and away-from-work lives.
