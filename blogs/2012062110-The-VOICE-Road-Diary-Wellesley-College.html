The VOICE Road Diary: Wellesley College

Kate Meersschaert

June 21, 2012

Dinner last-night was at a great Indian spot in Cambridge called Tamarind Bay! No pubs for us... but thank you for the suggestion Michelle! We then turned-in early at our lodge - the mid-century modern-styled Wellesley College Club. After a bit of an adventure setting-up our studio space (see pic below) in the basement of the Wellesley College Library otherwise known as the Knapp Media Technology Center! Today we will conduct two rounds of interviews with Barbara Beatty. Learn more about her work here. We then head to UMass Amherst, which is about 2.5 hours away for another round of interviews! Delicious dinner at Tamarind Bay in Cambridge! Historic Wellesley... The "Club!" Breakfast... delivered by the Wellesley version of CulinArt!
