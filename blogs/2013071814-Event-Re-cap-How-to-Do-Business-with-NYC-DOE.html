Event Re-cap: How to Do Business with NYC DOE

Mable Yiu

July 18, 2013

This morning, Brian S. and I had the opportunity to attend the How to Do Business with the NYC Department of Education event at the Columbia Business School. Led by Tiwana Barnes from the Office of Equal Opportunity department of the NYC Department of Education (DOE), this workshop discussed ways in how you can market your small business to the DOE and how to become a vendor. One thing that Ms. Barnes kept stressing was how important it is for people to be comfortable talking to others about their work and networking, so at the end we got to meet other people at the event to learn more about companies aiming to help NYC schools.
