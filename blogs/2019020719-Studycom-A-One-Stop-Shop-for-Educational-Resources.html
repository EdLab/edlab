Study.com: A One-Stop Shop for Educational Resources

Porsha.Monroe

February 7, 2019


"Study.com’s company mission is to make education accessible through the use of short, instructor-led videos designed to help students master any subject and achieve their educational goals." (Study.com)

Making Studying Simpler

The founders, former California Polytechnic students, noticed a lack of affordable educational tools and formed study.com to fill this gap and address the rising costs of college. These video-based online courses, comprised of over 10,000 individual lessons, allow students to earn college credit and enable them to make informed decisions about their education and career goals. For students, the platform offers a wide breadth of subjects to explore with over 4,000 full courses. Lessons cover the topics of math, history, psychology, social science, and business, to name a few. Test prep and professional development provide users with options to pursue specific areas of interest as well. Each lesson also includes a transcript of the video narration in addition to on-screen space to take time-stamped notes. Students can also select lessons and add them to their own custom course.

Enhancing Classroom Learning

Study.com is also designed to meet the needs of educators. With it’s Google Classroom integration, teachers can grant students access to study.com lessons while tracking student usage and assessment results. Study.com also allows for sharing directly from the platform, and the interactive quiz function enables teachers to engage their classes through the use of tablets and other shared wi-fi enabled devices.

Study.com meets its goal of providing accessible educational resources for students. This platform can serve as a helpful introduction to online learning for students looking to further their educational pursuits.
