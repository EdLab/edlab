Portable Pod Libraries Can Make Any Time Story Time

Carmen James

August 14, 2014


The Uni is a portable, open-air reading room that began operating in the fall of 2011. Developed by the Uni Project, it is a place where people can sit and read, alone or with others, share books, and learn. Its portable, lightweight infrastructure is well adapted to an urban environment. It offers books and learning experiences. The Uni is also designed to be aesthetically appealing and so it has the dual purpose of improving public space.

The Uni Project is based in New York City, and since it began operation, sixty Unis have popped up across the five boroughs. Five Uniâ€™s have also been taken abroad with the support of the US Consulate and in 2013 the Uni Project was awarded an Innovations in Reading Prize from the National Book Foundation. The Uni Project is focused on underserved or emerging communities and has partnered with over 25 organizations and public libraries to expand its reach.
