WOT: Web of Trust

Arianna Choi

December 8, 2009

D'OH! Have you ever had an awesome idea, but then you quickly find that your awesome idea has already been realized by someone else - and done pretty well? Yesterday, I blogged about EdLab team's participation in Jetpack Learning Design Challenge. We're excited about entering the second phase of this competition which pledges to provide us with technical training and support needed to develop a Firefox web browser add-on. For those who want to see a chunk of our idea turned into a working add-on for slightly different purpose (but much larger group of users), check out Web of Trust. It's one of Firefox's most popular add-ons and there's been 7,604,574 downloads to date. This won't stop our Check Republic project. Just means that we need to continue to design 'n refine it better for our specific context and audience. And please remember that we're always open to any comments, suggestions, and critiques!
