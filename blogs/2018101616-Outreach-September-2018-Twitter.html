Outreach, September 2018 - Twitter

Cinthia.Fabian

October 16, 2018

INTRODUCTION 

Pop Quiz! Out of the four Twitter accounts managed by the experiences team, which one is the most popular — @EdLabTC, @TCRecord, @NLTTC, or @Vialogues? 


You’re right, the answer’s too obvious: No one gets hollered at more than the oh-so-riveting, century-old Teachers College Record with a total of 2088 followers as of September 2018. 


But what is it that makes TCR the reigning champ? And how do our other social media identities stack up? Join me as I take a deeper dive into the numbers behind the tweets.


OVERVIEW 

A cursory glance at the numbers has TCR leading in all respects. TCR has the largest presence on social media...


The largest number of eyes on it (Note: Impressions = numbers of people who might have seen a post)...


And, as you could guess, the largest number of mentions (people @ing us in the Twittersphere). 


Now that we have a sense of how large (or small) each account looms, let's dig into the actual impact of our posts. The following chart shows the total numbers of engagements of any type —  retweet, like, link clink, etc — by account.


Are our tweets getting people off their social media feeds and onto our apps? Here's a closer look at one particular type of engagement— link clicks.


INFLUENTIAL FOLLOWERS

Our most influential follower gained in September on three of our four accounts (EdLab, NLT, and Vialogues) is Jennifer Williams, the co-founder and lead program developer for Calliope Global, with a Twitter audience of 47.3K. 


Our most influential follower gained in September on TCR is Lisa Gurthie, facilitator at Piedmont Open IB Middle School in Charlotte, North Carolina, with a Twitter audience of 2,490.
