The Learning Technology Community Review Team Needs Your Help!

Demetri Lales

August 3, 2012

*Private Please* The team formerly known as ALT has added many educational technologies in the database but of course we are always looking for more to add to it. So now we are asking if anyone in the Lab, and beyond, would like to contribute to the Learning Technologies database. We may have come up with an easy way to do this. First find an interesting learning software. Second fill out this Super Short Survey(SSS). The answers in the survey will fill out the meta-data we would need for the database, info such as software name, subject, price, platform, etc. It would be great to see what other learning software are out there that maybe we don't have in our database yet. If you know anyone else who runs into learning softwares on the regular feel free to share this Super Short Survey! If you have any feedback or questions about the survey them let me know.
