EdSurge Instruct - Teachers Edition

Janet Jang

February 14, 2012

As most of you may already know, EdSurge is an email newsletter about all things EdTech startups and tools. This morning, they sent out their brand new teacher's edition of the newsletter - EdSurge Instruct. They use discussion topics via Twitter chats on #esinstruct to collect the themes and ideas needed for each subsequent issue. Last week, the discussion on Twitter centered around language learning and early language acquisition, so the theme of this week's newsletter is all about that. They're staying true to their word. In the midst of this EdTech boom, it's important to feature what educators are doing as well as what entrepreneurs are doing. Hopefully this will bridge the gap between teachers and developers, resulting in more effective tools for all.
