STEM Classroom Can Affect Learning?

rendy

January 30, 2014

Since it was proving pointless to continue pursuing their old methods, education leaders have changed their approach to STEM. This article sheds light on how leaders plan to resolve this. One idea is to make sure classroom layouts are compatible with the method of STEM teaching schools have adopted. For example, if the school has endorsed a collaborative learning, there should be desks and chairs that match this idea. Teachers should have the right equipment and the proper materials to teach the class. Having the proper materials is essential in learning and it makes things more practicable.
