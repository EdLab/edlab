More best practices for high school classrooms : what award-winning secondary teachers do

Victoria Lebron

December 15, 2010

Title: More best practices for high school classrooms : what award-winning secondary teachers do Author: Randi Stone Publisher: Thousand Oaks, Calif. : Corwin, c2010. Call No.: LB1777.2 .S76 2010 From the Publisher: Outstanding high school teachers share award-winning practices for managing classrooms and using technology, and for teaching science, mathematics, language arts, social studies, music, art, and physical education. "The variety of activities sparks ideas for different subjects. The book presents content in an easy-to-follow framework, provides very doable lessons, and clearly identifies the necessary materials." (Linda D.
