Bobo Explores Light

Demetri Lales

December 16, 2013


Bobo Explores Light is a fun interactive ebook iPad app that teaches young students some basic physics concepts. The app’s friendly robot mascot and bright colors make it appealing to children. The creators of the app intended to introduce young students to the concepts of light and inspire them to learn more about science.

Players are accompanied in game by a robot character called Bobo and they explore each concept as they move through pages. For each light concept, like reflection or refraction, the app gives users the ability to explore and interact with physics concepts to promote understanding. For example, users can play with lasers and mirrors to learn about reflection. Additional text and video information is also available on each page if students want to learn more.

Pros:

The visuals and animations on this app are smooth and of very high quality. Each page provides fun facts about the properties of light that are informative and entertaining for users. More information can be layered on the page by clicking Bobo’s antenna to learn more about a concept. There is a heavy emphasis on interaction and animations to capture the attention of younger students.

Cons:

The app goes over many properties of light but it does not explore each subject in depth. It could be interesting to add some interactive games or puzzles to each page to encourage self-assessment of newly gained knowledge. The play time in the app is short and may leave users wanting more.

Our Takeaway:

Bobo Explores Light is a fun way to introduce young students to some basic concepts of physics without overwhelming them; though if users wish to learn more deeply about the subject of light they will need to look elsewhere. The greatest strength of this app is in the presentation of hands-on experiences of different physics concepts. The app lets users experiment with light and experimentation is very important in STEM learning. Bobo Explores Light is an engaging yet appropriately short learning experience.

The Bottom Line:

Bobo Explores Light is a fun physics adventure that will light you up.
