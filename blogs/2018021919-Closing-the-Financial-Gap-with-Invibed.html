Closing the Financial Gap with Invibed

EdLabStudios

February 19, 2018


Millenials are at a particular disadvantage when it comes to financial literacy and security. Young adults ages 19 into their late 30s are making 20% less than their parents did at that age. Factor in student loans, credit card debt, the cost of living in major cities to better access the high concentration of jobs in urban areas, not to mention an overwhelming dearth of personal finance education in American schools and it’s easy to see that this generation is in dire need of some guidance. Founded by high school best friends, Dani Pascarell and Korrie Martinez Invibed is a fintech company and Wealth Coaching® Program that makes successful money management a reality for young professionals. In January of 2018, Dani Pascarella visited EdLab to outline the financial challenges facing millennials today and the unique ways in which Invibed can help.Watch the full seminar on Vialogues.
