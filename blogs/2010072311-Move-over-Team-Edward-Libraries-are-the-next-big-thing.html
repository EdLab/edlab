Move over Team Edward... Libraries are the next big thing

jvazquez

July 23, 2010

Making reference to the lab-approved Old Spice parody video, this NPR article details reasons why libraries are experiencing a vibrant resurgence in communities nationwide. I doubt the phrase, "Remember when libraries were cool?" will ever be uttered by anyone. Ever. Libraries aren't about being "cool" or popular... they are the knowledge centers of the world and librarians are the information architects that connect you to the information you need. Alright, so yeah... I guess that could be considered cool. But for the people who are worried that libraries will become a fad, tossed aside as soon as the next viral video sensation debuts,you shouldn't worry. Even as the excitement for these humble structures of knowledge and wisdom wanes, the people who have always loved libraries will remain. And even if libraries are left more broke than ever after their resources have been depleted by the hoards of excited users getting their fill, the library will replenish itself via its expansively growing digital collections. And really... it's about time we get our spotlight in pop culture! Nobody puts libraries in the corner!
