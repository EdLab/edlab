Business as usual (with a little help)

jvazquez

February 25, 2010

This past Friday, the EdLab Venture Competition group decided to get schooled in entrepreneurship at the New York City Business Solution's Upper Manhattan branch. The topic was “10 steps to starting your own business” and, if you are planning on starting a venture, it was definitely informative. NYC Business Solutions is a great resource for entrepreneurs, regardless of what stage they are in. They offer a wide variety of classes and workshops for the business-minded. The best part: they are free! Even their consulting services are free. I can see us going to more of their events in the future or providing them as a resource for our participants.
