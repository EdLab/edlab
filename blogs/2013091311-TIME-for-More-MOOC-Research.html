TIME for More MOOC Research

Brian.Sweeting

September 13, 2013

*Private* Yesterday Time published Jon Marcus' piece from the Hechinger Report MOOCs keep getting bigger. But do they work? under the title All Hail MOOCs! Just Don't Ask if They Actually Work about TC's call for more research into the effectiveness of online learning.

“At this point, there's just no way to really know whether they're effective or not,” said Shanna Jaggars, assistant director of the Community College Research Center at Columbia University's Teachers College, which has produced some of the most recent scholarship about online education.
