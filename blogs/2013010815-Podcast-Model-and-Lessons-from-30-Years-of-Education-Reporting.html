Podcast Model and "Lessons from 30 Years of Education Reporting"

Kate Meersschaert

January 8, 2013

Besides being a great podcast with interesting learning-focused takeaways from Jay Matthew's 30-year career as an education reporter, the podcast format might be interesting to explore for future EdTech Talks. I particularly like the "ad" space at the beginning of the recording. I believe this format and player is proprietary, as they have an app and fully integrated experience with no sign of a third party. What are your thoughts? Could we implement any of these features via Pressible? This continues a dialogue started by Janice in this post, please feel free to respond with additional free sites and services you use for podcasting!
