Cognitive Load & Presentations

Kate Meersschaert

January 21, 2015

Great round-up of research (Richard Mayer, Multimedia Learning Theory, 2001) and best presentation practices to reduce cognitive load for your audience. What are your thoughts re: these tips and tricks? Will you modify your personal presentation style as a result? As with MANY things, old habits are hard to break, but, I will try to integrate a few of these into my presentation game! Findings summarized & a great video included in the article:

  • Images without text are much better presentation support than text that mirrors content you are sharing verbally
  • Explain topics in your own words
  • Use "I" and "You" to personalize your presentation material
