NY Tech Meetup has Launched #StartupStories

Kate Meersschaert

January 22, 2013

We are apparently on the right track with our Ed-tech & "edupreneurship" focused EdLab #EdTechTakes & #EdTechTalks... NY EdTech Meetup has recently launched their own series of videos focused on (from their site):

NYTM is proud to introduce a new series called #StartupStories. This series, co-produced with NASDAQ OMX and HD Made, features the voices of members of the New York technology community telling their stories and giving insight into building a startup, all organized around key topics like pitching, failure, and building a team. Enjoy!
Although there are not yet any active videos featured, what do you think of their entry into creating resources to support entrepreneurship and share founder stories? Another favorite resource is Stanford'd e-Corner videos and podcasts. Any additional sources for entrepreneurship videos and audio podcasts that you have found? Any that are #EdTech focused?
