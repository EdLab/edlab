Education Content Providers Among America's Top Young Entrepreneurs

Frank Webster

September 18, 2008

Leading the story on this year's 25 nominees is a 22-year-old who: ...turned down a job offer from McKinsey and deferred admission to Harvard Business School to start New York's Academic Earth, an online hub for videos of university lectures and other educational content.
