Overall engagement with our content decreased across all of our Twitter accounts, likely because we tweeted less in November than we did in September (holidays!). For December our focus will be on returning to our normal tweeting rate (about 40ish tweets per month for @NLTTC and @TCRecord) to amp up our overall engagement.


The following chart describes the number of engagements per type (like, retweet, and/or URL click) per tweet.

While @TCRecord receives less likes per post than @NLTTC, our followers are more likely to be lured in by a link to TCR than a link to NLT.


Though we don’t tweet via our general account, @EdLabTC, very frequently, these posts tend to garner the most consistent engagement per post.
