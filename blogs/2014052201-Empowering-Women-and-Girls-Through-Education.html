Empowering Women and Girls Through Education

abagigah92

May 22, 2014

Education presents opportunities to grow and develop emotionally, socially and psychologically. For women and girls, it is very important. Throughout the world, women and girls are oppressed and one of the best ways to make a difference is through education. Investing in education for girls has many positive rewards such as reducing poverty rate. Empowering women through education gives them a voice and an opportunity to succeed in life. Girls that are educated are more likely to marry later and have smaller and healthier families. Girls with little or no education are likely to be domestically abused, poor or marry at an early age. Women that are educated recognize the importance of health and also gain much confidence in deciding the outcome of their future.
