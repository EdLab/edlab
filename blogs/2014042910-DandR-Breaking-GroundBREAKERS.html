D&R: Breaking GroundBREAKERS

Kate Meersschaert

April 29, 2014

Hi Team D&R! Today we are going to explore the EdLab GroundBREAKERS series (formerly known as "Edtech Talks/Takes")! Group Activity #1: Once we get to the group activity you will need to: -Search "#edtechtakes" on vialogues.com -Pick 3 GroundBREAKERS vialogues from the 6 pages of results to watch on your own -Then discuss the pros & cons of the vialogues you watched as a group -Pick three of your overall favorites to present to the wider group Group Activity #2: Finally you will address the following as a group in the 2nd half of the group work. As a group brainstorm: -A new name for the series -Three new questions for the team to consider -Decide the best place to promote/share these videos & podcasts? (Here is a current option on Pressible!) Please add your group's notes below!
