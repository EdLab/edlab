Free Online Business School Classes

Ting Yuan

December 5, 2008

A former Professor of Management at New York University (NYU Stern School of Management), is offering free on-line business classes to anyone who wants to learn. According to his page at Ning, each class is about three months. The class includes weekly videos and readings and there is no pre-requisite requirement. The courses will cover cases from many industries, as well as many ways to understand strategy including the work of Peter Drucker, Michael Porter, and approaches from McKinsey, Bain, Boston Consulting Group, General Electtric, The Balanced Scorecard, and a number of strategies for Innovation. I think, together with other open course opportunities around the world, it can be a great resource for the curriculum design of EdLab's graduate program in the near future.
