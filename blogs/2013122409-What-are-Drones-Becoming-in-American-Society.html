What are Drones Becoming in American Society?

Oumar Soumahoro

December 24, 2013

Drones are becoming a usual discussions in our society. They're being used in every possible way to fulfill humans needs. An example of a possible good use for drones is Amazon Prime Air, a service drone that will help Amazon's customers receive their package in half an hour time. however, as their grow in popularity, drones are seen to be a violation of American's privacy by certain people. While surfing the internet, I came across this video in which a part of the population of Deer Trail, Colorado see drones as a violation of their privacy, a kind of declaration of war from the federal government. Due to this fact, they engaged themselves in drones hunting. This however does not come with opposition as, another part of the population see as a federal attack, and an considerable surveillance from FAA.
