Knewton & Houghton Mifflin Harcourt to Partner and Provide Blended Learning to Millions

Kate Meersschaert

June 6, 2013

Knewton has announced a new partnership with publishing behemoth, Houghton Mifflin Harcourt (HMH). Knewton has developed an adaptive learning platform that creates personalize learning experiences that will be paired with HMH's more traditional print materials. Both parties hope the partnership will create new blended learning experiences for the millions of students in the HMH network. From VentureBeat:

As schools shift to new models of blended and online learning, startups like Knewton claim they will form the “foundation” for a new way of teaching. By teaming up with HMH and Pearson, Knewton can augment core curriculum materials that are widely used in schools.
