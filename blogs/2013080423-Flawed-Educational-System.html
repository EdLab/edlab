Flawed Educational System

abagigah92

August 4, 2013

In the article, Why College Isn't for Everyone, Karen W. Arenson discusses our nation's flawed educational system. Although I agree education is not for everyone, money should not be the reason why the government should allow many people to be uneducated. The number of people enrolled in college has risen from two million to 16 million, which is causing many economical problems for the government. Education should be available for all at a certain level such as high school but higher education should be earned not given for the sack of merit. Higher education should be provided for those who are simply qualified and the government should help the people who cannot afford because of income issues. The standard of education in society should be met by the government by improving K-12 education to make sure students are well prepared for higher education and beyond. If the government can concentrate on K-12 system and making it better, many people will go to college prepared and money will not be wasted for grooming a student.
