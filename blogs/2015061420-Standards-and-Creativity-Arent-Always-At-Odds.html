Standards and Creativity Aren't Always At Odds

Carmen James

June 14, 2015


For many educators who are unsure of how to evaluate countless digital tools, the edtech space is still the wild west. ISTE, the International Society for Technology in Education, has designed standards to assist educators implementing new technologies. Standards are designed for students, teachers, administrators, and coaches aiming to leverage technology tools to promote student learning and success.

The standards are a set of skills and knowledge needed for the "technology-powered world." As Caroline Sykora, the senior director of the ISTE Standards Program, says, the standards are "a compass" in a confusing world of technology options. They aid in understanding what skills and experiences educators can expect students to gain that will directly impact student learning.
