Event Recap: NY EdTech Meetup @ Knewton

Kate Meersschaert

July 16, 2014

Tweets by @CuriousBrooklyn Show Me the Growth! #NYEdTech Meetup @ Knewton HQ Last night we (me, Mason, Laura, Jackie & Yue - thank you all for joining me!!! ) ventured down to the latest #NYEdtech Meetup. Held at the @Knewton headquarters, the event featured a 4 presenting companies (ReadWorks, KDSI, ImagineEasy & Flocabulary) and offered the community a chance to ask presenters questions and network. Check-out my past blog posts re: the event here & here. Thoughts & Recap: Overall, the event was organized and facilitated well (as usual, thanks NYEdTech Meetup team!), with presenters demoing and talking for about 10 minutes followed by 2 audience questions. Then, at the end of the event, the organizers opened up the floor to more questions. This model seemed to work well, however, I would have shortened the demos and lengthened Q&A. Also, perhaps a panel table for presenters to see & be seen as they field questions would be great. I really liked that the presenting company's had an assortment of questions from the organizers that they HAD to answer. For example, "What are your next steps? What was your process for gaining initial users?" Etc. One of the most striking elements of the networking portion was the # of early stage company's that responded when I made a request for leads at the end... many were near the "end of their runway" (funding) and hope to be covered at GroundBREAKERS and EdLab Seminar participants.
