"Our mission is to inspire students to learn and explore knowledge in a collaborative community." (About Brainly)

An Online Community Helps You Through Hard Times

Have you ever studied alone, struggled with a homework assignment, and just couldn't answer it no matter how hard you tried? A new company aims to change that painfully familiar dilemma. Brainly is a student social learning network that allows for peer-problem solving during challenging homework episodes.

Sharing Homework Expertise Around the Clock

Brainly is a free question and answer platform that provides homework help for middle and high school students. Members post challenging homework problems, others suggest answers, and moderators verify responses to ensure quality. To access these vetted answers, users must first provide answers from their own areas of expertise (I’m good at physics, you’re good at history, let’s exchange). By answering other members’ questions, Brainly users earn points that allow them to ask their own. As a result, Brainly encourages everyone to play to their strengths and help one another within their domains of knowledge. The mentor-mentee relationship endlessly moves back and forth depending on who has a difficult question and who has a helpful response.

Crowdlearning Around the World

Brainly’s moderators are picked through trusted membership on the site. Over 30 million users across 35 countries teach and learn in 12 languages, providing answers in a jiffy. Brainly has global cred—its diverse team comes from 17 different countries and speaks 21 languages.
