Edlab @ Sloan C, ET4OL 2011, Day 2

Megha Agarwala

July 12, 2011

Day 2 started with presenting Vialogues in the morning. Vialogues got a great response & we had a standing room at the sesssion. It was also actively spoken about on twitter. A summary of the initial user responses from twitter & elsewhere: "this is a REALLY cool idea . . . this is going to help my classroom discussion" Very cool that @Vialogues can be embedded in Bb. A great way to make video interactive. "Absolutely! Keep out those crazy YouTube commenters!" "Vialogues can be embedded or mashed up in Bob or Moodle = WIN!" "I like the value proposition of @Vialogues" "Not only is vialogues interface polished, code is visible and commented. See also UX Survey. Sweet!: See for yourself"
People also greatly appreciated the info card with all the Edlab presentations & posters along with the QR code! In the evening, we had poster sessions & networking. Hui Soo, George & Ankit were in complete action with their posters for Automatic Author Feedback Tool, Ednode & DocDel respectively.
