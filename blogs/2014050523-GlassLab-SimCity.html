GlassLab: SimCity

Youssef Ballo

May 5, 2014

As I continue with my theme on educational video games and simulations, I came across GlassLab and some of its games and apps. GlassLab is a digital game that has learning environments and provides real-time assessments of student learning. It provides students and teachers with the tools to improve the process of learning and accelerate progress on the path toward careers. Most of the games on GlassLab are powerful, data rich learning environments that improve learning with formative assessments teachers can use. Additionally, it is creating original games using existing commercially successful games that are designed to support and enhance critical skills. SimCity is one of the more famous games from GlassLab. It is a tool and content that makes learning come alive for every student. In the game, students play the role of mayor doing the challenging work of addressing environmental effects while balancing the needs (e.g. employment, health) of his constituents. The game also provides formative assessment information about student's ability to solve problems, read texts and diagrams. SimCity delivers skills and feedback to students to help them improve.
