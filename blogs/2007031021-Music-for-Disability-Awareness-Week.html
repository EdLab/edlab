Music for Disability Awareness Week

Anthony Cocciolo

March 10, 2007

If you haven't heard about it yet, we are running special music during the opening and closing of the library for Disability Awareness week. The group running the disability awareness events gave us a CD with 14 tracks. I have added 2 additional tracks from my own collection (tracks 15 and 16). Track 15- Cher: The Academy Award winner is dyslexic Track 16- Britney Spears: What about temporary disabilities? According to E!, she has postpartum depression.
