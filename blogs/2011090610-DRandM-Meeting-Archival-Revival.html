DR&M Meeting: Archival Revival

LauraCostello

September 6, 2011

*Please note: the DR&M meeting has been rescheduled for next Tuesday, September 13th Rebecca Chovnik and I came up with a few ideas for an archival and materials blog: -Videos featuring archival materials and public domain music -Picture contrasts with other items from the collection or modern creative commons photos -Materials from the collection with found or created poetry 1) On your own or in groups, please review one or more of these Pocket Knowledge resources and generate potential post ideas for Archival Revival: - John K. Norton family correspondence - Rothman Lantern Slide Collection - The Millbank Memorial Library -An Almanac for the First Century - Ziegfeld Collection of International Children's Art You can post your ideas in writing on the blog or mock up a post on my Prezi. 2) Please take this short survey to help determine the focus of Archival Revival: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dE9DSHFMNm5iajY4d2NoMFUxSGdqbFE6MQ
