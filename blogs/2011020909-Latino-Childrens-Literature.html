Latino Children's Literature

Veronica Garza

February 9, 2011

Our TC library has many resources from which to begin exploring the world of Latino Children's Literature.  One of the books I used greatly as a teacher was A magical encounter: Latino children's literature in the classroom .  This resources provides many hands on activities that one can use for virtually any book one comes across, as well as a list of great children's books.

If you're starting to build your collection and need ideas, the Association for Library Service to Children website features a list of  Pura Belpre Award winners.  "The Pura Belpré Award... is presented to a Latino/Latina writer and illustrator whose work best portrays, affirms, and celebrates the Latino cultural experience in an outstanding work of literature for children and youth."
