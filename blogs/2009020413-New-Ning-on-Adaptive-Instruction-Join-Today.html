New Ning on Adaptive Instruction — Join Today!

bhughes

February 4, 2009

Some of you may know I have a secret (or not so secret) affinity for Nings. Well, I've just created a Ning as part of EdLab's involvement in Hewlett's Center on Continuous Instructional Improvement (CCII) grant... called Adaptive Instruction! Check it out, and more importantly, if you run across anything relating to CCII's goal:

[To] stimulate, support, conduct, and disseminate the results of research and development efforts in support of a set of closely related approaches to improving instruction sometimes known as adaptive instruction, sometimes known as personalization, and sometimes simply as formative assessment, as well as related improvement strategies such as learning progressions, knowledge management systems, and continuous improvement processes.
... post it to the Ning!
