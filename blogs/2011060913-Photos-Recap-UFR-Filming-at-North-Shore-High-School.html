Photos + Recap: UFR Filming at North Shore High School

Kevin M. Gong

June 9, 2011

Yesterday, six of us headed out for a road trip to North Shore High School on Long Island to document a UFR curriculum in action. Teacher Chris Zublionis taught concepts in international debt and religious views on debt to a group of over 20 tenth grade students, many of whom are enrolled in AP European History. Chris's lesson incorporated a variety of teaching methods, including videos, articles, small group discussions, and socratic seminars. Interviews with students afterwards revealed that the discussions on current affairs, notably the relationship between China and the US in regards to debt, was a unanimous favorite among the students. Students were very eager to learn about contemporary issues, especially since their history classes rarely made connections between historical events and modern ones. Meanwhile, the educational videos and small group discussions were selected as the preferred learning methods. Overall, a very worthwhile trip. Full gallery of photos here.
