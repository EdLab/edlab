Vialogues is officially on EDUCADE.ORG!

Demetri Lales

January 16, 2014

While testing Educade.org for an Edlab Review article I wrote (coming soon on NLT) I submited Vialogues to their digital edtech resources library. I was trying to see how long it would take for them to approve a resource submission since they claim to be very selective when it comes to making additions to their library. A month after submission Vialogues is finally included in their library. Though this may not seem like a big deal to most people, to me it means that Vialogues will get more exposure, and hopefully more signups. I did a similar thing with Gooru and saw in the analytics that there were some visits to Vialogues from the site. Maybe the same can be done for our other tools.
