Impressions and Questions ﻿🤔﻿

  • A new metric we’ve begun tracking is how many days in advance people book rooms, and how these numbers differ by device (i.e., site vs. app). Early numbers point to website room bookers thinking farther in advance than app users. However, one thing to note is that these numbers represent an average out of 120 days. In the future, we can ignore outliers to see if it affects the reported numbers.
  • The number one action on the app continues to be reserving rooms. Why do you think this app-specific behavior trumps all others?
