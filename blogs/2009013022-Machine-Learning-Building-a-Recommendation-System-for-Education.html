Machine Learning - Building a Recommendation System for Education

Michael Chiu

January 30, 2009

From the New York Times :

"Recommendation systems, also known as collaborative filtering systems, try to predict whether a customer will like a movie, book or piece of music by comparing his or her past preferences to those of other people with similar tastes. Such systems will look at, say, the last 10 books, movies or songs a customer has rated highly and try to extrapolate an 11th."
I would like the EdLab to build a recommendation system for the education sector. What article should I assign my students? Which book should I read for this class? Which courses should I enroll in? What song should I download onto my mobile phone? This Machine Learning Course at CU-CS, which I am auditing, might be of interest to people in the EdLab.
