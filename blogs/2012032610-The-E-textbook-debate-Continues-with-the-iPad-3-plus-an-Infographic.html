The E-textbook debate Continues with the iPad 3... plus an Infographic!

Kate Meersschaert

March 26, 2012

How much has changed since January? The infographic embedded below was created earlier this year... do you think that, besides being one of the longest on record, it still effectively expresses the prohibitive cost to schools involved in wide-spread ipad/eTextbook adoption? This Mashable op-ed further explores why the tablet-based textbook might not be the saving-grace it was predicted by many to be. iPad 3... Do you think that the new iPad 3 announced earlier this month will help or hinder school tablet adoption? McGraw Hill Education VP, Vineet Madan explains in this DailyTech.com article why the publisher feels the new features of the iPad 3 (higher res screens, faster connectivity etc.) will help the eTextbook cause. Maden also brings-up the point that accessibility will be increased through the now lower-priced, but still powerful, iPad 2.
