Super Earths

Elysee Dougan

January 22, 2014

I was exploring different groups on Google+ when I came across a space group named SPACE.com. They have numerous facts and articles talking about the known universe. One article in particular, which caught my attention, talked about exo-planets living in the Milkyway Galaxy. Essentially, scientists have found a planet that share the same characteristics as Earth's but only several times bigger and thus creating more gravity. Although these planets are light-years away I feel, as the development of technology continues, reaching these newly found planets won't be so difficult. After all we only started sending people to the moon as recent as the mid 1900s and in the early 2000s we were sending people to Mars.
