D&R Meeting: "And now presenting... EdLab, to the world: How would you do it?"

Janice Joo

March 19, 2013

Week 10: Daniel Um You will need the following: EdLab has been working tirelessly since its inception in 2005 and we suppose it's about time to treat ourselves to a makeover and the whole shebang. Today, Daniel will lead the team in a brainstorming session to design ways to present EdLab, our brand, our mission, and our products to those outside of TC. We will be creating mood boards to facilitate the communication of our ideas. You probably already know what a mood board is... it's basically a collage of visual ideas (see below), from which we can gather a theme... like "yellow", "rustic", "country", "autumn"...
