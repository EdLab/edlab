Examples of Academic Room Reservation Forms

Frank Webster

July 6, 2005

Examples of Academic Room Reservation Forms: Arizona State University: I like the layout but they want you to print out a form and fax/mail it in. http://www.asu.edu/xed/asudt/roomreserve.html MIT: I would have expected better. http://web.mit.edu/learningcenter/www/reserve.htm Rowan University: Way too heavy on policy. http://www.rowan.edu/studentaffairs/studentcenter/room_reservations/ Rutgers: I like the pull-downs. http://reservelabs.newark.rutgers.edu/ UConn: How NOT to do it! http://vm.uconn.edu/~ushndbks/instrctr.html SUNY Albany Library: Not bad forms but very bad integration with calendars, etc. http://library.albany.edu/services/reserve/ UC San Diego School of Computer Science and Engineering: Frank Comments: If you can figure out how to use this system, you get a degree in computer science and engineering. http://www.cse.ucsd.edu/deptadmin/csehelp/otherresources/software/ctimerooms.html
