New Perspectives in Global Learning

Renae Lesser

December 21, 2016


All year long we’ve been keeping tabs on how learning is changing around the world. These vialogues highlight some of our favorite global trends.

1. Developing Empathy in Global Crises
This immersive virtual reality project was mounted to help users develop empathy while learning about the Syrian conflict.

2. The Bright Orange Box
This solar-powered device contains a projector, PC, speakers, and a video camera, making digital education accessible, even in places without electricity.

3. The Invaluable Benefits of Going Global
This vialogue shows that as our economy becomes more globalized, there is an increased need for study abroad experiences.

4. Making Global Educational Advocacy Personal
This teenage girl escaped the Syrian conflict and is now fighting for greater educational access at refugee camps in Jordan.

5. Pedaling It Forward
As a child in rural India, Anurudh didn’t have access to necessary vaccines. Now, the teenage inventor has designed a new low-cost way of safely transporting medicine.
