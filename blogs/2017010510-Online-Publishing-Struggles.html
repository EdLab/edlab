Online Publishing Struggles

gnatriello

January 5, 2017

A vibrant online publishing sector seems essential for an internet that supports learning in the broadest sense. It is thus concerning that Medium is struggling to find a successful business model and is in the process of reducing staff and closing offices. Unless we identify alternative publishing models supported by readers, we may be left with only content that meets the interests of corporate sponsors. Take a look at how this news is covered by Forbes, recode and Engadget.
