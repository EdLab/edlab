The Art of Writing Proposals

Ting Yuan

September 30, 2009

As a few of us have been talking about (grant) proposal writing and how to gather more information on this topic, I found this article worth reading. Here are some quotes:

- Questions are clearly posed. - Avoid jargon as much as you can. - "Missing even a single reference can be very costly if it shows failure to connect with research directly relevant to one's own." - "Good proposals demonstrate awareness of alternative viewpoints and argue the author's position in such a way as to address the field broadly, rather than developing a single sectarian tendency indifferent to alternatives." - A final product - Three months in advance
