Unleashing Creativity Through the Possibility Project

Laura Scheiber

March 18, 2013


The Possibility Project is an awarding-winning program aimed at empowering teenagers by using the performing arts to foster the next generation of creative and engaged community leaders. Participants engage in intensive issue-oriented discussions, intermixed with the creation of scenes and writing. Based on what comes out of the discussions, the youth then write, produce and perform an original performing arts production grounded in their ideas for change. During the final phase of the project, they design and lead community action projects associated with the issues they discussed. Through discussions that incorporate critical reflection, The Possibility Project encourages creativity and real-world problem solving using a unique pedagogical approach.
