Schoolyard Brawl!

Fred Rossoff

June 7, 2011

Dear Edlabbers: Skanda and I have been cooking up a new series on educational issues called “Schoolyard Brawl.” The basic format will be a back-and-forth, talking-heads type dialogue on educational issues. We'll each develop (somewhat exaggerated) pseudonymous personalities with which stake out opposing sides and argue over things like value-added teaching, or for-profit online universities. Our goal will be two-fold: 1. Provide a more entertaining and engaging platform for people to learn about what's going on in the educational world today. 2. Attempt to develop meaningful conclusions on these issues, so as to leave the audience more enlightened than before. We'd like to start out just on the EdLab blog, but will hopefully (if we get some positive feedback from you guys!) expand onto a pressible blog, Vialogues, and maybe a podcast format. Stay tuned...
