Photos from Gawker -- Walkabout NYC

Josh Lopez

June 13, 2011

As part of Walkabout NYC Jo, Joyce, Kate and I toured Gawker's office. Gawker is the parent of several online blogs including Gizmodo, Lifehacker, io9, Jezebel, and Jalopnik. The setup there reminded me a little of our own office. Two large work areas, and a separate meeting space. But there were a few extra's there and I got some pictures to show everyone. First, there's a large board showing the trending topics on their blogs, with real time updates and snazzy animation. I think it would interesting to have something like this at EdLab, maybe tracking which Vialogues are seeing heavy traffic. At the end of all the desk rows is a big board showing the front pages of all of the Gawker sites. There was one other thing there which we don't have at Edlab. Did anyone else find a working shower in any of the offices they toured last week? I guess if you really want people to feel at home while they're at work, give them everything they'd have at home.
