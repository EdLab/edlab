Luyen Chou at the EdLab Seminar

EdLab Studios

August 28, 2017


Luyen Chou is more than familiar with the evolution of technology in the classroom. Now the Chief Product Strategy Officer at Pearson, Chou has worn many hats in the education sector over the last three decades including Senior Vice President of Schoolnet, Associate Head of The School at Columbia University, President, Founder, and CEO of Learn Technologies, Director of the New Laboratory for Teaching and Learning at The Dalton School in New York City, as well as high school history and social studies teacher. In August of 2017, Luyen Chou shared his presentation on the history and trajectory of product development at Pearson at the Smith Learning Theater.
