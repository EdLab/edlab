Trends in Ed, 11.17.09

Ting Yuan

November 17, 2009

Google (Legal) Scholar?

Today Google announced a big move in its Google Scholar. Now legal opinions from the Supreme Court and federal and state courts in general are available! When visiting Google Scholar, click on the "Legal opinions and journals" button and you can search any legal term you want, e.g., the key words "separate but equal" will take you to famous cases such as Plessy v. Ferguson and Brown v. Board of Education.
