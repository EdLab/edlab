Website Annotation Has Never Been Easier

saraehardman

January 12, 2017


Diigo is an online annotation tool that lets you highlight, bookmark, and create sticky notes on web pages, PDFs, and images, then save these pages in your library to return whenever you would like to. It’s similar to one of our favorite recent reviews Flexcil, except geared toward the computer instead of a tablet or phone.

Pros:

Diigo is an online extension that works on any website or PDF and provides users with many tools to collect, annotate, and organize these sources. Users can highlight passages with four different colors, post sticky notes to the page, and bookmark sites for later reading. Diigo collects all saved and annotated pages into your Diigo Library. Once articles are saved there, users can open a notepad beside each article to take notes and extract quotes while reading. Diigo’s notepad feature is automatically formatted to help you organize your research, links, and references effectively, but it can also be personally customized.

In addition to these features, Diigo allows users to share their notes and articles with others. Users can share knowledge by joining groups or sending their saved information directly to an individual. This collaborative feature makes Diigo a useful tool for the classroom and among colleagues.

Cons:

Despite the fact that Diigo collects all saved articles into a personal library, once there, the articles don’t always adhere to the Diigo platform. On occasion, Diigo directs you back to the original site, which prohibits the notepad feature. Despite this, these articles seem rare, and Diigo allows users to embed web addresses into the site in the case of broken links.

Our Takeaway:

Diigo is a useful tool for anyone who wants to take notes while reading articles or PDFs online. While it could be utilized for personal learning, its collaborative features make it effective as a group learning and knowledge-sharing tool, as well. Diigo would be a particularly helpful research and reading tool for anyone writing an essay, as this platform allows for simple and structured note taking, all organized in one place.
