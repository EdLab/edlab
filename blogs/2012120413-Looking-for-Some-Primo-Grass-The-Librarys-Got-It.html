Looking for Some Primo Grass? The Library's Got It

jvazquez

December 4, 2012

That's a real lawn you're looking at! Cornell University's Olin Library installed the lush flooring in an effort to calm the nerves of stressed students during finals week. It appears to be a continuation of an exhibit that opened last year at their Mann Library. Cornell has an acclaimed agriculture program so I have no doubt that they know what they're doing. Pretty clever, guys!
