Trail U

EdLab Studios

June 18, 2014


Since its creation in 1920, the New York-New Jersey Trail Conference has been maintaining, promoting, and educating the public about the 2,000 miles of trails in the metropolitan area that offer outdoor escape for so many urban residents. Now the conference educates new and old hikers, conservators, and future stewards through Trail University. Trail University is the Trail Conference's innovative solution for preparing the next generation of stewards and volunteers: in-situ workshops on everything from trail design and accessibility to building stone walls, safe chainsaw use, and moving boulders across mountainous landscape with high-line rigging! These workshops build from basic terminology to on-the-ground problem solving. They connect students to each other, their public lands, and a professional's ability to see how much hard work it takes to ensure continued access to the great outdoors.
