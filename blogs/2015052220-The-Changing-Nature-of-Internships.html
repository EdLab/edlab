The Changing Nature of Internships

Oumar Soumahoro

May 22, 2015

An internship is often seen as one of the best ways for students to gain real world experience before the real world. Therefore, landing a good and useful internship has become essential for most college students, more so than ever. Companies are skipping traditional routes of recruiting (campus booths, job postings, etc.,) and recruiting straight from their intern pool. This has changed the very nature of the internship.
