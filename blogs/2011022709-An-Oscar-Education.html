An Oscar Education

melissa cardinali

February 27, 2011

What: The 83rd Academy Awards (the Oscars) When: February 27th (this Sunday, 8pm in NYC) Who: Anne Hathaway and James Franco co-host

What else: You may have already known all of that. But what else do you know about this year’s Oscar nominees? Here at TC, we believe in teacher preparedness. Taking the short Oscar quiz may help you: a) get up to speed on the nominees b) increase the amount of insignificant trivia you already know c) teach you something scandalous about James Franco d) all of the above

In an attempt to make this a fair assessment, I’ve included some modifications for advanced students of the Oscars: click here or here for more sophisticated quizzes.
