Social Media, Privacy and the Classroom: TODAY at Noon in 305 Russel

Julia Martin

March 19, 2012

1417354742


http://youtu.be/7YvAYIJSSZY TODAY! at Noon I will be talking about utilizing social media in the classroom, both as a student, and as a teacher. We will talk about Blogging, Twitter, Facebook, the Google Suite, Slideshare, and newer things like Pinterest. Because of the nature of it's use, we will talk about which of these programs allows what types of privacy settings, and how to find/change them to protect your users. We will also talk about how to safely use and benefit from tools without privacy settings. Please stop by with questions, projects or to get some snacks!
