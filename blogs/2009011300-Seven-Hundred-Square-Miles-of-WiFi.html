Seven Hundred Square Miles of WiFi

HSC

January 13, 2009

Almost three years ago I blogged about WiMax. Unlike WiFi, the range of WiMax can be miles. Well Clearwire just launched WiMax in Portland Oregon and the early reviews seem promising. But how will this technology compete with existing WiFi and 3G networks (you can read more about the debate here)? Will WiMax even be relevant once tethering on devices like the iPhone become more commonplace? Are developing countries and/or parallel worlds the best market WiMax can hope for?
