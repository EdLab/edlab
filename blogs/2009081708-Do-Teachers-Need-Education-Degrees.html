"Do Teachers Need Education Degrees?"

bhughes

August 17, 2009

Don't miss this Room for Debate article on education degrees. From our very own Margaret S. Crocco:

...As any reflective teacher will tell you, however, teaching and learning don't necessarily go hand in hand, no matter how good the talker in front of the classroom. There is nothing as critical to the learning process as method, what John Dewey characterized as the effective arrangement of subject matter for learning...
And from Arthur Levine:
...These comments should not be taken as a repudiation of teacher education. Every child in America is entitled to a qualified teacher, who prior to entering a classroom as the teacher of record, should be educated in a subject field and pedagogy and have the experience of applying both in a school setting...
