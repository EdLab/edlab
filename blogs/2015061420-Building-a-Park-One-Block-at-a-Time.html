Building a Park, One Block at a Time

Dana Haugh

June 14, 2015


The State Government of Adelaide, Australia has challenged thousands of students in grades 4-7 to create their perfect national park. The drafting software? None other than the intensely popular gaming platform, Minecraft. These students now have the opportunity to use their virtual building skills to develop facilities and structures they'd like to see in the 3D world. The winning class will be invited to participate in a full day's excursion in South Australia's National Park in Belair as well as the opportunity to see their ideas come to life.
