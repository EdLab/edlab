Monthly Library Traffic Report - Feb 2018

AbdulMalik

March 20, 2018

February is universally known as the month of love. Inspired by the love of data, I looked at patron traffic on several floors of the library including stacks via our TrafSys system. After comparing the total for February with last month, I discovered that February had more traffic even though it had less days. I was curious about how Valentine's day affected patron traffic, I also looked at the hours that generated the most traffic during that day. February 14th was consistent with the other days of the month in that the busiest time of the day was 5PM.
