Free Data Visualization Webinars

Kate Meersschaert

June 5, 2012

Hi Team EdLab! I just thought I would share this free data viz resource with you all, as I know that data visualization and mining are important topics here at the EdLab (just search "Data Visualization" on this blog)! The Non-profit Technology Network (NTEN) is hosting these webinars and is an interesting organization with over 2K active members who work in technology for the non-profit space. Learn more about NTEN in in the Vialogue below... This series of webinars will run for three weeks and are backed by funding from Google. Here is the schedule and topics: June 7 - Strategy: What Data to Visualize? June 14 - Process and Tools: How to Visualize Your Data June 21 - Putting Your Visualizations to Work Learn more and register here - it's FREE!
