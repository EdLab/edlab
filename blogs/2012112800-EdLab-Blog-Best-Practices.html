EdLab Blog Best Practices

Nantwi

November 28, 2012

Here are some best practices for posting on the EdLab blog:

  • For images, please always use “width=344 height=425” if it is a large image as those tend to break the blog pages
  • Always hyperlink any outside resources
  • For videos, please use Vialogues embed
  • Indicate at the very beginning of the blog post if you want it to remain private by writing ***PRIVATE PLEASE
  • No slang or expressions for blogs that will be made public (e.g. lol, smiley faces, etc.,)
  • If you want to share your blog post with someone from outside or made public immediately after posting, please email me (gn2177@columbia.edu) so I can do so right away. I usually make them public later on in the day
These tips are what immediately came to mind. If you have any additional helpful hints for the posting on the blog, please comment below. Thanks.
