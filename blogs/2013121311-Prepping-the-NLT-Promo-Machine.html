Prepping the NLT Promo Machine

Kate Meersschaert

December 13, 2013

Private! Hi everyone! The D&R Outreach & Community Engagement team is preparing for a month-long NLT push (fresh focus for the new YEAR!). Please share your suggestions and feedback regarding the following winning* ideas that were generated in our last COE meeting and were shared as a part of this blog post. Winning* COE Meeting Ideas...

  • Email TCR Book Reviewers
  • Write something really controversial
  • Approach a major publication & ask them to feature our content (syndication)
  • Real-life media: Post NLT cover page in Cafe
  • Tabling: Finals recovery station (energy drinks, coffee, candy & NLT sign-up)
Is there a social media campaign you would like to see happen? A Facebook contest? G+ blitz? Now is your chance to weigh-in! Think outside the box while you help us to make the new year even MORE successful for new NLT user acquisition and deeper engagement for our existing readership! *Collaboratively voted to be the winners!
