To tweet or not to tweet - EdLab on Twitter?

Frederik G. Pferdt

July 6, 2009

Recently, EdLabbers blogged a lot about the question of the usefulness of Twitter as a new form of communicating and sharing. See for example: here, here, here, here, here, here or here Exchanging pros and cons about twittering in general, the question about EdLab on Twitter was raised in some informal discussions in the last weeks. My personal opinion is that EdLab should definitely start a Twitter account and build on the success story of the EdLab Blog. Not only to share our thoughts about topics concerning education, innovation and technology but also to start some useful discussions with other researchers, designers and producers and to start a solid network inside and outside EdLab.
