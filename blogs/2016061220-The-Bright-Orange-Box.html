The Bright Orange Box

dinosossi2

June 12, 2016


LumenEd has developed a video technology called the Bright Orange Box (or BOB, for short). Itâ€™s an all-in-one solar-powered device that combines a projector, speakers, and a video camera, making it possible for kids from all over the world to communicate with one another. Kids record, share, and project media to develop video pen pal relationships with students in every corner of the world.

How else could this technology be used to promote international dialogue and awareness? Share your thoughts with us in the ongoing discussion on Vialogues.
