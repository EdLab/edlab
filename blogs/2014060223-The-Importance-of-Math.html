The Importance of Math

Kafoumba Doumbia

June 2, 2014

Mathematics is the basic foundation of everything we do and we don't even notice. For instance, we calculate money before spending. However many students, myself include, have taken higher-level math course often to ask, what is the purpose of these courses? Are we going to use this knowledge in the real world? This question is hard to answer theoretically unless it is put into application. Math is a very important skill to have because it teaches one how to logically and critically think. To be able to think critically and logically is very important because everything we do require some kind of thinking such as explaining steps we take arrive at a solution to a specific problem.

Many entry-level jobs require some sort of mathematical skills. For instance, a cashier needs to be able to count in order to interact with customers. Students interested in mathematics can pursue a career in engineering, medicine and research. The result is often a high paying job. Math is found in everything we do and we will need more students to fill the ton of math jobs available.
