Reflecting on 4/1 Seminar: Our Public Text Map and Resources...

Doug Beacom

April 1, 2009

Hello All, Skye and I are running off to a shoot in Brooklyn now, but I wanted to post our collaborative work here before it slips out of my mind. Please check back to this post later for resources (links and our flickr set)
View edlab seminar map in a larger map I think we did a great job--think about it--we collaboratively built a representative, online text in the middle of a seminar! think of where we could go with this--as a lab, as educators and especially as researchers. Something I'd like to note is that I've zoomed in on the NYC region of this, though we do have representation from other parts of the U.S. (and maybe the world?)--do 'go to larger map' if you have time and continue to build this map and see other data!
