More Than An Inkling: The New Textbook Is Here

Carmen James

April 16, 2014


How can textbooks be more dynamic? Inkling has an idea. This video explores the educational advantages of transforming traditional high school and college textbooks into interactive tablets where students can click through for more information in the form of definitions, videos, 3D models, and illustrations. Trying to imagine what the models look like for a chemistry problem you are solving? Just click the image in the textbook to animate the models.

"Textbooks don’t have to be text, they don’t have to be books," the inkling video shares. How can we imagine a book without a binding? Inkling has re-imagined books as tablets that users can manually manipulate while highlighting and adding notes. At the heart of the 3.0 textbook is the ability to dialogue about passages with others. "To have your entire social network turned into a learning network" is what Inkling believes is most transformational about their product and what will most dramatically change the landscape of education.
