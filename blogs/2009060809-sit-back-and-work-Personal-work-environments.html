sit back & ... work - Personal work environments

Frederik G. Pferdt

June 8, 2009

The effort about Re-Designing the EdLab Atelier brought me to these existing ideas of computer chairs: iMedia ergonomic chair Netsurfer PCE Personal Computing Environments I wrote an eMail to G-Tech International Co., Ltd. in Korea and they send me the following description of their model: PC Chair NEBER. They could ship it for 845 Dollar/set. For the EdLab, I could see two possible options for the chair. The first one is a "Welcome Visitior Chair". One or two chairs could be placed in the EdLab tower or the entrance of the Atelier for visitors who have an appointment with an EdLabber. They could enjoy some introductory videos of EdLab and some background information, do last minute preparation before their appointment or just check eMail and relax. The second one is "EdLab workstation". Specific tasks could be attached to the EdLab workstation. For example blogging. Every EdLabber who wants to share some ideas on the blog could use the chair and enjoy a relaxed way of blogging. WouldnÂ´t that be relaxed work?
