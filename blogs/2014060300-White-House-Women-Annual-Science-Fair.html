White House Women Annual Science Fair

rendy

June 3, 2014

During the recently completed White House Science Fair, President Obama stated that, for him, the fair is much more important than a visit from the Super bowl champions. Students from across the country presented their project at the fair, from cancer and flu research to robots that can search for bodies in the cold.. To be able to invent new things requires imagination, inspiration and at times luck. As we can see in this article, a young female wants to become the first collegiate head football coach. Inspired by her cell phone case, she was able to use gel and memory foam inserts to create a helmet that can avoid more concussions.

The White House also announced a $35 million Department of Education competition in the hopes of attracting 100,000 STEM teachers. This is part of the Education to Innovate initiative, and should help boost up low-income students over the summer. The program should bring employment opportunities for college students so they can practice what they learn and earn some money.
