CourseRank

Pranay Dharmale

December 12, 2009

CourseRank - a Stanford Database Group (InfoLab) project, is a Course Recommendation System. It provides students a tool which assists them in choosing the right courses. It gives them access to highly detailed information regarding the course and a variety of statistics related to it. It also allows students to rank the courses they've taken, find the class statistics (avg. grade, course ratings, reviews etc). Along with this, it also provides personalized recommendations, a scheduler to plan their courses, shop for course books and a lot more. Their aim was to built something "simple yet effective". I find this tool useful and cool at the same time :)
