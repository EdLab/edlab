Projecting A More Creative Classroom

Brian.Sweeting

May 21, 2015


Classroom technology has come a long way since the days of bulky overhead projectors beaming oversaturated scribbles onto a white board. Quality projectors that retract from view and display high definition scenes are now affordable enough for schools to transform traditional learning spaces into imaginative environments. New products are expanding this technology, projecting images that students can interact with and creating scenes that will transport students' minds and inspire creativity.
