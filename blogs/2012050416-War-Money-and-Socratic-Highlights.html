War, Money, and Socratic Highlights

Jennifer

May 4, 2012

“Can We Stop Wars?” was the subject of the Socratic Conversation conducted by Ron Gross on Friday, May 3rd -- and a prevailing view of the discussants was "Follow the money!" A conflict resolution practitioner with experience ranging from Israel/Palestine to the Philippines said, "All wars are really over economic interests." Another participant advised: "If you want to know what the U.S. priorities are, just look at a pie-chart of the federal budget, and see how fat that military piece is." But others in the group contended that we might yet see beyond belligerent self-interest, and cited the optimistic views expressed in recent books like The End of War by John Horgan, and The Better Angels of Our Nature by Stephen Pinker.
