Warning: if the idea of censoring content makes you uncomfortable, you may not want to read about it here and here and here. Disclaimers on college syllabi about potentially traumatic or uncomfortable material are themselves causing an uproar. What began as an attempt to help students with PTSD has become a point of ridicule, with trigger warnings issued for everything from Downton Abbey to [TRIGGER WARNING] animals in wigs.
