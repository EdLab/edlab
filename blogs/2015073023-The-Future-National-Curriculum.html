The Future National Curriculum

AbdulMalik

July 30, 2015

Education is widely seen as one of the most essential aspects of every individual's life. However, how do we build a curriculum that will enable students to gain access to quality education? Several researchers have proposed that students should be able to face life after school based on knowledge they acquire in school. The "future national curriculum" will ensure that students reach their full potential. I believe if schools design a curriculum that students could relate more to, it will encourage them to work harder in order to achieve academic success and other personal goals. Imagine a national curriculum that enables every graduate to understand things that range from how to file their taxes to relationships.
