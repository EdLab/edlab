Love in Our Neighborhood Science Fair (Part 2)

Alvaro.Ortiz-Vazquez

March 2, 2017

In my previous blog post I shared my work volunteering with with community activists from the Tayshanna "Chicken" Murphy Foundation to organize the Love in the Neighborhood STEM fair. I am pleased to share with you that our work has had much success. We have worked closely with three young students from the performing arts high school on 129th and Amsterdam over the past few months to put together projects that seek to positively impact the health, environment and relationships within their community.

This Saturday (March 4th) from 11AM to 2PM, we will host the LION STEM Fair where the students will present their work.  There will also be presentations from others on their work or research methods they use in their studies. We have also invited students from West Harlem middle and high schools to attend the fair. I would like to invite anyone in the EdLab to attend or if they feel up to it, to present! I will be personally adapting a recent presentation I gave at the weekly Development and Research presentations for the young students.
