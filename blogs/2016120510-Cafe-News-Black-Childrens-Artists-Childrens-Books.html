Cafe News: Black: Children's Artists, Children's Books

Jennifer

December 5, 2016

Hi, Everyone. We wish to share some ideas for the next Cafe book display (mid January through the end of February, Black History Month), guest curated by Ilya Washington, TC student and founder of Black, "a collective studio and research organization seeks to build curricula around the histories of Africa and the African diaspora with communities that don't have direct and/or consistent access to such information." Black: Children's Artists, Children's Books features classic works of children's literature that highlight beloved authors, illustrators, themes, and concerns.
