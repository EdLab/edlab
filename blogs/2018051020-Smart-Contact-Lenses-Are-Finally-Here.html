Smart Contact Lenses Are Finally Here

Ryan.Allen

May 10, 2018


Smart glasses have mostly flopped in recent years, as designs have been too intrusive. Turns out, people don’t want to walk around looking like Cyclops from the 2000’s X-Men movies. While some companies are beginning to recognize past failures of smart glasses, the future might actually be in smart contact lenses.

Researchers have created a contact lens that can collect information and display it to the person wearing them. The prototype lens can analyze glucose levels in eye moisture and a tiny LED can then signal the results to the wearer.

While these smart contacts are still in the early stages of development, it’s easy to envision this technology being widely used in the future. What could be some innovative uses for smart contacts? Would you like to try them? Add your voice to the discussion on Vialogues.
