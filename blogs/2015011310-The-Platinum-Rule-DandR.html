The Platinum Rule D&R

LauraCostello

January 13, 2015

**private please** Today we're going to do an assessment of our own work styles based on Tony Alessandra's The Platinum Rule. I'll be going through the questions up front, but if you'd rather read or you want to work ahead: a link to my presentation. After you're done scoring, take a look at this description of the four personality types. This guide also contains short descriptions of the 16 sub-styles and suggestions for adapting to different personality types. Here are the questions we are going to discuss in groups and post individually to the blog: Do you think your results accurately describe you at work? Are there implications here for reforming our group processes? What are some practical applications of this idea for you as a team member? Bonus question! Do you think this tool is a useful framework for understanding individuality at work?
