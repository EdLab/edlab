The Rise of Artificial Intelligence

Henry Adjei

July 1, 2014

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is building a machine to do intelligent things that humans frequently do. AI has been an issue since its inception. How can they relate to humans? Will it change civilization or improve it? Those are questions people ask but the main question is how can we build an AI system similar to the human brain? Human beings are self-conscious. We have emotions and feelings that make as sensitive. To develop a strong and a friendly AI will help civilization. There are so many advantages to a strong Al in teams of health and education. As AI gets better, society will benefit as a result. Will human beings be less effective as a result? This video describes how AI will affect humans and how efficient they will be.
