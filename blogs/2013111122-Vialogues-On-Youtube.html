Vialogues On Youtube

Francisco.Mendoza

November 11, 2013

As I was looking for a video on YouTube to upload for Vialogues, I decided to type in "Vialogues" on YouTube. This video is a tutorial on how to use Vialogues from a teachers point of view. The teacher talks about her use of Vialogues and some of the difficulties she has encountered. This other video, posted about eight months ago, also explains how to use Vialogues. Both videos highlight some problems users have had on the site. I have been recruiting new users for Vialogues mainly through classmates, people from my community, and also some friends. It's been a challenge trying to recruit users so I've decided to stick to one topic so that I can target a specific audience. My recent Vialogues has focused on criminal justice, and I was able to have one of my professors to take a look at Vialogues. He subsequently emailed all of his students to visit and navigate the site. There have been a lot of updates on Vialogues recently. If someone can make one of these tutorials, or if need doing so, please let me know. Also if someone will make the video, showing teachers and students points of view that will be very helpful. We can then use these videos in our promotional efforts, as visitors to the site will have an idea on how to use it before they visit. Will we be able to work on a tutorial on how to use the latest version of Vialogues?
