Markdown, no Markup!

Panisuan Chasinga

February 19, 2015

**Private** Hi, I'm wondering if this blog can in the future have Markdown integrated for writing instead of partial HTML? Markdown is more enjoyable to write and is becoming the de facto in writing on online forums and blogs. Also, without the '<' and '>' of HTML markup we all can be a step further away from getting Carpal tunnel syndrome and it's just more writing fun! For those writing junkies who have not written anything in Markdown, go play around in this or this editor. You might not want to go back to MS Word again.
