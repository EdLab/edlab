Sharecropper! (part 1)

Skye MacLeod

September 10, 2009

I hope you enjoy this experiment that I have been working on over the last few weeks. It follows Leah Gauthier, an artist that works with natural materials, as she installs her newest work in other people's backyards. It's been a remarkable experience getting to know Leah, her volunteers, and the people who have donated their space. What really strikes me about this project is how community oriented Leah is, and how she connected to people using her blog and flickr account. It's a really neat project, with a lot of educational potential (by the people involved as well as schools/educational institutions.) Let me know what you think!
