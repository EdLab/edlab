Holiday Open House Fortune Cookies are Ready 4 Production!

Kate Meersschaert

December 6, 2011

After an extremely productive last-hour emergency cookie meeting the final 15 fortunes for the EdLab Open House have been crafted & voted-on from a list of 31: 1) A lifetime of learning is not nearly long enough. 2) Teach what you know; learn what you don't. 3) Dream big. Act accordingly. 4) Look and ye shall find educational data. 5) You will become the LeVar Burton of educational video. 6) The time is right to make new friends. 7) On the road of life you definitely want EdLab on your side! 8) Learn to say "Educational innovation for the next century": Ed-LAB! 9) He who is happy probably works in the EdLab. 10) Don't rewind, fast forward. 11) Education helped you change. Now you can help change education. 12) Love is grand; rethinking education is a hundred grand. 13) The world is your oyster, and the EdLab is the pearl. 14) You will learn something new every Wednesday. We promise! 15) Hey, I'm just a cookie. Find your real fortune at EdLab! Thank you to the emergency cookie council for your quick work and brilliant ideas!
