When Your Hand Becomes A Wand

DebraLee

March 11, 2018


This drone was designed as a toy for small children six years and up, but its novel ability to respond to hand commands is delighting people of all ages. Aura’s controller is a stretchy glove secured by adjustable velcro straps. Once you’re suited up the sky’s the limit, and with just a little player imagination Aura will fly like a superhero.

With simple hand gestures the device can fly forward and backward, left and right, change altitude, and perform a sideways or 360 degree flip trick. The technology was previously only available to the military until roboticist Dr. Pramod Abichandani created GestureBotics, in the LocoRobo Innovations lab, and introduced a whole new way to fly. Beyond a cool high-flying toy, how do you think GestureBotics could be used on a day-to-day basis? Join the discussion on Vialogues.
