A chance to enter Vialogues and other tools in the list of top 100 tools for learning, 2011

Megha Agarwala

June 22, 2011

Jane Hart is building the 5th annual Top 100 tools for learning list, based on the contributions of people! - Source Credit: Ankit Ranka "What is a learning tool? This could mean what you use to create or deliver learning content/solutions for others, or what you use for your own personal learning." Email your list of Top 10 Tools for Learning to jane.hart@c4lpt.co.uk to help build the Top 100 Tools for Learning 2011 and include Vialogues and other Edlab tools in your list. The list will be finalized Nov 13, 2011.
