Kate & Megha's visit to Socratic Labs @ Alley NYC

Kate Meersschaert

May 2, 2013

***Private! Today Megha & I visited Alley NYC in the Garment district (site of the Seen in NY embedded below) for an hour-long demo of EdLab tools to the Socratic Labs cohort of startups. Startups in attendance included: Penpal News: The easiest way to connect your classroom to the world. Careerosity: Solve career questions together. Career advice for us, by us. Edventory: Discover innovative teaching tools; explore everything you need to know before purchasing; develop best practices Takeaways: The experience was interesting and helpful overall! Interesting because I had never presented our work to a cohort of startups in such an informal way and did not know what to expect. Helpful in that I was able to learn an effective presentation style on the fly... give & take with no formal agenda (despite preparing a presentation!). Wins of the experience... Participants seemed genuinely EXCITED by Vialogues & curious about our work at EdLab! See more detailed feedback and questions from Megha listed below. Most exciting is the fact that the cohort co-leader Ash, wants the team to use Vialogues to refine their pitches for the next Socratic Demo night! Great news! Also, the team wants to participate in EdTech Take & Talk interviews and attend their colleague Katie's upcoming seminar (See her EdTechTake interview here! Final thoughts... I will be very curious to see if Socratic Labs can grow and sponsor future conferences and events (possible co-hosting of NYEdTech Meetups!) that as Ash describes, "bring together technologists and educators!" "Sticker-way" Megha on our way in... Kate presenting (& flapping her "wings" ;))
