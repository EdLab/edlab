Handwriting and Small Screens

Mable Yiu

July 25, 2013

As I was looking for High Five ideas, I came across 2 additional articles I wanted to share with you guys. First, is a fun Edudemic infographic about What Your Handwriting Says About You. Not really sure how accurate this can be, but apparently some of the traits my handwriting tells me I have are that I'm adaptable, skeptical of others, hard to read, good with commitment, and open to new experiences. I don't think all of those are true, and I think I usually write the way I do because I like certain letters to look a certain way. What does your handwriting say about you? Second, is a Business News Daily article on How Small Screens Can Win Big. This one is basically saying how people spend more time on smaller devices like smart phones and tablets, and more than you think. They actually talk about how more and more people are watching TV on their phones (which I didn't know), but I guess we can apply it to more than just TV. So it is very important to make sure our mobile version of NLT is the best it can be!
