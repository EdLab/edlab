C/I

EdLab Studios

June 1, 2015


"What do you want to be when you grow up?" asks C/I program director, Tom O'Connell. No matter the response, he always follows up with, "you need to code to do that!" C/I or Code Interactive, aims to empower students with that very skill and then some in order to prepare todayâ€™s youth for the tech careers of tomorrow. With a focus on underserved communities in the Bronx (like the Bronx International High School) and upper Manhattan, C/I works with teachers to institute a year-round computer science curriculum in the after school classroom to teach hard tech skills like programming and product design as well as professionalism and entrepreneurship. Through participation in opportunities like hackathons, internships, tech office visits, competitions, conferences, and incubator events, C/I exposes students to the possibilities of this burgeoning and increasingly crucial field and increases their likelihood of gainful employment.
