Promoting Gender Diversity in Technology

Toland.Lawrence

March 14, 2019


Technica is the largest all-women hackathon, hosted annually at the University of Maryland. In 24 hours, the participants form teams, create something new, and immerse themselves in tech culture. Participants also have the opportunity to attend workshops on VR, web development, cyber security, and other relevant topics taught by experts in the field.

Women are still underrepresented in technology. The organizers believe that creating a welcoming space where women can explore this interest is important to correcting this imbalance.
