Trends in Ed, 8.31.09

Arianna Choi

August 31, 2009

Upgrading Trends in Ed The Trends in Ed series began two seasons ago framing itself as the newest CSG product, connecting you to the ever-expanding world of teaching and learning. (Moment of reminiscence: the very first post on 1/29/09 by Jo) Since then, Jo, Rebekah, Calli and I have been posting the results of our monitoring in regards to, literally, Trends in Education. We kept our topic broad to see where it would take us and in retrospect, it helped us navigate better or get lost at various points in time. So, now what? According to our college calendar, a new season is starting this week and the same goes for our series. At this mark, we'll present two changes by having new 'monitors' on board and refocusing our sub-topics within the future of teaching and learning.
