Vialogues & Language Acquisition

Kate Meersschaert

September 15, 2011

Private! Laura and I are working on a language acquisition project that will showcase vialogues as a tool to support this learning. Our first focus will be on Mandarin Chinese and we have created the following resources to support this initial exploration: -Vialogues blog entry listing the archival materials Laura has collected for the project. -An mSchool course(in progress!): Vialogues Language Acquisition Hub -We have also created a series of Vialogues - search for any of the following hash tags #Chinese, #Beginner, #Intermediate & #Advanced. We hope to reach-out to Chinese language experts here at TC and beyond and will create a survey to find-out how they would like to use video to teach Chinese language, what existent resources and technology they currently use and to find-out what research TC is involved with. Do you have any additional thoughts or ideas regarding Vialogues as a tool to support language acquisition? Any favorite language-based resources? What is the next language we should highlight with vialogues?
