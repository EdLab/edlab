EdLab World Cup Pick'em Results

Ryan.Allen

July 18, 2018

Well, the World Cup is finally over. What a wild, wonderful month. Do we really have to wait another four years?


As for the EdLab Pick'em, I am a bit embarrassed to announce that I have won my own tournament. Nothing shady about the results, the Cup was in Russia after all. In my defense, I rode France all the way to victory and picked some early upsets that gave me an early lead. Malik roared back with his pick of Les Bleus in the finals to earn 2nd place, so he's basically our Croatia. Just for a counterfactual, if Belgium would have beat France and won the World Cup instead, Hui Soo would have been the victor here at EdLab, so basically like the real Belgium team. 


Anyways, it was fun and thanks for participating. I understand if you want to give me the new nickname of "Les Champ" around the office. Here are the final results:
