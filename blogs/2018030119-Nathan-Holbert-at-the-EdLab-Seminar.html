Nathan Holbert at the EdLab Seminar

EdLabStudios

March 1, 2018


In addition to being the 2017 Strange Junior Faculty Prize recipient, Dr. Nathan Holbert is an Assistant Professor of Communication, Media and Learning Technologies Design in the Department of Mathematics, Science and Technology at Teachers College, Columbia University. After founding the Snow Day Learning Lab in 2014, Dr. Holbert set out to explore the gender imbalance and lack of diversity prominent within the maker movement. He spoke to EdLab in January of 2018 about the "Bots for Tots" Project, a series of workshops in which elementary school students, chiefly girls, designed and built dream toys for younger students at their school. Watch the full seminar on Vialogues.

@01:02:49 Ryan Allen: Fascinating how different genders gravitate towards different materials. I like how Dr. Holbert and his team are thinking about reshaping ownership of certain spaces, making them more egalitarian.
