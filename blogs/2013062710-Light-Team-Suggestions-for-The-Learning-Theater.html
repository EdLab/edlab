Light Team Suggestions for The Learning Theater

Brian.Sweeting

June 27, 2013

The Light Team recommends creating a lighting atmosphere that inspires creativity by using the following customizable and interactive elements:

  • Movable electrochromic walls aka "Smart Glass" will allow students to control the shape, size, and location of their space. The electrically switchable glass will change to allow different levels of translucence, creating openness or privacy.
  • Programmable LED walls are relatively inexpensive and energy efficient. With programmable presets on a mobile/tablet interface, students can control the brightness of their environment. And with an open-source code they can develop simple programs to project data onto the wall. Imagine seeing a life-size bar graph in front of you, or having the color of the room change to reflect the results of a group survey or activity.
  • Blackout Curtains can be rolled down over the windows to create a completely dark space or emphasize the luminosity of the LED walls.
For some light reading on the effects of brightness, check out this study that suggests dim light can actually inspire creativity.
