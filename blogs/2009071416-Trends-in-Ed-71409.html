Trends in Ed 7.14.09

Calli Kosch

July 14, 2009

Free Online Tech Tools for Teachers (and even students) A recent article in So You Want to Teach provides links to some new (and free) tech tools for educators and students. I recognize some on the list from what is currently used here at EdLab, such as Moodle, however, there are some others that I think would be of interest to EdLab. I looked through the list, and here are the tech tools that I think have the most potential and demonstrate the most innovation:

  • Notely
    • - An organizational tool for students that aids in note-taking during lectures.
  • Ooovoo
    • - A video chat application that allows conference chats with up to 6 participants. For teachers, it is suggested for video parent-teacher conferences and video chats with colleagues.
  • Evernote
    • - Allows you to capture images anywhere by using a mobile device or computer, the Evernote system recognizes it, and then sends it to all of your subscribed devices. If you have a chance, take a look through the list of 25 applications. Which ones interest you?
