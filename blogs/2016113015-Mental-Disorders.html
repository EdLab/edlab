Mental Disorders

Carlos.Pena

November 30, 2016

Around 26% of Americans over the age of 18 suffer from some sort of mental disorder. A variety of mental disorders may even be unknowingly close to you. The causes of mental disorders varies; genes and other biological factors are the most frequent cause of what President-elect Donald Trump considers an “epidemic”. Society’s depiction of what constitutes mental disorder is totally wrong. They paint individuals with mental disorders as lonely and sad. This image can be detrimental not only to those with the disorder, but to family members who have to bear with the consequences of the disorder on their loved ones. Individuals with mental disorders, when properly treated, can make valuable contributions to society.
