2012 Social Enterprise Boot Camp, Great Success!

Laura Scheiber

November 19, 2012

KEEP PRIVATE This weekend I attended Social Enterprise Boot Camp, which turned out to be an awesome event. Throughout the weekend, I kept an eye and ear opened regarding what entrepreneurial learners were looking for in the hopes that this could inform our future Launch Pad work.

Above is a quick video clip of a few participants describing why they attended and what they learned. Steven and Heidi are co-founders of Sun Giant Energy, an initiative aimed at bringing clean affordable solar energy to Liberia. Their mission took an interesting turn after Hurricane Sandy. They had sun lanterns ready to be shipped to Liberia, but then realized there were a whole bunch of forgotten victims of Hurricane Sandy in the Far Rockaways, who still don't have electricity. They hustled with their fundraising and ended up giving much needed light sources to these underserved areas of New York. What is hard to get across is the enthusiasm and dedication that Heidi and Steven exude when talking about their work. In a nutshell, they and other Boot Camp participants like them are truly inspirational. More to come soon...
