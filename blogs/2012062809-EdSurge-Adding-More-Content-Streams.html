EdSurge Adding More Content Streams

Michelle Lee

June 28, 2012

For NLT writers and ALT team (or was it BLT?) - take a look at a new content stream EdSurge has created for its Instruct Edition. EdSurge is including interesting snapshots in "A Day In The Life of..." real teachers and how they use edtech. I can see how this would be useful to teachers who would rather hear how a "real live" teacher is using edtech and need more guidance on how to choose a variety of tools to structure their classroom. Thinking about a recent conversation the NLT team had on content, I have to give it up to EdSurge for consistently tweaking their content streams based on user feedback. Any thoughts on how we could apply this to NLT or ALT?
