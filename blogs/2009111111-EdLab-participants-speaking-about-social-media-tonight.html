EdLab participants speaking about social media tonight!

Gus Andrews

November 11, 2009

Gary and I will be presenting briefly on research on social software tonight on the third floor of the library. Here's the details: Please join The Society for Anthropological Studies for: The Social Web: The Practice of Online Life Professor Varenne will moderate a discussion with Gus Andrews, Cyrille Adam, Matt Curinga and Dr. Gary Natriello who will be sharing their research on a variety of topics related to online social networks: from blogging to wikis, the politics of online identity, and language and online practice to name a few. When: Wednesday, November 11th, 7-9pm Where: Teachers College- Russell Hall 3rd floor Library Room 305 There will be food. (Let the school feed you twice today! ;))
