"Flipping Classrooms" and Vialogues

Demetri Lales

September 22, 2011

I was watching this video below of a math teacher explaining the difficulties of teaching students with different skill levels (high, middle, and low). She proposed a solution to this issue: "flipping" her classroom. Flipping involves providing a video for a lecture the day before class online, and having her students discuss and ask questions on the video lecture. I want to focus on this part of her solution because it sounds like she can use Vialogues for this. She can post her videos on Vialogues and have students watch it as many times as they wish. Students can then discuss, comment, and ask questions, from which she can answer on Vialogues. I think this can be a good sales pitch to attract more teachers into using Vialogues. What do you guys think? Can we use this in our advertisements for Vialogues? Maybe we can include "flipping" for our reasons to use Vialogues? Feedback would be awesome!
