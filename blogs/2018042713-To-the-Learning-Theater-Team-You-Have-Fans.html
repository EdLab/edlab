To the Learning Theater Team: You Have Fans!

Cinthia.Fabian

April 27, 2018

About a month ago, the Learning Theater hosted Digital Learning @TC, an event meant to highlight the various ways that TC affiliates have conceptualized digital learning. 


Kyle Matthew Oliver, the lead curator for the event, gives a powerful endorsement for the Learning Theater in his blog. Describing the Learning Theater as “one of the most tool-rich spaces” he’s ever worked with, Oliver reflects on the power of tools to expand our thinking, promote dialogue, and shape learning experiences. 


What are some of your favorite tools (“broadly conceived”)? Are there any tools you’d like to see in the Learning Theater, the Library, or EdLab? 


To learn more about the event, keep an eye on NLT for the soon-to-be-published Learning Theater Spotlight!
