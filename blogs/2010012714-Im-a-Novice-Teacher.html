I'm a Novice Teacher

Ting Yuan

January 27, 2010

(Please keep this post private.) As we were talking about an ambitious "song" idea to celebrate the ending of SECTOR event and to show the talent of our Edlab singers and musicians, I think it can be an exciting trial for us to make a song together (though this time is only for SECTOR). I quickly jotted down some very draft lyrics on this open Google doc as follows and would love to have more of you join us! =) If it is not so realistic to try it this time, it's also fine. We may save it for future trials. =) I'm a Novice Teacher Singers: Jo,Chingfu ...? Composers: Josh...? Lyrics: Ting...? [Female 1] I'm a novice teacher,

starting my first year.

I know that I'm fresh.
Professors I meet tell me "[another voice speaking]You will learn as you practice".
And willingly I believe.
[Female 2] I'm a novice teacher,
inexperienced as a newborn.
Twitters, wikis, blogs, and videos are all around. How do I use some of those? [Female 1] Totally overwhelmed am I,
to face a naughty class.
Nervous and uncertain am I,
to survive in a new tech-world.
[Female 2] How anxious I am to learn more,
wanting to find support.
How eager I am to connect to veterans,
to communicate and be advised.
[Male 1] You are a novice teacher,
who will be teaching for the future.
I am here for you. Anxious, nervous and tried are you,
to face an unpredicted world.
[Male 2] You are a novice teacher,
who will be teaching for the future.
I am here for you. Let me guide you,
to find the cue,
to walk through this thorny road.
[Male 1] I am so willing to help,
to show you all the tips.
I am so excited to be
a witness of your endeavor.
[Male 2] I am so willing to help,
to facilitate your creativity.
I am so glad to be
a part of your brave journey.
(Repeated)
[Chorus]
[Males together] You will be fine. [Females together] I will be fine. [Males together] You should try. [Females together] Let me try. [Males together] We are standing with you. [Females together] I am standing with you. [All together] Let's try it. Let's make it. Be confident. Let's collaborate, in this new digital world. The future of teaching will be full of surprises, joys, and promises...
