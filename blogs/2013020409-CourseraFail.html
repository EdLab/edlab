#CourseraFail

Kate Meersschaert

February 4, 2013

When good intentions...educational technology and MOOCs collide, the result isn't always positive. As organizers and participants discovered when Coursera's new course focused on educational technology in the classroom "melted-down" recently. Learn more through this InsideHigherEd article and the tweets embedded below. Is this a sign of an impending MOOC implosion? Or just an unfortunate tech fail that amplifies the dangers of thousands of learners virtually "tuned-in" for class. After all, we have all been in front of a group when tech fails (Smartboards, laptops, sound, etc.,) and thankfully only had a room-ful notice. Imagine an order of attention magnified by "networked" learning and social media. What are your thoughts? Tweets about "#coursera"
