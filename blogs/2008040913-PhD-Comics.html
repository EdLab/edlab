PhD Comics

Jessica Pang

April 9, 2008

I am sure alot of you have seen this somewhere before. But I just found out about PhD Comics today through facebook applications. I have to share it with someone...here's my favorite one. We should all print it out and bring it to a seminar ha ha ha. Also, I have just read an article on a new virtual reality game being developed to help diagnosis Schizophrenia patients. The game proves to be useful in identifying 85% of the patients, not to mention it helps researchers to accurately measure the cognitive functions of people with schizophrenia. Finally a psychology game that's not just about marketing. For Abstract, see: http://ajp.psychiatryonline.org/cgi/content/abstract/163/3/512
