Plain View and/or Suspicious Smell

Francisco.Mendoza

August 4, 2013

As part of the Digi-Q team, I am more than happy to be one of the first to participate in the Vialogues Distinguished Scholars Program. I will do my best to post about the our New York Police Officers. I have always been interested in the job of a New York police officer: the training, both physically and mentally as well as the laws that they have to learn, which are very interesting. A while ago I posted a Vialogue about police officers stopping and frisking random people as part of the city's overall efforts to reduce crime. The stop and frisk program has been a controversial program from the beginning and its the most talked about of the city's anti-violence programs. Please watch this video if you haven't done so and share your thoughts.
