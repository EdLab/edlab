Vialogues Ambassador Program

Francisco.Mendoza

September 20, 2013

One of the best experiences I've had in my time here at the EdLab thus far has been participating in the Vialogues Ambassador Program. The program has allowed me to research educational related videos and also even offered me a chance to find and create discussions on videos related to my eventual career in criminal justice. One of the other major components of the Vialogues Ambassador Program is to find potential users. So far, the majority of my promotion has focused on my individual relationships. Most people that I've spoken to sign up, but it has very challenging for me to get them to become active users. I spoke to other members of the Ambassador program and since all of us are current college students, we devised a strategy in which we will have one of our professors sign up and post a video that focuses on the content they are teaching in the course. The goal is to get our professors to share the video with the students via Vialogues and have them all register and participate in the video as part of their class participation.
