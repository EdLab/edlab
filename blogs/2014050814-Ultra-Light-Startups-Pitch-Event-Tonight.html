Ultra Light Startups Pitch Event Tonight

Kate Meersschaert

May 8, 2014

Tonight I will attend another Ultra Light Startups pitch event at Microsoft. These pitch events are always interesting for a variety of reasons (different verticals are represented, a few edtech companies and a more "classic" pitch night format). We discovered a number of "friends of EdLab" and OpenAssembly at these events! I will be sure to try to live Tweet & share a re-cap. (If you are interested in attending, please be sure to check with Hui Soo first, as there is a $30 fee associated.) Listed below are the panelists and presenters: Investor Panelists Murat Aktihanoglu - Founder at ER Accelerator Somak Chattopadhyay - Managing Partner at Armory Square Ventures Brian Cohen - Chairman at New York Angels Pankaj Jain - Venture Partner at 500 Startups Startup Pitches AdhereTech - Josh Stein RentHackr - Zeb Dropkin Shareswell - Emily Washkowitz Now In Store - Charles Brun Unlockable - Zach Goodman Lessonface - Claire Cunningham Lifedots - Rafael de Haro Weekdone - Juri Kalundi
