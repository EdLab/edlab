Very Interesting Dissertation

Anthony Cocciolo

January 28, 2009

There is this really interesting dissertation that dovetails very well with my talk I gave at the EdLab seminar 2 weeks ago. If you are at all interested in the content from that talk, you may want to check out: Schafer, Mirko Tobias. Bastard Culture! User participation and the extension of cultural industries. Seeing a dissertation that reminds you very much of your own is both a good thing and a bad thing. The good thing is you don't feel like you are the only person who gives a damn about what you are writing about (other than Gus of course!). It also makes you think it is actually worthy of "scholarly inquiry." The bad thing is you know have to figure out how it works with what you have already written. Overall though, more good than bad. Luckily, his is more theoretical and mine is more applied, so I think they are complimentary.
