Vialogues Wins of the Week: 07.17.14

Kate Meersschaert

July 17, 2014

Private!!!! Hi Everyone! Please find our "wins of the week" for vialogues.com listed below! As always, please feel-free to share your thoughts and any additions in the comments below! Thanks! Kate & Jo 7/9-7/15 Users- 96 Vialogues- 58 Visits- 7,181 7/2-7/8 Users- 84 Vialogues- 61 Visits- 23,212 Our ears were burning At University of Rhode Island's Digital Literacy Summer Institute, a session on "cool tools"-- featuring Vialogues-- was apparently a standout! Participants got a hands on demo where one person decided "Vialogues.com is an awesome tool for discussing and critiquing videos collaboratively with your students." Boom. UNCG celebrates one of their own Dr. Julie Edmunds's Voice episode was used to enhance an article on her research. Just in case you're not on the Trail Conference listserv... SiNY coverage of Trail U was mentioned in the NY-NJ Trail Conference "Press Clippings" section. Hikers may have been in the know, but now you are, too. A review of Vialogues using Voicethread This reviewer likes Vialogues almost as much as she loves Voicethread, and that's saying a lot. One thing she likes about Vialogues? When you use it, "you are part of a learning community." Read more. Blink and you'll miss us In a presentation on "Leveraging Digital Tools for Guided Inquiry," we find ourselves in familiar company. Summer institutes are so hot right now In what appears to be a page used as part of a workshop from the North Carolina Dept of Public Instruction, Vialogues is listed along with EdPuzzle as a resource for videos.
