Grockit

Dana Haugh

January 18, 2015


Grockit is an online test prep platform for students preparing for standardized exams such as the GMAT, SAT, ACT, and GRE. The platform adapts to each user’s skill level to deliver questions that will help students advance through the program quickly and effectively. Users learn strategies for approaching different types of questions as well as insight into how these questions are formed. Students can track their progress, target weak areas, and collaborate or compete with their peers.

Pros:

This platform combines social, independent, and collaborative exam prep techniques to motivate and enhance the student learning experience. Grockit offers prep courses on all major standardized tests, making it a valuable resource for students from high school to pre-med. After selecting the desired course, students are brought to a main dashboard where they have the option to take practice exams, create study plans, peruse instructional videos, request tutoring, and assess subject-specific strengths and weaknesses. The platform automatically adjusts to each user’s individual needs, revisiting problem areas, offering interactive hints and explanations, and monitoring the student’s retention and progress rates. Grockit’s intelligent interface enables students to see exactly where they need to improve; students can simply click on a problem area and begin reviewing and practicing the topic immediately. If students are looking for a little extra one-on-one help, they have the option of hiring one of Grockit’s ‘‘rockstar’’ tutors to ensure that they make the grade.

Cons:

As of January 2015, Grockit’s test prep dashboard is in beta mode (i.e., under construction) but full access to the platform still requires a subscription fee. Prices can be a bit steep, especially for users interested in short term access. The website’s blog, updated weekly since 2007, hasn’t published new content in five months.

Our Takeaway:

Students interested a comprehensive standardized test prep platform should consider using Grockit. The website offers extensive prep materials for all major exams on a convenient and organized interface. With Grockit, learners are able to identify and focus on their problem areas, making for a more productive and valuable study session. Although the platform requires a commitment-based subscription, the prices are comparable to traditional test prep manuals. Furthermore, the platform adapts to each individual user’s study needs, creating a more personalized material review.
