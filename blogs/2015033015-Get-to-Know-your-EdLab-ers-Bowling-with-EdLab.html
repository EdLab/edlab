Get to Know your EdLab-ers: "Bowling with EdLab"

JHeltz

March 30, 2015

**Keep Private** Greetings fellow co-workers! Newly displaced on the ground floor and confined to our cubicles, there's no better time to get to know the people you work with. If you have a few minutes to spare (this is a bowling pun) check out of fun pilot experiment video on Vialogues! Here's the link: https://vialogues.com/vialogues/play/20792/ Just sign in, request viewing access, and take satisfaction in the fact that you now know that face at the water cooler just a little better. Also, a big thanks to all participants, willing and unwilling alike. Enjoy!
