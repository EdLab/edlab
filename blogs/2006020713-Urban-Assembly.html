Urban Assembly

Anna Beard

February 7, 2006

I thought you might be interested in checking out the website for the Urban Assembly (an organization that starts public schools in NYC for kids that would otherwise have to attend low income schools): Urban Assembly The Founder Richard Kahan is a real dreamer. He comes up with ideas for theme-based high schools (ex: The UA School of Business for Young Women, The Academy for Careers in Sports, etc) and then is a miracle-maker when it comes to securing the funds for the schools. All of the schools are partnered with organizations (such as museums, zoos, professional sports teams, courts, etc) so that the kids get exposed to professions in fields related to their interests. So far the schools beat the competition when it comes to graduation rates, sending kids to four year colleges, etc. I wish I could have gone to one of Mr. Kahan's schools when I was growing up! A
