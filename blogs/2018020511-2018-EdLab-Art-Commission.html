2018 EdLab Art Commission!

ZKL

February 5, 2018

Are you an artist? Do you know any? If so this is a great opportunity. Funded by the Myers Foundation and supported by our very own Design Team, the yearly EdLab Art Commission has made the Gottesman Libraries a place to find great art at the intersection of Education, Technology and Creativity.


Please spread the word, info below and on Rhizr!


EdLab now accepting entries for 2018 Art Commissions:


EdLab is offering funding up to $20,000, exhibition space and support to artists who explore the intersections between education, technology, data, art, and media.Â 

The goal of EdLab Art Commissions is to create novel, meaningful, and transformative experiences in the context of a graduate school of education, health, and psychology. Selected artists complete multimedia art projects, which lead to public outcomes including installations and performances, seminars, public discussion, and publication.

Please visit the 2018 Art Commission page for further information, dates, and application guidelines.

Application deadline is March 5th 2018.
