Snapshot of Trends @ SXSW 2012

Kate Meersschaert

March 12, 2012

Embedded below is an infographic-based view of trending topics from this past Saturday's SXSW festival's social media chatter. One of the top trending events was Apple's announcement that they will open an Austin, TX-based campus. It is also interesting to note that the main SXSW site advertises the festival's official magazine, "SXSW World" which is available as both a physically and as an e-pub version. The publication is basically half original content and half advertising.
