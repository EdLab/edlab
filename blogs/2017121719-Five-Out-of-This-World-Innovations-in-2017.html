Five Out of This World Innovations in 2017

Brian.Sweeting

December 17, 2017


This year innovators looked to other worlds, both outer space and augmented reality, to find answers to real life terrestrial problems. Here are some out-of-this-world vialogues you may have missed during 2017.

1. Google Street View... in Space!
What do Google, the International Space Station, and Thinkspace have in common? Their collaborative space station is a monument to international cooperation and world peace.

2. NASA Challenge Has Students Launching Mini-Satellites into Deep Space
Citizen scientists and engineers rise to the Cube Quest Challenge, which will deploy satellites into deep space from the Orion spacecraft in 2019.

3. AR is Changing the Way We Perceive Our World
Who would have thought Pokémon Go would release happiness endorphins in us, or that AR would let us layer information onto a real world?

4. Alien Cartoon Helps Traumatized Children Talk in Therapy
Gender neutral aliens convince children to open up about their problems by putting their minds at ease.

5. A Shape-shifting Robot Inspired by Nature
Nature’s doppelganger? The sky’s the limit for this bot that acts like a tendril, lengthening its body to move through obstacles.
