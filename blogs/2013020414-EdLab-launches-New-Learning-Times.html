EdLab launches New Learning Times

John Sarubbi

February 4, 2013

This past Thursday, January 31st, the lobby of TC's Zankel Hall was busier than usual, as Teachers College commenced its year-long celebration of its 125th year. EdLab had a table at the kick off event to promote its newest app, New Learning Times (NLT) which is part of a series of new learning technologies available to the TC academic community. New Learning Times is a publishing project that highlights new ideas for the transformation of the Education Sector. NLT, which began publishing daily in October 2012, features content that illustrates current developments and future possibilities in education that promise to make learning more accessible, more powerful, and less costly. Please visit New Learning Times at http://www.newlearningtimes.com To learn about all the exciting projects at EdLab, please visit http://edlab.tc.columbia.edu/ and select PROJECTS.
