Griti

Jenny Shen

July 24, 2016


"Griti provides students everywhere with convenient, affordable and effective learning support online to help them stay caught up and motivated in their courses. We work with schools directly to offer students live tutoring and mentorship aimed at improving measured learning outcomes." (About Griti)

Video Support in College Courses

If you're familiar with the online learning scene, you're surely no stranger to the likes of Khan Academy, Coursera, and edX, all giants in the free online learning community. Griti is a little different. Although still centered around structured videos, its library is not filled with universities’ courses developed specifically for the online public. Instead, it features an array of tutorial videos designed to help students better understand the concepts behind each course. The videos are developed by Griti experts, students who already took the course and received an A. Experts have to apply through Griti, providing proof of how well they fared, and each course provides a brief description of the experts responsible for creating the videos.

A Different Kind of Tutoring

Depending on how a student prefers to learn or be tutored, Griti is either the perfect resource or just another contribution to information overload. If a student who is taking intro to chemistry at UC Berkeley needs a tutor to help in a few difficult areas, Griti’s sweeping array of videos may not be the best solution. But if another student needs to have the content explained in a different, step-by-step way by someone other than the professor, Griti might be the perfect option for her.
