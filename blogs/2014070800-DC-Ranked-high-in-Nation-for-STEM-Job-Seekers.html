D.C. Ranked high in Nation for STEM Job Seekers

rendy

July 8, 2014

Just graduated? Seeking for a job in STEM? Well, there's good news. People don't need to look for jobs in San Francisco or Boston for a career. According to recent news,, D.C. is currently ranked third in the nation for STEM job seekers. STEM jobs take over 12 percent of D.C.'s market, and average wage is about $99,000 or higher.

The report's analyst stated that he wasn't surprised at D.C. being ranked that high, and believes there are more opportunities for recent STEM grads in our nation's capital. This comes on the heels of a recent report by the Brookings Institute that highlights the lack of quality skilled workers in STEM. Some estimates note that STEM jobs take about twice as long to fill up than any other job.
