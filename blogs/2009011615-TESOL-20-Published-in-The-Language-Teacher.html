TESOL 2.0 Published in The Language Teacher

Joachim Castellano

January 16, 2009

Skye MacLeod, Tara Tarpey, and I had an article published in the January 2009 issue of The Language Teacher, a monthly publication of the Japan Association for Language Teaching (JALT). The article will be available here for free download in January 2010. However, for all of you who don't have a subscription to The Language Teacher, you won't have to wait a year to read it! It's a short lesson plan intended to give teachers ideas for classroom activities. In the piece we discuss creative ways of using blogs in a foreign language learning environment. I'm attaching a PDF of the final draft before submitting it to the journal. "TESOL 2.0 Using Blogs in three ways" first appeared in The Language Teacher (Volume 33 Issue 1, January 2009), published by the Japan Association for Language Teaching."
