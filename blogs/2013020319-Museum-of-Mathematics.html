Museum of Mathematics

EdLab Studios

February 3, 2013


There is but one museum of mathematics in North America and it just opened its doors to the public in NYC. With the goal to create exhibits that are all interactive, engaging and even beautiful, MoMath hopes to shatter connotations of math being dry and boring and help visitors realize math is all around you, particularly as technology becomes even more central in our daily lives.
