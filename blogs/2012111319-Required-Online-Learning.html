Required Online Learning

Janice Joo

November 13, 2012


A small number of states are finally advocating technology literacy through new education policies. Alabama, Florida and Michigan already require one online class in order to graduate from high school. New Mexico also requires one online class credit to graduate, but offers alternative equivalents.
