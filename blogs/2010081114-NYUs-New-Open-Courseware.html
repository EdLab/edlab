NYU's New Open Courseware

Nichole Hornig

August 11, 2010

While some universities are shying away from making video databases available online due to cost and feasibility, NYU is attempting an ambitious new project in open courseware. However, this material would be available only to NYU students, saving the university from dealing with certain copyright restrictions. Interestingly, this material would also be incorporated into a social-networking platform. Check out the article "Mass Video Courses May Free Up Professors for More Personalized Teaching" in the Chronicle of Higher Education.
