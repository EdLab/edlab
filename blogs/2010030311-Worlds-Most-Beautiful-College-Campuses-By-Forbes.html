World's Most Beautiful College Campuses By Forbes

Ellen Qian

March 3, 2010

Forbes asked a panel of architects and campus designers to nominate their picks for the most beautiful campuses in the world. There are 14 universities on the list,10 of which are from the U.S with one each from England, Italy, Ireland and China. 1. Kenyon College,Ohio, U.S 2. Oxford, England 3. Princeton, New Jersey, U.S 4. Scripps College, California, U.S 5. Stanford University, California, U.S 6. Trinity College, Ireland 7. Tsinghua University, BJ, China 8. United States Air Force Academy, Colorado,U.S 9. University of Bologna, Italy 10. University of California-Santa Cruz, California, U.S 11. University of Cincinnati, Ohio, U.S 12. University of Virginia, Virginia, U.S 13. Wellesley College, Massachusetts, U.S 14. Yale University, Connecticut, U.S

I think this kind of rank mechanism is a little bit funny as there are a lot of schools with beautiful campuses which do not appear on the list such as Harvard. Furthermore, architectures and designers may have different flavors but Forbes didn't say who they were and the criteria they used in creating the list.

I am just wondering that maybe in the future there could be a university rank based on other things such as dining to inform prospective students who may view dining as a key factor in the school they choose to attend.
