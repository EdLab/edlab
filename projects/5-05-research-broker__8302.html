5.05 ResearchBroker

ResearchBroker aims to connect researchers at Teachers College, Columbia University with the companies and organizations who are looking for researchers to analyze data, study their system or design a research study. In this way, the researchers can get hands-on experience working with professionals in the education industry and also potentially use the research towards their thesis or dissertation.


This project has ended on January 2018
