5.02 Collaboration Opportunities at EdLab

EdLab Seminars

EdLab Seminars bring speakers from around the corner and around the world to the Gottesman Libraries in a continuing exploration of new possibilities for advancing education for the post-industrial, information-centric world. These weekly seminars are open to guests interested in learning more about a range of EdLab programs and services. For more information click here.


EdLab Summer Internships for High School Students, College Students, and Teachers

The summer internship program provides students in college or high school and teachers at all career stages with an opportunity to learn how to do advanced production work in the field of education. EdLab interns can be involved in software development, media design, video production, publishing, and research and consulting activities. For more information click here.


Engagement Opportunities for PK-16 Teachers and Students

EdLab at Teachers College is committed to developing deep on-going opportunities for engagement with teachers and students in the PK-16 education sector as part of its mission of developing the infrastructure for education in the post-industrial, information-based era. EdLab welcomes inquiries from teachers and students interested in the future of education and the creation of new possibilities for teaching and learning. Current activities that might involve teachers and students are listed below, but we welcome new ideas for other types of collaboration from PK-16 students, teachers, and administrators.


Advancing Change Agendas with the EdLab Collaborative Solutions Group

The Collaborative Solutions Group at EdLab offers a range of uniquely customized consulting and planning services to schools, school districts, and other educational organizations seeking to meet the increasing demands for education in the post-industrial age. The CSG works to with clients to develop high-impact development opportunities to advance a variety of change agendas. For more information click here.


EdLab/HBO Watch It & Learn Events

In partnership with HBO, the EdLab Collaborative Solutions Group will work with schools to develop an educational event drawing on an HBO film or documentary television program. EdLab/HBO learning events typically involve activities in the classroom along with visits to the HBO screening facilities in mid-town Manhattan. For more information click here.
