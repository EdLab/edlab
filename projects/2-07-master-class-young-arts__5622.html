2.07 Master Class/Young Arts

There is a growing interest in supporting schools to provide students with an education in the arts. EdLab is collaborating with faculty in the Department of Arts and Humanities to develop digital assets to accompany a study guide produced to support the use of the HBO series Master Class to encourage student interest in the arts. EdLab is developing the online learning site featuring educational resources. EdLab will also develop face-to-face professional development workshops for teachers to be offered at the Gottesman Libraries and online professional development videos. In addition, EdLab will provide a classroom blogging platform to each teacher using the study guide. A viral marketing campaign designed and executed by EdLab will highlight all of the project activities, and a research and evaluation component will track the progress of the project.
